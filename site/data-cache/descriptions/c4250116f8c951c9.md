## Your Mission

We are looking for a motivated HR Intern to support the Human Resources team with day-to-dayadministrative activities across core HR functions. This internship provides hands-on exposure to HRoperations, including employee records management, employment documentation, and administrativesupport. The role offers an excellent opportunity to gain practical HR experience in a professional,collaborative environment.

## Sneak preview of your future tasks

<ul><li>Maintain and update employee records (paper and electronic), ensuring data accuracy and completeness.</li><li>Support HRIS data entry related to new hires, changes, and terminations.</li><li>Assist in preparing HR documentation, including employment contracts, amendments, onboarding documents, and termination paperwork, in line with legal and internal requirements.</li><li>Act as a point of contact for basic employee HR queries, providing accurate information or redirecting to the appropriate HR contact.</li><li>Support the administration of training programs by maintaining training records and assisting with session organization.</li><li>Provide administrative support related to employee benefits, including record updates and basic employee inquiries.</li><li>Maintain internal HR knowledge bases (e.g. SharePoint or Confluence), ensuring content is accurate and up to date.</li><li>Assist with internal communication materials, events and initiatives.</li></ul>

## Your Profile

<ul><li>Currently pursuing or recently completing a degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.</li><li>Good proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint); ability to quickly learn HR systems.</li><li>Good written and verbal communication skills with a professional approach.</li><li>Strong attention to detail.</li><li>High level of discretion and ability to handle confidential information.</li><li>Collaborative mindset and ability to work effectively across teams.</li><li>Proactive attitude, willingness to learn, and readiness to take ownership of assigned tasks.</li><li>Fluency in English</li></ul>
