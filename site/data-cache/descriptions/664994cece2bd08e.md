## About the company

Openprovider is an ICANN-accredited domain registrar and technology company founded in 2004 in Rotterdam, Netherlands. <br><br>We are a fully-remote organisation with more than 100 team members spread across 20+ countries. Remote working means no office, no painful commuting, and no stressful traffic - all you need is yourself, a laptop, and a cup of coffee!<br><br>A diverse and inclusive organisation, we support a healthy work/life balance and are constantly looking for new ways to help the well-being of our people. We support a flexible schedule and are 100% performance and result-oriented.<br><br>At Openprovider, we're not just a company; we're a mission-driven team dedicated to transforming the domain industry. We believe in providing a trusted digital identity to every business. <br><br>Our innovative approach is reshaping the industry from a traditional transactional model to a more customer-centric, subscription-based approach. We pay subscriptions for music, newspapers, and movies - why should domains be any different?<br><br>Come join us on our journey to create a trusted digital world.

## About the role

<p>You’ll be joining a global team of support professionals working fully remotely across multiple time zones. The department is divided into 1st line support, focused on fast, customer-facing requests, and 2nd line support, handling deeper technical investigations and more complex cases.</p><p>Our team’s priority is making customers happy by helping them solve issues through self-service and providing quick, effective solutions whenever personal assistance is needed. </p><p><strong>This role operates on rotating shifts, including nights, weekends, and holidays, to ensure we provide 24/7 coverage. Please review the schedule carefully before applying to make sure it works for you.</strong></p><p><strong>Working Schedule (Bogotá Time)</strong></p><p>First 2 months (Onboarding &amp; Training)<br>Monday–Friday: 3:00 AM – 11:30 AM</p><p>After onboarding (Night Shift Rotation)<br>Monday–Friday: 3:00 PM – 11:30 PM</p><p>Weekend Rotational Shifts <em>(two days off per week are maintained)</em></p><ul><li>Shift 1: 11:00 PM – 7:30 AM</li><li>Shift 2: 7:00 AM – 3:30 PM</li><li>Shift 3: 3:00 PM – 11:30 PM</li></ul>We’re looking for a self-organised, passionate support specialist who’s hungry to learn, enjoys figuring out complicated problems, loves communicating with people, and wants to grow real technical depth.

## Key responsibilities

<ul><li><p style="margin-top:0px;margin-right:0px;margin-bottom:0px;">Work in rotational shifts to provide 24/7 support to customers</p></li><li><p>Providing excellent quality of service to our customers and solving their problems effectively communicating via phone, chat and ticketing system</p></li><li><p>Following Out of Office procedures, keeping an open line of communication with the team and respecting time shifts.</p></li><li><p>Solving a variety of tasks using internal toolsets around products like SSL certificates, domains, Plesk licenses and Spamexperts</p></li><li><p>Actively using, maintaining and improving our knowledge base, making it a single point of truth for customers and peers</p></li><li><p>Working closely with other departments around the world to solve customers' problems effectively &amp; improve the quality of our products and services</p></li><li><p>Analyzing your day-to-day operations and processes within the department to generate ideas on manual work automation and processes improvement</p></li><li><p>Actively learning, on your own and with the team, about our products, technologies, and tools</p></li><li><p>Taking care of abuse request and enduser queue</p></li></ul>
