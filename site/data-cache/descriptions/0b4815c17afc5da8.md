<p>Role is remote in the United States&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>You will:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Directly manage a team of LE Ops agents (FTE and BPO leads), including goal-setting, performance management, coaching, and career development.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Own queue health and operational performance for a major slice of LE work (e.g., U.S. content requests, international requests, preservation &amp; emergency), delivering on SLA, quality, and compliance targets.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Design and maintain standard operating procedures, playbooks, and training for your area (onboarding, recurrent training, updates for new laws/tooling).&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Partner with Legal, Policy, Privacy Eng, and Safety to implement new legal or regulatory requirements (e.g., DSA/OSA, CLOUD Act, preservation, automation changes) into day-to-day workflows.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Work with Privacy Engineering and tooling teams to scope, test, and roll out automation and new LE tools, reducing manual handling of PII and error rates.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Lead incident-driven and media-sensitive workstreams (e.g., missing-minor cases, SIEs tied to LE requests), ensuring tight coordination with Critical Harms, Comms, and Public Policy teams.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Own metrics, dashboards, and reviews for your domain: volumes, turnaround, QA trends, rework, and capacity planning; present findings and plans to LE Engagement and Legal/Public Policy leadership.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Contribute to multi-year roadmap and headcount planning for LE Ops (e.g., BPO vs. FTE mix, regional coverage, specialization).&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Represent LE Ops in cross-functional strategy forums (e.g., LE automation, Trusted Flagger, outreach programs) and help align operational reality with external commitments.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years in&nbsp;law enforcement response, trust &amp; safety operations, legal operations, or similar, with at least 2+ years people-management experience (direct reports).&nbsp;</li>
<li>Deep familiarity with US and International criminal legal process, ECPA, CLOUD Act, content vs. non-content, emergency disclosures, and cross-border constraints.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Proven track record managing an operations queue at scale (high volume, SLA-driven, quality-controlled environment).&nbsp;</li>
<li>Experience building or refining SOPs, training programs, QA frameworks, and operational dashboards.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Demonstrated success leading cross-functional projects with Legal, Policy, Eng, and Safety, including change-management and stakeholder alignment.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication skills; comfortable presenting to senior leadership and translating nuanced operational risk into clear recommend</li>
</ul>
