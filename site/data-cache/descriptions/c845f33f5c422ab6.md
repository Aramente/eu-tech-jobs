<p>The Quantum Systems Land Domain is developing leading-edge autonomous UGV technology for defense — a domain where no blueprint exists. The product strategy starts with a UGV that is useful on today’s battlefields and can be built with current technology, then progressively increases the authority of the autonomy system while reducing the need for human supervision — across ever more complex operating environments, up to fully autonomous operation in proximity to civilians.</p>
<p>As a senior system safety expert, you will own and shape this discipline from the ground up: define the safety case architecture, build the processes, and set the standard. You will work across product management, design assurance, product security, engineering teams, and V&amp;V to ensure safety is embedded in the product from hazard analysis through to operational deployment. As the product matures, you will grow your domain into a team.</p>

<h2>How you will leave your footprint</h2>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain the system safety process and safety case architecture.</li>
<li>Define and maintain the Operational Design Domain (ODD) — environmental, operational, and scenario boundaries for safe operation.</li>
<li>Conduct CONOPS scenario analysis — operational scenarios, mode transitions, degraded modes, and edge cases.</li>
<li>Conduct hazard analysis and risk assessment (HARA) — system-level hazards, risk ratings, safety functions, and operational risk reduction measures.</li>
<li>Conduct SOTIF analysis — performance limitations and triggering conditions that lead to hazardous behavior without component faults.</li>
<li>Define safety functions and safety integrity targets without prescribing implementation architecture.</li>
<li>Define system-level validation criteria for safe behavior in operational scenarios.</li>
<li>Own system-level risk acceptance — residual risk arguments, release conditions, and operational restrictions.</li>
<li>Define operational measures — operator procedures, training requirements, warnings, and use restrictions.</li>
<li>Manage the regulatory register and safety-relevant compliance matrices (Machinery Regulation, AI Act).</li>
<li>Allocate safety functions and integrity targets to Design Assurance and Product Security; arbitrate safety-security conflicts (e.g., fail-open vs. fail-secure); coordinate cross-domain risk management.</li>
<li>Communicate safety functions and ODD constraints as system requirements to engineering teams; review architecture proposals for hazard coverage; participate in scenario reviews and field trials.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Quantum Systems core group on safety aspects of the C3 system (MOSAIC, GCS) and multi-domain operation.</li>
<li>Define validation scenarios and acceptance criteria for V&amp;V including edge cases, degraded modes, and SOTIF triggering conditions; review and accept validation evidence for safety claims.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain AI-assisted workflows for safety analysis, compliance auditing, and traceability management.</li>
</ul>
