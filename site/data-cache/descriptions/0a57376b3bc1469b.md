Spotify’s Audiobooks Mission is transforming how creators and listeners experience long-form storytelling. We’re building the foundation that enables audiobook content to flow seamlessly from publishers to listeners — including the systems that power ingestion, processing, and delivery across a rapidly growing global catalog.

We’re looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join a small, high-impact team within the Audiobooks Pipeline area. This team is responsible for building and scaling systems that ingest, process, and transform audiobook and eBook content, metadata, and royalties — ensuring the right content reaches listeners efficiently and with the highest quality.

While this role is full stack in scope, the majority of work will lean toward backend and data-focused development. We’re looking for someone who thrives in ambiguity, loves to learn, and is eager to stretch beyond their comfort zone to solve challenging problems across the stack.

## What You’ll Do

- Design and build systems that power audiobook ingestion, transformation, and delivery — ensuring performance, accuracy, and scalability across millions of titles.
- Contribute across the stack, from backend services and APIs to lightweight interfaces or operational tools, depending on the team’s evolving needs.
- Collaborate with engineers, PMs, and designers to translate sophisticated workflows into elegant, maintainable, and reliable systems.
- Be responsible for your impact — take operational responsibility for the services you build and drive continuous improvement in reliability and performance.
- Adapt and T-shape: Step into areas beyond your comfort zone (e.g., data pipelines, internal tooling, or deployment infrastructure) as the mission evolves.
- Author and review RFCs, sharing your insights and shaping technical decisions that affect the broader Audiobooks ecosystem.
- Embrace the Audiobooks Mission expectations: operate with “short toes,” take ownership beyond team boundaries, and value pragmatic, reversible decisions.
- Mentor and collaborate with peers, sharing knowledge and contributing to an inclusive, psychologically safe engineering culture.

## Who You Are

- You have 5+ years of software engineering experience, ideally across backend systems and distributed architectures.
- You’re highly proficient in Spotify's backend tech stack (Java/gRPC/GCP).
- You have some data experience and a willingness to work across the stack when needed — not because you have to, but because you’re curious.
- You care about clean, maintainable, and testable code and take ownership of quality and operational excellence.
- You thrive in ambiguous, fast paced environments, enjoy context-switching, and value quick, pragmatic decision-making.
- You demonstrate data proficiency, curiosity about the business domain, and a desire to understand the broader audiobook ecosystem.
- You’re collaborative, humble, and growth-minded — adopting Spotify’s playful culture and Audiobooks Mission ethos of experimentation, ownership, and continuous learning.

## Where You’ll Be

- This role is based in London or Stockholm.
- We offer you the flexibility to work where you work best! There will be some in person meetings, but still allows for flexibility to work from home.
