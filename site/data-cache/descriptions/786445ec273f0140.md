### **Role overview**

We are looking for a **strategic storyteller** to join our Business Intelligence team. You will act as a critical "backend engine," enabling data-driven decisions for both our supply (platforms) and demand (sales) sides.

You will translate complex data insights into high-impact visual narratives—such as pitch decks, Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs), and strategic proposals—that represent our company to global platforms like TikTok, Microsoft, Google, X, Spotify and others.

## What you'll do:

- **Turn data into clear business narratives.** Transform complex datasets, market insights, and sales performance metrics into clear, visually compelling stories that support strategic decision-making across the organization.
- **Create high-impact presentations and materials.** Design polished presentations, pitch decks, and reports (PowerPoint / Google Slides) used in executive briefings, partner discussions, and strategic initiatives.
- **Support strategic projects across the business.** Contribute to projects such as platform RFP responses, quarterly business reviews (QBRs), and strategic analyses that shape Aleph’s partnerships and revenue strategy.
- **Collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders.** Work closely with data analysts, strategists, sales teams, and regional leadership to gather inputs, synthesize insights, and ensure information is communicated clearly.
- **Translate insights into actionable recommendations.** Help teams understand performance trends, opportunities, and risks by packaging insights into structured, easy-to-consume outputs.
- **Elevate the quality of business communication.** Establish clear storytelling frameworks, templates, and visual standards that improve how Aleph communicates data and strategy internally and with partners.
- **Contribute to global initiatives.** Support projects across **APAC, LATAM, MENA, EMEA** and platforms, gaining exposure to global advertising markets and a wide range of strategic business challenges.

## Who you are:

- **A strong communicator and storyteller.** You can turn complex information into clear messages and compelling narratives tailored to different audiences.
- **Highly skilled in presentation design.** You are confident creating structured, visually engaging presentations using PowerPoint or Google Slides and have a strong eye for layout, clarity, and visual storytelling.
- **Business-minded and analytical.** You enjoy working with data and insights and can connect numbers with business context to highlight key opportunities or trends.
- **Comfortable working with stakeholders.** You can collaborate with cross-functional teams and communicate effectively with colleagues at different levels of the organization.
- **Structured and detail-oriented.** You bring clarity to complex information, organize ideas logically, and ensure deliverables meet a high quality standard.
- **Experienced with basic data tools.** You are comfortable using Excel or Google Sheets (e.g., pivot tables, basic analysis) to understand and organize data.
- **Interested in digital media or advertising.** Experience in digital advertising, media agencies, consulting, research, or similar environments is a plus but not required.
- **Curious and eager to learn. Whether you come with a few years of experience or are an early-career professional, you are motivated to learn quickly and grow in a global, fast-paced environment.**
