<p>Operations is a core pillar of Klaviyo’s global Go-to-Market organization. We provide the insights and design, build, and maintain the systems and processes our customer-facing teams rely on to accelerate revenue. In APAC, we partner closely with GTM leadership across Sales, Partnerships, Marketing and Customer Success to achieve our revenue targets for the region. As Klaviyo continues to grow in APAC and expand into new markets, the APAC Operations team plays a critical role in leading strategy &amp; planning, driving operational excellence and connecting the dots across lead management, pipeline analytics, forecasting and customer growth and retention. The team also owns management reporting and the administration of the company’s sales policy and systems.&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>About the Role</strong></h2>
<p>As the Senior Sales Operations Manager APAC, you will own the analytics, processes, and reporting that empower our customer-facing GTM teams to exceed pipeline and ARR targets. You’ll join at an inflection point—helping to build scalable frameworks in a hyper-growth environment, while shaping the future of the APAC organization. You are a hands-on analyst with an ability to distill complex data from Salesforce, Tableau and SQL into actionable insights. You thrive under tight deadlines, can juggle conflicting priorities, and are energized by partnering cross-functionally and working with colleagues across different functions and in different regions to solve complex problems. Reporting to the Operations Director (APAC), you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive operational excellence across pipeline management and sales forecasting</li>
<li>Partner with GTM leadership to optimize funnel performance and drive sales productivity improvements</li>
<li>Establish monitoring for top accounts including key account engagement, customer health and adoption</li>
<li>Own regional lead routing rules, lead scoring enhancements and campaign attribution</li>
<li>Own territory management and alignment for and across PAM, PSS, BDR, SA and CSM&nbsp;</li>
<li>Build dashboards and reports and conduct analyses in support of regional and global business reviews</li>
<li>Contribute to the localization of GTM technology and data enrichment&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
