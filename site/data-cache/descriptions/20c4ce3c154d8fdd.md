<p><strong>Senior CRM Manager – SumUp Pay</strong></p>
<p><strong>About the team</strong></p>
<p>SumUp is building a full-featured personal account for consumers across Europe - making it easy to spend globally, manage money, and get rewarded for everyday spending.</p>
<p>This is the first dedicated CRM hire for the Consumer team – a rare opportunity to build lifecycle strategy from scratch and own how we communicate with customers across every stage of their journey, from first install through to long-term habit.</p>
<p>High ownership, high impact, with real scope to shape how SumUp Pay talks to its customers for years to come.</p>
<p><strong>What you'll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the customer lifecycle strategy to enhance engagement and retention with a relentless focus on the moments that drive growth</li>
<li>Develop and launch multi channel (Email, Push, In-App, WhatsApp) campaigns by forming hypotheses. Taking a test-and-learn approach and move fast – this is not a set-and-forget role</li>
<li>Proactively monitor product usage to identify friction points and translate them into CRM strategies and/or product improvement recommendations</li>
<li>Define and manage user segments, run A/B tests, and continuously iterate on activation, engagement, and retention across six European markets</li>
<li>Partner with the Go to Market (GTM) team to align messaging, timing, and targeting with broader SumUp growth initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You'll be great for this role if you…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Have hands-on, end to end ownership of customer lifecycle campaigns and strategy within a high-growth consumer product – fintech or neobank experience a strong plus</li>
<li>Think in funnels, cohorts, and activation/engagement rates – not just open rates and click-throughs</li>
<li>Are comfortable with leveraging event-driven architecture to design real-time, automated customer journeys that respond instantly to behaviours and lifecycle triggers</li>
<li>Acts as a strategic liaison between technical and non-technical teams, translating complex business requirements into actionable CRM campaigns and measurable results</li>
<li>Have built from scratch before – you're energised by the absence of a playbook, not slowed down by it</li>
<li>Are hands-on enough to unblock yourself: you can read event schemas, navigate a data model, and work alongside engineers as a peer</li>
</ul>
