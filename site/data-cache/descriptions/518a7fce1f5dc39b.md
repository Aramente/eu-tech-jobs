## Unlocking the full potential of forests, both ecologically and emotionally.

<strong style="font-weight:bolder;color:rgb(33,37,41);font-family:Montserrat, sans-serif;font-size:16px;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">What we do<br></strong><span style="color:rgb(33,37,41);font-family:Montserrat, sans-serif;font-size:16px;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;text-transform:none;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);display:inline;">Pina Earth enables forest owners to access carbon markets the easy way. We build software that automatically assesses forests and simulates its future development to allow us to quantify carbon stock changes over time. Forest owners can store additional carbon in their forests by adopting sustainable forest management practices. Each additional ton of carbon stored generates a "carbon credit". By investing in forest carbon projects, companies contribute to climate protection. Through the sale of carbon credits, forest owners benefit from a recurring income that is reinvested to create diverse, climate-resilient forests.</span><br style="color:rgb(33,37,41);font-family:Montserrat, sans-serif;font-size:16px;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;text-transform:none;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"><br style="color:rgb(33,37,41);font-family:Montserrat, sans-serif;font-size:16px;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;text-transform:none;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"><span style="color:rgb(33,37,41);font-family:Montserrat, sans-serif;font-size:16px;font-style:normal;font-weight:400;text-transform:none;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);display:inline;">Pina Earth is a fast-growing climate tech startup operating in Europe, based in Munich, Dornbirn, Austria, and with two subsidiaries in Berlin and Winterthur, Switzerland.
