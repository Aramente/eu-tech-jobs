### ⭐ **Mission**

Join us as a Senior Product Manager for our Connect team and help us shape the future of Qonto’s API and integration ecosystem, serving hundreds of thousands of SMEs. Acting as a strategic pillar for our team, you will drive the vision for our native integrations and Public APIs directly impacting our revenue and user retention.

### 👩‍💻🧑‍💻 **As a Senior Product Manager Connect at Qonto, you will:**

* **Define and execute the product strategy** for Qonto’s API and integration products, mapping the competitive landscape and aligning your roadmap with revenue, expansion, and retention goals.
* **Manage and scale our core integration products**, including native integrations (accounting, invoicing, ERPs), our Public API and the Developer Portal.
* **Build and nurture relationships with external partners**, ensuring smooth collaboration and building trust while maintaining Qonto’s high velocity.
* **Champion product quality**, ensuring that our integrations not only ship fast but also deliver a consistently reliable and exceptional experience for our users.
* **Collaborate cross-functionally** with Engineers, Market Directors, Customer Success, and other Product teams to support their specific integration needs across our different European markets.

### 🤔 **What you can expect:**

* **Market/Team Context:** You will join a highly impactful domain—roughly a third of our users actively use a Connect integration today.
* **Methodologies, how we work, and what we value:** We value deep strategic thinking over just shipping volume, with a strong emphasis on maintaining a high bar for product quality and user satisfaction.
* **Examples of successful projects we recently worked on:** We continuously build out our Qonto offering and country-specific native integrations that act as key levers for our Market Directors.
* **Tools we use:** You will operate in a technical ecosystem driven by public APIs, partner portals, and emerging technologies, keeping a close eye on how AI and Model Context Protocols (MCP) are changing the integration landscape.

### 🏅 **About You**

* **Experience:** You have a strong track record as a Product Manager working deeply with API products, Banking-as-a-Service, or complex native integrations, and you are genuinely passionate about this space.
* **Strategic thinker:** You can easily map the competitive landscape (looking at best-in-class API products beyond fintech) and define what to build—and what *not* to build.
* **Partner management:** You are highly comfortable engaging with external technical partners, challenging them when necessary, and building lasting trust.
* **Quality obsession:** You have a strong sensitivity to product quality and maintain high standards for reliability and user satisfaction beyond the initial feature release.
* **Excellent communicator:** You have strong cross-team communication skills to align diverse internal stakeholders (from engineering to market directors) and remain curious about emerging tech trends.
