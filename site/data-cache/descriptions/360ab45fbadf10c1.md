<p><strong>Role Description</strong></p>
<p>We're looking for a driven and commercially sharp Field Sales Consultant to join our growing Irish team. <p>If you're energised by targets, thrive in a face-to-face sales environment, and want to play a meaningful role in empowering Irish businesses with the financial tools they truly deserve, we want to hear from you.</p>
