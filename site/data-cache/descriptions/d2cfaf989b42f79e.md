<p>We are looking for an Internal Audit Manager to lead day-to-day audit execution in a regulated, high-volume payment business. This role sits at the core of the Internal Audit function and is responsible for converting audit plans into high-quality, actionable audit outcomes.</p>
<p>You will manage audit engagements end-to-end, supervise audit staff, and work closely with process owners across Operations, Technology, Finance, Risk, and Compliance to assess control effectiveness and drive remediation.</p>
<h3>Minimum Qualifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Minimum 2 years of experience in Internal Audit, Risk, or related functions within financial services, payments, fintech, or technology companies.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of internal controls, audit techniques, and risk assessment.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, communication, and people management skills.</li>
<li>Fluency in Indonesian and English.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Preferred Qualifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Exposure to transaction processing, system access controls, reconciliations, and operational risk is highly preferred</li>
<li>Professional certifications (CIA, CPA, CISA) are a plus.</li>
</ul>
<h3>You May Be a Good Fit If</h3>
<ul>
<li>You enjoy digging into processes and figuring out how things <em>actually</em> work.</li>
<li>You can balance attention to detail with big-picture risk thinking.</li>
<li>You communicate findings firmly but constructively.</li>
<li>You are comfortable working in fast-growing, evolving organizations.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Key Responsibilities and Expected Outcomes</h3>
<p><strong>Audit Planning &amp; Execution</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute internal audits based on the approved annual audit plan.</li>
<li>Define audit objectives, scope, and testing approach for assigned audits.</li>
<li>Perform walkthroughs, control testing, and root cause analysis for identified issues.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Team Supervision</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervise and review work performed by Internal Audit Staff.</li>
<li>Ensure audit documentation and evidence meet internal standards.</li>
<li>Provide coaching and on-the-job guidance to junior auditors.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Reporting &amp; Follow-Up</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Draft clear, concise audit reports highlighting risks, control gaps, and practical recommendations.</li>
<li>Discuss findings constructively with process owners and management.</li>
<li>Monitor remediation progress and validate closure of audit findings.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Compliance, Risk, Technology, Operations, and Finance teams to understand evolving processes and risks.</li>
<li>Support regulatory exams and external audits where relevant to internal audit scope.</li>
</ul>
