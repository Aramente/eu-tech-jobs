<ul>

<li>Drive the revenue month-end close process, ensuring consistent, timely delivery and continuous improvement in close quality and efficiency</li>
<li>Serve as a thought partner to the Director of Revenue Accounting by surfacing operational insights, risks, and improvement opportunities that inform the revenue accounting strategy</li>
<li>Elevate issues, patterns, and opportunities observed in execution into clear, actionable input that informs strategic decisions for the revenue accounting function</li>

</ul>
