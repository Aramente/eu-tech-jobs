We are looking for a highly skilled and experienced **Senior Systems Administrator** to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate brings deep expertise in Linux systems management, networking, web server administration, monitoring, hardware, databases, cybersecurity, and public cloud platforms — specifically AWS and GCP.

This role requires the ability to work independently, collaborate effectively with internal teams and external partners, and thrive in a fast-paced environment.

## What you'll do:

**Linux & Virtualisation**

- Install, administer, and maintain Linux operating systems, including Debian and RHEL distributions.
- Design, deploy, and maintain virtualisation environments (libvirt) and containerisation tools (LXC, Docker).
- Manage Kubernetes (K8s) clusters — deployments, services, scaling, and troubleshooting in production environments.

**L3 Networking**

- Strong knowledge of networking protocols and services: IP, routing (static, OSPF, BGP), DNS, VPN, HA technologies (bridging, keepalive), firewalls, and Cisco Meraki.
- Design and maintain secure, high-availability network infrastructure across distributed environments.

**Cloud Infrastructure — AWS & GCP**

- **AWS:** EC2, VPC, IAM, S3, RDS, EKS, CloudWatch, CloudTrail, Lambda. Experience with cost optimisation (Cost Explorer, Savings Plans) and security groups/policies.
- **GCP:** Compute Engine, GKE, Cloud Storage, Cloud SQL, VPC, IAM, and Cloud Monitoring. Familiarity with Terraform or similar IaC tools for cloud resource management.
- Experience managing multi-tenant cloud environments and migration projects (on-prem → cloud).
- Understanding of cloud security principles: encryption, secrets management (HashiCorp Vault, AWS Secrets Manager), Zero Trust networking.

**Web Servers & Databases**

- Configure and manage web servers: Nginx, Apache, PHP.
- Database administration with MySQL/MariaDB, including replication technologies (bin-log, Galera).
- Experience with Redis, Memcache, Supervisord, and Logstash.

**Monitoring & Security**

- Infrastructure monitoring using Nagios, Zabbix, Grafana, Prometheus, and Netdata.
- Apply cybersecurity principles including vulnerability management, SIEM, and XDR.
- Certificate lifecycle management (PKI, Let's Encrypt) and vulnerability scanning.

**Automation & IaC**

- Infrastructure as Code using Terraform, Ansible, or Puppet.
- Scripting in Bash, Python, or similar languages to automate repetitive operational tasks.
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (GitLab CI, GitHub Actions, or equivalent) is a plus.

**General Responsibilities**

- Infrastructure.
- Provide occasional L2 technical support assistance.
- Create and maintain technical documentation, knowledge base articles, and runbooks — Atlassian Stack (Confluence, Jira).
- Manage IT assets including hardware, software licences, and peripheral devices.
- Collaborate closely with external support teams and the R&D department on technology projects.

## What we are looking for:

- Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field — or equivalent practical experience.
- 5+ years of experience in Linux systems administration and IT infrastructure management.
- Proven hands-on experience with AWS and/or GCP in a production environment.
- Strong understanding of the technologies listed above.
- Excellent problem-solving skills with the ability to troubleshoot complex issues independently.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills; ability to collaborate effectively across teams and with external stakeholders.
- Demonstrated ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities simultaneously.
- Relevant certifications are a strong plus: RHCE, CCNA, AWS Solutions Architect, GCP Associate Cloud Engineer, or equivalent.
- Fluent written and spoken English.
