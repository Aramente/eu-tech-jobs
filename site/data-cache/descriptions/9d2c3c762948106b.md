<h2><strong>About the role:</strong></h2>
<p>Join us in redefining customer experience through Agentic AI and make sure our finance tech stack scales just as fast as we do. As our <strong>SAP Solution Architect – Finance Systems</strong>, you will own the end-to-end solution architecture across key finance domains (Order-to-Cash, Revenue Accounting, Procure-to-Pay, General Ledger, Tax, Treasury) and their integrations. You’ll translate business needs into clean, secure, and maintainable SAP architectures driving operational excellence through standardization, automation, and smart system design.</p>
<h2><strong>Areas of ownership:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical leadership by creating and reviewing high-quality architecture designs and technical solution concepts across the Finance Systems domain.</li>
<li>Lead solution design and support technical implementations for <strong>Order-to-Cash, Revenue Accounting, Procure-to-Pay, Record-to-Report</strong>, and cross-system integrations.</li>
<li>Own architecture decisions for <strong>SAP S/4HANA + Subscription Management </strong>and their touchpoints with adjacent modules and third-party tools.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Finance, RevOps, Business Operations, Data, and Engineering to streamline processes, reduce manual work, and improve controllership through better system flows and data quality.</li>
<li>Drive integration strategy and execution (e.g., APIs, middleware, event-based patterns where applicable), ensuring reliability, scalability, auditability, and clear ownership.</li>
<li>Establish and maintain architecture documentation, process maps, and enablement materials to support global finance users and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to project methodologies, change control, and operational best practices (testing, monitoring, incident handling, access controls).</li>
<li>Act as a trusted advisor to senior stakeholders—balancing business outcomes, technical constraints, and long-term maintainability.</li>
</ul>

<h2><strong>Who you are:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s or advanced degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Accounting, Engineering, Business - or equivalent practical experience.</li>
<li><strong>5+ years of progressive SAP hands-on experience</strong> in consulting and/or IT, including <strong>multiple large-scale, complex, global implementations</strong>.</li>
<li>Proven end-to-end experience in at least one full lifecycle <strong>SAP subscription billing </strong>deployment (Design, Build, Test, Support).</li>
<li>Strong expertise in <strong>SAP S/4HANA Cloud, Subscription Management and/or BRIM&nbsp;</strong></li>
<li><strong>Solution architecture capabilities</strong> across Finance, Order-to-Cash, and Procure-to-Pay processes</li>
<li>Understanding of <strong>integration patterns</strong> (APIs, middleware, SAP Integration Suite) and data migration strategies</li>
<li>Good working knowledge of SAP adjacent areas such as <strong>BW / SAC</strong> and <strong>SAP Security</strong> fundamentals (roles/authorizations concepts, segregation of duties awareness).</li>
<li>Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills - able to engage effectively across all levels of Business and IT.</li>
<li>Strong ownership mindset: you work independently, prioritize well, and can drive multiple initiatives in parallel.</li>
