</ul></div><h2>The Role</h2>
<p>At Gigs, we’re building the operating system for mobile services – a platform powering the next generation of connectivity products. Our Fintech customers are at the center of this story. As a <strong>Strategic Partner Manager</strong>, you’ll own and grow our most important relationships.</p>
<p>This isn’t a standard account management role. It’s part commercial growth, part customer success, part business strategy. You’ll act as a <strong>trusted advisor</strong> to executives at fast-scaling fintechs, while ensuring Gigs becomes a critical growth lever for their business. Your mission: drive revenue, retention, and long-term partnership value.</p>
<h2>What You Will Do</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Drive growth with our most strategic customers. </strong>Own, build, and expand partnerships with Gigs’ largest and most complex fintech accounts. Negotiate renewals and expansions, identify new revenue streams, and ensure customers see measurable ROI from Gigs.</li>
<li><strong>Act as a trusted advisor. </strong>Develop a deep understanding of your customers’ businesses, use cases, and KPIs. Anticipate needs, surface risks, and guide them as they scale into new markets, products, and geographies.</li>
<li><strong>Operate in a high-growth environment. </strong>Roll up your sleeves in a scaling company where playbooks are still being written. Help design and refine processes that enable both Gigs and our customers to grow faster, smarter, and more sustainably.</li>
<li><strong>Work cross-functionally. </strong>Partner with Go-To-Market, Marketing, Support, Implementation, and Product to deliver seamless launches, campaigns, and product rollouts. Ensure a best-in-class customer experience from onboarding to renewal.</li>
<li><strong>Balance commercial acumen with customer success. </strong>Own a revenue target and negotiate commercial terms while also focusing on adoption, value delivery, and retention. Bridge the worlds of account management and customer success for long-term growth.</li>
</ul>
