<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><p><strong>ABOUT THE TEAM:</strong></p>
<p>The Member Accession Marketing Team (India) plays a crucial role in driving Tide’s growth in the Indian market by acquiring and onboarding new members from small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). The team tailors marketing strategies to engage potential members, guide them through seamless onboarding, and ensure they are well-equipped to leverage Tide’s suite of financial and administrative tools. By creating localized campaigns, building strong relationships, and leveraging data for continuous improvement, the team fosters long-term member engagement—ensuring Tide becomes the go-to platform for Indian SMEs.</p>
<p><strong>ABOUT THE ROLE:</strong></p>
<p>As an Inside Sales Team Lead (UK Market), you’ll be responsible for leading, coaching, and inspiring a team of Inside Sales Executives who engage directly with Tide’s UK business members. Your mission is to drive performance, uphold quality standards, and deliver consistent commercial results across a portfolio of UK SMEs, ensuring every member conversation is high-impact and aligned with Tide’s growth strategy.</p>
<p>You’ll work closely with other Team Leads and Managers to support cross-functional projects, provide structured feedback to Product and Marketing, and help scale new initiatives across our sales teams.</p>
<h4>IN THIS ROLE YOU WILL:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Lead and motivate a team of Inside Sales Executives to achieve and exceed sales and quality KPIs across our UK SME members</li>
<li>Coach and develop team members through regular 1:1s, QA reviews, and performance sessions</li>
<li>Analyse sales data and performance metrics to identify trends, opportunities, and training needs specific to the UK market</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Managers and cross-functional teams (Product, CRM, BI, Marketing) to refine lead strategies and campaign execution for UK audiences</li>
<li>Ensure accurate CRM usage and data hygiene across your team</li>
<li>Maintain a strong understanding of current and upcoming Tide products, ensuring your team is equipped to pitch effectively to UK small business owners</li>
<li>Handle escalations or complex member interactions as a point of expertise</li>
<li>Support recruitment, onboarding, and continuous improvement initiatives across the Inside Sales function</li>
<li>Take ownership of team morale, engagement, and a culture of accountability and excellence</li>
</ul>
<h4>WHAT MAKES YOU A GREAT FIT:</h4>
<p>We’re looking for a sales-driven leader who combines commercial acumen with a people-first mindset. You’ll be:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experienced in outbound telesales or inside sales (minimum 3 years), with at least 1 year in a leadership or coaching role</li>
<li>Proven in delivering and exceeding sales targets within the UK market</li>
<li>Skilled in consultative and value-based sales with small business customers, focused on a phone based selling approachn</li>
<li>Confident in analysing performance data and using insights to improve outcomes</li>
<li>Comfortable in a high-growth, fast-paced fintech environment with evolving priorities</li>
<li>A strong communicator with a professional command of English (C1 level preferred)</li>
<li>Highly organised, detail-oriented, and proactive in identifying blockers and solutions</li>
<li>Proficient with CRM tools (e.g., Kustomer or Salesforce) and experienced in data-driven environments</li>
<li>Passionate about empowering small businesses and excited by Tide’s mission</li>
</ul>
<h3>Bonus Points For:</h3>
