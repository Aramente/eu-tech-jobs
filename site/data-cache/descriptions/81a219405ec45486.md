AI can already do a lot — but only if it’s actually integrated into real workflows.

In the role of **Software Automation Engineer**, you’ll turn AI from “something interesting” into practical tools teams use every day. You’ll work across different functions, build automation, and help shape how AI is applied inside the company — from small improvements to end-to-end solutions.

**In this role, you will:**

* Design, build, test, deploy, and maintain AI-powered automation workflows and AI agents.
* Analyze business needs of internal teams, validate technical feasibility, and propose optimal solutions (AI models, APIs, n8n logic, etc.).
* Explore new tools and workflows, and propose AI solutions tailored to each team’s needs.
* Collaborate with internal teams to understand their workflows and identify automation opportunities.
* Integrate various APIs and services into automation workflows.
* Run workshops and demos to showcase practical AI use cases and drive adoption.
* Independently manage small automation projects end-to-end: from requirements gathering and planning to communication and delivery.
* Monitor existing automations, fix issues, and proactively improve stability and performance.

**Skills you’ll need to bring:**

* 3+ years of experience as a Software Engineer.
* Understanding of AI fundamentals: how LLMs work, Prompt Engineering, Model Context Protocol (MCP).
* Knowledge of AI agent architecture (planners/reasoners, tools, memory, critics/verifiers).
* Hands-on experience building automation workflows using n8n or similar tools, including triggers, branching, and execution logic.
* Experience integrating APIs (HTTP requests and working with different services).
* Ability to work with and transform data (e.g. JSON) within workflows.
* Understanding of secure data handling practices.
* Practical coding experience with TypeScript.
* Strong communication skills: ability to run workshops, gather requirements, and explain solutions.
* Proactive and curious mindset, with the ability to work autonomously.
* At least an Upper-Intermediate level of English and fluent Ukrainian.

**As a plus:**

* Experience with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and vector databases/vector search.
* Familiarity with Vertex AI or BigQuery.
* Experience with AI-assisted coding.
* Experience with [Node.js](http://Node.js) or Python.
