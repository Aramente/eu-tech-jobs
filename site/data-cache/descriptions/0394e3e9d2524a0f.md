<div class="content-intro"><p>Elastic, the Search AI Company, enables everyone to find the answers they need in real time, using all their data, at scale — unleashing the potential of businesses and people. The Elastic Search AI Platform, used by more than 50% of the Fortune 500, brings together the precision of search and the intelligence of AI to enable everyone to accelerate the results that matter. By taking advantage of all structured and unstructured data — securing and protecting private information more effectively — Elastic’s complete, cloud-based solutions for search, security, and observability help organizations deliver on the promise of AI.</p></div><h3>What is the role:</h3>
<p>The Contextual Security Applications team is responsible for building core capabilities that power Elastic’s next-generation security experiences. Our focus is on delivering context-rich solutions that enhance detection, response, and security insights across the platform.</p>
<p>The team is driving key expansions to our SIEM foundations and advancing our Cloud Detection &amp; Response (CDR) capabilities, including turning findings into an insight-rich experience, introducing Security Readiness insights and reports, delivering AI-driven Compliance Reporting, and more.</p>
<p>As a Senior Software Engineer on the Contextual Security Applications team, you will help design and build&nbsp; workflows and user-facing capabilities that turn raw security data into actionable context. You’ll collaborate closely with other security engineering teams , as well as with product and design, to deliver resilient, high-scale features used by security practitioners around the world.</p>
<h3><strong>What you will be doing:&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Design and implement features that power contextual security workflows across SIEM, CDR, compliance, and cloud security.</li>
<li>Build and evolve APIs that correlate entities, findings, signals, and configuration data into coherent security stories.</li>
<li>Contribute to the architecture of compliance reporting, readiness insights, and other cross-cutting security experiences.</li>
<li>Develop scalable, high-performance systems within the Elastic ecosystem and cloud-native environments.</li>
<li>Own the reliability, observability, and operational health, from design to production.</li>
<li>Collaborate with product, design, and other engineering teams to refine requirements and deliver impactful, user-centered solutions.</li>
<li>Write production-grade code in TypeScript/Node.js (and potentially other languages) as part of a high-quality, well-tested codebase.</li>
<li>Participate in code reviews, technical design discussions, and ongoing improvements to engineering practices.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What you bring:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Solid programming experience in Javascript/TypeScript/ with a particular emphasis on React.js and Node.js frameworks.</li>
<li>Experience designing and implementing APIs, data models, and services that support rich application workflows.</li>
<li>Ability to take ownership of problems end-to-end: from clarifying requirements and proposing designs to delivering, monitoring, and iterating in production.</li>
<li>Comfort working in a distributed, async-first environment and collaborating with colleagues across time zones.</li>
<li>A proactive mindset: you ask the right questions, challenge assumptions, and look for ways to improve both product and engineering processes.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and the ability to explain complex technical topics to both technical and non-technical partners.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Bonus points:&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with Kibana or Elasticsearch</li>
<li>Contributions to open source</li>
