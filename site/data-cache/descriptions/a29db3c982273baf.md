## 📄 JOB DESCRIPTION

- The Site Inventory Leader will be responsible for ensuring the accuracy, availability, and traceability of inventory in a high-demand logistics environment with tight flows. Their primary mission will be to ensure that inbound, warehousing, replenishment, and outbound processes are executed precisely, minimizing discrepancies and optimizing operational efficiency.
- This position requires a profile with training in engineering (industrial, logistics, organization or similar) and previous experience in high turnover environments, preferably in companies such as e-commerce, retail or distribution.

## 🎯 TASKS

- Ensure inventory accuracy through cycle checks, internal audits, and variance analysis.
- Coordinate and supervise inventory control teams, ensuring compliance with operational processes and standards.
- Analyze data to identify stock errors, breakages, obsolescence or loss patterns, proposing and implementing corrective actions.
- Collaborate closely with the Inbound and Outbound teams to ensure the correct receipt, location, preparation, and dispatch of merchandise.
- Design, improve and standardize processes that increase the efficiency of stock flow and reduce discrepancies.
- Manage inventory management tools, WMS, and tracking systems.
- Provide regular reports on key KPIs (accuracy, shrinkage, cycle count compliance, aging, etc.) to the Site Operations Manager.
- Lead investigations into critical inventory incidents and coordinate actions with Operations, Safety and Quality.
- Ensure compliance with internal regulations, good storage practices, and safety standards.
- Participate in continuous improvement projects (Lean, Kaizen, 5S, Six Sigma).

## 👉 MUST HAVE skills

- Degree in Industrial Engineering, Logistics, Organization or related disciplines.
- Minimum of 3-5 years of experience in inventory control or logistics management roles in high-volume environments.
- Advanced knowledge of WMS systems, ERP and data analysis tools (advanced Excel, Power BI, etc.).
- Analytical skills, attention to detail, and a problem-solving orientation.
- Leadership and team coordination skills.
- High level of communication and ability to work in multidisciplinary teams.
- Lean/Six Sigma certification is a plus.
