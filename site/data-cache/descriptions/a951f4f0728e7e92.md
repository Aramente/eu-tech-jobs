<p><strong>Summary:</strong></p>
<p>The Human Resources (HR) Manager II will play a critical role in driving strategic HR initiatives while ensuring smooth operational processes. This position requires a combination of strategic planning and day-to-day HR management to support organizational goals and enhance employee experience.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement HR strategies that align with the company's objectives.</li>
<li>Manage and resolve complex employee relations issues, facilitating conflict resolution.</li>
<li>Oversee and ensure compliance with all local, state, and federal employment laws and regulations.</li>
<li>Lead and mentor HR team members, fostering a collaborative and high-performance culture.</li>
<li>Collaborate with department heads to determine staffing needs and plan for talent acquisition and retention.</li>
<li>Manage compensation, benefits, and performance management systems.</li>
<li>Utilize HR software and data analytics to drive informed decision-making and reporting.</li>
<li>Serve as a liaison between employees and management, communicating key information and addressing concerns.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Required Skills:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience in HR management with a strong understanding of HR practices and employment law.</li>
<li>Exceptional communication skills for effective stakeholder engagement and conflict resolution.</li>
<li>Demonstrated leadership abilities to manage a team and guide HR strategy.</li>
<li>Proficiency in HR software and data analysis tools for reporting and decision-making.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to handle multiple priorities and projects simultaneously.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.</li>
<li>PHR or SHRM-CP certification is a plus.</li>
