<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Sourceability® is a global digital distributor of electronic components transforming how modern businesses bring products to market. With innovation, quality and logistics as the backbone of the company, Sourceability’s cutting-edge products and services expedite the procurement process across a wide range of industries, including communications/cellular, consumer electronics, and auto manufacturing.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>We are looking for a dynamic Business Development Manager to become a vital part of our vibrant Sales team, working across the APAC region.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>You will play a crucial role in the expansion of our customer pipeline, and the speed with which clients and prospects engage with our sales team. This work will be split across all our key business focus areas, and your specified territory and/or specialist vertical. You will own the messaging and delivery to sell Sourceability, not individual products specifically. You will be a senior influence in our organisation and will be expected to conduct your business and yourself in an exemplary manner, providing inspiration and aspirational capabilities to the wider team.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>As a Business Development Manager, you’ll be comfortable working in focused business areas and developing key strategic approaches in partnership with various internal stakeholders. Travel will be a large part of this role, and you will be an experienced national and international traveller. Your experience will allow you to take a balanced, mature approach to collaborate with customers for long term partnership based on trust and mutual benefit. You’ll be competitive but will recognise the value and function of the wider team. You’ll enjoy coaching those around you and most importantly you’ll enjoy winning.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our goal is to have a sophisticated team equipped with expert technical skills in addition to keen business acumen. Each member of our team adds unique value to the business and the customer. Sourceability is committed to a culture where we attract the best talent in the industry.</p>
