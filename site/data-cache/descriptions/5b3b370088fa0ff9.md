<p><strong>The job</strong></p></div><p>The Creative Lead plays a critical leadership role within Flo’s Creative Studio, overseeing a multidisciplinary team responsible exclusively for high-volume, performance-driven user acquisition creative.</p>
<p>This role leads a team of ~10 in-house creatives (graphic designers, motion designers, and video editors) and manages a freelance bench of ~6, ensuring the delivery of high-performing UA assets that directly impact acquisition KPIs and revenue.</p>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Content &amp; Creative, the Creative Lead ensures creative output is fully aligned with UA strategy, channel plans, and brand guidelines. The role sits at the intersection of creative excellence, operational efficiency, and commercial performance.</p>
<p>A significant portion of the role focuses on people leadership, creative review and sign-off, AI-enabled workflow optimisation, workload management, and maintaining strong cross-functional alignment with UA Managers.</p>
<p>While primarily a leadership role, this position also requires strong hands-on design capability. The Creative Lead is expected to step into high-priority briefs, concept development, or resourcing gaps where required.</p>
