<p><strong>Your day-to-day&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>This role is all about collaborating with your team to make a difference to your customers. As a backend engineer you’ll work in a squad alongside other disciplines like product managers, marketers, user researchers, designers, mobile engineers, web engineers, data analysts, business analysts, writers and more!&nbsp;</p>
<p>Together you’ll build and support a particular part of Monzo. Our squads belong to our wider&nbsp;<em>collectives</em>&nbsp;(a word we use to describe self-governing business units of ~100 people). They are; Core Banking, Business Banking, Wealth, Borrowing, Growth, Payments, Platform, Fincrime, Security &amp; Expansion.</p>
<p>They’re all looking for additional Backend Engineers right now, we do a standard interview process across all our collectives and at the end we will find the best match for you based on your skills, experience, preferences and aligning with the business need!&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our backend engineers have a variety of different backgrounds. As long as you enjoy learning new things, we’d love to talk to you. We do not ask for formal qualifications or degree requirements for any of our engineering roles.</p>
