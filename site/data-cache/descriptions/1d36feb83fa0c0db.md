<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><h3>ABOUT THE TEAM:</h3>

<h3>ABOUT THE ROLE:&nbsp;<span style="color: rgb(224, 62, 45);"><br></span></h3>
<p>We are seeking a commercially driven, enterprise-minded SVP of Admin Services to lead and scale this multi-product division into a category-defining business.</p>
<p>This is a senior CEO-style role, with ownership of strategy, execution, and financial performance across multiple product lines. You will be responsible for continuing to make Admin Services a high-growth business within Tide’s product suite, while ensuring operational excellence and a seamless member experience.</p>
<p>You will operate as a business owner first, with a strong emphasis on commercial strategy, market expansion, and breaking through execution barriers leveraging your leadership team for deep product and domain expertise.</p>
<p>As SVP of Admin Services, your responsibilities will include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strategic Leadership &amp; P&amp;L Ownership: Define the vision and strategy across all Admin Services product lines, owning full P&amp;L accountability and driving growth, profitability, and long-term scalability.</li>
<li>Product Line Expansion: Scale existing services such as invoicing, payroll, expense management, registrations and accounting &amp; tax&nbsp; into meaningful business lines, while identifying and building adjacent opportunities, both in the UK and internationally.</li>
<li>Commercial &amp; Go-to-Market Ownership: Own pricing, packaging, monetisation, and distribution strategies. Drive acquisition, engagement, retention, and cross-sell across the portfolio.</li>
<li>Team Leadership &amp; Organisation Building: Build and lead a high-performing, multi-layered team, with strong leadership across product, operations, and commercial functions.</li>
<li>Operational Excellence: Establish scalable processes across onboarding, servicing, compliance, and support, ensuring high reliability and efficiency.</li>
<li>Technology &amp; AI Enablement: Leverage automation and emerging technologies, including AI, to transform service delivery and improve efficiency.</li>
<li>Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner closely with Product, Engineering, Finance, Risk, Legal, and Marketing teams to execute the division’s strategy.</li>
<li>Market &amp; Customer Insight: Develop a deep understanding of SME needs, competitive landscape, and regulatory environment to inform strategy and innovation.</li>
</ul>
