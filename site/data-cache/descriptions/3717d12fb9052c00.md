<p><strong>About Nanonets</strong></p>
<p>Nanonets has a vision to help computers see the world starting with reading and understanding documents. Our product helps businesses automate document related workflows for enterprise office teams such as invoice data entry for AP teams, KYC automation for banks and insurance etc. Some of the companies we work with include Toyota, Boston Scientific, Bill.com and Entergy to name a few.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We recently raised a series B round of $29 million. Now we’re on a mission to hire the very best and are committed to creating exceptional employee experiences where everyone is respected and has access to equal opportunity.&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>Job description</strong></h3>
<p>At Nanonets, we are building AI-powered automation agents to eliminate manual healthcare admin tasks - helping customers streamline operations and deliver better patient care.</p>
<p>We are looking for a <strong>Full Stack Software Engineer</strong> who thrives in fast-paced startup environments, enjoys shipping impactful products quickly, and can work across the stack to bring our AI agents to life.</p>
<p>You’ll work with a small, high-performing team building production-grade AI agents - combining distributed systems, AI integration, and intuitive web platforms.</p>
<h3><strong>What You’ll do :&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, and deploy AI agent services that automate healthcare workflows.</li>
<li>Build scalable APIs, backend services, and sleek frontend interfaces.</li>
<li>Ship high-quality, maintainable code - fast.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with product and AI teams to iterate on agent workflows.</li>
<li>Monitor, debug, and improve production systems continuously.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What we expect from you :</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>3-6 years of hands-on experience in full stack engineering.</li>
<li>Experience working in a fast-moving startup, with a track record of shipping products quickly.</li>
<li>Proficiency in at least one backend language (<strong>Go</strong> or <strong>Python</strong>) and one frontend framework (preferably <strong>React + TypeScript</strong>).</li>
<li>Experience with using <strong>LLM APIs</strong> and LLM-based applications.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of web architecture, distributed systems, APIs, and relational databases.</li>
<li>Ability to write clean, testable code and solve production issues effectively.</li>
<li>A builder’s mindset: ownership, initiative, and bias for action.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Nice to have :</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with US healthcare or other regulated domains.</li>
<li>Knowledge of DevOps practices, cloud infrastructure, and CI/CD pipelines.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Our Tech Stack :</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Backend:</strong> Go, Python</li>
<li><strong>Frontend:</strong> React, TypeScript</li>
<li><strong>Databases &amp; Infra:</strong> PostgreSQL, Redis, AWS/GCP</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Why join us ?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Work on cutting-edge AI agents transforming the healthcare industry.</li>
<li>Be part of a small, high-impact, no-bureaucracy team.</li>
<li>Ship products that make a real-world difference for customers.</li>
<li>Learn fast, grow fast - with competitive compensation &amp; career growth opportunities.</li>
</ul>
