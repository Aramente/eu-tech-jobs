<p>APAC represents one of Gigs' most significant growth frontiers. We're looking for a GTM Lead to own our APAC strategy and execution end-to-end — from winning lighthouse customers and building operator partnerships to standing up compliant local operations and growing a high-performance team.</p>
<p>You'll work closely with our VP of Growth and global leadership to define Gigs' presence across key APAC markets including Singapore, Japan, and Southeast Asia. This is a founding market role - a consultative, long-cycle deal-maker's job: you'll be convincing sophisticated fintechs and enterprises to embed an entirely new business line. High ownership, direct founder visibility, and a real mandate to build.</p>
<p>If you thrive in zero-to-one environments, have a deep network across APAC's fintech, telecom, and technology ecosystems, and can pair strategic thinking with relentless hands-on execution — this role is built for you.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Own the APAC market strategy:</strong> Define and execute Gigs' go-to-market approach across priority markets, in close collaboration with global leadership.</li>
<li><strong>Drive commercial growth:</strong> Identify, negotiate, and close high-value partnerships with fintechs, and enterprise customers across the region.</li>
<li><strong>Build Gigs' APAC presence:</strong> Represent Gigs at key industry events, build your personal brand, and establish Gigs as a credible player across the APAC ecosystem.</li>
<li><strong>Build and lead a team:</strong> Recruit and develop a lean, high-performance local team as the market scales.</li>
<li><strong>Ensure compliance and operational excellence:</strong> Work cross-functionally with Legal, Finance, and Ops to establish compliant, scalable local operations across diverse regulatory environments.</li>
<li><strong>Connect the dots:</strong> Serve as the bridge between APAC market realities and Gigs' global product, engineering, and leadership teams.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Strong commercial instincts: you can close deals, build partnerships, and identify high-value opportunities before they're obvious.</li>
<li>Deep network and personal credibility across APAC's fintech and tech ecosystem - particularly in Singapore, Australia, Japan, or SEA.</li>
