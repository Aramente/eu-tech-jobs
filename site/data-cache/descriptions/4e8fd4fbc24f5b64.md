**Role Summary:**

We have exciting opportunities for Sales Development Representatives to join PatSnap and drive new business growth within our Life Sciences vertical.

This role is built for individuals with a Life Sciences background who wants to build a career in high-performance sales — using your curiosity, competitiveness, and communication skills to win new business.

If you’re motivated by targets, progression, and uncapped earning potential, and want to develop into a top-performing salesperson, this is your opportunity.

As an SDR, you’ll be on the front line of revenue generation — prospecting, engaging, and qualifying new customers across pharma, biotech, and medical devices.

You will:

* Take a hunter mindset to outbound — owning high-volume outreach across calls, email, and LinkedIn
* Use your scientific credibility to break into conversations with senior R&D and innovation leaders
* Create and qualify pipeline, turning cold prospects into real commercial opportunities
* Handle rejection, stay persistent, and consistently hit and exceed activity and pipeline targets
* Build the foundations of closing, influencing, and deal progression
* Partner with Account Executives to convert pipeline into revenue

This is a high-activity, target-driven sales role where success comes down to your drive, resilience, and hunger to win. If you bring the work ethic and mindset, the progression and earning potential are there to match.

*This is a hybrid position based in our London office, with the option to work hybrid throughout the week (Mon, Tues, Thurs) or fully in-office.*
