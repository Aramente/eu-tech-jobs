<p>As a Solution Architect at Elastic, you help customers and partners design, implement, and optimize solutions using the Elastic Stack. Working closely with our Inside Sales team you act as a technical expert, guiding them through architecture decisions, deployment strategies, and best practices.</p>
<h3><strong>What You Will Be Doing</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with account teams to understand customer requirements.</li>
<li>Conduct technical discovery sessions and solution workshops.</li>
<li>Design architectures for search, observability, and security use cases.</li>
<li>Deliver product demonstrations and proof-of-concepts.</li>
<li>Assist with deployment planning and configuration.</li>
<li>Provide guidance on scaling, performance tuning, and security.</li>
<li>Document solutions and share technical knowledge internally and externally.</li>
</ul>
<h3>What You Bring</h3>
<ul>
<li>Solid understanding of distributed systems and data indexing/search.</li>
<li>Experience in a technical customer-facing role.</li>
<li>Ability to map business needs to technical solutions.</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving and communication skills.</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).</li>
<li>Fluent in English.</li>
</ul>
