<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Job Summary:</strong></span></p>
<p>Symphony is seeking an ambitious and results-oriented&nbsp;<strong>Business Development Senior Specialist</strong>&nbsp;to play a crucial role in expanding our market presence and driving the top-of-funnel sales pipeline. This individual will be responsible for identifying, qualifying, and engaging new strategic accounts within the financial services sector. The ideal candidate has a strong understanding of fintech and capital markets, excels at proactive outreach, and is skilled at clearly articulating the unique value of the Symphony platform to senior stakeholders.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></span></p>
<p>The Business Development Senior Specialist is primarily responsible for establishing initial contact with high-value prospects and qualifying new opportunities to fuel the sales pipeline:</p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively research and identify new target accounts and key contacts within the financial services and institutional sectors.</li>
<li>Execute high-volume outbound activities (calls, emails, social outreach) to strategically engage potential clients.</li>
<li>Personalize outreach using market intelligence and industry trends to articulate Symphony's specific value proposition.</li>
<li>Conduct initial discovery and qualification calls to assess fit and readiness based on established sales criteria&nbsp;</li>
<li>Consistently meet or exceed targets for qualified opportunity generation.</li>
<li>Clearly articulate Symphony's compliant communication and collaboration solutions to senior-level decision-makers.</li>
<li>Maintain a disciplined and accurate record of all prospecting activities and pipeline status&nbsp;</li>
<li>Successfully hand off qualified opportunities to the appropriate Account Executives for further development and closure.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Sales and Marketing to support and execute targeted outbound campaigns and initiatives.</li>
<li>Stay current on the competitive landscape and client pain points to effectively position Symphony in the market.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Qualifications:</strong></span></p>
<p><strong>Required</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>4+ years of professional experience in a Business Development Representative (BDR), Sales Development Representative (SDR), or equivalent outbound sales role, preferably within Fintech or Enterprise SaaS.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to consistently meet or exceed monthly/quarterly targets for qualified opportunity generation.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with a proven ability to engage and persuade senior-level decision-makers.</li>
<li>Strong self-starter with a high degree of motivation, resilience, and a competitive drive.</li>
<li>Proficiency with CRM software (e.g., Salesforce) and sales engagement tools (e.g. LinkedIn, SalesLoft, Outreach).</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Specific knowledge of capital markets workflows or experience selling collaboration, security, or compliance solutions.</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, or a related technical field.</li>
<li>Experience navigating and prospecting into large, complex financial institutions.</li>
</ul>
