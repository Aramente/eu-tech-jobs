Hello. We're Prismic.

At Prismic, we believe the future is all about autonomous websites & hyper-personalised user journeys. Our mission is clear: enable marketers & engineers to shape highly empathic web journeys at scale.

We are entering a new phase for Prismic’s Page Builder.

As agentic workflows become a first-class way to create and manage content, the editor experience cannot be a thin wrapper around “AI actions.” It must be the place where those actions feel natural, fast, and trustworthy from the first prompt to the final publish.

Agentic workflows introduce new interaction patterns across the entire editing lifecycle: discovering the right content through smart search, watching work happen through streamed responses, and running multi-step operations that may span pages, slices, assets, and collaborators. These workflows only succeed if the Page Builder provides the right primitives: real-time feedback, clear progress and status, predictable performance, and UX that keeps users in control.

We are evolving the Page Builder into an **agent-ready surface,** a rich editing environment where humans and agents collaborate seamlessly.
