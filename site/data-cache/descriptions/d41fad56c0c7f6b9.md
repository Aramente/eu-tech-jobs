<p><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Technical Implementation:</strong> Translate user stories and business requirements into well-designed, scalable, and secure technical solutions. You will primarily leverage the Salesforce platform, focusing on the Sales Cloud and CPQ domains.</li>
<li><strong>Code Quality &amp; Collaboration:</strong> Participate actively in Pull Request (PR) reviews to maintain overall code quality and consistency. Work closely with cross-functional teams, including business stakeholders, analysts, and other engineers, to drive projects from concept to production.</li>
<li><strong>Testing &amp; Reliability:</strong> Write comprehensive unit tests for all new logic to ensure high code coverage, and contribute to the growth of our automated test regression suite.</li>
<li><strong>Deployment Support:</strong> Assist with the deployment cycle, which includes documenting changes, performing validation, and resolving environment-specific issues.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Required Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Salesforce Development Expertise (3+ years):</strong> Solid experience with Salesforce development, including proficiency in Apex, Process/Flow Automations, and Lightning Web Components (LWC).</li>
<li><strong>Software Development Fundamentals (1+ years):</strong> A strong foundation in general software engineering principles, with experience in languages like Java, Python, or JavaScript.</li>
<li><strong>Domain Knowledge:</strong> Foundational understanding of Sales Cloud and Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) or related domains.</li>
<li><strong>DevOps/CI/CD:</strong> Familiarity with continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, such as Jenkins and Salesforce DX (SFDX).</li>
<li><strong>Security Focus:</strong> Understanding of Salesforce security features and best practices for developing secure and reliable solutions.</li>
<li><strong>Soft Skills:</strong> Strong communication, problem-solving, and a clear eagerness to learn and collaborate with a team.</li>
<li><strong>Global Collaboration &amp; Ownership:</strong> Demonstrated strong ownership and the ability to communicate effectively and collaborate seamlessly with cross-functional, global teams and stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications (Extra Credit)</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Salesforce certifications (e.g., Platform Developer I, CPQ Administrator).</li>
<li>Demonstrated interest in learning about Salesforce DevOps practices.</li>
<li>Experience with modern AI agent frameworks, orchestration techniques, or Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines.</li>
