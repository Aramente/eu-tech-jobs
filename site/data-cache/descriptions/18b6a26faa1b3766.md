<div class="content-intro"><p>We are a global team of innovators and pioneers dedicated to shaping the future of observability. At New Relic, we build an intelligent platform that empowers companies to thrive in an AI-first world by giving them unparalleled insight into their complex systems. As we continue to expand our global footprint, we're looking for passionate people to join our mission. If you're ready to help the world's best companies optimize their digital applications, we invite you to explore a career with us!</p></div><p style="line-height: 1.2;"><strong>Your opportunity</strong></p>
<p>New Relic is looking for a Senior Business Value Engineer&nbsp;to join our Value Strategy team, specifically covering the&nbsp;<strong>EMEA region</strong>. In this role, you will be the primary value architect for our accounts across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. You will bridge the gap between technical capabilities and business outcomes, helping our customers understand the financial and operational impact of the New Relic platform.</p>
<p>You will act as a trusted advisor to both our internal sales teams and our customers' C-suite executives, driving the "Value Realization" process from initial discovery to long-term success.</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.2;"><strong><br>What you'll do<br></strong><em><br></em></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Build Compelling Business Cases:</strong>&nbsp;Create and deliver Business Value Assessments (BVAs) including complex ROI modeling, TCO analysis, and value realization benchmarking.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Strategize with Sales:</strong>&nbsp;Partner closely with Sales Leadership and Account Executives to develop deal strategies that emphasize business justification over feature-set comparisons.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Quantify Technical Impact:</strong>&nbsp;Translate technical metrics (like MTTR, Error Rates, and Cloud Cost) into business KPIs (like Revenue at Risk, Subscriber Churn, and Operational Efficiency).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Drive Value Realization:</strong>&nbsp;Ensure customers are co-creators in their value roadmap, moving beyond the initial sale to track and report on actual value achieved post-implementation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Educate &amp; Enable:</strong>&nbsp;Mentor and train the broader GTM teams on value-based selling best practices to increase deal win rates and deal size.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Lead Discovery Workshops:</strong>&nbsp;Drive high-impact business value discovery sessions with Customers to uncover pain points and map them to New Relic Value Pillars (Business Uptime, Engineering Excellence, and Digital Experience).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p style="line-height: 1.2;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.2;"><strong>This role requires<br></strong><em><br></em></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Experience:</strong> Extensive relevant work experience ie Value Engineering, Management Consulting, Investment Banking, Strategic Pre-sales, or Strategic Finance (with a focus on Deal Structuring or Sales Operations) or a Master’s degree with relevant professional exposure.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Communication:</strong>&nbsp;Exceptional presence with the ability to influence C-level executives and technical stakeholders alike.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Financial Acumen:</strong>&nbsp;Strong proficiency in creating financial models (ROI, NPV, Payback Period) and building executive-level presentations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Analytical Depth:</strong>&nbsp;Ability to work on complex issues requiring an evaluation of identifiable factors and intangible variables.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Technical Literacy:</strong>&nbsp;Ability to understand complex distributed systems and observability concepts (APM, Infrastructure, Log Management).</p>
</li>
</ul>
