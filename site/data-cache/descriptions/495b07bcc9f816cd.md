<p>Make Wayve the experience that defines your career!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p></div><h2><strong>The role&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p>Wayve is deploying its autonomous driving technology into real-world robotaxi operations supported by strategic partners, with ambition to scale to multiple markets and cities in the near term. This role sits at the heart of that effort, as part of a cross-functional team realising our strategic goal to bring AI-powered mobility to new use cases and cities around the world.</p>
<p>As Robotaxi Safety Programme Lead, you will own the day-to-day execution of the safety programme required to deploy and scale operations across multiple cities. You will translate Wayve’s safety approach, Safety Management System (SMS), and safety case into a structured, scalable programme aligned with our partners safety requirements as well as in compliance with various regulatory environments.</p>
<p>This is a high-impact role. You will work across engineering, operations, legal, policy, and partner teams to ensure we deliver a safety programme that is credible, scalable, and defensible.</p>
<p>Key responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ownership and scaling of robotaxi pilot safety plan(s) and underlying artefacts</li>
<li>Structure, track and execute key safety management deliverables with the support of key cross-functional teams from engineering, product, regulatory affairs and more</li>
<li>Liaise with external partners to align requirements and translate to delivery plans</li>
<li>Track, aggregate and report key safety performance indicators and other metrics</li>
<li>Identify, plan and execute needs for safety plan expansion as Wayve identifies new markets for deployment</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>About you</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</h2>
<p>In order to set you up for success as a Robotaxi Safety Programme Lead at Wayve, we’re looking for the following skills and experience.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Essential&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with creating and executing safety management activities, spanning safety policy, assurance, risk management and safety promotion</li>
<li>Applied experience with safety in a fleet operations environment or similar</li>
<li>Partner and stakeholder engagement experience</li>
<li>Risk management tools and evaluation</li>
<li>Cross-functional project management</li>
</ul>
<p>Desirable&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Safety management system experience in an automotive, aerospace or transportation setting</li>
<li>Operations or safety experience with platform marketplace businesses, such as ride-sharing, delivery, micromobility</li>
<li>Familiarity with global autonomous vehicle test and deployment regulations and reporting requirements</li>
</ul>

We operate core working hours so you can determine the schedule that works best for you and your team.&nbsp;</p>
