<h3>Role Overview</h3>
<div>We are looking for a highly motivated and experienced Sales Development Representative (SDR) to join our growing APAC team. As a senior SDR, you will take ownership of outbound pipeline generation, lead qualification, and early-stage deal acceleration. You’ll work closely with both marketing and sales to build predictable pipeline and contribute strategic insights that drive market expansion.</div>
