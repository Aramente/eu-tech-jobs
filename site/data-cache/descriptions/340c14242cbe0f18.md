<p><strong>Principal Engineer Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<p>A Principal Engineer at Monzo is a hands on engineering leadership (IC) position. As a Principal Engineer you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with and guide Engineering and Product Leaders to <strong data-start="815" data-end="876">shape the technical and product direction of Monzo</strong>, providing company-shaping leadership across collectives (100+ engineers) and driving alignment with company-wide priorities, owning problems that materially impact Monzo’s trajectory over the next 1-5 years.</li>
<li>Remain hands-on in both <strong>coding and architecture</strong> where it adds the most leverage, especially in complex, high-risk or novel areas.</li>
<li>Actively identifying high-leverage opportunities for Monzo and setting the direction of Engineering effort towards the most impactful outcomes.</li>
<li><strong data-start="2010" data-end="2079">Mobilise teams across Monzo around high-impact problems</strong>, creating clarity, alignment and momentum, injecting urgency, or slowing things down to ensure safety and long-term sustainability.</li>
<li>Lead and influence architectural direction for the most complex systems, driving consistency and reuse across domains, <strong data-start="2503" data-end="2626">aligning local decisions with the broader system strategy</strong>.</li>
<li>Set and uphold a high bar for technical excellence across Monzo, <strong data-start="2882" data-end="3020">identifying and driving </strong>improvements in quality, reliability and scalability, and fostering a culture where engineers hold each other to that standard.</li>
<li>Proactively mentor, sponsor and <strong data-start="3229" data-end="3276">develop senior engineers and future leaders</strong>, multiplying your impact by enabling others to operate at a higher level.</li>
</ul>
