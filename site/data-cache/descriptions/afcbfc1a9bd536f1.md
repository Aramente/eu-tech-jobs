<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><div class="p-rich\_text\_section"><strong data-stringify-type="bold">ABOUT THE TEAM:</strong></div>
<div class="p-rich\_text\_section"><br>Our 40+ engineering teams are working on designing, creating and running the rich product catalogue across our business areas (e.g. Payments Services, Business Services). We have a long roadmap ahead of us and always have interesting problems to tackle. We trust and empower our engineers to make real technical decisions that affect multiple teams and shape the future of Tide’s Global One Platform. It’s an exceptional opportunity to make a real difference by taking ownership of engineering practices in a rapidly expanding company!<br>We work in small autonomous teams, grouped under common domains owning the full lifecycle of some microservices in Tide’s service catalogue. Our engineers self-organise, gather together to discuss technical challenges, and set their own guidelines in the different&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">Communities of Practice</strong>&nbsp;regardless of where they currently stand in our Growth Framework.</div>

<div class="p-rich\_text\_section"><strong data-stringify-type="bold">ABOUT THE ROLE:</strong></div>
<ul class="p-rich\_text\_list p-rich\_text\_list\_\_bullet p-rich\_text\_list--nested" data-stringify-type="unordered-list" data-list-tree="true" data-indent="0" data-border="0">
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Contribute to our event-driven Microservice Architecture (currently 200+ services owned by 40+ teams). You will define and maintain the services your team owns (you design it, you build it, you run it, you scale it globally)</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Use&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">Java 17</strong>,<strong data-stringify-type="bold">&nbsp;Spring Boot</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">JOOQ&nbsp;</strong>to build your services.</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Expose and consume&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">RESTful APIs</strong>. We value good API design and we treat our APIs as Products (in the world of Open Banking often times they are gonna be public!)</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Use&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">SNS</strong>+<strong data-stringify-type="bold">SQS</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">Kafka</strong>&nbsp;to send events</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Utilise&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">PostgreSQL&nbsp;</strong>via Aurora as your primary datastore (we are heavy AWS users)</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Deploy your services to Production as often as you need to (this usually means multiple times per day!). This is enabled by our CI/CD pipelines powered by&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">GitHub</strong>&nbsp;with&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">GitHub actions</strong>, and solid JUnit/Pact testing (new joiners are encouraged to have something deployed to production in their first 2 weeks)</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Experience modern GitOps using&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">ArgoCD</strong>. Our Cloud team uses&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">Docker, Terraform, EKS/Kubernetes</strong>&nbsp;to run the platform.</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Have&nbsp;<strong data-stringify-type="bold">DataDog</strong>&nbsp;as your best friend to monitor your services and investigate issues</li>
<li data-stringify-indent="0" data-stringify-border="0">Collaborate closely with Product Owners to understand our Users’ needs, Business opportunities and Regulatory requirements and translate them into well-engineered solutions</li>
</ul>
