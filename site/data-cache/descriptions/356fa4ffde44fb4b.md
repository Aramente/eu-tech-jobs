<p>As an <strong>Inside Sales Specialist</strong>, you will be responsible for managing and converting inbound leads — potential restaurant and retail partners who have already expressed interest in working with Wolt. Your primary focus will be to quickly qualify opportunities, run an efficient sales process, and close deals in a fast-paced, target-driven environment.</p>
<p>You will own the full sales cycle from A to Z for inbound opportunities. This includes qualifying and prioritizing leads, conducting remote sales meetings (and occasional market visits where needed), presenting Wolt’s value proposition, negotiating commercial terms, and closing partnerships efficiently.</p>
<p>You will work closely with cross-functional teams including Sales, Merchant Services, and Account Management to ensure a smooth transition from signed deal to onboarding. You will also support the ongoing success of newly acquired partners by ensuring proper setup, alignment on expectations, and strong initial performance.</p>
<h3><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll be:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Managing a high volume of inbound leads and maintaining a healthy, fast-moving pipeline</li>
<li>Quickly identifying high-potential partners and prioritizing efforts accordingly</li>
<li>Conducting structured sales conversations focused on conversion and speed</li>
<li>Negotiating partnership terms, including commissions and commercial conditions</li>
<li>Closing deals against ambitious sales targets and KPIs</li>
<li>Ensuring seamless handover to onboarding and account management teams</li>
<li>Supporting existing partners when needed with ownership changes or commercial updates, following internal processes</li>
<li>Continuously optimizing your sales approach to position Wolt as the partner of choice</li>
</ul>
