<p>As a Senior Full-Stack Engineer, you’ll design and build complex, data-heavy user interfaces and own customer-facing workflows end-to-end. While this role has a strong frontend focus, engineers are expected to work comfortably across the whole stack, collaborating on backend services, APIs, and system integrations as needed. We’re looking for T-shaped engineers who bring deep frontend expertise while remaining pragmatic and effective beyond UI tasks.</p>
<p><strong>What will you do?</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build scalable UI systems that handle large datasets, async operations, and real-time state changes</li>
<li>Own features end-to-end, from initial design through production launch and long-term maintenance</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with product, design, and other engineering teams to deliver new features</li>
<li>Partner on API design and system contracts</li>
<li>Participate in an on-call rotation to support ClickPipes in production, helping diagnose incidents, and mitigate issues</li>
<li>Take ownership of production quality, including monitoring, debugging, performance tuning, and reliability improvements</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What you will bring:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience building and shipping production full-stack applications</li>
<li>In-depth knowledge of web fundamentals (HTML/CSS, JavaScript, DOM, HTTP)</li>
<li>Solid experience in designing and building applications with React and TypeScript</li>
<li>Experience writing and maintaining automated tests (unit, integration, and E2E) as part of a CI/CD workflow</li>
<li>Practical knowledge of backend systems, REST and/or GraphQL APIs, and service-oriented architecture</li>
<li>Great communication skills and the ability to work effectively in a distributed, async-friendly team</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with Go, Node, or other server-side languages</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud infrastructure concepts and infrastructure-as-code tools such as Terraform</li>
<li>Experience working on data-intensive products or platforms at scale</li>
</ul>
