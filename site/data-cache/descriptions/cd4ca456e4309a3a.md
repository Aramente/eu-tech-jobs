**Role Summary:**

We have an exciting opportunity for an Account Executive to join Patsnap’s sales team based in Singapore, with responsibility for driving new business growth across the Australian market.

Australia is a new and strategic growth region for Patsnap, making this a rare opportunity to help establish our presence from the ground up. With no fixed vertical focus, you will have the autonomy to sell across industries, identify early market traction, and actively shape our go-to-market approach in the region.

You will own the full sales cycle end-to-end, from outbound prospecting and discovery through to negotiation and close, using a consultative sales approach to help customers leverage Patsnap’s innovation intelligence platform to accelerate R&D, navigate complex IP landscapes, and bring products to market faster.

This is a high-impact, entrepreneurial role for a commercially driven AE who thrives in ambiguity, enjoys building new markets, and wants to play a foundational role in Patsnap’s international expansion.

*This is an in-office position based in our Singapore office.*
