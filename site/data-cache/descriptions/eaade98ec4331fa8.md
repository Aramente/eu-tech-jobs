<ul>

<li>Skilled at managing technical projects and working with full stack development teams using a variety of project management techniques</li>

<li>Proficient in grasping the infrastructure of your program domain, and skilled at identifying the technical interfaces between teams</li>
<li>Able to maintain a strategic view of our software engineering processes while supporting and optimizing the operations of the team</li>
<li>Inspired to innovate in areas where automation can replace manual work. The team works primarily with JIRA and Google Docs so programming proficiency with either or both tools is a plus</li>
<li>Skilled at communicating across a diverse set of engineers and stakeholders</li>
<li>Hyper-organized and capable of juggling all of the data associated with projects and task-tracking</li>
<li>Knowledgeable of agile methodologies and experienced with implementing project management best practices</li>

<li>Interested in learning everything about MongoDB</li>

<ul>
<li>Execute and improve the processes and tools that inform the daily life of engineering teams</li>
<li>Drive coordination between product development teams and internal stakeholders to prioritize the team's backlog and plan its release roadmap</li>
<li>Identify risks to the team's delivery, mitigating them where possible</li>
<li>Own the data around the health of each team's project and ticket backlog</li>
<li>Know when a project/team requires more hands-on management and know when it is time to operationalize and develop a process framework for the project and/or team(s)</li>
