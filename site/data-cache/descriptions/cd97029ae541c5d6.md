### **The Job**

We are looking for a **Project Manager** to join our team and drive the successful delivery of client projects in a fast-paced **SaaS / MarTech environment**. You will play a key role in bridging business needs and technical execution, ensuring that customer requirements are clearly defined, translated, and delivered efficiently.

Working closely with cross-functional teams (Product, Engineering, Sales, and Customer Success), you will support the implementation of data-driven marketing solutions and contribute to continuous product improvement.

**Key Responsibilities**

* **Lead client projects end-to-end**, from initial scoping to delivery, ensuring timelines, quality, and stakeholder satisfaction and respect budget.

* Act as the **main point of contact for clients and internal stakeholders**, managing communication and expectations throughout the project lifecycle
* **Translate business requirements and customer brief into live marketing campaigns, actionable project plans and technical specifications**, ensuring clarity and alignment across teams
* *Coordinate with* ***engineering and design teams*** *to prioritize, track, and deliver project milestones*
* **Monitor project progress**, identify risks, and proactively propose solutions to ensure successful outcomes
* Support **testing and deployment phases**, ensuring deliverables (campaigns/programs/data connection flows) meet agreed requirements and quality standards
* Facilitate collaboration between **cross-functional teams** (designers, product owner, development team) to ensure smooth execution and continuous improvement
* Contribute to **process optimization and best practices** in project delivery within a SaaS environment, including end to end testing and documentation.
* Gather client feedback and insights to help **inform product enhancements and innovation**
