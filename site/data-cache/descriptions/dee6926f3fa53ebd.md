<p>Are you a master of organization with a passion for recruiting and do you thrive in a fast-paced, high-energy environment? If so, we have the perfect role for you! <p>As a Recruiting Coordinator, you will be responsible for providing a seamless and positive interview experience for candidates and hiring managers. You will work closely with recruiters and hiring managers to schedule interviews, manage candidate communications, and ensure that all interview logistics are well-coordinated. This is a fantastic opportunity to work with a team of passionate recruiters and make a real impact on the growth of Ripple!</p>
<p><strong>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</strong></p>
<ul>

<li>Ensure a positive candidate experience through timely communication</li>
<li>Support 3+ recruiters in scheduling recruiting activities across global offices</li>
<li>Proactively find opportunities to improve existing processes and/or implement new ones</li>
<li>Book candidate travel and manage expenses</li>
<li>Spearhead candidate experience initiatives (i.e. swag programs)</li>
<li>Support Recruiting Operations programs</li>
</ul>
