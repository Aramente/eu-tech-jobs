</div></div><p><span style="font-size: 18pt;"><strong>The Role:</strong></span></p>
<p>We are looking for a software engineer to join the Neo4j Sharding Engineering Team. We own both <a href="https://neo4j.com/docs/operations-manual/current/scalability/sharded-property-databases/overview/)">Infinigraph</a> and <a href="https://neo4j.com/docs/operations-manual/current/scalability/composite-databases/concepts/">Composite</a> products within Neo4j. Composite is the technology behind distributed query execution in the Neo4j Database Management System. Infinigraph is our database sharding offering for large data volumes, utilising a distributed database architecture.</p>
<p>Both involve distributed query execution, which is a complex and interesting topic, involving query language design and implementation, as well as performant networking and concurrency.</p>
<p>As part of an energetic and fun team of engineers, you will be working at the frontier of designing and implementing new capabilities for the Neo4j database.</p>
<p>The amount of data handled by our customers is ever-growing, so sharding is ever more important.</p>
<p>What The Work Is Like:</p>
<ul>
<li>Designing and implementing features in different parts of the database stack, from the Cypher language implementation down to cluster networking. We have a development process focused on taking time to achieve quality and correctness. However, there is plenty of room for pragmatism and fast paced problem solving.</li>
<li>Our methodology involves daily stand-ups, frequent pair programming, design meetings and retrospectives.</li>
<li>Apart from coding features, we also track down both easy and tough bugs, fix downstream test failures, document features, participate in cross-team design meetings, and study the latest in database research.</li>
<li>You will also spend time learning, thinking and experimenting. Specialising in topics that interest you and helping to grow yourself, the team and the product.</li>
</ul>
<p>What You Will Bring:</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience developing production level software</li>
<li>Knowledge of Java, Scala or similar languages</li>
<li>Ability to work independently within a flexible software development organisation</li>
<li>Ability to communicate about, and collaborate on, complex topics.</li>
</ul>
<p>Nice to Bring:</p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of the internals of databases, compilers or query languages</li>
<li>Experience with asynchronous and reactive programming</li>
<li>Experience of distributed systems through usage, administration or development.</li>
<li>An interest in recent distributed systems developments (academic or industrial).</li>
