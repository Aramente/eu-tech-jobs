<p><strong>THE POSITION<br></strong>Our roster has an opening with your name on it</p>
<p>FanDuel is seeking an Algorithmic Trading Manager to assist in the build of our algorithmic trading team that will leverage our world-class sports pricing algorithms . Reporting to the Market Making Senior Director of Quants &amp; Algorithmic Trading, this leader will be instrumental in building a team to operate and evolve sophisticated automated trading systems that optimize our ongoing positions within the market. This role will work at the intersection of sports betting, quantitative modeling, and real-time trading to ensure we optimize for expected revenue and capital allocation.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This role will be involved with the development and performance of our algorithmic market-making trading strategies, collaborating closely with quants, product and engineering, and commercial teams.
