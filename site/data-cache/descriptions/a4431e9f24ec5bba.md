<p>As the <strong>Head of People Analytics &amp; Innovation,</strong> you will build and lead ZoomInfo's people analytics function from the ground up. Reporting to the SVP of Organizational Effectiveness, you will own the end-to-end analytics strategy for HR — designing and shipping AI-powered tools, predictive and prescriptive models, dashboards, and research that drive executive decision-making on talent, workforce planning, and employee experience. This is a hands-on, player-coach role: you will set the vision for what we build, write the code to build it, and hire and develop a small, high-impact team (2 ICs) to scale the function. The ideal candidate combines deep technical fluency (Python, statistics, SQL, AI/ML) with the strategic judgment to translate people data into business outcomes and the builder mentality to stand up new capabilities from scratch.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>What you'll do:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Build and lead ZoomInfo's People Analytics &amp; Innovation function, including hiring, developing, and managing two individual contributors</li>
<li>Define and execute the people analytics strategy and roadmap, translating HR and business priorities into a pipeline of high-impact analytics projects and AI-powered tools</li>
<li>Design and build custom AI tools, automation, and applications (using Python, MCPs, LLMs, and related technologies) that fundamentally improve how HR operates and makes decisions</li>
<li>Develop predictive and prescriptive models across workforce planning, attrition/retention, engagement, talent mobility, and employee experience</li>
<li>Create and maintain executive-ready dashboards, reports, and data visualizations that surface actionable insights for senior leadership</li>
<li>Own the design, deployment, and analysis of enterprise-wide qualitative instruments (e.g., employee surveys, pulse checks) and synthesize findings into strategic recommendations</li>
<li>Partner with HR leadership and cross-functional stakeholders to identify high-value analytical questions, scope projects, and deliver data-driven recommendations</li>
<li>Transform complex analytical findings into clear, compelling narratives and presentations for executive audiences, including the ELT</li>
<li>Establish scalable data infrastructure, governance frameworks, and measurement processes that enable a self-service analytics culture over time</li>
<li>Stay current on emerging trends in people analytics, AI/ML, and HR technology — and proactively identify opportunities to apply them at ZoomInfo</li>
<li>Collaborate with global Compliance, Privacy, Legal, and Risk teams to ensure people analytics initiatives adhere to regulatory requirements, data privacy standards, and internal governance policies</li>
<li>Partner with enterprise Data Engineering and Infrastructure teams to architect, test, and deploy analytics solutions and AI tools into production environments</li>
<li>Foster a team culture of experimentation, intellectual curiosity, and bias toward action</li>
</ul>
<h2>What you'll bring:</h2>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of progressive experience in people analytics, workforce analytics, HR data science, or a related quantitative field</li>
<li>3+ years of experience managing or leading an analytics team, including hiring, coaching, and developing talent</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience building AI/ML tools, applications, or automation from scratch in a professional setting (not just model training — full product delivery)</li>
<li>Strong hands-on proficiency in Python and SQL; experience with data manipulation, statistical modeling, and building production-grade scripts and applications</li>
<li>Experience working with modern AI/ML tools and frameworks (e.g., LLMs, GenAI APIs, MCP architecture, NLP)</li>
<li>Proven ability to design and execute enterprise survey programs and translate qualitative data into quantitative insights</li>
<li>Exceptional data storytelling skills — ability to synthesize complex analysis into clear, executive-ready communications</li>
<li>Experience working with HRIS platforms (e.g., Workday) and analytics/BI tools (e.g., Snowflake, Tableau, Looker, or similar)</li>
<li>Demonstrated track record of operating as a player-coach: setting strategy while personally delivering technical work</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field (e.g., Data Science, Statistics, I/O Psychology, Economics, Computer Science, or related)</li>
</ul>
<h3>Preferred:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Master's or Doctoral degree in a quantitative, business, or social science field (e.g., I/O Psychology, Data Science, Statistics, Organizational Behavior, Economics, Computer Science)</li>
<li>Experience building a people analytics function or team from scratch</li>
<li>Familiarity with MCP (Model Context Protocol) architecture and building AI agent workflows</li>
<li>Experience with Workday RAAS reporting and Snowflake data environments</li>
<li>Background in building self-service analytics platforms or democratizing data access within an organization</li>
