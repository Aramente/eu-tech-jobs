<p>This new team will architect the platform foundation and infrastructure that enables a "build once, run anywhere" model, ensuring our AI-powered modernisation suite operates seamlessly regardless of a client's security or network constraints. We are looking for engineers to join this high-visibility initiative, where you will solve unique distributed systems puzzles and help shape the future of how global enterprises leverage GenAI.</p>
<p>We are looking for an experienced software engineer who thrives on solving infrastructure constraints and building enterprise facing platforms.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a hands-on technical leader who can architect complex distributed systems, mentor engineers, and collaborate with product teams to deliver a platform that minimizes deployment friction and meets customers’ compliance requirements.</p>
<p>This role can be based out of our Gurgaon office or Remotely in India.</p>
<h3>The ideal candidate for this role will have</h3>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of software development and operations experience, with a focus on building platforms and distributable software infrastructure.</li>
<li>2+ years of experience leading, coaching, and mentoring a team of engineers to achieve high-impact results.</li>
<li>Deep experience designing distributable applications that run in air-gapped or highly restricted network environments.</li>
<li>Strong proficiency in containerization and orchestration, with the ability to design systems where the host executes containerized applications under strict security monitoring.</li>
<li>Experience building Service Host architectures that provide shared routing, proxy layers, and API gateways for multiple underlying services.</li>
<li>Experience managing persistent storage solutions (blob/file storage) within distributed systems.</li>
<li>Understanding of security-first design, specifically regarding the execution of untrusted code, fine-grain access control, and rigorous input validation.</li>
<li>Experience designing auto-update mechanisms for software that cannot directly access the public internet.</li>
<li>Curiosity, a positive attitude, and a drive to continue learning.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Position Expectations</h3>
<ul>
<li>Provide technical leadership and mentorship to a team of engineers, fostering a culture of innovation, quality, and continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Drive the distributed infrastructure for the Application Modernisation Platform, ensuring it supports a self-service SaaS product model while operating within client-controlled environments.</li>
<li>Lead the design and implementation of a sophisticated orchestration layer that manages authentication, telemetry, and the lifecycle of modernization tools.</li>
<li>Architect a client-side application that manages developer workflows and communicates securely with hosted services.</li>
<li>Solve complex "Day 2" operations challenges, such as designing interfaces to solve DevOps challenges to manage users, secrets, and updates.</li>
<li>Collaborate with security and product teams to ensure the platform can execute code generation and LLM-based tasks securely, protecting both client data and MongoDB IP.</li>
<li>Act as a hands-on leader, guiding the team through technical challenges related to container security, local vs. hosted logic distribution, and cross-service data sharing.</li>
</ul>
