<h2><strong>What you'll do&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join the Patient Online Booking feature team in the Patient Health Platform domain.</p>
<p>Our team is responsible for enhancing the booking experience for our 90 million patients, with over 300 million appointments expected to be booked this year. Empower your team to build better products, playing a pivotal role in the improvement of one of France’s most downloaded apps!</p>
<p>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with product managers, designers, and other engineers to define and implement features to improve healthcare access for patients</li>
<li>Take a leading role within the team and contribute to establishing a technical vision that ensures the performance, security, and usability of the application, taking into account the future needs and the high scalability needs.</li>
<li>Bring your expertise and experiences to help us continuously improve the way we design, implement, test, and monitor high-quality features</li>
<li>Improving the performance of key features implemented in Java and Ruby on Rails, sometimes completely challenging the status quo</li>
<li>Taking leadership on delivering roadmap items (together with the team)</li>
<li>Contributing to establishing a technical vision and co-drive the creation of the roadmap for next quarters</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Who you are</strong></h2>
<p>If you don’t meet all the requirements below but believe this opportunity matches your expectations and experience, we still encourage you to apply!</p>
<ul>
<li>You are proactive, curious, collaborative and eager to learn</li>
<li>You are primarily a <strong>backend engineer with expert knowledge of Java and a good knowledge of Ruby on Rails</strong> (or similar frameworks). You have a solid understanding of web technologies and software development life cycle</li>
<li>You have a <strong>“user first” mindset</strong> and want to build a product focused on bringing value to its users, through secure, well-tested and pragmatic code</li>
<li>You <strong>embrace and champion AI-augmented engineering</strong>, leveraging AI coding tools (GitHub Copilot, Claude Code...) to boost your own productivity and help the team adopt these practices effectively</li>
<li>You are <strong>fluent in English</strong></li>
</ul>
