<h4>Team Summary</h4>
<p>The Product Intelligence team drives product excellence by delivering comprehensive, customer-centric insights that connect customer needs with product innovation. We employ a systematic methodology that combines hands-on product testing, detailed documentation, research and strategic analysis to enable data-driven decision-making across the organization.<br>Testing and competitive intelligence serve as core pillars of our work. By combining real-world testing with competitive analysis, market research, and user feedback, we build a contextual understanding of product performance — what works, what doesn’t, and why.<br>Working from an end-user perspective, we identify patterns, uncover gaps, and translate findings into actionable recommendations that support product and business decisions.</p>
<h4>Job Summary</h4>
<p>We’re looking for a thoughtful and analytical professional to join our Product Intelligence team — someone who enjoys solving complex problems, learning continuously, and contributing to meaningful product outcomes. The ideal candidate brings a strong sense of critical thinking, common sense, and adaptability to a fast-paced environment where priorities can shift quickly. This role is a great fit for someone who is naturally curious, works well independently, and values being part of a collaborative team that drives real impact across product development.<br>You’ll have the opportunity to engage with cross-functional teams, explore a wide range of tasks and technologies, and grow your skills while helping shape user-centered solutions.</p>
<h4>Responsibilities and Duties</h4>
<ul>
<li>Perform hands-on testing of both hardware and software components in the laboratory by simulating real-world usage scenarios to evaluate usability, stability, performance, and overall product readiness from an end-user perspective</li>
<li>Design and execute exploratory and structured test cases aligned with product requirements and business priorities</li>
<li>Collect, analyze, and interpret data from testing, competitive, and market insights to evaluate product behavior, identify usability gaps, performance issues, and risks, and translate findings into clear, actionable recommendations</li>
<li>Perform competitive intelligence and monitor market trends, competing products, and emerging technologies</li>
<li>Retrieve data from multiple internal and external sources; aggregate and synthesize findings into clear, structured summaries</li>
<li>Analyze user feedback and support data to validate findings and support prioritization</li>
<li>Evaluate end-to-end customer journeys to uncover friction points and areas for improvement</li>
<li>Create clear, structured reports and presentations to communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders</li>
<li>Stay up to date with internal product roadmaps, updates, and documentation to ensure alignment with the latest specifications</li>
<li>Adhere to internal security protocols when handling confidential or proprietary hardware and software</li>
</ul>
