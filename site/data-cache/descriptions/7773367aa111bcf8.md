<p><strong>You are a recent graduate, looking for your first full time Data Science opportunity.</strong></p>
<p>As a Data Scientist you will work on a broad range of subjects actively contributing to the design and evolution of our suite of products focused on fraud detection, anti-money laundering, and claims automation. &nbsp;We are dedicated to providing innovative solutions, and you'll be part of a team with extensive technical and professional expertise in data science, data engineering, coding, business understanding, and client interactions. Additionally, we tackle a diverse array of data types, including structured data, unstructured text, documents, and images.</p>
<p>This opportunity is perfect for you if you're seeking a permanent role, and can come to the London office 2-3 times per week. Shift is the ideal place to kickstart your career journey!&nbsp;</p>

<div><strong>What you'll do...</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>You will actively participate in the development of our suite of products: fraud detection, anti-money laundering, and claim automation; and working on various data types such as structured data, free text, documents, and images.</li>
<li>Implementation of the data engineering, usually from client extracts to the insertion of the data in our data stores (<strong>SQL, ElasticSearch</strong>)</li>
<li>Developing, testing, tuning models, and putting them into production for tasks such as <strong>fraud detection</strong> and automation detection in complex environments.</li>
<li>Automate key business tasks by implementing them in our production process framework in <strong>C# </strong></li>
<li>Conduct meetings with clients and interact with external stakeholders, whether it is for direct user feedback, presenting business cases, or defining the roadmap of evolutions.</li>
</ul>
