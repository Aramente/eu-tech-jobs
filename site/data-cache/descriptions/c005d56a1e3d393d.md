<ul>

<li><strong>Full-Funnel Vision:</strong> Deep understanding of non-paid focus areas; experience building reporting for organic growth, brand health, and <strong>Share of Voice (SoV)</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>Technical Proficiency:</strong> Experienced and confident in operating across the full technical stack from Snowflake to Dbt and BI tools.</li>
<li><strong>Stack Knowledge:</strong> Experience navigating CRM and automation systems (Salesforce, HubSpot) and modern BI tools (Sigma, Tableau, or Looker).</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Communication:</strong> The ability to command a room of executives, translating complex data structures into a clear narrative.</li>
<li><strong>Language:</strong> Professional fluency in written and spoken English.</li>
<li>Demonstrates strong automation and AI capabilities.</li>

<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Advanced Attribution Modeling:</strong> Lead the development and refinement of attribution models that capture the value of Marketing and various TOFU streams, ensuring a holistic view of the customer journey.</li>
<li><strong>Non-Paid &amp; Brand Analytics:</strong> Establish the framework for measuring all marketing channels. Build robust reporting for <strong>Share of Voice</strong>, organic reach, and brand sentiment to balance our performance marketing focus.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Planning &amp; Forecasting:</strong> Drive the annual and quarterly planning process for marketing investments, providing data-backed recommendations on budget allocation.</li>
<li><strong>LTV &amp; Predictive Modeling:</strong> Build and maintain projected Lifetime Value (LTV) models to guide acquisition and retention strategies, ensuring high-quality signals are fed back into ad platforms.</li>
<li><strong>Full-Funnel Optimisation:</strong> Analyse the B2B funnel from initial awareness to closed-won revenue, identifying friction points and white-space opportunities for the growth team.</li>
<li><strong>Data Governance &amp; Integrity:</strong> Act as the guardian of marketing data. Partner with RevOps to ensure data flowing from HubSpot and Salesforce remains the "single source of truth."</li>
<li><strong>Mentorship &amp; Leadership:</strong> While this role may not have initial direct reports, you will act as a technical lead, mentoring senior analysts and setting the standard for analytical excellence across the team.</li>
</ul>
