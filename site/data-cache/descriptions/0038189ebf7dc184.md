<h2><strong>Role background (Fixed-Term Contract)</strong></h2>
<p>This is a <strong>1year fixed-term opportunity</strong> within Wolt’s High-Volume Talent Acquisition team, created to support a period of transformation in how we hire for high-volume, frontline roles.</p>
<p>As our new High-Volume Talent Acquisition Partner, you will focus on hiring for Support Associate and Grocery Associate roles in the Greece market. You will support day-to-day recruitment while also contributing to the rollout and embedding of a more scalable, data-driven, and AI-supported hiring model.</p>
<p>This role is ideal for someone who is curious, adaptable, and motivated to grow within Talent Acquisition while gaining hands-on experience during a transformation phase.</p>
<h2><strong>Why this role matters</strong></h2>
<p>Support Associates and Wolt Market Grocery Associates are essential to Wolt’s day-to-day operations. They support customers, courier partners, venues, and in-store operations, making efficient, high-quality hiring critical to business continuity and growth.</p>
<p>As Wolt prepares for the next phase of high-volume hiring, this role will help ensure strong delivery, continuity, and candidate experience throughout the transition.</p>
<h2><strong>What you’ll be doing</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Support and lead end-to-end recruitment for high-volume roles in Greece, including attraction, assessment, and offer processes for Support and Grocery Associate positions</li>
<li>Contribute to the rollout and adoption of new, AI-supported and data-driven hiring processes and tools</li>
<li>Use recruitment data and reporting to monitor funnel performance, identify improvement opportunities, and support efficiency gains</li>
<li>Deliver a consistent, inclusive, and positive candidate experience during a period of change</li>
<li>Experiment with and support new sourcing and attraction approaches, while executing core recruiting best practices</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with your sub-team and the broader Talent Acquisition organization to share learnings, ideas, and solutions</li>
<li>Partner with Employer Branding and other talent teams to support campaigns and initiatives related to high-volume hiring</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What we’re looking for</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>You have hands-on recruitment experience and enjoy partnering with stakeholders while leading end-to-end hiring processes. You have recruited associates, customer service representatives, or other blue-collar roles in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Comfort working in dynamic, evolving environments and openness to learning new tools and processes</li>
<li>Passion for candidate experience and a quality-focused mindset</li>
<li>Fluent communication skills in <strong>English and Greek</strong>, both written and verbal.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Curiosity for data and willingness to use insights to improve hiring outcomes</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What this role offers</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience operating within a mature, AI-driven high-volume recruitment model</li>
<li>Exposure to scalable recruitment tooling and data-driven decision-making</li>
<li>Opportunity to build strong operational TA skills in a shared-service / hub environment</li>
<li>A clearly defined, time-bound role with meaningful impact and learning opportunities</li>
</ul>
