<h2 data-section-id="s57sc6" data-start="294" data-end="312">About JetBrains</h2>
<p data-start="314" data-end="626">JetBrains creates intelligent software development tools used by more than 11 million professionals worldwide. Since our founding in 2000, we’ve focused on building products that help developers write better code faster and more efficiently. Today, 90 of the Fortune Global 100 companies rely on JetBrains tools.</p>
<p data-start="628" data-end="887">We are a global company with over 2,500 team members working across Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, and Asia. We care deeply about meaningful work, long-term impact, and creating a collaborative environment where people can grow and do their best work.</p>
<h2 data-section-id="1wdgaw5" data-start="889" data-end="906">About the role</h2>
<p data-start="908" data-end="1226">We’re looking for a <strong>Technical Account Manager (TAM)</strong> to join our team and <strong>support our enterprise customers</strong>. In this role, you will be responsible for managing enterprise support engagements and professional services projects, ensuring customers receive high-quality technical guidance and a seamless service experience.</p>
<p data-start="1228" data-end="1515">As a Technical Account Manager, you will serve as the primary technical point of contact for assigned accounts. You’ll work cross-functionally with Sales, Customer Success, Support, Engineering, and Product teams to drive successful outcomes and help customers achieve their technical and business objectives.</p>
