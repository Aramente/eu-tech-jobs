<h3><strong>About Wolt</strong></h3>
<p>At Wolt, you can grow your sales career with a fun and fast-scaling global business. Part of the DoorDash team, we’re building a global logistics platform that can deliver almost anything in your city in minutes. Today, our products are used by 55+ million registered users who order from 200,000+ merchants across 30+ countries.</p>
<p>To support our expanding merchant offering, we are scaling our&nbsp;<strong>Commerce Platform</strong>, which consists of two core products:</p>
<h3><strong>Wolt Commerce Platform Products in a Nutshell</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Wolt Drive</strong>&nbsp;- A delivery-as-a-service solution (DaaS) enabling merchants to add fast, reliable last-mile delivery to their own ordering channels.</p>
<p><strong>Wolt Storefront</strong>&nbsp;- A white-label online ordering platform that helps merchants create branded digital stores with seamless checkout and increased conversion.</p>
<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Commerce Platform Sales Specialist</strong>&nbsp;role focuses purely on&nbsp;<strong>acquiring new partners</strong>&nbsp;for both products - selling the value, closing the deals, and handing partners over to onboarding teams.</p>
<h1><strong>What you’ll be doing</strong></h1>
<p>As&nbsp;<strong>Commerce Platform Sales Specialist</strong>, your mission is clear:&nbsp;<strong>identify, pitch, and close new merchant partnerships</strong>&nbsp;for Wolt Drive and Wolt Storefront. You will build a strong pipeline, run a highly structured sales process, and secure high-quality, scalable partnerships.</p>
