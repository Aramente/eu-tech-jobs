<p>Bitwarden is the trusted identity security leader for millions of users worldwide, empowering enterprises, developers, and individuals to securely manage and share sensitive information anywhere. Bitwarden makes it easy for all users to extend robust security across their devices with password management, secrets management, and passwordless and passkey innovations. The company is headquartered in Santa Barbara, California. Learn more at <a href="https://bitwarden.com/">bitwarden.com</a>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>As an Associate QA Manager, you will serve as a hands-on leader within the QA team, balancing direct testing contributions with people management responsibilities. You will work closely with QA engineers on a day-to-day basis, actively participating in testing efforts while mentoring a team of four SDETs and collaborating with engineering, product, and design teams. You will help ensure our software meets both functional and performance standards and that releases are reliable and bug-free.</p>
<p>This is an all-remote team and we’re looking for someone located in the US. We do not offer visa sponsorship at this time.&nbsp;</p>
