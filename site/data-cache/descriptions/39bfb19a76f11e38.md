<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p>As Country Sales Lead, you will be responsible for driving both restaurant and SMB retail acquisition across Cyprus and Malta. You’ll set strategy, coach and develop your team, and use data to guide decision-making and identify opportunities. Working closely with onboarding and support leads, you’ll ensure new partners are successfully integrated and set up for long-term success.</p>
<h3><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Build and lead the SMB acquisition teams in both markets, setting KPIs, monitoring performance, and providing structured feedback</li>
<li>Develop and execute plans to target gaps and opportunities in both markets, including identifying SMBs that are a great fit for Wolt</li>
<li>Build relationships with potential key SMB partners and manage the sales pipeline systematically</li>
<li>Negotiate partnership terms while analyzing potential performance to set profitable business agreements</li>
<li>Make sound business decisions by combining market intuition with data insights</li>
<li>Foster a positive team culture that enables growth and collaboration</li>
<li>Lead by example by living Wolt’s values and behaviors</li>
</ul>
<h2>Our humble expectations&nbsp;</h2>
<ul>
<li>6–8 years of experience in B2B field sales, ideally in marketplaces, tech, or hospitality.</li>
<li>2–4 years leading sales teams: hiring, onboarding, coaching, and guiding performance toward clear goals.</li>
<li>Own regional acquisition plans and KPIs, setting meaningful targets and holding your team accountable.</li>
<li>Built repeatable sales processes and champion CRM hygiene, pipeline discipline, and reliable forecasting.</li>
<li>Negotiate with senior SMB partners and structured win-win deals that balance partner value and solid unit economics.</li>
<li>Make data-driven decisions, blending local know-how with performance dashboards and weekly insights.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with onboarding, support, and marketing teams to remove friction and deliver smooth, on-time launches.</li>
<li>Foster a strong, supportive team culture, offering clear direction, giving feedback early and often, and creating a low-ego, high-ownership environment.</li>
<li>Communicate clearly and confidently in both English and Greek.</li>
</ul>
