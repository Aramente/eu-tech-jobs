<p><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">Process &amp; Knowledge Management (PKM) ensures that Customer Service operates with clear, simple, and sustainable processes. The team owns operational knowledge across CS and partners closely with Process Design &amp; Innovation (PDI) to support the full lifecycle of process design, rollout, stabilization, and long-term maintenance.</span></p>
<p>Within PKM, the Tooling &amp; Agent Experience SME focuses on the systems and automation layer that enables CS processes,&nbsp; ensuring that Zendesk, CAP, chatbot solutions, and AI-driven tooling effectively support both agents and customers. This is a newly evolving area within PKM and plays a central role in strengthening CS efficiency, automation, and scalability.</p>
<p><br><strong>Your Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p><strong>Core Responsibility</strong></p>
<p>Own the tooling and system experience within Customer Service, ensuring that CS platforms, automation solutions, and AI-driven tools effectively support operational processes, agent productivity, and customer self-service.</p>
<p><strong>Process &amp; Tooling Design Partnership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute tooling and system expertise to PDI-led initiatives, ensuring that new or revised processes are supported by scalable and sustainable system configurations.</li>
<li>Translate process requirements into tooling configurations and system enhancements in collaboration with Product &amp; Engineering (P&amp;E).</li>
<li>Partner closely with regional SMEs (e.g., DEU, UK) to ensure system changes are aligned with updated Work Instructions and domain-specific documentation prior to rollout.</li>
<li>Support rollout readiness together with domain SMEs by validating that tooling changes are configured, tested, and operationally understood before go-live.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>System Evolution &amp; Continuous Improvement</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the evolutionary maintenance of Zendesk, CAP, and related CS systems, partnering with Product Teams to continuously improve configurations, workflows, and automation rules.</li>
<li>Deploy and maintain AI-driven solutions, including chatbots and ticket deflection tools, ensuring alignment with CS knowledge and operational needs.</li>
<li>Monitor system usage data and analytics to identify friction points, inefficiencies, and automation opportunities.</li>
<li>Prioritize and own CS feature requests to Product ensuring business impact and agent experience are clearly represented.</li>
<li>Represent CS tooling needs in cross-functional discussions with Product, Engineering, and Steering teams.</li>
<li>Drive improvements that enhance agent experience, reduce manual effort, and increase self-service adoption.</li>
</ul>
