<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<p>The Field Engineer is a hands-on role responsible for installing, calibrating, maintaining, and troubleshooting Pronto’s autonomous technology on heavy equipment at a mining site in Saudi Arabia. This role will support the on-site deployment of Pronto’s autonomous systems, working closely with our client, <strong>Ma’aden</strong>. The position requires strong electrical and mechanical skills and comfort working in outdoor industrial environments.</p>
<p><strong>Installation &amp; Deployment</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Perform on-site installation of Pronto hardware and software solutions, including sensor systems, DBW kits, power integration, and vehicle interfaces.</li>
<li>On-site installation of peripheral hardware such as network stations, gate systems, and office infrastructure.</li>
<li>Conduct system calibrations and validation tests to ensure installations meet operational standards.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Engineering and Customer teams to ensure field deployments align with specifications and best practices, as well as collaborating on R&amp;D projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Maintenance &amp; Troubleshooting</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Carry out scheduled maintenance tasks to ensure system health, reliability, and long-term performance.</li>
<li>Monitor and log equipment conditions, identifying signs of wear, degradation, or environmental impacts</li>
<li>Diagnose and repair hardware, software, and connectivity issues in the field.</li>
<li>Utilize diagnostic tools and logs to identify root causes and implement fixes with minimal downtime.</li>
<li>Communicate findings clearly to engineering teams for deeper analysis and continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Customer Site Support</strong></p>
<ul>

</ul>
