<h1><strong>Business Intern - Project Management&nbsp;</strong><strong><br></strong>(6 Month Internship)</h1>
<p><strong>Bitmovin</strong>&nbsp;is a global Series-C SaaS scale-up that provides award-winning video streaming technology to world-renowned brands including Disney, Warner Brothers Discovery, BBC, Red Bull Media House, Hulu, and The New York Times, to name just a few.&nbsp;</p>

So we are an inclusive flexible employer of some of the most impressive and diverse talent across the globe, with a culture that enables you to see success by being your best self.<br><br><em>"We’re looking for curious, fast learners who are excited to use cutting-edge technology and engage with customers to drive success. If you’re a problem solver who loves tech and connecting with people, join us and make an impact at Bitmovin."&nbsp; ~&nbsp;</em>Ian Baglow, CRO/President</p>
<p data-start="166" data-end="405"><strong data-start="166" data-end="201">As a Project Management Intern,</strong> you will support the coordination and execution of cross-functional projects across Europe and APAC, working closely with CX team leads, Customer Success, Solutions Engineering, and Revenue Operations.</p>
<p data-start="407" data-end="650">You will take ownership of day-to-day project coordination, stakeholder alignment, and operational follow-ups, enabling senior Project Managers and Team Leads to focus on technical delivery, escalations, and strategic stakeholder engagement.</p>
<p data-start="652" data-end="829">This role is perfect for a business student with a strong interest in project management and operations, offering hands-on experience in a fast-paced, international environment.<br><br></p>
<h3 data-start="836" data-end="887"><strong data-start="836" data-end="887">As a Project Management Intern, you will cover:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li data-start="889" data-end="1160"><strong data-start="889" data-end="926">Project Coordination &amp; Execution:</strong> Support the delivery of small-scale projects by maintaining timelines, tracking milestones, and ensuring alignment across stakeholders. Assist in coordinating “Base” integrations (e.g., VOD or Player) and help keep projects on track.</li>
<li data-start="1162" data-end="1443"><strong data-start="1162" data-end="1200">Meeting Coordination &amp; Follow-ups:</strong> Organize meetings across multiple time zones (Europe &amp; APAC), prepare agendas, and ensure the right stakeholders are involved. Capture key decisions, translate them into clear action items with owners and deadlines, and follow up on progress.</li>
<li data-start="1445" data-end="1702"><strong data-start="1445" data-end="1480">Cross-Functional Collaboration:</strong> Work closely with CX, engineering, sales, and revenue operations teams to ensure smooth execution of projects. Act as a central coordination point to streamline communication and resolve day-to-day operational challenges.</li>
<li data-start="1704" data-end="1912"><strong data-start="1704" data-end="1737">Project Tracking &amp; Reporting:</strong> Maintain project trackers, charters, and milestone logs to ensure transparency and visibility across teams. Identify risks, flag blockers, and support regular status updates.</li>
<li data-start="1914" data-end="2103"><strong data-start="1914" data-end="1952">Client Onboarding &amp; Documentation:</strong> Support onboarding processes by managing questionnaires, collecting client inputs, updating documentation, and ensuring readiness for project kickoff.</li>
<li data-start="2105" data-end="2328"><strong data-start="2105" data-end="2151">Operational Support &amp; Process Improvement:</strong> Assist with professional services administration, including tracking hours, supporting SOW updates, and improving internal processes to enhance efficiency and delivery quality.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li data-start="2371" data-end="2607"><strong data-start="2371" data-end="2409">Educational Background &amp; Interest:</strong> Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Business, Management, or a related field, with a strong interest in project management, operations, and working in an international environment.</li>
<li data-start="2609" data-end="2790"><strong data-start="2609" data-end="2648">Highly Organized &amp; Detail-Oriented:</strong> Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks, track details, and maintain structured documentation across projects.</li>
<li data-start="2792" data-end="2963"><strong data-start="2792" data-end="2830">Strong Communicator &amp; Coordinator:</strong> Confident in working across teams and time zones, with the ability to keep stakeholders aligned and ensure clear, timely follow-ups.</li>
<li data-start="2965" data-end="3109"><strong data-start="2965" data-end="2999">Proactive &amp; Solution-Oriented:</strong> Able to identify gaps, follow up on open items, and proactively drive tasks forward in a dynamic environment.</li>
<li data-start="3111" data-end="3252"><strong data-start="3111" data-end="3154">Technical &amp; Tools Interest (Desirable):</strong> Exposure to project management tools, data tracking, or AI tools in business processes is a plus.</li>
