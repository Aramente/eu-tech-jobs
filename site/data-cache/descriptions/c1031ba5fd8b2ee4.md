<div class="content-intro"><p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Airbnb was born in 2007 when two hosts welcomed three guests to their San Francisco home, and has since grown to over 5 million hosts who have welcomed over 2 billion guest arrivals in almost every country across the globe. Every day, hosts offer unique stays and experiences that make it possible for guests to connect with communities in a more authentic way.</span></p></div><p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #000000;"><strong>The Community You Will Join: </strong></span></p>
<p>The mission of the Airbnb Global Safety and Risk Management (GSRM) team, as a component of Fraud and Safety Operations (FSO), is to foster a safe, secure, and healthy workplace for our employee community and peace of mind for Airbnb’s global community. Airbnb GSRM&nbsp; programs include Global Threat Assessment; Open Source Investigations; Insider Threat Prevention; Organized Criminal Syndicate Investigations; Global Law Enforcement Engagement; and Law Enforcement Operations.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #000000;"><strong><br></strong></span><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #000000;"><strong>The Difference You Will Make:</strong></span></p>
<p>The Law Enforcement Operations (LE Ops) Manager will serve law enforcement and Airbnb in responding to data requests across APAC. This individual should be committed to collaboration between Airbnb and law enforcement (LE) in protecting our hosts, guests, and the global community while ensuring the privacy and protection of data. The LE Ops team requires vigilance, dedication, and problem solving skills as it identifies threats and takes appropriate action. The APAC LE Ops team is a critical component to global operations coordinating communication and responses with LE on a 24/7/365 basis.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #000000;"><strong>A Typical Day: </strong></span></p>
<p>We are looking for a passionate and relational leader to shape strategy, pursue excellence and efficiency, and develop the members of the APAC team, while also contributing to GSRM’s broader safety efforts and ensuring compliant and timely responses to LE requests. The successful candidate is a strategic problem solver and communication specialist with a creative mentality who can lead people, manage workflows, and work collaboratively across senior functional leadership with cross-functional teams.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The Law Enforcement Operations Manager will report directly to the Senior Manager, Global Law Enforcement Operations.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #000000;"><strong>Your Expertise:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage performance and growth of a small team of Law Enforcement Operations Associates and how we respond to law enforcement authorities across the APAC region</li>
<li>Work closely with our Legal team on Airbnb’s response to law enforcement requests for user data</li>
<li>Work with other Airbnb teams to develop and coordinate active matters with law enforcement&nbsp;</li>
<li>Assist in the development, refinement, and execution of policies, processes, and workflows relating to the production of data in response to legal requests from law enforcement&nbsp;</li>
<li>Develop and assess accuracy, efficiency, and other mission success data in support of continuous improvement</li>
<li>Lead the team to track, analyze, and accurately respond to a variety of legal requests for user data working directly with law enforcement and security services, including search warrants, court orders, international requests and subpoenas</li>
<li>Support operational escalations related to critical matters from global team members,&nbsp; cross-functional partners, and collaborate on operational decisions&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ensure that all requests are scrutinized and handled in strict accordance with applicable laws, our terms of service, and our law enforcement guidelines</li>
<li>Develop and deliver familiarity education to law enforcement partners</li>
<li>Support the expansion of healthy law enforcement engagement and communication</li>
<li>Lead LE Ops APAC in planning, staffing, budgeting, and managing expenses&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #000000;">Requirements:</span></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of relevant work experience, including 2 years of hands-on leadership experience, or relevant senior level LE response function at a similar company or LE agency.</li>
<li>Knowledge and experience related to data protection legislation and online investigations</li>
<li>Significant experience working collaboratively with LE agencies, security services, and data protection authorities</li>
<li>Proven capacity to manage and resolve complex problems and sensitive issues using sound judgment and communication in handling emergent matters&nbsp;</li>
<li>Experience prioritizing multiple functions and tasks while managing core work responsibilities without compromising quality</li>
<li>Effective at identifying talent and building effective teams through trusted relationships</li>
<li>Executive presence with outstanding communication skills including written, verbal and presentation skills with the ability to create executive communications and presentations</li>
<li>Sound judgment &amp; ability to work independently with minimal supervisory oversight</li>
<li>Flexibility and willingness to travel and work off hours, including weekends and occasionally holidays, in order to support emergency matters and interact with law enforcement and employees located across the globe</li>
<li>Demonstrated commitment to Airbnb’s core values.</li>
</ul>
<p>Preferred Experience: Proficiency in a second APAC language is desirable.</p>
