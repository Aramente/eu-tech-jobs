## Your (space) mission

As the Sales Engineer at cosine you help bridge cutting-edge space instrumentation and the customers who rely on it to unlock new insights about our planet and beyond. You operate at the intersection of technology and commerce, supporting the Remote Sensing Director in shaping opportunities, building strong customer relationships and turning innovative optical instrument concepts into awarded contracts. From early lead engagement through to proposal and offer phase, you play a key role in bringing space hardware from idea to orbit. You will see your work materialize into instruments that are designed and built in-house and ultimately launched into space for leading commercial and institutional customers.

## Tasks & responsibilities

<p>In this role you will:</p><ul><li>Follow up on incoming leads and actively support business development activities in the remote sensing market</li><li>Prepare, coordinate and write technical-commercial proposals and offers for institutional and commercial customers</li><li>Translate complex technical solutions into clear, compelling value propositions</li><li>Support the standardization and structuring of cosine’s commercial offering and sales processes</li><li>Work closely with engineering, project management and leadership teams to align customer needs with technical solutions</li></ul>

## Your profile

<p>We are looking for candidates with the following qualifications:</p><ul><li>A technical background in aerospace engineering, physics, remote sensing or a related field</li><li>Experience or strong affinity with sales engineering, technical sales or proposal management in a high-tech environment</li><li>Ability to understand complex optical or space systems and communicate their value clearly to customers</li><li>Strong organizational skills and a structured way of working in a dynamic project environment</li><li>A proactive and collaborative mindset with the ability to take initiative and drive tasks forward independently</li><li>Excellent communication skills in English, both written and spoken, Dutch is considered an advantage</li></ul>
