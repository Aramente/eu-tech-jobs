<p>We’re hiring a <strong>Mid-Market Customer Success Account Executive</strong> to own and grow a portfolio of 100+ accounts in the SMB to lower mid-market segment. <p>You’re a CS-minded seller who drives adoption, protects revenue, and turns healthy customers into growing customers.
