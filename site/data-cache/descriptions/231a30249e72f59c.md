## What you will do

- You will report to the Director, Analytics and AI Transformation.
- Partner directly with business stakeholders to identify core challenges, define key performance indicators (KPIs), and translate high-level needs into structured technical plans.
- Own the full analytics workflow, from transforming raw data in dbt to building dashboards in Sigma and presenting findings that influence strategy
- Write SQL to solve complex modeling challenges and maintain a "single source of truth" across the organization.
- Deliver high-impact, data-driven insights and recommendations; collaborate with leadership to influence key decisions and optimize the growth funnel
- Help shape data governance and foster data literacy, empowering stakeholders to self-serve with accurate data

## What you will bring with you

- 5+ years of experience in Analytics Engineering, Data Engineering, or a highly technical Data Analyst role with a focus on end-to-end data delivery.
- Proven experience with modern BI platforms (Sigma, Looker, or PowerBI), with a deep understanding of how to optimize these tools for performance.
- Knowledge of reporting front ends and the best practices for structuring data for visualization
- Strong proficiency in SQL with hands-on experience in complex data modeling and query optimization.
- Hands-on experience with dbt (development, testing, and documentation) and a proven track record of building production-grade ETL/ELT pipelines.

## What we look for

- Someone with a proven ability to bridge the gap between backend data and frontend business value by translating diverse use cases into optimized SQL and intuitive dashboards.
- An expert who understands how to bridge the gap between raw backend data and frontend business value using modern data stack tools.
- A mindset geared toward partnering with Sales, Product, and Engineering leadership to source data and establish global data standards.
- Someone excited to learn and grow skills in backend automation and advanced BI functionality as our environment evolves.
- Someone who doesn't just build; but documents, tests, and advocates for data quality to ensure the organization is always working with the best information possible.
