<h3><strong>Role Overview</strong></h3>
<p>We are looking for a seasoned DevOps Engineer who is passionate about building scalable infrastructure while maintaining a relentless focus on cloud unit economics. You will lead our FinOps initiatives, ensuring our cloud footprint (AWS/GCP/Azure) is high-performing, secure, and cost-efficient.</p>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Infrastructure Ownership:</strong> Lead the design and management of cloud infrastructure using <strong>IaC (Terraform/Pulumi)</strong>, ensuring cost-efficiency is baked into the architecture from day one.</li>
<li><strong>FinOps Leadership:</strong> Establish and drive cloud cost-optimization strategies, including <strong>rightsization, lifecycle management, and committed use discounts (RIs/Savings Plans).</strong></li>
<li><strong>Cost Visibility &amp; Reporting:</strong> Build automated dashboards (using tools like CloudHealth, Kubecost, or native Quicksight/Looker) to provide granular visibility into spend by department, project, or microservice.</li>
<li><strong>CI/CD Optimization:</strong> Maintain and evolve CI/CD pipelines (GitLab CI, Jenkins, or GitHub Actions) with a focus on reducing "build waste" and optimizing ephemeral environment costs.</li>
<li><strong>Anomaly Detection:</strong> Implement automated alerting for cost spikes and unexpected resource utilization to prevent "cloud bill shock."</li>
<li><strong>Collaboration:</strong> Work closely with Finance and Engineering leadership to set cloud budgets, track KPIs (like cost per transaction), and advocate for a "cost-conscious" engineering culture.</li>
<li><strong>Observability:</strong> Manage full-stack monitoring (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK) to correlate performance metrics with cost data.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Qualifications</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Experience:</strong> 5+ years of professional experience in DevOps, SRE, or Cloud Engineering.</li>
<li><strong>Cloud Expertise:</strong> Deep hands-on experience with <strong>AWS </strong>, GCP, or Azure. Professional-level certifications are a plus.</li>
<li><strong>FinOps Proficiency:</strong> Proven track record of reducing cloud spend without compromising performance. Familiarity with the <strong>FinOps Framework</strong> (Inform, Optimize, Operate).</li>
<li><strong>Automation:</strong> Advanced scripting skills in <strong>Python or Go</strong> for automating cost-saving tasks (e.g., auto-stopping dev environments, identifying orphaned volumes).</li>
<li><strong>Containerization:</strong> Strong experience with <strong>Kubernetes (EKS/GKE)</strong> and knowledge of how to optimize k8s resource requests/limits.</li>
<li><strong>Analytical Mindset:</strong> Ability to translate complex cloud billing data into actionable business insights.</li>
<li><strong>Communication:</strong> Strong ability to influence engineering teams to adopt cost-saving best practices.</li>
</ul>
