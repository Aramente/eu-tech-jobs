<p><strong>Identity Senior Solution Specialist - Identity Governance</strong></p>
<p>As a Senior Solution Specialist, you will be part of the presales team that delivers sales presentations and product demonstrations to educate customers on the best ways to implement Okta Identity Governance(OIG) solutions. You will report to the Manager in the OFCTO organization.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll be doing&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Strategic Customer Engagement:</strong></li>
<ul>
<li>Support strategic customer conversations aligning with technical and engineering leaders and stakeholders.</li>
<li>Deliver on emerging trends, industry best practices, and identity-driven digital transformation.</li>
<li>Assist in preparing executive-level messaging and participate in strategic discussions to align Okta’s solutions with customer business outcomes.</li>
<li>Develop customer-centric points of view that translate Okta’s solutions to customer problems.</li>
<li>Lead technical discovery sessions with security and infrastructure leaders to uncover challenges related to endpoint security, passwordless adoption, and identity threat vectors.</li>
<li>Deliver compelling, customized demonstrations and architectural sessions focused on Okta Identity Governance&nbsp;</li>
<li>Design and execute outcome-focused Proof of Concepts (POCs) that prove Okta’s governance solutions integrate across SaaS, on-premises and disconnected applications</li>
<li>Maintain a broad and deep technical understanding of the Okta identity product lines and the underlying technologies and protocols</li>
<li>Craft engaging solution content to position Okta’s value across multiple business units, including software engineering, operations, product management, and business stakeholders</li>
</ul>
</ol>

<p><strong>Position Requirements:</strong></p>
<p><strong>Identity &amp; Directory Services Mastery</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Deep Infrastructure Knowledge: Expert-level understanding of Active Directory (AD) and Azure AD (Entra ID) or similar, including Group Policy Objects (GPOs), Kerberos, and OIDC/SAML protocols.</li>
<li>Multi-IdP Ecosystems: Proficiency in managing and securing diverse Identity Providers (IdPs) like Okta, Ping Identity, and Google Workspace.</li>
<li>Hybrid Identity: Experience managing the complexities of identity synchronization between on-premises environments and the cloud.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Identity Governance &amp; Security Mastery</strong></p>
<ul>
<ul>

<li>Architecture viability: Ability to translate complex requirements around Joiner-Mover-Leaver flows that account for software rationalization and automation tasks</li>
</ul>
</ul>
