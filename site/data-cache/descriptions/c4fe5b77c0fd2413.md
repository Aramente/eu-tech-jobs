<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><h3>ABOUT THE TEAM</h3>
<p>The National Sales Manager – Loans Against Securities (LAS) will be responsible for driving pan-India sales and revenue growth across products such as Loans Against Shares, Loans Against Mutual Funds, ESOP Funding, and Promoter Funding. The role requires building a high-performing sales organization, deep engagement with wealth ecosystems, and strong coordination with credit and risk teams to ensure profitable and compliant growth.</p>
<h3>ABOUT THE ROLE</h3>
<p><strong>1. Business Strategy &amp; Growth&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and execute the national sales strategy for LAS products, including: Loans Against Shares,&nbsp;Loans Against Mutual Funds,&nbsp;ESOP funding,&nbsp;Promoter funding / structured financing.</li>
<li>Own annual business plans, budgets, and growth targets.</li>
<li>Identify and expand into new geographies, segments, and channels (HNIs, UHNIs, promoters, startup founders, family offices).&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>2. Sales &amp; Channel Development</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and scale multiple sales channels:</li>
<li>Wealth and private banking teams</li>
<li>Relationship managers and LAS specialists</li>
<li>IFAs, MFDs, brokers, and family offices</li>
<li>Startup ecosystems for ESOP funding</li>
<li>Drive cross-sell of LAS to existing wealth, liability, and corporate clients.</li>
<li>Ensure strong pipeline management, lead conversion, and deal closures.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>3</strong>. <strong>Promoter Funding &amp; Structured LAS </strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead sales of promoter funding and high-ticket LAS transactions.</li>
<li>Work closely with credit, legal, and risk teams on deal structuring, security cover, and covenants.</li>
<li>Engage with promoters, CFOs, and treasury teams for long-term relationships.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>4. ESOP &amp; Startup-Focused Financing </strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a national ESOP funding program targeting startup founders and senior employees.</li>
<li>Develop partnerships with startup accelerators, VC funds, and HR platforms.</li>
<li>Ensure seamless execution with clear communication on valuation, liquidity events, and risk disclosures.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>5. Team Leadership &amp; Performance Management</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and manage a pan-India LAS sales team including regional heads, cluster managers, and RMs.</li>
<li>Define KPIs, incentive structures, and productivity benchmarks.</li>
<li>Conduct regular sales reviews, training, and capability-building sessions.</li>
<li>Foster a culture of performance, compliance, and customer-centricity.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<h3>WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR</h3>
<ul>
<li>12-15 years of experience in LAS, wealth lending, secured lending, or capital market–linked products.</li>
<li>Strong track record in sales leadership and high-value deal closures.</li>
<li>Experience working with HNIs, promoters, startups, and wealth ecosystems.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Deep understanding of equity, mutual funds, ESOPs, and promoter structures.</li>
<li>Strong negotiation, stakeholder management, and deal-structuring skills.</li>
<li>Strategic mindset with hands-on execution capability.</li>
<li>High ethical standards and risk awareness.</li>
<li>Ability to lead large, distributed sales teams.&nbsp;</li>
<li>MBA / Postgraduate degree in Finance, Marketing, or Management preferred.</li>
<li>Capital market or lending certifications are an advantage.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
