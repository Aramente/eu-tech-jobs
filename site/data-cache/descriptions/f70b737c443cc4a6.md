<p>As a Senior Talent Acquisition Manager at Doctolib Germany, you'll be instrumental in building exceptional Sales &amp; Operations teams that drive our ambitious growth in the German market. You'll lead strategic recruitment efforts in a competitive landscape where building Doctolib's brand recognition and talent pipeline requires strong outbound strategies and top-funnel innovation. Your expertise in stakeholder management will be crucial as you partner with leadership to establish Doctolib as the employer of choice in German healthcare technology.</p>
<p><strong>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Top Funnel Excellence:</strong> You will develop and execute comprehensive recruitment strategies focused on top-funnel optimization to attract top-tier Sales &amp; Operations talent in Germany's competitive market</li>
<li><strong>Market Positioning &amp; Brand Building: </strong>In collaboration with our central Employer brand Talent Marketing team, you will craft compelling employer value propositions tailored to the German market, establishing Doctolib's presence and credibility in a landscape where our brand recognition is still developing</li>
<li><strong>Outbound Recruitment Mastery:</strong> You will spearhead proactive talent sourcing initiatives, building robust pipelines through advanced outbound techniques, market mapping, and competitive intelligence to identify and engage passive candidates</li>
<li><strong>Advanced Stakeholder Management: </strong>You will lead high-level stakeholder partnerships, serving as a strategic advisor to Sales &amp; Operations leadership teams, translating business objectives into actionable talent acquisition strategies while managing expectations across multiple senior stakeholders</li>
<li><strong>Team Leadership &amp; Development: </strong>You will lead and coach a team of 8 recruiters, elevating their strategic thinking and execution capabilities while fostering a culture of excellence in candidate experience and assessment</li>
<li><strong>Recruitment Innovation &amp; Excellence</strong>: You will drive operational excellence by implementing cutting-edge AI-powered tools, data-driven processes, and innovative recruitment methodologies to maximize team performance and competitive advantage</li>
<li><strong>End-to-End Strategic Hiring</strong>: You will personally manage recruitment for senior and critical Sales &amp; Operations profiles, ensuring seamless execution from initial market research through successful onboarding</li>
</ul>
