We move with urgency and believe great outcomes come from high standards</p></div><p><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p>As a SDET, you will play a critical role in shaping and executing our automation-first quality strategy across web, API and AI-driven systems.</p>
<p>You’ll work closely with Engineering and Product teams to ensure that quality is designed into every feature, enforced through strong automation, and protected by strict release gates. This is a hands-on, technically deep role with real ownership and impact.<strong><br></strong></p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Design, build, and maintain scalable automation frameworks using Playwright and SerenityJS</li>
<li>Contribute to the enhancement and scalability of existing automation frameworks</li>
<li>Build and maintain REST API automation using Postman or equivalent tools</li>
<li>Integrate and maintain automated test execution within CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, GitHub Actions)</li>
<li>Investigate test failures, perform root cause analysis, and collaborate with engineering teams to resolve issues</li>
<li>Ensure automated test coverage aligns with defined acceptance criteria and release requirements</li>
<li>Participate in automation code reviews to maintain consistency, readability, and maintainability</li>
<li>Support execution of regression, integration, and end-to-end test suites across environments</li>
<li>Collaborate during story refinement and design discussions to identify test scenarios, risks, and edge cases</li>
<li>Contribute to automation reporting, metrics, and continuous quality improvements</li>
<li>Work closely with cross-functional teams to promote quality best practices</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who you are</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience in QA Automation / SDET roles</li>
<li>Strong hands-on experience with modern automation frameworks (Playwright preferred)</li>
<li>Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript for building maintainable test automation</li>
<li>Solid understanding of REST API testing concepts and tools such as Postman</li>
<li>Experience integrating and running automated tests within CI/CD pipelines</li>
<li>Familiarity with BDD practices and frameworks such as Cucumber</li>
<li>Working knowledge of Git and modern version control workflows</li>
<li>Experience working in containerized environments (e.g., Docker); exposure to Kubernetes is a plus</li>
<li>Understanding of regression, integration, system, and end-to-end testing principles</li>
<li>Ability to debug automation failures and analyze application issues effectively</li>
<li>Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate with developers and product teams</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Even better if you have</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Exposure to accessibility testing and understanding of WCAG principles</li>
<li>Experience with visual regression or cross-browser testing</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud-native or SaaS-based platforms</li>
<li>Exposure to AI-assisted testing tools or testing AI-driven features</li>
<li>Experience contributing to automation dashboards or quality metrics</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You will thrive in this role if</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Believe quality is a shared responsibility across Engineering, QA, and Product</li>
<li>Prefer preventing defects through strong automation and release gates</li>
<li>Enjoy building systems that scale, rather than relying on manual effort</li>
<li>Comfortable enforcing 100% pass-rate mandates for critical workflows</li>
<li>Like working on complex systems involving CI/CD, containers, and AI-driven logic</li>
<li>Care deeply about reliability, trust, accessibility, and real-world user experience</li>
