<p><strong>Job Description:</strong></p>
<p>Responsible for research and coverage of high end investment products, this role ensures the firm maintains a live universe of curated investment ideas, supported by data, rationales, and manager intelligence.</p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Track new product launches across MF, PMS, AIFs, Structured Products.</li>
<li>Build and maintain a central repository of data, research, and collaterals.</li>
<li>Liaise with asset managers to form house views and provide inputs for product whitelisting.</li>
<li>Develop rationales for product approvals to the Investment Committee.</li>
<li>Maintain active touchpoints with fund managers, recording insights and updates.</li>
<li>Support Wealth Partners with product intelligence and joint calls.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Candidate Requirements:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>2-3 years in wealth/product research.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and financial writing skills.</li>
<li>Understanding of PMS, AIF, and structured products.</li>
<li>Relationship management capability with AMCs/fund houses.</li>
<li>2-3 years in wealth/product teams at private banks or AMCs</li>
</ul>
