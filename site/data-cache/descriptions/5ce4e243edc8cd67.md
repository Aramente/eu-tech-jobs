**About the Role**

We’re looking for a Principal Product Designer to lead design within CaptivateIQ’s Incentive Compensation Management (ICM) product line. This is a senior individual contributor role for a designer who thrives in complexity, owns large problem spaces end to end, and leads through influence, judgment, and craft—setting vision and shaping product direction across teams and the broader organization.

You’ll operate with high autonomy and partner closely with Product, Engineering, CX, and executive leadership to solve complex, high-impact problems and establish what great looks like for enterprise-scale systems.

## What You’ll Do

- Define and drive the design vision for a major product area, aligning closely with Product and Engineering leadership
- Lead and guide design across multiple teams, reviewing and approving work, and setting the bar for craft, systems thinking, and execution
- Identify and lead the most complex, high-impact initiatives across the product and organization, shaping new problems and opportunities that drive customer and business impact
- Design and evolve foundational systems, architectures, and reusable patterns, championing platform and systems thinking to ensure cohesion and quality
- Act as a force multiplier by mentoring designers and helping teams raise their execution bar over time
- Co-lead with senior Product and Engineering leaders to influence product strategy and prioritization
- Improve how teams work together, proactively identifying gaps in execution, collaboration, or quality and helping teams course-correct
- Stay current on industry trends and emerging technologies (including AI), using that insight to raise the bar for CaptivateIQ’s product experiences
- Communicate clearly at the org and company level, including writing crisp documents that articulate vision, direction, and tradeoffs
