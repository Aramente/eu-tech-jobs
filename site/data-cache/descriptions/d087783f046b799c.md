<h2><strong>Your Team</strong></h2>
<p>You’ll join our <strong>Finance &amp; ERP Engineering team</strong>, a core group responsible for building and maintaining the SAP backbone that powers SumUp’s financial, logistics, and compliance operations across multiple markets.</p>
<p>This team plays a critical role in some of SumUp’s most impactful initiatives — from <strong>electronic invoicing across EU countries</strong>, to <strong>field sales enablement</strong>, to <strong>automation of accounting and tax processes</strong> supporting SumUp’s IPO readiness.</p>
<p>As a Junior SAP Developer, you’ll work closely with experienced SAP Developers, functional experts, and business stakeholders. You’ll be supported to <strong>learn SumUp’s SAP landscape end-to-end</strong>, contribute to real production systems, and progressively grow your technical ownership while helping keep mission-critical finance systems running smoothly.</p>

<h2><strong>What You’ll Do 🛠️</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Learning &amp; Training (first months)</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Get hands-on onboarding into SumUp’s SAP landscape (S/4HANA, Fiori, CPI, BTP)</li>
<li>Learn our clean code principles, development standards, and delivery practices</li>
<li>Shadow senior developers to understand business processes across finance and logistics</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Developing &amp; Contributing</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain SAP programs, reports, and interfaces with guidance</li>
<li>Support testing, debugging, and quality assurance of SAP applications</li>
<li>Assist in building and extending SAP Fiori applications</li>
<li>Work on integrations with other systems (e.g. Kafka, Snowflake, Salesforce)</li>
<li>Document development work clearly and consistently</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Progressing Your Impact</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Take ownership of small features and enhancements over time</li>
<li>Contribute to sprint planning, reviews, and team ceremonies</li>
<li>Participate in code reviews and gradually contribute to technical discussions</li>
<li>Support incident resolution and continuous improvement of existing solutions</li>
</ul>
