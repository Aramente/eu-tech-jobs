**At Nord Security, we’re creating a safer cyber future.**

We help people and businesses take back control of their online security, privacy, and data. From VPNs to password managers, threat intelligence to eSIMs for travel—our teams turn complex problems into solutions trusted by millions worldwide.

Life is online. In this role, you’ll help people own it.

You will build and maintain the internal AI-powered tools that help our teams work smarter. From front-end interfaces to back-end services, you'll own the full development lifecycle - designing, shipping, and scaling applications that drive our transition to an AI-first company.

## Main Responsibilities

- Build & Ship AI Tools: Develop full-stack internal applications (front-end + back-end) that leverage AI - taking them from idea to production;
- Integrate with Enterprise Systems: Connect AI-powered tools to internal platforms and data sources (e.g., Jira, Confluence, Slack, BigQuery) through well-designed APIs;
- Own the Infrastructure: Deploy and manage applications using Docker, Kubernetes, and cloud services (AWS/Azure/GCP), ensuring they're reliable and scalable;
- Collaborate Across Teams: Work closely with stakeholders from different departments to understand their needs and translate them into practical, user-friendly tools;
- Maintain Quality & Security: Ensure applications follow security best practices, especially when handling sensitive data and AI pipelines.

## Core Requirements

- Full-Stack Development: Solid experience building web applications end-to-end (e.g., **Python**/Node.js + React/Vue );
- Cloud & DevOps: Comfortable with Docker, Kubernetes, and managing cloud resources (AWS, Azure, or GCP);
- API Development: Experience designing and integrating RESTful APIs to connect systems and services;
- Security Awareness: Understanding of secure coding practices, particularly around data handling;
- Self-Starter Mindset: High level of independence, ownership, and a can-do attitude;
- Strong Communication: Ability to work effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders in a fast-paced environment.

## Bonus Points For

- Experience working with AI/ML APIs or building AI-powered features;
- Familiarity with vector databases and observability tooling;
- Experience administering third-party SaaS platforms and managing access controls (SSO, permissions).
