Join us as a **Media Manager** to orchestrate Qonto's brand awareness campaigns across France, managing both offline media partnerships and internal online campaign execution. You'll play a critical role in building Qonto's presence as we scale toward serving one million SMEs by 2026.

As a Media Manager, you will work closely with **Marine Masson**, our Paid Marketing Lead, and collaborate with our media strategist and the broader acquisition team to create integrated media plans that drive brand awareness and consideration for Qonto's 600,000+ customers.

👩‍💻🧑‍💻 **As a Media Manager at Qonto, you will**

• Manage and execute offline media campaigns (TV, radio, outdoor advertising) in partnership with our external agency, ensuring alignment with brand objectives and budget optimization

• Create, launch, and optimize online campaigns across social media platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Meta, Google) to drive brand awareness and traffic

• Develop comprehensive media plans and budget allocation strategies, working closely with agencies and internal teams to maximize campaign impact

• Build and maintain strategic relationships with key media and agency partners

• Collaborate with the paid acquisition team to ensure seamless integration between upper-funnel brand campaigns and lower-funnel performance marketing efforts

🤔 **What you can expect**

• **Market Context**: You'll work in France's competitive fintech landscape, managing significant media budgets across both traditional and digital channels while Qonto continues its rapid growth trajectory

• **Team Collaboration**: Work in a collaborative and data-driven team

• **Methodologies**: Master strategic planning with operational excellence, you'll be hands-on with campaign creation while contributing to broader media strategy discussions

• **Tools**: Work with industry-standard platforms including Google Ads, Meta Business Manager, TikTok Ads Manager, plus internal analytics and planning tools

• **Measurement Focus**: Contribute to building Qonto's media measurement capabilities, tracking brand awareness, consideration metrics, and campaign performance across channels

🤝 **About your future manager**

Your manager will be [Marine Masson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/marinefmmasson/), our Lead Media & Paid Marketing.

• **Her path?** Marine scaled from digital marketing specialist to leading paid acquisition across Western Europe in under 8 years. She built and managed high-performing teams at ProntoPro and now oversees 8-digit budgets while launching new markets across Europe at Qonto.

• **What she can bring to the team?** Marine delivers results - she's launched 4 new markets, built teams from scratch, and mastered the art of scaling performance marketing internationally. Her hands-on approach combined with strategic vision means you'll get direct mentorship from someone who's done the operational work and now drives market expansion. She speaks 4 languages, understands European markets inside-out, and will push you to connect every media campaign to measurable business impact.

• **Media Campaign Experience**: You have **3+ years** managing both online and offline media campaigns, with hands-on experience creating and optimizing campaigns across social platforms and working with media agencies

• **Budget Management**: You've successfully managed significant media budgets with experience in budget allocation, planning, and optimization across multiple channels

• **Agency & Stakeholder Management**: You're comfortable building relationships with external agencies and media partners, with proven ability to coordinate complex campaigns across multiple stakeholders
