<p>While net new acquisition is your main objective, you will also be responsible for managing a book of existing SMB and Mid-Market customers. You’ll ensure they are realizing value from Amplitude, identify opportunities for expansion, and drive long-term retention.</p>
<p>This role is central to scaling Amplitude’s presence across the DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) market. It requires a hunter mindset paired with strong relationship-building skills, and the ability to navigate both short sales cycles and ongoing account growth. Monthly travel across the DACH region is expected to support both prospective and current customer relationships.</p>
<p>Travel is a key aspect of this role. You will be expected to travel on an ongoing, as-needed basis to meet with customers and prospects in person, strengthen relationships, accelerate deal cycles, and support expansion conversations. Regular attendance and participation at key regional industry events across the DACH ecosystem is also an important expectation.</p>
