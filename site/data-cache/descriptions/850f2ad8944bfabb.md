<h2><strong>What You’ll Be Doing</strong></h2>
<p>You’ll be joining the Global Business and Corporate Development team. We are a small group of experienced deal professionals responsible for DoorDash’s most complex partnerships across DoorDash, Wolt, and Deliveroo, architecting bespoke mechanics where conventional playbooks fall short.</p>
<p>Here’s what your day-to-day might look like:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Originate Commercial Theses</strong>: Partner with Wolt leadership to diagnose growth constraints and develop the strategies required to unlock incremental impact through partnerships.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Build from First Principles:</strong> Structure "first-of-kind" commercial partnerships, creating original deal mechanics for ambiguous opportunities that don't fit standard templates.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Lead High-Stakes Negotiations</strong>: Act as the primary representative for Wolt with senior counterparts at global platforms and significant local market players.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Align Executive Leadership:</strong> Develop trusted relationships across Product, Finance, and Marketing to secure the internal resourcing and commitment needed to bring complex deals to life.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Own the Deal Trajectory: </strong>Work in lockstep with Legal to refine and close agreements, ensuring terms serve as a de-risked roadmap for long-term execution and measurable impact.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What You’ll Need to Thrive</h2>
<p>Our ideal candidate will bring strong expertise in some of these areas and curiosity to grow in others:</p>
<ul>
<li>Significant Experience in Complex Dealmaking: Proven ability to close non-standard, high-impact deals within digital platforms, consumer marketplaces, or financial services.</li>
<li>Elite Commercial Judgement: A track record of converting ambiguity into clear commercial models that both executives and technical teams can rally behind.</li>
<li>Mastery of Negotiation: Seasoned experience driving the full deal process from ideation to execution, knowing when to lean in and when to walk away from deals that won't move the needle.</li>
<li>Strategic Stakeholder Management: The ability to navigate sophisticated, multi-faceted organisations and align disparate internal views into a single, unified thesis.</li>
<li>Executive Presence: Experience engaging directly with C-suite counterparts at global brands, driving results through high-level pattern recognition and relationship building.</li>
</ul>
