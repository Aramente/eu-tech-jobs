As we expand our offering, we are investing in new capabilities that combine real-time data, scalable systems, and intuitive user experiences to unlock the next phase of growth.</p>
<p>We’re looking for a <strong>Staff Engineer</strong> to join the Wolt AdTech &amp; Promotions organization, who can drive technical direction across this space and help us build reliable, scalable systems that directly impact revenue and advertiser success.</p>
<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<ul>
<li>Drive the technical vision and architecture for Wolt’s AdTech and Promotions platforms, ensuring they are scalable, reliable, and high-performing.</li>
<li>Provide technical leadership across multiple teams and projects, bringing architectural consistency to complex, large-scale systems.</li>
<li>Work across the <strong>end-to-end domain</strong>, contributing to backend services, data pipelines, and advertiser-facing tools.</li>
<li>Design and evolve systems that support <strong>campaign execution, targeting, ranking, allocation, and reporting</strong> in real-time or near real-time environments.</li>
<li>Partner with engineering, product, and data stakeholders to align priorities, improve campaign performance, and manage trade-offs in a revenue-driven environment.</li>
<li>Lead large cross-team projects, guiding them from concept to rollout across multiple regions and time zones.</li>
<li>Ensure strong operational excellence, including observability, reliability, and incident readiness for revenue-critical systems.</li>
<li>Promote best engineering practices, mentor engineers across teams, and support their growth in scalability and system design.</li>
<li>Act as a bridge between technical and non-technical colleagues, ensuring clarity and alignment across functions.</li>
<li>Contribute to the technical roadmaps of AdTech and Promotions platforms by collaborating with other senior engineers.</li>
</ul>
<p>This position is based in <strong>London</strong>&nbsp;</p>

<li>Experience working with <strong>data-intensive and real-time or near real-time systems</strong>.</li>

<p>You will play a key role in <strong>building and evolving the next generation of advertisement tools and platforms</strong>, shaping how advertisers create, manage, and optimize campaigns across Wolt. This is not just about maintaining systems, but about <strong>defining and scaling new capabilities in a fast-growing domain</strong>.</p>
