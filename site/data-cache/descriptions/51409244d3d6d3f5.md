<h3><strong>About Yugabyte</strong></h3>
<p>At <strong>Yugabyte</strong>, we are on a mission to become the default <strong>transactional database</strong> for enterprises building cloud-native applications. YugabyteDB is our PostgreSQL compatible distributed database for cloud-native apps. Resilient, scalable, and flexible, it runs on any cloud and enables developers to become instantly productive using well-known APIs.We are looking for talented and driven people to join us on our ambitious mission and help us build a lasting and impactful company.The transactional database market is estimated to grow to $150B by 2030. YugabyteDB is cloud-native by design, has on-demand horizontal scalability, and supports geographical distribution of data using built-in replication. This means that we are well-positioned to meet market demand for geo-distributed, high-scale, high-performance workloads.</p>
<p>Yugabyte offers a culture of <strong>innovation, ownership, and technical excellence</strong>, with opportunities to impact a cutting-edge distributed SQL database used by global enterprises. As a member of the DST team, you’ll help meet growing demand for high-scale, high-performance, globally distributed transactional workloads — instrumental to our product’s success.</p>
<h2><strong>Role Overview</strong></h2>
<p>As a <strong>Staff Engineer on the Distributed Storage &amp; Transactions (DST) team</strong>, you will play a critical role in designing, building, and scaling the <strong>core distributed storage, replication, and transaction foundations</strong> of YugabyteDB.</p>
<p>You will work on some of the most challenging problems in distributed systems — including consistency, durability, fault tolerance, performance, and scalability — while collaborating closely with query layer, platform, and cloud teams. This role is ideal for engineers who thrive in low-level systems work and want to shape the future of a globally distributed database.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead the <strong>design, development, testing, and delivery</strong> of core storage and replication features in YugabyteDB.</li>
<li>Write <strong>high-quality C/C++ code</strong> with comprehensive automated tests; actively participate in design discussions and code reviews.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve <strong>correctness, stability, and performance issues</strong> in complex distributed storage and transactional subsystems.</li>
<li>Improve <strong>database scalability and throughput</strong> as cluster sizes, data volumes, and transaction rates continue to grow.</li>
<li>Build and streamline <strong>database management operations</strong>, including:</li>
<ul>
<li>Horizontal cluster scale-out</li>
<li>Incremental and point-in-time backups</li>
<li>Online schema and index operations</li>
<li>Rolling upgrades and blue-green deployments</li>
</ul>
<li>Identify and implement <strong>performance improvements</strong> across the storage engine, transaction processing, and replication layers.</li>
<li>Contribute to the <strong>open-source YugabyteDB project</strong>, helping evolve its storage architecture and operational reliability.</li>
<li>Mentor and technically influence other engineers in distributed systems design, performance engineering, and systems-level debugging.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Required Qualifications</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>8+ years of professional software engineering experience</strong>, with a strong foundation in <strong>systems programming</strong> using <strong>C/C++</strong>.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s, Master’s, or PhD in <strong>Computer Science</strong> (or related field), or equivalent practical experience.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of <strong>distributed systems fundamentals</strong>, including:</li>
<ul>
<li>Replication and consensus</li>
<li>Transactions and consistency models</li>
<li>Fault tolerance and recovery</li>
</ul>
<li>Experience working on <strong>storage engines, databases, or other infrastructure-level systems</strong>.</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to operate effectively in a collaborative, distributed team environment</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Preferred Skills</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience with <strong>distributed storage systems</strong>, transactional engines, or consensus protocols.</li>
<li>Familiarity with <strong>LSM-tree based storage engines</strong>, WALs, snapshots, or compaction strategies.</li>
<li>Experience with <strong>PostgreSQL internals</strong> or other relational database engines is a strong plus.</li>
<li>Prior contributions to <strong>open-source systems or database projects</strong>.</li>
