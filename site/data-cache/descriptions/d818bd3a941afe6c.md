## Your mission

<span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-size:14px;font-family:Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">This is a leadership role in KAYA which heads the Climate Department consisting of seven specialised staff members. The responsible colleague will lead the conceptualisation and design of a portfolio of largescale nature conservation and landscape restoration projects in forest, grassland and arable ecosystems in tropical and sub-tropical climatic regions. They will be accountable for the climate integrity and process adherence in the development of the projects. They will report to the Chief Executive Officer and, together with the Head of Finance, Head of Legal, Head of HR and Head of Operations, be an active contributor to the development of the organisation and the business. Extensive travel will be an integral part of this role.</span>
