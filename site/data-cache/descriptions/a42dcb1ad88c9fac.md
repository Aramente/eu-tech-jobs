<h3><strong>About the role</strong></h3>
<p>As an IT Engineer, you will be working across identity, devices, SaaS platforms, and supporting end users. You should be organized, collaborative, and eager to learn while working within established systems and processes.</p>
<p>Remote Working 2-3 days a week in the office Amsterdam.</p>
<h3><strong>Responsibilities&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Provide reliable, hands-on IT support for our in-office and remote employees by efficiently resolving Help Desk tickets while delivering excellent customer service.</li>
<li>Support Together’s MDM fleet comprising macOS and some Linux devices.</li>
<li>Provide in-office support by troubleshooting basic network issues and our A/V systems.</li>
<li>Help manage and optimize Okta and our core SaaS tooling by administering and improving integrations and role-based access controls.</li>
<li>Support daily procurement and asset management operations by coordinating hardware purchasing and tracking while maintaining accurate asset inventory and device assignments throughout their lifecycle (purchase to decommission).</li>
<li>Help maintain clear, organized, and accessible IT documentation by creating easy-to-understand technical documentation for both technical and non-technical audiences.</li>
<li>Contribute to IT initiatives by supporting project execution, managing priorities, and ensuring clear communication with stakeholders through regular updates.</li>
<li>Help maintain a secure environment by supporting basic security hygiene to include access reviews and basic identity security tasks.</li>
<li>Support onboarding and offboarding of Together employees, interns, and contractors.</li>
<li>Support the IT team by creating simple, reliable automations that reduce manual work and improve operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Works well with a team, but also able to work autonomously when needed: working in tandem with other locations and having consistency in practices, documentation, asset mgmt.,etc.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Requirements </strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Practical experience administering Okta, including users, groups, and basic app integrations (Terraform a plus).</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Google Workspace.</li>
<li>Hands-on MDM experience with macOS, ideally with FleetDM.</li>
<li>Ability to support macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android devices.</li>
<li>Familiarity with Notion and Linear for documentation and project management.</li>
<li>Experience participating in procurement and asset management processes.</li>
<li>Clear, concise technical writing skills for documentation and knowledge base content.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and customer service abilities.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of security principles and best practices.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice To Haves</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience with low‑code automation tools (Serval, Okta Workflows, Gumloop).</li>
<li>Scripting familiarity with common languages (Bash, Python).</li>
<li>Exposure to security and compliance frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST, CIS, FedRAMP).</li>
<li>Experience with AI tools, agents, MCPs, automations, and prompting.&nbsp;</li>
<li>HRIS integration experience with systems like HiBob and Workday.</li>
<li>Understanding of API integrations and webhooks and how to use them effectively.</li>
</ul>
