<h2><strong>General Ledger Accountant Internship</strong></h2>
<p>Are you ready to kickstart your career in finance and accounting within the exciting world of FinTech?</p>
<p>Join our team as a motivated and enthusiastic <strong>General Ledger Accountant Intern</strong> and gain hands-on experience supporting procure-to-pay and core accounting processes. At SumUp, our cutting-edge technology solutions are transforming how businesses manage their finances—and we’re looking for future talent ready to grow with us.</p>
<h2><strong>The Role</strong></h2>
<p>This is a <strong>6-month paid internship</strong>, ideally <strong>full-time (40 hours per week, Monday to Friday)</strong>, and <strong>office-based in Sofia</strong>. We’re looking for interns who can be fully present and engaged with the team on-site.</p>
<p>There is <strong>potential to extend the internship for an additional 6 months</strong>, and importantly, <strong>interns graduating this year may be offered a permanent contract</strong> based on performance and business needs. Our goal is to invest in talent who want to grow long-term with us.</p>
<p>While the role is full-time, we are open to considering <strong>a 6-hour workday arrangement</strong> in certain cases.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Participate in automation and new business projects, including automation of intercompany invoicing</li>
<li>Support team members with document management activities</li>
<li>Verify and process inventory-related and fixed asset vendor invoices</li>
<li>Issue and book intercompany invoices</li>
<li>Perform calculations in Excel under the guidance of senior team members</li>
<li>Maintain strong relationships with internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Provide supporting documentation and reports for audits</li>
<li>Prepare regular and ad-hoc reports to support business needs</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>You’d Be Great for This Role If You:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Are a <strong>final-year student</strong>, ideally graduating this year, in Accounting, Finance, or a related field</li>
<li>Are fluent in <strong>English and Bulgarian</strong> (written and spoken)</li>
<li>Have strong knowledge of <strong>MS Office</strong>, especially Excel</li>
<li>Have previous <strong>ERP experience</strong> (a plus)</li>
<li>Demonstrate high attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Have strong communication and interpersonal skills</li>

<li>Have the <strong>right to work as an intern in Bulgaria</strong> and are able to <strong>work on-site in Sofia daily</strong></li>
</ul>
