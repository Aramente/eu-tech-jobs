**At Nord Security, we’re creating a safer cyber future.**

We help people and businesses take back control of their online security, privacy, and data. From VPNs to password managers, threat intelligence to eSIMs for travel—our teams turn complex problems into solutions trusted by millions worldwide.

Life is online. In this role, you’ll help people own it.

**Our Legal team** is a team of attentive professionals who deal with a variety of issues, including privacy, intellectual property, employment, compliance, mergers, and acquisitions. As legal business partners, we ensure the quality and efficiency of the organization.

## Main Responsibilities

- Draft, review, and negotiate a broad range of commercial agreements, including:
- Sales and distribution agreements
- Marketing and advertising agreements
- Supplier and procurement contracts
- Online tools, software, and IT systems agreements (SaaS, data processing, service level agreements, etc.)
- Support the procurement lifecycle, including vendor onboarding, compliance checks, and process documentation
- Collaborate with marketing and procurement teams to drive process improvements, develop contract templates, and implement best practices
- Advise internal teams on contractual rights, obligations, and risk mitigation strategies
- Provide risk-balanced legal guidance to support strategic decision-making
- Ensure compliance with internal policies, corporate governance standards, and applicable international legal and regulatory frameworks.

## Core Requirements

- Master’s degree in law
- 2+ years of experience in commercial/contract law, ideally including both law firm and in-house roles
- Demonstrated experience drafting and negotiating complex commercial agreements in an international environment
- Solid understanding of digital/IT contracting, data protection considerations, and procurement processes
- Practical, solution-oriented mindset with strong business acumen and ability to balance legal risk with commercial priorities
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced, cross-functional, multicultural environment
- Fluent in English (additional languages a plus).

## Salary Range

- Gross Salary 2200 - 5200 EUR/Month.
