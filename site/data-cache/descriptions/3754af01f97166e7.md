About the role:

As a **Photo Quality Reviewer** on this team, you will be **responsible for ensuring that every photo delivered to our customers meets Snappr's high standards of quality and creativity**. Your keen eye for detail and understanding of visual aesthetics will directly impact the customer experience and contribute to Snappr's rapid growth.

## What you'll do:

- Review and assess photos submitted by photographers to ensure they meet Snappr's quality standards
- Provide constructive feedback to photographers to help them improve their work
- Collaborate with the editing team to ensure consistency and quality across all visual content
- Monitor and maintain quality metrics, identifying areas for improvement
- Assist in developing and updating quality guidelines and best practices
- Work closely with other departments to ensure seamless operations and high customer satisfaction
- Hustle - take ownership of Snappr's growth and do whatever it takes to get the job done

## What you'll need:

- 2-3 years of experience in photography, photo editing, or quality assurance roles
- Strong attention to detail and a good eye for visual aesthetics
- Ability to provide constructive feedback in a clear and professional manner
- Experience with Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop is a plus
- Fluency in written and spoken English
- A "let's get it done" mindset
- Should be willing to report 100% onsite in Ortigas.
