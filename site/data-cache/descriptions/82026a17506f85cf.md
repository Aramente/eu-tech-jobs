<p>We are seeking a dedicated SOC Analyst to join our dynamic security team. The SOC Analyst will be responsible for monitoring, detecting, and responding to security incidents across our corporate infrastructure, SaaS platforms, data centers, and customer services. This role involves close collaboration with our MSSP partners to maintain a robust security posture as well as working with Product, R&amp;D, IT and the different Datacentres.</p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Security Monitoring: Continuously monitor security alerts and events across various platforms, including Office 365, corporate SaaS applications, and data centers.</li>
<li>Incident Detection and Response: Identify potential security incidents, conduct initial triage, and coordinate with internal teams and MSSPs for effective response.</li>
<li>Threat Analysis: Analyze security threats and vulnerabilities to assess potential impact on the organization.</li>
<li>Collaboration: Work closely with MSSP partners and internal stakeholders to enhance detection capabilities and improve incident response processes.</li>
<li>Documentation: Maintain detailed records of security incidents, including analysis, response actions, and lessons learned.</li>
<li>Continuous Improvement: Participate in the development and refinement of SOC processes, playbooks, and procedures to enhance operational efficiency.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Education: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Security, or a related field.</li>
<li>Experience: Minimum of 2 years of experience in a SOC or similar security-focused environment.</li>
<li>Technical Skills:</li>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in using Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tools.</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud security principles and experience with platforms such as Office 365 and other SaaS applications.</li>
<li>Understanding of network protocols, intrusion detection systems, and incident response methodologies.</li>
</ul>
<li>Certifications: Relevant certifications such as CompTIA Security+, Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), or Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) are preferred.</li>
</ul>
