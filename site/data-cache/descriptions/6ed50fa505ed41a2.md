<h3>Role Summary</h3>
<p>The Senior Strategy &amp; Operations Lead is a high-impact partner to IT leadership, responsible for driving strategic alignment, operational excellence, and execution rigor across all IT pillars. This role leads IT-wide planning, governance, and communications to ensure the CIO’s priorities translate into measurable business outcomes.</p>
<p>We are looking to speak to candidates who are based in Gurugram for our hybrid working model.</p>
<h3>Key Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead IT Strategic Planning &amp; Governance<strong><br></strong>- Drives the definition of IT OKRs, quarterly business reviews, and multi-year strategic planning cadences to align priorities and track value delivery</li>
<li>Drive Executive Decision Enablement<strong><br></strong>- Build insights, narratives, and recommendations that inform CIO/leadership decisions, including board materials and enterprise updates</li>
<li>Orchestrate Cross-Functional Initiatives<strong><br></strong>- Coordinate strategic programs that span pillars, ensuring ownership clarity, risk management, and measurable outcomes</li>
<li>Evolve IT Operating Model<strong><br></strong>- Deploy “run IT like a product” standards across agile delivery, resource planning, intake, and change management</li>
<li>Champion Operational Excellence<strong><br></strong>- Standardize ways of working, reporting frameworks, and collaboration mechanisms across IT</li>
</ul>
<h3>Qualifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>8–10 years in technology strategy, PMO leadership, or chief-of-staff–style roles, ideally within a CIO’s office</li>
<li>Experience partnering with senior executives and driving cross-functional impact</li>
<li>Strong strategic thinking, communication, and organizational leadership skills</li>
<li>Familiarity with product-centric delivery, agile, and enterprise planning tooling</li>
</ul>
