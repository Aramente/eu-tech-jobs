<h2>An overview of this role</h2>
<p>As an <strong><strong class="textBold">Ecosystem Sales Manager</strong></strong> on GitLab's Ecosystem team, you'll help grow and strengthen the partner relationships that expand GitLab's reach across the <strong>Alps<strong class="textBold">&nbsp;region</strong></strong>. In this role, you'll work with strategic partners and GitLab's field sales organization to build plans that advance pipeline growth, revenue generation, and long-term partner success. You'll engage across both pre-sales and post-sales motions, helping connect partners to the right opportunities and position them to help customers as their needs become more complex. This is a highly collaborative role with meaningful visibility, and we'll rely on you to work with internal stakeholders and external partners to shape territory strategy, drive execution, and improve partner performance in a remote, values-based environment.</p>
<p>Some examples of our projects:</p>
<ul>
<li value="1">Building and managing strategic relationships with <strong><strong class="textBold">system integrators, solution providers, managed services partners, and hyperscalers</strong></strong> such as AWS and Google across The Alps</li>
<li value="2">Designing joint business plans with partners that include account mapping, go-to-market activities, governance, and measurable goals for pipeline and revenue growth</li>
</ul>
<h2>What you’ll do</h2>
<ul>
<li value="1">Lead strategic partner initiatives that support long-term ecosystem growth across your assigned Alps territory.</li>
<li value="2">Build, maintain, and manage strong relationships with GitLab's field sales organization, including proactive engagement with account executives, area sales managers, and geographic leadership.</li>
<li value="3">Work closely with regional partners, including <strong><strong class="textBold">system integrators, solution providers, managed services partners, and hyperscalers</strong></strong>, to support customer success and sales objectives.</li>
<li value="4">Design and execute comprehensive joint business plans with partners, including account mapping, go-to-market strategies, and governance models.</li>
<li value="5">Coordinate and facilitate involvement from GitLab team members such as sales leadership, support, and other cross-functional stakeholders as needed to help meet sales targets and ecosystem goals.</li>
<li value="6">Identify and support territory-specific demand generation and pipeline-building activities with strategic partners.</li>
<li value="7">Contribute to <strong><strong class="textBold">quarterly business reviews</strong></strong> and annual planning activities within the Ecosystem organization.</li>
<li value="8">Prepare forecasts, progress updates, presentations, territory plans, and reports that help track partner performance and inform decision-making.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What you’ll bring</h2>
<ul>
<li value="1">Experience selling <strong><strong class="textBold">software development tools</strong></strong>, application lifecycle management solutions, or related technology through strategic partnerships.</li>
<li value="2">Strong knowledge of the <strong><strong class="textBold">partner ecosystem in the Alps region</strong></strong>, with an established network and understanding of regional market dynamics.</li>
<li value="3">Expertise in cloud and partner motions, with the ability to work effectively with a range of ecosystem partners and align them to customer and sales needs.</li>
<li value="4">Experience with <strong><strong class="textBold">business-to-business sales</strong></strong> and a results-oriented approach to partner development, pipeline creation, and revenue growth.</li>
<li value="5">Interest in GitLab, open source software, and the role partners play in helping customers adopt and expand their use of the platform.</li>
<li value="6">Effective written and verbal communication skills in <strong><strong class="textBold">English</strong></strong>, with the ability to present clearly and build trust across internal and external audiences.</li>
<li value="7">Ability to build productive working relationships with internal and external stakeholders, make informed decisions, and effectively manage multiple competing priorities in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li value="8">Proficiency with <strong><strong class="textBold">Salesforce</strong></strong> and GitLab, along with willingness and ability to travel up to <strong><strong class="textBold">50%</strong></strong> in line with company policy.</li>
</ul>
<h2>About the team</h2>
<p>On the Ecosystem team, we work to make partners a meaningful part of how GitLab grows, serves customers, and scales across markets. We partner closely with Sales and other cross-functional leaders to engage the right partners throughout the sales cycle, from early opportunity development through ongoing customer growth. You'll join a team that values strategic thinking, strong execution, and clear accountability, with a focus on building partner capability and capacity in regions where customer needs are becoming more advanced. In the Alps, that means working across a diverse landscape of partners and cloud relationships while helping us create durable, high-impact routes to market through the ecosystem.</p>
