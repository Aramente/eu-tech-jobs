<p><strong>The job</strong></p></div><p>Our Vendor Management department is all about the end-to-end procurement process, including P2P, sourcing, negotiations, contracting, supplier relationship management, and business partnerships with other departments. <p>Our Senior Procurement Manager will be responsible for the end-to-end procurement process with a good balance of operational and business partnership responsibilities.&nbsp;</p>

<p>As part of our team of 6, you’d be working with people who are experts in their area and are continuously looking for innovations, process improvements, and simplifications to maintain a high-performing Vendor Management function within the organisation.</p>
