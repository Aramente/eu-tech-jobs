<p class="p1">The Experience Specialist (Guest Services) On-Call is a key ambassador of the Sensei guest journey, responsible for delivering&nbsp;seamless, personalized service from pre-arrival through departure. This role manages daily guest flow, welcomes and checks in guests, supports itinerary building, and executes real-time changes with precision&nbsp;and care. Working cross-functionally with all departments, the Experience Specialist (Guest Services) On-Call ensures accurate&nbsp;scheduling, clear communication, and proactive problem-solving to create a smooth and elevated&nbsp;experience for every guest. By anticipating needs and supporting both guests and internal teams, this&nbsp;position plays a vital role in creating a smooth, thoughtful, and elevated wellness experience for every&nbsp;guest.</p>
<p class="p1"><strong>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following:</strong></p>
<p class="p1"><strong>On Property Guest Support&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1"><strong>Primary On-Property Guest Contact </strong>– Serves as the dedicated point of contact for guests&nbsp;throughout their stay, delivering warm, personalized service from check-in to departure. Manages&nbsp;real-time itinerary adjustments, fulfills special requests, and provides thoughtful&nbsp;recommendations on dining, activities, and resort offerings. Receives guest handoff from the Pre-Arrival Team 72 hours prior to arrival to ensure a seamless experience and proactively resolves&nbsp;issues to uphold the highest standards of service.</li>
<li class="p1"><strong>Guest Welcome &amp; Check-In </strong>– Welcome guests with warmth and professionalism, executing seamless check-in and check-out of appointments in accordance with established protocols and standards.</li>
<li class="p1"><strong>Itinerary Management </strong>– Build, update, and fine-tune guest itineraries throughout their stay, ensuring alignment with their state intentions, preferences, and adherence to established protocols and standards.</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1"><strong>Room Only &amp; External Wellness Requests</strong></p>
<ul>
<li class="p1"><strong>Room Only Booking Requests (LEP)</strong> – Manage wellness service booking requests from Four Seasons Lāna’i Experiences Planners (LEP) for Sensei room-only guests. <li class="p1"><strong>Manele VIP Booking Requests</strong> – Manage wellness service booking requests from Four Seasons Executive Assistants for Four Seasons Manele Beach Resort VIP guests. <li class="p1"><strong>Island Club Booking Requests </strong>– Manage wellness service booking requests from Island Club Members. <li class="p1"><strong>Internal Space Reservation Requests</strong> – Manage internal wellness space reservation requests. Confirm bookings are handled promptly and in compliance with established scheduling protocols.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Living and Working on the Island of Lāna</strong><strong>ʻ</strong><strong>i</strong></p>
<p>Lānaʻi is the third-smallest island in the Hawaiian Islands and home to a close-knit family-orientated community of approximately 3,300 residents contributing to its strong sense of community and local pride.&nbsp; 98% of the island is owned by Oracle founder and Sensei Wellness co-founder Larry Ellison, who also owns both on-island Four Seasons resorts located just under 9 miles from each other and the average speed limit in the city is 20 mph. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Lanai is home to one K-12 school, a gas station, a few owner-operated eateries and sundries stores, a two-screen movie theatre, and the newly renovated Lāna’i City Bowling Alley.</p>
