<p>The IT SaaS Engineering Team plays a critical role in optimizing MongoDB sales processes, streamlining customer interactions, and maximizing the efficiency of our sales efforts. <h3>Role Overview</h3>
<p>We are looking for a highly capable <strong>Senior Salesforce Engineer</strong> to design, build, and maintain scalable CRM solutions that support MongoDB’s Sales organization. This role combines strong hands-on <strong>Salesforce development</strong>, <strong>solution design</strong>, <strong>cross-functional collaboration</strong>, and a modern engineering mindset to deliver reliable, maintainable, and high-quality solutions across the core Salesforce platform.</p>

<li>Own delivery across the full <strong>solution lifecycle</strong>, including design, development, testing, deployment, documentation, and production support</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with <strong>globally distributed engineering teams</strong> to deliver <strong>reliable</strong>, <strong>secure</strong>, and <strong>well-governed solutions</strong> in an Agile environment</li>
<li>Drive strong <strong>engineering practices</strong> across <strong>code quality</strong>, <strong>documentation</strong>, <strong>release management</strong>, and <strong>platform governance</strong></li>
