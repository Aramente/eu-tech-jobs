Our **PR&D team** is looking for a **Software (Cocoa) Engineer** to join small, high-speed unit. This is a unique opportunity for someone who wants to move fast, experiment with AI, and see their ideas become real products in days, not months.

We focus on fast, AI-first development of useful, single-usecase apps. We work from a backlog of ideas prioritized with the team and execs. We take an idea, make it real fast enough to test, and then decide if it lives or die. Most won’t. Some might.

First tasks: working on a Text-to-Speech app, a Multi-agent chat client, and supporting Refolder.
