<h3><strong><span data-ccp-props="{}">The role</span></strong></h3>
<p><span data-ccp-props="{}">Nebius is looking for a <strong>Senior Salesforce Administrator. </strong></span>As a Senior Salesforce Administrator in our hyper-growth environment, you will play a foundational role in building and scaling our CRM ecosystem from day one. This is a position where you will not only configure and manage Salesforce, but also analyze business processes, design intelligent workflows, and apply data-driven insights to help teams move faster.</p>
<p>You will partner with Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Product, and Engineering to architect a next-generation CRM platform that supports rapid scaling, automation-first operations, and AI-driven decision-making. This is an opportunity to shape the backbone systems of a fast-growing AI company—creating a CRM foundation that is flexible, automated, and built for high-velocity go-to-market teams.</p>
<p><span data-ccp-props="{}">You’re welcome to&nbsp;<strong>work remote from India</strong>.</span></p>
<p><strong><span data-contrast="auto"><span data-ccp-charstyle="Strong">Your responsibilities will include:</span></span></strong></p>
<p><strong>Administration, Configuration &amp; Automation</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own day-to-day Salesforce administration, including user access, permissions, security controls, page layouts, objects, record types, and data governance.</li>
<li>Build scalable automation using automation to replace manual processes and enable operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Work with stakeholders to map business processes, identify gaps, and design technical solutions aligned with the needs of a hyper-growth GTM engine.</li>
<li>Translate business requirements into clear user stories, functional specs, and structured workflows.</li>
<li>Analyse CRM usage patterns and funnel metrics to recommend operational and process improvements.</li>
<li>Use Salesforce reporting and dashboards to surface insights that help leaders make data-backed decisions.</li>
<li>Maintain documentation of data models, configurations, integrations, and process maps.</li>
<li>Collaborate with GTM teams to build lead management flows, sales processes, customer lifecycle models, and AI-powered workflow automations.</li>
<li>Deliver training, onboarding, and ongoing support to ensure strong user adoption across fast-scaling teams.</li>
<li>Manage data imports/updates, deduplication, and data cleansing initiatives.</li>
<li>Support integration efforts with GTM systems, marketing automation platforms, billing systems, and internal tools.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot issues proactively and act as the go-to expert for platform reliability and performance.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span data-contrast="auto"><span data-ccp-charstyle="Strong">We expect you to have:</span></span></strong><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:true,&quot;134233118&quot;:true}">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li>4–7+ years in Salesforce Administration with Enterprise CRM experience.</li>
<li>Strong expertise in Salesforce configuration, automation (Flows), data modelling, and security.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience acting in requirements gathering, process design, workflow mapping, and reporting.</li>
<li>Experience supporting high-velocity GTM teams (Sales, Marketing, Customer Success).</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to translate raw CRM data into insights and actionable recommendations.</li>
<li>Familiarity with integrations, connected apps, APIs, and middleware (no coding required, but technical comfort is essential).</li>
<li>Experience with lead routing, funnel analytics, lifecycle automation, or revenue operations tooling is highly valued.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills with the ability to influence stakeholders in a fast-changing, high-pressure environment.</li>
<li>Salesforce certifications such as Admin, Advanced Admin, or Platform App Builder preferred.</li>
</ul>
