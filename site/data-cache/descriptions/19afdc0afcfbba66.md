<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<ul>
<li>Successfully onboarding and training venues together with Account Managers, providing them with all the tools and information to start working as soon as possible. Hired candidates will first learn the basics of onboarding new partners to our platform, which will take approximately one month.</li>
<li>Driving and managing Month over Month operations excellence metrics, growth of our current venues and improving venue performance by measuring critical KPIs, strong analysis, and frequent quality and satisfaction checks.</li>
<li>Owning the partner's assortment - suggesting which products should be listed initially, and working with the partner to constantly add/remove items to improve customer conversion.</li>
<li>Working with the partner to improve overall sales and conversion rates by sharing tools and best practices to help ensure the partner's success.</li>
<li>Getting feedback from our partners and translating it into product, marketing and operations improvement</li>
<li>Key communicator between Wolt and Restaurant partners.</li>
<li>Working closely together with the marketing team to plan and execute campaigns together with Restaurant partners</li>
</ul>
