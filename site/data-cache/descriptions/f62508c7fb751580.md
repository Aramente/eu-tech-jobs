<p>Location: Australia (required) | Covering Australia, Singapore, India</p>
<p><strong>Your opportunity</strong></p>
<p>New Relic is building a new Commercial Sales motion across APJ focused on driving expansion and renewal within our install base ($25K–$100K segment).</p>
<p>This is a builder-first leadership role. You will inherit a small team (3 reps on day one) and be responsible for scaling it based on performance, while defining how we operate across a distributed region.</p>
<p>You will lead a team that fully owns the customer lifecycle—from renewal through expansion—operating in a high-velocity environment while supporting more complex opportunities within existing accounts.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and scale a distributed Commercial Sales team across Australia, Singapore, and India</li>
<li>Own a team quota across renewal and expansion revenue</li>
<li>Operate as a player-coach, stepping into deals and setting execution standards</li>
<li>Drive full-cycle ownership across the install base, including renewals and expansion</li>
<li>Establish operating rigor across pipeline management, forecasting, and deal inspection</li>
<li>Build and refine the commercial playbook for install-base growth (segmentation, prioritization, engagement)</li>
<li>Balance high-velocity execution with support for complex, multi-threaded deals</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally with PLG, Marketing, Customer Success, and Sales Engineering</li>
<li>Hire, onboard, and develop talent as the team scales</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>This role requires</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience leading commercial or mid-market SaaS sales teams</li>
<li>Proven success in full-cycle sales environments, including renewals and expansion ownership</li>
<li>Experience operating in high-velocity sales motions with strong pipeline discipline</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to build and scale teams in ambiguous or early-stage environments</li>
<li>Experience leading distributed teams across multiple geographies</li>
<li>Strong operator mindset with the ability to build structure and drive execution</li>
<li>Ability to balance hands-on deal involvement with team leadership</li>
<li>Strong cross-functional collaboration skills</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What will make you successful</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Builder-first mindset, especially in the first 12 months</li>
<li>Bias for action and ability to create structure where it doesn’t exist</li>
<li>Ability to operate across both high-velocity and complex deal environments</li>
<li>High standards for accountability and performance</li>
<li>Comfort owning a number across both retention and growth</li>
</ul>
