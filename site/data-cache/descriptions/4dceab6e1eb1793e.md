**At Nord Security’s B2B team, we build cybersecurity products that enable businesses to protect themselves and their data.**

From product and engineering to sales, marketing, and data — our teams work closely together to deliver secure, scalable solutions trusted by companies around the world.

**Your impact?** Empowering businesses everywhere to keep their people, systems, and data safe.

**[Key qualities and behaviors of a successful leader at Nord Security](https://nordsecurity.com/leadership)**

## Main Responsibilities

- Set the product marketing strategy across a multi-product B2B portfolio in the cybersecurity space
- Define the positioning and messaging framework for the portfolio — establishing the standard for how each product, segment, and persona is addressed across all market-facing surfaces
- Lead go-to-market planning across the team, ensuring launches are well-orchestrated, cross-functional, and tied to measurable business outcomes
- Build and own the sales enablement strategy — defining what the sales team needs to win and ensuring the team delivers it at a high standard
- Establish a competitive intelligence function that systematically informs positioning and product roadmap decisions across the portfolio
- Lead, develop, and grow a multi-disciplinary team — setting direction, raising the bar, and building a culture of strategic rigor and accountability
- Work closely with the Head of Marketing and senior leadership to align product marketing priorities with broader business goals

## Core Requirements

- 5+ years of experience in B2B product marketing, with at least 2 years in a senior leadership role managing multi-disciplinary teams
- Proven track record of building and scaling a product marketing function across a multi-product portfolio — not just a single product line
- Experience in cybersecurity, SaaS, or a technical B2B product category; a deep understanding of how security and IT buyers evaluate and purchase
- Strong strategic instincts on positioning, messaging, and category narrative — with the credibility to drive alignment across Product, Sales, and Marketing leadership
- Demonstrated ability to translate market, competitive, and customer insight into positioning and GTM decisions that move the business forward
- Skilled at leading cross-functional initiatives and influencing senior stakeholders without direct authority
- Analytical and outcome-driven — you define the right success metrics and hold the team accountable to them
- Fluency in English
