## About tonies:

tonies is the globally leading interactive audio platform for children with more than 10 million Tonieboxes and 125 million Tonies sold. The intuitive and award-winning audio system has changed the way young children play and learn independently with its child-safe, wireless, and screen-free approach. Tonieboxes have been activated in over 100 countries, the content portfolio includes around 1,400 Tonies in several languages. Since its inception, tonies has achieved significant milestones, including listing on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange in November 2021 as well as a globally present team of over 550 dedicated professionals operating from various countries.<br><br>Joining our tonies team means being part of an inspiring mission to create innovative and meaningful experiences for children around the world. Together, we’re shaping the future of play, learning, and imagination.

## You as part of tonies:

<p>As <strong>NPI Manager (all genders),</strong> you play a key role in driving the successful operational execution of new product launches and product changes. Reporting directly to the Head of NPI Operations, you are responsible for managing NPI programs end-to-end - from industrialization readiness through ramp-up into stable serial production.<br><br>Your mission is to ensure that new products and changes are launched on time, at target quality, and within planned costs by orchestrating cross-functional execution across tonies’ global value chain. You act as the operational backbone of the launch process, ensuring structure, transparency, and reliable delivery.<br><br>This role sits within the Global Product Industrialization organization and works closely with Product Development, Product Industrialization, Quality, Procurement, Supply Chain, and Product &amp; Portfolio Management.</p>

<strong>NPI Launch Management</strong><br><ul><li>Own the operational execution of NPI projects in alignment with tonies’ Product Development &amp; Product Variations Process (PDP).</li><li>Plan, coordinate, and track all operational NPI milestones from the prototype phase through ramp-up and handover to serial production.</li><li>Ensure full launch readiness across product, process, supply chain, and production.</li><li>Actively drive, track, and ensure the closure of all open topics, gaps, and action items throughout the launch lifecycle.</li></ul><strong>Launch Control Tower &amp; Performance Transparency</strong><br><ul><li>Operate and maintain Launch Control Tower structures as the single source of truth for NPI status, risks, and dependencies.</li><li>Identify, assess, and actively manage risks, issues, and mitigation actions.</li><li>Prepare, facilitate, and follow up on Launch Readiness Reviews, Ramp-up Reviews, and escalation forums.</li><li>Ensure full transparency and accountability for all open points, including clear ownership, timelines, and escalation where needed.</li></ul><strong>Cross-Functional Orchestration</strong><br><ul><li>Act as the operational interface between Product Development, Product Industrialization, Quality, Procurement, Supply Chain and Production.</li><li>Support a seamless transition from development to serial production in close coordination with manufacturing sites and suppliers.</li><li>Align closely with Product &amp; Portfolio teams on launch priorities and timelines.</li></ul><strong>Continuous Improvement &amp; Operational Excellence</strong><br><ul><li>Identify improvement opportunities within NPI processes, tools, and cross-functional interfaces.</li><li>Contribute to lessons-learned activities and feedback loops between development and industrialization.</li></ul>
