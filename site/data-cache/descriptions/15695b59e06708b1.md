<p>The Customer Accounting team is responsible for the integration with our Servicing Bank with a focus on accounts and transactions to provide those to the customer and to other services on the Raisin platform. Most importantly, account life cycle, reconciliations and transaction compliance are in the domain of this team.</p>
<p>The team is part of the “Customer Tribe”, which consists of 5 teams that collaborate closely on the mission to build and run state of the art Customer and Transaction Account experiences to grow Raisin at scale.</p>
<p>The Customer Accounting team provides:</p>
<ul>
<li>the full lifecycle of the transaction accounts</li>
<li>overview of the account balance and transaction history</li>
<li>compliance monitoring on transactions</li>
</ul>
