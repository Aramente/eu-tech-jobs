<p><strong>About the job</strong></p>
<p>This position is needed to to develop the future platform of communications. Twilio SMS Engineering is looking for a Software Engineer to join our team to work on our SMS connectivity layer with the purpose to build and optimize for delivery. You will be developing a complex distributed platform in Java and be concerned with availability, throughput, latency, and data integrity. At the core are cloud technologies that enable deployment and management of computing resources globally.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>In this role, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, test and deploy features alongside an experienced, distributed collaborative team</li>
<li>Participating in code reviews to ensure code quality and adherence to coding standards.</li>
<li>Work independently to troubleshoot/determine resolution for issues in your team's domain</li>
<li>Managing your work through the use of Github, Jira, and our build/deploy systems</li>
<li>Ensure quality by writing unit-, integration- and load-tests</li>
<li>Collaborating with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.</li>
</ul>
