<p>Plume is seeking a hands-on, technically skilled <strong>Professional Services Engineer</strong> to implement Plume solutions for ISP customers. This role works directly with customers, partners, and cross-functional teams to configure, integrate, test, and validate solutions defined in the Statement of Work (SOW).&nbsp; The Professional Services Engineer also produces technical documentation, provides knowledge transfer and training, collaborates closely with Project Managers and Pre-Sales Architects, and ensures a smooth handoff to the Customer Success Team.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a highly technical, customer-facing person that possesses deep problem-solving skills, excellent communication, and strong ownership of solution delivery.</p>
<p><strong>Primary Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute all technical components of the solution as defined in the SOW</li>
<li>Configure, validate, and optimize Plume platform components for ISP deployments</li>
<li>Integrate Plume solutions with customer systems including OSS/BSS, authentication, provisioning, and monitoring systems</li>
<li>Conduct API-based integrations, data mapping, configuration scripting, and end-to-end functional validation</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues during installation, integration, and testing phases</li>
<li>Serve as the primary technical resource during implementation</li>
<li>Work directly with customer engineering, NOC, operations, and partner teams to ensure solution readiness and alignment</li>
<li>Provide guidance and technical recommendations throughout the deployment lifecycle</li>
<li>Communicate complex concepts clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders</li>
<li>Develop customer-specific implementation guides, configuration records, runbooks, and integration documentation</li>
<li>Produce detailed as-built documentation for use by customers and internal teams</li>
<li>Provide structured knowledge transfer sessions for customer technical teams</li>
<li>Deliver light training on system operation, configuration workflows, and best practices</li>
<li>Conduct system, integration, regression, and acceptance testing aligned to SOW requirements</li>
<li>Validate end-to-end solution performance, data flows, and reliability prior to launch</li>
<li>Create and execute test plans, tracking defects and ensuring resolution</li>
<li>Ensure deployment quality meets or exceeds Plume standards</li>
<li>Work with PreSales Architects to align solution design with implementation details</li>
<li>Collaborate with Project/Program Managers on timelines, risks, issues, and technical dependencies</li>
<li>Interface with Engineering, Support, and Customer Success teams for escalations, bug triage, or advanced troubleshooting</li>
<li>Provide technical feedback to improve Plume’s products, tools, and deployment processes</li>
<li>Prepare all technical documentation required for Customer Success handoff</li>
<li>Ensure customer environments are fully validated and stable before transition</li>
<li>Support early operational ramp-up and address lingering issues to guarantee a clean handover</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualification Requirements:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of experience as a Professional Services Engineer, Implementation Engineer, Systems Engineer, Network Engineer, or similar technical role</li>
<li>Strong technical skills with ISP environments including networking, Wi-Fi, routing, switching, cloud architecture, OSS/BSS, authentication, and provisioning systems</li>
<li>Hands-on experience configuring and debugging network devices, CPE, or cloud-managed platforms</li>
<li>Experience with APIs, JSON, scripting, and integration workflows</li>
<li>Excellent communication and customer interaction skills</li>
<li>Ability to produce detailed technical documentation and deliver training</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to diagnose complex issues</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Wi-Fi and home network expertise, including wireless optimization and troubleshooting</li>
<li>Experience with large-scale ISP deployments or partner ecosystem integrations.</li>
<li>Knowledge of Linux, command-line tooling, or automation frameworks</li>
<li>Familiarity with cloud services, microservices, or distributed systems</li>
<li>Experience with Plume’s product ecosystem (OpenSync, HomePass, WorkPass, Haystack, etc.) is a strong plus</li>
<li>Background in customer-facing consulting or professional services delivery</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Success Indicators:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>High-quality, on-time technical implementation aligned with SOW requirements</li>
<li>Clear and complete technical documentation delivered to customers and Customer Success</li>
<li>High customer satisfaction with technical execution and knowledge transfer</li>
<li>Low number of escalations during or after handoff</li>
<li>Strong internal feedback from PreSales Architects, PMs, Engineering, and Customer Success</li>
<li>Consistent adherence to Plume deployment best practices</li>
</ul>
