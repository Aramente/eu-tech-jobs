<h3><strong>The role</strong></h3>
<p>The HSE Specialist will serve as a critical link between strategic safety objectives and operational execution across our French Logistics Hubs. You will be responsible for fostering a robust safety culture, ensuring strict compliance with French labor laws, and maintaining high safety standards within both our physical facilities and our last-mile delivery operations. This role requires a proactive professional who can navigate complex regulatory landscapes while remaining deeply connected to the daily operations of a fast-paced logistics environment.</p>
<h3><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Translate corporate HSE policies into actionable operational plans tailored for distribution centers and delivery hubs.</li>
<li>Lead comprehensive risk assessments (DUERP), conduct internal audits, and perform deep dive incident investigations to prevent recurrence.</li>
<li>Ensure all sites adhere to the French work (Code du Travail) and environmental regulations. Maintain a high level of visibility on the shopfloor, conduct workplace inspections and coach operational leads on hazard identification.</li>
<li>Utilize process management methodologies to optimize safety workflows and elevate the organization’s safety maturity level.</li>
<li>Guide teams through organizational shifts, ensuring HSE standards remain a top priority during periods of growth or transition.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What you’ll bring</strong></h3>
<ul>

<li>In depth knowledge of French HSE legislation (Code du Travail), including specific requirements for logistics (e.g. CACES certifications, fire safety standards like ERP/ERT and ICPE regulations).</li>
<li>3–5 years of relevant HSE experience, ideally within a distribution center, warehouse or high-volume logistics environment.</li>
<li>Knowledge in ISO standards (ISO 45001, 14001) is an advantage.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>

<li>Skilled in scenario-based thinking, identifying "the big picture" to connect data into a safety strategy.</li>
