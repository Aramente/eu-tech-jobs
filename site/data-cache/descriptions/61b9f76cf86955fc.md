We deploy skilled workers into real commercial and residential environments - kitchens, warehouses, hotel floors, homes - to capture the high-fidelity task data that the world’s leading Robotics labs use to train their foundation models. <ul>

<li><strong>Issue Resolution.</strong> Diagnose and resolve day-to-day technical issues in the field, document recurring failures, and escalate more complex problems to engineering teams.</li>

<li><strong>Operator Enablement.</strong> Support field operators with fit adjustments, setup guidance, charging workflows, and best practices to ensure reliable data capture.</li>
<li><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration.</strong> Coordinate with robotics leadership, data ops, QA, and engineering teams to continuously improve hardware reliability, usability, and field performance.</li>
