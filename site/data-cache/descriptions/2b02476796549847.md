We move with urgency and believe great outcomes come from high standards</p></div><h4><strong>About the role</strong></h4>
<p>We are looking for a Lead Frontend Engineer to own the frontend craft for the “candidate site” or the take-home assessment product. You will work on a React/Next.js and TypeScript codebase that is required to perform reliably under real-world conditions at a significant scale. Performance, accessibility, and reliability are treated as core product attributes rather than secondary concerns because the quality of this experience directly affects whether a developer can demonstrate their true ability.</p>
<p>Beyond the core product, this role sits at the centre of HackerRank's AI-era shift. You will contribute to building AI-powered assistants that help candidates inside the assessment experience, and work alongside a broader engineering push to embed AI agents across the internal SDLC&nbsp; engineering, QA, review, and delivery. Paid access to leading AI development tools is standard, and applied AI work here is production-bound rather than experimental.</p>
<h4><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Lead the development of intuitive, scalable user experiences across the hiring journey in close partnership with product and design.</li>
<li>Architect and build reusable frontend systems and component libraries that scale across HackerRank products.</li>
<li>Set and uphold high standards for performance, accessibility, testing, and overall code quality.</li>
<li>Contribute to building AI-powered assistants in the candidate experience and to internal AI agents that speed up the team's engineering workflows.</li>
<li>Own and continuously improve key web performance metrics such as Core Web Vitals and responsiveness.</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally to influence product direction and elevate user experience.</li>
<li>Mentor engineers and drive end-to-end ownership of features from ideation to production.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Who you are</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>An experienced frontend engineer 6–8 years of experience building production-grade frontend applications, with strong expertise in React.</li>
<li>Deep knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, with a sharp eye for detail and accessibility.</li>
<li>Proven ability to design scalable frontend architectures and reusable component systems.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of modern frontend tooling, bundling, and performance optimization.</li>
<li>Comfortable leading projects, mentoring engineers, and collaborating with cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Hands-on with AI-native development; ideally has already built or shipped AI-powered features and uses agentic tools as a core part of the workflow.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Even better if you have</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Experience with frontend security and protecting user trust.</li>
<li>Experience shipping AI-powered product features or working with LLM APIs and agentic tooling.</li>
</ul>
