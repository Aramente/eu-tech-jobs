<p><strong>How you’ll make an impact:</strong></p>
<p>The Infrastructure Security team builds the internal platforms, systems, and services that serve as the foundational building blocks for how our engineers build securely every day. As a Senior Software Engineer on Infrastructure Security, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ship foundational security services to enable Klaviyo engineering to move faster with confidence</li>
<li>Design and develop systems and processes that enable highly available &amp; scalable systems</li>
<li>Design, build and deliver software to dramatically improve the availability, scalability, latency, and efficiency of Klaviyo’s services</li>
<li>Achieve break-throughs in systems throughput by identifying and eliminating bottlenecks</li>
<li>Leverage technology such as Python, Golang, AWS, Django, Kubernetes, Bash, Terraform, MySQL, Redis, Clickhouse, Iceberg, DataLake, Istio, Postgresql, and more to advance Klaviyo’s platform</li>
<li>Champion best practices by actively collaborating with other teams in a culture that values whiteboarding and technical design review</li>
<li>Contribute to the company as a subject matter expert in multiple areas, constantly pushing yourself to be a better engineer and to level up all of your peers within your team and within Klaviyo.</li>
<li>Mentor and pair with other Klaviyo engineers to build better software by focusing on performance, self-healing system, configuration as code; defensive programming, application security, etc.</li>
<li>Participate in periodic on call duties with a focus on solving issues when they are discovered, preventing recurrences and minimizing alert fatigue&nbsp;</li>
<li>Prototype and advocate for architectural improvements to achieve breakthrough results in Klaviyo systems’ operational scalability and reliability</li>
<li>Work hand-in-hand with product-facing engineers to ship impactful code</li>
<li>Perform quantitative investigation to understand and scale Klaviyo systems and manage the cross-functional effort to resolve scalability issues</li>
<li>Produce and advocate for preventative, upstream solutions with internal stakeholders and external vendors and dependencies</li>
<li>Confidently make informed, data-driven choices in a fast paced environment with competing priorities</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Who you are:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You care deeply about building software for the long term, balancing technical quality, delivery pace, and business impact.</li>
<li>You have strong hands on experience with Linux operating systems and computer networking</li>
<li>You’re comfortable and experienced writing code in a programming language such as Python, Ruby, Go, etc.</li>
<li>You have deep experience with cloud infrastructure, operating in a high scale environment</li>
<li>You have strong hands-on experience building and operating highly available systems, and are comfortable owning complex problems end to end.</li>
<li>You’ve led the delivery of significant projects or features independently, including discovery, technical design, implementation, rollout, and ongoing maintenance, and you’re comfortable providing technical direction to others.</li>
<li>You have deep expertise in one or more areas of modern web engineering (e.g. backend services, data pipelines, internal platforms, or AI-enabled systems) and a solid understanding of scalable, multi-tenant architectures.</li>
<li>You regularly use AI-powered tools in your development workflow and are curious about applying AI thoughtfully to improve engineering productivity and system capabilities.</li>
<li>You take ownership of operational excellence for the systems you build, including performance, reliability, observability, and on-call participation where required.</li>
<li>You enjoy questioning convention and continuously improving how things work, whether that’s architecture, tooling, workflows, or team practices.</li>
<li>You are a clear communicator and collaborative teammate, comfortable leading technical discussions, writing design docs or RFCs, and keeping stakeholders aligned.</li>
<li>You enjoy working in small, autonomous, agile teams, shipping incrementally, and partnering closely with product managers and other engineers to deliver better outcomes.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
