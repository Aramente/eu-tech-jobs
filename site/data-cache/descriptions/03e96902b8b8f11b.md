<p>Dataiku is looking for a <strong>Senior Engagement Manager</strong> to lead strategic services engagements across <strong>Southern Europe</strong>, primarily supporting <strong>Manufacturing &amp; Energy</strong> customers.</p>
<p>This role goes beyond project management: you will act as a <strong>Deployment and Value Architect</strong>, influencing the customer’s <strong>AI deployment roadmap</strong>, shaping and prioritising their <strong>use case portfolio</strong>, aligning executives on <strong>KPIs and ROI</strong>, and ensuring <strong>measurable business impact</strong> and <strong>scalable adoption</strong>.</p>
<p>You will partner closely internally with Account Executives, Customer Success Managers, Technical Account Managers and Data Scientists to create a stable, secure, and scalable Dataiku platform, while accelerating the path from idea to production to replication across several business units.</p>
<h2><strong>How You’ll Make an Impact on our customers</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>1) Program management</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead end-to-end <strong>project management</strong> for new customer implementations of Dataiku.</li>
<li>Drive<strong> program management</strong> for strategic accounts by coordinating multiple workstreams, covering platform scaling, governance, enablement, use cases, and adoption, with clear milestones and executive-level reporting.</li>
<li>Ensure smooth collaboration across Dataiku (AE/CSM/SE/FE/DS/Partners) and customer teams.</li>
<li>Drive predictable delivery across a complex portfolio, with strong risk anticipation and scope control.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>2) Use Case Roadmap</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Drive <strong>use case selection and prioritisation</strong> using value/feasibility criteria.</li>
<li>Translate industry value chains (Manufacturing / Energy) into a <strong>use-case-driven roadmap</strong> (where AI/agents create measurable impact).</li>
<li>Facilitate <strong>cross-BU replication</strong> (templates, standards, reusability, industrialisation path).</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>3) Value Engineering</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Establish value hypotheses and success metrics early (KPIs, ROI drivers, adoption metrics) to quantify value upfront and then prove it downstream.</li>
<li>Track and communicate <strong>realized business value</strong> over time, ensure learnings feed the roadmap and renewal/expansion narrative.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>4) Executive alignment &amp; customer leadership</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Align executive sponsors on goals, trade-offs, and progress; produce <strong>crisp, exec-ready updates</strong> (SteerCo/QBR/QSR style).</li>
<li>Become a <strong>trusted advisor</strong> for sponsors and key stakeholders: manage stakeholder dynamics, push back when needed, and keep scope/value stable despite shifting demands.</li>
<li>Contribute to <strong>services scoping and commercial shaping</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>5) Practice Development, Capitalisation &amp; Continuous Improvement</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to our Professional Services practice scale by capturing patterns, improving templates/playbooks, and sharing learnings to accelerate delivery and make it more repeatable over time.</li>
<li>Identify systemic blockers (TTV, governance, adoption) and help drive reusable fixes.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Need to Be Successful</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>8+ years</strong> of experience in analytics/data / AI delivery, consulting, or complex enterprise programs.</li>
<li>Proven ability to lead <strong>multi-stakeholder initiatives</strong> with exec presence and strong written communication.</li>
<li>Organised, process-oriented, resourceful and highly motivated by a hands-on approach to customer management, routinely interacting with customers and cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Experience with value frameworks and driving adoption at scale.</li>
<li>Understanding of deploying analytics/ML/GenAI solutions into real operations (governance, run, scaling patterns).</li>

</ul>
