<h2><strong>What you will work on / How you will leave your footprint</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain the design assurance process, ensuring safety functions are correctly implemented through the technical architecture.</li>
<li>Conduct hardware safety analysis — FMEA/FMECA, failure rate analysis, diagnostic coverage, component derating, and environmental qualification.</li>
<li>Conduct software safety analysis — architecture assessment, identification of safety-relevant software components, and definition of software safety requirements.</li>
<li>Conduct ML element assurance — data governance, model validation, robustness testing, performance monitoring, and uncertainty quantification for safety-relevant ML components.</li>
<li>Contribute to component selection for safety-critical functions — evaluate against reliability data, failure mode profiles, and supply chain maturity.</li>
<li>Define safety mechanisms — redundancy, monitoring, plausibility checks, safe-state transitions, and watchdogs.</li>
<li>Drive fault injection test design — fault models, injection points, expected responses, and coverage targets.</li>
<li>Contribute to cost-benefit tradeoffs between alternative safety architectures and recommend the most efficient path to required integrity.</li>
<li>Contribute to technical risk acceptance — assess residual failure probabilities and confirm design meets allocated integrity targets.</li>
<li>Maintain safety concept documents defining safety architecture, fault models, diagnostic strategy, and safe-state definitions.</li>
<li>Own and maintain the system requirements baseline — translate safety measures, security controls, and design needs into verifiable requirements with full traceability.</li>
<li>Manage compliance matrices for design-level regulations (Low Voltage Directive, EMC Directive, Battery Regulation, REACH, RoHS, POPs).</li>
<li>Maintain environmental compliance documents (LCEP, EDC, ETS), substance register, and project handbook.</li>
<li>Coordinate with System Safety to receive safety function allocations and integrity targets; provide feasibility and cost-benefit feedback; escalate when targets are infeasible.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Product Security on shared technical architecture — fail-safe vs. fail-secure behavior, component selection criteria, and unified software development guidelines.</li>
<li>Translate system requirements into hardware design rules, software coding guidelines, and integration test requirements for engineering teams; participate in design reviews and FMEA workshops.</li>
<li>Define verification criteria and fault injection test cases on system requirements for V&amp;V; specify coverage targets; review and accept V&amp;V evidence for design-level safety claims.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain AI-assisted workflows for requirements management, compliance auditing, and traceability verification.<br><br></li>
</ul>
