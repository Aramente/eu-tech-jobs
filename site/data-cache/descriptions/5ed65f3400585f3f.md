<p data-start="159" data-end="610">We are seeking a QA Automation Engineer to own the quality engineering function end-to-end. In this role, you will be responsible for designing automation frameworks, setting standards, and embedding quality into every step of the development lifecycle. As a core member of the team, you will not be a seat at the back of the sprint, but will work closely with engineers, product, and design to ship reliable, production-grade Web3 experiences. Mesh is a global company with headquarters in San Francisco, California USA. This candidate will work closely with the Engineering and Product teams spread across North America, Europe and India.</p>
<p><strong>What You'll Do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Architect, build, and maintain robust end-to-end test automation frameworks using tools like Playwright.</li>
<li>Design test strategies that cover the full stack: UI, API, smart contract interactions, and wallet flows</li>
<li>Write and maintain tests for Web3-specific user journeys including wallet connection, transaction signing, contract interactions, and on-chain state verification</li>
<li>Integrate automated test suites into CI/CD pipelines and own the health of those pipelines</li>
<li>Leverage AI-powered development tools (Copilot, Cursor, Claude, etc.) to accelerate test authoring and framework development, and bring strong opinions on how to do this well</li>
<li>Identify gaps in test coverage, prioritize ruthlessly, and drive continuous improvement</li>
<li>Champion quality culture across the engineering org</li>
<li>Stay ahead of the curve on new testing tools, frameworks, and Web3 ecosystem shifts</li>
</ul>
