<h2><strong>Job Description</strong></h2>
<p>As the <strong>Product Strategy Manager – ANZ</strong>, you will help turn the product strategy into execution across Australia and New Zealand. You will focus on the <strong>physical product and commercial strategy</strong>—including menu, recipes, concepts, and themes—rather than software or app management. This role is vital because the weekly menu is our core product, directly impacting customer satisfaction and growth.</p>
<p>This role is based in <strong>Chippendale (Sydney)</strong> and reports to the <strong>Associate Director of Product Strategy</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>You will…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Analyse recipe and SKU-level performance to translate data into clear recommendations and portfolio insights.</li>
<li>Maintain and deliver the merchandising calendar, covering seasonal themes, cultural moments, and cuisine spotlights.</li>
<li>Support the rollout of new concepts, portfolio changes (such as Classic, Healthy, or Family), and innovation ideas.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Menu Planning and Culinary teams to ensure weekly menus accurately reflect planned themes and concepts.</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally with Marketing, Procurement, and Operations to communicate timelines and keep projects on track.</li>
<li>Prepare structured slides, reporting packs, and documentation for senior stakeholder meetings and business reviews.</li>
<li>Own and maintain dashboards and trackers to ensure product data is accurate and up to date.</li>
<li>Proactively identify opportunities to improve processes and templates to speed up decision-making.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>You have…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>3–5 years’ experience</strong> in category management, merchandising, FMCG, strategy consulting, or retail operations.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with confidence working in <strong>Excel or Google Sheets</strong> and a high eye for detail.</li>
<li>Excellent presentation skills and the ability to turn complex analysis into simple, clear summaries or slides.</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple tasks and stakeholders while meeting deadlines with high execution quality.</li>
<li>A proactive, hands-on mindset and a genuine interest in food and customer behavior.</li>
</ul>
