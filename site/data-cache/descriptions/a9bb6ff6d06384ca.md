<p>In addition, you’ll support <strong>Spanish- and Portuguese-speaking sellers based in North America</strong> (e.g., Boston, Denver, Atlanta) who sell into LATAM accounts. <p>This role is ideal for someone who thrives as an independent operator, builds strong relationships in the field, and enjoys adapting enablement to meet regional needs while staying aligned with global standards. This role will report into the our global Field Enablement team based in New York City and continue partnering with Field Enablement Managers globally across all regions and segments.&nbsp;</p>

<ul>
<li>Deliver live and virtual enablement sessions focused on <strong>ongoing ramp and everboarding</strong> for Sales and Customer Success teams across LATAM.</li>

<li>Support Spanish- and Portuguese-speaking sellers based in North America who sell to LATAM customers.</li>
<li>Adapt and localize globally developed enablement programs to ensure relevance across <strong>language, culture, market context, and regional sales motions</strong>.</li>
<li>Design and facilitate reinforcement activities (e.g., role plays, deal clinics, skill practice sessions) that help sellers apply core concepts in real customer scenarios.</li>

<li>Fluent in <strong>Spanish, Portuguese, and English</strong> (required).</li>

<li>Willing and able to travel approximately <strong>30%</strong> across Latin America and North America.</li>
