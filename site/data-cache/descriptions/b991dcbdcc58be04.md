<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<div>
<p class="s9"><span class="s5">As a Requirements Engineer, you are responsible for establishing and maintaining the foundational "source of truth" for Helsing’s complex mechatronic and software-defined programmes.</span></p>
<p class="s10"><span class="s5">You will act as the crucial linchpin between high-level operational needs, system architecture, and detailed engineering design. As part of our cross-functional team, you will directly participate in our work with industry partners and defence forces to translate abstract capabilities and regulatory standards into actionable, verifiable engineering specifications. Your work will guarantee that we build exactly what is needed to maintain our technological advantage and ensure mission success on the battlefield.</span></p>
<p class="s10"><span class="s5">Working closely with mechanical, electrical, software, and AI engineering teams, you will not just be a tool administrator. You will own the requirements lifecycle, facilitate technical alignment, and ensure that our physical platforms and AI integrations are built on a foundation of clear, unambiguous, and traceable requirements. In this role, you are responsible for the meticulous documentation, traceability, and change management that underpins the on-time and high-quality delivery of our defence systems.</span></p>
</div>

<div class="s6"><span class="s4">•<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span><span class="s5">Collaborate with product managers, system architects, and subject matter experts to capture high-level stakeholder needs and operational concepts (CONOPS).<br><br></span></div>
<div class="s6"><span class="s4">•<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span><span class="s5">Analyse and refine raw inputs into clear, unambiguous, and testable system and subsystem requirements, enforcing best practices (e.g., INCOSE standards, EARS syntax).<br><br></span></div>
<div class="s6"><span class="s4">•<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span></span><span class="s5">Own and administer the enterprise Requirements Management (RM) tool (e.g., Jama Connect, DOORS, Polarion) for assigned mechatronic projects.<br><br></span></div>
