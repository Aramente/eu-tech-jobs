<h2><strong>About the role: What's in the Box</strong></h2>
<p>The position is part of the Consumer Alliance - Deliveries and Orders Squad, which has the mission to maximize retention of new and active customers by managing the complete order lifecycle. This squad is accountable for some of HelloFresh's most critical backend systems that process millions of orders globally.</p>
<p>The service provider will work with backend event-driven systems assisting millions of customers globally in the journey from subscription activation through to the cutoff moment—when orders are finalized and handed off to fulfillment teams. The focus is on ensuring customers have the right deliveries scheduled with rich flexibility features and correct orders successfully created at a right time of user choosing. This role does not include people management responsibilities.</p>
<p>At HelloTech, flexibility and cross-functional collaboration are core to how we work. While this role is aligned to a specific Alliance, strong candidates may also be considered for opportunities across different teams or projects.</p>
