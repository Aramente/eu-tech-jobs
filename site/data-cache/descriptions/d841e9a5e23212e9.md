<p>As <strong>Senior Manager, Commercial Partnerships, APAC</strong>, you’ll lead the development and growth of Klaviyo’s channel and alliances ecosystem across Asia–Pacific. This is a highly strategic, hands‑on role that combines <strong>team leadership, partner enablement, and market expansion</strong>.</p>
<p>You will be responsible for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Managing and growing our <strong>top‑tier Commercial partner book of business in Australia &amp; New Zealand (ANZ)</strong>, managed by our Commercial PAMs.</li>
<li>Building and scaling a <strong>high‑performing Commercial partner team in Asia and developing a reseller and alliances channel across Asia</strong> to accelerate regional growth.</li>
</ul>
<p>You will treat Channel &amp; Alliances as a <strong>core revenue engine</strong> – not a side program – by running a disciplined, data‑driven operating rhythm, embedding structured co‑sell motions with Sales and CS, and putting <strong>AI‑first practices</strong> at the center of how the team plans, executes, and reports.</p>
<p>This role is ideal for a player‑coach who can scale Klaviyo’s presence across diverse markets while driving revenue through strong partner‑led motions and building the leadership bench for APAC.</p>
<h2><strong>How You’ll Make an Impact</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Set and execute the <strong>APAC Commercial Channel &amp; Alliances strategy</strong>, including the ANZ Commercial partner portfolio and Asia reseller motion, aligned to regional ARR, NDR, and multiproduct goals.</li>
<li>Drive <strong>predictable partner‑sourced and partner‑influenced pipeline and ARR</strong> through a disciplined operating rhythm, inspection, and accurate forecasting.</li>
<li>Lead, coach, and scale a <strong>high‑performing Commercial partner team across APAC</strong>, creating a high‑accountability, high‑support culture.</li>
<li>Embed <strong>structured co‑sell motions</strong> with Sales and CS so partners are consistently involved in real customer work across new business, onboarding, and expansion.</li>
<li>Design and run a <strong>scaled, high‑velocity partner motion</strong> for smaller agencies and resellers alongside the managed tier, balancing depth with breadth across the Commercial base.</li>
<li>Elevate <strong>partner experience and alliances</strong> via scalable enablement programs, joint GTM plays, and strong governance (rules of engagement, deal registration, and QBRs).</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>How Success Will Be Measured</strong></h2>
<p>You’ll be measured on a mix of <strong>outcomes and leading indicators</strong>, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner‑sourced and partner‑influenced <strong>ARR and pipeline</strong> in Commercial segments.</li>
<li><strong>Pipeline coverage and conversion</strong> for partner‑attached opportunities (by market and segment).</li>
<li><strong>Multiproduct attach rate</strong> (e.g. SMS, WhatsApp, MA, K:Service, Reviews, CDP) via partners.</li>
<li><strong>Activation, productivity, and health</strong> of managed partners (time‑to‑first‑deal, deals per partner, QBR participation, certifications).</li>
<li><strong>Team performance and development</strong>: attainment vs goals, progression, and internal partner/Sales feedback.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Required Skills &amp; Qualifications</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Proven experience</strong> in partnerships, channel sales, or business development, with a focus on <strong>Commercial or mid‑market SaaS</strong>.</li>
<li>Track record of <strong>building and managing partner teams and reseller ecosystems</strong>, including owning revenue and pipeline targets.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of <strong>APAC markets</strong>, with direct experience in ANZ and Asia highly preferred.</li>
<li>Excellent <strong>communication and relationship‑building skills</strong> with both internal and external stakeholders, including C‑level.</li>
<li><strong>Data‑driven operator</strong> with expertise in forecasting, pipeline management, and partner performance analysis.</li>
<li>Ability to thrive in a <strong>fast‑paced, high‑growth environment</strong> with competing priorities.</li>
<li>Demonstrable <strong>AI‑first thinking</strong>: proven ability to identify, test, and implement AI‑driven workflows that increase team efficiency, effectiveness, and scalability.</li>
<li>Comfort experimenting with AI in work or personal projects, with a desire to <strong>responsibly explore new AI tools and workflows</strong> to make work smarter and more efficient.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Experience building or leading <strong>Channel &amp; Alliances motions</strong> (agencies, SIs, BPOs, ISVs, resellers) across multiple APAC markets.</li>
<li>Exposure to <strong>ecommerce and martech ecosystems</strong> (e.g. Shopify, WooCommerce, SFCC, Magento, or adjacent platforms).</li>
<li>Prior experience designing and running <strong>co‑sell programs and GTM plays</strong> with agencies and global alliances partnerships.</li>
<li>People management experience in high‑growth environments, especially managing <strong>Partner Account Managers or Channel sellers</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Klaviyo in APAC</strong></h2>
<p>We are starting small but have big plans for Klaviyo in APAC. Our Sydney team began to take shape throughout 2022 and has now built out our go‑to‑market teams in sales, customer success, partnerships, marketing, recruiting and product support.</p>
<p>As the team grows, we will see a continued expansion of Klaviyo’s customer base and the building of a fantastic working culture in line with what we see elsewhere in Klaviyo, but with its own local identity within our Sydney‑based and broader APAC teams. This is your unique opportunity to join us at a very early stage and help shape <strong>the future of Klaviyo in APAC – and the leadership engine we build through Channel &amp; Alliances</strong>.</p>
<p>One of our values as a company is <strong>“always learning”</strong>, and you can feel that in APAC. People are looking to grow and develop, and Klaviyo provides a great platform to do so. As we are still relatively early in our journey in APAC, there is significant headroom to grow and develop within your role – we are looking for the leaders of the future.</p>
<p>At Klaviyo, managers are often promoted up into their roles, and people are regularly promoted or move into new functional areas – it really is an education through experience here.</p>
