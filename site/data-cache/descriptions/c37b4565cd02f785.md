Our holistic approach includes a team of peer recovery specialists, benefits specialists and clinicians. Our teams focus on meeting each individual where they are and walking with them side by side as a trusted guide and partner on their journey to better health.</div>

<div>firsthand's team members use their lived experience to build trust with these individuals and support them in reconnecting to the healthcare they need, while minimizing inappropriate healthcare utilization. Together with our health plan partners, we are changing the way our society supports those most impacted by SMI.</div>

<div>We are cultivating a team of deeply passionate problem-solvers to tackle significant and complex healthcare challenges with us. This is more than a job—it's a calling. Every day, you will engage in work that resonates with purpose, gain wisdom from motivated colleagues, and thrive in an environment that celebrates continuous learning, creativity, and fun.</div>
<div>&nbsp;</div></div><p>We use data infrastructure to enable more timely and personalized engagement with individuals living with SMI at scale. This allows our team of peers, social workers, and nurse practitioners to plan appropriate care and focus on critical tasks rather than manually searching for information across silos in the healthcare system. This is a unique opportunity to help drive the impact and capabilities of our data team and infrastructure. You will build impactful healthcare technology on a modern technology stack.</p>
<p><strong>This is a remote, individual contributor role reporting directly to the CTO. You'll be the most senior data engineer on a team of 3 (within a 5-person engineering department). This is a technical leadership role - no people management, but broad ownership, autonomy and responsibility to : define and drive excellence in our data platform.&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Our data infrastructure is built on Snowflake, DBT (Cloud), Airflow, and Python, with tools like Fivetran and Hightouch. The foundation is solid but not pristine - there's a solid mix of improvements to handle, new features to enable, and running-the-business systems to improve and automate.</p>
<p><strong>Role expectations:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Responsible for designing, building, and optimizing scaled data infrastructure, serving as a technical lead and mentor within the organization.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Directly collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders to effectively gather requirements and understand business needs in order to create durable pipelines, infrastructure, and ELT models that enable the company to ingest data in various formats and from various sources and then turn them into usable business facing data products.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Align and coordinate your team's work against strategic business priorities and help define and manage the data roadmap.</li>
<li>Drive operational excellence by ensuring data quality through automated testing and validation, robust pipeline monitoring and alerting, and establishing clear incident response practices when things break.</li>
<li>Provide technical leadership through mentorship, architecture reviews, and maintaining high technical standards across the codebase.</li>
<li>Own data architecture, infrastructure, and platform improvements. Drive systems improvements and upgrades with a long-term view.</li>
<li>Use systems thinking to solve problems and add capabilities. Adopt tools, technologies, and patterns to provide leverage. Build a platform, don't just get tasks done.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>The experience you bring to this role:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of data engineering experience, with deep expertise in technologies like Snowflake, DBT, Airflow, and Python. You've architected data systems for reliability and scale, not just built pipelines to spec.</li>
<li>A track record with ambiguous initiatives that required breaking down complex problems, coordinating across engineering and business teams, and delivering outcomes.</li>
<li>Strong technical communication skills, including the ability to represent your team's work and direction to non-technical leadership, and to translate business needs into technical strategy that others can follow.</li>

</ul>
