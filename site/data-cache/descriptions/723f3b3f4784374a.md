### **Who is Sonar?**

Sonar is driving the future of agent-centric software development. As the leader in AI code review and verification, we solve a critical problem: ensuring that software generated by AI-assisted developers or autonomous agents is reliable, secure, and maintainable.

Integrating seamlessly with Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, and Devin, we help over 75% of the Fortune 100 build trusted, reliable, compliant software. Customers who use Sonar are 44% less likely to report an outage due to AI-generated code.

We believe code verification is the critical missing link in the Agent-Centric Development Cycle (AC/DC). Industry giants like Nvidia, ServiceNow, Booking.com, Goldman Sachs, AstraZeneca, and Ford Motor Company count on us to provide independent, explainable, consistent review and governance of their AI-generated code via products like:

* SonarQube: The world’s leading AI code review and verification platform.
* SonarQube Foundation Agent: Currently topping the leaderboards for agentic software repair.
* SonarSweep & Sonar Context Augmentation: Providing the enterprise-grade context and constraints agents need to be truly effective.

Our team operates across global hubs in Austin, Bochum, Dubai, Geneva, London, Singapore, Tokyo, and Washington D.C. We move with a mindset we call CODE:

* Committed to our customers and community.
* Obsessed with quality.
* Deliberate in our decisions.
* Effective as one team.

With over $400M in revenue and profitable, fast-paced growth, we are building the backbone of the AI software revolution. If you’re hungry to have an impact, want to build at a fast pace, and ready to work at the forefront of AI, we want to hear from you.

## What You Will Do Daily:

**Internal Alignment & Proactive Partner Enablement**

- Aligning with their sales counterpart, the Partner Manager. They review the partner pipeline, discuss any technical roadblocks partners are facing in current deals, and strategize on which partners need focused technical enablement in the coming weeks. This ensures that technical and sales efforts are perfectly in sync.
- Partner Enablement Session This is a core activity. The PSE leads a virtual and in person technical training session for partners. this could include deep dives into the latest product release, new API endpoints and integration best practices. The goal is self-sufficiency: coaching the partner's team on how to deliver effective product demonstrations and handle common technical objections themselves.
- Deal Support & Strategy The PSE works with partner's Solutions Engineer on qualification, and evaluation of Sonar products.. The PSE acts as an "expert resource," helping the partner's SE architect a tailored solution, anticipate complex technical questions, and position the joint value proposition effectively. You aren't leading the customer call, but you are ensuring the partner is perfectly prepared to do so.
- **Asset Creation & Focused Work**
- Developing Scalable Resources To avoid answering the same questions repeatedly, the PSE dedicates time to creating reusable assets for the entire partner network. This could involve writing a standardized demo script, building a code sample for a common integration, or recording a short video tutorial on a complex feature. This work is crucial for scaling their impact.
- Monitoring Partner Engagement to ensure partners are staying on top their learning it may be necessary to manage and evalngelise certification and badging programmes.
- **Strategic Initiatives & Internal Feedback**
- Joint Solution Design with a Technology Partner The PSE meets with engineers from a technology partner company to architect a new integration. This is a highly strategic and technical discussion requiring a deep understanding both products, defining the scope of the integration, and building a business case for why it will create shared value. This involves mapping the partner's value chain and identifying growth opportunities.
- Internal Feedback Loop with Product Management The PSE acts as the voice of the partner ecosystem to the internal product team. They consolidate feedback gathered from several partners about a missing feature that is creating friction in their sales cycles. They present this feedback with specific use cases, helping the product manager understand the business impact and prioritize the request on the roadmap.
- Planning & Follow-up Documenting action items from meetings, sending follow-up resources to the partners they have trained and maintaining the Partner Enablement Plan
