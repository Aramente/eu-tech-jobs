<h3>The role</h3>
<p>To fuel this growth, we are hiring a Senior GTM Intelligence Analyst to build the data-driven analytics team. This role is a key member of our growing GTM Analytics team and is responsible for building and maintaining data infrastructure used by our global salesforce and leadership team, with a strong emphasis on stakeholder collaboration.</p>
<h3>You will have the opportunity to</h3>
<ul>
<li>Get insights and experience first-hand how an effective, fast-paced organization operates through learning about marketing/sales processes, collaboration and tools</li>
<li>Work with various BI tools, develop analytics frameworks and see how data is used to drive business outcomes&nbsp;</li>
<li>Accelerate your career through exposure to the most effective sales methodology and through working side by side with the best sales professionals in the industry</li>
<li>Establish a strong network of peers and mentors</li>
<li>Gain insight into a $60b+ technology industry that is rapidly transforming</li>
</ul>
<h3>Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate closely with various stakeholders to understand their data needs and requirements, especially converting high-level business challenges into actionable, technical solutions.</li>
<li>Undertake project management responsibilities to effectively plan, organize, and oversee data analysis projects with minimal supervision, while handling competing priorities and stakeholders independently</li>
<li>Proactively navigate ambiguity by synthesizing unclear or complex business objectives into clear, actionable analytical frameworks, dashboards, or tools, operating with minimal direction or oversight</li>
<li>Independently gather requirements from non-technical stakeholders, analyzing their needs, and translating those into scalable technical tools, dashboards, or processes that align with broader business objectives</li>
<li>Build tools to put data in the hands of our GTM teams to allow them to make data-driven decisions and increase their productivity</li>
<li>Create our reporting frameworks that allow senior leadership to make data-driven decisions about the business</li>
<li>Partner with executive business stakeholders across the company to give them data-driven insights into the performance of the sales organization</li>
<li>Lead and conduct ad hoc analyses and effectively communicate findings to non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Scale and manage multiple concurrent projects across a diverse range of responsibilities and stakeholders, ensuring effective prioritization, progress tracking, and meeting deadlines</li>
<li>Review, update, and audit key sales-facing dashboards that track pipeline, productivity, attainment, and other key metrics</li>
<li>Work with the centralized data architecture team to build schemas that help measure critical business metrics for sales</li>
</ul>
<h3>Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of work experience, ideally in a quantitative role</li>
<li>SQL experience</li>
<li>Dashboarding experience in Tableau or equivalent dashboard tools</li>
<li>Experience in Sigma and CRM-Analytics will be an advantage</li>
<li>Ability to communicate efficiently and effectively with senior stakeholders and convert their requirements into clear technical deliverables</li>
<li>Hands-on experience working with large datasets to conduct analysis, identify issues, and quantify impact</li>
<li>Proactive ability to navigate ambiguity and distill complex, high-level business needs into clear, actionable technical requirements and tools, while maintaining a self-driven approach to problem-solving</li>
<li>Knowledge of Google Sheets and/or Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce reports &amp; dashboards preferred</li>
</ul>
