We utilize the latest technology to reshape the composites industry by manufacturing high quality composite parts faster and more affordably than anyone else. Our customers are developing tomorrow's high tech products, and we are building technology to accelerate their success using scalable automation.

We are also a team of experienced founders, product leaders, and engineers who have built and supported some of the fastest-growing brands in the US. We have raised funding from advisors and institutional investors, including [Founders Fund](https://foundersfund.com/), [Lux Capital](https://www.luxcapital.com/) and [Haystack](https://haystack.vc/).
