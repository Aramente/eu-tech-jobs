<p><strong>About the job</strong></p>
<p>This position is needed to own and accelerate our ecosystem across EMEA North (UKI &amp; Nordics). This isn't a "maintenance" role—we need a heavy hitter who can build a regional powerhouse from the ground up. You’ll be the bridge between our most strategic partners and our internal teams, turning high-level alliances into massive, revenue-generating engines.</p>
<p>If you have a deep network in the UKI and Nordic markets, thrive on closing complex deals through partners, and want the autonomy to run your territory like a business owner, this is your seat</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>In this role, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive Growth: Own a portfolio of top-tier System Integrators and Resellers to achieve partner-sourced and partner influence revenue targets.</li>
<li>Master the GTM: Build and execute the playbook for EMEA North, aligning local wins with our global vision.</li>
<li>Build High-Level Alliances: Get into the room with C-suite stakeholders and VPs to align business goals and create joint value.</li>
<li>Win Together: Partner with our Sales teams to map accounts, joint-sell, and accelerate the pipeline.</li>
<li>Be the Expert: Act as the "Voice of the Partner" internally, ensuring our Product and Marketing teams are building what the market actually needs.</li>
<li>Show Up: Be the face of our partner program at major events, executive briefings, and QBRs.</li>
</ul>

<p>Twilio values diverse experiences from all kinds of industries, and we encourage everyone who meets the required qualifications to apply. <li>Executive Presence: You’re comfortable leading a room of C-level executives and can simplify complex ideas into winning strategies.</li>
