<h1>What You’ll Do</h1>
<p>You’ll be the connective tissue between our engineering team, our ERP partners, and the broader business. Day to day, that means:</p>
<h2>Roadmap &amp; Strategy</h2>
<p>Own and maintain the product roadmap end to end. Prioritize features and integrations based on partner needs, customer feedback, and business impact. Communicate the “what,” “why,” and “when” clearly to engineering, leadership, and external stakeholders.</p>
<h2>ERP Partner Management</h2>
<p>Act as Payra’s primary point of contact with ERP companies (think partner portals, developer programs, certification processes, and co-selling motions). Build and maintain strong working relationships with partner product and technical teams. Stay deeply current on each ERP ecosystem’s release cycles, API changes, and marketplace requirements.</p>
<h2>Unblocking Engineering</h2>
<p>Handle the business and partnership side of developing within ERP partner systems: securing sandbox/dev environments, managing partner developer accounts and credentials, navigating approval and review processes, resolving licensing or compliance questions, and chasing down documentation or technical contacts so engineers can stay focused on building.</p>
<h2>Cross-functional Coordination</h2>
<p>Translate business requirements into clear specs and user stories. Run sprint planning and backlog grooming alongside engineering leads. Define success metrics and track them post-launch.<br><br>Customer Insight</p>
<p>Gather and synthesize feedback from customers, partners, and the sales team to inform what we build next.</p>
