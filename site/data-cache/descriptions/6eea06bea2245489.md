<h3><strong><span data-contrast="auto">The role</span></strong><span data-ccp-props="{}">&nbsp;</span></h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="219" data-local-id="de82a677e8c5">The <strong>Field Structured Cabling Senior Technician</strong> is responsible for managing and controlling structured cabling activities on-site, ensuring installations are completed according to approved designs, specifications, and project schedules. This role serves as the main field interface between the design team, project managers, contractors, logistics, and vendors.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="582" data-local-id="d00d6c97c2cd">The position is field-focused and plays a key role in translating design drawings into accurate, well-executed cabling installations while maintaining proper change control and documentation.</p>
<p>You’re also welcome to work on-site at our office in <strong>Amsterdam</strong>, <strong>the Netherlands.&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></p>

</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1697" data-local-id="206e8240cd48">Coordinate with logistics teams and vendors to ensure timely delivery, handling, and staging of cabling materials, including large palletized deliveries.</p>
