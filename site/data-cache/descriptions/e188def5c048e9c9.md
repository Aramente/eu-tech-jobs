<p><strong>About the role<br></strong></p>
<p>SumUp is looking for an <strong>Endpoint &amp; MDM Manager</strong> to join our Corporate IT team. In this role, you will be the technical lead and architect for our global device landscape, acting as the bridge between high-level security requirements and day-to-day operations. Your mission is to transform our high-volume environment into a "silent" operation where every device is compliant, tracked, and managed without manual intervention.&nbsp; You will manage a fleet of 3,800+ devices across multiple regions, with a roughly 2:1 MacBook to Windows split, requiring deep fluency in both ecosystems.</p>
<p>You will join a global team consisting of System Administrators, and external admins across the EU and the Americas, reporting to the Head of Global IT. You will build a team, create detailed documentation, and provide strategic training to the wider global team.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct a comprehensive audit of our current Azure/Entra setup and lead the "clean up" to ensure our security and compliance posture is top-tier.</li>
<li>Review and manage our Zero-touch deployment strategy using ABM, DEP, and Windows Autopilot to ensure a perfect first-day experience for every new hire.</li>
<li>Drive the adoption of AI-powered tooling and automation to reduce manual device management toil and scale the team's operational output.</li>
<li>Partner closely with the Systems Engineering function to ensure device compliance policies and identity controls are aligned, with clear ownership boundaries between endpoint and identity.</li>
<li>Solve the complex puzzle of hardware/software lifecycles by integrating the CMDB with our Service Desk, bringing automated asset recovery and sustainable e-wasting to life.</li>
<li>Act as the primary stakeholder for Security and People teams, ensuring 100% device compliance and frictionless onboarding/offboarding.</li>
<li>Determine if and when our infrastructure is ready for its next evolution, managing migrations from current setups to specialized tools like Kandji (Iru).</li>
<li>Collaborate with Finance and Procurement on hardware budgets, leasing contracts, and asset lifecycle costs.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You’ll be a great fit for this role if</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You have 5+ years of experience in a Senior IT Operations or Systems Engineering role, with a track record of acting as a technical lead or mentor.</li>
<li>You are an expert in Azure and Entra ID, with a deep understanding of managing identity and devices within the Microsoft ecosystem.</li>
<li>You have mastered Zero-Touch Deployment and have proven experience architecting solutions with Windows Autopilot and Apple Business Manager.</li>
<li>You have successfully navigated MDM migrations, specifically moving fleets to specialized tools like Kandji or Jamf.</li>
<li>You possess expert-level knowledge of both macOS and Windows 10/11 troubleshooting and configuration.</li>
<li>You have a "Systems-Thinking" mindset, preferring to build automated solutions rather than applying "band-aid" fixes to root causes.</li>
<li>Comfortable with scripting and automation (Bash, PowerShell, or Python) - you don't need to be a developer but you should be able to build and maintain your own tooling.</li>
<li>You are energized by "cleaning up": You aren't looking for a mature, fully documented fleet and ideally want to be the one to build the global blueprint for how SumUp scales.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to have</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience with specialized Mac MDM tools like Kandji, Jamf, or Mosyle.</li>
<li>Experience in Fintech or regulated environments (understanding SOC2 or ISO compliance).</li>
<li>Experience negotiating and managing contracts with international hardware and leasing partners.</li>
</ul>
