<h4><strong>Job Overview:</strong></h4>
<p>We are seeking a highly skilled Frontline Senior Technical Solutions Engineer with over 10 years of experience to join our Platform Support team. This role is pivotal in delivering exceptional support for our Databricks Data Intelligence platform, addressing complex technical challenges, and ensuring the seamless operation of our data solutions. As a frontline engineer, you will be the primary point of contact for critical issues, working closely with both internal teams and customers to resolve high-impact problems and drive platform improvements.</p>
<h4><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Frontline Support:</strong>&nbsp;Serve as the primary technical point of contact for escalated issues related to the Databricks Data Intelligence platform. Provide expert-level troubleshooting, diagnostics, and resolution for complex problems affecting system performance and reliability.</li>
<li><strong>Customer Interaction:</strong>&nbsp;Engage with customers directly to understand their technical issues and requirements. Provide timely, clear, and actionable solutions to ensure high levels of customer satisfaction.</li>
<li><strong>Incident Management:</strong>&nbsp;Lead the resolution of high-priority incidents, coordinating with various teams to address and mitigate issues swiftly. Conduct thorough root cause analyses and develop preventive measures to avoid recurrence.</li>
<li><strong>Collaboration:</strong>&nbsp;Work closely with engineering, product management, and DevOps teams to share insights, identify recurring issues, and drive improvements to the Databricks Data Intelligence platform.</li>
<li><strong>Documentation and Knowledge Sharing:</strong>&nbsp;Create and maintain detailed documentation on support procedures, known issues, and solutions. Contribute to internal knowledge bases and create training materials to assist other support engineers.</li>
<li><strong>Performance Monitoring:</strong>&nbsp;Monitor and analyze platform performance metrics to identify potential issues before they impact customers. Implement optimizations and enhancements to improve platform stability and efficiency.</li>
<li><strong>Platform Upgrades:</strong>&nbsp;Manage and oversee the deployment of Databricks Data Intelligence platform upgrades and patches, ensuring minimal disruption to services and maintaining system integrity.</li>
<li><strong>Innovation and Improvement:</strong>&nbsp;Stay abreast of industry trends and advancements in Databricks technology. Propose and drive initiatives to enhance platform capabilities and support processes.</li>
<li><strong>Customer Feedback:</strong>&nbsp;Collect and analyze customer feedback to drive continuous improvement in support processes and platform features.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Qualifications:</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Experience:</strong>&nbsp;Minimum of 8 years of hands-on experience in a technical support or engineering role related to Databricks Data Intelligence platform, cloud data platforms, or big data technologies.</li>

<li><strong>Customer Focus:</strong>&nbsp;Proven experience in managing high-impact customer interactions and ensuring a positive customer experience.</li>
