<p>The internet does not have a content problem. It has a judgment problem. And with the rise of AI, the cost of publishing has collapsed while the cost of trust has only increased. <p>Pulley is looking for a Senior Content Program Strategist to define and mature our content system. This person will bring a deliberate, principled, narrative-driven approach to what we create - ensuring our content is concise, differentiated, and worthy of the founders and teams we serve.</p>
<h3><strong>🚂</strong> About Pulley</h3>
<p>Pulley’s mission is to make it easier for anyone to start a company. We believe that more startups should exist, and that founder-led companies are more successful over the long term. Starting a company is hard enough. Managing equity shouldn’t be.</p>
<p>Pulley helps founders and growing teams understand, manage, and optimize their equity with modern cap table management tools built for today’s startups. Pulley is growing quickly with over 8,000 customers including startups like Linear, Runway, Fathom, and Roam. We’re backed by top investors including Founders’ Fund, Stripe, General Catalyst, Caffeinated Capital, 8vc, Elad Gil, among other great angels.</p>
<p>We’re a remote-first company and we’re building a thoughtful, high-performing marketing organization that values first-principles thinking, experimentation, good judgment, and performance. We care deeply about the quality of what we ship, not just the volume - and we’re excited to work with people who feel the same way.</p>
<h3>🌟&nbsp;About the Role</h3>
<p>Pulley is looking for a senior content program strategist to own and grow our content program across formats, segments, and channels. This role requires strong storytelling instincts, strategic rigor, and sharp editorial judgment. You’ll shape our narrative through <em>what</em> we create, <em>why</em> we create it, and <em>how</em> it shows up in the world.</p>
<p>This role is responsible for building a deliberate, high-signal content system that compounds over time. You’ll define our core content pillars, guide the work produced by our content agency, and ensure everything we publish is intentional, differentiated, and genuinely useful to founders and growing teams. You’ll also steward Pulley’s overarching narrative, ensuring consistency and coherence across touchpoints.</p>
<p>Content doesn’t end at publication. This role owns distribution strategy - determining where content lives, how it’s adapted across formats and channels, and how its impact is measured across audiences. Distribution should be considered at the brief stage, not retrofitted after the fact. You’ll use both data and qualitative feedback to refine strategy over time while maintaining a high bar for clarity and taste.</p>
<p>This is a senior individual contributor role with significant ownership and influence. You’ll collaborate closely with Growth, Product Marketing, Design, and external partners, and you’ll be trusted to exercise judgment - knowing when to push, when to cut, and what to build from scratch.</p>

<h4>Company Narrative &amp; Story Stewardship</h4>
<ul>

<li>Maintain narrative consistency across touchpoints, ensuring that what we publish reinforces who we are and how we think - internally and externally.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Content Strategy &amp; Pillars</h4>
<ul>
<li>Own Pulley’s content strategy across segments and formats, defining clear content pillars and themes that guide what we create and for who.</li>
<li>Translate company priorities, product strategy, and customer insights into a cohesive, long-term content program that compounds over time.</li>
<li>Regularly assess our content portfolio to ensure it remains focused, differentiated, and aligned with Pulley’s positioning.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Editorial Direction &amp; Agency Management</h4>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the strategic and editorial lead for our content agency, setting direction, providing thoughtful feedback, and holding a high bar for clarity, usefulness, and differentiation.</li>
<li>Occasionally produce or heavily shape high-impact pieces directly when strong judgment or domain nuance is required.</li>
<li>Provide thoughtful editorial feedback on long-form and short-form content, ensuring consistency in voice, narrative, and point of view.</li>
<li>Ensure our voice remains consistent, confident, and aligned with Pulley’s values.</li>
</ul>

<li>Ensure content is designed for modern discovery, including traditional search and AI-driven retrieval, applying evolving search and AI trends thoughtfully rather than reactively.</li>
<li>Extend core ideas into multiple formats (short-form written, visual, video, or audio) in partnership with design and external collaborators to maximize reach and resonance.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to responsibly leverage AI tools and automation to enhance content production, distribution, and discoverability.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Measurement, Learning &amp; Iteration</h4>
<ul>
<li>Define success metrics for different content types and audiences, balancing qualitative and quantitative signals.</li>
<li>Analyze performance data to understand what’s resonating and what’s not. You should be able to use those insights to improve strategy over time.</li>
<li>Share learnings and recommendations with the broader Growth and Product Marketing teams to inform future bets.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Cross-Functional Collaboration</h4>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Growth, Product Marketing, Design, and other partners to ensure content supports broader go-to-market efforts.</li>
<li>Help translate complex topics into clear, compelling narratives that are accessible to founders and operators.</li>
<li>Act as a thoughtful steward of Pulley’s voice, advocating for clarity, restraint, and good judgment across the organization.</li>
</ul>
