**OUR STORY:**

🇪🇺 Join Scaleway and shape the sovereign cloud of tomorrow !

Since 1999, we have been designing secure, sustainable infrastructures aimed at supporting the most ambitious companies.

Historically known for our dedicated servers (Dedibox), we made a strategic shift to cloud computing in 2015. Staying true to our principles of simplicity, flexibility, and technical excellence, we have become one of the leading players in Europe in the sector.

With the rise of artificial intelligence, we have strengthened our commitment, supported by the Iliad Group, which is investing €3 billion to develop a serious, sovereign AI alternative to American and Asian giants.

Every day, thanks to our fast-growing portfolio of cloud and AI products (bare metal, containerization, serverless, AI, etc.), Scaleway proudly serves thousands of customer across the private and public sector, from corporations like France Télévisions or Hachette Livre, to fast-growing startups like Photoroom and Biolevate, to institutions like the City of Copenhagen.

📍 Our offices are located in **Paris, Lille, Toulouse, Rennes, Rouen, Bordeaux and Lyon.**

**WHY WE NEED YOU ?**

Our growth is driving us to strengthen our **Network Products Engineering team** to support the development of critical connectivity services.

Your mission will be **to design, build, and improve scalable network connectivity products** in order to **deliver reliable and high-performance networking capabilities to our cloud customers**.

**YOUR FUTURE TEAM**

We work in a collaborative and international environment where the diversity of Scalers, combined with a spirit of sharing, helps bring new projects to life every day, advancing our ambitions together.

You will be part of a **team of 7 engineers**, distributed across France and working mostly remotely. The team is responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining **Scaleway’s connectivity products**, including solutions such as **InterLink, Site-to-Site VPN, and Public Gateway**. These products are critical components of our cloud infrastructure and are continuously evolving.

The team brings together engineers with **junior to senior profiles**, working collaboratively on complex networking and system programming challenges. Developers often collaborate in pairs on key topics, and knowledge sharing is encouraged through open discussions, optional pair programming, and continuous learning initiatives.

You will also work closely with other teams across Scaleway, including **product, documentation, design, frontend, devtools, and support**, ensuring that our products provide the best possible experience for customers.

Manager information: **Charles-Henri GUERIN**

**YOUR DAILY ROUTINE**

Develop and maintain backend services for Scaleway’s network connectivity products

Design and implement **gRPC APIs in Rust** for cloud networking services

Work on features related to **InterLink, Site-to-Site VPN, and Public Gateway**

Debug complex networking or distributed systems issues

Investigate and resolve **customer-related technical incidents** in collaboration with the support team

Participate in architectural and system design discussions

Contribute to improving the reliability and performance of networking services

Collaborate with teammates and other departments to deliver high-quality products

Participate in team rituals such as roadmap reviews, weekly retrospectives, and technical discussions

Contribute to experimentation, proof-of-concepts, and continuous learning initiatives

**ABOUT YOU**

**HARDSKILLS**

Strong experience with **Rust development**

Experience designing or developing **gRPC APIs**

Solid knowledge of **networking fundamentals** (TCP, IP, UDP, BGP)

Experience working with **PostgreSQL or relational databases**

Familiarity with **VPN technologies such as IPSec**

Experience with networking tools or stacks such as **FRR, GoBGP, or similar**

Experience building or contributing to **network infrastructure products**

SOFTSKILLS

Strong collaboration mindset and team spirit

Curiosity and willingness to explore complex technical topics

Good written and verbal communication skills

Ability to work autonomously and take ownership of tasks

Open-minded attitude and willingness to learn outside your comfort zone
