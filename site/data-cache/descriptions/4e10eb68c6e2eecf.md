<div class="content-intro"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>About Faire</strong></span></p>
<p>Faire is a technology wholesale platform built on the belief that the future is local. Independent retailers around the globe collectively represent a multi-hundred-billion-dollar wholesale market that has historically been fragmented and offline. At Faire, we're using the power of tech, data, and machine learning to connect this thriving community of entrepreneurs across the globe. Picture your favorite boutique in town — we help them discover the best products from around the world to sell in their stores. With the right tools and insights, we believe that we can level the playing field so businesses can grow and local communities can thrive.</p>
<p>We’re looking for smart, resourceful and passionate people to join us as we power the shop local movement. If you believe in community, come join ours.</p></div><p><strong>About this role</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for a Strategy &amp; Operations Senior Associate to play a pivotal role in driving informed, data-backed decisions across our international business. In this role you’ll leverage a unique combination of skills, including analytical rigor, strategic intuition, and execution, to drive growth for our business.&nbsp;</p>
<p>As a core member of our International Strategy team, you’ll have the chance to lead mission-critical projects end to end. You’ll partner cross-functionally with Product, Sales, Operations, Marketing, and Finance teams to inform the strategic roadmap by generating insights and helping translate them into action.</p>
<p>This is an ideal role for someone who wants to work as a high-impact individual contributor in a fast-paced, highly collaborative, and data-driven environment and have the opportunity to gain exposure to partnering with a variety of functions.</p>
<p>This role is based out of our London, UK office (3 days in the office)</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Break down complex and ambiguous problems to uncover actionable insights</li>
<li>Lead large cross-functional strategic initiatives that move company metrics in partnership with Product, Sales, Finance, Operations, Marketing, etc.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities, develop hypotheses, execute in-depth analysis, and make recommendations to Faire’s leadership team based on your findings</li>
<li>Provide thought partnership to cross-functional teammates to ensure the organization is using data thoughtfully and correctly</li>
<li>You will be required to regularly demonstrate the ability to exercise independent discretion in analytical rigor, strategic intuition and execution.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Must possess a bachelor's degree</li>
<li>3+ years experience in analytical, financial or strategic roles&nbsp;</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with SQL is a strong plus</li>
<li>Analytical rigor: ability to work with data, customer feedback, and team input to rapidly dig to the root, draw insights, and synthesize the answer.</li>
<li>Strategic intuition: understanding of customer, product, business, and financial foundations to quickly narrow in on only the most important variables.</li>
