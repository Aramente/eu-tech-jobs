<p><strong>We’re particularly interested in engineers who have:</strong></p>

<h2><strong>What will I be doing?</strong></h2>
<p>As a Senior AI Infrastructure Engineer focused on model training and inference, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Implement and scale <strong>training pipelines</strong> for large transformer and LLM models, from data ingestion and preprocessing through distributed training and evaluation.</li>
<li>Build and optimize <strong>inference services</strong> that deliver low‑latency, high‑reliability experiences for our customers, including autoscaling, routing, and fallbacks.</li>
<li>Work on <strong>GPU‑level performance</strong>: tuning kernels, improving utilization, and identifying bottlenecks across our training and inference stack.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with ML scientists to implement cutting edge training and inference methods and bring them to production.</li>
<li>Play an active role in <strong>hiring, mentoring, and developing</strong> other engineers on the team.</li>
<li>Raise the bar for <strong>technical standards, reliability, and operational excellence</strong> across Intercom’s AI platform.</li>
</ul>
