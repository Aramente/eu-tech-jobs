<p>This position will support front office treasury activities within Nebius Group, with a focus on execution of FX transactions, liquidity management, investments, market analyses, banking products and strategic Treasury projects. This role provides strong exposure to financial markets, banking relationships, and treasury systems, while building core treasury expertise in a dynamic international environment.</p>
<p><strong><span data-contrast="auto"><span data-ccp-charstyle="Strong">Your responsibilities will include:</span></span></strong><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:true,&quot;134233118&quot;:true}">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong>Cash &amp; Liquidity Management</strong><br>• Support daily cash positioning and liquidity monitoring across global accounts<br>• Assist in execution of funding, intercompany loans, and cash pooling activities<br>• Monitor bank balances and ensure optimal utilization of available liquidity<br>• Support short-term cash forecasting and variance analysis</p>
<p><strong>FX Dealing &amp; Front Office Execution</strong><br>• Assist in execution of FX trades (spot and forwards) under supervision<br>• Monitor FX exposures and support hedge execution processes<br>• Maintain trade documentation and ensure accurate deal capture in systems<br>• Track market movements and provide basic FX analysis</p>
<p><strong>Financial Products</strong><br>• In-depth knowledge of various banking products, including guarantees, money market funds (MMFs), investment/funding solutions, corporate debt, and corporate cards.<br>• Understanding of the legal documentation related to the financial products.<br>• Monitoring and analyzing markets, competitors, and (existing) covenants.</p>
<p><strong>Banking &amp; Counterparty Management</strong><br>• Support communication with banks, FX counterparties, and internal stakeholders<br>• Assist in onboarding of new bank accounts and maintenance of signatories<br>• Ensure timely settlement and confirmation of treasury transactions</p>
<p><strong>Treasury Systems &amp; Operations</strong><br>• Execute transactions and maintain data integrity in Kyriba and banking platforms<br>• Support reconciliation of treasury transactions and cash balances<br>• Assist in improving treasury processes and automation initiatives</p>
<p><strong>Reporting &amp; Controls</strong><br>• Prepare daily and weekly treasury reports (cash, liquidity, FX positions)<br>• Ensure compliance with internal controls and treasury policies<br>• Support audit requests and documentation</p>
