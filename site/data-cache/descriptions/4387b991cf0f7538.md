<h3><strong>About Iso</strong></h3>
<p>Isomorphic Labs (IsoLabs) was launched in 2021 to advance human health by building on and beyond the Nobel-winning AlphaFold system. Since then, our interdisciplinary team of drug discovery experts and machine learning specialists has built powerful new predictive and generative AI models that accelerate scientific discovery at digital speed.</p>
<p>Our name comes from the belief that there is an underlying symmetry between biology and information science. By harnessing AI’s powerful capabilities, we can use it to model complex biological phenomena to help design novel molecules, anticipate how drugs will perform and develop innovative medicines to treat and cure some of the world’s most devastating diseases.</p>
<p>We have built a world-leading drug design engine comprising AI models that are capable of working across multiple therapeutic areas and drug modalities. We are continually innovating on model architecture and developing cutting-edge capabilities to advance rational drug design.</p>
<p>Every day, and with each new breakthrough, we’re getting closer to the promise of digital biology, and achieving our ambitious mission to one day solve all disease with the help of AI.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div><h3><strong>Your impact&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<p>As part of the ongoing expansion of our Drug Design and Medical Research function, we are recruiting a CRO manager to work alongside our world leading AI/ML Drug Design Platform teams. We don’t currently have wet labs, so you will directly contribute to our drug discovery projects by collaborating with internal stakeholders and being the primary point of contact for CRO relationships and deliverables. As a member of the Drug Discovery team you will be responsible for managing our CRO network, negotiating contracts, establishing working practices and optimising processes to speed up our Design-Make-Test-Analyse cycle.&nbsp;If you are a skilled and ambitious individual looking to take the next step in your career, joining Isomorphic Labs will lift you out of your comfort zone and provide you with an amazing opportunity for personal growth and development.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>What you will do&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Drive the creation and implementation of a CRO management system and create effective ways of working.</li>
<li>Manage vendor relationships, monitoring efficiency and effectiveness of projects, and holding CROs accountable for deliverables.</li>
<li>Be involved in selecting new CROs where appropriate, based on your knowledge of the market as well as the criteria that you will set out in partnership with drug discovery project team members.</li>
<li>Work with Project Leads, Legal, Finance and Project Management to draw up new contracts, including working through the various administrative elements and vendor assessments.</li>
<li>Assist in developing and implementing tools to simplify internal processes and improve user experience.</li>
</ul>
