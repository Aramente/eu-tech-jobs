## About tonies:

tonies is the globally leading interactive audio platform for children with more than 10 million<br>Tonieboxes and 125 million Tonies sold. The intuitive and award-winning audio system has<br>changed the way young children play and learn independently with its child-safe, wireless,<br>and screen-free approach. Tonieboxes have been activated in over 100 countries, the content<br>portfolio includes around 1,400 Tonies in several languages. Since its inception, tonies has<br>achieved significant milestones, including listing on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange in<br>November 2021 as well as a globally present team of over 550 dedicated professionals<br>operating from various countries.<br><br>Joining our tonies team means being part of an inspiring mission to create innovative and<br>meaningful experiences for children around the world. Together, we’re shaping the future of<br>play, learning, and imagination.
