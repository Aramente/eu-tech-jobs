<h3><strong>About the role</strong></h3>
<p>The Starburst <strong>Partner Solution Architect – Dell Alliance</strong> is a trusted technical advisor responsible for enabling, growing, and scaling Starburst’s strategic partnership with <strong>Dell Technologies</strong>. In this highly technical, partner-facing role, you will work closely with Dell technical teams, alliance stakeholders, and field sellers to design, validate, and deliver joint solutions that showcase the combined value of Starburst and the Dell technology portfolio.</p>
<p>You will play a critical role in driving technical alignment across Dell infrastructure, data, and analytics platforms (e.g., PowerScale, ECS, ObjectScale, Dell APEX, on-prem and hybrid architectures), supporting co-selling motions, and accelerating customer adoption through validated architectures and joint go-to-market execution.</p>
<p>You will work cross-functionally with Starburst Partner Sales, Product, Engineering, and Marketing teams to ensure the Dell partnership is technically differentiated, field-ready, and scalable — from enablement and solution development to customer deployments and reference wins.</p>
<h3><strong>As a Partner Solution Architect – Dell Alliance, you will:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the <strong>primary technical owner of the Dell partnership</strong>, acting as the day-to-day technical liaison between Starburst and Dell solution architects, engineers, and alliance teams.<br><br></li>
<li>Enable Dell field and partner teams to successfully position, demonstrate, and deploy Starburst solutions on Dell infrastructure and platforms.<br><br></li>
<li>Design, build, and maintain <strong>joint Dell + Starburst solutions</strong>, including reference architectures, validated designs, demos, integrations, and deployment blueprints.<br><br></li>
<li>Collaborate with Dell and Starburst partner sales teams to support <strong>co-selling opportunities</strong>, providing technical strategy and architectural guidance for joint customer engagements.<br><br></li>
<li>Lead technical enablement sessions, hands-on workshops, and deep-dive trainings for Dell architects, engineers, and field sellers.<br><br></li>
<li>Drive and support <strong>proof-of-concept (POC)</strong> and pilot engagements with Dell to validate joint solutions and accelerate enterprise adoption.<br><br></li>
<li>Act as the <strong>technical voice of Dell within Starburst</strong>, advocating for integration enhancements, deployment optimizations, and roadmap alignment.<br><br></li>
<li>Work closely with Starburst Engineering, Product Management, and Support teams to resolve Dell-specific technical challenges and influence joint solution roadmaps.<br><br></li>
<li>Develop and publish technical content such as solution guides, best practices, reference architectures, and deployment patterns tailored to Dell customers.<br><br></li>
<li>Represent Starburst as a technical thought leader at <strong>Dell events, joint field engagements, conferences, and alliance initiatives</strong>.<br><br></li>
</ul>
