<h1><strong>Business Process Manager</strong></h1>
<p>📍 Barcelona, Spain or Berlin, Germany | Office-first setup</p>
<h2><strong>About the team</strong></h2>
<p>The Business Architecture team sits at the heart of SumUp’s Global Operations, ensuring that our processes, systems and ways of working scale effectively as we grow.</p>
<p>We connect strategy with execution, designing and governing workflows that power consistent, high-quality customer experiences across markets.</p>
<p>As a Business Process Manager, you’ll play a key role in shaping how operations run end-to-end.</p>
<p>You’ll work across teams to bring structure to complexity, turning data, insights, and system understanding into scalable process solutions.</p>
<p>This is an opportunity to step into a high-impact role where you’ll not only improve processes, but also help define how process excellence and governance are built at scale.</p>
<h2>&nbsp;</h2>
<h2><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li data-section-id="wsk591" data-start="44" data-end="150">Own and lead end-to-end process improvement initiatives, from scoping to implementation and monitoring</li>
<li data-section-id="l203dp" data-start="151" data-end="252">Map and optimise business processes using BPMN, identifying inefficiencies and evaluating changes</li>
<li data-section-id="12kenkv" data-start="253" data-end="360">Analyse multi-source data to uncover root causes and translate insights into actionable recommendations</li>
<li data-section-id="3hucjx" data-start="361" data-end="464">Define business requirements and ensure alignment between processes, systems, and operational goals</li>
<li data-section-id="1shc2v9" data-start="465" data-end="600" data-is-last-node="">Collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders, contributing to governance frameworks and driving continuous improvement and adoption</li>
</ul>
