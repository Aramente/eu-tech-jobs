<p id="isPasted"><strong>Job Summary:<br></strong></p>
<p>The Operations Manager is responsible for overseeing and optimizing the day-to-day operations of the organization to ensure efficiency, productivity, and profitability. This role involves managing their departments’ local P&amp;L statements and KPIs, developing and implementing strategic initiatives that align with company objectives, and leading field operations teams. The Operations Manager will play a key role in ensuring operational efficiency, safety, quality, and customer satisfaction.</p>
<p><strong>Supervisory Responsibilities:<br></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Supervises the Field Service Manager, Project Scheduler, Project Coordinator, Project and Assistant Project Managers, and Billing Specialist.</li>
<li>Manages the department's financial budget, merit, and reviews.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Duties/Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<p>Leadership and Team Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and develop a high-performing operations team.</li>
<li>Provide mentorship, training, and performance evaluations for team members.</li>
</ul>
<p>Operational Planning and Execution</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop best practices and strategic initiatives for future growth.</li>
<li>Oversee the implementation and execution of plans from start to finish, including schedules, resource allocation, and workflow management.</li>
<li>Ensure timely and efficient project completion.</li>
</ul>
<p>Quality Assurance and Compliance</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain and enforce quality standards and procedures.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with industry regulations and safety standards.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Conduct regular audits and inspections to identify areas for improvement.</li>
<li>Manage department fleet and test equipment needs and condition.</li>
</ul>
<p>Budget and Financial Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage operational budgets and control costs.</li>
<li>Monitor financial performance and make data-driven decisions to achieve targets.</li>
<li data-pasted="true">Monitor equipment rental costs and develop CAPEX requests.</li>
<li>Manage billing, local P&amp;L, and change orders.</li>
</ul>
<p>Process Improvement</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with teams to achieve initiatives and improve quality and efficiency.</li>
<li>Resolve issues promptly and implement process improvements.</li>
<li>Monitor key performance indicators and drive continuous improvement initiatives.</li>
</ul>
<p>Safety Management</p>
<ul>
<li>Promote a culture of safety and develop and enforce safety policies and procedures.</li>
<li>Collaborate with safety managers to address safety incidents promptly and implement preventive measures.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with safety regulations and standards.</li>
</ul>
<p>Additional responsibilities and tasks as required to support the organization's goals and adapt to evolving business needs.</p>
