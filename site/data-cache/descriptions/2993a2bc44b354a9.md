<p><strong>The Role</strong></p>
<p>As a Senior Security Engineer, you will lead the design and implementation of security tooling, harden our cloud and IT infrastructure, and serve as a key responder to incidents. You will work across a globally distributed team and partner closely with Engineering to manage AI-enabled risk.<br><br><strong>Key Responsibilities<br></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead security and IT projects to enhance the security posture of infrastructure and the company</li>
<li>Detect, investigate, and remediate security incidents using SIEM, EDR, and SOAR tooling (e.g. CrowdStrike, SentinelOne)</li>
<li>Conduct threat hunting across cloud and endpoint environments using a Zero Trust framework</li>
<li>Administer company-wide IAM and security monitoring infrastructure</li>
<li>Contribute to security policies, incident response, forensics, and endpoint management programmes</li>
<li>Partner with Engineering to assess and mitigate risks from AI-enabled tools and workflows</li>
<li>Explore and implement AI/automation in detection, analysis, and response pipelines</li>
<li>Engage closely with North American and international staff across time zones</li>
</ul>
