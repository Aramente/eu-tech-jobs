<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p>We are seeking a <strong>hands-on Marketing Manager</strong> to drive our B2B growth in Israel. This role requires connecting strategy to execution, transforming ideas into practical assets, and generating business impact through lead conversion. The role reports directly to the <strong>Head of Marketing, Israel</strong>.</p>

<ul>
<li>Define the local marketing strategy, translating goals into clear positioning, messaging, and go-to-market plans, with a focus on hands-on execution.</li>
<li>Drive <strong>full-funnel B2B marketing</strong>, from market awareness and lead generation to product adoption across Israeli companies.</li>
<li>Establish <strong>Wolt Benefits</strong> as a trusted brand within the <strong>HR community in Israel</strong> through strategic campaigns, partnerships, and targeted content.</li>

<li>Manage relationships with key <strong>agencies, suppliers, and partners</strong> for efficient execution.</li>
</ul>
