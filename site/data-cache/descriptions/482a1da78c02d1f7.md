<h3>About Life360</h3>
<p>Life360’s mission is to keep people close to the ones they love. Our category-leading mobile app and Tile tracking devices empower members to protect the people, pets, and things they care about most with a range of services, including location sharing, safe driver reports, and crash detection with emergency dispatch. Life360 serves approximately 88 million monthly active users (MAU) across more than 150 countries.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Life360 delivers peace of mind and enhances everyday family life with seamless coordination for all the moments that matter, big and small. By continuing to innovate and deliver for our customers, we have become a household name and the must-have mobile-based membership for families (and those friends that basically are family).&nbsp;</p>
<p>Life360 has more than 500 (and growing!) remote-first employees. For more information, please visit <a href="http://life360.com/">life360.com</a>.</p>

<h3>About the Role</h3>
<p>As we grow Life360 Internationally we are looking to add an experienced Lifecycle Marketing Manager to support our expansion. With proven success in the US, Canada, United Kingdom and Australia, a strong International user base and a compelling value proposition around family safety, we are continuing to build out our international team to support our growth.</p>
<p>We are looking for a technically skilled LCM Marketing Manager who will report to the LATAM Integrated Marketing lead and will take a leading role in defining the strategy and execution of Lifecycle marketing campaigns that help build awareness, acquire, engage, and retain Life360 members in our LATAM region, with the focus in Spanish, Portuguese and English. You will own upsell &amp; engagement comms in our priority LATAM markets as well as playing a key role in launching new market initiatives and bringing new products to our international members.&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>This role can be based in London, with a hybrid model of remote and in-person/office meetings (2 -3 days a month), or remotely from Mexico.&nbsp;</em></p>
<p><em>The UK-based salary range for this position is 81,500 to 97,000.Additionally we offer equity.&nbsp; We take into consideration an individual's background and experience in determining final salary - therefore, base pay offered may vary considerably depending on job-related knowledge, skills, and experience.</em></p>
<h3>What You’ll Do</h3>
<h4>Primary responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Structure the first regional LCM plan for LATAM with a strategic opportunity to drive impact and scale.</li>
<li>Be well versed in building email, in-app, and push notification comms on our Braze marketing engagement platform</li>
<li>Manage the end-to-end cross-channel (email, push, in-app messages) marketing campaign process from concepting, brief kickoffs, design advisory, translation/localisation and targeting strategy</li>
<li>Work with the CRM/Lifecycle Marketing team to run tests that help define content, cadence, and channels that optimise for customer engagement and retention</li>
<li>Define best practices for International LCM infrastructure including segmentation, translations, channel performance and the implementation of new channels</li>
<li>QA campaigns with test engineering and marketing QA teams</li>
<li>Aggregate, summarise, and share campaign results that have clear insights and takeaways</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product Managers, Marketers, and Engineering to implement Lifecycle marketing plans and any required technical changes on our marketing platform (Braze) for seamless campaigns</li>
<li>Partner with stakeholders in Product, Creative, Testing, Legal, Finance and more to support our international marketing roadmap</li>
</ul>
