**About the Role**

AI agents are becoming central to how CaptivateIQ delivers value — from automating complex commission workflows to enabling customers to interact with their data through natural language. We're looking for a \*\*Senior Engineering Manager\*\* to own our AI agent platform and inform the strategic vision for how AI capabilities are adopted across the product.

This is not a pure execution role. You'll be an important voice in defining CaptivateIQ's AI strategy — partnering directly with Product leadership and the executive team to shape the company's AI roadmap and setting a technical vision for the AI platform that balances near-term product impact with long-term investment. You'll be accountable for translating that vision into shipped capabilities that move business metrics.

You'll lead the \*\*AI Platform\*\* team — responsible for our internal agent SDK, LLM orchestration layer, and the evaluation and observability infrastructure that underpins it all. Beyond your team's direct output, you'll serve as the senior technical voice for AI across the engineering organization, driving thought leadership on how every pillar adopts and integrates AI capabilities into their products. As the senior engineering leader for AI at CaptivateIQ, you'll have outsized influence on both the technology and the culture of how we build with AI.
