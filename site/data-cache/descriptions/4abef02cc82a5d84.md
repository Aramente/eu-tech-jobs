<h2>About the Role</h2>
<p>As a Large Scale Training Engineer, you will play a key role in enhancing the training throughput of our internal framework and enabling researchers to pioneer new model concepts. This role demands excellent engineering skills for designing, implementing, and optimizing cutting-edge AI models, alongside writing robust machine learning code and understanding supercomputer performance deeply. Your expertise in performance optimization, understanding distributed systems, and bug elimination will be crucial, as our framework supports extensive computations across numerous virtual machines.</p>
<p>This role is designed for individuals who are not only technically proficient but also deeply passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI and machine learning through innovative engineering and collaborative research.</p>

<ul>
<li>Profile and optimize the training process to ensure efficiency and effectiveness, including optimizing multimodal data pipelines and data storage methods.</li>
<li>Develop high-performance TPU/GPU/CPU kernels and integrate advanced techniques into our training framework to maximize hardware efficiency.</li>
<li>Utilize knowledge of hardware features to make aggressive optimizations and advise on hardware/software co-designs.</li>
<li>Collaboratively develop model architectures with researchers that facilitate efficient training and inference.</li>

</ul>
