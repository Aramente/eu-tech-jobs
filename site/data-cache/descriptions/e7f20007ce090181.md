As MacPaw continues to grow internationally, the complexity of our tax landscape grows with it across jurisdictions, regulations, and evolving global standards. This creates both responsibility and opportunity: to ensure smart, future-proof tax processes that not only warrant compliance, but also furnish strategic business decisions.

We’re looking for a **Senior Tax and Reporting Specialist** to take ownership of key international tax and reporting areas within our Finance team. In this role, you’ll help shape our global tax framework, manage cross-border complexities, and turn structured financial data into clear insights that support decision-making at scale.

If you’re someone who combines deep tax expertise with a structured mindset, strong ownership, and a drive to improve processes, we’d love to meet you.

#### **In this role, you will:**

* Shape and evolve MacPaw’s Global Tax Policy, ensuring smart worldwide tax position while maintaining rigorous compliance.
* Proactively identify tax risks across complex transactions, operational changes, and cross-border activities, and turn them into well-managed, structured solutions.
* Partner with external tax advisors and auditors across the Group to align on key tax matters and drive high-quality, timely outcomes.
* Oversee Transfer Pricing documentation and ensure accuracy and alignment with global standards.
* Prepare and review Controlled Foreign Company (CFC) reports and other international tax documentation for Group entities.
* Contribute to financial statements and audits by preparing accurate tax disclosures and reviewing Group financial reports from a tax perspective.
* Build and maintain clear, insightful dashboards that translate complex tax and financial data into visual stories that support decision-making.
* Deliver ad hoc analytical reports by connecting data across multiple sources and uncovering meaningful insights.

**Skills you’ll need to bring:**

* **4+ years of experience** in Taxation and International Accounting, preferably within a product IT company.
* Strong knowledge of international taxation with hands-on experience across multiple jurisdictions (US, UA, CY, UK).
* Solid understanding of IFRS and US GAAP reporting requirements.
* Proven experience working with large volumes of financial data and the ability to see patterns, risks, and opportunities within complexity.
* Advanced Excel and Google Sheets skills, with confidence in building structured models and reports.
* High level of independence and ownership.
* Strong problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to effectively multitask across parallel priorities.
* Advanced (С1) English and strong communication skills to collaborate with internal stakeholders and external advisors.
* A Master’s degree in Accounting or Taxation.

**As a plus:**

* ACCA, CPA, or relevant tax certifications (completed or in progress).
* Experience working in Big4 or other leading advisory firms.
* Hands-on experience with Transfer Pricing documentation, Group Tax policies, and CFC reporting.
* Experience reviewing prior periods and managing open tax years in cooperation with external consultants.
