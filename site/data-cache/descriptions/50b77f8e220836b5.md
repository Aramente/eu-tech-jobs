<h2><strong>Job Description</strong></h2>
<p>As our Brand Manager, you will be the driving force behind bringing the HelloFresh brand strategy to life in fresh and creative ways across New Zealand. Reporting to the Associate Director Brand for Meal Kits ANZ, you’ll join a team of passionate marketers dedicated to changing the way people eat forever.</p>
<p>This is a hands-on, strategic role based in our Chippendale, Sydney HQ, that requires frequent travel across the ditch to work closely with our NZ-based team and local agency partners. If you’re an ambitious marketer who loves food and thrives in a fast-paced, global environment, we want to hear from you.</p>
<p><strong>You will...</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the strategic development and hands-on execution of the HelloFresh NZ brand plans.</li>
<li>Deliver a world-class, integrated brand marketing calendar, collaborating with creative, product, and growth teams locally and globally.</li>
<li>Become the authority on the New Zealand consumer and media landscape, identifying the local nuances that make the market unique.</li>
<li>Build and nurture high-performing relationships with external creative, PR, and media agencies to deliver insight-led campaigns.</li>
<li>Plan and manage the yearly NZ brand budget, ensuring all activity is delivered on time and provides a strong return on investment.</li>
<li>Work with Global Consumer Insights and data partners to evaluate campaign performance and report findings to C-level stakeholders.</li>
<li>Partner with CRM, Performance, and SEO teams to ensure brand consistency across every customer touchpoint.</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience briefing and managing creative and PR agencies to deliver high-quality, localized work.</li>

</ul>
