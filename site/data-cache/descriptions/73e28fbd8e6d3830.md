<h2><strong>Job Description</strong></h2>
<p>As a Human Resource Business Partner, you'll be a key strategic player, partnering with assigned business units to turn organisational goals into real-world people strategies. You’ll be critical in making sure HelloFresh ANZ has the best talent, a thriving culture, and the leadership capability needed to hit our commercial targets.</p>
<p>This role is based in Chippendale, Sydney and reporting to the Senior HR Business Partner.</p>
<p><strong>You will…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive business-aligned people strategies by immersing yourself in business unit operations and translating stakeholder KPIs into measurable HR interventions.</li>
<li>Own talent pipeline health by identifying critical roles, building succession plans, and leading internal mobility to prevent capability gaps ahead of the business roadmap.</li>
<li>Retain and develop top performers by partnering with managers on targeted retention plans and measuring impact through voluntary attrition rates.</li>
<li>Build leadership capability by coaching managers through complex people challenges—performance, conflict, and team dynamics—to build their own leadership muscle.</li>
<li>Deliver people analytics to business leadership by analysing retention, exit interview, and engagement data, presenting people metrics with the same rigour as commercial KPIs.</li>
<li>Act as a custodian of culture and values, embedding them into business processes and acting as an early-warning system for morale or cultural issues.</li>
<li>Manage employee relations matters by providing timely, consistent, and legally sound advice on performance management, misconduct, and grievances.</li>
<li>Lead and support change management, partnering with business leaders to plan and execute organisational changes with clear communication and support.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You have…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 3–5 years' experience in an HR business partnering or generalist role with a proven ability to influence senior stakeholders.</li>
<li>A tertiary qualification in Human Resources, Business, Psychology, or a related discipline (preferred).</li>
<li>Proven experience using people data and analytics to diagnose workforce trends and drive business decisions.</li>
<li>Strong coaching capability with experience in developing manager capability across performance, conflict, and leadership challenges.</li>
<li>Strong commercial acumen, demonstrating an ability to align HR activity to business unit goals and KPIs.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You are…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>A natural coach who empowers managers and builds capability across the business.</li>
<li>Data-driven and comfortable presenting people metrics to senior leadership.</li>
<li>A strategic and commercially focused partner who understands business operations.</li>
<li>Legally astute, providing consistent and compliant advice on employee relations matters.</li>
</ul>
