<h2><strong>Role background</strong></h2>
<p>Our Talent Acquisition team is now a global team of 130+ based across multiple European countries, Tokyo and Tel Aviv. We are open to considering top TA talent in any of the 32 countries where we do business!</p>
<p>As part of our phenomenal growth, we are now looking for an experienced Talent Acquisition Partner to join our talent acquisition team, responsible for finding exceptional talent across our general and administrative functions — G&amp;A.</p>
<p>You will bring extensive experience hiring corporate, commercial, and specialist talent across functions such as Finance, HR, Legal, Policy, Communications, and related G&amp;A areas. We are especially interested in talent acquisition professionals who have hired for big tech companies, high-growth technology businesses, or large enterprise corporate organisations, where they have supported complex stakeholder groups and high hiring-bar environments.</p>
<p>As a Talent Acquisition Partner, you’ll own the end-to-end recruitment process while managing relationships with your key stakeholders, always putting candidate experience and quality of hire at the heart of everything you do.</p>
<p>In this role, your primary focus will be end-to-end delivery for a defined portfolio of commercial G&amp;A roles. This is a hands-on delivery role, suited to someone who enjoys owning complex stakeholder relationships and driving hiring outcomes in a fast-moving environment.</p>
<h2><strong>What you'll be doing</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Be the project lead on recruitment in your assigned hiring teams and together plan a solid hiring roadmap; agree on processes and train interviewers where needed.</li>
<li>Provide guidance, understanding and insight to your hiring managers based on your experience, know-how, market understanding and well informed data points.</li>
<li>Build candidate pipelines that are relevant to your hiring teams.</li>
<li>Use data to make informed decisions and utilize talent acquisition tooling &amp; reporting to drive efficiencies in your recruiting funnel, increasing candidate pass-through ratios over time &amp; removing bottlenecks that impact KPI’s such as monthly fill rate, time to hire, cost per hire and more.</li>
<li>Invent new and innovative ways to attract the best people globally to join Wolt, while excelling at basic recruiting best practices.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Collaborate with your sub-team within the broader TA team and together share ideas and wins and help each other solve hiring related challenges</li>
<li>Partner with different talent teams, such as Employer Branding to drive optimal advertising, events, social media campaigns and other initiatives to promote opportunities within the department you support.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Our humble expectations</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>You have previous in-house recruitment experience, ideally gained within big tech, high-growth technology companies, or large enterprise corporate organisations.</li>
<li>You have a proven track record of hiring corporate, commercial, and specialist talent across G&amp;A functions such as Finance, HR, Legal, Policy, Communications, or similar.</li>
<li>You are confident partnering with senior hiring managers and business leaders, acting as a trusted advisor and leader of hiring processes.</li>
<li>You have experience navigating complex stakeholder groups, structured hiring processes, and high hiring-bar environments.</li>
<li>You are an advocate for candidate experience excellence and consider this in every decision you make.</li>
<li>You enjoy working in a fast-paced international environment and are comfortable collaborating with colleagues across the globe.</li>
<li>You are comfortable stepping into a fixed-term role and making an immediate impact, with minimal ramp-up time.</li>
<li>You are able to collaborate and proactively communicate clearly, both verbally and in written form, with various stakeholders in English.</li>
<li>You care about data and are accustomed to transparently sharing pipeline insights, funnel metrics, and other important data with your hiring teams.</li>
</ul>
