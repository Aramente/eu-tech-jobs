<h4><strong>Team Summary</strong></h4>
<p>Our Product Design team consists of 8 designers. We’re a user-centric, fast-paced and collaborative team that works closely with other design, product, and engineering teams. Alongside experienced designers and UX researchers, you will create scalable solutions that deliver value to customers worldwide.</p>
<h4><strong>Job Summary</strong></h4>
<p>To work on the design system, we are looking for a strong designer to bridge the gap between granular component design and the strategic scaling of our web ecosystem. You will be responsible for maintaining our design system as a high-velocity source of truth, ensuring documentation is both actionable and accessible to cross-functional teams.</p>
<p>Beyond component maintenance, you will promote the latest trends by integrating AI-driven workflows and automation into our standard practice. As an advocate for the system, you will mentor the team on best practices and drive the evolution of our design language to meet the company’s long-term needs.</p>
<p>This position is suitable for people with disabilities and veterans with relevant experience.</p>
<h4>Responsibilities and Duties</h4>
<ul>
<li>Create new components, collaborating with feature teams and design system developers on specs, structure, and rules</li>
<li>Make updates to existing components as needs arise</li>
<li>Utilize AI-integrated workflows to automate documentation and design system maintenance</li>
<li>Prepare component structure and usage documentation, as well as write information on&nbsp;paradigms, processes, and similar topics</li>
<li>Review design specs built by feature designers to assure they follow the most up-to-date&nbsp;patterns</li>
<li>Be a resource for design and non-design audiences to consult with for advice on&nbsp;accessibility</li>
<li>&nbsp;Work with teams to determine effective delivery dates of design system output&nbsp;</li>
<li>Utilize data and research to drive design decision-making&nbsp;</li>
<li>Onboard new teammates to the rules and processes of the design system&nbsp;</li>
<li>Write effectively to communicate and justify design decisions to stakeholders</li>
<li>Сreate and maintain processes for managing, scaling, and updating the design&nbsp;system, along with continuing to be an advocate for the system</li>
</ul>
