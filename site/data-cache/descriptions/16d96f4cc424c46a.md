<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Your Main Tasks Will Be</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and optimize Flo’s presence across AI-generated search environments (e.g. ChatGPT, Gemini), ensuring accurate, high-quality brand representation. Monitor how Flo is represented across AI systems, identify gaps or misrepresentation, and proactively act to correct them</li>
<li>Build and manage Flo’s reputation across authoritative sources used by LLMs, including Reddit, Quora, and Wikipedia</li>
<li>Own Wikipedia strategy and governance, ensuring accuracy, neutrality, and strong positioning as a primary AI citation source</li>
<li>Rework and structure Flo’s website as an AI-native source, creating content that answers real user queries and is consistently surfaced in LLM responses</li>
<li>Strengthen Flo’s presence in mid-funnel LLM conversations, ensuring the brand is preferred in comparison and evaluation queries</li>
<li>Leverage Reddit and Quora as both reputation and discovery channels, shaping narratives and driving qualified organic traffic</li>
<li>Build and scale systems for generating and managing external signals (reviews, discussions, third-party content) that influence how LLMs interpret the brand</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally with PR, Product, Social, Customer Support, and Marketing to align messaging and ensure consistency across all external touchpoints</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>We Expect From You</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in online reputation, digital PR, content, or related roles</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with or strong understanding of Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), including how LLMs source, weight, and surface information</li>
<li>Understanding of how LLMs source and surface information</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with platforms like Reddit, Quora, and Wikipedia</li>
<li>Experience shaping brand perception across external, non-owned platforms</li>
<li>Strong analytical mindset, connecting reputation signals to business impact</li>
<li>Ability to build systems and scale processes, not just execute tasks</li>
<li>Strong cross-functional collaboration skills and comfort in fast-moving environments</li>
</ul>
