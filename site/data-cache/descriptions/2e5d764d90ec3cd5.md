<h2><strong>About Parloa</strong></h2>
<p>Parloa’s mission is to make every customer conversation feel effortless for both customers and the companies serving them. As agentic AI accelerates, Parloans are shaping the foundation of a new era in customer experience - one where customer support is no longer transactions, but meaningful exchanges. It is not just a vision; Parloa has powered over ONE BILLION interactions between global enterprise brands and their customers, with companies like Booking.com, HealthEquity, Allianz, SAP, BarmeniaGothaer, and TUI already deploying Parloa at scale.</p>
<h2><strong>About the role:</strong></h2>
<p>As our <strong>Business Development Executive</strong> for Southern Europe, you’ll play a pivotal role in launching Parloa’s presence in a new market. You’ll be part of building our revenue engine from the ground up in the region - developing pipeline, establishing relationships with enterprise prospects, and setting the foundation for long-term growth in Southern Europe.</p>
<p>This is not a “follow the playbook” role - you’ll help write it. You’ll work closely with our Head of Growth, the Regional Sales Director, and the enterprise sales team to turn market insights into a tangible pipeline, helping Parloa become the go-to conversational AI platform for leading Southern European enterprises.</p>
<h2><strong>Areas of ownership:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Pioneer Parloa’s Southern Europe market entry by identifying, engaging, and developing relationships with enterprise-level prospects.</li>
<li>Generate and own an outbound pipeline, leveraging creative, data-driven outreach strategies.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with our Account Executives to build scalable revenue processes for the region.</li>
<li>Build and nurture long-term relationships with prospects, positioning Parloa as a trusted partner for AI-powered customer experience.</li>
<li>Act as the voice of the Southern Europe market internally, sharing insights, feedback, and learnings that shape our regional strategy.</li>
<li>Continuously refine messaging and outreach based on market dynamics, ensuring strong alignment between client challenges and Parloa’s solutions.</li>
</ul>
