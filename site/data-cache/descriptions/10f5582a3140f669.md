## Deine Position

<p>As a member of the B2C domain, you will build a virtual power plant leveraging our energy manager "Heartbeat", and continuously ship innovative energy market solutions to our customers.</p><p>You will be responsible for developing scalable, robust backend services that enable seamless integration of our energy management solutions with various energy markets.</p><p>Key responsibilities include:</p><ul><li><p>Work collaboratively with product managers, data engineers, and data scientists to understand problems, support discoveries, and create solutions our customers love</p></li><li><p>Build and optimize backend services for VPP operations and energy market integrations</p></li><li><p>Design and implement APIs and abstraction layers for seamless communication between different components of our energy market integration solutions</p></li><li><p>Optimize backend services for scalability and support for a large number of connected users</p></li><li><p>Create, implement, evaluate, and deploy software solutions to solve complex problems in a simplified way</p></li><li><p>Foster a culture of experiment-driven innovation within the team</p></li><li><p>Promote teamwork and successful project execution</p></li><li><p>Continuously improve developer satisfaction by avoiding/reducing tech debt, automating processes, and good documentation</p></li><li><p>Work across your boundaries and interact in close collaboration with our stakeholders and other teams</p></li></ul><p>Technologies you will work with include:</p><ul><li><p>TypeScript, NestJS</p></li><li><p>Google Cloud Platform (Cloud Run, Cloud Functions, PubSub, CloudSQL, Firestore, BigQuery)</p></li><li><p>Domain-driven design architecture</p></li><li><p>Datadog</p></li><li><p>Terraform</p></li><li><p>GitHub Actions</p></li></ul>
