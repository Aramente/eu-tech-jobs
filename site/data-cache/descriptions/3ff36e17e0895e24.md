In this position, you will support our relentless pursuit of operational excellence across all regions by delivering data-driven insights that optimize how our pool of couriers functions. Your work will directly impact platform quality for our users by enhancing courier onboarding, activation, retention, and overall supply performance.</p>
<p>You’ll collaborate closely with dedicated analytics and product teams, leveraging market context to drive impactful decisions. If you’re passionate about using data to solve complex challenges and thrive in a fast-paced, results-oriented environment, this is the role for you!</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Analyze and improve all aspects of courier supply operations, including onboarding processes, courier activation, and retention strategies.</li>
<li>Conduct ad hoc analyses to address immediate operational needs while also contributing to larger, cross-functional projects that build the foundation for the future of supply.</li>
<li>Use data-driven insights to enhance operational efficiency and platform quality across regions.</li>
<li>Partner with analytics, product, and other operational teams to align on goals and execute initiatives.</li>
<li>Leverage tools like SQL, Python and machine learning models to uncover trends and opportunities.</li>
<li>Explore and implement AI-driven solutions to streamline day-to-day workflows and decision-making.</li>
<li>Communicate findings and recommendations effectively to stakeholders at all levels.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What We’re Looking For</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>A results-driven mindset with a sense of urgency</li>
<li>A strong analytical foundation: terms like regression, SQL, Python, or ML models excite rather than intimidate you</li>
<li>Understanding of causal inference and statistical concepts such as p-values</li>
<li>Comfort tackling both quick, tactical analyses and strategic, long-term projects.</li>
<li>Curiosity and enthusiasm for leveraging AI to enhance your work.</li>
<li>Experience with or willingness to learn and use BI tools like Looker</li>
<li>Collaborative spirit and the ability to thrive in a cross-functional, dynamic environment.</li>
<li>Bonus: Background in operations research and optimization. Familiarity with supply chain, logistics, or marketplace operations.</li>
</ul>

If you’re ready to make an impact with data and innovation, we’d love to hear from you!</p>
