**About the Role:**

As an Enterprise Account Executive, you will play a critical role in expanding our footprint within large Japanese enterprises (1,000+ employees). This position requires a strategic, consultative approach to sales, working closely with C-level executives and key decision-makers across IT and business units to help drive large-scale digital transformation.

## Your responsibilities?

- Strategize and execute plans to acquire and expand relationships with large enterprise customers in Japan.
- Build and maintain trusted relationships with senior executives (CIOs, CTOs, business leaders), fostering strong partnerships that drive mutual growth.
- Navigate long, complex sales cycles and manage the intricacies of enterprise procurement processes.
- Use account-based selling strategies to develop personalized solutions, increasing customer lifetime value.
- Ensure alignment with the broader team by collaborating with Marketing, Solutions Engineers, and Customer Success to deliver exceptional value to customers.
- Use Salesforce to track and manage pipeline health, providing clear visibility into sales progress and forecasts.

## About you?

- 5+ years of experience in enterprise SaaS sales, with proven success in managing large-scale deals and long sales cycles.
- Extensive experience working with C-level executives, influencing key decision-makers in the IT and business sectors.
- Deep understanding of enterprise sales cycles, procurement processes, and the ability to manage multi-stakeholder decisions.
- Account-based selling experience and a proven track record of developing long-term relationships with enterprise customers.
- Native-level Japanese proficiency and strong knowledge of Japanese business customs and protocols.
