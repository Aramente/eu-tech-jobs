<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p>As Account Management Team Lead, you will lead and develop the account management team while owning overall country performance and key business metrics. You will be responsible for driving growth, strengthening relationships with our most important restaurant partners, and ensuring your team consistently delivers measurable commercial impact. This role combines leadership, strategic execution, and operational excellence.</p>
<h3><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead, coach, and develop a team of Key Account Managers, ensuring high performance and strong ownership of targets</li>
<li>Take full responsibility for country-level performance, including key metrics such as growth, retention, and partner success</li>
<li>Drive revenue and business growth by supporting your team in closing and negotiating strategic partnerships with key restaurant partners</li>
<li>Guide your team in building strong, long-term relationships with partners to identify opportunities for account expansion</li>
<li>Monitor and improve the performance of existing venues through data analysis, regular reviews, and quality checks</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with the marketing team to plan and execute impactful partner campaigns</li>
<li>Continuously analyze the local market and identify growth opportunities and risks</li>
<li>Track competitor activities and translate insights into actionable strategies</li>
<li>Establish and optimize account management processes and best practices</li>
<li>Define team KPIs, track progress, and provide regular feedback and performance coaching</li>
<li>Foster a positive, high-performing team culture aligned with company values and behaviors</li>
<li>Set KPIs for the local Restaurant Key Accounts team and provide regular performance feedback</li>
<li>Create a positive work environment that enables the team to thrive and grow</li>
<li>Lead by example by demonstrating Wolt values and behaviors</li>
</ul>
