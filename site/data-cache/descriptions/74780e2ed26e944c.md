<h2><strong>About the Role</strong></h2>
<p>We are seeking our <strong>first-ever Partnership Director</strong> to build and scale CarbonChain’s partner ecosystem. This is a high-impact, entrepreneurial role at the forefront of our go-to-market motion, reporting directly to the Head of Sales and working closely with co-founders, Marketing, and Product.</p>
<p>Your mission: <strong>establish, grow, and manage strategic partnerships that generate 7+ figures of pipeline annually</strong>, with a particular focus on</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Big 4 consultancies and global SIs like Accenture </strong>as a primary goal, and<br><br></li>
<li>Select boutique consultancies and sustainability-focused service providers as a secondary goal.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<p>You’ll bring a track record of creating, structuring, and growing partnerships from scratch — including the messy, hands-on work of opening doors, aligning value propositions, and securing executive buy-in.</p>
<p>This is a role for a builder: someone equally comfortable in the boardroom with global executives and in the trenches working hand-in-hand with boutique SI founders.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Partnership Strategy &amp; Execution:</strong> Design and execute CarbonChain’s partnership strategy, starting with a focus on consultancies and global SIs, scaling to a broader partner ecosystem over time.</li>
<li><strong>Origination &amp; Deal-Making:</strong> Initiate and negotiate partnership agreements that unlock pipeline and revenue - you’ll be directly responsible for generating partner-sourced pipeline.</li>
<li><strong>Executive Engagement:</strong> Build strong relationships with senior leaders at partner organizations, ensuring CarbonChain is positioned as a priority partner.<br><strong>Partner Enablement:</strong> Work cross-functionally to ensure our partners are equipped to market, sell, and deliver CarbonChain’s solutions effectively.</li>
<li><strong>Pipeline &amp; Revenue Ownership:</strong> Set and achieve partnership-driven pipeline targets</li>
<li><strong>Market Intelligence:</strong> Act as the voice of the partner ecosystem internally, sharing insights on market trends, customer needs, and competitive positioning.</li>
<li><strong>Team Building:</strong> Over time, build and lead a high-performing partnerships team as the function scales.</li>
</ul>
