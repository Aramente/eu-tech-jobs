The Business Protection Team is Spotify’s corporate security function, dedicated to safeguarding our people, assets, and operations around the world. As the **Business Protection Coordinator for the Middle East and South Asia**, you’ll support the Regional Business Protection Manager to ensure security and resilience across our regional operations. This role is **based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates** and is a 12-month contract, subject to extension.

## What You'll Do

- Ensure security and safety of Spotify’s people, sites, and business processes across the Middle East and South Asia
- Oversee day-to-day performance of contracted security teams at Spotify sites and events
- Manage and administer access control and security systems
- Lead on-site security operations during events, campaigns, and activations
- Support visiting Spotify staff and business travellers on security matters
- Respond to and manage security incidents in the region
- Conduct and document risk assessments to support operations in higher-risk areas
- Perform office security audits and recommend improvements
- Advise on physical security design for new offices and renovations
- Deliver security awareness and training to staff, fostering a security-conscious culture
- Contribute to wider Business Protection and Employee Experience initiatives globally
- The role may require travel across the Middle East and South Asia regions

## Who You Are

- You have proven experience in corporate security, intelligence, or crisis management—ideally within the Middle East and South Asia regions
- You hold a bachelor’s degree in security management, risk management, political science, international relations, or a related field
- You have strong knowledge of modern security systems and technologies
- You thrive in dynamic environments, balancing multiple priorities and making sound decisions under pressure
- You’re fluent in English; proficiency in regional languages is an advantage
- You embody Spotify’s values—**Innovative, Sincere, Passionate, Collaborative, and Playful**—and apply them in your work

## Where You'll Be

- The role is based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates and payroll will be through a third party
