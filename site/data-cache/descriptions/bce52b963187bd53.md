<p>Make Wayve the experience that defines your career!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p></div><h2><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>The role&nbsp;</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">As an SRE in Vehicle Software, you will keep Wayve’s autonomous driving fleet reliable, observable, and safe while it operates on public roads. You will work at the boundary of software, hardware, and operations, turning real-world incidents and performance bottlenecks into lasting engineering improvements. This role offers a direct line of sight from what you build to safer deployments, faster iteration, and greater fleet scale.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Key responsibilities</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Own and improve the reliability, availability, and performance of vehicle software systems used across the dev fleet.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Take part in a team on-call rotation, providing out-of-hours support for live systems when required.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Build and operate monitoring, logging, alerting, and on-call tooling that enables fast detection, diagnosis, and recovery.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Drive incident response and post-incident learning, translating root causes into durable fixes and preventative controls.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Design and deliver automation for fleet operations, deployments, and repetitive workflows to reduce manual intervention.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Partner closely with Vehicle SW, operations, and platform teams to define SLOs, reliability metrics, and release readiness.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Continuously harden the production environment through capacity planning, change management, and reliability-focused reviews.</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>About you</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></h2>

<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Proven experience in an SRE, production reliability, or platform operations role for complex distributed systems.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Strong Linux fundamentals and hands-on experience with CI/CD, containers (Docker), and orchestration (Kubernetes).</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Proficiency in at least one systems or scripting language (Python, C++, or Rust) with a bias for automation.</span></li>
