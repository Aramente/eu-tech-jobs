<p>Token Factory is a part of Nebius Cloud, one of the world's largest GPU clouds, running tens of thousands of GPUs. We are building a high-performance inference and fine-tuning platform designed to push foundation models to their hardware limits. Our mission is to maximize throughput, minimise latency, and optimise cost-per-token across tens of thousands of GPUs.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Some directions we are currently working on, and which you can be a part of:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Inference Optimization: Identifying LLM inference bottlenecks to drive production speedups. Squeezing the maximum performance for a wide range of LLM architectures at scale (e.g., GPT-OSS, Kimi K2.5, DeepSeek V3.1/V3.2, GLM-5).</li>
<li>Inference engines support: Implement novel speculative decoding architectures, optimise&nbsp;components of various LLM designs (dense/MoE, autoregressive/parallel), and contribute to open-source inference engines.</li>
<li>Low Precision Training &amp; Inference: Design and productionise low-precision (FP8, NVFP4/MXFP4) training and inference pipelines with measurable gains in throughput and cost-efficiency.</li>
</ul>
