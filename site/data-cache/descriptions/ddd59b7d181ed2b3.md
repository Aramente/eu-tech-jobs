<p><strong>About Osmosis</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At Osmosis, we believe that everyone who cares for someone can learn by Osmosis. Our </span><a href="http://www.osmosis.org/world"><span style="font-weight: 400;">global team's</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> mission is to empower clinicians and caregivers with the best learning experience possible, increase healthcare capacity, and ultimately improve patient outcomes. In 2021, we partnered with Elsevier - the world’s leading publisher and health information company. Together, we’ve translated our content into more languages and expanded our international reach all while continuing to create up-to-date&nbsp; content that supports today’s healthcare learners through their educational journey. We are excited to keep working towards our goal of educating a billion people by 2025 and creating a more caring world by developing the most caring people!</span></p>
