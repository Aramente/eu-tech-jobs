<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Own End-to-End Marketing Strategy &amp; Creative B2B Growth</strong> Develop and execute multi-channel B2B and B2B2C campaigns that drive brand awareness, enterprise pipeline generation, partner activation, and end-user adoption. <p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Content, GEO, and Thought Leadership</strong> Create a world-class content engine that supports enterprise penetration, technical storytelling, and developer education. <p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Data-Driven Decision Making</strong> Champion a culture of experimentation and analytics. <p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Global Collaboration and GTM Integration</strong> Partner closely with Product Marketing (housed within the Product team), Sales, Engineering, and Regulatory teams to align marketing execution with the broader GTM strategy. <p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Event Marketing and Brand Initiatives</strong> Own the strategy and execution for Alpaca’s global event footprint (conferences, partner summits, and regional activations) to drive enterprise leads and brand equity.
