<h2 style="text-align: center;" data-start="0" data-end="22"><strong data-start="0" data-end="22">Study Participants</strong></h2>
<h3 style="text-align: center;" data-start="24" data-end="41"><em>About Prolific</em></h3>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="43" data-end="171">Prolific is not just another research platform – we are building the biggest pool of quality human research data in the world.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="173" data-end="341">Over 35,000 researchers, educators, and organizations use Prolific to run studies with paid participants who bring a wide variety of experiences, knowledge, and skills.</p>
<h3 style="text-align: center;" data-start="343" data-end="354"><em>The role</em></h3>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="356" data-end="796">We’re looking for <strong data-start="374" data-end="396">Study Participants</strong> to take part in academic and applied research studies for global businesses and educational faculties. If you have the necessary experience, we’ll send you a quick <strong data-start="561" data-end="591">10- to 15-minute screening</strong> to assess your suitability for research studies. If successful, you’ll be invited to join Prolific as a participant, where you’ll get paid to contribute to a wide range of studies and research projects.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="798" data-end="1004">Researchers looking for your profile tend to pay <strong data-start="847" data-end="883">up to $18/hr per study completed</strong>. You must be prepared to complete paid studies that require <strong data-start="944" data-end="978">one hour of uninterrupted work</strong>, though many are shorter.</p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;" data-start="2140" data-end="2200"><em>Why Prolific is a great platform to join as a Participant</em></h3>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="2202" data-end="2537">Joining our platform as a Prolific participant will give you the chance to contribute to meaningful research used by universities, researchers, and global organizations. Once you pass our screening, you can join Prolific in just 15 minutes, and start enjoying competitive pay rates, flexible hours, and the ability to work from home.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="2539" data-end="2875">We've built a unique platform that connects researchers and organizations with a global pool of participants, enabling the collection of high-quality, ethically sourced human behavioural data and feedback. This data helps researchers answer important questions, improve products and services, and advance knowledge across disciplines.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="2877" data-end="3172">We believe that the best research outcomes come from diverse human perspectives and experiences.
