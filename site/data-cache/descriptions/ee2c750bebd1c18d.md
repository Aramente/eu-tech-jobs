**Doctor of Podiatric Medicine (DPM) | Southwest Florida**

**The Practice**

Join a premier, rapidly growing multi-location practice dedicated to delivering high-quality, patient-centered podiatric care. We are seeking a motivated, career-oriented **Podiatrist (DPM)** who is looking for more than just a job—we are offering a long-term professional home.

You will work within a collaborative medical environment that values clinical excellence, procedural confidence, and a healthy work-life balance in one of the most beautiful regions of Florida.

**Clinical Scope & Responsibilities**

**Comprehensive Patient Care**

**Diagnosis & Treatment:** Evaluate and treat a wide range of foot and ankle conditions in an outpatient clinical setting.

**Advanced Wound Care:** Manage chronic and complex wounds using evidence-based protocols.

**Procedures:** Perform minor surgical procedures, exploratory treatments, and therapeutic/diagnostic injections.

**Diagnostics:** Utilize and interpret musculoskeletal diagnostic ultrasound to guide treatment plans.

**Rehabilitation:** Prescribe and manage physical therapy and Durable Medical Equipment (DME).

**Hospital & Continuity of Care**

**Shared Call:** Participate in a rotating hospital call schedule.

**Collaboration:** Partner with multidisciplinary hospital teams to ensure seamless continuity of care for inpatient needs.

**Administrative Excellence**

**EHR Mastery:** Maintain accurate, real-time medical documentation.

**Revenue Integrity:** Apply expert knowledge of CPT and ICD-10 coding to ensure billing accuracy and reduce claim denials.

**What We Look For**

**Clinical Judgment:** Strong procedural confidence and the ability to manage both routine and complex cases.

**Adaptability:** Comfort practicing across multiple locations in a fast-paced environment.

**Team Synergy:** A collaborative, team-focused attitude with excellent interpersonal communication skills.

**Growth Mindset:** A long-term, career-oriented professional committed to the success of the practice and the community.

**Requirements & Qualifications**

**Education:** Doctor of Podiatric Medicine (DPM) from an accredited institution.

**Licensure:** Active (or eligibility for) Florida Podiatry License and DEA license.

**Certification:** Board Qualified or Board Certified is highly preferred.

**Experience:** Open to both experienced providers and high-potential residents looking to establish their practice in Southwest Florida.

**Why Southwest Florida?**

**Strategic Schedule:** Our hours are structured to support provider efficiency and genuine work-life balance.

**Collaborative Culture:** Work alongside peers who value shared knowledge and professional mentorship.

**Location:** Enjoy the lifestyle benefits of Florida’s Gulf Coast while building a lucrative, stable practice.
