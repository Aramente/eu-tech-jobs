**WHY WE NEED YOU ?**

Our growth is driving us to strengthen our **Hardware Engineering** team to support the automation, reliability, and availability of our image solutions.

Your mission will be to **build, maintain, and validate Linux kernels and firmware** in order to **deliver cutting-edge infrastructure tools to internal teams and customers, ensuring our processes remain efficient, scalable, and secure.**

**YOUR FUTURE TEAM**

We work in a collaborative and international environment where the diversity of Scalers, combined with a spirit of sharing, helps bring new projects to life every day, advancing our ambitions together.

You will be part of the **Hardware Development Team**. This rapidly growing group is at the heart of our infrastructure, handling R&D, qualification, prototyping, and tool development. You will work closely with internal teams (hardware, QA, software) to support production hardware quality tools and ensure the smooth operation of automated image management pipelines.

**YOUR DAILY ROUTINE**

- Build, maintain, and debug Linux kernels, initrd/initramfs, and squashfs images.

- Validate OS and firmware (BIOS/UEFI, BMC, microcode) compatibility with hardware platforms.

- Check for vulnerabilities, test fixes, and manage firmware flashing and validation.

- Implement and manage PXE boot environments for bare-metal deployment.

- Automate image building and deployment using scripts (Bash, Python), Ansible, and CI/CD tools.

- Perform hardware bring-up and validate OS deployment on real hardware.

- Manage containerized test environments for integration testing.

- Collaborate with internal teams to support production hardware quality tools.

- Take ownership of the image lifecycle and propose solutions to improve infrastructure efficiency.

- Write and maintain documentation for firmware and OS validation processes.

**ABOUT YOU**

**Hard Skills**

- Strong knowledge of Linux internals (Kernel configuration, boot process, initrd/initramfs) and filesystem images.

- Experience with firmware/hardware validation (BIOS/UEFI, BMC) and hardware bring-up.

- Proficiency in deployment protocols (PXE, TFTP, DHCP) and scripting (Bash, Python).

- Experience with automation tools (GitLab CI/CD, Ansible) and containerization (Docker).

- 2–5 years of experience in DevOps, Platform, or System Engineering roles.

**Soft Skills**

- Autonomous and proactive mindset, with strong ownership of complex tasks.

- Excellent cross-team collaboration skills (hardware, QA, software teams).

- Rigorous approach to documentation and process improvement.

- Problem-solving attitude toward hardware and software integration challenges.
