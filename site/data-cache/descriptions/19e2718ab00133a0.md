<h2><strong>About the Role</strong></h2>
<p><strong>This is a hybrid role based in Helsinki, Finland, with relocation support available.</strong></p>
<p>We're looking for a Program Manager, Technology &amp; Insights to join our EMEA team, based in Helsinki. Reporting to the Senior Manager for Global Technology Strategy &amp; Operations.</p>
<p>In this role, you will own and deliver complex, enterprise-scale technical programmes across our EMEA and global operations. You will be joining the Technology &amp; Insights function, working autonomously across multiple brands (DoorDash, Wolt, Deliveroo) and navigating complex stakeholder landscapes at VP+ level. You will be part of the mission in shaping how we scale our technology operations across regions, driving both strategic planning and hands-on delivery.</p>
<p>We are looking for someone who thrives in ambiguity, has experience building operational capability from scratch, and is ready to move from advisory into an in-house role with real ownership.</p>
<h2><strong>You're excited about this opportunity because you will…</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Lead Major Programmes Independently:</strong> You will own end-to-end delivery of complex, cross-functional programmes, from integration workstreams to platform transitions . You will operate with significant autonomy, bringing structure to ambiguous problem spaces and driving outcomes without requiring constant oversight.</p>
<p><strong>Operate at Senior Stakeholder Level:</strong> You will engage directly with senior leaders across Technology, HR, Finance and Operations. You will build trusted relationships, influence decision-making, and represent the Technology &amp; Insights function in cross-functional forums.</p>
<p><strong>Drive Regional and Cross-Brand Coordination:</strong> You will anchor EMEA capability for the team, working across Helsinki and London, and coordinating across US timezones as needed. You will navigate the complexity of operating across multiple brands and entities, ensuring alignment and effective delivery.</p>
<p><strong>Shape Strategy Alongside Execution:</strong> You will contribute to strategic planning for the function, identifying opportunities for operational improvement, automation and efficiency. You will balance hands-on programme delivery with thinking ahead on capability building and team development.</p>
<p><strong>Build and Scale Operational Excellence:</strong> You will establish governance frameworks, documentation standards and ways of working that enable the team to scale. You will mentor and guide junior team members, contributing to the growth of EMEA capability over time.</p>
