<p>This role is part of the ANZ Partner Presales team, a member of the Office of the Field CTO, based in Sydney or Melbourne supporting Partners across Australia and New Zealand.</p>
<p>As part of the global Partner Presales team you’ll work closely with our Regional Alliances Managers (RAM) and Leadership, Presales field teams, product, engineering, and global partner teams to help partners build differentiated offerings, accelerate partner outcomes, and help our partners grow scalable, profitable Okta practices.</p>
<h4>The Role</h4>
<p>As a Partner Solution Architect, you are a senior, partner-facing technologist and trusted advisor. You combine deep technical expertise with strong consultative and communication skills to influence partners, customers, and internal stakeholders.</p>
<p>You will work hands-on with partners to design architectures, build blueprints to orient their service practices around, build partner solution proofs of concept, provide technical confidence around product features and practical application for customers, and help Partners achieve technical wins — always working backwards from customer business needs. You’ll help ensure partner and customer decisions today align with long-term architecture and business strategy.</p>
<p>At the Principal level, this role operates with increased scope, autonomy, and strategic impact - shaping partner strategy and driving pipeline, influencing regional outcomes, and driving long-term platform adoption at scale.</p>
<p><em>\*This role requires in-person onboarding and travel to our San Francisco, CA HQ office during the first week of employment.&nbsp;</em></p>
