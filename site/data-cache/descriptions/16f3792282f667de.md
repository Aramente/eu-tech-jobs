<div class="content-intro"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>About Faire</strong></span></p>
<p>Faire is a technology wholesale platform built on the belief that the future is local. Independent retailers around the globe collectively represent a multi-hundred-billion-dollar wholesale market that has historically been fragmented and offline. At Faire, we're using the power of tech, data, and machine learning to connect this thriving community of entrepreneurs across the globe. Picture your favorite boutique in town — we help them discover the best products from around the world to sell in their stores. With the right tools and insights, we believe that we can level the playing field so businesses can grow and local communities can thrive.</p>
<p>We’re looking for smart, resourceful and passionate people to join us as we power the shop local movement. If you believe in community, come join ours.</p></div><h4><strong>About this role</strong></h4>
<p>As the Systems Administrator, Operations, you’ll play a critical role in improving stability, cost efficiency, and customer experience across Risk, CX, and Marketplace Operations as our workflows and system complexity grow. You’ll lead high-impact systems work—from routing, to integrations, to automations, to ticket enrichment. This is a highly technical, hands-on-role that bridges AI, Operations, and platform development to create a seamless, intelligent experience for our teams.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>We’re looking for a hands-on systems builder, who is equal parts AI builder, Zendesk expert, and systems integrator, who can design with vision, execute with speed, and collaborate with empathy.</p>
<h4><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Design, build and manage case management routing in Zendesk (or similar), including complex workflow logic, SLAs, escalations, and operational controls.</li>
<li>Build and maintain reliable integrations between case management tooling, snowflake using iPaas tools , webhooks etc&nbsp;</li>
<li>Own platform health by monitoring data sync fidelity between Ops tools and backend systems; proactively troubleshoot issues and outages.</li>
<li>Implement case enrichment to give agents better context from back end tools and reduce manual investigation.</li>
<li>Own end to testing and deployment, and stand up robust monitoring systems to identify issues&nbsp;</li>
<li>Guide and uphold development best practices to ensure high-quality implementations, and stable integrations&nbsp;</li>
<li>Drive adoption of major platform changes through clear enablement and stakeholder partnership.</li>
<li>Serve as a bridge between technical and non-technical stakeholders—translating complex requirements into usable systems.</li>
<li>Support broader Ops strategy through thoughtful system design, Ops UX enhancements, and AI-first process optimization.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Qualifications</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>3–5 years business / ps systems experience in a SaaS, high-growth, and/or B2B environment.</li>
<li>Proven ownership of routing and automation in Zendesk (or another case management platform).</li>
<li>Proven ownership of integrations (APIs/webhooks and/or iPaaS like Workato/Hightouch), including operating in production and debugging failures.</li>
<li>Strong SQL skills used to validate data quality, investigate sync issues, and reason about end-to-end data flows (Snowflake a plus).</li>
<li>Track record of translating business needs into scalable system solutions and driving work from concept to adoption.</li>
<li>Able to manage multiple priorities, navigate ambiguity, and execute independently.</li>
<li>Experience leading a migration or large-scale workflow change with minimal disruption.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to have</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with LLM-powered tooling for classification, tagging, or workflow automation—and an intuition for where AI meaningfully improves ops vs. adds complexity.</li>
<li>Scripting ability in Python or JavaScript for lightweight automation or data manipulation tasks.</li>
<li>Prior experience working in or closely alongside a Support, CX, or Marketplace Operations team.</li>
<li>Familiarity with job orchestration concepts—dependencies, retries, alerting—for scheduled syncs and data pipelines.</li>
</ul>
