<p>As a Production Engineering Technician, you will play a critical role in building high-quality safety technology products that support Axon’s mission. You will be responsible for assembling, testing, and verifying sensor systems in a structured production environment. Your work directly contributes to product reliability, customer trust, and the overall performance of our manufacturing operations.</p>
<h4><br>What You’ll Do</h4>
<p><strong>Location: </strong>Kassel, Germany office 5 days a week<br><strong>Reports to: </strong>Sebastian Peter</p>
<ul>
<li>Assemble sensor systems and components according to defined work instructions, drawings, and quality standards</li>
<li>Perform functional tests and basic troubleshooting to ensure product performance and compliance</li>
<li>Follow standardized processes to ensure consistent quality, safety, and efficiency across production lines</li>
<li>Identify and report deviations, defects, or process issues in a timely and structured manner</li>
<li>Support continuous improvement initiatives by providing feedback on assembly processes and workflows</li>
<li>Maintain accurate documentation of production activities, test results, and material usage</li>
<li>Ensure proper handling of materials, tools, and equipment in line with EHS guidelines</li>
<li>Collaborate with team members, engineering, and quality teams to resolve production issues</li>
<li>Contribute to maintaining a clean, organized, and efficient work environment (5S principles)</li>
</ul>
<h4>What You Bring</h4>
<ul>
<li>Completed vocational training (technical field preferred) or equivalent hands-on experience in manufacturing or assembly</li>
<li>Initial experience in production, assembly, or electronics manufacturing environments is beneficial</li>
<li>Ability to read and follow technical work instructions and basic drawings</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and commitment to quality standards</li>
<li>Reliable, structured, and team-oriented way of working</li>
<li>Basic problem-solving skills and willingness to learn and improve processes</li>
<li>Openness to working in a fast-paced, evolving production environment</li>
</ul>
