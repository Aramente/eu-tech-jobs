<h2><strong>Your Impact</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for an AI Product Manager to join one of our in-house startups : <em>Consultation Assistant</em>.</p>
<p>Your mission will be to own a standalone AI Scribe product at Doctolib, helping build and scale a dedicated AI Scribe experience that makes clinical documentation faster, higher-quality, and less painful for practitioners. You will work in a feature team developing AI-powered clinical documentation solutions, contributing directly to reducing admin burden for 400,000 health professionals across Europe and unlocking more time for patient care, with measurable adoption and satisfaction outcomes.</p>
<p>Working in the tech team at Doctolib means building innovative products and features to improve the daily lives of care teams and patients.</p>
<h2><br><strong>What you'll build</strong></h2>
<p>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Own the end-to-end product strategy for our AI Scribe solution, driving quality improvements and market expansion with high autonomy</li>
<li>Build and refine Clinical ASR quality through tight collaboration with Data Scientists and ML Engineers on model evaluation, iteration loops, and production trade-offs (accuracy, latency, privacy)</li>
<li>Drive the Germany market launch, coordinating across engineering, design, and local teams to ensure successful go-to-market execution</li>
<li>Develop structured evaluation frameworks and monitoring systems for ML quality in production, defining metrics like WER, accuracy by segment, and user satisfaction</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with clinicians to understand documentation workflows, trust requirements, and specialty-specific needs, translating insights into product features</li>
<li>Champion a user-centric approach, balancing model performance with real-world constraints (time pressure, accuracy requirements, regulatory compliance)</li>
</ul>
