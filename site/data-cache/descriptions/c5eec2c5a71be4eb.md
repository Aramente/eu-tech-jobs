<h2><strong>The day-to-day</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Assist in the preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform general ledger entries, including accruals, prepayments, and adjustments&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reconcile bank statements, balance sheets, and general ledger accounts&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processes, ensuring accurate and timely invoicing and payments&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support the month-end and year-end close processes&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Help prepare documentation for audits and liaise with external auditors as needed&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Maintain accurate and up-to-date financial records in accounting and financial systems&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Work with the finance team to ensure compliance with internal controls and policies&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
