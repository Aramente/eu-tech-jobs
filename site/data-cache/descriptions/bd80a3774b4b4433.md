<p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">About The Team:</span></strong></p>
<p>This is a strong opportunity to join a fast-moving, customer-centric business where transport planning directly impacts the end customer experience. We are looking for a Transport Planner with a clear bias toward B2C home delivery, final mile efficiency, and hands-on optimisation of multi-drop routes.While the role spans end-to-end transport planning, the primary focus is on home delivery planning: improving drop density, driving route efficiency, challenging system-generated plans, and reducing cost per delivery while protecting service. You will play a key role in closing capability gaps by actively refining plans rather than accepting tool outputs at face value.&nbsp;You will work closely with courier partners, haulage providers, and internal logistics teams to deliver high-quality, executable plans that support reliable, on-time delivery to customers.</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>What You Will Be Doing:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Building and executing daily multi-drop van delivery plans for B2C home delivery</li>
<li>Actively reviewing and refining computer-generated routes to improve drop density, driving time, and overall efficiency</li>
<li>Identifying and implementing route-level tweaks to reduce cost per delivery while maintaining customer service</li>
<li>Working closely with courier and DSP partners to improve on-road execution and performance</li>
<li>Reviewing base schedules and delivery patterns to unlock continuous improvement opportunities</li>
<li>Building and executing weekly outbound linehaul plans to support final mile delivery</li>
<li>Planning regional distribution sequences to ensure on-time arrival at final mile depots</li>
<li>Working with 3PL partners to meet connections and improve performance through smarter scheduling</li>
<li>Building inbound stock plans aligned to outbound and home delivery requirements</li>
<li>Managing pallet counts across production sites</li>
<li>Working closely with Supply Chain and Production to maintain a smooth and reliable despatch schedule</li>
<li>Visiting distribution sites regularly to ensure plans translate into safe, efficient execution</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Who You Are:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 2 year of experience in transport planning with B2C or home delivery exposure</li>
<li>A demonstrable track record of improving route efficiency, increasing vehicle fill, and reducing cost</li>
<li>Strong understanding of UK road haulage and relevant regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Hands-on experience using transport planning tools, with confidence to challenge and improve system-generated plans</li>
<li>Experience working with drivers, couriers, and hauliers (3PLs)</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, able to work with stakeholders across operations, planning, and production</li>
</ul>
