<p>We're looking for a Product Manager to drive the product strategy for Screen, HackerRank's core hiring platform. This is a high-impact role where you'll shape how companies evaluate technical talent at scale, working at the cutting edge of AI-powered assessment.</p>
<p>You'll own the future of candidate evaluation—defining how we leverage AI to assess technical skills, problem-solving ability, and collaboration in ways that are both innovative and trustworthy. This is cutting-edge AI evaluation work that's defining new standards for the industry.</p>
<p>The platform operates at significant scale and carries a meaningful technical legacy, serving customers of various sizes, shapes, and needs—from startups running a handful of interviews to enterprises processing thousands of assessments. This isn't about greenfield product work; it's about making sophisticated decisions in a constrained environment and delivering measurable impact.</p>
<p>The role comes with high visibility across the organization, including direct exposure to our CEO, and requires close collaboration with platform teams working on integrity, IDE infrastructure, and AI capabilities.</p>
<h4><strong>What you'll do</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Own the product strategy and roadmap for Screen, making critical decisions about how we evolve our core evaluation platform while balancing innovation with platform stability</li>
<li>Drive AI-first evaluation capabilities, defining what signals matter, how AI can surface insights that human reviewers miss, and how to make evaluation both more effective and more fair</li>
<li>Navigate technical complexity at scale, working deeply with a team of X engineers (including X staff engineers) and an Engineering Manager to balance technical constraints with user needs</li>
<li>Balance competing needs across multiple personas—recruiters, hiring managers, and candidates—designing solutions that work across their often conflicting requirements</li>
<li>Guide customers through platform evolution, spending time understanding their hiring challenges, managing migrations to new experiences, and ensuring we solve real problems</li>
<li>Collaborate across teams and time zones, working with multiple product and platform teams, and often serving as the bridge between technical complexity and customer value</li>
<li>Lead with visibility and impact, working directly with senior leadership and driving cross-functional initiatives that shape the future of technical hiring</li>
</ul>
