## Your (space) mission

At cosine, our cleanrooms are where precision meets space. Here, we build and test advanced optical instruments for ESA missions such as NewAthena and future space explorations.<br><br>As the Cleanroom Manager, you are the central point of contact and operational owner of all cleanroom activities. You ensure that scientist-engineers, lab technicians, contractors and supporting teams work in a well-structured, safe and contamination-controlled environment.<br><br>You safeguard cleanroom integrity by ensuring adherence to procedures, safety standards and quality requirements, while enabling teams to focus on delivering high-end space hardware. In this role, you provide clarity, coordination and consistency across all cleanroom operations.<br>You are present on the floor, proactive in coordination, and take responsibility for smooth daily operations. At the same time, you act in a coaching and mentoring role and fostering a collaborative environment across cultures and disciplines We want someone who helps and not just supervises.

## Tasks & responsibilities

In this role you will:<br><ul><li>Coordinate daily cleanroom operations, including scientist-engineers, lab technicians, contractors, and facility activities</li><li>Ensure staff and contractors comply with cleanroom entry and working requirements</li><li>Act as the primary point of contact for all cleanroom users (internal and external)</li><li>Ensure compliance with project requirements, internal procedures, and safety and contamination control standards</li><li>Promote correct cleanroom behavior and support contractors in a constructive manner</li><li>Monitor operational flow, workload balance, and execution quality to ensure consistency</li><li>Act as People Lead for lab technicians within cosine</li><li>Drive continuous improvement in cleanroom organisation, usage, and coordination</li></ul>

## Your profile

We are looking for candidates with the following qualifications:<br><ul><li>Proven experience in cleanroom environments, ideally in the semiconductor or space industry</li><li>Strong technical background (HBO level or equivalent hands-on engineering/technician experience)</li><li>Solid knowledge of contamination control and cleanroom standards</li><li>Experience working with external contractors in a clear and constructive way</li><li>Strong communicator who builds trust and alignment across diverse teams and cultures</li><li>Fluent in English and Dutch, both written and spoken</li><li>Structured and proactive, with a coaching mindset and the ability to balance discipline with collaboration</li></ul>
