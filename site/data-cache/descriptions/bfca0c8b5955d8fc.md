<p>Intercom’s websites, including<a href="https://intercom.com"> intercom.com</a> and<a href="https://fin.ai"> fin.ai</a>, are strategic growth levers for the company — powering storytelling, brand, experimentation, and product discovery. <h2><strong>What will I be doing?</strong></h2>
<p>As a senior technical leader, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Own and evolve the architecture of Intercom’s web stack.</li>
<li>Define the long-term technical strategy for the web team, focusing on scalability, performance, developer productivity, observability, and system reliability.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with marketing, design, analytics, and data science stakeholders to ensure the platform supports their goals with accuracy, performance, and agility.</li>
<li>Lead and influence the design and implementation of MarTech systems for event tracking, attribution, funnel reporting, A/B testing infrastructure, and more.</li>
<li>Partner with engineers across web, infrastructure, and data to create a high-quality, cohesive technical ecosystem.</li>
<li>Mentor and elevate engineers across the team and organisation — providing guidance on architecture, data modeling, system design, and engineering best practices.</li>
<li>Set the standard for technical excellence in reliability, maintainability, code quality, and operational readiness.</li>
<li>Provide technical leadership and insight to Engineering Managers, Product Managers, and executive stakeholders — communicating risks, trade-offs, and opportunities clearly.</li>
<li>Contribute hands-on to the codebase — leading by example and helping unblock and accelerate key projects.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>12+ years of software engineering experience.</li>
<li>Experience in a high-scale growth-focused web environment.</li>
<li>A track record of technical leadership and influencing technical direction across multiple teams or departments.</li>
<li>Deep familiarity with modern web stacks and infrastructure.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of data infrastructure, including event instrumentation, and analytics tooling.</li>

<li>Exceptional communication skills and a history of collaborating with cross-functional teams (product, marketing, analytics, design, etc.)</li>

</ul>
