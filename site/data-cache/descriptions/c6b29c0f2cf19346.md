<p>A Senior Staff Engineer at Monzo is a hands on engineering leadership (IC) position. As a Senior Staff Engineer you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Engineering Directors to <strong data-start="815" data-end="876">shape the technical and product direction of a collective</strong>, providing leadership across multiple squads (30–70+ engineers) and ensuring alignment with company-wide priorities.</li>
<li>Be <strong>hands on</strong> in both coding and architecture, leading from the front.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Product and Design leadership to <strong data-start="1698" data-end="1832">define strategy, shape roadmaps, and make high-quality trade-offs that maximise business and customer impact across the collective</strong>.</li>
<li><strong data-start="2010" data-end="2079">Mobilise teams across your collective around high-impact problems</strong>, creating clarity, alignment and momentum, injecting urgency when needed, or slowing things down to ensure safety and long-term sustainability.</li>
<li>Lead and influence architectural direction for the most complex systems, <strong data-start="2503" data-end="2626">ensuring cohesive, scalable designs across multiple teams and aligning local decisions with the broader system strategy</strong>.</li>
<li>Set and uphold a high bar for technical excellence across the collective, <strong data-start="2882" data-end="3020">driving improvements in quality, reliability and scalability, and fostering a culture where engineers hold each other to that standard</strong>.</li>
<li>Proactively mentor, sponsor and <strong data-start="3229" data-end="3276">develop senior engineers and future leaders</strong>, multiplying your impact by enabling others to operate at a higher level.</li>
</ul>
