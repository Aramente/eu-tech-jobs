<p><strong>About Fetch</strong></p>

<h3><strong>About the Role</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Overview</strong></p>
<p>Fetch is seeking a driven, proactive and detail-oriented Business Development Associate to join its Breeder team. In this role, you will work closely with Business Development leadership to identify, engage and grow relationships with strategic partners in the Breeder space.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This is a high-impact, pipeline-building role ideal for someone who enjoys prospecting, building relations from the ground up, and contributing directly to channel growth. You’ll play a key role in expanding Fetch’s footprint and driving new partnership opportunities in the Breeder space.
