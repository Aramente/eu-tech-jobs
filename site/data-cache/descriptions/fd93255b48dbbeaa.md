<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p>As an Israel Data Analyst, you’ll play a key role in scaling and optimising Wolt’s support operations in one of our largest on-demand markets. You’ll own capacity planning, forecasting accuracy, and cost optimisation, working closely with regional and global stakeholders to balance customer experience, agent wellbeing, and business efficiency.</p>
<h3><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Build and continuously optimise capacity plans across multiple sites, translating demand forecasts into staffing and hiring needs.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Product, Operations, Finance, and the global Recruitment team to ensure plans support SLAs and reflect business changes.</li>
<li>Create and maintain performance and analytical reporting, tracking forecast accuracy, service levels, occupancy, and costs.</li>
<li>Run scenario modelling and retrospective analyses to improve forecasting accuracy (MAPE) and operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Co-own support budget planning and cost tracking, providing insights on variances vs. plan.</li>
<li>Drive operating model improvements through data, automation, and SQL-based analysis.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Our humble expectations</h2>
<ul>
<li>Experience in forecasting, capacity planning, and scenario modelling within a Workforce Management environment.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to turn data into clear, actionable insights.</li>
<li>Advanced Excel skills and ability to run SQL queries (Python is a plus).</li>
<li>Comfortable working independently in ambiguous environments and proactively driving improvements.</li>
<li>Proven ability to identify and implement automation and efficiency opportunities.</li>
<li>Strong stakeholder management and communication skills, ideally in a global, multi-site setup.</li>
<li>Ownership mindset with the ability to balance customer experience, agent wellbeing, and business goals.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What we offer</h2>
<ul>
<li>A high-impact role shaping how we scale support operations in one of the world’s largest on-demand markets.</li>
<li>The opportunity to influence strategic workforce, cost, and service-level decisions.</li>
<li>Collaboration with a highly skilled, global Workforce Management team and a world-class lead focused on growth and development.</li>

</ul>
