**The world’s most advanced VPN, and a whole lot more.**

If you’re a curious problem-solver who carves their own path, join the team behind Threat Protection Pro, the NordLynx protocol, and the fastest VPN on the planet—tools that put privacy, security, and control back in people’s hands.

**Your impact?** Helping millions take back control of their online security, privacy, and data.

NordVPN operates a globally distributed VPN and DNS infrastructure serving millions of users. Your responsibility is the service itself - deploying, managing, and improving VPN nodes worldwide while ensuring every client connection is fast, stable, and secure.  
  
You'll work closely with application teams to guarantee smooth client connectivity end to end. Quality and reliability are the job.

## Main Responsibilities

- Deploy and manage globally distributed VPN and DNS services
- Ensure connection quality, stability, and performance across the entire node fleet
- Collaborate with application teams on client connectivity and protocol behavior
- Optimize VPN service performance - kernel tuning, flamechart analysis, profiling
- Manage security posture - firewalls, PKI, certificate lifecycle, hardening
- Manage authentication and authorization infrastructure
- Own configuration management and automated provisioning of infrastructure
- Clear written incident communication and postmortem ownership

## Core Requirements

- Own critical VPN services end-to-end including architecture, scalability, security and monitoring
- Deliver OKRs and key commitments within agreed timelines
- Write RFCs/ADRs for new features, refactors, etc
- Raises engineering quality through standards, design reviews, knowledge sharing and mentorship
- Balances reliability, security and delivery speed with strong operational judgment
- Advanced networking - VPN protocols, tunneling, routing, DNS
- Linux at depth - kernel internals, tuning, module management
- Firewall management
- Performance analysis - flamecharts, profiling, bottleneck identification
- OSI model fluency -  L2 through L7, TCP/IP stack, SSL/TLS
- Security fundamentals -  PKI, certificate management, hardening
- Authentication and authorization - RADIUS, access control
- Configuration management and automated deployments
- Load balancing and failover for distributed services
- Docker
