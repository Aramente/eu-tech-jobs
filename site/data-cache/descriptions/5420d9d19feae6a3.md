<p><strong>POSITION SUMMARY</strong></p>
<p>The CSRE Lead Software Engineer is responsible for leading the design, development, and delivery of software solutions that support Corporate Services &amp; Real Estate functions, including facilities management, real estate operations, workplace services, and sustainability initiatives.</p>
<p>This role serves as the technical lead within the software development team, translating CSRE business needs into scalable, secure, and maintainable applications, while setting engineering standards and mentoring developers.</p>
<p><strong>ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Software Engineering Leadership</li>
<li>Act as technical lead for CSRE-focused software development initiatives</li>
<li>Define and enforce engineering best practices, architecture standards, and coding guidelines</li>
<li>Review solution designs, code, and technical documentation</li>
<li>Mentor and guide software engineers, fostering high-quality engineering practices</li>
<li>Application Design &amp; Development</li>
<li>Design, develop, and maintain applications supporting:
<ul>
<li>Real estate portfolio management</li>
<li>Facilities and maintenance operations</li>
<li>Workplace services and employee experience platforms</li>
<li>Capital project tracking and reporting</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Build scalable APIs, services, and integrations with enterprise systems (ERP, HR, Finance)</li>
<li>Ensure solutions are secure, reliable, and compliant with enterprise IT standards</li>
<li>Business Partnership (CSRE Focus)</li>
<li>Partner closely with CSRE stakeholders to understand workflows, pain points, and strategic goals</li>
<li>Translate business requirements into technical solutions and roadmaps</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance on system capabilities, limitations, and trade-offs</li>
<li>Delivery &amp; Operations</li>
<li>Lead technical delivery in an agile environment (planning, estimation, execution)</li>
<li>Support production deployments, monitoring, and issue resolution</li>
<li>Drive automation, testing, and CI/CD practices to improve quality and speed</li>
<li>Ensure proper documentation, support handover, and knowledge transfer</li>
<li>Data, Reporting &amp; Insights</li>
<li>Enable data integration and reporting for CSRE systems (dashboards, analytics, KPIs)</li>
<li>Support sustainability, space utilization, and cost optimization initiatives through data-driven solutions</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>5–7+ years of professional software development experience</li>
<li>Prior experience as a technical lead or senior engineer</li>
<li>Experience delivering enterprise applications in a regulated or corporate environment</li>
<li>Exposure to real estate, facilities, or corporate services systems</li>
</ul>
