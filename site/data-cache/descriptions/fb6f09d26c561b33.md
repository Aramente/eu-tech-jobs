<h2><strong>Team description</strong></h2>
<p>The Sales Enablement team at SumUp exists to make sure our sales reps have exactly what they need, when they need it, to sell confidently and effectively. As SumUp's sales engine continues to grow across inside sales, field sales, and key accounts, the demand for well-structured, accessible, and high-quality content has never been higher. In this role, you'll be the person who owns that content ecosystem: building, curating, and maintaining the resources that thousands of sales interactions depend on, and helping us explore smarter, AI-powered ways to get the right content into the right hands at the right time.</p>
<p><em>This is an 8-month parental leave cover contract, with the possibility of extension.</em></p>
