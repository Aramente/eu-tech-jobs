<p>Postman is the world’s leading API platform, used by more than 45 million+ developers and 500,000 organizations, including 98% of the Fortune 500. <p>Postman is the world’s leading API platform, helping organizations across every industry collaboratively design, build, test, document, share, and manage their APIs.</p>
<p>Our rocket continues to reach new heights, which is why we are looking for an experienced Solutions Engineer to support our rapidly growing enterprise customer base in the Nordics region.</p>
<p>As a Solutions Engineer at Postman, you will partner closely with the Sales, Product, and Customer Success teams to deliver technical expertise throughout the sales cycle. <p>If you are passionate about APIs, software development, and solving complex customer challenges, we would love to have you on the team!</p>
