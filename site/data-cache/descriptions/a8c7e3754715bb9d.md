Mayflower is a technology company that alters the entertainment industry to a new level of perception and engagement.

We are developing a partner advertising high-load system with a large RPS, convenient and modern interface. We are growing exponentially and successfully implementing new technologies to accelerate this growth and the system as a whole.

We are looking for a DevOps Engineer.

#### Job Responsibilities

* **Kubernetes (OKD) Management:**   
  Deploy, manage, and troubleshoot applications in OKD;  
  Create and maintain Helm charts and Kubernetes manifests;  
  Work closely with the PaaS team to resolve complex infrastructure issues.
* **CI/CD & GitOps:**   
  Deep integration of Kubernetes deployments with GitLab CI;  
  Debug and optimize delivery pipelines;  
  Support Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible).
* **Linux Administration:** Administer and maintain virtual machines running CentOS 9 (configuration, updates, troubleshooting).
* **Application Support:** Ensure the stability of PHP (Nginx Unit -> FrankenPHP) and Go applications.
* **Data & Messaging:** Manage stateful services: Redis (Cluster), NATS, RabbitMQ, ClickHouse, and ScyllaDB on VMs.
* **Observability:** Maintain the logging and monitoring stack (Vector + Elasticsearch + Kibana, Sentry).
* **Networking:** Manage traffic proxying and load balancing (Nginx, Caddy + Consul, Cloudflare).
* **QA Support:** Support the infrastructure for automated testing (Allure, Selenium).
