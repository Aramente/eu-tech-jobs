</ul></div><h3>The Role</h3>
<p>Gigs is growing fast across markets, and we need someone to build the operational systems that make that growth possible. That includes compliance -- but this isn't a compliance role. It's a BizOps role where regulatory work is one of the hardest and most important problems you'll solve.</p>
<p>You'll build workflows, design processes, ship tooling, and work across Business Development, Legal, Product, and Engineering. You'll enable how we launch new markets, how we stay compliant across jurisdictions, and how we keep internal operations tight as we scale.</p>
<p>This is our first hire in this area. You'll shape the function from scratch.</p>
<h2>What You Will Do</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Build our operational infrastructure for market expansion.</strong> Own the end-to-end process for launching in new markets: regulatory readiness, product compliance checks, internal workflows, and cross-functional coordination.</li>
<li><strong>Turn regulatory requirements into systems.</strong> Work with Legal to understand what's required, then build clear, repeatable processes that the team can follow without bottlenecks. Don't just document rules -- make them run.</li>
<li><strong>Automate before it breaks.</strong> Partner with Engineering and Product to embed controls into our systems early. Reduce manual work. Build things that scale before they need to.</li>
<li><strong>Own filings, registrations, and reporting.</strong> Manage our ongoing regulatory obligations across jurisdictions. Track deadlines. Make sure nothing slips.</li>
<li><strong>Run audits.</strong> Develop audit frameworks for internal operations and customer-facing processes. Keep them current.</li>
<li><strong>Track compliance health.</strong> Define metrics, build dashboards, flag problems early, fix them fast.</li>
<li><strong>Keep documentation sharp.</strong> Maintain policies, procedures, and process docs that are clear, findable, and up to date.</li>
<li><strong>Stay ahead of regulatory changes.</strong> Monitor new requirements across target markets. Build implementation plans before deadlines arrive.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What We Are Looking For</h2>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in business operations, ideally at a fintech, payments company, or other regulated tech business.</li>
<li>You've built or scaled operational systems in a fast-moving environment. You know how to take a messy process, structure it, and make it run.</li>
<li>Real experience with compliance or regulatory work -- not as a lawyer, but as someone who's turned regulatory requirements into working processes across areas like product compliance, vendor risk, or cross-border operations.</li>
<li>Comfortable with tooling: GRC systems, databases, workflow automation. You don't need to be an engineer, but you can build things using AI.</li>
<li>Strong communicator. You can explain a regulatory filing to an engineer and a process gap to a lawyer.</li>
</ul>
<p>Bonus if you have experience in telecoms, fintech, or B2B SaaS.</p>
