<p>Our company is seeking a highly skilled Senior Site Reliability Engineer to join our team. We are a SaaS company specializing in securing large-scale systems. This role is a blend of software engineering and systems administration, where you'll be responsible for building and maintaining highly reliable, scalable, and secure infrastructure. You will be a key contributor, applying your expertise to automate manual processes and proactively solve complex problems before they become incidents, handling incidents, and includes on-call shifts.</p>

<h3><strong>Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Platform &amp; Reliability:</strong> Design, build, and maintain the core infrastructure that underpins our security SaaS offerings, ensuring high availability, performance, and scalability. This includes building and operating the tooling for our Snowflake data systems.</li>
<li><strong>Automation:</strong> Develop robust automation using code to eliminate toil and ensure consistency across our environments. You'll be a key driver in automating everything from infrastructure provisioning to application deployment and incident response.</li>
<li><strong>Security &amp; Compliance:</strong> Work closely with our security teams to embed a security-first mindset into all our processes and infrastructure. You will be responsible for ensuring our systems and data platforms are compliant with industry standards.</li>
<li><strong>Incident Response:</strong> Participate in on-call rotations and be a primary responder for critical incidents, leading root cause analysis and implementing preventative measures to ensure issues don't recur.</li>
<li><strong>Collaboration:</strong> Partner with development, data science, and security teams to provide expert guidance on architectural decisions, best practices, and the implementation of new services.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Key Skills &amp; Qualifications</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>U.S. Person Status (e.g. a U.S. Citizen, National, Lawful Permanent Resident, Refugee, or Asylee)</strong></li>
<li><strong>Strong Coding Skills:</strong> You are a developer at heart and are comfortable writing production-level code to solve complex operational challenges.</li>
<li><strong>Infrastructure as Code (IaC):</strong> Deep experience with <strong>Terraform</strong> for provisioning and managing cloud infrastructure and services.</li>
<li><strong>Continuous Delivery:</strong> Familiarity with modern CI/CD practices and tools, particularly <strong>Spinnaker</strong>, to automate and standardize our release pipelines.</li>
<li><strong>Containerization &amp; Orchestration:</strong> Expertise in container technologies and hands-on experience managing large-scale, production-ready clusters with <strong>Kubernetes</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>Database Migrations:</strong> Experience with database schema management tools like <strong>Flyway</strong> for safely and reliably handling database changes.</li>
<li><strong>Data Systems:</strong> Direct experience with large-scale data systems, specifically with the <strong>Snowflake</strong> platform.</li>
<li><strong>AI/ML Experience (a plus):</strong> Experience or a strong interest in AI/ML, particularly how these technologies can be applied to improve reliability, security, and operational efficiency (e.g., AIOps, predictive analysis).</li>
<li><strong>Problem-Solving:</strong> Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with a proactive approach to identifying and addressing potential issues.</li>
