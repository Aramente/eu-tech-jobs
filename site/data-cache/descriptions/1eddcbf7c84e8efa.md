<h4>What You'll Do</h4>
<h4><strong>Location: Brussels, Belgium&nbsp;</strong></h4>
<p>This role is based out of our Brussel office and follows a hybrid schedule. We rely on in-person collaboration and ask that team members work onsite with some flexibility, unless there is an approved workplace accommodation. We believe that connection fuels innovation, and our in-office culture is designed to foster meaningful teamwork, mentorship, and shared success.</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive innovative product development from conceptualization to manufacturing</li>
<li>Utilize creative problem solving skills to lead ideation of new product architectural concepts for subsystem and system level designs</li>
<li>Construct and analyze high fidelity proof-of-concept prototypes</li>
<li>Perform detailed mechanical analysis to ensure proper function through FEA, tolerance analysis, testing, and other exploratory means</li>
<li>Collaborate regularly with both domestic and international stakeholders</li>
<li>Work autonomously and collaboratively in an empowering and fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Hone your engineering expertise in a dynamic learning environment focused on personal and professional development</li>
</ul>
<h4>What you bring</h4>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent experience</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of product design experience in high-end consumer electronic products</li>
<li>Proficiency with PTC Creo (preferred) or similar 3D CAD software</li>
<li>Knowledge in the design of plastic injection molded, stamped metal, die-cast, and machined parts</li>
<li>Familiarity with adhesives and bonded joint design</li>
<li>Experience designing for a high level of dust and water ingress protection</li>
<li>Experience designing thermal management solutions for dissipating heat from electronics</li>
<li>Experience with PCB/PCBA flex circuit and other electro-mechanical integration</li>
<li>Strong DFM/DFA skills, with emphasis on part/assembly tolerance management, to ensure robust designs for high volume manufacturing</li>
<li>Ability to interpret product requirements and translate into quantifiable engineering deliverables</li>
<li>Experience preserving Industrial Design artistic intent in mechanical design implementation</li>
<li>Effective communication and strong presentation skills</li>
<li>Ability to balance and prioritize conflicting design constraints through analysis, testing, and creativity</li>
<li>Collaboration with peers in a candid cross-functional team culture</li>
</ul>
