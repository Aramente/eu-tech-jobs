<p>Remote is looking for a data-driven <strong>Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Specialist</strong> to lead our paid acquisition strategy for high-value target accounts. This role focuses on scaling our presence across <strong>Employer of Record (EOR)</strong>, <strong>Contractor Management</strong>, and <strong>Global Payroll</strong> services. You will own the execution of paid campaigns across EMEA, Americas, and APAC, utilizing advanced ABM platforms to target opportunities from mid-market and enterprise customers.</p>
<h1><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h1>
<h2><strong>ABM Strategy &amp; Platform Management</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Audience Architecture:</strong> Build and manage Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) audiences within <strong>ABM platform</strong>, including precise exclusion lists for existing customers, prospects, and competitors.</li>
<li><strong>Intent-Driven Orchestration:</strong> Leverage ABM platform intent data to trigger personalized ad experiences across LinkedIn, Meta, and Google.</li>
<li><strong>Form Optimization:</strong> Implement and manage <strong>HubSpot Smart Form Fills</strong> to enrich lead data and improve conversion rates for targeted accounts.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Paid Media Execution</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Multi-Channel Management:</strong> Develop and implement search and social strategies, including audience targeting (1p, 2p, 3p data) and sizing recommendations.</li>
<li><strong>Performance Optimization:</strong> Conduct daily and weekly pacing audits, quarterly deep-dive health checks, and ongoing A/B testing of text ads and creative formats.</li>
<li><strong>Channel Specifics:</strong> Manage high-match-rate LinkedIn segment syncs (30+ active segments) and optimize Google Ads through first-party contact integration.</li>
<li><strong>Marketplace Presence:</strong> Oversee listing descriptions and review collection campaigns on platforms like Gartner Digital Markets (GDM) to improve brand awareness and CVR.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Cross-Functional Alignment</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Sales Collaboration:</strong> Align paid strategies with Sales to win orchestrate approach, providing specialized landing pages and account-specific support.</li>
<li><strong>Analytics &amp; Tracking:</strong> Partner with the Analytics team to build a measurement plan for ABM</li>
<li><strong>Lifecycle Integration:</strong> Partner with lifecycle on nurture sequences across stages (Lead → MQL → Opportunity → Deal) to ensure consistent messaging for target personas.</li>
</ul>
<h1><strong>Qualifications</strong></h1>
<ul>
<li><strong>Experience:</strong> 6+ years of experience in B2B performance marketing, specifically managing $3M+ annual budgets across paid search and social.</li>
<li><strong>Technical Proficiency:</strong> Advanced knowledge of <strong>Clay, ZoomInfo</strong>, <strong>HubSpot</strong>, <strong>Salesforce</strong>, Meta ads manager and LinkedIn Campaign Manager.</li>
<li><strong>Analytical Rigor:</strong> Expertise in UTM architecture, conversion tracking installation, and data-driven decision-making.</li>
</ul>
