<p><strong>What You'll Do</strong></p>
<p><strong>Months 1-2: Ramp up and start owning pipeline</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan to be in the office daily during your first two months. You'll ramp faster with the team around you, and we'll make sure you get the time you need with the team</li>
<li>Get deep on Ledgy's value proposition for VC-backed startups and growing private companies: option plans, cap table management, and investor reporting</li>
<li>Take ownership of inbound leads. A significant portion of our SMB pipeline comes inbound, and you'll be the first person qualifying and running those conversations</li>
<li>Shadow deals being run by the team. Understand how we sell, what objections come up, and how we handle them</li>
<li>Start leading real deals by month 2. You'll own smaller opportunities early with direct support from the team</li>
<li>Begin building outbound muscle. You were excellent at outbound before, and we want you to keep that skill sharp. You'll target founders, Heads of People, and finance leads at companies in our ICP</li>
<li>Bring what you know about prospecting. We want to hear what's worked for you, what tools you've used, and what you'd do differently here. You'll have a real say in how we build our outbound motion</li>
<li>Get up to speed on the tech stack: Claude, n8n, Notion AI, Gong, and HubSpot</li>
<li>Joining and participating in our Ledgy hosted and partner co-branded events in London</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Months 3-6: Build pipeline, close deals, hit your stride</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Start self-sourcing first deals by month 3</li>
<li>Own the complete sales cycle from first touch through to close. Cycles in SMB typically run 2-6 weeks, sometimes shorter</li>
<li>Build and manage a pipeline that supports your €530k ARR annual target, with typical deal sizes of €10-15k ARR</li>
<li>Develop outbound plays targeting founders and senior leaders at companies in the 20-150 employee range</li>
<li>Test and iterate on outbound strategies. What sequences work, what channels convert, what messaging resonates. You'll own this and feed it back to the wider team</li>
<li>Close your first deals and start building references</li>
<li>Work closely with Customer Success to ensure smooth handoffs and identify early expansion signals</li>
<li>Feed back what you're hearing from the market. You'll be talking to more companies than anyone, and your insights on objections, competitive dynamics, and pricing will shape how we sell</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Months 6-12: Become the go-to AE for SMB</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Consistently hit or exceed quota. At this point you're a proven closer</li>
<li>Refine your outbound playbook and share what's working with the team</li>
<li>Start building strategic relationships with VC firms and accelerators who can refer portfolio companies</li>
<li>Pioneer new AI use cases that improve how we prospect, prep, and close</li>
<li>Contribute to sales process improvements, email sequences, demo flows, and proposal templates</li>
<li>Help define the outbound strategy for the next hire. By this point, what you've built should be repeatable</li>
<li>Show that you're ready for the next step, whether that's moving upmarket or taking on a mentoring role</li>
</ul>
