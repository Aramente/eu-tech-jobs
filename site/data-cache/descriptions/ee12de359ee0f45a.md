<p>As a Commercial Development Manager, you will play a key role in building and scaling the Wolt Benefits service in Finland. You will drive merchant expansion and onboarding across all Benefits categories, including restaurants, sport and culture and more, while collaborating closely with internal stakeholders to strengthen strategic partnerships.</p>
<p>This is a high-impact role combining hands-on commercial execution with long-term business development. It is well suited for someone who is commercially driven, structured, and motivated by developing new processes and scaling technology-enabled solutions.</p>
<h2><strong>In this role, you will</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead the growth of the Wolt Benefits merchant network across Finland</li>
<li>Support SMB sales related to the Wolt Benefits offering</li>
<li>Leading and developing a growing team within sales and account management professionals</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with sales, product, onboarding, legal, and marketing teams to ensure best-in-class processes and support product expansion in Finland</li>
<li>Develop and optimize sales materials and onboarding frameworks across all merchant verticals</li>
<li>Track and analyze KPIs, pricing, and commission structures, and audit onboarding quality to improve efficiency and profitability</li>
<li>Manage partner concerns and escalations, ensuring a smooth and responsive experience</li>
<li>Localize campaigns and merchant communications in collaboration with marketing</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Qualifications</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience in sales, business development, or commercial operations, ideally within employee benefits, e-commerce, fintech, payments, or other tech/platform businesses</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field</li>
<li>Strong communication and stakeholder management skills</li>
<li>Commercial mindset with the ability to identify and act on growth opportunities</li>
<li>Experience working cross-functionally with operations, support, and marketing teams</li>
<li>Data-driven approach with familiarity in CRM tools and BI platforms such as Looker or Power BI</li>
<li>Fluency in Finnish and English</li>
</ul>
