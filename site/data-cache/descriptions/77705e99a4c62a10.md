<p>As Revenue Strategy &amp; Operations Manager, you will play a key role in improving commercial execution, operational structure, and cross-functional alignment across a fast-moving regional business. You will work closely with senior commercial leaders and partner teams to translate strategic priorities into clear plans, scalable processes, and measurable outcomes.</p>
<p>This role is ideal for someone who enjoys solving ambiguous business problems, creating structure, driving execution across multiple stakeholders, and improving how teams operate at scale.</p>
<p><strong>Your responsibilities will include:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Partnering with regional commercial leadership on strategic and operational topics requiring problem solving, prioritisation, communication, and execution.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Supporting alignment across Sales, Revenue Operations, Analytics, Account Management, Merchant Operations, Onboarding, and other cross-functional teams.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Helping translate business priorities into clear workstreams, ownership models, timelines, and deliverables.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Improving coordination across markets to strengthen focus, prioritisation, and execution quality.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Supporting planning and execution on emerging business needs, commercial priorities, and high-impact operational initiatives.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Preparing leadership materials, frameworks, recommendations, and communication plans to support decision-making and local execution.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Driving improvements in sales and operations support for new verticals, including clearer ownership, better coordination, and stronger follow-through.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Working with onboarding and merchant-facing teams to improve backlog prioritisation, follow-up, and conversion into live outcomes.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Supporting merchant performance initiatives, including churn reduction, merchant development, activity improvements, content quality, and broader operational improvement efforts.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Coordinating product enablement initiatives and helping commercial and operational teams adopt new tools, processes, and ways of working.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Building scalable operating foundations through SOPs, enablement materials, training, onboarding support, reporting improvements, and collaboration with analytics teams.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Providing focused support on new vertical and merchant-related topics, ensuring visibility, coordination, and execution across relevant teams.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Identifying capacity gaps, execution risks, and operational bottlenecks early, and proactively proposing practical solutions.<br><br></li>
<li style="line-height: 1.2;">Turning ambiguous or fast-moving topics into clear priorities, action plans, and next steps for leadership and local teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Key focus areas</strong></p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Strategic support to commercial leadership</strong><br>Act as a trusted partner to regional commercial leaders on topics requiring structured thinking, sound judgment, alignment, and rapid execution.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Regional coordination and execution</strong><br>Create stronger structure, governance, and follow-up across regional priorities, helping local teams focus on execution and business impact.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Backlog and merchant performance improvement</strong><br>Work closely with onboarding, sales, and merchant-facing teams to improve backlog conversion, reduce churn, strengthen merchant performance, and execute key operational levers.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Product and operational enablement</strong><br>Support the rollout and adoption of product-related initiatives, while strengthening scalable operating foundations through SOPs, training, onboarding, reporting, and analytics collaboration.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>New verticals support and ownership</strong><br>Provide structure, coordination, and follow-through for new vertical sales and operations topics, helping close ownership gaps and improve execution across markets.</li>
</ol>

<ul>
<li>Strong experience in Revenue Operations, Sales Strategy, Business Operations, Consulting, Commercial Operations, or a similar role.</li>
<li>Proven ability to partner with senior stakeholders on complex topics requiring structure, prioritisation, and alignment.</li>
<li>Experience driving cross-functional coordination across multiple teams in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Strong planning and communication skills, with the ability to turn complexity into clear actions and practical next steps.</li>
