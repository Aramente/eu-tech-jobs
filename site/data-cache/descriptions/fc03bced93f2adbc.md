**About HomeMade**

HomeMade is an approved aged care provider dedicated to improving its customers' lives by empowering older people to self-manage their service providers with the assistance of our clinical and customer support teams.

Our transformative technology platform helps thousands of people retain choice and control by allowing them to create unique support plans that meet their specific needs and optimise their funds.

By working at HomeMade, you can play a crucial role in helping older people maintain their independence and live fulfilling lives in their own homes. You’ll be joining a team that is passionate and dedicated to making a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

To find out more, visit <https://homemadesupport.com.au/>

**About the role**

You’ll work collaboratively and proactively through telehealth to help customers stay safe and independent at home. You’ll triage clinical concerns with care, confidence, and clarity, providing expert clinical input to guide decisions.

Most importantly, you’ll be part of a forward thinking team reshaping aged care that genuinely values your knowledge and the impact you make every day!

Role responsibilities:

## Effectively deliver on all requirements of the Clinical Roster including:

- Monitor and triage the Clinical Inbox and wellbeing notes
- Complete clinical assessments and reviews (e.g., VATs, CNAs)
- Support customers and families with informed decision-making
- Guide palliative care and advance care planning
- Respond to incidents and clinical escalations
- Contribute to continuous improvement and clinical quality initiatives
- Provide clinical input across our broader team and provider network
- Align your practice with the Aged Care Act, Quality Standards, and our Clinical Governance Framework

## Your skills and experience:

- AHPRA Registration as a Registered Nurse
- First Aid certification
- Experience in Home and Community Care, ideally with aged care clients
- Strong clinical decision-making and case management skills
- A warm, human approach and genuine passion for empowering others
- Excellent communication and customer service skills
- Bonus points if you know your way around self-managed or consumer-directed care
