<h2><strong>🌍 About the Team</strong></h2>
<p>You will join the <strong>Healthcare Access stream</strong> within the <strong>Patient Domain</strong> at Doctolib. This stream sits at the very heart of our product: it owns the end-to-end journey patients take to access care, from discovering the right practitioner to booking their appointment and beyond.</p>
<p>Our teams are tackling some of the most exciting and high-impact challenges in digital health: <strong>optimizing search and discovery experiences</strong> to help patients find the right care faster, <strong>building seamless appointment booking funnels</strong> that work at massive scale, <strong>crafting a delightful and reliable patient app experience</strong>, and <strong>expanding access to healthcare</strong> for new users and new use cases across Europe.</p>
<p>As a Staff Engineer in this stream, your work will directly shape the interfaces and systems used by <strong>millions of patients every day</strong>. This is where web performance, frontend architecture, and user experience are not just engineering concerns - they are the foundation of how people access their health.</p>
<h2>&nbsp;</h2>
<h2><strong>🎯 Your Impact</strong></h2>
<p>As a <strong>Staff Engineer</strong>, you will be a key technical driver for the Healthcare Access stream. With a strong frontend expertise, you will shape the technical vision of our patient-facing products, ensure architectural excellence, and have a lasting impact on the way we build and evolve our platform at scale.</p>
<p>In a stream where organic search and discoverability are key growth levers, you will also be a champion of <strong>web performance and search visibility</strong>, ensuring our patient-facing surfaces excel on Core Web Vitals, SEO, and emerging Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) practices.</p>
<p>You will work in close partnership with Engineering Managers, Principal Engineers, Product Managers, and Designers to deliver high-impact projects that simplify and improve how patients access healthcare.</p>
