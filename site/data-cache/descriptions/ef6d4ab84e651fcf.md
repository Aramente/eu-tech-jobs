**About the role:**

As an **Operations Team Lead**, you will be challenged to guide and empower a team of Photo Editors to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of our operations. You will play a crucial part in ensuring the successful completion of tasks, driving key performance indicators, and fostering a collaborative and results-driven team culture.

## What you'll do

- Lead and manage day-to-day operations, ensuring efficiency, quality, and adherence to established processes.
- Manage and mentor a team of Specialists, providing guidance, support, and fostering a collaborative team environment.
- Drive and monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) to ensure the team meets or exceeds performance targets
- Design and implement performance improvement plans for team members who require such assistance
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to enhance communication and coordination across departments.
- Develop and implement strategies to improve operational processes and streamline workflows.
- Continuously assess and optimize operational procedures to enhance productivity
- Act as a point of contact for escalated issues and provide timely resolutions.
- Communicate with Senior Operations Manager/s and Head of Operations to ensure projects are on track.
- Monitor and produce regular performance reports
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement, innovation, and excellence within the operations team.

## What you'll need

- Minimum of 5 years of progressive experience in operations, with at least 2 years in a leadership role.
- A track record of running performance improvement plans
- Proven experience in effectively managing and leading a team.
- Demonstrated experience in driving and achieving Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong leadership skills
- Great spreadsheet skills are an advantage
- Familiarity with Adobe Photoshop is preferred but is not a hard requirement
- Should be willing to work with creative people.
- Should be willing to report onsite every day.
- Should be open to working in a mid-shift.
