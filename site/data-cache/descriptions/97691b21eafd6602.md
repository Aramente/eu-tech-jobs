<p><strong>Role Summary&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>Axle&nbsp;</strong>is&nbsp;seeking an <strong>AI Engineer II</strong> with experience designing and delivering production AI systems to lead the development of intelligent applications that support translational research and scientific operations. This role involves architecting retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems, agentic pipelines, document understanding workflows, and the shared platform services that make these systems reliable and reusable.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>A significant portion of this role involves working directly with clients — scientists, program managers, and operational staff — who understand the problem domain but may not have a technical background. You will partner with them to elicit requirements, translate domain needs into well-defined technical solutions, and ensure delivered software meets their real-world needs. The role requires a self-directed engineer who can make sound architectural decisions, take ownership of technical components end-to-end, mentor junior team members, and deliver iteratively while managing client relationships and expectations.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Architect, develop, and maintain AI-powered applications in Python, including conversational assistants, agentic tools, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems.</li>
<li>Design and optimize retrieval pipelines: embedding generation, vector search, hybrid search, and reranking against large domain-specific corpora.</li>
<li>Build document ingestion and parsing pipelines that extract structured content (text, tables, figures, citations) from scientific and technical documents for downstream use by AI systems.</li>
<li>Integrate structured knowledge — ontologies, controlled vocabularies, and knowledge graphs — with unstructured text to produce evidence-grounded, provenance-aware AI outputs.</li>
<li>Develop and operate shared platform services that accelerate delivery of new AI applications: model gateways, agent-tool integrations, authentication, API key management, and observability.</li>
<li>Lead technical design decisions and establish best practices for code quality, testing, evaluation, and deployment of AI systems.</li>
<li>Provision, deploy, monitor, and troubleshoot services on Kubernetes and cloud infrastructure, including container builds, manifests, and CI/CD pipelines.</li>
<li>Design and maintain relational and vector databases, including schema design, indexing, migrations, and incremental data updates.</li>
<li>Define and track quality metrics for AI systems (retrieval quality, answer faithfulness, latency, cost) and drive measurable improvements over time.</li>
<li>Engage directly with non-technical clients to gather requirements, clarify goals, manage expectations, and validate that delivered solutions align with their objectives.</li>
<li>Translate high-level client and mission needs into actionable technical specifications and development plans.</li>
<li>Mentor junior engineers and contribute to a culture of technical excellence.</li>
<li>Participate in and help facilitate standups, sprint planning, and cross-team review meetings across multiple project streams.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
