<p><a href="https://foundation.mozilla.org/en/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mozilla Foundation</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is a non-profit building a better technology future — powered by people, open by design, fueled by imagination.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We deliver people-first alternatives to today’s extractive systems. We build side by side with developers, innovators and advocates, united in the belief that a better technology future is not only possible — it’s ours to create.</span></p>
<p><strong>The Team</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Development team at Mozilla Foundation drives the philanthropic support that fuels our mission to ensure the internet is a global public resource, open and accessible to all. The team builds meaningful relationships with individual donors, foundations, and partners who share our vision for a healthier internet. Through strategic fundraising initiatives, we secure the resources needed to advance Mozilla's mission of promoting openness, innovation, and opportunity online.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mozilla Foundation’s grassroots donor community is one of its most distinctive assets: a globally distributed, multilingual base of supporters who believe in an open internet and choose to fund that belief.</span></p>
<p><strong>The Position&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Director, Grassroots Engagement is responsible for the overall strategy and performance of Mozilla Foundation’s grassroots engagement and fundraising program. This role leads how Mozilla identifies, engages, and retains grassroots supporters across channels and languages, with particular responsibility for the email program, supporter experience, and revenue performance. The role also provides leadership across team management, program strategy, vendor relationships, and cross-functional collaboration.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This role reports to the Vice President, Development, partnering closely with cross-functional leaders in Strategic Communications and Community while bringing its own point of view to how digital fundraising and civic engagement intersect in a fast-changing landscape. The role also directly manages the Manager, Grassroots Fundraising, Email Production Specialist and Email Production Associate.</span></p>
<p><strong>Job responsibilities</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Here are some projects you'll likely work on in your first 6 months:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Set the strategic direction and goals for Mozilla Foundation’s grassroots engagement and fundraising program, including key performance indicators and revenue targets.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Assess and strengthen the email program, including supporter journeys, segmentation, messaging coordination, production flow, and testing approaches.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Review email list health and donor engagement performance, and identify opportunities to improve deliverability, double opt-in conversion, unsubscribe rates, and long-term retention.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Support and develop the grassroots engagement team through clear priorities, coaching, and operating rhythms.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Build strong cross-functional partnerships with Strategic Communications, Community, and Development Operations to align program strategy, systems, and supporter experience.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Review vendor relationships, tooling, and budget needs to ensure the program is well positioned for execution and growth.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As a&nbsp; Director, Grassroots Engagement at Mozilla, you will be responsible for …</span></p>
