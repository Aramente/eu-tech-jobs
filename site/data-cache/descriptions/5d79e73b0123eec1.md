<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<div>
<div data-panel-type="info" data-local-id="887ee4bcbcdb" data-prosemirror-content-type="node" data-prosemirror-node-name="panel" data-prosemirror-node-block="true" data-pm-slice="2 2 [&quot;expand&quot;,{&quot;title&quot;:&quot;Example Job Advert (Do not edit anything in yellow)&quot;,&quot;\_\_expanded&quot;:true,&quot;localId&quot;:&quot;d21981834788&quot;}]">
<div data-panel-content="true">
<p data-local-id="5fdc5bdbaa73" data-prosemirror-content-type="node" data-prosemirror-node-name="paragraph" data-prosemirror-node-block="true">Helsing's Resilience Factory in Plymouth designs and produces the SG-1 Fathom; a subsurface glider that redefines underwater surveillance. Mass-manufacturable and designed for deployment at scale, hundreds of SG-1s can monitor vast underwater areas, patrolling in swarms or holding position on the seabed. Integrated with Helsing's Lura platform, SG-1 provides real-time detection and classification at the edge, in environments where bandwidth is low and data volumes are high.</p>
<p data-local-id="82d122ef908a" data-prosemirror-content-type="node" data-prosemirror-node-name="paragraph" data-prosemirror-node-block="true">As a Maritime Production Technician, you will be responsible for assembling, testing and quality-assuring the SG-1 glider as it moves from design into production. This is a hands-on role at the heart of Helsing's hardware capability. You will work closely with the production engineering team to build and refine the processes that allow us to manufacture at the pace and standard our mission demands. The work requires precision, attention to detail and a commitment to maintaining high standards in both product quality and workplace safety. For someone at the start of their career in defence manufacturing, this is an opportunity to contribute directly to a capability that is already reshaping how democratic nations protect their waters.</p>
