<h3><strong>Your Role</strong></h3>
<p>The Talent Acquisition Associate position gives you the opportunity to have a large impact on our firm’s growth, as you learn to identify and select our largest asset: our people. While there is no typical day on the team, you’ll have exposure to a variety of areas, including:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Candidate Experience: </strong>Act as a brand ambassador for our firm and be the first point of contact to help thousands of candidates understand why AlphaSights is a great place to work; manage candidate communications and help guide them through the interview process from application to hire; proactively sells the AlphaSights vision and value proposition to candidates; demonstrates a candidate first mentality at all times</li>
<li><strong>Pipeline Generation: </strong>Responsible for generating and managing a high quality and active pipeline of candidates; proactively source candidates by leveraging different channels (e.g. LinkedIn); use creative approaches to outreach and engage with high potential candidates with the goal of converting them into hires</li>
<li><strong>Candidate Evaluation:</strong> Responsible for screening resumes, reviewing preliminary test results, and conducting phone and over time, conducting first round interviews</li>
<li><strong>Coordination &amp; Operations: </strong>Manage candidate pipeline in a timely and efficient manner including scheduling interviews and responding to candidates’ enquiries; managing &amp; updating job postings accurately;preparing the offer letters &amp; employment contracts; populating onboarding information and related trackers&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>Campus Recruitment: </strong>Work alongside the Manager/ Senior Recruiter to manage our campus recruiting logistics, organize events, ship materials, book travel, and troubleshoot real-time issues; support the team on developing and executing talent attraction and employer branding activities such as marketing campaigns, in-office events etc.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
