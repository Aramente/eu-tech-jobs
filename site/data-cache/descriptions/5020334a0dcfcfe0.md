Founded in 2013, Voodoo is a tech company that creates mobile games and apps with a mission to entertain the world. Gathering 800 employees, 7 billion downloads, and over 200 million active users, Voodoo is the #3 mobile publisher worldwide in terms of downloads after Google and Meta. Our portfolio includes chart-topping games like Mob Control and Block Jam, alongside popular apps such as BeReal and Wizz.

**Team**

Our Publishing team plays a strategic role in the company’s growth and serves as a launchpad for Voodoo’s future leaders. The team brings together top performers from consulting, finance, and tech with a passion for product, creativity, and execution.

As part of Voodoo Gaming, we partner with studios to build games from initial concept to global launch, aiming to create the next wave of iconic, chart-topping hybrid-casual hits. With over eight billion downloads and counting, Voodoo is the world’s largest and most successful hybrid-casual publisher.

You will join a small and high performing team and your main responsibilities will be:
