<p><strong>Who We Are&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Nuro is a self-driving technology company on a mission to make autonomy accessible to all. Founded in 2016, Nuro is building the world’s most scalable driver, combining cutting-edge AI with automotive-grade hardware. Nuro licenses its core technology, the Nuro Driver™, to support a wide range of applications, from robotaxis and commercial fleets to personally owned vehicles. With technology proven over years of self-driving deployments, Nuro gives the automakers and mobility platforms a clear path to AVs at commercial scale, empowering a safer, richer, and more connected future.</p>
<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>We’re hiring a People and Compliance Site Lead to support our Houston, TX<strong>&nbsp;</strong>Operations &amp; Vehicle Facility. This is a hands-on, on-site role focused on ensuring strong daily execution, workforce readiness, and adherence to site standards and regulatory requirements.</p>
<p>You’ll act as an on-site operational partner to site leadership, focused on observing how work gets done, auditing processes and systems, and identifying gaps early to keep teams ahead of safety, compliance, and execution risks. This role ensures issues are addressed before they escalate and does not own employee relations investigations.</p>
<p><strong>About the Work<br></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor day-to-day site execution, workforce readiness, and adherence to policies and procedures</li>
<li>Conduct regular observations and audits across shifts to identify gaps in operations, safety, and manager consistency</li>
<li>Administer on-site drug screenings and readiness checks in line with company policy</li>
<li>Perform audits across attendance, onboarding, training completion, and documentation quality</li>
<li>Identify patterns, risks, and process breakdowns; document and escalate as needed</li>
<li>Flag fitness-for-duty concerns and initiate appropriate escalation</li>
<li>Partner with frontline managers to drive corrective actions and track follow-through</li>
<li>Maintain clear documentation of observations, issues, and trends</li>
<li>Conduct breakroom and team space facility audits and communicate any findings with the workplace team</li>
<li>Assist with post shift-supervisor timecard audit review and to ensure that team time edits are all submitted on-time</li>
<li>Co-lead team chat sessions with site leads to gather valuable team feedback and assist in developing team action plans.</li>
<li>Assist site leads with review of attrition data and provide insights and support follow-up to address trends</li>
<li>Leverage and implement AI tooling to improve workflows, reporting and controls activity to maximize site operations and compliance</li>
<li>Provide regular insights to site leadership and HRBPs on risks and improvement opportunities</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience in fast-paced or 24/7 environments</li>

<li>Experience with audits, inspections, or process reviews</li>
<li>Ability to identify patterns and root causes across operational issues</li>
<li>Clear communicator who can document and escalate effectively</li>

</ul>
