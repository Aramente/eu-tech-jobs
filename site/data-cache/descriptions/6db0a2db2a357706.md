## Your day-to-day:

- **Sourcing and Headhunting:** actively source, and headhunt candidates to build and maintain a robust talent pipeline by yourself or in cooperation with the Talent Sourcers
- **Interviewing:** conduct in-person interviews to assess candidates' knowledge, skills, experience, and fit for the position
- **Organization:** coordinate the recruitment process, including scheduling and facilitating interviews together with hiring managers
- **Partnership:** collaborate closely with hiring managers, HR Business Partners to understand hiring needs, and position specifications; provide expert advice on recruitment strategies and best practices
- **Offer Management:** extend and manage job offers, ensuring a positive candidate experience and alignment with company policies
- **Reference and Background Checks:** conduct thorough reference and background checks to verify candidates' qualifications and suitability for the role
- **Data Management:** maintain and update the Applicant Tracking System (ATS) with accurate recruitment data, analyze recruitment metrics to improve hiring strategies
- **Innovation and Improvement:** stay up-to-date with industry best practices and proactively implement new ideas and process improvements to enhance recruitment strategies
- **Employer Branding:** promote the company's employer brand to attract top talent and ensure a positive candidate experience throughout the recruitment process
