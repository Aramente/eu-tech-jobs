<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>As Head of Operations, you will be the operational backbone of the business—owning company-wide execution, ensuring strategic alignment, and building the systems and processes that allow us to scale. Reporting directly to the CEO, you will partner with leadership across product, engineering, sales, and customer success to drive efficiency, performance, and growth.</p>

<li>Partner with the CEO and leadership team on company OKRs, board reporting, and investor communications.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Business Operations &amp; Process Optimization</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build scalable processes across finance, people, legal, and compliance.</li>

<ul>

<li>Champion company culture as we scale, ensuring employee engagement and retention.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Financial &amp; Growth Operations</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with finance to manage budgets, forecasts, and business analytics.</li>
<li>Support fundraising activities, due diligence, and board preparation.</li>
<li>Ensure metrics-driven decision making across go-to-market and customer success.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Risk &amp; Compliance</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage vendor contracts, legal processes, and compliance requirements.</li>
<li>Build risk management practices appropriate for a fast-scaling startup.</li>
<li>Requirements</li>

<li>Strong background in building and scaling processes, with a track record of improving operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage cross-functional teams and influence without direct authority.</li>
<li>Financial acumen: comfort with budgets, forecasts, and key SaaS metrics.</li>

<li>Entrepreneurial mindset: thrives in ambiguity, builds structure from scratch, and executes with urgency.</li>
</ul>
