We are looking for a mid-level **Graphic & Motion Designer** to bring the Evry Health brand to life across every surface our members, brokers, and partners encounter. You will report directly to our Director of Communications & Member Engagement and serve as the primary creative production resource for all campaigns, communications, and member-facing materials, while also supporting design needs across Sales, Clinical, and Operations.

The right candidate not only creates visuals that look great, but that help people quickly scan and understand information. You are equally comfortable crafting a polished sales deck and animating a micro-interaction for a mobile onboarding flow. You bring graphic design rigor and motion craft, with the technical fluency to hand off production-ready assets directly to our engineering team. ***While this is a remote role, you must reside in the United States and in the Eastern or Central time zone.***

## What You'll Do

**Graphic Design & Brand Execution**

- Translate complex healthcare and benefits information into clear, engaging visual formats across channels—reducing audiences’ cognitive load and improving understanding
- Create decision-support and explanatory visuals that help people take action with confidence
- Design marketing and communications assets — email templates, direct mail, digital ads, presentations, sales collateral, event materials, and branded documents that create understanding, drive action and help to achieve Evry Health’s goals
- Maintain and apply Evry Health's visual identity across all internal and external materials
- Support multi-channel member engagement campaigns and co-marketing programs with wellness and strategic partners
- Serve as a shared design resource for teams across the company, from Sales enablement to Clinical content

**Motion Design & Animation**

- Create 2D animations for mobile and web — onboarding flows, micro-interactions, loading states, and feature illustrations
- Develop animated assets using After Effects, Rive, Lottie, and/or Blender, producing files ready for direct engineering implementation
- Produce motion graphics for marketing use cases including animated email headers, social content, and presentations
- Collaborate with Engineering and Product to ensure assets are performant and correctly integrated into our React Native app and web platform

**Asset Production & Developer Handoff**

- Export deliverables in all required formats — Lottie/JSON, SVG, WebP, GIF, MP4, and PNG
- Produce clean, developer-ready files with documentation on behavior, trigger conditions, and looping logic
- Build and evolve scalable design systems, templates and organized asset libraries to ensure consistency and efficiency across all stakeholders
