This is a long-term strategic investment for JetBrains and a key pillar of our vision for the future of software development.<br><br>JetBrains Air is the agentic development environment (ADE) within the platform where developers and teams collaborate on agent-driven development and build structured automated workflows.<br><br>Air team is looking for <strong>a Product Marketing Manager </strong>to help developers understand, adopt, and succeed with Air in their daily agentic development workflows. In this role, you will work at the intersection of product, developer experience, and go-to-market to ensure Air’s capabilities are clearly understood and effectively adopted by developers. You will collaborate closely with product, design, and marketing teams to translate product capabilities into clear positioning and practical guidance.</div>
