We are looking for a highly organized and proactive **Office Manager** to join our Cyprus office in **Limassol**. In this role, you will ensure the smooth day-to-day running of the office while providing administrative support to directors and different departments. The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, reliable, and comfortable coordinating with both internal teams and external partners.

As our Office Manager, you’ll be the **heartbeat of the office**, ensuring a smooth and welcoming environment for employees, visitors, and partners.

## What you'll do:

- Provide administrative support to directors and other departments within limited responsibility.
- Manage day-to-day office operations including mail, phone calls, couriers, deliveries, and office supplies.
- Ensure the office is organized, well-stocked, and running efficiently.
- Coordinate with cleaning contractors and other service providers to maintain a professional work environment.
- Manage office setup projects including office moves or workspace changes.
- Coordinate the selection and purchase of office furniture and equipment.
- Work with external contractors and service providers to execute office improvements and projects.
- Fully organize business trips including flights, accommodation, and travel logistics.
- Prepare travel orders and provide comprehensive support for international visitors.
- Support employee relocations and coordinate with legal advisors on migration-related matters.
- Manage administrative workflows through digital tools and email.
- Coordinate with internal and external IT support on office or employee-related technical matters.
- Support the Sales team by preparing reports and entering data into internal systems within limited responsibility.
- Support the Finance team by preparing reports, entering data, and assisting with invoice and IO preparation.
