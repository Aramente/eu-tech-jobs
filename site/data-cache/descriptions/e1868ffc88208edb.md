<p><strong>Team Lead, Implementation, Southeast Asia &amp; India</strong></p>
<p>As Team Lead, Implementation, Southeast Asia &amp; India, you will be based in our Singapore office, leading and growing a high-performing team of Implementation Engineers across our offices in Singapore and Bangalore. You’ll combine strong people leadership with technical depth to ensure our customers launch quickly and sustainably, with robust and scalable integrations, while also driving continuous improvement across the broader APAC and global Implementation function.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<p>Lead &amp; define success</p>
<ul>
<li>Set direction and strategy for Implementation in South East Asia &amp; India, aligned to long-term regional and global priorities.</li>
<li>Drive implementation excellence by ensuring customers go live efficiently with scalable, production-grade integrations.</li>
<li>Coach and develop your team through clear goals, frequent feedback, mentoring, and performance management.</li>
<li>Be the escalation layer and sparring partner for complex technical projects, stakeholder management, and customer outcomes.</li>
<li>Foster an inspiring, feedback-driven culture that reflects the Adyen Formula and raises the quality bar as the team scales.</li>
</ul>
<p>Deliver results &amp; scale impact</p>
<ul>
<li>Deploy talent to the highest-impact work, balancing customer outcomes, learning opportunities, and business priorities.</li>
<li>Act as a process multiplier, improving tooling, ways of working, and delivery velocity, favoring long-term solutions over quick fixes.</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally (Sales, Account Management, Product, Support, and Ops) to remove blockers and improve end-to-end customer experience.</li>
<li>Contribute to global initiatives that improve the scale, consistency, and quality of Implementation across regions.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who you are</strong></p>
<p>Leadership &amp; team building</p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of people leadership experience, managing Implementation Engineers, Technical Consultants, or similar technical delivery teams.</li>
<li>Proven ability to hire, retain, and develop top talent, including making tough calls to maintain a high quality bar.</li>
<li>You build strong, inclusive teams and lead beyond your direct scope to strengthen culture and collaboration.</li>
</ul>
<p>Cross-functional collaboration</p>
<ul>
<li>Proven track record of working cross-functionally with Account Management, Sales, Tech (Product and Engineering), Support, and other Operations teams to ensure cohesive merchant experiences.</li>
<li>The ability to foster strong cross-team collaboration, partnering seamlessly across Adyen to remove blockers, align on goals, and provide an exceptional, end-to-end customer experience.</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical &amp; execution strength</p>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on experience delivering complex integration projects in enterprise environments (payments/fintech preferred).</li>
<li>Track record of driving operational improvements, process changes, tooling, and scalable methodologies.</li>
<li>Comfortable and effective operating in a global organization, collaborating across time zones and cultures.</li>
</ul>
<p>Language &amp; location</p>
<ul>
<li>Fluent in English (written and spoken).</li>
<li>This role is based in Singapore and expects strong in-person collaboration (office first). The role will also require traveling to India occasionally to spend time with the team and key stakeholders in Bangalore and/or Mumbai.</li>
</ul>
