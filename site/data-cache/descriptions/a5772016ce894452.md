<p>Airtable is the AI-powered app platform that empowers teams to build custom workflows, automate processes, and manage critical business data — all without needing to code. From marketing and product to operations and HR, organizations of all sizes rely on Airtable to move faster and work smarter.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The Role&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>We’re looking for a <strong>Field Marketing Marketing Manager (Contractor) </strong>based in Sydney to drive pipeline generating marketing programs across the ANZ region. This is a pipeline-first role: you'll design and execute regional marketing programs that directly generate and accelerate enterprise pipeline in ANZ.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Reporting into Field Marketing, AMER/ANZ, you’ll work closely with the local sales leadership, cross-functional marketing teams, and local partners to create impactful regional programs tailored to the Australian and New Zealand markets. You'll own a region end-to-end, with direct line of sight to pipeline and revenue impact. At Airtable, you won't just run programs — you'll build them with AI, using the same tools you're marketing to your customers.</p>
<h1><strong>What you'll do</strong></h1>
<p><strong>Drive Pipeline Through Field &amp; Demand Programs&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Plan and execute integrated marketing programs (digital campaigns, executive roundtables, field events, webinars, partner activations) working alongside the AMER Field Marketing team&nbsp;</li>
<li>Partner closely with Sales to align on account priorities and pipeline goals</li>
<li>Support ABM initiatives in collaboration with global marketing and regional sales teams&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Build and Execute Regional Marketing Strategy&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and implement the ANZ regional marketing plan aligned to global priorities and regional revenue targets&nbsp;</li>
<li>Identify growth opportunities within key industries and accounts in the region</li>
<li>Localize global campaigns and messaging to resonate with the ANZ market</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Collaborate Cross-Functionally&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the primary ANZ marketing point of contact for regional stakeholders and partners</li>
<li>Coordinate with global campaign, content, digital, and product marketing teams</li>
<li>Manage external agencies or vendors as needed&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Measure &amp; Optimize&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Track program performance, pipeline impact, and ROI&nbsp;</li>
<li>Provide regular reporting and insights to regional and global leadership</li>
<li>Continuously test, learn, and optimize regional tactics</li>
</ul>
<h1><strong>Who you are</strong></h1>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of B2B marketing experience, preferably in SaaS or technology</li>
<li>Experience owning regional or field marketing programs in ANZ&nbsp;</li>
<li>Genuine enthusiasm for building with and using AI in your work&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong understanding of demand generation, ABM, and pipeline marketing</li>
<li>Proven ability to work cross-functionally in a fast-paced, global environment</li>
<li>Strong project management and communication skills&nbsp;</li>

<li>40 hours / week commit&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to Have&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>

<li>Strong understanding of PLG -&gt; enterprise motions&nbsp;</li>
<li>Experience marketing to enterprise and mid-market audiences&nbsp;</li>
<li>Familiarity with the ANZ technology ecosystem&nbsp;</li>
<li>Experience working with CRM and marketing automation tools&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
