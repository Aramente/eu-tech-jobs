<p>JetBrains is a well-known international software development company. Ever since we started, back in 2000, we have strived to make the strongest, most effective developer tools on earth. Our products have more than 12.5 million users worldwide, and we count 88 of the Fortune Global 100 companies among our customers. As JetBrains continues to expand its business activities, we are seeking a proactive legal professional to support our internal procurement and business stakeholders.</p>
<p>We are looking for a legal professional to join our Legal team and support the procurement function across the company. The role focuses on the lifecycle management of supplier and vendor agreements, ensuring that commercial objectives are achieved while maintaining appropriate legal and operational safeguards. You will partner with Procurement, Finance, Security, Product, IT, Data Privacy, and Compliance to ensure supplier relationships are structured effectively and risks are appropriately managed. This role combines commercial contract review, stakeholder coordination, and structured risk management within a fast-paced global environment.</p>
<p>This is a hybrid position. Most team members work partly from home, but we also meet regularly in the office.</p>
<h2><strong>In this role you will:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Own and drive the end-to-end contract management process for procurement-related agreements, from request validation through execution and post-signature follow-up.</li>
<li>Review, draft, and negotiate supplier agreements, including vendor contracts, service agreements, technology procurement agreements, and related documentation.</li>
<li>Assess incoming contracts (e.g., JetBrains templates vs. supplier templates) and define an appropriate review and negotiation strategy based on risk, scope, and business needs.</li>
<li>Act as a project manager for contract negotiations with suppliers, coordinating internal stakeholders and consolidating feedback into a clear and consistent position.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Procurement and business stakeholders to ensure contracts support operational and strategic objectives.</li>
<li>Identify contractual and operational risks associated with supplier agreements and prepare structured risk assessments where required.</li>
<li>Drive appropriate internal reviews with relevant stakeholders (e.g., Security, Privacy, Compliance, Finance) and ensure alignment with company policies.</li>
<li>Manage contract execution processes, including e-signature workflows, quality control, and adherence to internal approval standards.</li>
<li>Ensure proper post-signature governance by archiving agreements, tracking contractual obligations, and documenting non-standard terms.</li>
</ul>
<p>Experience in one or more of the following areas is considered an advantage: vendor and supplier contracting, technology procurement, SaaS or IT services agreements, or structured risk management.</p>
<h2><strong>Requirements</strong></h2>
<p>Our new colleague should possess and demonstrate:</p>
<ul>
<li>A degree in Law, Business Administration, or a related field.</li>
<li>5+ years of relevant post-university experience, ideally in a multinational corporation or international business environment.</li>
<li>Strong experience in commercial contract review and negotiation, particularly in supplier or vendor agreements.</li>
<li>Practical understanding of risk assessment and the ability to escalate and communicate complex issues clearly.</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple stakeholders and drive processes across cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to manage multiple parallel negotiations.</li>

</ul>
