<p>You will make an impact that matters by:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vision and Strategy</strong>: Own and develop the product vision and strategy for the Live Encoding product, ensuring alignment with company goals, market trends, and customer feedback.</li>
<li><strong>Customer Engagement</strong>: Directly interact with customers and sales opportunities to deeply understand their needs, pain points, and use cases. Own the technical validation and integration process to ensure successful adoption of our product.</li>
<li><strong>Requirements Gathering and Prioritization</strong>: Work closely with internal and external stakeholders to gather, analyze, and prioritize product requirements, translating them into actionable roadmaps.</li>
<li><strong>Product Development and Launch</strong>: Oversee the entire product lifecycle, from requirement specification and design to technical implementation, launch, and go-to-market activities.</li>
<li><strong>Market Analysis</strong>: Stay informed about market trends and the competitive landscape, conducting analyses and user research to identify opportunities and challenges.</li>
<li><strong>Product Optimization</strong>: Continuously assess and enhance the product to improve user experience, adoption, and overall value.</li>
<li><strong>KPI Management</strong>: Define and own key performance indicators (KPIs) that measure product success and drive progress toward company objectives.</li>
<li><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong>: Work effectively with Engineering, Sales, Marketing, and other teams to ensure successful product development and launch.</li>
</ul>

<li>Extensive product management experience in product management, ideally in the streaming media industry, with a focus on SaaS products and customer-facing responsibilities.</li>
<li>Solid technical understanding of product architecture, enabling effective collaboration with engineering teams, and the ability to guide customers through technical integrations.</li>

<ul>
