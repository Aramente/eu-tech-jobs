<h3>ABOUT THE TEAM</h3>
<p>We are a dynamic, international team developing a UWB-based Real-Time Locating System (RTLS) that powers solutions for industrial applications. To achieve the precision and scalability required for modern IoT solutions, we build full stack of software components, including firmware, backend, and frontend applications.</p>
<p>Join our full-stack team to scale existing solutions and create innovative KINEXON products in close collaboration with colleagues from our software and hardware departments.</p>
