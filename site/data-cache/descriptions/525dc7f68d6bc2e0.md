<p>The primary responsibility of the Partner Manager (AI Boutiques) is to build and develop our ecosystem of AI-native boutiques, specialist SIs and consultancies.</p>
<p>The Anthropic Alliances team's function is to extend and accelerate adoption of Claude and its developer platform by recruiting and developing a portfolio of consulting, services, technology and selling partners.</p>
<p>This role focuses on partners who are building Claude-first practices—typically smaller, more agile firms with deep AI implementation expertise. These partners deliver proof points, train the talent pool, and drive adoption velocity in the mid-market to complement our developing GSI relationships. You will work alongside a GSI-focused counterpart who also manages larger regional SIs.&nbsp; This is a hands-on role suited to a 0-1 operator capable of balancing external-facing partner management with internal-facing sales enablement. You will report to the Head of Emerging Partnerships, International and form a key part of the local GTM team, working in lock-step with them to build Claude's presence and drive market share in your market. You will collaborate closely with sales and Applied AI, in addition to marketing, customer success and centralised programme management.</p>
<p>This position requires a candidate with experience building and managing service-focused partners and the ability to juggle multiple strategic priorities simultaneously: enabling existing partners to drive immediate impact, embedding partners into active sales cycles, and prospecting new partners to address coverage gaps.</p>

<h2><strong>How we're different</strong></h2>
<p>We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.</p>
<p>The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI &amp; Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.</p>
