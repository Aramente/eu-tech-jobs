<p>The Head of Benefits leads the global strategy, design, governance, and delivery of Nebius’ employee benefits programs. You will ensure our benefits framework supports employee wellbeing, reinforces our high-performance culture, and remains competitive and scalable during rapid company growth.</p>
<p>Reporting to the Director of Total Rewards, you will serve as a strategic partner to HR leadership, Finance, Legal, and senior business stakeholders to ensure benefits programs are cost-effective, compliant, and aligned with our global people strategy.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<h3>Key responsibilities</h3>
<p><strong>Benefits Strategy &amp; Architecture</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and evolve global benefits programs including health &amp; wellbeing, retirement/pension, risk benefits (life, disability) and time-off policies. Ensure offerings are competitive, equitable, and aligned with company growth and workforce demographics.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Global Governance &amp; Policy Frameworks</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish global eligibility frameworks, harmonised policies, and governance standards while allowing for necessary local market variation. Ensure consistency and fairness across geographies.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Financial Oversight &amp; Vendor Management</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own benefits budgeting, forecasting, and cost modeling. Lead relationships and negotiations with brokers, insurers, and third-party administrators to optimize value, service quality, and risk management.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Employee Experience &amp; Communication</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build a clear benefits narrative and create communication materials (FAQs, guides, enrollment materials, manager toolkits) to ensure employees understand and value their total rewards package. Drive benefits education and engagement strategies.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Key/specific qualifications, knowledge &amp; experiences</h3>
<ul>
<li>Significant experience leading global benefits strategy in high-growth, international environments (technology sector experience preferred).</li>
<li>Deep technical knowledge of health &amp; welfare, retirement, risk benefits, and wellbeing program design across multiple jurisdictions.</li>
<li>Familiarity with major global brokers, benefits administration platforms, and HRIS systems.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Competencies &amp; behavioural traits</h3>
<ul>
<li>Executive experience: High credibility when interacting with C-suite leaders and Board members.</li>
<li>Strategic Clarity: Ability to translate complex equity concepts into simple, persuasive narratives for diverse stakeholders.</li>
<li>Principled Judgment: Strong integrity and a governance-first mindset when handling sensitive executive data and exceptions.</li>
<li>Operational Discipline: Exceptional attention to detail in a role where minor errors have significant financial and legal consequences.</li>
<li>Navigating Ambiguity: Comfort building scalable frameworks in a fast-paced, evolving environment.</li>
</ul>
