<h3><strong>Our mission is to protect life.</strong></h3>
<p>We’re out to make the world a safer place by solving big problems and taking on the public safety challenges of our time. From our company's inception building the TASER to a full suite of hardware and software solutions, we are focused on providing police agencies with state-of-the-art devices and services they need to successfully serve and protect us. In the next few years, we're going to eliminate the burden of paperwork in policing, so officers can increase the time they spend building relationships and serving in their communities. We’ll put video at the heart of police records so our justice system can get to the truth faster. And we won't stop innovating until the bullet is rendered obsolete.<br><br>It’s a big mission, but it’s one we’ll pursue relentlessly every single day. <br><br></p>
<h3><strong>Your Impact</strong></h3>
<p>As a senior bilingual member of our Technical Support team, you will serve as a critical bridge between Axon and our Spanish-speaking customers and law enforcement partners across the U.S., Latin America, and Europe. You will deliver expert-level technical support in both Spanish and English via phone and email, helping resolve complex product issues while ensuring a seamless, culturally aware customer experience. You will work cross-functionally on projects and provide support to improve the overall customer experience. This is a customer-focused role that sets Axon apart from our competitors. By providing a world class bilingual experience to our customers, you will drive further adoption of our products and make the world a safer place.</p>
<h4><strong>What You’ll Do </strong></h4>
<p><strong>Location: </strong>Bucharest, Romania<br><strong>Reports to: </strong>Director of International Support<br><strong>Direct Reports: </strong>N/A</p>
<ul>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to AXON Hardware and Software products</li>
<li>Become a subject matter expert in Axon’s product base</li>
<li>Provide world class support to assist customers with complex issues while maintaining service levels</li>
<li>Identify and report product bugs and partner with internal departments to help drive resolution</li>
<li>Use data and customer feedback to identify and surface product insights and trends</li>
<li>Advocate on behalf of users to optimize and improve our products and user experience</li>
<li>Provide bilingual (Spanish/English) technical support via phone, email, and chat to Spanish-speaking customers, ensuring clear and accurate communication in both languages</li>
<li>Translate and localize technical documentation, knowledge base articles, and customer-facing communications as needed</li>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact for Spanish-language cases, managing the Spanish support queue and ensuring timely resolution</li>
<li>Support the onboarding and training of Spanish-speaking customers on Axon products and solutions</li>
<li>Collaborate with international support teams to align on best practices for serving Spanish-speaking markets</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong interpersonal and communication skills, including excellent listening, conflict resolution and customer empathy</li>
