<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The role</h3>
<p>As an FP&amp;A Analyst, you’ll play a key role in shaping commercial decisions across the business, partnering closely with leaders to turn insight into action.</p>
<p>This is a hands‑on role where you’ll influence outcomes, support growth, and help teams understand the numbers behind their decisions.</p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">What you'll be doing</h3>
<p><strong>Financial analysis and reporting</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Analyse departmental performance against targets and identify trends, risks and opportunities</li>
<li>Prepare monthly management accounts and contribute to board reporting</li>
<li>Deliver clear variance analysis with practical, commercial recommendations</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Budgeting and forecasting</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Partner with leaders to build annual budgets and long‑term forecasts</li>
<li>Track performance against plan and provide proactive guidance on cost and resourcing</li>
<li>Continuously improve forecasting models and accuracy</li>
<li>Work closely with Financial Control and Treasury through month end</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Business partnering</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Act as a trusted advisor to department heads, supporting better decision‑making</li>
<li>Provide financial insight across business initiatives, investments and ad‑hoc projects</li>
<li>Monitor commercial performance and help stakeholders connect activity with outcomes</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">What you'll need to succeed</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Bachelor’s degree in finance, accounting, or related field.</li>
<li>Proven experience in working with department leaders or similar role.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, financial modelling, and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities to work effectively with cross-functional teams.</li>
<li>Proficiency in financial software, including ERP systems and advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel. Previous experience using Anaplan is desirable.</li>
<li>Ability to handle confidential information with discretion and professionalism.</li>
</ul>
