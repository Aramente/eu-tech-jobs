<h2 data-start="318" data-end="353"><strong data-start="321" data-end="353">The Role (What’s in the Box)</strong></h2>
<p>As a <strong>Senior Business Development Manager</strong>, you will lead new business growth for HelloFresh’s Retail Media offering across the Nordics (Denmark, Sweden, Norway).</p>
<p>This is a sales role in the advertising space, where you’ll focus on acquiring new advertisers and agency partners, <strong>while also growing and scaling partnerships with existing advertisers and agencies</strong>, selling our digital and physical media solutions, and driving long-term revenue.</p>
<p>You’ll play a key role in positioning HelloFresh as a <strong>strategic Retail Media partner</strong> for brands, while building a strong pipeline and closing high-impact deals.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Drive new business</strong>: Prospect, pitch, and close advertising deals with brands and media agencies across the Nordics</li>
<li><strong>Build a strong pipeline</strong>: Own outbound activity and manage opportunities through the full sales cycle</li>
<li><strong>Sell Retail Media solutions</strong>: Position HelloFresh’s ad inventory (onsite, offsite, and physical) as part of clients’ media mix</li>
<li><strong>Develop client relationships</strong>: Engage senior stakeholders and grow long-term partnerships</li>
<li><strong>Create compelling proposals</strong>: Use data and insights to demonstrate ROI (ROAS, CPC, CPM)</li>
<li><strong>Own revenue targets</strong>: Forecast accurately and track performance via CRM (HubSpot)</li>
<li><strong>Collaborate cross-functionally</strong>: Work with Retail Media, Marketing, Partnerships, and global teams to deliver campaigns</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What You Bring</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Experience in <strong>sales, business development, or media/advertising (Retail Media, digital ads, media agencies, adtech, or e-commerce)</strong></li>
<li>Proven ability to <strong>win new business and drive revenue growth</strong></li>
<li>Strong understanding of <strong>advertising metrics and media planning</strong></li>
