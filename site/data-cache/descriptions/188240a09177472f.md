**About the Role**

We are looking for a seasoned HR Business Partner to lead & manage the 3P / Contract Workforce across multiple Teams and locations. This role will act as the primary HRBP for all third-party employees, driving workforce governance, employee relations, compliance, & productivity in close partnership with Operations. The incumbent will manage a team of 4 HRBPs and work closely with Staffing Partners, TA, HR Ops, Legal, and Business managers to ensure seamless lifecycle management, strong compliance discipline, and a high-performance field workforce. This is a highly field-intensive role requiring strong stakeholder management, ER expertise, data-driven decision-making, & the ability to handle escalations with maturity & speed.

## What you will do

- **Workforce Planning & Hiring**
- Partner with Business Manager and Biz finance teams on manpower planning & workforce budgeting.
- Maintain visibility on headcount status, vacancies, redeployments, & attrition trends across Teams.
- Coordinate with staffing vendors and TA to support hiring for 3P roles & ensure timely fulfillment.
- **Onboarding & Documentation**
- Oversee onboarding processes including documentation, BGV, contract issuance, & HRMS updates.
- Ensure statutory compliance, minimum wages adherence, & labour law norms during employee onboarding.
- Coordinate contract renewals and maintain complete lifecycle documentation.
- Attendance, Payroll & Workforce Governance
- Validate payroll inputs, leave records, and attendance data in partnership with vendors & HR Ops.
- Conduct regular audits to ensure compliance, documentation accuracy, & audit readiness.
- Track productivity, attendance discipline, and overall workforce hygiene across Teams.
- **Employee Experience & Engagement**
- Conduct regular employee connects, field visits, & skip-level meetings to proactively identify concerns.
- Standardize employee communication and drive awareness of policies and code of conduct.
- Implement structured grievance redressal mechanisms and ensure timely closure of employee concerns.
- Drive performance management processes for 3P employees in collaboration with Business teams
- **Employee Relations & Compliance**
- Manage ER cases related to misconduct, fraud, absenteeism, performance issues,
- and policy violations.
- Conduct investigations, maintain documentation, and recommend appropriate
- disciplinary actions.
- **Retention & Attrition Management**
- Conduct exit interviews, analyze attrition drivers, & recommend targeted retention strategies.
- Partner with operations and vendors to address workforce stability & redeployment needs.

**Our Mission**

Democratising internet commerce for everyone — Meesho (Meri Shop) started with a single idea in mind: to be an e-commerce destination for Indian consumers and to enable small businesses to succeed online.

We provide our sellers with benefits such as zero commission and affordable shipping solutions in the market. Today, sellers nationwide are growing their businesses by tapping into Meesho’s large and diverse customer base, state-of-the-art tech infrastructure, and pan-India logistics network through trusted third-party partners.

Affordable, relatable merchandise that mirrors local markets has helped us connect with internet users and serve customers across urban, semi-urban, and rural India. Our unique business model and continuous innovation have established us as a part of India’s e-commerce ecosystem.
