<p><strong>THE WORK:</strong></p>
<p>If you’re ready to make an immediate impact and help scale a sophisticated, global payroll environment, join us and play a key role in delivering accurate, compliant, and scalable payroll operations across our European footprint and select global entities.</p>
<p><strong>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead end-to-end payroll processing across multiple regions including,&nbsp;UK, Ireland, Switzerland, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Austria, Germany, Brazil, and Iceland</li>
<li>Handle monthly payroll cycles across all assigned entities, ensuring;&nbsp;Payroll processing that is detailed and delivered in a timely manner.&nbsp;Compliance with local statutory, tax, and labor regulations.&nbsp;Alignment with global payroll standards.</li>
<li>Be responsible for and process equity-related transactions, including:&nbsp;RSUs, stock options, and tender-related payments.&nbsp;Coordination with Equity, Tax, and external vendors.&nbsp;Validation of gross-to-net calculations and payroll reporting impacts</li>
<li>Partner closely with ADP Celergo (or equivalent global payroll providers) to:&nbsp;Ensure accurate data transmission and payroll execution.&nbsp;Troubleshoot payroll issues and handle partner concerns&nbsp;Support ongoing system enhancements and integrations</li>
<li>Support global mobility payroll activities, including:&nbsp;Cross-border tax implications.&nbsp;Shadow payrolls (where applicable).&nbsp;Coordination with tax advisors and mobility partners.</li>
<li>Manage pension, social security, and statutory remittances across all countries:&nbsp;Validate employer/employee contributions.&nbsp;Ensure timely and accurate filings and payments.</li>
<li>Own payroll bookkeeping and GL account matching processes:&nbsp;Prepare and review payroll journal entries.&nbsp;Reconcile payroll to the general ledger.&nbsp;Partner with Accounting and FP&amp;A on variance analysis and reporting.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact for:&nbsp;Internal collaborators (HR, Finance, Treasury, Equity, Tax)&nbsp;External vendors and payroll providers.&nbsp;Audit and compliance requests.</li>
<li>Support month-end and year-end close activities, including:&nbsp;Payroll accruals and reconciliations.&nbsp;Statutory filings (e.g., P60s, annual returns, etc.)&nbsp;Audit readiness and documentation</li>
<li>Drive process improvements and transformation initiatives, including:&nbsp;Payroll standardization across entities.&nbsp;Automation and system enhancements.&nbsp;Integration of newly acquired entities (M&amp;A payroll onboarding)</li>
</ul>
