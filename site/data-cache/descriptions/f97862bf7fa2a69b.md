<h2>About CarbonChain</h2>
<p>Want to work on the most pressing challenge of our generation? If you have experience building exceptional products, know how to execute at scale, and want to help build a company for the long term, we'd love to hear from you.</p>
<p>CarbonChain helps the world’s biggest supply chains measure and reduce their carbon emissions, using verified primary data where possible, and robust defaults where it isn’t. We build software that turns messy, real-world operational and supplier information into decision-grade carbon and compliance insights, so teams can act: improve sourcing, meet regulations like CBAM, and deliver credible reporting to customers, investors, and regulators.</p>
<p>We’re a product-led company operating in a high-stakes domain. Data quality, auditability, reliability, and trust matter as much as speed. If you enjoy building systems that customers rely on, and shipping improvements that measurably change behaviour across a network, you’ll feel at home here.</p>
<h2>Our Product</h2>
<p>Our platform combines real-world emissions data with our customers' supply chain information to create actionable insights. We help sustainability teams answer critical questions: Where are the emissions hotspots in our supply chain? Which suppliers should we prioritize? What's the carbon impact of switching vendors or routes?</p>
<p>A major focus of what we’re building right now is supplier engagement and CBAM data management:</p>
<ul>
<li>enabling installations (goods producers) to prepare, manage, and share CBAM datasets through guided product carbon accounting workflows;</li>
<li>improving our supplier database and connection management to support sharing across supply chains;</li>
<li>and building ingestion pipelines that turn messy customer files into structured, provenance-tracked, insights-ready data.</li>
</ul>
<p>Beyond CBAM, our platform supports product and corporate carbon footprinting used by some of the world’s largest commodities producers and traders, helping teams move from data collection to credible reporting and decision-making</p>
