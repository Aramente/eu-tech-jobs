Mayflower is a technology company that alters the entertainment industry to a new level of perception and engagement.  
We are building the future of live entertainment. We believe that by providing people with the best and highest-quality live streaming experience in entertainment, we can tackle the world’s “problems” better, together!  
We are seeking an experienced **Talent Acquisition Supervisor** to join our passionate team.

The main purpose is to drive the end-to-end hiring strategy and execution for the Technology & Product Stream.   
You will act as the Lead for your domain, balancing team leadership (4-5 recruiters), high-level stakeholder management, and personal delivery of critical roles. Your goal is to transform recruitment into a predictable, data-driven partner function.

### **Key Responsibilities**

1. **Stakeholder & Expectation Management**

* Lead intake meetings for complex/high-priority roles to align on scope, compensation reality, and timelines.
* Manage "tough" stakeholders by using data to justify hiring strategies or pivot when necessary.
* Escalate business misalignment early with proposed solutions, not just problems.

**2. Team Leadership & Development**

* Translate company-level goals into clear weekly/monthly KPIs for your recruiters.
* Conduct weekly 1:1s focused on both delivery (blocker removal) and professional coaching.
* Perform regular Funnel Reviews to identify systemic bottlenecks before they become escalations.
* Facilitate Team Retros to refine sourcing and assessment techniques.

**3. Operational Excellence & Analytics**

* Provide realistic monthly and quarterly closing predictions.

* Monitor Time-to-Hire, Offer Acceptance Rate (OAR), and Funnel Conversion.

* Ensure every recruiter maintains high data hygiene in the ATS for real-time reporting.

**4. Personal Contribution (Lead by Example)**

* Directly manage and close 2**–4** senior or complex roles at a time.
* Support the team in high-stakes candidate negotiations and closing strategies.
* Stream-Level Projects (Tech & Product Focus)
