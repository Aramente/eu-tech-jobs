<p>We are currently looking for a reliable, proactive, and people-oriented HR Specialist to join our HR team. <h3><strong>What you’ll do&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Onboarding</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate onboarding for new employees in Munich and remote locations.</li>
<li>Prepare and manage employment contracts and documentation.</li>
<li>Manage student and internship employment processes, including contracts, onboarding, and internship changes.</li>
<li>Support employees during their first few weeks and organize onboarding activities.</li>
<li>Coordinate payroll information and benefits administration.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Employee administration</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare employment confirmations and contract amendments.</li>
<li>Maintain and update internal knowledge materials.</li>
<li>Maintain employee records, HR system data, and documentation.</li>
<li>Respond to employee inquiries and provide support with HR tools (HiBob).</li>
<li>Contribute to HR process improvements, internal documentation, and HR projects.</li>
<li>Support employee terminations and offboarding processes, including documentation and benefit cancellations.</li>
<li>Assist in resolving conflicts.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What we are looking for&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>At least three years of experience in HR.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Knowledge of German labor and social security laws and payroll systems.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Fluency in German and English, with the ability to communicate clearly and professionally in both languages.</li>
<li>Experience in the employment of foreign citizens.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Experience in preparing personnel documentation and maintaining personal files.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Good organizational skills and a detail-oriented mindset.</li>
<li>Knowledge of HR software and Microsoft Office.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills, the ability to work in a team, and a sense of responsibility.</li>
</ul>
