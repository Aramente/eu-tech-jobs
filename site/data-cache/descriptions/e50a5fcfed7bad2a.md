<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<div>
<p>As Senior Commercial Manager for Helsing Germany, you will play a key role in successfully taking our industry leading AI and Software products to market with both our strategic defence partners and military customers. Working closely with our German Business Development and Customer Success teams, you will develop innovative commercial strategies to propel the Helsing mission to provide AI to defend our democracies. You will be instrumental in unlocking the power of AI in Defence. This is an exciting opportunity to build an important new AI led defence tech company which will help redefine military capability.</p>
