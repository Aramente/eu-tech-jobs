<h4><strong>Your Impact</strong></h4>
<p>As a Senior Sales Engineer, Public Safety (Mountain or Pacific Region)<strong>,</strong> you are both a technical expert and a storyteller. You’ll work closely with Account Executives (AEs), Regional Sales Managers (RSMs), and customers to translate complex technical requirements into compelling solutions. You will lead discovery, configure and demonstrate our platform, manage technical validations, and ensure a smooth handoff to post-sales implementation.<br><br>This role is ideal for someone who understands the intersection of <strong>technology, public safety operations, and customer success</strong>, and who thrives in a mission-driven, fast-paced environment. <br><br><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>What You’ll Do </strong></span></p>
<p><strong>Pre-Sales Solutioning</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with AEs and RSMs to qualify opportunities, understand customer challenges, and design tailored technical solutions.</li>
<li>Conduct in-depth discovery with 911 centers, emergency management agencies, and public safety IT teams.</li>
<li>Deliver engaging product demonstrations, technical workshops, and proof-of-concept sessions tailored to the agency’s environment.</li>
<li>Lead and respond to RFPs, RFIs, and security questionnaires with accuracy and professionalism.</li>
<li>Translate complex technical concepts into clear, benefit-focused explanations for non-technical audiences.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Technical Leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the subject-matter expert for Prepared’s platform architecture, integrations, APIs, and data workflows.</li>
<li>Provide configuration and system design guidance based on customer requirements, existing CAD/NG911 systems, and IT infrastructure.</li>
<li>Advise agencies on best practices for network security, system interoperability, and deployment planning.</li>
<li>Stay up-to-date with public safety software trends, standards (NG911, CAD, RMS, PSAP operations), and competitor offerings.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Sales Enablement &amp; Collaboration</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Work alongside Sales, Product, and Engineering to continuously improve our demo environments, messaging, and field readiness.</li>
<li>Collect and relay customer feedback to influence product roadmap and technical documentation.</li>
<li>Support proposal teams with technical content, diagrams, and specifications.</li>
<li>Assist marketing and partner teams in developing technical collateral, webinars, and events.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Customer Advocacy</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build trust with agency decision-makers, IT administrators, and operations leaders by demonstrating deep understanding of public safety workflows.</li>
<li>Ensure technical confidence throughout the sales cycle to accelerate buying decisions.</li>
<li>Represent Prepared at tradeshows, conferences, and industry events as a technical ambassador.</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>What You Bring</strong></span></h4>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of progressive experience in <strong>public safety, emergency communications, or PSAP operations</strong>, with senior-level leadership experience strongly preferred (Director, Assistant Director, Executive Director, or equivalent).</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience <strong>selling, implementing, or enabling public safety software</strong> (e.g., NG911 call handling, CAD, RMS, ESInet, radio systems, mass notification, or emergency communications platforms).</li>
<li>Proven ability to operate as a <strong>field-facing technical advisor</strong> to Sales—owning discovery, solution design, demonstrations, technical validation, and RFP/RFI responses end-to-end.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of <strong>PSAP workflows, operational constraints, funding models, procurement cycles, and compliance environments</strong>, including experience presenting to boards, executives, and elected officials.</li>
<li>Strong technical fluency across <strong>cloud-based platforms, integrations/APIs, network infrastructure, data workflows, and system interoperability</strong> within public safety environments.</li>
</ul>
