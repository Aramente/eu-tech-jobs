## About Tandem

Tandem is the leading global language learning community, where millions of members from over 200 countries practice languages together. The concept of learning from each other is not only an integral part of the Tandem community, but it also shapes our everyday work. Through this shared connection, we help each other grow and enrich the language journeys of our members.<br><br>With <em>Tandem DeutschConnect</em>, we are running an EU-funded project that supports people in learning German as a foreign language (DaF) and in their integration into German society. A key part of this initiative is building and maintaining strong partnerships with institutions, organisations, and volunteers who make language learning and integration possible.<br><br><br>
