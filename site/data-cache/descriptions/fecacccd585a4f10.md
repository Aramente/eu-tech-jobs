<p>MongoDB’s Marketing Technology team is looking for a Senior Manager, Martech Transformation to support the implementation and ongoing optimization of our Adobe technology ecosystem. This is a senior individual contributor role with program management ownership, focused on driving the adoption, configuration, and integration of Adobe solutions across MongoDB’s marketing organization.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate brings deep hands-on expertise with Adobe enterprise products, a strong product mindset, and the ability to operate as a strategic partner to marketing, IT, and analytics teams. You’ll serve as the internal subject matter expert on Adobe, owning the roadmap for implementation, governance, and continuous improvement across the suite.</p>
<h3>Main Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead the implementation, configuration, and rollout of Adobe suite products across Marketing, including parts of AEM, Marketo Engage, Workfront, CJA, RTCDP, etc.</li>
<li>Act as the primary program manager for Adobe initiatives: defining project scope, timelines, stakeholder alignment, and delivery milestones</li>
<li>Partner with Campaign Operations, Architects, Platform owners, IT, and Engineering to integrate Adobe products into the broader MarTech ecosystem</li>
<li>Translate marketing requirements into scalable technical configurations and workflows across Adobe products</li>
<li>Establish governance frameworks, documentation standards, and change management processes for the Adobe practice</li>
<li>Evaluate and recommend new Adobe capabilities and integrations that align with MongoDB's marketing strategy</li>
<li>Serve as the internal expert and go-to consultant on Adobe products. Enabling teams to maximize platform value</li>
<li>Support vendor relationship management with Adobe, including license governance and product roadmap alignment</li>
</ul>
<h3>Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of experience in marketing technology, marketing operations, or enterprise platform implementation roles</li>
<li>Hands-on experience implementing and administering Adobe suite products, including: Marketo, Experience Manager (AEM), Workfront, Analytics, Target, Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, or Campaign</li>
<li>Demonstrated program management experience, including ownership of complex, cross-functional technology initiatives</li>
<li>Strong understanding of marketing processes, campaign operations, and the broader MarTech landscape</li>
<li>Ability to translate business needs into platform configurations, workflows, and scalable solutions</li>
<li>Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills. Comfortable working across technical and non-technical audiences</li>
<li>Proven ability to build documentation, governance frameworks, and operational best practices</li>
<li>Curious, structured, and proactive — able to manage ambiguity and drive clarity in complex environments</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Information Systems, or a related field (or equivalent experience)</li>
</ul>
<h3>Bonus Points</h3>
<ul>
<li>Familiarity with APIs, data integrations, and system schemas within the Adobe ecosystem</li>
<li>Exposure to AI-assisted marketing workflows or Adobe Sensei-powered capabilities</li>
<li>Prior experience in high-growth SaaS or enterprise technology environments</li>
<li>People management experience or demonstrated ability to scale a practice and mentor others</li>
</ul>
