<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Fivetran is the backbone of the modern data stack, powering Open Data Infrastructure for thousands of global enterprises. As a Senior Sales Engineer specializing in Customer Expansion for ASEAN, you will be at the forefront of a booming market, driving digital transformation for our most strategic existing customers.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">In this role, you aren't just maintaining accounts—you are an architect of growth. Leveraging your expertise in Data Replication (CDC), ELT/ETL, Cloud Data Warehousing, and Data Lakehouses, you will identify high-impact expansion opportunities. You will partner with Account Managers to build faster, more secure data foundations that enable our customers to win in the era of AI. Supported by a world-class global SE team and an agile APAC sales organization, you will be the technical pivot point for Fivetran’s continued dominance in Southeast Asia.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This is a full-time, remote position based in Singapore.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Your focus will be on maximizing customer lifetime value (CLV) by:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Strategic Land &amp; Expand: Penetrating new business units and subsidiaries within global conglomerates.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Use Case Innovation: Identifying whitespace for new data pipelines, from real-time analytics to GenAI readiness.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Product Evangelism: Introducing advanced connectors (SAP, Oracle, HVR) and new platform capabilities.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Architecture Evolution: Ensuring Fivetran remains the core standard as customers scale their data volume and complexity.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Drive Technical Win for Expansion: Lead technical discovery and solution design for upsell and cross-sell motions across a high-value Commercial/Enterprise account base.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Strategic Partnership: Collaborate with Account Managers (AMs) and Customer Success Managers (CSMs) to interpret usage signals and identify "trigger events" for expansion.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Architect for Scale: Help customers evolve their legacy architectures to support modern cloud-scale volumes and hybrid-cloud environments.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Champion the Platform: Position the value of Fivetran’s high-volume agents and enterprise-grade security to technical stakeholders and C-suite executives.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Community &amp; Enablement: Act as a thought leader in the ASEAN data community. You will produce technical content, lead bespoke training, and mentor partner teams on Fivetran’s competitive edge.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Regional Expertise: Leverage your deep understanding of the ASEAN business landscape to navigate complex, multi-faceted decision-making groups.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Skills We're Looking For</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Enterprise Experience: 5+ years of operational experience with Tier-1 databases (SAP, Oracle, SQL Server) or supporting large-scale database operations as a Systems Integrator.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Expansion Mindset: A proven ability to identify growth signals and translate technical needs into a compelling business case for expansion.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">The Technical Stack:</span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Deep CDC Knowledge: Expert-level understanding of Data Replication/Integration and Change Data Capture.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Cloud Platforms: Hands-on proficiency with AWS, GCP, or Azure.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">The Data Destination: Experience with Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery, or Redshift, including Lakehouse architectures (Iceberg, Delta Lake).</span></li>
