<p><strong>A Day in the Life of a Summer Associate</strong></p>
<p>As a Summer Associate in the Client Service team, you’ll be the link between our clients and the expert knowledge they seek. Throughout your workday, you’ll work on multiple projects from investors, strategists and dealmakers at top-tier client organizations seeking expert insights to inform their decision-making. <p>Your responsibilities will include:</p>

<li>Understanding the project and market context and the legal and regulatory environment in which your clients and experts operate.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Taking ownership of every aspect of your projects, from initiating and managing client-expert communications to meticulously scheduling calls to ensure seamless collaboration between them.</li>
</ul>
