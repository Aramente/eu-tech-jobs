<h2>What you'll be doing</h2>
<p>As a Senior Revenue Data Analyst, you will play a crucial role in supporting the roll out and maintenance of our revenue subledger;&nbsp; ensuring accuracy, and providing actionable insights to drive business growth. Your expertise in revenue accounting and proficiency with various database tools will be instrumental in delivering valuable revenue-related information and supporting decision-making processes.<br><br><br>Day-to-day:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support the continued implementation of our revenue subledger to scale our revenue accounting to the next level</li>
<li>Performs proactive in-depth accounting analysis, measurement, and reconciliations of key revenue accounts</li>
<li>Prepare journal entries, balance sheet reconciliations and flux analysis at month-end close for revenue related accounts</li>
<li>Support cross functional processes with Product, Analytics, Engineering and other Accounting process owners, to collect and implement accounting requirements from the business related to revenue data flow and contributing to key system initiatives and improvements</li>
<li>Bring creative problem-solving to ambiguous challenges – not every problem has a clean solution, and we value the ability to think on your feet, move in the right direction, and iterate rather than wait for perfect</li>
<li>Leverage AI tools (e.g., Claude Code, Cursor) to accelerate data analysis, automate routine reconciliation tasks, and draft documentation – demonstrating fluency in AI-assisted workflows as part of the role’s operating standard</li>
<li>Detect and resolve data issues and reconciliation gaps – digging into the lowest level of detail when needed to find the source of truth and implement end to end solutions</li>
<li>Contribute to process improvement and automation across revenue data lineage – identifying opportunities to reduce manual effort, streamline month-end close, and scale our subledger operations for new products and markets; considering both upstream and downstream impact so changes don’t create new friction for other teams</li>
<li>Prepare and/or review deliverables for external audit review and testing, and SOX control documentation for internal audit teams&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ad hoc analyses and projects as assigned</li>
</ul>
<h2>Our humble expectations</h2>
<ul>
<li>Degree in Accounting, Business, Data Science, or related field</li>
<li>5-7 years of experience in revenue accounting/finance, data analytics, and/or related field</li>
<li>Skilled at working across functions – able to collaborate effectively with Engineering, Product, and Finance partners, and translate accounting requirements into language non-finance teams can act on</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of IFRS 15/accounting principles required; US GAAP and SOX, a plus</li>
<li>Intermediate/advanced SQL skills and ability to work with large volumes of transactional data</li>
<li>Active, proven usage of AI tools (LLMs and coding assistants) in day-to-day work; be prepared to discuss specific examples</li>
<li>Experience with NetSuite, Snowflake, Looker, dbt preferred</li>
<li>Experience in the gig economy or marketplace company a plus</li>
<li>Comfortable using English as a working language</li>
<li>Must be eligible to work in the E.U.</li>
</ul>
