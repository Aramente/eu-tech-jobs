</div></div><p><strong>Job Description:</strong></p>
<p>As a member of our team, you will drive the growth of our pipeline and play a key role in expanding Neo4j’s footprint across strategic markets. The SDR role is fully focused on pipeline creation, high-quality discovery, and partnering closely with Account Executives to develop opportunities within priority accounts.<br><br><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and respond to a high volume of inbound leads (Contact Us requests, MQLs, event leads, and hand-raisers) within an assigned territory, delivering rapid follow-up and a best-in-class prospect experience.</li>
<li>Qualify inbound interest through structured, high-quality discovery conversations to uncover business challenges, technical requirements, and buying intent.</li>
<li>Convert inbound engagement into Qualified Sales Opportunities by clearly articulating Neo4j’s value and securing next steps with aligned Account Executives.</li>
<li>Maintain strong speed-to-lead performance and SLA adherence while balancing personalization and quality at scale.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Account Executives to ensure seamless lead handoff, shared account context, and aligned messaging within the patch.</li>
<li>Provide clear, actionable insights from inbound conversations to support pipeline progression and account strategy.</li>
<li>Conduct discovery calls with prospects in the Corporate segment (sub-$1B revenue), identifying technical and business challenges that Neo4j can solve.</li>
<li>Build familiarity with Neo4j use cases, target industries, and buyer personas to confidently guide inbound conversations and position long-term value.</li>
<li>Leverage intent data and account research to prioritize inbound activity and tailor engagement.</li>
<li>Invest in learning Neo4j’s technology, competitive landscape, and enterprise use cases as preparation for an outbound, Enterprise-focused SDR role.</li>
<li>Consistently meet or exceed KPIs for meetings held, opportunities created, inbound conversion rates, and overall pipeline contribution.</li>
</ul>
<p><br><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Have a minimum of 1-year applicable work experience as a BDR/SDR in a software company, and a B.A./B.S. degree.</strong></li>
<li>Experience in outbound or inbound prospecting, enterprise engagement, or selling data/analytics/information-management technologies is a major plus.</li>
<li>Be a hard-working, self-starter, and willing to achieve a high volume of activity every day while maintaining a positive and energetic persona.</li>
<li>Have positive, effective phone skills: including excellent listening, presentation, and objection handling, as well as strong writing skills.</li>
<li>Be willing and able to learn new technologies.</li>
<li><strong>Demonstrate attention to detail, ensuring accurate entry and management of lead data in our Salesforce</strong><strong>&nbsp;CRM system.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Be proficient with standard corporate productivity tools (e.g., Google Docs, MS-Office, Salesforce</strong><strong>, Web-conferencing, Outreach, Zoominfo)</strong></li>
<li>Be a team player with the highest level of integrity, and a willingness to contribute to the continued growth and evolution of the BDR program.</li>
