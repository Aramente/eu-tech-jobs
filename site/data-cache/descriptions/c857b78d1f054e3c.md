<p><a href="https://foundation.mozilla.org/en/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mozilla Foundation</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is a non-profit building a better technology future — powered by people, open by design, fueled by imagination.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We deliver people-first alternatives to today’s extractive systems. We build side by side with developers, innovators and advocates, united in the belief that a better technology future is not only possible — it’s ours to create.</span></p>
<p><strong>Team</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Imagination &amp; Strategic Growth (I&amp;SG) team is an executive function within Mozilla Foundation that treats imagination as a core institutional capability for technology development. Working at the intersection of culture, strategy, and long-term growth, the team shapes how creative intelligence becomes new technology, partnerships, and sustained investment. I&amp;SG stewards the Foundation's creative R&amp;D program, Creative Futures, and the Fellowships program as flagship expressions of this work, while leading Mozilla Foundation’s strategic global engagements and co-designing new funds and collaborations.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mozilla Foundation operates at the intersection of emerging technology governance, global policy, and the institutions and movements shaping what gets built and who benefits. The Head of Global and Strategic Intelligence is I&amp;SG's dedicated intelligence position: synthesizing geopolitical and technology landscape signals, distilling incoming intelligence into organizational knowledge, while extending Mozilla's surface area in key corridors and contexts. This is a senior individual contributor role with a quiet profile and high autonomy, reporting directly to the VP.</span></p>
<p><strong>Job responsibilities</strong></p>
<p><strong>Here are some projects you'll likely work on in your first 6 months:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Build and stabilize Mozilla Foundation’s field intelligence function, including landscape briefs, stakeholder maps, signal memos, and CRM practices.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Establish strong briefing and pre-briefing rhythms for the VP, ED, and senior leadership ahead of high-stakes engagements.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Produce actionable intelligence on shifts across emerging and frontier technology governance landscapes, with particular attention to where those shifts should influence Mozilla Foundation strategy.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Prepare Mozilla Foundation’s positioning, leadership readiness, and intelligence outcomes for major international convenings and multilateral moments.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strengthen the systems and practices that ensure field relationships, intelligence, and strategic context accumulate as institutional knowledge rather than remaining fragmented across teams or engagements.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>As the Head of Global and Strategic Intelligence at Mozilla, you will focus on…</strong></p>
<h4><strong>Organizational Intelligence &amp; Coordination — 35% of time</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Serve as the connective intelligence layer across Mozilla Foundation’s work, tracking what is happening in the field, identifying where signals in one workstream should inform decisions in another, and keeping cross-programmatic work coherent and forward-looking.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Pre-brief the VP, ED, and, as relevant, senior leadership before high-stakes engagements, ensuring Mozilla Foundation enters critical rooms with full situational and contextual awareness.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Maintain Mozilla Foundation’s field intelligence function, including landscape briefs, stakeholder maps, signal memos, and CRM, keeping ecosystem movements and institutional positioning visible, usable, and compounding across the team.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Identify and shape where Mozilla Foundation’s work intersects with national and multilateral technology agendas, and pipeline the government and institutional partnerships that follow from those alignments.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Partner with Mozilla Foundation’s Technology Platforms team to design and maintain the intelligence infrastructure, including CRM-based network mapping, signal tracking dashboards, and knowledge management systems, that makes intelligence actionable.</span></li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Strategic Intelligence Synthesis — 30% of time</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Track and synthesize shifts across the emerging and frontier technology governance landscape, including AI, data agency, digital infrastructure, and government industrial and development policy, and translate them into actionable intelligence for internal and external use.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Produce shareable intelligence artifacts, co-developed with Strategic Communications, that translate Mozilla Foundation’s field read into external positioning.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ensure Mozilla Foundation’s field intelligence reflects global contexts, not just U.S. and European ecosystems, with particular attention to where the most relevant work in tech is happening outside mainstream visibility.</span></li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Global Moment Stewardship — 25% of time</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Lead Mozilla Foundation’s preparation for and presence at major international convenings, including UNGA, flagship AI Summits, and multilateral institutional engagements, with sharp positioning, leadership readiness, and documented intelligence and positioning outcomes.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ensure Mozilla Foundation leaves key moments having shifted a narrative, converted a key relationship, or surfaced intelligence that would not be visible from outside the room.</span></li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Institutional Memory — 10% of time</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Build and maintain the knowledge management systems that ensure relevant field relationships, intelligence, and strategic context accumulate as organizational knowledge across the Foundation and pan-Mozilla entities, rather than disappearing into meeting notes.</span></li>
</ul>
