<p>As a <strong>Senior Finance Data Scientist, Existing Business</strong>, you will be the architect of the systems that predict Intercom’s revenue future. You will move beyond static reporting to build production-grade forecasting models that translate complex customer behaviors into financial signals.</p>
<p>You will work on high-impact, open-ended problems, such as predicting expansion propensity and modeling long-term customer LTV. This role requires a hybrid of financial intuition and technical rigor: the ability to navigate raw data warehouses and the strategic mindset to explain the "why" behind the numbers to our leadership team.</p>
<h3><strong>The Impact You Will Have</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Own and Evolve the Revenue Engine:</strong> Build and maintain predictive models for usage-based revenue, renewals, and expansion that outperform traditional linear forecasts.</li>
<li><strong>Unlock Predictive Insights:</strong> Develop propensity models to identify expansion opportunities and churn risks before they materialize in the ledger.</li>
<li><strong>Architect Finance Data:</strong> Design and maintain curated datasets that serve as the single source of truth.</li>
<li><strong>Model Customer Value:</strong> Define and iterate on our LTV frameworks, providing a clear linkage between product engagement and long-term financial outcomes.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Drive Scalability:</strong> Build automated, code-based forecasting workflows that increase the speed, reliability, and granularity of our financial planning.</p>
<h2>What will I be doing?&nbsp;</h2>
<p><strong>Predictive Modeling and Forecasting Systems</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and own <strong>probabilistic and time-series models</strong> that project ARR performance across renewals and usage-based motions.</li>
<li>Incorporate behavioral signals, such as product adoption, seat utilization, and feature engagement, into <strong>expansion propensity and LTV frameworks</strong>.</li>
<li>Design models that account for cohort dynamics, seasonality, and product-led growth (PLG) signals.</li>
<li>Evaluate model performance through <strong>backtesting and iteration</strong>, ensuring our "financial weather forecast" is constantly improving.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Data and Analytical Infrastructure</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the end-to-end data pipeline for finance, transforming raw product usage and billing data into <strong>curated, model-ready datasets</strong> in our data warehouse.</li>
<li>Write and optimize production-quality <strong>SQL and Python</strong> to work with large-scale datasets and automate complex FP&amp;A workflows.</li>
<li>Ensure data integrity and consistency across all predictive systems and executive dashboards.</li>
<li>Contribute to the long-term data strategy for how Intercom tracks and predicts Existing Business health.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Analytical Problem Solving</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Translate ambiguous business questions (e.g., "Which usage signals best predict a 2x expansion?") into structured data science projects.</li>
<li>Connect ARR outcomes to underlying drivers like product adoption, customer health scores, and GTM activity.</li>
<li>Perform <strong>scenario modeling and sensitivity analysis</strong> to help the business understand the range of possible outcomes for NRR.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Business Partnership &amp; Communication</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Sales, Product, and Data Engineering to align our financial models with actual customer behavior and product roadmaps.</li>
<li>Translate complex statistical outputs into <strong>clear, decision-oriented narratives</strong> for the CFO and executive leadership.</li>
<li>Build executive-ready materials, including predictive dashboards and strategic presentations.</li>
</ul>
