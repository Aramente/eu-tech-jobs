<p>We are looking for an experienced and driven Software Engineer II to join our Audio Visual (AV) &amp; Unified Communications (UC) team. In this role, you will act as the software engineering backbone for our AV/UC infrastructure, helping us scale our global communication tools through intelligent automation.</p>
<p>You will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining automations for our core SaaS platforms, developing internal microservices, and implementing infrastructure-as-code to eliminate manual overhead. The ideal candidate is an independent problem-solver who enjoys working at the intersection of software development and enterprise communication systems.</p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>SaaS Platform Automation: Design and deploy automated workflows for our primary AV/UC SaaS platforms (e.g., Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Webex, Slack) using their native APIs and webhooks to manage user lifecycles, provisioning, and reporting.</li>
<li>Internal Services Development: Build, maintain, and scale internally built services, middlewares, and self-service tools that empower end-users and streamline AV/UC operations.</li>
<li>Infrastructure Automation: Write and maintain scripts and infrastructure-as-code (IaC) to automate the deployment, configuration, and monitoring of AV hardware and UC software environments.</li>
<li>System Integration: Connect disparate enterprise systems (e.g., HRIS, IT ticketing, and UC platforms) to ensure seamless data flow and process automation.</li>
<li>Monitoring &amp; Alerting: Develop automated health checks, proactive monitoring, and alerting mechanisms for critical AV/UC infrastructure to minimize downtime.</li>
<li>Collaboration &amp; Code Quality: Participate in code reviews, maintain clear technical documentation, and write unit/integration tests to ensure highly reliable code deployments.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Required Skills and Experience Level:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience: 2–4 years of professional experience in software development, backend engineering, or a Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) / DevOps role.</li>
<li>Programming Languages: Strong proficiency in at least one modern programming language such as Python, Go, JavaScript/Node.js, or C#.</li>
<li>API Expertise: Proven experience interacting with and designing RESTful APIs, Webhooks, and handling JSON/XML data payloads.</li>
<li>Infrastructure &amp; Cloud: Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).</li>
<li>Automation Tools: Experience with Infrastructure as Code (e.g., Terraform, CloudFormation) and configuration management tools (e.g., Ansible, Chef, or Puppet).</li>
<li>Version Control &amp; CI/CD: Solid understanding of Git workflows and building CI/CD pipelines (e.g., GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins).</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Skills:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Direct experience working with APIs from major Unified Communications platforms (Zoom, MS Teams, Google Meet, Cisco, etc.).</li>
<li>Familiarity with AV hardware ecosystems and their management interfaces.</li>
<li>Experience building chatbots or chatops integrations (e.g., Slackbot, Teams bot).</li>
<li>Works on complex issues where analysis of situations or data requires an in-depth evaluation of variable factors, including inter-organizational impact</li>
<li>Interact with senior internal and external personnel on event production systems matters, standards, and related projects&nbsp;</li>
<li>Proficiency with Google Suite tools.</li>
<li>Security Compliance Certification ISC2&nbsp;</li>
<li>Familiarity with QSC, Dante, Crestron, Shure, and AV over IP&nbsp;</li>
<li>Collaborative thought leader with the ability to take a proactive approach to dynamic technical aspects of the role</li>
<li>Okta Essentials Certification is a plus&nbsp;</li>
<li>Jamf Associate Certification is a plus&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
