<p><strong>Payment Partnerships Lead</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for a commercially savvy Payments Partnerships India Lead to join our Payment Partnerships team in India. In this role, you will lead a team responsible for managing and growing Adyen’s payment partnerships across the region — a critical component of Adyen’s global payments offering.</p>
<p>You will work closely with the Country Manager, India leadership, product managers and developers to drive strategic initiatives with local, regional, and global partners. As a lead, you will help shape the partnerships strategy while mentoring and supporting team members to deliver strong outcomes.</p>
<p>We are looking for someone who is thoughtful, adaptable, and able to balance strategic thinking with hands-on execution while building strong relationships across the payments ecosystem.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and develop a team responsible for managing and expanding Adyen’s payment partnerships</li>
<li>Drive the strategy and prioritization of key partnership initiatives, including structuring and negotiating new opportunities</li>
<li>Oversee ongoing partner management and strengthen relationships with key payment partners</li>
<li>Elevate Adyen’s influence in the ecosystem through joint product development, co-marketing, and other strategic initiatives</li>
<li>Develop and execute programs that support Adyen’s strategic goals and overall positioning in key markets and segments</li>
<li>Act as a subject matter expert on payment trends, rules, and regulations, sharing insights with internal stakeholders</li>
<li>Support Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs) with key merchants by providing insights on payment partnerships and developments</li>
<li>Partner with product teams to support the development and rollout of new payment products and capabilities</li>
<li>Manage partner escalations and ensure issues are resolved with the appropriate internal teams</li>
<li>Foster strong cross-functional collaboration across global teams including product, engineering, and commercial teams</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who you are</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>13+ years of experience managing partnerships or strategic accounts, preferably within payments, fintech, or financial services</li>
<li>At least <strong>5 years of people leadership experience</strong>, with a proven ability to lead, mentor, and develop high-performing teams</li>
<li>Strong understanding of the payments ecosystem and emerging industry trends</li>
<li>Commercially minded with experience structuring partnerships and negotiating agreements</li>
<li>Excellent stakeholder management skills and the ability to build strong relationships internally and externally</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to translate data into insights and actions</li>
<li>Comfortable working across commercial, product, and technical discussions</li>
<li>Experience working in global, cross-functional environments</li>
<li>Proven track record of delivering results and driving complex initiatives</li>
</ul>
