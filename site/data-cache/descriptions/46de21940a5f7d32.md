<h2><strong>Who we are</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>About Stripe</strong></h3>
<p>Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies—from the world’s largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups—use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities. Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet, and we have a staggering amount of work ahead. That means you have an unprecedented opportunity to put the global economy within everyone’s reach while doing the most important work of your career.</p>
<h3><strong>About The Team</strong></h3>
<p>An Account Executive, SMB at Stripe partners with small and medium-sized businesses to help them leverage Stripe’s financial infrastructure to accept payments, grow revenue, and scale efficiently. These customers range from high-growth startups to established SMBs, often looking to expand their use of Stripe products to optimize operations and unlock new revenue streams.</p>
<p>The SMB team is a strategic priority for Stripe, and SMB AEs play a critical role in driving customer growth, adoption, and retention. They act as trusted advisors to business owners and operators, helping clients maximize value from Stripe’s products while scaling their businesses.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h2>
<p>SMB AEs engage with both existing and prospective customers, managing the full sales lifecycle—from lead generation and discovery to solution design, negotiation, and post-sale adoption. They work cross-functionally with Product, Risk, Marketing, and Operations teams to deliver tailored solutions that address each customer’s unique needs.</p>
<h3><strong>Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Partner with a growing SMB to expand their payments or financial infrastructure, driving adoption of additional Stripe products</li>
<li>Lead contract renewals, upsell opportunities, and commercial negotiations to increase revenue and market share</li>
<li>Generate new business through outbound prospecting, cold outreach, research, and networking within their territory</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product, Risk, and Operations teams to solve complex client needs and improve Stripe’s platform and offerings</li>
<li>Deliver compelling proposals and business cases to help SMBs understand the long-term value of Stripe</li>
<li>Develop repeatable strategies, scalable processes, and go-to-market programs that drive product adoption, retention, and growth across the SMB segment</li>
</ul>
