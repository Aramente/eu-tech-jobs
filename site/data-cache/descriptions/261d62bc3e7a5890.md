<h3><strong>Role Summary</strong></h3>
<p>We are seeking a technically proficient Project Coordinator to join our Talent Acquisition, focusing on the India and APJC regions. You will collaborate with the India recruitment team to align local execution with global practices while using <strong>reporting and analytics</strong> to drive accountability. We are looking for a self-motivated individual with a first-principles mindset who is eager to master AI tools and take full ownership of the regional hiring ecosystem.</p>

<ul>

<li>Execute advanced scheduling and coordinator tasks within <strong>GoodTime</strong> and our <strong>ATS (Greenhouse)</strong> to maintain a high-velocity hiring pace.</li>
<li>Utilize <strong>reporting and analytics</strong> to track recruitment health, providing data-driven insights to the TA leadership team.</li>
