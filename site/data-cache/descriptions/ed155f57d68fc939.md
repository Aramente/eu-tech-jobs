**OUR STORY:**

🇪🇺 Join Scaleway and shape the sovereign cloud of tomorrow !

Since 1999, we have been designing secure, sustainable infrastructures aimed at supporting the most ambitious companies.

Historically known for our dedicated servers (Dedibox), we made a strategic shift to cloud computing in 2015. Staying true to our principles of simplicity, flexibility, and technical excellence, we have become one of the leading players in Europe in the sector.

With the rise of artificial intelligence, we have strengthened our commitment, supported by the Iliad Group, which is investing €3 billion to develop a serious, sovereign AI alternative to American and Asian giants.

Every day, thanks to our fast-growing portfolio of cloud and AI products (bare metal, containerization, serverless, AI, etc.), Scaleway proudly serves thousands of customer across the private and public sector, from corporations like France Télévisions or Hachette Livre, to fast-growing startups like Photoroom and Biolevate, to institutions like the City of Copenhagen.

📍 Our offices are located in **Paris, Lille, Toulouse, Rennes, Rouen, Bordeaux and Lyon.**

**WHY WE NEED YOU ?**

Our growth is driving us to strengthen our Product Documentation team to clarify and elevate the user experience of the Scaleway Console.

Your mission will be to design clear, concise, and consistent content for our Console interface, adopting a content-first approach in product discovery and delivery, in order to improve usability, ensure consistency across products, and scale our documentation practices.

**YOUR FUTURE TEAM**

We work in a collaborative and international environment where the diversity of Scalers, combined with a spirit of sharing, helps bring new projects to life every day, advancing our ambitions together.

You will be part of a team of 7 people composed of 5 Tech Writers and 2 UX Writers.

The team is organized by product expertise (Compute, Network, etc.), and each writer owns a specific domain. You will work in close collaboration with Product Designers, Product Managers, and Developers within product squads to improve Console clarity, align UX and content, and contribute to scalable documentation practices.

**YOUR DAILY ROUTINE**

- Write and improve in-product content for the Scaleway Console (labels, flows, microcopy, error messages, onboarding content)

- Participate in product squad rituals (weekly meetings, discovery sessions, refinements)

- Contribute to design reviews with a content-first and UX-driven mindset

- Ensure consistency with the style guide and global content standards

- Collaborate closely with Designers, PMs, Developers, and Tech Writers

- Review and refine existing wording to improve clarity and usability

- Occasionally contribute to technical documentation updates

- Work with tools such as Figma, Git, and Markdown (docs-as-code environment)

- Simplify complex cloud concepts for a technical B2B audience

- Support content scalability and translation quality
