<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><h3>ABOUT THE ROLE</h3>
<p>We’re looking for a seasoned insurance leader to head our <strong>Insurance vertical</strong> at Tide. As the <strong>National Head – Insurance</strong>, you’ll be responsible for building and scaling our insurance distribution across <strong>Health, Life, and Affinity</strong> products.</p>
<p>You’ll lead the strategic development of the business, forge strong partnerships with insurers and brokers, and build a high-performing team across <strong>field sales, tele-sales, and agency networks</strong>. This is a leadership role that combines deep market knowledge, relationship management, and team-building capabilities.</p>
<h3>What you’ll be doing</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Strategic Leadership:</strong> Define and execute Tide’s insurance distribution strategy across Health, Life, and Affinity categories.</li>
<li><strong>Partnership Development:</strong> Build and nurture relationships with insurance providers and brokers to co-create innovative, SME-focused products.</li>
<li><strong>Network Expansion:</strong> Establish and grow a national <strong>agency and partner network</strong> to drive insurance distribution across geographies.</li>
<li><strong>Sales Team Building:</strong> Recruit, train, and manage high-performing <strong>field and tele-sales teams</strong> to deliver consistent growth.</li>
<li><strong>Product Development:</strong> Collaborate with partners and internal teams to design and launch differentiated insurance offerings for Tide members.</li>
<li><strong>Operational Excellence:</strong> Develop scalable processes, compliance frameworks, and performance tracking systems to ensure quality and efficiency.</li>
<li><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration:</strong> Work closely with Product, Marketing, and Member Engagement teams to ensure seamless integration of insurance offerings into the Tide ecosystem.</li>
<li><strong>Market Insights:</strong> Monitor industry trends, competitor movements, and regulatory changes to keep Tide’s insurance strategy agile and compliant.</li>
</ul>
<h3>WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>15+ years of experience</strong> in the insurance or financial services industry, with a focus on distribution, partnerships, or bancassurance.</li>
<li>Proven success in <strong>building and scaling sales networks</strong> (field, tele, and agency).Strong relationships with <strong>leading insurers and brokers</strong> in India.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of insurance products, pricing structures, and regulatory frameworks.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to <strong>lead diverse teams across geographies</strong> with strategic clarity and operational discipline.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, negotiation, and stakeholder management skills.</li>
<li>Entrepreneurial mindset with the ability to thrive in a fast-growing fintech environment.</li>
<li>Preferably based out of <strong>NCR region</strong> or willing to relocate</li>
</ul>
