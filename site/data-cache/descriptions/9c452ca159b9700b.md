### **About Ibexa**

Ibexa is a European marketing orchestration platform that empowers organisations to deliver seamless, data-driven customer experiences across the entire digital journey.

By unifying content management, customer data, engagement, product information, and interactive data collection capabilities — including solutions such as Qualifio, Raptor, Quable, and Actito — Ibexa enables marketing and digital teams to break down silos and orchestrate high-impact, personalised experiences at scale.

We are a team of more than 350 professionals across Europe. As we continue to expand our presence in the Nordic region and beyond, we are looking for ambitious sales professionals eager to help organisations modernise their marketing ecosystems and unlock new growth opportunities.

### **Your Role**

📍 Aarhus, Denmark (Hybrid)

As a **SDR – Nordics**, you will generate and qualify pipeline across the Nordic markets (Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland). You will work closely with Account Executives and partners to engage upper mid-market and enterprise prospects and secure qualified meetings.

This role is ideal for someone looking to build a strong foundation in B2B SaaS sales within an international and fast-growing European tech company.
