<p><strong>Senior Strategy Consultant- Cherry (AVENIR GLOBAL)</strong></p>
<p><strong>Location: London (minimum 3 days per week in office)</strong></p>
<p><strong>The role</strong></p>
<p>The Senior Planner is a senior strategic leader responsible for setting and driving the strategic direction of client marketing and communications programmes across medical communications and creative work.</p>
<p>Operating with a high degree of autonomy, they lead strategy from brief through to delivery, translating complex scientific and commercial challenges into clear positioning, compelling narratives, and effective, compliant campaigns.</p>
<p>They act as a trusted senior partner to clients, bridge creative and scientific disciplines, and play a central role in driving organic growth and new business through strong strategic thinking.</p>
<p><strong>Key responsibilities</strong></p>
<p><strong>Strategy leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and lead strategic thinking across multiple clients, brands, and therapy areas.</li>
<li>Develop clear brand strategies, positioning, value propositions, and single-minded propositions.</li>
<li>Create and own commercial and scientific narratives, message architectures, and strategic frameworks.</li>
<li>Build deep understanding of clinician, patient, payer, and commercial team behaviour and motivation.</li>
<li>Lead insight generation through research, HCP interviews, workshops, client immersion, field visits, desk research, and social listening.</li>
<li>Apply persuasive frameworks appropriately (e.g. Challenger, AIDA, SPIN) to shape strategy and messaging.</li>
<li>Write exceptional creative springboard briefs that inspire strong, effective work.</li>
<li>Maintain strategic integrity throughout creative, writing, and scientific development.</li>
<li>Provide strategic direction and quality control across outputs.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong><strong>Client leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as a senior strategic partner to clients, offering clarity, challenge, and confidence.</li>
<li>Influence and engage senior stakeholders with authority and credibility.</li>
<li>Independently lead and facilitate client workshops (strategy, positioning, messaging, brand planning).</li>
<li>Present and sell strategic thinking through clear, compelling storytelling</li>
<li>Work effectively with both regional and global client teams.</li>
<li>Demonstrate strong understanding of medical regulations and compliance requirements across EMEA.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Collaboration and internal leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner closely with Account Handling, Creative, and Medical Writing teams.</li>
<li>Ensure effective collaboration across disciplines with clear expectations, inputs, and timings.</li>
<li>Act as a senior strategic voice within the agency, providing guidance and challenge.</li>
<li>Lead or significantly shape the strategic direction of pitches and new business opportunities.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Commercial impact and growth</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively identify opportunities to grow existing accounts through strategic value.</li>
<li>Support Account Directors in scoping and pricing strategic work appropriately.</li>
<li>Contribute to new business development through strong strategic leadership.</li>
<li>Demonstrate commercial acumen and understanding of how strategy drives client and agency success.</li>
<li>Generate new business opportunities through personal network and outreach.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Ways of working</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to the evolution of the agency’s strategic tools and processes</li>
<li>Embed strategic best practice across the business.</li>
<li>Maintain strong awareness of market dynamics, competitor activity, and emerging trends.</li>
<li>Build close networks across the other AVENIR GLOBAL brands.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>People leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead by example, modelling strong strategic behaviours and standards.</li>
<li>Informally coach and mentor junior strategists and cross-functional team members.</li>
<li>Be influential and persuasive while remaining collaborative and constructive.</li>
<li>Actively listen for strong ideas and incorporate them into strategic thinking.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Experience</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Approximately 10+ years’ experience in medical communications and/or creative agencies.</li>
<li>Strong background in strategy, planning, or account handling.</li>
<li>Proven experience independently developing positioning, narratives, and campaign strategies.</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience leading client workshops and pitches.</li>
<li>Experience working with regional and global pharmaceutical clients.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of medical regulations and compliance across EMEA.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Skills and attributes</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Outstanding strategic, analytical, and problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Strong ability to uncover, interpret, and articulate powerful insights.</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Confident facilitator and presenter, comfortable leading senior stakeholders.</li>
<li>Able to balance creativity with scientific and regulatory rigour.</li>
<li>Structured, logical thinker comfortable managing complex challenges.</li>
<li>Highly self-motivated, organised, and effective working autonomously.</li>
<li>Collaborative, commercially minded, and outcome-focused.</li>
</ul>
