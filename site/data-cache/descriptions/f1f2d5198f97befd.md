<p>With deep expertise in Salesforce administration, platform governance, automation, and system operations, the team continuously enhances the Salesforce CRM platform to meet evolving business needs. <h3>What you’ll do</h3>
<ul>
<li>We are looking for a Senior Salesforce Administrator – CPQ &amp; CLM to manage and enhance the Salesforce CRM system supporting MongoDB’s Sales organization</li>
<li>Effectively work both autonomously and collaboratively across Salesforce platform administration, configuration, release support, security, and audit-related activities</li>
<li>Work closely with Tech Leads, Program Managers, Developers, DevOps teams, and Business Stakeholders to understand requirements and deliver scalable declarative solutions</li>
<li>Contribute across multiple functions including platform administration, CI/CD participation, release support, access governance, security improvements, and audit readiness</li>
<li>Ensure appropriate controls, documentation, and governance practices are followed to maintain a strong and effective control environment</li>
</ul>
<h3>Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Administer, configure, and maintain the Salesforce platform, including users, roles, profiles, permission sets, sharing settings, page layouts, record types, and other core administrative functions</li>
<li>Administer and support Salesforce CPQ and CLM processes, including product configuration, pricing rules, quote templates, approval workflows, contract generation, contract lifecycle workflows, and related business process automation</li>
<li>Partner with Sales Operations, Revenue Operations, Deal Desk, Legal, and other business stakeholders to improve Quote-to-Cash and Contract Lifecycle Management processes</li>
<li>Build and enhance Salesforce solutions using declarative tools such as Flows, Validation Rules, Approval Processes, Lightning App Builder, Dynamic Forms, and page layouts</li>
<li>Support DevOps and CI/CD activities including deployment, validations, release readiness, environment coordination, and change migration across Salesforce environments</li>
<li>Participate in audit processes by supporting evidence collection, validating controls, documenting configurations, and helping close identified gaps</li>
<li>Maintain a strong understanding of Salesforce security architecture, including profiles, roles, permission sets, field-level security, object access, and sharing rules</li>
<li>Support platform stability and day-to-day operational activities by troubleshooting issues, resolving user requests, and maintaining data integrity</li>
<li>Partner with business stakeholders to understand requirements, improve processes, and recommend scalable platform solutions</li>
<li>Maintain clear documentation for system configurations, release activities, controls, support procedures, and governance practices</li>
<li>Research platform capabilities, best practices, and new Salesforce features to continuously improve system effectiveness and administration maturity</li>
</ul>
<h3>Minimum Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business, or equivalent experience</li>
<li>6+ years of Salesforce Administration experience in a mid-to-large enterprise environment</li>
<li>Salesforce Certified Platform Administrator, Salesforce Certified App Builder, Salesforce Certified Advanced Administrator, Salesforce Certified Sharing &amp; Visibility Designer certifications are required</li>
<li>Salesforce CPQ Specialist certification strongly preferred</li>
