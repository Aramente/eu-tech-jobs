<p><strong>About this opportunity:</strong><strong><br></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 300;">Freenome is looking for a strategic, high-energy Director of Downstream Marketing to lead the commercial launch and market expansion of our blood-based cancer screening tests. As the first dedicated downstream leader, you will be the bridge between our breakthrough science and the healthcare providers, systems, and patients we serve.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 300;">You will own the go-to-market (GTM) execution, demand generation, and sales enablement strategies that turn our clinical evidence into a market-leading commercial brand.<br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 300;">This role will report to the VP, Marketing and &nbsp;is a remote or hybrid/onsite role with minimal travel requirements.&nbsp;<br></span></p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Commercial Launch and Campaign Execution: Execute Go-to-Market (GTM) tactics for new products and indications by delivering integrated campaigns and cross-channel collateral and sales enablement tools that translate complex early-detection science into compelling messaging for PCPs and health system stakeholders.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Customer acquisition and digital marketing: Develop and optimize digital marketing campaigns to drive system, HCP and patient acquisition. Oversee paid media strategy, SEO/SEM, email marketing, and conversion optimization. Use data to continuously test, refine, and scale campaigns based on performance.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Sales Enablement &amp; Field Support: Partner closely with the Sales and L&amp;D teams to develop high-impact collateral, training modules, and "objection-handling" tools that accelerate the sales cycle.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Brand Stewardship: Ensure a consistent, high-integrity brand voice across all physician-facing and patient-facing touchpoints.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Market Intelligence: Monitor the competitive landscape (Liquid Biopsy, Cologuard, etc.) to refine our positioning and maintain Freenome’s competitive edge.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Agency &amp; Budget Management: Manage external creative and media agencies to deliver high-ROI campaigns while maintaining a disciplined marketing budget.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">External Stakeholder Relations: Build relationships with industry/market thought leaders, customers, societies, internal stakeholders and provide key insights to cross-functional teams.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Provider Marketing &amp; ABM: Design and execute Account-Based Marketing (ABM) strategies to engage referring physicians and healthcare professionals (HCPs). Partner with field teams to identify target segments and develop tailored content that drives referrals and builds brand trust.<br></span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Must haves:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Bachelor’s degree in a business, life sciences, or related field.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">10+ years of experience in marketing, with at least 5 years specifically in downstream/commercial marketing within the molecular diagnostics, biotech, or medical device space.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Ability to translate dense clinical data (Sensitivity, Specificity, NPV) into a narrative that a busy physician can understand in 30 seconds.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Drive to thrive in the "center of the wheel," working across Sales, Medical Affairs, and Product Management to ensure everyone is aligned on the commercial mission.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Demonstrated data-driven decision making. You don't just "run ads"; you measure conversion rates, CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), and physician engagement metrics to iterate constantly.<br></span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to haves:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Experience launching a PCP-focused diagnostic test is a significant plus.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Familiarity with the reimbursement landscape and how it affects downstream adoption.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">MBA or advanced degree in life sciences preferred.<br></span></li>
</ul>
