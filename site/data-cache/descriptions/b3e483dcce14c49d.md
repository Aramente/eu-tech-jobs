<p>We are looking for a results-driven <strong>Scaled Renewals Manager</strong> to join our team in Bangalore. In this role, you will act as the commercial owner for a portfolio of existing customers based in <strong>North America</strong>.</p>
<p>The team’s primary goal is to drive <strong>Net Revenue Retention (NRR)</strong>. You will be responsible for managing the commercial lifecycle: securing on-time renewals, identifying growth opportunities, and ensuring customers realize the full value of the platform.</p>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<ol>
<li><strong> Revenue Growth &amp; Pipeline</strong></li>
</ol>
<ul>
<li><strong>Commercial Ownership:</strong> Manage the renewal process for your book of business to ensure retention targets are met or exceeded.</li>
<li><strong>North American Portfolio:</strong> Manage a high-volume portfolio of customers across North America, ensuring successful commercial outcomes in their specific time zones.</li>
<li><strong>Expansion:</strong> Proactively identify opportunities to cross-sell and up-sell by understanding customer goals and positioning relevant features or add-ons.</li>
<li><strong>Lead Management:</strong> Qualify complex or high-value expansion opportunities and collaborate with the Account Management team to facilitate the right level of engagement.</li>
</ul>
<ol>
<li><strong> Retention &amp; Account Health</strong></li>
</ol>
<ul>
<li><strong>Proactive Engagement:</strong> Monitor account health metrics to identify risks early. Engage with customers to resolve commercial inquiries and ensure a smooth path to renewal.</li>
<li><strong>Value Demonstration:</strong> Conduct discovery calls to reinforce the product's value and ROI, helping to prevent churn and secure long-term commitment.</li>
<li><strong>Negotiation:</strong> Manage the negotiation process for renewals, addressing customer objections, and ensuring commercial terms align with company standards.</li>
</ul>
<ol>
<li><strong> Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong></li>
</ol>
<ul>
<li><strong>Customer Advocacy:</strong> Act as a bridge between the customer and internal teams (such as Support, Billing, and Product) to resolve inquiries and improve the customer experience.</li>
<li><strong>Market Feedback:</strong> Share trends, customer feedback, and insights with the broader team to help refine our customer engagement strategies.</li>
<li><strong>Operational Hygiene:</strong> Maintain accurate data in our CRM (Salesforce) to ensure reliable forecasting for renewals and expansion opportunities.</li>
</ul>
