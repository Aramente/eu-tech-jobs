Must Have Skills – Sewer / Excavation Apprentice Laborer

- Basic understanding of underground utilities, sewer lines, and excavation practices.
- Ability to safely operate or assist with hand tools (shovels, picks, tampers, cutting tools).
- Familiarity with or ability to learn trenching and shoring safety procedures.
- Ability to support operation of small machinery (pipe lasers, compactors, trench boxes) under supervision.
- Basic knowledge of pipe installation, connections, and grade checking (or willingness to learn).
- Physical & Labor Skills
- Ability to perform heavy physical labor: lifting 50–100 lbs, digging, backfilling, hauling materials.
- Comfortable working in all weather conditions, confined spaces, and uneven terrain.
- Strong stamina for long shifts and physically demanding tasks.
- Safety & Compliance
- Understanding of OSHA safety basics or willingness to complete training.
- Ability to follow jobsite safety rules, PPE requirements, and supervisor instructions.
- Awareness of underground utility hazards (locates, gas/electric safety).
- Work Ethic & Reliability
- Consistent attendance, punctuality, and reliability.
- Strong willingness to learn the trade and follow direction from lead technicians/excavators.
- Positive attitude, teamwork, and professionalism on residential and commercial jobsites.
- Communication & Problem-Solving
- Ability to communicate clearly with crew members and supervisors.
- Basic problem solving skills when handling unexpected jobsite conditions.
- Ability to document work or relay information as needed.
- Qualifications / Requirements
- Valid driver’s license
- Must pass pre employment screenings if applicable.
- Willingness to work overtime or emergency calls when needed.
