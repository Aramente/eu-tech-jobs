<li>Identify and unlock new budgets, brands, categories and geographic growth opportunities</li>
<li>Align regional teams around global priorities and translate strategy into coordinated market execution</li>
<li>Engage in frequent in-person client meetings and international travel to build relationships, drive alignment and unlock growth across markets</li>
<li>Create scalable frameworks that reduce duplication, remove friction and increase speed across markets</li>
<li>Maintain clear visibility into pipeline, risks and opportunities across assigned partnerships</li>
<li>Use AI to accelerate analysis, planning and preparation, applying judgment and verification to ensure accuracy and quality</li>
<li>Build AI-enabled workflows to automate repeatable tasks, synthesize information and improve productivity in a high-volume environment</li>

<p><strong>What we’re looking for:</strong></p>
<ul>
