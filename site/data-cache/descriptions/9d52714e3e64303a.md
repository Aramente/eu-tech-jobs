<div class="content-intro"><p>From Fivetran’s founding until now, our mission has remained the same: to make access to data as simple and reliable as electricity. We’re proud that more organizations continue to leverage our technology every day to become truly data-driven.</p></div><p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>About the Role</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">We’re looking for a customer-facing Solution Architect role, focused on helping customers successfully establish secure network connectivity with Fivetran. Engagements are typically initiated through pre-qualified customer requests (via a ticketing system) and involve designing, validating, and guiding the implementation of networking resources required for Fivetran’s SaaS and Hybrid products.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">As a Solution Architect specializing in Network &amp; Connectivity, you will design and implement secure network architectures that enable Fivetran to reliably exchange data with customer environments. Your focus will be on private connectivity, network isolation, secure data flows, and the authentication and credential mechanisms required to establish and maintain those connections across cloud and on-premise infrastructures.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">You will work directly with customer security, network, and platform teams to design solutions using technologies such as PrivateLink / Private Service Connect, VPNs, SSH tunneling (direct and reverse), subnet peering, proxy agents, and hybrid networking patterns. While you are not directly responsible for selling, you will act as a trusted technical advisor, guiding customers through architectural decisions, security tradeoffs, and implementation details.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">You will collaborate closely with internal teams including Customer Support, Customer Success, Field Sales teams, Product, and Engineering to ensure customer connectivity requirements are met efficiently and securely.</span><br><span style="font-size: 10pt;">&nbsp;<br></span><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This is a full-time position based remotely in India and requires working hours aligned with North American business hours (9:30:00 PM 5:30:00 AM IST).</span></p>
