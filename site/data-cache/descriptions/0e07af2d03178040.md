## Deine Position

As a member of our Finance Team, you work directly with the CFO of Heartbeat AI GmbH and play a pivotal role in shaping the financial foundation of a fast-growing tech company committed to revolutionizing the global energy sector. In your role you will:<br><ul><li><strong>Support financial planning &amp; investor relations:</strong> You will assist in calculating and preparing financial scenarios for investors, helping to communicate the company's financial outlook clearly and compellingly</li><li><strong>Own real finance responsibilities from day one:</strong> You will support the monthly closing process (Monatsabschluss) of a scaling tech company, gaining hands-on experience across accounting, controlling, and reporting</li><li><strong>Drive financial insight across the business:</strong> You will work closely with internal stakeholders to build financial models, evaluate cost and revenue structures, and translate numbers into actionable recommendations</li></ul>
