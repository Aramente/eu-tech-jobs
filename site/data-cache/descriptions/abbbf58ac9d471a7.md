<h1><strong>About us&nbsp;</strong></h1>
<p>N26 is Europe’s leading digital bank. With a full German banking license and a presence in over 20 markets, we have moved past the "disruptor" phase and are entering a period of institutional maturity.</p>
<p>Our strategy is clear: we want to be the primary account for our customers. To achieve this, we are expanding far beyond basic transactions into a holistic financial ecosystem, including our recent launch of N26 for under 18s, bringing families into our digital-first world. This evolution requires a new level of robustness and scalability. We are sharpening how we build, ship, and sustain products at scale, ensuring our core is as reliable as it is innovative.</p>

<h1><strong>About the role&nbsp;</strong></h1>
<p>As the Director of Accounting and Reporting, you aren't just managing a function; you are the architect of our financial integrity. This is a high-impact, high-intensity role that requires you to get deep into the weeds. We are currently in a phase of heavy-lift construction. You will spend a significant amount of your time setting up the infrastructure and refining the plumbing of our financial systems. This isn't a role for someone who wants to walk into a finished house; it’s for a leader who can navigate extreme ambiguity, make high-stakes decisions with incomplete information, and maintain unwavering resilience while building the engine of a global bank.</p>
<p>Reporting into CFO, based in Berlin</p>
<h1><strong>Your impact&nbsp;</strong></h1>
<ul>
<li>Build the Infrastructure: Drive the heavy lifting of building out our accounting systems and ERP (Workday), ensuring the architecture can support millions of primary accounts and new products like kids' cards.</li>
<li>Deep-Dive Data Ownership: Get into the weeds of our data sets, subledgers, and source systems. You will ensure our financial "source of truth" is industrial-grade and perfectly aligned with our product reality.</li>
<li>Own the Fast Close: Take end-to-end ownership of the book-close process, relentlessly optimizing workflows to achieve a 3-business-day fast close without compromising on integrity.</li>
<li>Decision-Making in Ambiguity: Navigate a rapidly evolving regulatory landscape, making firm, strategic calls even when the path forward isn't perfectly paved.</li>
<li>Resilient Leadership: Lead your team through a phase of radical transformation. You must provide clear direction and maintain high standards during periods of high pressure and tight deadlines.</li>
<li>Strategic Interface: Act as the critical link between Finance, Product, and Risk, ensuring every new launch—from crypto to family banking—is seamlessly integrated into our financial reporting.</li>
<li>Compliance &amp; Policy: Design and implement the accounting frameworks that allow us to scale across Europe while remaining strictly compliant with HGB and IFRS.</li>
</ul>
<h1><strong>What you bring&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></h1>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of experience in a leadership role within a Bank or Fintech.</li>
<li>A university degree with a focus on Finance, Controlling, Audit, or a related field.</li>
<li>Professional Certification: Wirtschaftsprüfer, CPA, ACCA, or equivalent is highly preferred.</li>
<li>Grit &amp; Resilience: You have the mental stamina to handle the pressure of a maturing bank and the "building" phase of a department</li>
<li>Comfort in the Weeds: You are a hands-on leader who enjoys fixing broken processes and investigating data discrepancies at the granular level</li>
<li>Technical Mastery: Profound knowledge of IFRS and German GAAP (HGB).</li>
<li>Systems Architecture: Experience with the design and implementation of reporting and closing processes. Workday knowledge is a significant plus.</li>
<li>Decisiveness: The ability to provide clear direction and make sound judgments in an environment where things move fast and ambiguity is the norm.</li>
<li>Language: Fluency in English is required; German is a major plus.</li>
</ul>
