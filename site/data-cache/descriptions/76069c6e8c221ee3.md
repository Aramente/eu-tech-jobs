**About the role:**

We're looking for a Product Manager who thinks commercially and is energized by the challenge of turning pricing and packaging decisions into measurable business impact. You'll own a critical piece of Omnisend's revenue model — and have real influence over how we grow revenue from 150,000+ customers.

This isn't a role for someone who waits to be told what to optimize. You'll be expected to find the opportunity, frame the problem, design the test, ship the change, and measure the result.

**About the team:**

The Expansion team owns the commercial engine behind Omnisend's growth. We're responsible for Omnisend's pricing plans and add-ons as a product — designing how we package and price value, managing the monetization levers, and maintaining the billing system that keeps everything running. While core product teams build the features, we decide how they're priced, bundled, and surfaced to customers.

We're a small, focused squad sitting at the intersection of product, data, and commercial strategy. We move fast, rely heavily on data, and run experiments to learn what drives expansion revenue across our current customer base.

## What you'll do at Omnisend:

- Own the pricing and packaging strategy for Omnisend's plans and add-ons — from research and hypothesis through to implementation
- Identify and size opportunities across the monetization funnel: upgrade triggers, plan limits, add-on discovery, trial conversion
- Design and run pricing and packaging experiments, drawing meaningful conclusions from behavioral and revenue data
- Work with billing engineering to maintain, improve, and evolve the billing system
- Partner with core product teams to define commercial terms for new features and add-ons
- Collaborate with marketing, sales, and customer success on go-to-market and pricing communication
- Track north star metrics for expansion revenue — and dig into the data when something looks off
