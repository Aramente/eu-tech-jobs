<h4><strong>About the team</strong></h4>
<p>The Processing Acquiring team is the engine room of SumUp. We manage the complex integrations with 3rd party acquirers, payment processors, and global card schemes that keep our "acquiring machine" running.</p>
<p>As our ecosystem grows, so does the operational workload of maintaining these critical connections. We are introducing this new Product Operations role to bring clear ownership and structure to operational change management. Your mission is to own and streamline operational change across our payments ecosystem, ensuring that partner updates, scheme mandates, and compliance requirements are implemented accurately and reliably. You will report to the Product Lead and work alongside a cross-functional squad of engineers and product managers in our Sofia hub.</p>
<h4><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Own operational change:</strong> Act as the single accountable owner for all partner-driven updates within the acquiring ecosystem (e.g., file changes, MCC updates, scheme mandates).</li>
<li><strong>Translate requirements</strong>: Interpret complex specifications from external partners (acquirers, Visa, Mastercard) and translate them into clear, actionable requirements for Engineering, Operations and Risk teams.</li>
<li><strong>Manage the intake process</strong>: Establish and maintain a structured process for tracking and prioritizing incoming operational changes to ensure visibility and predictability.</li>
<li><strong>Coordinate cross-functionally:</strong> Drive alignment across Engineering, Partnerships, Compliance, and Risk to deliver mandatory updates smoothly and timely.</li>
<li><strong>Improve documentation: </strong>Standardise documentation of recurring partner updates and configuration changes to strengthen long-term operational stability.</li>
<li><strong>Identify friction:</strong> Identify recurring bottlenecks in operational workflows and propose structured improvements to reduce manual overhead.</li>
</ul>
