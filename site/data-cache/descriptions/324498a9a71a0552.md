**OUR STORY:**

🇪🇺 Join Scaleway and shape the sovereign cloud of tomorrow !

Since 1999, we have been designing secure, sustainable infrastructures aimed at supporting the most ambitious companies.

Historically known for our dedicated servers (Dedibox), we made a strategic shift to cloud computing in 2015. Staying true to our principles of simplicity, flexibility, and technical excellence, we have become one of the leading players in Europe in the sector.

With the rise of artificial intelligence, we have strengthened our commitment, supported by the Iliad Group, which is investing €3 billion to develop a serious, sovereign AI alternative to American and Asian giants.

Every day, thanks to our fast-growing portfolio of cloud and AI products (bare metal, containerization, serverless, AI, etc.), Scaleway proudly serves thousands of customer across the private and public sector, from corporations like France Télévisions or Hachette Livre, to fast-growing startups like Photoroom and Biolevate, to institutions like the City of Copenhagen.

📍 Our offices are located in **Paris, Lille, Toulouse, Rennes, Rouen, Bordeaux and Lyon.**

**WHY WE NEED YOU ?**

Our growth is driving us to strengthen our Logistics & Warehouse Operations team to ensure the continuity and reliability of our deployment flows across our three sites.

Your mission will be to manage the reception, storage, preparation, and shipment of IT equipment while ensuring stock accuracy and strict compliance with safety procedures, in order to support our European growth and the success of the Brooklyn project.

**YOUR FUTURE TEAM**

We work in a collaborative and international environment where the diversity of Scalers, combined with a spirit of sharing, helps bring new projects to life every day, advancing our ambitions together.

You will be part of a team of 12 people distributed across three sites (CDS, DC5, and DC2/DC3). The team operates with a weekly rotation system to ensure versatility and full control of the entire IT logistics chain.

This position is a one-to-one replacement to maintain team stability and operational excellence.

You will work in close and constant collaboration with your teammates, as well as with the Production department, third-party service providers, carriers, and Opcore.

**YOUR DAILY ROUTINE**

- Receive, inspect, and store incoming IT equipment and components

- Prepare and package hardware for internal deployment or external shipment

- Ensure accurate stock management and real-time updates in Odoo

- Use computer tools and Zebra tablets to track logistics flows

- Coordinate daily with Production teams to align on deployment priorities

- Manage urgent delivery requests, especially for Opcore

- Organize and optimize storage areas to maintain operational efficiency

- Ensure compliance with safety rules and internal procedures

- Communicate proactively with carriers and external partners

- Participate in the site rotation schedule and support workload balancing
