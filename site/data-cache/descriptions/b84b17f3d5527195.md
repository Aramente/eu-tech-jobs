<p>Nebius is looking for a DC &amp; IT Tools Development Lead to join the Hardware Infrastructure team. You are welcome to work in our Amsterdam office.</p>

<ul>
<li>ITDC Terminal (a specialised task tracker used by local DC staff)</li>
<li>Test/Disposal WH stands (functional testing and decommissioning)</li>
<li>Logistics &amp; Stocktage tools (asset intake, inventory, movements, and dispatch)</li>
<li>DC tools (maintenance for engineering and IT equipment in our DCs)</li>
</ul>
<p>Your focus is to own the direction: architect systems, shape the roadmap, coordinate delivery with a neighbouring backend development group and lead stakeholder engagement. Hands-on work is welcome but not required.</p>

<li>Drive product practices: discovery, requirements, RFC/ADR, design reviews and clear documentation.</li>
<li>Partner with stakeholders (DC operations, logistics/warehouse, testing/repairs, security, finance).</li>
<li>Proactively initiate improvements.</li>
</ul>
