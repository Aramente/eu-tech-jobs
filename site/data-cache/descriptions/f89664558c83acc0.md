**In this role, you'll be responsible for:**

- Install and replace electrical panels, circuit breakers, and service upgrades to meet modern electrical demands. Complete knowledge of installations or new circuits, EVSE, Panel upgrades and updates
- Perform full home and commercial rewires, including removal of outdated wiring and installation of new circuits.
- Install surge protection devices for whole-home and commercial electrical systems.
- Set up indoor and outdoor lighting systems, including recessed lighting, security lighting, and landscape lighting.
- Wire and install pool and hot tub electrical systems, ensuring proper grounding and bonding.
- Install and replace switches, outlets, dimmers, and GFCI/AFCI devices for safety and functionality.
- Install standby and portable generators, including Generac and other leading brands.
- Wire and configure automatic transfer switches (ATS) to ensure seamless backup power operation.
- Perform load calculations and ensure proper electrical integration with main service panels.
- Conduct generator start-up, testing, and troubleshooting to verify system performance.
- Install EV chargers for residential and commercial customers.
- Set up washer and dryer electrical hook-ups, ensuring proper circuit configuration.
- Install and replace ceiling fans with appropriate wiring and mounting.
- Troubleshoot and repair electrical faults in homes and businesses.

**Qualifications**

- 5+ years of experience and electrical license .
- Understanding basic circuitry.
- Conduit installation.
- Light commercial experience is a plus.
- Generac Trained.
- Great customer service skills.
- Proven experience as an electrician with a strong understanding of electrical systems and components.
- Proficiency in using hand tools, power tools, voltmeters, and ohmmeters.
- Familiarity with the National Electrical Code (NEC) and safety regulations.
- Experience in residential and commercial settings preferred.
- Ability to read blueprints and schematics, with knowledge of high and low voltage systems.
