<h1><strong>About the opportunity</strong></h1>
<p>We are the N26 Regulatory Reporting Team, an enthusiastic team, we are committed to precision and teamwork. Our mission is to ensure seamless compliance with regulations, and we navigate the complexities of multiple authorities with dedication and adaptability.</p>
<p>We are looking for a Regulatory Reporting Manager for Germany to join our expanding team. If you have a strong interest in reporting and making a meaningful impact, we encourage you to apply.</p>
<h2><strong>In this role, you will</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Ensure flawless and timely reporting processes.</li>
<li>You take care of the plausibility check, control and documentation of the regulatory reporting process.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Be responsible for statistical and financial regulatory reports of N26 Bank and Group.</li>
<li>Enhance the Reporting function for effectiveness and compliance with all applicable directives and regulations.</li>
<li>Implement new reporting processes as required due to regulatory changes or product launches.</li>
<li>Represent the Reporting Team in cross-functional alignments with various stakeholders within the organization.</li>
<li>You take care of the automation of processes.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What you need to be successful</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>At least 5 years of experience in banking, financial outsourcing companies or financial institutions.</li>
<li>You have a degree in Business Administration, Business Engineering or alternatively a bank education with appropriate further qualification.</li>
<li>Knowledge of financial and banking legislation (CRR III,&nbsp; CRD VI and KWG), COREP &amp; FINREP and to work with regulatory reporting software.</li>
<li>Experienced with regulations on banks’ reporting and technical specifications.</li>
<li>Familiar in working on projects and setting up new processes.</li>
<li>Ability to take ownership and meet deadlines.</li>
<li>Exceptional attention to details and analytical skills.</li>
<li>Structured way of thinking and strong communication skills</li>
<li>Fluent in both German and English.</li>
</ul>
