<p><strong>The job</strong></p></div><p>Join our Health, Localisation, and Internationalisation team as a Senior Product Analyst. You will focus on scaling our reach into new territories and optimizing the product journey to ensure it resonates with users in every region.</p>
<p><strong>You'll be responsible for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Working closely with Product Managers, UX Researchers, Designers, and Engineers to support data-driven decision-making across the product.</li>
<li>Identifying new growth opportunities, including underserved user segments, use cases, and regions with high potential for product expansion.</li>
<li>Helping the team understand which company-level goals can be influenced through experimentation, defining relevant proxy metrics, and monitoring them over time.</li>
<li>Supporting hypothesis formulation and prioritization using data: estimating segment sizes, potential impact, expected uplift, and defining clear success metrics, while accounting for user behavior patterns.</li>
<li>Helping the team prioritize ideas and initiatives based on expected impact, confidence level, and effort.</li>
<li>Participating in product planning and roadmap discussions, providing analytical input to shape priorities and investment decisions.</li>
<li>Evaluating experiment results in terms of their overall impact on core product metrics, not just local effects.</li>
<li>Translating analysis results into clear, actionable recommendations for the team and stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
