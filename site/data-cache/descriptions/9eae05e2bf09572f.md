**OUR STORY:**

🇪🇺 Join Scaleway and shape the sovereign cloud of tomorrow !

Since 1999, we have been designing secure, sustainable infrastructures aimed at supporting the most ambitious companies.

Historically known for our dedicated servers (Dedibox), we made a strategic shift to cloud computing in 2015. Staying true to our principles of simplicity, flexibility, and technical excellence, we have become one of the leading players in Europe in the sector.

With the rise of artificial intelligence, we have strengthened our commitment, supported by the Iliad Group, which is investing €3 billion to develop a serious, sovereign AI alternative to American and Asian giants.

Every day, thanks to our fast-growing portfolio of cloud and AI products (bare metal, containerization, serverless, AI, etc.), Scaleway proudly serves thousands of customer across the private and public sector, from corporations like France Télévisions or Hachette Livre, to fast-growing startups like Photoroom and Biolevate, to institutions like the City of Copenhagen.

📍 Our offices are located in **Paris, Lille, Toulouse, Rennes, Rouen, Bordeaux and Lyon.**

**WHY WE NEED YOU ?**

Our growth is driving us to strengthen our **Operations** team to centralize and elevate our Service Level Agreement (SLA) strategy.

Your mission will be to **coordinate the entire lifecycle of SLAs**—from definition and measurement to contractual application and reporting—in order to **ensure maximum customer satisfaction while  significantly improving internal productivity on this topic.**

**YOUR FUTURE TEAM**

We work in a collaborative and international environment where the diversity of Scalers, reporting combined with a spirit of sharing, helps bring new projects to life every day, advancing our ambitions together.

You will be part of the **Lead Operations team**, composed of 7 experts. This is a high-impact role with a total scope over SLA topics, requiring you to work transversally with most teams across the company, including Engineering, Sales, Finance, Product, and Customer Care.

**Manager information:** You will report directly to **Albane Bruyas**, COO.

**YOUR DAILY ROUTINE**

- Coordinate the definition, measurement, and application of SLAs across Scaleway’s product portfolio.

- Establish a comprehensive roadmap for SLA management, auditing the current state and defining the target vision.

- Manage the integration of SLA terms into client contracts and oversee the penalty/credit policy in collaboration with Finance and Sales.

- Produce and automate accurate reporting on service levels using tools like Metabase, Grafana, and Redash.

- Collaborate with Engineering and Product teams to define technical standards and incident management protocols regarding SLAs.

- Monitor compliance with SecNumCloud requirements regarding service levels.

- Act as the primary point of contact for all SLA-related inquiries, providing visibility to the leadership team.

- Drive continuous improvement initiatives to enhance operational rigor and business alignment.
