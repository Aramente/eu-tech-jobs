<h3><strong>About the Role</strong></h3>
<p>As the <strong>Business Insights and Planning Manager</strong>, you will partner across the Americas Sales and Revenue Operations teams to accelerate growth through strategic analytics and planning. This is a high-impact revenue intelligence position focused on generating insights and influencing execution. A critical component of this role is serving as the primary advocate for the Americas, representing regional performance and requirements to global teams to drive cross-functional alignment. You will translate complex data into clear, actionable decisions across the revenue organization, acting as a self-starter who can manage independent workstreams while serving as a trusted thought partner to leadership.</p>
<h3><strong>What You'll Do</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Strategic Revenue Insights: Conduct hypothesis-driven analysis to identify trends and risks. Move beyond "what happened" to clearly articulate "why," recommending specific commercial actions to accelerate growth.</li>
<li>Annual Sales Planning &amp; Modeling: Lead the analytical engine for annual planning, including quota and capacity modeling, and provide data-driven recommendations for territory segmentation to optimize sales coverage.</li>
<li>Executive Presentation: Build compelling data packs and decks for quarterly business reviews (QBRs), strategic planning and pipeline generation reviews, presenting key regional performance insights to senior and VP-level stakeholders.</li>
<li>Revenue Intelligence Framework: Support and refine the end-to-end revenue intelligence model, ensuring a unified view across pipeline, bookings, funnel performance, and sales onboarding.</li>
<li>Metric Clarity &amp; Data Stewardship: Drive metric consistency across Salesforce, Google Suite, Forecasting tools. Lead data cleanup initiatives to ensure high-quality inputs and partner with global teams to improve data usability.</li>
<li>Global Alignment &amp; Representation: Serve as the lead voice for the Americas in global forums; ensure regional nuances are represented in global reporting frameworks and drive alignment between regional execution and global strategy.</li>
<li>Audience-Scaled Communication: Deliver tailored insights ranging from simple, actionable signals for field sales to high-level trend implications and strategic choices for the executives.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Desirable Skills, Knowledge, and Experience</h3>
<ul>
<li>Professional Experience: 8+ years of experience in Sales/Revenue Operations, Revenue Intelligence, or Management Consulting.</li>
<li>Education: Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Finance, Economics, Analytics, Data Science, or a related field.</li>
<li>Technical Proficiency: Advanced proficiency in Google Suite, Power BI and Excel (including SQL, data modeling, and DAX) to perform complex performance analysis across multiple systems.</li>
<li>AI Leverage: Demonstrated ability to leverage AI tools to accelerate data analysis, automate reporting workflows, and enhance the speed of insight generation.</li>
<li>Planning Expertise: Direct experience in sales capacity modeling, quota setting, and account segmentation methodologies.</li>
<li>Executive Presence: Proven ability to influence senior stakeholders and represent regional interests effectively within a complex, matrixed global organization.</li>
</ul>
