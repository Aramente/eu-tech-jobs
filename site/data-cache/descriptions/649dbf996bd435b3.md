<h2><strong>Join our mission, join Doctolib!</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for an <strong>Engineering Manager</strong> to join Doctolib's <strong>Automation Platform</strong> (AUTOP) team, which empowers all Doctolib engineers to build, test, and deploy software with confidence and speed.</p>
<p>Part of the Platform organization, Autop’s mission is to <strong>build and maintain the automation infrastructure and developer tools</strong> that enable our engineering teams to deliver high-quality software efficiently. <strong>Every feature shipped</strong> at Doctolib — from appointment booking to teleconsultation to AI-powered clinical tools — <strong>flows through AUTOP's automation infrastructure</strong>. We're the platform behind the platform. When product teams ship faster, safer, and with more confidence, it's because AUTOP built the rails they run on.</p>
<p>AUTOP (Automation Platform) is responsible for <strong>critical developer-facing systems</strong> including: CI/CD automation, testing infrastructure (unit, integration, E2E, contract, visual regression), local and ephemeral development environments, release management, dependency management, and <strong>developer productivity tooling</strong>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>What you will do</strong></h3>
<p>As the Engineering Manager for AUTOP, your responsibilities include, but are not limited to:</p>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">People leadership</span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Build and lead a high-performing <strong>team of 6-10 engineers</strong> who are passionate about developer productivity</li>
<li>Create a culture where <strong>engineers own outcomes, not tasks</strong> — where platform adoption and developer satisfaction are the north stars</li>
<li><strong>Recruit, mentor, and retain</strong> top platform engineering talent in a competitive market</li>
<li>Conduct impactful 1:1s, performance reviews, and career development conversations that help engineers reach their full potential</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Technical Strategy &amp; Execution</span></h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Define and drive</strong> the technical vision for your team's domain (CI/CD, testing infrastructure, developer environments, or release automation)</li>
<li>Make high-impact technical decisions that balance short-term delivery with long-term sustainability and scale</li>
<li>Set the bar for automation, quality, reliability, and security — your systems must be highly reliable because <strong>600 engineers+ depending on them daily</strong></li>
<li>Champion a <strong>"Platform as a Product" mindset</strong>: treat internal developers as customers, measure adoption and satisfaction, iterate based on feedback</li>
<li>Partner strategically with product teams, Infrastructure Platform, and Applicative Platform to solve cross-cutting challenges</li>
<li>Drive <strong>measurable improvements in developer productivity metrics</strong>: build times, deployment frequency, test reliability, time-to-production… supporting Doctolib teams to monitor their metrics (DORA, performance, costs, etc).</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Impact &amp; Influence</span></h4>
<ul>
<li>Be a visible technical leader across the organization — your team's work <strong>enables everyone else's success</strong></li>
<li>Influence engineering culture: establish best practices, contribute to technical strategy beyond your team</li>
<li>Navigate ambiguity and lead through influence, not authority — <strong>platform adoption requires trust and advocacy</strong>, not mandates</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>About our tech environment</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Our automation platform supports a <strong>hybrid architecture</strong>: a large Ruby on Rails monolith and an expanding ecosystem of modern microservices (Java, Kotlin, TypeScript)</li>
<li>We maintain <strong>sophisticated CI/CD pipelines</strong> using GitHub Actions, testing frameworks across multiple languages, and <strong>infrastructure-as-code</strong> solutions</li>
<li>Our systems handle thousands of CI builds daily, manage comprehensive test suites across multiple languages, and manage deployments across multiple environments for <strong>200+ development teams</strong></li>
<li>Our stack includes: Ruby, Java, Kotlin, Go, TypeScript, Docker, Kubernetes, GitHub Actions, Terraform, and various testing frameworks</li>
<li>We intentionally&nbsp;<strong>leverage AI </strong>to improve developer productivity and reliability, in <strong>a sustainable manner</strong> .</li>
</ul>
