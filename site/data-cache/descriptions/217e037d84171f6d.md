The team of over 1400 employees includes more than 400 highly skilled engineers with deep expertise across hardware and software engineering, as well as an in-house AI R&amp;D team.</p></div><h2 id="Your-responsibilities-will-include:" data-local-id="c43f1fe7d3ff" data-renderer-start-pos="923">The Role</h2>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="560" data-local-id="188631b6e839">We’re looking for a&nbsp;<strong data-renderer-mark="true">Network Site Reliability Engineer (NetSRE)</strong> to help build and run the fundamental part of Nebius - the Network - the infrastructure everything else depends on. This is an engineering-first SRE role: you’ll set clear reliability targets, build the tooling and automation to meet them, and make the network safer to operate as we scale quickly.</p>
<h4 id="Your-responsibilities-will-include:" data-local-id="c43f1fe7d3ff" data-renderer-start-pos="923">Your responsibilities will include:</h4>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-local-id="d1e280d7-a2c9-4aec-b316-88cdc6688470" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="962" data-local-id="db8d27337cbf">Define and own reliability goals for network services and critical paths (SLIs/SLOs, availability targets, error budgets where it makes sense)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1108" data-local-id="3262115d31ed">Drive reliability improvements across the whole network: not only services, but also site readiness, inter-site connectivity (DCI), and operational standards</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1269" data-local-id="045f789fbfe3">Own incident response for your areas, lead investigations/postmortems, and turn failures into durable fixes (not repeated firefighting)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1408" data-local-id="eeee0fc6efcd">Build and evolve observability: actionable metrics/logs/traces, alerting, and faster debug loops during and after incidents</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1535" data-local-id="7c2e9a1a964f">Design safer change workflows: automation, CI/CD, test/staging environments, canarying, rollbacks, and auditability for network changes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1674" data-local-id="44d9e3bfabd8">Work closely with network engineers and platform teams to embed operability into designs and keep operations practical and fast</p>
</li>
</ul>
