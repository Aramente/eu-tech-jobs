<p><strong>THE WORK:</strong></p>
<p>Are you a seasoned <strong>Lead Technical Recruiter</strong> with a precision attitude and a deep understanding of the quantitative landscape? Look no further! Ripple is seeking a Lead Technical Recruiter to join our dynamic team and help us secure the elite talent necessary to drive our high-frequency growth and algorithmic innovation. As the Lead Technical Recruiter, you will be a crucial architect of our talent pipeline, owning the end-to-end process for specialized Quantitative Engineers and Researchers. You will partner closely with hiring managers to identify and attract the world's brightest mathematical and technical minds.</p>
<p>In this role, you will have the opportunity to work with a versatile team and play a key role in crafting the future of Ripple. You will work closely with our Talent Acquisition team to develop and implement a sophisticated recruiting strategy that aligns with our strategic objectives, while providing an exceptional candidate experience for high-stakes talent. Join us and be part of a company that is revolutionizing the way we think about global payments through innovative engineering!</p>
<p><strong>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Own the specialized recruiting process</strong> from sourcing to complex offer negotiations for Quantitative Engineering and Algorithmic Trading positions.</li>
<li><strong>Develop high-level strategies</strong> to meet aggressive hiring goals, working alongside Engineering Leads to align technical priorities and performance objectives.</li>
<li><strong>Plan out-of-the-box sourcing strategies</strong> to attract elite talent from niche environments like Kaggle, GitHub, ArXiv, competitive programming forums (Codeforces), and quantitative finance conferences (NeurIPS, ICML).</li>
<li><strong>Ensure a white-glove candidate experience</strong>, keeping sophisticated candidates advised and well-prepared for rigorous technical assessments throughout the entire process.</li>
<li><strong>Partner with hiring managers</strong> to deeply understand the nuances of specific quant stacks, modeling requirements, and performance engineering needs.</li>
<li><strong>Own and drive the intake meeting</strong>, interview team kick-off, and technical debrief process to ensure a data-driven hiring bar.</li>
<li><strong>Deliver weekly performance analytics</strong> and progress reports to hiring managers and senior leadership.</li>
</ul>
