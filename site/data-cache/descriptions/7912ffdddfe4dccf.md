**Job Description**

- Experience operating CCTV/sewer-inspection camera systems: setting up the camera, launching from manholes, positioning, controlling lighting, capturing video.
- -Knowledge of wastewater collection systems, manholes, sewer mains, laterals, and how condition issues appear on video (e.g., cracks, root intrusion, infiltration, blockages).
- -Ability to interact with other teams (maintenance/repair crews, engineers, customers/community) and provide feedback on inspection results or required remediation.
- -Ability to write clear inspection reports, document findings (video logs, photographs, condition codes).
- Knowledge of safety protocols: traffic control when working on street or in roadways, personal protective equipment (PPE), OSHA or equivalent; perhaps confined-space training/certification.

**Qualifications**

- Service Titan Knowledge preferred
- CDL license preferred (not required)
- Operate a skid-steer
- Run a backhoe machine
- 3-5 years of exterior sewer and water experience
- Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
- Strong customer service orientation with a high sense of urgency.
- High school diploma or GED.
- 3+ years of related field experience.
- Some exposure to plumbing, utility work, or mechanical/electrical systems.
- Comfortable working outdoors and in confined spaces.
