## Your Mission

As Executive Assistant, you will play a vital role in ensuring the smooth and efficient functioning of daily operations. In this position, you will manage a broad range of administrative and organizational responsibilities, acting as a trusted partner and key point of coordination within the department and across teams.

## Sneak preview of your future tasks

<ul><li>Managing the Head of Department’s calendar, prioritizing appointments, and ensuring an organized and efficient schedule</li><li>Preparing, moderating, and following up on department-wide meetings, including agenda setting, documentation, and tracking of action items</li><li>Planning and coordinating internal workshops—preparing agendas, managing logistics, and ensuring the smooth execution of workshop days</li><li>Coordinating all aspects of business travel, including bookings, itineraries, and post-trip expense reimbursements</li><li>Handling reimbursements for business-related lunches, dinners, and events in a timely and accurate manner</li><li>Acting as a key contact for internal and external stakeholders, ensuring clear, professional, and timely communication</li><li>Preparing high-quality presentations, reports, and briefing materials for internal and external leadership meetings</li><li>Collaborating closely with assistant teams across departments to support cross-functional alignment and day-to-day operations</li><li>Supporting internal projects, taking initiative, and contributing to continuous improvements within administrative processes</li></ul>
