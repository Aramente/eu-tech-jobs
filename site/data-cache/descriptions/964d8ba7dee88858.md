<li>The AI Agent: Optimizing the retrieval layer that allows LLMs to reason over data they weren’t trained on, ensuring they have the "ground truth" needed&nbsp; to synthesize accurate, high-stakes business actions.</li>

<p>This isn't a solved problem. You will be tackling "Search" in its most evolved form:</p>
<ul>

<li>Dual-Optimization: Fine-tuning ranking models that satisfy both human readability and LLM-ready context.</li>
<li>High-Stakes Accuracy: In a world of Agents, poor search quality leads to hallucinations. You will build the guardrails and relevance scoring that ensure our AI stays grounded in reality.</li>
<li>Data Heterogeneity: Connecting the dots across structured SQL tables, unstructured docs, and real-time business metrics.</li>
</ul>
