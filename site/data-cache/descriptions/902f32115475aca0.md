<p><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p>The Merchant Operations (MOps) Excellence team is the backbone of our first-line defense (CDD, Credit Risk, Fraud, AML). As our product suite and global regulations evolve, the "compliant journey" becomes increasingly complex.</p>
<p><br>We are not looking for a traditional Project Manager to simply track deadlines. We are looking for an Excellence Manager who will take ongoing ownership of the operational health and readiness of our business. You won't just deliver a project and move on; you will ensure that our operations are aligned, visible, and prepared for every launch, migration, and strategic shift.</p>
<p>In this role, you are the guardian of operational health. You will sit at the intersection of Product, Compliance and Merchant Ops, ensuring that every change, new launch or migration isn't just "delivered," but is successfully integrated into our ecosystem. You will move beyond tracking deadlines to exercising high-level judgment- assessing business impact, identifying structural bottlenecks, and ensuring our first-line teams have the visibility and confidence to scale. If you are a structured, business-minded problem solver who thrives in ambiguity, this is your opportunity to drive true operational excellence.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Assess Business Impact: </strong>Move beyond the "what" to the "how." You analyze how new products or regulatory changes affect MOps workflows and ensure the business is ready for scale.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Own launch &amp; migration readiness: </strong>You are the driver of the ‘Go/No-Go’ process. You don't just track a launch; you assess if the business is actually prepared to support it, ensuring end-to-end visibility across all stakeholders.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Process Improvement:</strong> Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies in current operational workflows and propose scalable solutions, aligning with our "Build for Scale" principle.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Drive Operational Preparedness:</strong> Identify bottlenecks before they happen. You own the transition from "project" to "business as usual," ensuring first-line teams have the documentation, logic, and confidence to execute.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Cross-Functional Integration:</strong> Act as the primary connector between Product, Compliance, and Merchant Operations. You ensure that product roadmaps are aligned with operational capacity and tool-stack capabilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Meeting Facilitation:</strong> Support the preparation and execution of strategic leadership meetings by contributing to agenda development, facilitating discussions, and ensuring clear takeaways, owners, and timelines are established.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Problem Solving &amp; Judgment:</strong> You will navigate ambiguous situations where there is no playbook. You use your judgment to prioritize what matters for the business over what is simply next on a list.</p>
</li>
</ul>
