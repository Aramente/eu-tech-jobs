<div class="content-intro"><p>From Fivetran’s founding until now, our mission has remained the same: to make access to data as simple and reliable as electricity. With Fivetran, customer data arrives in their warehouses, canonical and ready to query, with no engineering or maintenance required. We’re proud that more organizations continue to leverage our technology every day to become truly data-driven.</p></div><p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>About the Role</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">We’re looking for a Technology Partner Sales Manager (PSM) to join our Global Alliances team. In this role, you will be a key driver of revenue execution, specifically focused on scaling our co-selling ecosystem with major technology partners like Snowflake, Databricks, and hyperscalers (AWS, GCP, and Azure). You will act as the bridge between Fivetran’s internal sales teams and our partners' field organizations, ensuring we source, co-sell, and win high-impact deals together.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This is a full-time position based remotely in Singapore .</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Technologies You’ll Use</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Salesforce (SFDC): For opportunity tracking, pipeline management, and partner activity tagging.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Crossbeam: For account mapping, list enrichment, and identifying joint targets with partners.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Gong &amp; Looker: For identifying co-sell opportunities and analyzing pipeline health.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Suger: To scale day-to-day co-selling processes and system improvements.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Slack: For real-time co-sell alignment and communication with partner AE teams.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Execute joint sales motions with technology partners to drive revenue and meet influenced incremental booking quotas.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Drive pipeline creation by identifying co-sell opportunities within the Fivetran open pipeline and submitting them for partner alignment.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Lead co-sell calls and account workshops, owning the agenda, introductions, and "better together" value proposition messaging.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Enable internal Fivetran AEs on partner solutions, use cases, and joint GTM strategies to increase partner-influenced deals.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Source and execute partner enablements, including QBRs, hands-on labs (HOLs), and sales training for partner AEs.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Own account mapping efforts using Crossbeam to identify overlapping targets and provide recommendations to Fivetran and partner reps.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Establish sales leadership alignment by facilitating recurring cadences between Fivetran and partner sales managers.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Communicate field feedback to Partner Development Managers (PDMs) to continuously improve partner offerings and messaging.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Manage regional partner events, such as happy hours and conferences, to build Fivetran brand awareness and relationship depth.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Track and report on sales KPIs, ensuring all partner-influenced activity is accurately tagged and reflected in global dashboards.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Skills We’re Looking For</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Co-Selling Excellence: Proven ability to navigate complex co-sell cycles with hyperscalers and data platforms (Snowflake, Databricks, etc.).</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Strategic Relationship Management: Experience building and maintaining high-trust relationships with external partner AEs and sales leadership.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Sales Enablement: Skill in distilling complex technical integrations into compelling "why Fivetran" value propositions for partner sellers.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Data-Driven Pipeline Management: Proficiency in using CRM and account mapping tools to identify and prioritize high-value opportunities.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Collaboration &amp; Orchestration: Ability to coordinate cross-functional efforts between Sales, Marketing, and Partner Development teams.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Event Execution: Experience sourcing and managing logistics for regional sales events, lunch and learns, or QBRs.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Clear Communication: Strong ability to deliver joint win stories ("Win Wires") and updates that keep Fivetran top-of-mind for partners.</span></li>
</ul>
