<p>Figma is growing and moving fast. <li>Develop strong internal partnerships and collaborate cross functionally to increase revenue opportunities to improve deal progressions</li>
<li>Build, develop, and inspire a team of high-performing Account Executives and leaders by creating scalable, data driven processes that drive team and company goals</li>
<li>Be accountable for selling to existing customers and net new customers&nbsp;</li>

</ul>

<ul>

<li>Prior experience leading both Managers and Enterprise Account Executives&nbsp;</li>
<li>Proven experience leading and building successful, collaborative, and hard-working sales teams</li>
<li>A proven track record of leading Sales teams to consistently meet and exceed goals</li>
<li>Understanding of our technical and competitive landscape</li>
</ul>
