<p><strong>Mission</strong></p>
<p>Reporting to the EMEA People Partner Director, the&nbsp;<strong>Staff People Partner</strong> is a strategic advisor and catalyst for organisational excellence, partnering closely with senior leaders across Databricks’ European footprint.</p>
<p>This role operates at the intersection of business strategy, talent, and transformation, shaping a high-performance, future-ready organisation in the context of rapid growth and AI-driven change.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Travel:</strong> May involve travel to other EMEA locations and the US for quarterly business reviews, People team meetings and for other ad-hoc business reasons</li>
<li><strong>Focus:</strong> Field Engineering, Professional Services and Field Enablement teams, subject to change over time</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Act as a trusted strategic partner</strong> to senior leaders within the Field Engineering, Professional Services and Field Enablement teams, translating business priorities into forward-looking people strategies that drive performance, scalability, and innovation.</li>
<li><strong>Lead organisational transformation</strong> by designing and enabling structures, capabilities, and ways of working that support hyper-growth and evolving market demands.</li>
<li><strong>Elevate the talent bar</strong> through rigorous talent management, succession planning, and leadership development, ensuring we attract, develop, and retain exceptional talent.</li>
<li><strong>Champion a high-performance culture</strong> grounded in accountability, inclusion, and continuous growth, while enhancing the end-to-end employee experience.</li>
<li><strong>Coach and challenge leaders</strong> to build inclusive, resilient, and future-ready teams, particularly in navigating change driven by AI and new ways of working.</li>
<li><strong>Drive organisational effectiveness</strong> through strategic organisational design, while acting as a senior escalation point for complex employee relations matters, ensuring consistent and risk-aware outcomes.</li>
<li><strong>Partner across EMEA and globally</strong> to deliver a consistent yet locally relevant people experience, balancing global standards with regional nuance.</li>
<li><strong>Leverage data and insights</strong> to inform decision-making, measure impact, and continuously improve people outcomes.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What you’ll bring:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as a senior HR/People Business Partner in a high-growth, complex, and matrixed environment.</li>
<li>Curiosity and adaptability in the face of emerging trends, particularly the impact of AI on work, skills, and organisations.</li>
<li>Data-driven mindset with the ability to translate insights into action.</li>
<li>Strong commercial acumen with the ability to connect people strategy to business outcomes.</li>
<li>Track record of leading large-scale change and transformation initiatives.</li>
<li>Deep expertise across core people disciplines (organisation design, talent, performance, employee relations, rewards).</li>
<li>Skilled at influencing and coaching senior stakeholders with credibility and impact.</li>
<li>Passion for building inclusive, high-performing cultures and exceptional employee experiences.</li>
</ul>
