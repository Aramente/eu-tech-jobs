<h2>What you’ll do</h2>
<h3>Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Research and create outreach materials for high value prospects, in partnership with SDRs and AEs</li>
<li>Follow up with Marketing generated leads to qualify as sales opportunities. Move solid leads through the funnel connecting them to a salesperson, and arranging meetings</li>
<li>Execute outbound sales plays created by marketing</li>
<li>Initiate contact with potential customers through cold-calling or responding to inquiries generated from Marketing</li>
<li>Develop relationships with prospects to uncover needs through effective questioning to qualify interest and viability to prepare hand-off to sales</li>
<li>Follow-up with potential customers who expressed interest but did not initially result in a sales opportunity</li>
<li>Effectively work through lead list meeting/exceeding SLAs, consistently update activity and contact information within the CRM system and support weekly reporting efforts</li>
<li>Collaborate and provide feedback and insights to Marketing to help improve targeting and messaging</li>
</ul>
