Join us as a **Kaizen Officer** to lead the transformation of continuous improvement at Qonto. You will shift Lean from theory to practice, building a scalable culture of operational excellence across our fast-growing organization — and prove its measurable business impact, reporting to [**Olivia**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olivia-mella-52235583/), within the **Knowledge & Learning** team.

➡️ **What you'll do**

* **Make Lean work for the business, not the other way around:** You'll bring solid Lean expertise and put it fully at the service of our teams — practical, grounded, and always tied to real outcomes.
* **Build your network and embed a shared culture:** You'll connect with people across the organization, identify champions in every team, and partner with L&D to design and deliver the right learning experiences.
* **Get on the ground with teams:** You'll run hands-on improvement projects alongside operational teams — diagnosing real problems, facilitating Kaizen events, and driving tangible, measurable outcomes.
* **Drive cultural change from the inside:** You'll build trust, identify the right people to bet on, and use every tool at your disposal — including AI — to scale your impact and help teams work smarter.

➡️ **What we're looking for**

* **Change leader who influences without authority:** You've driven cultural transformation in complex, fast-moving organizations — getting buy-in from leadership and frontline teams alike.
* **Data-driven practitioner:** You've built ROI models for "soft" improvements and know how to make the business case for Lean in a language CFOs and CPOs understand.
* **Applied facilitator:** You've designed and run workshops that solve real problems — not training sessions that end with a certificate and zero follow-through.
* **Systemic thinker:** You've operated in hyper-growth or multi-geo environments and know how to navigate ambiguity, connect dots across silos, and find root causes before jumping to solutions.
* **Energy giver:** You make Lean feel energizing, not imposed — people follow you because they want to, not because they have to.

➡️ **What you'll get**

* **Real ownership from day one:** You'll shape this role significantly — defining what Lean looks like at Qonto's next stage of scale.
* **Direct exposure to leadership:** You'll work alongside exec-level stakeholders and report directly to **Olivia** in the **Knowledge & Learning** team.
* **A community ready to scale with you:** You'll partner with former Kaizen Officers now embedded across operational teams — the foundation is there, you make it fly.
* **A role that grows with you:** Clear path to lead team expansion with junior members and explore internal mobility as the function matures.

➡️ **Your future manager**

You’ll report to [**Olivia**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olivia-mella-52235583/), who has been at Qonto for 4 years and has seen the company grow from **~500 to ~1,700** Qontoers. Olivia started in **Onboarding** and progressively expanded her scope across **L&D, Knowledge Management and DEI**. Olivia’s management style will suit you if you want to be developed and coached, and if you want to grow in **autonomy and visibility**.
