<div class="content-intro"><p><strong>COMPANY OVERVIEW</strong></p>
<p>KKR is a leading global investment firm that offers alternative asset management as well as capital markets and insurance solutions. KKR aims to generate attractive investment returns by following a patient and disciplined investment approach, employing world-class people, and supporting growth in its portfolio companies and communities. KKR sponsors investment funds that invest in private equity, credit and real assets and has strategic partners that manage hedge funds. KKR’s insurance subsidiaries offer retirement, life and reinsurance products under the management of Global Atlantic Financial Group. References to KKR’s investments may include the activities of its sponsored funds and insurance subsidiaries.</p></div><p><strong>Position Summary</strong></p>
<p data-start="145" data-end="696">This role supports the execution, enhancement, and governance of the Economic Balance Sheet (EBS) process for a growing (re)insurance platform. The position sits at the intersection of actuarial modeling, financial reporting, controls, and process improvement, with direct exposure to Bermuda-based actuarial teams, auditors, and regulators. The individual will contribute to quarterly and annual EBS production, analyze and explain model results, onboard new reinsurance transactions, and help strengthen the control framework around the EBS process.</p>
<p data-start="698" data-end="1203">Success in this role requires strong technical fundamentals, intellectual curiosity, and a hands-on mindset. The ideal candidate is early in their actuarial career, actively pursuing ASA designation, and eager to deepen their understanding of life and annuity products, actuarial models, and regulatory expectations. This is a high-visibility role for someone who takes ownership, works well under pressure, and is motivated by both individual contribution and collaboration within a high-performing team.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with performing annual and quarterly EBS processes</li>
<li>Work with Bermuda actuarial team to analyze and explain model results for base and sensitivity/stress runs</li>
<li>Process improvement – building tools to streamline the EBS process.</li>
<li>Assist with the development of internal controls over the EBS process</li>
<li>Producing process and control documentation for internal and audit reference</li>
<li>Executing validation and UAT procedures on changes to EBS process</li>
<li>Onboarding new reinsurance deals into EBS process</li>
<li>Preparing documentation and support for audit and regulatory requests</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>1-3 years experience in the life (re)insurance or annuity industry required</li>
<li>ASA (or equivalent) or actively working toward Associate designation</li>
<li>SOX control and compliance experience preferred</li>
<li>Strong technical and communication skills</li>
<li>Strong Excel knowledge (Power Query a plus)</li>
<li>Coding language experience a plus (Python preferred)</li>
<li>Actuarial modeling software experience a plus (MG ALFA preferred)</li>
<li>Works well under pressure in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Is persistent when developing a solution to a problem</li>
<li>Balances the dichotomy between working with a high-caliber team and individual production</li>
<li>Takes ownership of their responsibilities</li>
<li>Thrives in an environment that prioritizes open and honest communication and feedback.</li>
</ul>
