Join us as a **Marketing Intern**and help shape Qonto's brand presence on the French market: from driving creative production to contributing to campaigns seen by hundreds of thousands of SMEs. You'll own real projects, work hand-in-hand with external agencies, and be part of some of Qonto's most exciting upcoming brand moments, under the guidance of Oleksandra, our France Brand Manager.

### ➡️ What you'll do

* **Drive creative production end-to-end:** Own the production workflow for French-market brand activations, tracking every milestone from brief to final delivery, chasing approvals, and making sure nothing slips.
* **Shape the next 2 years of our brand:** Run creative analyses and post-tests on our new brand equity campaign to extract insights and fuel the next waves of a concept that will live across all our channels.
* **Bring stunts to life:** Support the planning and execution of out-of-the-box activations, think nothing you'd find in a textbook.
* **Be the quality gate:** Review creatives across every format and spec, spotting the misplaced logo, the missing legal mention, the off-brand color, before they go live.
* **Use AI to move faster:** Leverage AI tools to streamline quality checks, creative research, and feedback consolidation — we produce a lot, and smart tooling makes all the difference.

### ➡️ What we're looking for

* **Marketing fundamentals:** You're enrolled in an M1 or M2 in marketing, communications, branding, media, or digital marketing — and you genuinely understand how brand campaigns are built.
* **Creative eye with structured feedback:** You can articulate *why* a creative works or doesn't — not just "I like it," but what's landing, what's off, and how to fix it.
* **Organization obsession:** You track 20 parallel steps across multiple campaigns without dropping the ball, and you flag risks before they turn into problems.
* **Bilingual French + English:** Native-level French is mandatory to be able to supervise the content production of French campaigns; fluent English to collaborate with the full team.
* **AI-curious mindset:** You already use AI tools to work smarter — or you're genuinely excited to start — and you see it as a real productivity lever, not a gimmick.

### ➡️ What you'll get

* **Real ownership, fast:** Oleksandra gives autonomy as soon as trust is built; expect to lead workstreams within weeks, not months.
* **End-to-end brand campaign exposure:** You'll see and touch the full cycle, agency briefing, creative feedback loops, production, and multi-format delivery across TV, OOH, digital, audio, and social.
* **Exciting brand moments ahead:** Help shape a new 2-year brand creative concept, contribute to stunts across markets, and support major upcoming launches (Mirror Card, credit licensing comms).
* **A small team that moves fast:** Four people, market-dedicated, where no two weeks look the same and every project has real visibility.
