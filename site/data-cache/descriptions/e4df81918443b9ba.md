<p>P.S: We highly recommend reading <a href="https://api-first-world.com/">The "API-First World" graphic novel</a> to understand the bigger picture and our vision at Postman.</p></div><h2><strong>The Opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>We are seeking a proactive and detail-oriented <strong>Renewal Representative</strong> to own the customer renewal experience in a fast-moving, product-led growth (PLG) SaaS environment. In this role, you’ll be responsible for managing the full renewal cycle across a portfolio of customers—ensuring timely renewals, identifying expansion opportunities, and minimizing churn.</p>
<p>As a key revenue contributor, you’ll work closely with Customer Success, Sales, and Product teams to deliver a seamless and value-driven renewal process that aligns with product usage and customer goals.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Manage a high volume of renewal opportunities across multiple customer segments, including self-serve and low-touch accounts.</li>
<li>Proactively engage customers to confirm intent to renew, answer questions, and communicate the value they’re receiving from the product.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Customer Success Managers on more complex or strategic renewals.</li>
<li>Identify upsell or cross-sell opportunities at the point of renewal and route qualified leads to Sales when appropriate.</li>
<li>Use product usage data and customer health metrics to prioritize outreach and inform renewal strategies.</li>
<li>Prepare and send quotes, process purchase orders, and manage billing/contract logistics in coordination with RevOps and Finance.</li>
<li>Track all activity and forecast renewals accurately using CRM tools (e.g., Salesforce, Clari).</li>
<li>Contribute to ongoing improvements in playbooks, messaging, automation, and processes to scale renewal outcomes.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>About You</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Required Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>2+ years of experience in a renewals, customer success, sales support, or operations role—ideally in a SaaS or PLG company.</li>
<li>Experience managing a book of business with high transaction volume.</li>
<li>Strong communication and negotiation skills, with a customer-first mindset.</li>
<li>Comfortable using tools like Salesforce, Gainsight, Stripe/Billing platforms, and similar platforms.</li>
<li>Highly organized with strong attention to detail and follow-through.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in a PLG model with freemium, usage-based, or self-serve customer motions.</li>
<li>Familiarity with tools like SalesForce, Gainsight, or similar platforms.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of SaaS contracts, licensing, and billing structures.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Key Performance Metrics (KPIs):</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Renewal Rate (Gross &amp; Net)</li>
<li>Churn Rate</li>
<li>On-Time Renewal %</li>
<li>Renewal Pipeline Coverage &amp; Forecast Accuracy</li>
