<h2><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>What you'll do</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">We are looking for a&nbsp;<strong>Staff Mobile Engineer</strong> with strong expertise in <strong>React Native</strong> to join our Patient domain at Doctolib.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">As a Mobile Staff Engineer, your mission will be to work on transforming the Doctolib mobile app into a full <strong>health companion powered by AI</strong> to help people lead healthier lives. You will play a key role in improving the quality, UX, and depth of our mobile application while contributing significantly to the technical foundations of mobile development for the team.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Working in the tech team at Doctolib involves building innovative products and features to improve the daily lives of care teams and patients. We work in feature teams in an agile environment, while collaborating with product, design, and business teams.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Establish and evolve mobile architecture patterns and technical standards for the patient domain</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Drive technical excellence across mobile development, ensuring high code quality and best practices</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Contribute to the technical roadmap and guide architectural decisions for the mobile platform</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Mentor and support mobile engineers in implementing robust, scalable solutions</span></li>
<li style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Lead by example in building features that enhance the user experience and app performance</span></li>
