<p><strong>About this opportunity:</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 300;">Freenome is seeking a high-impact contributor to bridge the gap between our innovative diagnostic technology and the clinical workflows of our health system partners. In this role, you will be the primary project lead for EHR integrations, digital products, and driving the "Order-to-Results" lifecycle from pre-sales consultation through to full operational scale.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 300;">Unlike a traditional development role, this is a Commercial-facing position. You will act as the technical architect of the customer relationship, ensuring that our integrations—primarily powered by Redox—are not just functional, but optimized for clinician adoption and business growth. You will partner closely with our Sales team and Health System partners to implement EHR integration solutions and generate proven commercial outcomes, serving as the subject matter expert who navigates the complexities of health system infrastructure to ensure Freenome’s products are deeply embedded and easy to use.<br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 300;">The role reports to the Director of Client Solutions &amp; Patient Experience. This role will be a remote role that may require some travel, up to 20% of the time.<br></span></p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Leverage Redox to develop strategic integrations with health systems and industry partners to enable order to results capabilities and digital health tools across major EHR vendors, including Epic.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Work with Orders to Cash Product lead and others to execute EHR and other digital health tools strategy.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Manage EHR implementation projects with Redox and customer partners to enable bidirectional interfaces.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Provide customer relationship management and pre-sale connectivity support to enable business growth.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Work with optimization and implementation team during customer onboarding to complete technical integration for EHR and other digital products.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Provide business reviews, performance dashboards, and analytics information to inform EHR strategy and to achieve account health and volume benchmarks.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Support customer success in efforts to help increase product adoption where technology can be used.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Provide general technical support and trouble shooting of Freenome’s software solutions beyond EHR.</span><span style="font-weight: 300;"><br></span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Must haves:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">EHR Program Leadership: At least 4 years of experience managing complex Electronic Health Record (EHR) implementations or healthcare interoperability programs.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Commercial &amp; Customer Facing Success: At least 3 years of experience in a customer-facing role with a proven track record of supporting sales cycles and managing external stakeholder expectations.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Clinical Workflow Knowledge: Deep understanding of hospital operations, provider workflows, and how clinical data moves within a health system environment.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Project Management Mastery: Proven ability to lead multi-phase technical projects, managing timelines across internal teams and external vendor/customer partners to meet commercial milestones.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 300;"><span style="font-weight: 300;">Educational Background: Bachelor’s degree in Healthcare Informatics, Business Administration, Information Technology, or a related field.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to haves:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Integration Middleware Expertise: Demonstrated experience working with Redox or similar healthcare integration platforms to facilitate bidirectional data exchange.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Strategic Analytics: Proficiency in developing performance dashboards and business reviews to communicate implementation success and ROI to executive-level stakeholders.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Regulatory Knowledge: Working knowledge of HIPAA, SOC2, and other applicable laws and regulations governing electronic health records and data privacy.</span></li>

<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Public Speaking: Strong presentation skills with the ability to simplify complex technical concepts for non-technical clinical and administrative leaders.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Working knowledge of applicable laws and regulations for electronic health records.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: 300;">Proficiency in presentations and business reviews for internal and external stakeholders.</span></li>
