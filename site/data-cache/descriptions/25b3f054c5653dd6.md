<p>We are looking for a <strong>Senior QA Engineer</strong> to join our team and help us deliver high-quality solutions. <p><strong>What you'll be doing:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Product Managers and engineers early in discovery and development to identify risks, edge cases, and gaps before implementation</li>
<li>Drive test strategy by defining methodologies, quality criteria, and risk-based coverage aligned with User Stories and business goals</li>
<li>Own end-to-end test planning, from scope definition to execution, ensuring consistent and reliable validation of features</li>
<li>Drive test automation with a focus on business impact and cost-efficiency, ensuring balanced coverage across the test pyramid and repeatable patterns</li>
<li>Proactively identify, prioritize, and communicate defects with clear, reproducible reports aligned with team standards</li>
<li>Leverage data analysis (e.g., SQL) to investigate issues, validate assumptions, and support quality decisions</li>
<li>Close the loop between production signals (crash analytics, non-fatals, user-impact metrics) and pre-release test design, contributing to incident postmortems</li>
<li>Act as a quality advocate within Agile teams, influencing processes to improve speed, predictability, and outcomes</li>
<li>Contribute to and elevate QA practices across the team, driving continuous improvement and knowledge sharing</li>
</ul>
