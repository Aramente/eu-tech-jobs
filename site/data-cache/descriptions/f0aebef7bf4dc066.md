<p id="isPasted"><strong>Job Summary:<br></strong></p>
<p>The OEM Field Service Technician 1 is responsible for inspecting and fixing small visual imperfections in customer equipment to guarantee a perfect product. Responsibilities may also include troubleshooting, maintenance, training, and consulting. The role requires basic electrical wiring skills and the ability to read wire schematics. The technician will collaborate closely with peers, managers, and customers.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Supervisory Responsibilities:<br></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>None.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Duties/Responsibilities:<br>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Point-to-Point Checks</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform point-to-point checks to ensure all connections are properly established and functioning.</li>
<li>Ensure all electrical connections meet quality and safety standards.</li>
</ul>
<p>Read Schematics</p>
<ul>
<li>Interpret and understand wiring schematics to accurately perform installations and repairs.</li>
<li>Utilize wiring schematics for troubleshooting purposes.</li>
</ul>
<p>Wire Whiskers Inspection</p>
<ul>
<li>Inspect wire whiskers for any defects or irregularities to guarantee a perfect product.</li>
<li>Address any defects found.</li>
</ul>
<p>Terminate Wires</p>
<ul>
<li>Properly terminate wires as per the specifications to ensure safe and reliable operation of equipment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Excel Work</p>
<ul>
<li>Utilize Excel to manage and track progress, data, and reports related to ongoing&nbsp;projects<a id="\_anchor\_1" href="https://workforcenow.adp.com/theme/admin.html#\_msocom\_1" name="\_msoanchor\_1">[1]</a>&nbsp;.</li>
</ul>
