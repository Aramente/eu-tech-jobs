<p>We are hiring a <strong>Project Manager - Ubuntu Embedded Systems at Canonical</strong></p>
<p>As a Project Manager for Canonical’s Devices business, this role is responsible for the coordination and completion of large complex customer-facing projects, overseeing all aspects of project delivery and acting as the primary delivery liaison between Canonical's engineering teams and Canonical's customers.</p>
<p>You will be managing the projects that involve the delivery of Ubuntu images on a wide variety of devices ranging from robots, edge gateways, and IoT devices to PCs and automotive systems. This is a high-profile project management position where you are expected to build and maintain working relationships with team members, customers, other partners/vendors (Silicon vendors, OEMs, ODMs, IHVs), and other Canonical departments/teams.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Location:</strong> This role will be based remotely in the AMERICAs and/or EMEA regions.</p>

<ul>
