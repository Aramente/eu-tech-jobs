<p>We are seeking a Procurement Business Partner to serve as Anthropic's procurement lead for all EMEA purchasing operations. This generalist role will own end-to-end procurement across EMEA markets with particular depth in Real Estate &amp; Workplace and Professional Services categories. You will be the go-to procurement partner for EMEA business stakeholders, building procurement infrastructure and vendor relationships outside the US as Anthropic scales globally.</p>
<p>This role requires someone who can operate independently across time zones, navigate EMEA contracting complexities, and bring strategic sourcing rigour to a high-growth environment.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>International Procurement Operations</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Own end-to-end procurement operations for all EMEA purchasing, from intake and sourcing through negotiation, contracting, and ongoing vendor management</li>
<li>Serve as the primary procurement point of contact for EMEA offices, ensuring consistent procurement practices and compliance across geographies</li>
<li>Manage vendor onboarding, purchase requisitions, contract management, and spend analysis for EMEA spend</li>
<li>Navigate EMEA contracting requirements including local regulatory compliance, currency considerations, tax implications, and cross-border vendor structures</li>
<li>Champion Zip adoption and compliance for EMEA business partners; drive workflow improvements and self-service enablement</li>
<li>Ensure PO hygiene, timely receipting, and clean metadata that supports invoice matching, reporting, and financial controls</li>
<li>Support month-end close activities including accrual submissions and PO reconciliation for EMEA spend</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Strategic Sourcing &amp; Category Management</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead strategic sourcing initiatives across EMEA categories with particular focus on Real Estate &amp; Workplace (office buildouts, facilities management, FF&amp;E) and Professional Services (Big 4 advisory, audit, consulting, legal services)</li>
<li>Develop and execute category strategies that balance cost optimisation, quality, and local market dynamics</li>
<li>Conduct market intelligence and benchmarking across EMEA markets to inform sourcing decisions and ensure competitive pricing</li>
<li>Lead complex vendor negotiations independently, structuring deals that account for local market conditions while protecting Anthropic's financial interests</li>
<li>Build and maintain preferred-vendor frameworks for key EMEA categories, driving consolidation and leverage where appropriate</li>
<li>Manage RFP processes for major EMEA engagements</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Business Partnership &amp; Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the embedded procurement partner to EMEA teams across Workplace, Finance, Legal, People, and other functions</li>
<li>Partner with Legal, Security, TPRM, and Finance to ensure EMEA vendor engagements meet compliance and risk requirements across jurisdictions</li>
<li>Translate complex sourcing and financial concepts for non-procurement stakeholders; build trust through responsiveness and results</li>
<li>Proactively surface risks, savings opportunities, and vendor performance insights to business owners</li>
<li>Collaborate with the US-based Procurement team to ensure alignment on global vendor strategies and procurement standards</li>
<li>Develop procurement documentation, training materials, and best practice guides tailored to EMEA operations</li>
</ul>

<h2><strong>How we're different</strong></h2>
<p>We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.</p>
<p>The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI &amp; Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.</p>
