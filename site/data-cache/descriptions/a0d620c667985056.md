<p>Postman is the world’s leading API platform, used by more than 45 million+ developers and 500,000 organizations, including 98% of the Fortune 500. <p>We are looking for a <strong>Strategic Account Director</strong> to lead growth within a set of Postman’s most important enterprise accounts. <p>You will be paired with a pod that includes a Principal Solutions Engineer and a Field CTO, forming a high-caliber account team focused on building long-term, value-based relationships. Together, you’ll engage senior technology leaders, uncover complex challenges, and drive adoption of Postman’s enterprise and platform solutions at scale.</p>
<p>This is not a traditional “low-hanging fruit” sales role—this is about <strong>strategic account expansion, multi-threaded enterprise engagement, and solution selling</strong>.</p>

<ul>
