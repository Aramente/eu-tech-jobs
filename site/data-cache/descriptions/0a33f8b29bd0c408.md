<p>The Director, Marketplace owns end-to-end marketplace performance across a defined portfolio of markets.</p>
<p>You are accountable for growth, profitability, and overall marketplace health. You operate with full ownership of the P&amp;L and are responsible for balancing demand, supply, delivery quality, customer experience, and financial performance at scale.</p>
<p>This role leads through influence; working cross-functionally across Merchant, Operations, Marketing, Support, Finance, and Product to drive sustainable results. The role does not include direct reports.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Be Doing</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>Own P&amp;L and Performance</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Own the Rx P&amp;L across assigned markets</li>
<li>Deliver against core OKRs like GOV, revenue, VP and contribution margin as well as the right inputs: selection, quality, and affordability.</li>
<li>Lead weekly and monthly performance reviews</li>
<li>Identify growth opportunities and operational risks and drive action</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Own Marketplace Health</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Act as the accountable leader for marketplace health</li>
<li>Balance supply and demand to maintain a strong customer experience and healthy unit economics</li>
<li>Make system-level trade-offs that may impact multiple functions</li>
<li>Ensure short-term execution supports long-term structural strength</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Drive Cross-Functional Execution</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Align stakeholders across Commercial, Operations, Marketing, Support, Finance, and Product</li>
<li>Set clear priorities and ensure disciplined execution</li>
<li>Influence resource allocation and strategic focus, allocate capital according to priorities for your markets</li>
<li>Remove roadblocks and escalate structural issues when needed</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Build and Scale the Marketplace Playbook</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead geographic expansion and market management initiatives</li>
<li>Strengthen merchant assortment and commercial performance</li>
<li>Drive self-delivery readiness and operational preparedness</li>
<li>Unlock growth through occasion-based strategies and day-part expansion</li>
<li>Codify and scale best practices across markets</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Act as a Culture Carrier and Representative</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Role model Wolt’s values and leadership principles</li>
<li>Lead with ownership, transparency, and accountability</li>
<li>Represent Wolt externally when needed with partners and stakeholders and the media</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Take ownership in existential situations</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>At times, our business faces unpredictable challenges without a clear DRI to begin developing a solution.</li>
<li>In moments like that, take ownership of the situation, either yourself or by appointing a DRI.</li>
<li>Follow up to make sure the situation gets resolved.</li>
</ul>
