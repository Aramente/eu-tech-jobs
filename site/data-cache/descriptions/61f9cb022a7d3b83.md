<p><strong>How You’ll Make an Impact:</strong></p>
<p>The Amplify team builds the internal platforms, automation, and AI-driven systems that power how Klaviyos work every day. As a Senior AI Software Engineer on Amplify, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, build, and own complex internal products and platforms, including internal web applications, APIs, data and event pipelines, and AI-powered workflows used daily by Sales, Marketing, Customer Service &amp; Success, R&amp;D, and Finance teams.</li>
<li>Lead the end-to-end lifecycle of projects and features – from discovery and technical design through implementation, rollout, and long-term ownership.</li>
<li>Partner closely with internal customers to understand their workflows and constraints, and translate real operational challenges into scalable, maintainable solutions that measurably improve productivity, quality, and reliability.</li>
<li>Take ownership of the technical and architectural evolution of parts of the Amplify platform, anticipating scalability, reliability, and observability needs as usage grows.</li>
<li>Define and maintain operational standards for the systems you own, including SLOs, monitoring, incident response, and follow-up on root-cause analysis.</li>
<li>Use modern AI tools as part of your day-to-day engineering workflow (e.g. LLM-based tooling for design, coding, testing, and documentation), and help shape best practices for how the team applies AI responsibly and effectively.</li>
<li>Contribute to shared patterns, tooling, and processes that make it easier for teams across Klaviyo to build high-quality internal tools and AI-powered systems consistently.</li>
<li>Mentor and support other engineers through design reviews, pairing, and constructive feedback, leaving the codebase and systems better than you found them.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with Product, Design, Data, and operational partners to define requirements, trade-offs, and success metrics, ensuring Amplify’s work focuses on the highest-value problems.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who You Are:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You care deeply about building software for the long term, balancing technical quality, delivery pace, and business impact.</li>
<li>You have strong hands-on experience building and operating highly available, full-stack SaaS systems, and are comfortable owning complex problems end to end.</li>
<li>You’ve led the delivery of significant projects or features independently, including discovery, technical design, implementation, rollout, and ongoing maintenance, and you’re comfortable providing technical direction to others.</li>
<li>You have deep expertise in one or more areas of modern web engineering (e.g. backend services, data pipelines, internal platforms, or AI-enabled systems) and a solid understanding of scalable, multi-tenant architectures.</li>
<li>You regularly use AI-powered tools in your development workflow and are curious about applying AI thoughtfully to improve engineering productivity and system capabilities.</li>
<li>You take ownership of operational excellence for the systems you build, including performance, reliability, observability, and on-call participation where required.</li>
<li>You enjoy questioning convention and continuously improving how things work, whether that’s architecture, tooling, workflows, or team practices.</li>
<li>You are a clear communicator and collaborative teammate, comfortable leading technical discussions, writing design docs or RFCs, and keeping stakeholders aligned.</li>
<li>You enjoy working in small, autonomous, agile teams, shipping incrementally, and partnering closely with product managers and other engineers to deliver better outcomes.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Nice to have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience building internal tools, platforms, or automations for developers and/or non-technical stakeholders, with a track record of improving reliability, productivity, or operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Hands-on experience in full-stack environments, including diagnosing and improving application performance, database queries, and complex, multi-service workflows.</li>
<li>Practical experience building or integrating AI-powered systems and agents; e.g., workflows using LLM APIs/SDKs, agentic or automated workflows, RAG pipelines, or evaluation/monitoring frameworks.</li>
<li>Experience working with cloud infrastructure (AWS preferred), infrastructure-as-code (Terraform), and containerised environments (Kubernetes), and designing services that run reliably at scale.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
