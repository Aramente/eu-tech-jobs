<p>We are looking for a Small Business Account Executive to join our team of high performing Account Executives.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Figma is growing and moving fast. Our next team member will have an agile, dynamic mindset, be comfortable embracing change and a true team-player. You won’t be alone! Figma has an incredible team and you’ll work closely with other groups including Support, Design, Engineering, Product Marketing, Customer Success, and Community. You will report directly to our SMB Sales Manager who is also based in London.</p>
<p>This is a full time role with hybrid working from our London office.&nbsp;</p>
<strong>What you'll do at Figma:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Focus 100% on new customer acquisition, this is primarily an outbound driven role</li>
<li>Prioritise your regional EMEA accounts, outbound prospect and build a strong pipeline</li>
<li>Manage a 360 deal cycle and exceed targets</li>
<li>Own sales activity and monthly revenue forecasting in Salesforce</li>
<li>Work to develop and circulate best practices in a collaboration first environment</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>We’d love to hear from you if you have:</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>SDR/BDR experience in a pure outbound function</li>
<li>AE closing experience&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong written prospecting and verbal communication skills</li>
<li>Proven track record of success in your prior roles</li>
<li>Competitive, adaptable, ambitious, collaborative and curious mindset</li>
<li>Experience with Salesforce, Outreach and Sales Navigator</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>While it’s not required, it’s an added plus if you also have:</strong></h4>

<li>Passionate about design</li>
