<p>Pinterest’s Security Privacy team is seeking a Sr. Fullstack Software Engineer to help architect and implement solutions that protect millions of users by building secure systems across backend and client platforms. You’ll focus on designing and delivering privacy-first infrastructure and services that ensure compliance, scalability, and trust, with upcoming opportunities to <strong>collaborate on mobile privacy initiatives that extend these protections to our mobile apps</strong>. The ideal candidate brings strong software development experience, expertise in privacy regulations, and the ability to mentor and lead cross-functional initiatives. While mobile development experience is not required, familiarity with or interest in mobile security will be a strong advantage as we continue to expand our privacy focus across all surfaces.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Design and implement scalable solutions that ensure privacy compliance and protect sensitive user data, including data handling and deletion processes.</li>
<li>Write, review, and maintain code that is well-documented, thoroughly tested, and compliant with security and privacy standards.</li>
<li>Monitor, identify, and address potential privacy vulnerabilities, leading root cause analysis and applying lessons learned to strengthen systems.</li>
<li>Work closely with compliance teams, engineers, and stakeholders to align on goals, define success metrics, and implement best practices for security and privacy.</li>
<li>Implement privacy and security frameworks to Pinterest’s mobile surfaces.</li>
<li>Drive efforts to resolve and prevent privacy-related incidents, ensuring effective remediation and long-term prevention strategies.</li>
<li>Conduct training sessions, mentor team members, and champion a culture of privacy and security across Pinterest.</li>
<li>Thoughtfully leverage AI tools to speed up design, coding, debugging, and documentation, while applying your own critical thinking to validate outputs and explain how you used AI in your workflow.</li>
<li>Shape our AI-assisted engineering practices by sharing patterns, guardrails, and learnings with the team so we can safely increase our impact without compromising code quality, reliability, or candidate expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What we’re looking for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in software engineering, with increasing responsibility.</li>
<li>1+ years of hands-on experience in privacy and security engineering, or equivalent experience tackling privacy-related challenges.</li>
<li>Proficiency in a dynamic programming language (e.g., Python, Java, Kotlin, or Swift), with an ability to write clean, maintainable, and secure code.</li>
<li>2+ years of Fullstack experience</li>
<li>Experience tackling technical challenges, with a focus on learning from and adapting to new and ambiguous environments.</li>
<li>Familiarity with privacy frameworks and ability to integrate privacy requirements into system design and architecture.</li>
<li>Experience with secure data handling, logging, monitoring, and incident response to identify and mitigate potential risks.</li>
<li>Collaborative mindset and leadership skills, with ability to mentor team members, foster cross-functional relationships, and contribute to team goals.</li>
<li>Enthusiasm for tackling complex privacy and security challenges with a strong commitment to creating impactful, user-centric solutions.</li>
<li>Experience with or interest in mobile development or mobile security, especially in privacy-conscious design for Android or iOS platforms.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in a related field such as Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related discipline, or equivalent experience. If you gained experience through different routes (e.g., bootcamps, self-taught programming), you’re welcome to apply.</li>
<li>Experience using AI coding assistants and agentic tools as a force-multiplier, and equally comfortable solving problems from first principles when those tools aren’t available.</li>
</ul>
