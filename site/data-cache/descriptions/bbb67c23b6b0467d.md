<p>At Axon, we build technology that helps protect life. Behind that mission are people who need to learn quickly, grow continuously, and perform at a high level.<br><br>As a Learning &amp; Development Program Manager II, you will design, build, and deliver learning programs that strengthen capability across the business. You will partner with regional and international stakeholders, with a primary focus on Vietnam, to identify needs, create effective learning solutions, and scale programs that improve performance, leadership, and employee development.<br><br>This role is ideal for someone who blends strong instructional design, facilitation, program management, and stakeholder partnership skills — and who knows how to turn learning needs into practical, high-impact solutions.</p>
<h3>What You’ll Do</h3>
<p><strong>Location:</strong> Hybrid in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam<br><strong>Work Location</strong><br>This role is based out of our Ho Chi Minh City office and follows a hybrid schedule: onsite Tuesday through Friday and remote on Mondays, unless an approved accommodation applies. We place a high value on in-person collaboration and building a strong in-office culture.</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead learning programs end to end, from needs analysis through design, delivery, measurement, and continuous improvement</li>
<li>Partner with regional and international stakeholders, with a primary focus on Vietnam, to identify capability gaps and learning priorities</li>
<li>Design and develop learning solutions across multiple formats, including live training, virtual sessions, workshops, and digital learning</li>
<li>Facilitate engaging learning experiences for employees, managers, and leaders</li>
<li>Build scalable learning journeys that support onboarding, role development, leadership capability, and organizational effectiveness</li>
<li>Collaborate with subject-matter experts and cross-functional partners to create relevant, high-quality content</li>
<li>Train and support internal facilitators to deliver learning programs effectively and consistently</li>
<li>Measure program impact using feedback, participation data, assessments, and other relevant metrics</li>
<li>Manage multiple projects and stakeholders in a fast-moving environment with changing priorities</li>
<li>Stay current on L&amp;D trends, tools, and best practices, and apply them thoughtfully</li>
</ul>

<li>Experience managing learning programs from strategy through execution and evaluation</li>
<li>Strong facilitation skills and confidence leading both in-person and virtual learning experiences</li>
<li>Experience designing content across multiple modalities, including instructor-led, virtual, and self-paced learning</li>
<li>Strong project management and stakeholder management skills</li>
<li>Excellent written, verbal, and presentation skills</li>

</ul>
