<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Associate Director (AD) Operations &amp; Inventory Retail Media: Strategic lead for the global product lifecycle and operational engine, driving scalability, inventory excellence, and flawless execution across all HelloFresh markets.</strong></p>
<p>As the <strong>Associate Director of Retail Media Operations &amp; Inventory</strong>, you will be a cornerstone of the <strong>Global Retail Media team</strong> based at our Amsterdam office. Reporting directly to the Senior Director of Global Retail Media, you will hold a critical global mandate to professionalise and scale our Retail Media Network (RMN) across all markets where HelloFresh operates.</p>
<p>Retail Media at HelloFresh is uniquely complex. Our campaigns directly affect the physical customer experience and our core food product. Your focus is owning the end-to-end product roadmap for both physical and digital assets, moving from strategic development to flawless execution. You will provide operational oversight by streamlining post-contract execution to drive maximum Distribution Centre (DC) efficiency, utilisation rates, and client retention. You will lead the transition of our operational capabilities from single-campaign to omnichannel multi-campaign delivery by unifying product strategy and operational rigour.</p>
<p>Above all, we are looking for people who will make HelloFresh better. We believe there are many different ways of developing skills, and we love diverse experiences! So even if you don’t “tick all the boxes” but think you’d thrive in this role, we would really like to learn more about you.</p>
<h2><strong>You will…</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead Global Product Strategy: Define and manage the end-to-end product roadmap for physical assets (sampling, inserts, recipe partnerships) and digital propositions (onsite/offsite), ensuring a unified global strategy.</li>
<li>Drive Inventory Excellence: Own the global inventory utilisation strategy. Implement yield frameworks to maximise the fill rate and commercial value of every box and digital impression.</li>
<li>Optimise Campaign Operations: Oversee post-contract execution, ensuring flawless campaign delivery, A/B-testing of physical and digital assets, Post Campaign Reporting, and adherence to strict Service Level Agreements (SLAs).</li>
<li>Enhance Distribution Efficiency: Partner with Distribution Centres (DC’s) and Data teams to improve multi-campaign capabilities and operational efficiency on the ground.</li>
<li>Enable Digital Scaling: Act as the operational bridge for our digital revenue plan, overseeing the monetisation enablement of assets owned by other internal teams as well as development of new onsite placements.</li>
<li>Bridge KPI’s: Lead impact analysis of our Retail Media offering on new and existing customers, and match this with the objectives of our organisation and Marketing department.</li>
<li>Standardise Global Governance: Lead and mentor local operations specialists across markets, ensuring global alignment and consistent execution standards.</li>
</ul>

<li>A Process Architect: You enjoy building systems from scratch and have a proven ability to standardise workflows across multicultural, matrixed teams.</li>
<li>Results-Oriented: Highly focused on key performance indicators, including campaign fulfilment rates, inventory utilisation, and AEBIT contribution.</li>

</ul>
