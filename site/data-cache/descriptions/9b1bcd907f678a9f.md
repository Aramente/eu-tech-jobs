### About the Job:-

You will play a critical role in ensuring the accuracy and reliability of our financial and transaction data. You’ll work at the intersection of **payments, finance, and operations**, reconciling high-volume transaction data across internal systems, payment gateways, and banking partners.

This role requires someone who is **detail-oriented, analytical, and proactive,** someone who enjoys digging into data, identifying discrepancies, and solving operational problems at scale.

## On the Job

• Perform daily transaction reconciliation between internal systems, payment gateways, and banking partners.

• Identify and investigate transaction mismatches, settlement discrepancies, and missing transactions.

• Work closely with payments, finance, and risk teams to resolve reconciliation breaks.

• Monitor settlement reports, refund flows, and chargeback records to ensure accuracy.

• Maintain clear documentation of recon processes, exceptions, and resolutions.

• Build and maintain recon reports and operational dashboards for transaction monitoring.

• Proactively identify process gaps, recurring discrepancies, or operational inefficiencies.

• Assist in improving reconciliation workflows and automation opportunities.

## Must Haves

• 1–3 years of experience in reconciliation, settlements, finance operations, or payment operations.

• Strong understanding of transaction reconciliation processes (bank, wallet, gateway, merchant).

• Good knowledge of Excel/Google Sheets (pivot tables, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, data reconciliation).

• Experience investigating transaction mismatches, settlement gaps, and financial discrepancies.

• Strong attention to detail with ability to work with large transactional datasets.

• Ability to work with cross-functional teams (finance, risk, payments, engineering).

• Strong documentation and reporting skills.

## Good to Have

- Experience in fintech, payments companies, or consumer internet startups.
- Familiarity with payment gateways, UPI flows, settlements, chargebacks, or refunds.
- Basic SQL knowledge for data extraction and reconciliation checks.
- Experience working with payment platforms, banking partners, or reconciliation tools.
- Exposure to fraud monitoring or transaction investigations.
- Exposure to UPI ecosystem and NPCI processes; candidates with prior experience working with NPCI or handling  UPI transactions will be strongly preferred.
