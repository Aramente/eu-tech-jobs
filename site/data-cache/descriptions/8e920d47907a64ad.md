## Your (space) mission

You turn space ambitions into reliable execution. Both on Earth and beyond.<br><br>As the Project Manager at cosine, you manage the projects that bring cutting-edge space instruments from design to launch. Working in the heart of our Remote Sensing business, you ensure that complex space missions are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality expected in the space industry. The project budgets you manage typically range from €5 million to €20 million.<br><br>You are the stabilizing force in a highly innovative environment. While engineers and scientists push the boundaries of optical and sensing technology for satellites, you keep the program grounded in reality: clear planning, controlled costs, aligned stakeholders, and predictable delivery. Your work directly contributes to instruments that operate in orbit and generate insights about our planet and universe.

## Tasks & responsibilities

In this role, your responsibilities will include:<br><ul><li>Own end-to-end project execution for space instrument development programs</li><li>Drive integrated planning across schedule, budget, and resources in multidisciplinary teams</li><li>Align and coordinate stakeholders, including institutional customers, partners, and suppliers</li><li>Track progress through all project phases, from design to AIT and launch preparation</li><li>Proactively identify risks and ensure structured mitigation in line with space project standards</li></ul>

## Your profile

We are looking for candidates with the following qualifications:<ul><li>Several years of experience as a Project Manager in space, aerospace, or other high-tech engineering environments</li><li>Proven ability to manage complex projects with strict requirements on time, cost, and quality</li><li>Familiarity with space project lifecycles (system engineering approach and mindset)</li><li>Strong stakeholder management skills in an international, technical environment</li><li>Structured, calm, and decisive under pressure, with a strong sense of ownership</li><li>Experience with institutional or governmental customers is a strong advantage</li><li>Excellent English communication skills; Dutch is a plus</li></ul>
