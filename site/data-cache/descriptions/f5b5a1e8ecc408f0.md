<h2 id="ABOUT-FANDUEL" data-local-id="f254491d5d14" data-renderer-start-pos="291"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">ABOUT FANDUEL</strong></h2>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="306" data-local-id="d514580d-06b6-4673-9123-1d69a6c9b003">FanDuel Group is the premier mobile gaming company in the United States and Canada. FanDuel Group consists of a portfolio of leading brands across mobile wagering including: America’s #1 Sportsbook, FanDuel Sportsbook; its leading iGaming platform, FanDuel Casino; the industry’s unquestioned leader in horse racing and advance-deposit wagering, FanDuel Racing; and its daily fantasy sports product. &nbsp;</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="709" data-local-id="264056429bce">In addition, FanDuel Group operates FanDuel TV, its broadly distributed linear cable television network and FanDuel TV+, its leading direct-to-consumer&nbsp;<span data-highlighted="true" data-vc="highlighted-text"><span class="\_kqswh2mm"><span class="\_5pioz8co \_189e1dm9 \_1il9buyh \_19lc184f \_d0altlke" data-testid="definition-highlighter">OTT</span></span></span>&nbsp;platform. FanDuel Group has a presence across all 50 states, Canada, and Puerto Rico.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="952" data-local-id="1da838ec6d32">The company is based in New York with US offices in Los Angeles, Atlanta, and Jersey City, as well as global offices in Canada and Scotland. The company’s affiliates have offices worldwide, including in Ireland, Portugal, Romania, and Australia.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1199" data-local-id="e91a5589176d">FanDuel Group is a subsidiary of Flutter Entertainment, the world's largest sports betting and gaming operator with a portfolio of globally recognized brands and traded on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE:&nbsp;<span data-highlighted="true" data-vc="highlighted-text"><span class="\_kqswh2mm"><span class="\_5pioz8co \_189e1dm9 \_1il9buyh \_19lc184f \_d0altlke" data-testid="definition-highlighter">FLUT</span></span></span>).</p>
<h3 id="THE-ROSTER…" data-local-id="dc068915-c2d7-412d-8455-0b1804290662" data-renderer-start-pos="1414"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">THE ROSTER</strong></h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1427" data-local-id="fdb6ea96-3144-4801-b7af-8534d6f282d7">At FanDuel, we give fans a new and innovative way to interact with their favorite games, sports and teams. We’re dedicated to building a winning team and we pride ourselves on being able to make every moment mean more, especially when it comes to your career. So, what does “winning” look like at FanDuel? It’s recognition for your hard-earned results, a culture that brings out your best work—and a roster full of talented coworkers. Make no mistake, we are here to win, but we believe in winning right. That means we’ll never compromise when it comes to looking out for our teammates. From creatives professionals to cutting edge technology innovators, FanDuel offers a wide range of career opportunities, best in class benefits, and the tools to explore and grow into your best selves. At FanDuel, our principle of “We Are One Team” runs through all our offices across the globe, and you can expect to be a part of an exciting company with many opportunities to grow and be successful.</p>

<h3 id="THE-POSITION-Our-roster-has-an-opening-with-your-name-on-it" data-local-id="479dfa10-f444-4d3b-bd74-bfd8045195a3" data-renderer-start-pos="2420"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">THE POSITION</strong><br>Our roster has an opening with your name on it</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2481" data-local-id="834bf073-44c2-4df1-9488-333aa06ee07e">We are looking for a Senior Platform Engineer to join our Cloud Fabric team in our Platform Engineering organization. The Cloud Fabric team builds platform-level tooling, used company-wide to support large-scale &amp; complex application microservice architectures across several dozen target deployment locations under unique geographic and regulatory constraints. To support our application engineers, this team defines our microservice communication architecture via API gateways and supports our service discovery &amp; service mesh implementations.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="3028" data-local-id="1976b22b-8734-423a-aca4-48d297ef9584">We are looking for passionate &amp; experienced technologists, strong communicators, and those with a commitment to continuous improvement. The ideal candidate will have the confidence to propose pragmatic solutions to complex problems and be eager to both learn from and educate others. In this role you will work amongst multidisciplinary teams, tackling complex problems to create world class infrastructure as we continuously build out our exciting and innovative developer experience.</p>
