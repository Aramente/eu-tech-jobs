<h1><strong>TeleSales team lead – DACH&nbsp;</strong></h1>
<p>📍 Berlin – On-site</p>
<h2><strong>About the team</strong></h2>
<p>Our DACH TeleSales team is at the heart of SumUp's growth — connecting small business owners across Germany, Austria, and Switzerland with the tools that help them thrive. As Team Lead, you won't just be managing from a distance; you'll be working side by side with your team, coaching calls, supporting deals, and setting the standard for what great sales looks like in this market. This is a role for someone who leads by doing — someone who brings energy, accountability, and genuine care for the people they work with.</p>
<p>🎥 Take a look inside our<a href="https://youtu.be/ZF8A9onHQCE?si=c-GZnVUkrOATN2Jg"> Berlin office</a> and see where you'll be based.</p>
<h2><strong>What you'll do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead and coach a team of Inbound Sales Representatives — running regular 1:1s, call shadowing sessions, and performance check-ins to help your team hit and exceed their targets</li>
<li>Stay close to the work — stepping in to support live calls, helping the team close deals, and modelling the behaviours you want to see</li>
<li>Track team performance data and turn it into clear, actionable insights that improve results week on week</li>
<li>Build a team culture where feedback is normal, learning is encouraged, and wins are celebrated</li>
<li>Work closely with other sales leaders to refine processes, share what's working, and contribute to the wider DACH sales strategy</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>You'll be great for this role if…</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Experience leading a sales team for three or more years, with a track record of developing people and hitting targets.</li>
<li>Proven ability to stay close to the work — comfortable jumping on calls, supporting deals directly, and leading by example.</li>
<li>Fluency in German (C1 or above) and strong business-level English.</li>
<li>Strong coaching instincts — able to give honest, constructive feedback while keeping team morale high.</li>
<li>Comfort working with performance data to identify patterns, spot opportunities, and adjust quickly.</li>
</ul>
