<p>Databricks is seeking a&nbsp;Lakebase Sales Specialist Account Executive&nbsp;to help customers modernize their operational data foundation with&nbsp;Databricks Lakebase, our&nbsp;fully-managed Postgres&nbsp;offering for&nbsp;intelligent applications. This high-impact role sits within the Lakebase Go-To-Market team and partners closely with regional Account Executives to drive adoption of Lakebase with platform, application, and data teams.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1800">Lakebase gives customers a unified, governed foundation for&nbsp;operational workloads&nbsp;and&nbsp;AI-native applications, helping them move away from a fragmented estate of point databases toward a modern, scalable, serverless Postgres service. If you want to be at the forefront of operational databases for AI and intelligent applications at one of the fastest-growing data and AI companies in the world, this is your opportunity.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2006"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">The impact you will have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive new Lakebase revenue&nbsp;by identifying, qualifying, and closing Lakebase opportunities within a defined territory, in partnership with regional Account Executives and the broader account team.</li>
<li>Lead with outcomes&nbsp;for key Lakebase personas — including&nbsp;platform teams and developers,&nbsp;data teams, and&nbsp;central IT&nbsp;— articulating how Lakebase helps them ship features faster, simplify operational data architectures, and improve governance and cost efficiency.</li>
<li>Sell the value of fully-managed Postgres for intelligent applications, positioning Lakebase as the optimal choice for operational workloads that power real-time, AI-driven experiences.</li>
<li>Run complex, multi-threaded sales cycles&nbsp;from discovery and value hypothesis through commercial negotiation and close, navigating executive, technical, and line-of-business stakeholders.</li>
<li>Orchestrate proof-of-value and POCs&nbsp;that validate Lakebase’s benefits for OLTP-style workloads, reverse ETL, and AI/ML-driven applications, in partnership with solution architects and specialists.</li>
<li>Compete and win&nbsp;against legacy and cloud-native operational databases by leveraging our compete assets, benchmarks, and customer references.</li>
<li>Align to measurable business outcomes&nbsp;such as performance, developer productivity, time-to-market for new features, cost reduction, and simplification of the operational data landscape.</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally&nbsp;with Product Management, Marketing, Customer Success, and Partner teams to shape territory plans, launch plays, and co-selling motions with key ISVs and GSIs.</li>
<li>Enable the field by sharing Lakebase best practices, success stories, and sales motions with broader sales teams, helping scale Lakebase proficiency across the organisation.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong data-renderer-mark="true">What we look for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Extensive enterprise SaaS sales experience, consistently exceeding quota in complex, multi-stakeholder deals.</li>
<li>Proven success selling&nbsp;data platforms, operational databases (e.g., Postgres, MySQL, cloud-native DBaaS), or adjacent data/AI infrastructure&nbsp;to technical buyers and business leaders.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of&nbsp;modern data and application architectures, including cloud-native services, microservices, event-driven systems, and how operational data underpins AI and analytics strategies.</li>
<li>Ability to sell to both&nbsp;technical stakeholders&nbsp;(developers, architects, data engineers) and&nbsp;business stakeholders&nbsp;(product leaders, operations, line-of-business owners).</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience leading&nbsp;specialist or overlay motions, working jointly with core Account Executives to create and progress opportunities.</li>
<li>Executive presence with the ability to&nbsp;whiteboard architectures, lead C-level conversations, and build trust with senior decision makers.</li>
<li>Strong&nbsp;value selling&nbsp;skills: adept at discovering pain, building a business case, and tying technical capabilities to clear, quantified outcomes.</li>
<li>Excellent&nbsp;communication, storytelling, and negotiation&nbsp;skills, with comfort presenting to both large and small audiences.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent practical experience.</li>
