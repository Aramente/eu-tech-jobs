<p>Make Wayve the experience that defines your career!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p></div><h2><strong>The Role&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p>As a <strong>ML Engineer</strong> within the Application Engineering team, you’ll lead critical initiatives that push the frontier of model-based autonomous driving—both in terms of core driving performance and feature-level intelligence such as personalization, comfort, and collaboration.</p>
<p>You’ll design and deliver ML-driven behaviours that scale from assisted to autonomous driving. Your work will span across model architecture, data pipelines, evaluation frameworks, and real-world deployment. You’ll collaborate deeply with AI Platform, Simulation, Robot SW and Model Release teams to build systems that are performant, adaptable, and ready for production.</p>
<h2>What You’ll Be Working On</h2>
<ul>
<li>Develop and improve end-to-end driving models with state-of-the-art performance, robustness, and generalization.</li>
<li>Lead projects on personalized and collaborative driving, including behavior conditioning, comfort tuning, and user alignment.</li>
<li>Build evaluation pipelines and metrics for both closed-loop and open-loop driving performance and product readiness.</li>
<li>Curate and mine real-world and synthetic data to drive scenario diversity, coverage, and feature-specific development.</li>
<li>Influence architecture choices, training methodologies, and deployment pathways for production-scale learning systems.</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally across various teams to ensure integration and iteration velocity.</li>
<li>Mentor senior engineers and shape the long-term technical direction across Autonomy.<strong><br><br></strong></li>
</ul>
<h2>About you&nbsp;&nbsp;</h2>
<h3>Essential&nbsp;</h3>
<ul>
<li>Extensive and proven track record of shipping deep learning systems to production.</li>
<li>Expert in deep learning (esp. sequential models, control, planning, or perception).</li>
<li>Proficient in Python and other relevant languages (e.g. C++ and CUDA) and ML frameworks (esp. PyTorch), with a solid foundation in software engineering practices.</li>
<li>Experience with real-time systems or robotics, ideally with simulation- or vehicle-in-the-loop components.</li>
<li>Ability to lead technical initiatives across teams, drive alignment, and mentor engineers.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Desirable&nbsp;</h3>
<ul>
<li>Prior work in autonomous driving, imitation learning, or trajectory prediction.</li>
<li>Familiarity with personalization, human behavior modelling, or driver intent inference.</li>
<li>Experience integrating ML systems into production hardware or multi-agent simulation.<br><br></li>
</ul>
