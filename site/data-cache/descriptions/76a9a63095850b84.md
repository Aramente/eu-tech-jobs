<h2><strong>The Role</strong></h2>
<p>We are seeking a <strong>Staff Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)</strong> to lead the evolution of our large-scale production systems. This role is designed for a technical expert who thrives on solving complex problems at scale and lives by the ethic: <em>"If you have to do it twice, automate it."</em> Based in the <strong>Washington, D.C. area</strong>, you will ensure our infrastructure maintains uncompromising reliability and performance while supporting critical national security missions in secure, restricted environments.</p>
<p><strong>Security Requirement:</strong> Must be able to obtain and maintain a U.S. security clearance (Secret or Top Secret) to the extent required by U.S. Government contracts.</p>
<p>The selected candidate may be subject to drug testing to the extent required by U.S. Government contracts.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Infrastructure Leadership:</strong> Design, build, and oversee Okta’s production infrastructure, ensuring architectural integrity and peak performance.</li>
<li><strong>Incident Engineering:</strong> Act as a senior escalation point for production incidents, conducting deep-dive root cause analysis and implementing permanent, automated preventive solutions.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Automation:</strong> Eliminate manual toil by developing sophisticated automation frameworks, evolving monitoring tools, and establishing rigorous technical documentation.</li>
<li><strong>System Resilience:</strong> Optimize a highly available, large-scale environment, ensuring "Always On" service delivery across complex network topologies.</li>
<li><strong>Mentorship:</strong> Provide technical guidance to the engineering organization, championing SRE best practices and a culture of self-education.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Bring</strong></h2>
<h3>Core Requirements</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Clearance: </strong>Active <strong>TS/SCI with Polygraph.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Compliance Expertise:</strong> Deep professional experience with <strong>FedRAMP</strong> and <strong>DoD IL6</strong> frameworks.</li>
<li><strong>Education:</strong> B.S. in Computer Science or equivalent technical experience.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Technical Expertise</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Networking &amp; Cloud Architecture:</strong> Mastery of <strong>AWS</strong> networking and security, including <strong>Transit Gateways, VPCs, Route Tables, ELBs,</strong> and <strong>NACLS</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>Infrastructure as Code (IaC):</strong> Advanced experience automating enterprise-scale infrastructure via <strong>Terraform</strong> or CloudFormation.</li>
<li><strong>Systems &amp; Scripting:</strong> Expert-level Linux systems administration with proficiency in <strong>Go, Python, Bash,</strong> or <strong>Ruby</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>Production Support:</strong> Proven success managing <strong>Docker</strong> containers and Java-based stacks (<strong>Apache/Tomcat</strong>) in high-security production environments.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Protocol Knowledge:</strong> Solid understanding of networking concepts, IP protocols, and <strong>multi-cloud</strong> infrastructure.</p>
