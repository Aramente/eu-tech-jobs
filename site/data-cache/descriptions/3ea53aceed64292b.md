Sitting at the heart of the consumer and marketplace business, this role is responsible for defining how Wolt drives sustainable, profitable growth through pricing levers.</p>
<p>This is a senior, high-impact leadership role with end-to-end accountability for strategy, execution, and financial outcomes. Director, Customer Pricing will own a significant portion of the consumer P&amp;L, including Cx fee revenue and investment, and will work directly with C-level leadership and senior functional owners to set direction, make trade-offs, and deliver results. The role also includes direct leadership of a small, high-performing team.</p>
<p>Combining strategic rigour, financial judgement, and operational leadership, this individual will act as the central decision-maker across Product, Marketing, Analytics, Finance, and regional teams.</p>
<p>This person reports to the Global Head of Marketplace Growth and Consumer.&nbsp;</p>
<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p><strong> Pricing Strategy</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own and define Wolt’s global pricing strategy, setting the overarching logic for how the company monetises demand and deploys across all consumer-facing levers.</li>
<li>Establish the long-term strategic direction for pricing as a core driver of growth, profitability, and competitive positioning, shaping how the marketplace evolves over time.</li>
<li>Determine the critical strategic priorities across markets and use cases, focusing leadership attention and resources on the initiatives that most materially impact enterprise value.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Product, AI and Tech Driven Growth</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with the Product and Engineering teams to develop our Consumer Pricing tech stack to drive step-change growth.</li>
<li>Leverage AI tools in the work of the organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong> Growth Economics &amp; Capital Allocation</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the economic model underpinning Wolt’s consumer business, with full accountability for how pricing drives revenue, growth, and contribution margin globally.</li>
<li>Set the principles and guardrails for capital allocation, determining where and how investment drives the highest long-term enterprise value.</li>
<li>Arbitrate trade-offs between growth, profitability, and customer experience at a global level, influencing resource allocation across markets, customer segments, and strategic priorities.</li>
<li>Partner with senior leadership to shape financial targets and ensure pricing strategy is a primary driver of Wolt’s overall business performance.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong> Operational Excellence</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish robust frameworks for experimentation, performance tracking, and decision-making across markets.</li>
<li>Define how pricing and performance is measured, monitored, and optimised globally.</li>
<li>Ensure strategies are translated into execution with clear ownership, pace, and measurable outcomes.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong> Cross-Functional Leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the senior point of ownership for pricing across the organisation.</li>
<li>Work directly with C-level stakeholders, Country Leaders, and functional leaders to align on priorities, investments, and execution.</li>
<li>Navigate complex trade-offs across Product, Marketing, Finance, and regional teams, ensuring decisions are grounded in data and enterprise-level impact.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Team Leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and develop a team of three, setting clear priorities, raising the performance bar, and ensuring strong delivery against business objectives.</li>
<li>Foster a culture of ownership, analytical rigour, and impact, while coaching team members to operate effectively in a high-autonomy, cross-functional environment.</li>
</ul>
