<p>We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Accountant to join our finance team in a hybrid working environment, with two set in-office days per week. The accountant will assist with day-to-day accounting tasks, ensure accurate financial records and support month-end and year-end closing processes. &nbsp;This role is ideal for a newly qualified accountant, or someone working for their qualifications.</p>
<p>Dataiku is part of a US incorporated group and this role would provide exposure to USGAAP and other European accounting standards, along with experience of entities in various EMEA locations.</p>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Assist with daily accounting operations, including data entry and account reconciliations</li>
<li>Prepare and post journal entries under supervision</li>
<li>Reconcile bank statements and general ledger accounts</li>
<li>Support month-end and year-end financial close activities</li>
<li>Support preparation of local GAAP financial statements</li>
<li>Help ensure compliance with accounting standards (both US GAAP and local GAAP), company policies and regulatory requirements</li>
<li>Assist with audits by providing documentation and explanations as needed</li>
<li>Maintain organised and up-to-date financial records</li>
<li>Other Ad Hoc tasks as required</li>
</ul>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<p><strong>Hybrid Work Expectations</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Work <strong>2 days per week in the office</strong> and <strong>3 days remotely</strong></li>
<li>Attend in-person training and key meetings on office days</li>
<li>Maintain a suitable home workspace with reliable internet access</li>
<li>Communicate clearly and consistently with the finance team</li>
<li>Use company-approved tools for collaboration and document management</li>
</ul>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<p><strong>Skills &amp; Qualifications</strong></p>
<p><strong>Required:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Understanding of accounting principals and financial reporting</li>
<li>ACCA, ACA or CIMA newly qualified or final year of training</li>
<li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and accuracy</li>
<li>Good organisation and time-management skills</li>
<li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li>
