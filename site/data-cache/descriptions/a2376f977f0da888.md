<p>Make Wayve the experience that defines your career!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p></div><h2><strong>The role&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p>The Global Logistics Manager is a critical leadership role responsible for defining, executing, and optimizing Wayve’s global logistics, customs, transportation, and potentially warehousing operations across the UK, US, Asia Pacific, and EU.</p>
<p>This role owns end-to-end inbound and outbound logistics, customs compliance, tariffs and duties, transportation strategy, and logistics partner management. The ideal candidate is a strategic, hands-on leader who can operate in a fast-moving, hardware-driven environment, balancing speed, cost, compliance, and risk while enabling critical program milestones.</p>
<p>This role will have help setting up the logistics roadmap as Wayve continue to scale up internationally</p>
<p>Key responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute a global logistics strategy aligned with company objectives, hardware programs, and regional growth plans</li>
<li>Own relationships with global and regional freight forwarders, carriers, and customs brokers and lead rate negotiations, contract renewals, and capacity planning across regions</li>
<li>Own and ensure full compliance with domestic and international trade regulations, customs rules, tariff/duty, and safety standards</li>
<li>Own and implement logistics-related processes and tools and ensure they are documented, standardized, and scalable</li>
<li>Ensure seamless integration between logistics systems and purchasing, warehouse, finance, and other stakeholders workflows</li>
</ul>
