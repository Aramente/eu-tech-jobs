<p><strong>Senior HR Business Partner - Operations &amp; Finance</strong></p>
<p>Adyen is looking for a strategic and hands-on Senior HR Business Partner to support our Operations &amp; Finance organizations in Amsterdam and globally. <br>In this position, you will act as a trusted advisor to the CFO and Finance leadership while also supporting the global strategy of one of Adyen’s Operations domains. You will work end-to-end across the employee lifecycle, co-creating and driving people strategies that enable business growth, strong leadership, and an exceptional employee experience.</p>
<p>This role is based in Amsterdam and works in close collaboration with regional HR Business Partners to ensure global consistency with strong local execution.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Co-create and execute the global people strategy for Finance and a key Operations domain, in close partnership with senior leaders and regional HRBPs.</li>
<li>Act as a trusted advisor to the CFO and Finance leadership, supporting organizational design, leadership effectiveness, and talent planning.</li>
<li>Partner with Operations leaders on strategic workforce planning, team growth, and leadership capability in a global, high-growth environment.</li>
<li>Provide strategic HR guidance and hands-on coaching to leaders on performance management, feedback, employee relations, and engagement.</li>
<li>Lead performance and development cycles, enabling high-quality assessments, growth conversations, and succession planning.</li>
<li>Collaborate with HR Centers of Expertise to design and implement people initiatives across the full employee lifecycle.</li>
<li>Use people data and insights to identify trends, anticipate challenges, and drive data-informed improvements.</li>
<li>Champion a culture of feedback, inclusion, and high performance, ensuring a strong employee experience at scale.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Who you are:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Min 6+ years of HRBP experience in a fast-paced, global, matrixed organization, partnering with all levels of leadership.</li>
<li>Deep expertise in HR practices, including performance management and employee relations.</li>
<li>Trusted advisor who builds strong relationships and thrives in an evolving, unstructured environment.</li>
<li>A "builder" and change agent with a continuous improvement mindset, comfortable challenging the status quo to drive efficiency and cultural alignment.</li>
<li>Adept at organizational dynamics, fostering healthy, motivated teams.</li>
<li>Hands-on yet collaborative, always seeking simpler, more automated solutions.</li>
<li>Exceptional problem-solver and communicator, able to develop and articulate fact-based solutions.</li>
<li>Skilled in project managing HR initiatives.</li>
</ul>
