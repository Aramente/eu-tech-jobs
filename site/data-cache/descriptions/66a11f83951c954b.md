<h2><strong>Team description</strong></h2>
<p>SumUp's People Operations team keeps our people experience running smoothly across Europe — and payroll sits right at the heart of that. As we grow rapidly across Southern Europe, we're looking for a Payroll Partner to join us on a one-year fixed-term contract, taking full ownership of the payroll cycle for Spain and Italy. This is a solid next step for someone with a few years of payroll experience who's ready to go beyond the basics — driving real process improvements, working across two markets, and contributing to a People team that's building smarter, more efficient ways of working.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>What you'll do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Own the end-to-end payroll cycle for Spain and Italy, ensuring accuracy and compliance with local laws and regulations</li>
<li>Spot and implement opportunities for automation and process improvement, using tools like Workday and Payslip to reduce manual effort and sharpen payroll quality</li>
<li>Handle employee payroll queries through our ticketing system, resolving issues with care and efficiency</li>
<li>Collaborate with People Operations, Compensation and Benefits, and Accounting to keep payroll data accurate and up to date</li>
<li>Support the regional payroll lead in managing relationships with external providers, including health insurers and pension providers</li>
<li>Review and update country-level payroll policies and procedures as the business evolves</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>What we’re looking for</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on payroll experience covering the full cycle, with familiarity processing payroll in Spain or Italy.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of Spanish and/or Italian labour and tax law, with the confidence to apply it independently.</li>
<li>Strong Excel skills, including pivot tables and VLOOKUPs, and a natural comfort with data accuracy and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Experience working cross-functionally and communicating clearly with different teams and stakeholders.</li>
<li>A proactive approach to process improvement, with an interest in automation tools and finding smarter ways to work.</li>
<li>Fluency in English plus Spanish or Italian.</li>
</ul>
