<p><strong>About this team and role:</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Firefox Data Science team partners with Product and Engineering to grow usage of our flagship product: </span><strong>Firefox</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, the privacy-minded web browser. Our work informs prioritization, investment decisions, and the long-term growth strategy across Firefox.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Senior Data Scientist will lead analytical support for a feature area to drive measurable user growth by framing ambiguous questions, designing measurement strategies, and delivering insights that shape product decisions. They will also contribute to the team's forecasting and growth modeling efforts, helping translate statistical models into actionable growth strategies. This role emphasizes judgment, communication, and influence, supported by strong technical execution.</span></p>
