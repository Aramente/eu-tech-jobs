<p><strong>THE WORK:</strong></p>
<p>Ripple Treasury (formerly GTreasury) is the leading innovator of integrated SaaS treasury and risk management solutions for the digital treasurer. Developed with the latest technology, Ripple Treasury empowers organizations on their path to strategic treasury. It enables total access to cash, liquidity, payments, and financial risk management.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We’re hiring a Client Support Analyst to join our growing global team. You’ll be the front line for clients using our treasury management platform—the person they rely on to solve problems, answer questions, and keep things moving.</p>
<p>You’ll work closely with teams across the business to deliver fast, thoughtful solutions and make sure clients feel supported every step of the way. Along the way, you’ll get hands-on exposure to how leading global organizations manage treasury and financial risk.</p>
<p><strong>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Be the front line for our clients and a trusted partner across the business—you’re who people count on when things matter most</li>
<li>Own issues end-to-end: troubleshoot, communicate clearly, and drive fast, high-quality resolutions that build trust and long-term relationships</li>
<li>Manage and prioritize incoming requests to hit SLAs without sacrificing quality (and know when to flex when things get busy)</li>
<li>Get hands-on with the systems behind the scenes—dig into payment formats, extracts, connectivity (inbound/outbound), and Alliance Lite 2 to diagnose and resolve issues</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Customer Success, Account Managers, Solutions, and Engineering to solve complex problems and keep clients moving forward</li>
<li>Escalate when needed with clear, well-documented context that helps Development move quickly</li>
<li>Spot patterns in client issues and turn them into actionable insights to improve our product, processes, and overall client experience</li>
<li>Continuously look for ways to make support faster, smarter, and more scalable as we grow</li>
</ul>
