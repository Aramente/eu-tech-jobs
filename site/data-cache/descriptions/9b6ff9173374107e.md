<p><strong>The mission:</strong></p>
<p>At Baubap, our Customer Success team raises technical support tickets that surface real issues impacting both our customers and the product. We’re looking for a Support Engineer to join our engineering team and independently own the investigation and resolution of these issues, with a strong focus on identifying and fixing root causes.</p>
<p>This role is designed for someone autonomous and execution-driven, who can move quickly from diagnosis to solution. You’ll work on a continuous stream of real operational problems, using support tickets as a direct signal to improve product quality, stability, and scalability. By resolving bugs and issues from the root and implementing backend fixes when needed. This role directly contributes to the long-term growth and reliability of the product.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The expected outcome:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Recurring product issues identified through Customer Success tickets are resolved at the root cause, resulting in a sustained reduction of repeat incidents.</li>
<li>Support tickets are consistently converted into concrete product and system improvements.</li>
<li>Bugs and issues move efficiently from ticket to resolution, with minimal need for oversight or coaching.</li>
<li>The product becomes progressively more stable and scalable, requiring less reactive support over time.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The day to day tasks:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Investigate and resolve technical issues raised by the Customer Success team through support tickets and product bugs.</li>
<li>Take ownership of fixing bugs and issues from the root cause, not just applying temporary workarounds.</li>
<li>Analyze patterns in support tickets to identify recurring problems and systemic weaknesses.</li>
<li>Design and implement backend solutions as needed to permanently resolve issues.</li>
<li>Work with APIs, workflows, and internal systems to diagnose failures and improve reliability.</li>
<li>Actively manage and coordinate stakeholders across Product, Customer Customer Success, Fincore, and Infrastructure to drive issues to resolution, align priorities, and ensure follow-through.</li>
<li>Improve internal tooling, workflows, or integrations that support Customer Success operations.</li>
<li>Document root causes, fixes, and learnings to reduce regressions and strengthen shared technical knowledge.</li>
</ul>
