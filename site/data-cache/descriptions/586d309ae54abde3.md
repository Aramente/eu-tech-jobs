<p>Make Wayve the experience that defines your career!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p></div><h2><strong>The role&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p>Wayve is building the AI Driver to power autonomous mobility at global scale. As we deploy robotaxi services with leading partners, we are hiring a Strategic Partnerships Manager to help scale our commercial ecosystem in London and Tokyo. This is a high-impact role at the intersection of autonomy, mobility platforms, and automotive OEMs — focused on enabling safe, scalable L4 deployment.</p>
<p>You will lead day-to-day commercial execution of Wayve’s robotaxi partnerships in your market, working closely with ride-hailing platforms, OEM partners and cross-functional internal teams to deliver successful pilot launches and fleet scale-up.</p>
<p>This role combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution across product readiness, operational launch, and commercial scaling.</p>
<p>Key responsibilities:</p>
<ul>
<li>Drive commercial execution of robotaxi deployments in London or Tokyo.</li>
<li>Serve as the primary partnership manager to interface with ride-hailing and broader ecosystem partners.</li>
<li>Drive launch readiness across technical integration, fleet ramp, and operational milestones.</li>
<li>Track performance KPIs and proactively manage commercial and operational risks.</li>
<li>Coordinate cross-functionally across Engineering, Product, Safety, Operations, Policy, and Marketing to ensure successful deployments.</li>
<li>Support market expansion planning and long-term scaling to production vehicles.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>About you</strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</h2>
<p>In order to set you up for success as a Strategic Partnership Manager at Wayve, we’re looking for the following skills and experience.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Essential&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Strong ownership mindset with a proven track record of driving initiatives from strategy through execution with measurable commercial or operational impact.</li>
<li>Commercially rigorous and action-oriented, with experience managing complex external partnerships with accountability for outcomes.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to operate in fast-moving, ambiguous environments, bringing structure, clarity, and momentum to evolving situations.</li>
<li>Credible cross-functional leader, able to align engineering, product, operations, safety, and policy teams around shared objectives.</li>
<li>Experienced in managing performance, risk, and competing priorities, using data and structured thinking to proactively resolve issues.</li>
<li>Balances long-term scaling vision with near-term delivery discipline, particularly in complex or regulated environments.</li>
</ul>
<p>Desirable&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>6+ years in strategic partnerships, mobility, autonomy, AI or technology platforms</li>
<li>Experience in regulated industries or operational market launches.</li>
<li>For Tokyo: strong understanding of the Japanese market and local business culture.</li>
</ul>
