</ul></div><h3>ABOUT THE TEAM:&nbsp;</h3>
<p>We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented analyst to join our growing and evolving Perpetual KYX team that sits within our First Line of Defence.&nbsp; In this role, you would be assisting in our battle against financial crime - to prevent money laundering, terrorist financing and other illicit financial activities. This would be achieved through the continual review of the criminal offenses of members and accurate risk management. <br><br>The Member Screening Analyst plays a crucial role in ensuring the organization's adherence to compliance regulations and mitigating risks by conducting thorough screenings of new and existing members. This position involves the assessment of individuals and entities against various watchlists, sanctions lists, and databases to identify potential risks such as fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities.</p>
<h3><br>ABOUT THE ROLE:&nbsp;</h3>
<ul>
<li class="p1"><strong>Member Screening</strong>: Conduct a full review on the screening alert for new and existing members, including individuals and entities, against national and international sanction lists, watchlists, PEP (Politically Exposed Persons) lists and Adverse media.</li>
<li class="p1"><strong>Risk Assessment</strong>: Analyze and review reported results to determine the level of risk for each member, providing recommendations for further investigation or approval.</li>
<li class="p1"><strong>Reporting &amp; Documentation</strong>: Prepare detailed reports and document any concerns or red flags,</li>
<li class="p1"><strong>Investigations</strong>: Collaborate with the second line of defence, to investigate and resolve flagged screening results or to escalate it by providing detailed analysis.</li>
<li class="p2"><strong>Process Improvement</strong>: Identify areas where the screening process can be improved, whether through automation, updated data sources, or improved reporting methods, and suggest improvements to enhance efficiency and accuracy.</li>
</ul>
