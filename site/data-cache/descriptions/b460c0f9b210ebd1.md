<h3><strong>About the Role</strong></h3>
<p>We are looking for a <strong>Senior</strong> <strong>Web Developer</strong> to take ownership of <strong><a href="http://Flipdish.com">Flipdish.com</a></strong>, our corporate website and a key brand asset. The role involves developing, maintaining, and optimising the site to ensure it delivers best-in-class user experience, performance, and scalability.</p>
<p>Craft CMS experience is highly desirable, as is a keen eye for design detail, strong communication skills, and the ability to thrive in sprint cycles. You will work closely with marketing, design, and other business stakeholders to ensure <a href="http://Flipdish.com">Flipdish.com</a> consistently reflects our brand and supports our global growth.</p>
<hr>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Manage and maintain <strong><a href="http://Flipdish.com">Flipdish.com</a></strong>, ensuring updates, security, uptime, and stability.</li>
<li>Handle hosting, server setup, Cloudflare, domains, SSL certificates, and backups.</li>
<li>Build and update landing pages using <strong>Twig templates and SCSS</strong>.</li>
<li>Ensure website speed, mobile responsiveness, and overall performance.</li>
<li>Implement and maintain basic <strong>technical SEO</strong> (meta tags, redirects, sitemaps, structured data).</li>
<li>Manage website forms and integrations with internal tools (e.g. CRM, analytics, marketing platforms).</li>
<li>Improve UI/UX of existing pages in line with brand guidelines.</li>
<li>Collaborate with project managers, designers, and management to deliver updates on time.</li>
<li>Conduct thorough QA and testing before deploying changes.</li>
<li><strong>Our expectations:</strong>&nbsp;To challenge poor decisions and propose better ways of working. Constructive challenge is a responsibility.&nbsp;Prioritising harmony over truth or progress is a failure of responsibility.</li>
<li>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
