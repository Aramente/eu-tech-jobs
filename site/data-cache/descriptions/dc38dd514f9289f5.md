<h2><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for a Senior Staff Machine Learning Engineer to join the Clinical team in the AI and Machine Learning Department. You'll lead the technical direction of AI systems that enhance clinical decision-making and patient care. You'll tackle the hardest problems in healthcare AI, from building voice-powered consultation assistants to establishing LLM evaluation frameworks that ensure clinical safety and efficacy. This role&nbsp;<strong>combines deep R&amp;D with strategic technical leadership</strong>, shaping how Doctolib deploys AI in regulated healthcare environments.</p>
<p>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<p><strong>Technical Strategy &amp; Architecture</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the long-term technical roadmap for clinical ML systems, from model architecture selection to production deployment patterns</li>
<li>Drive strategic build vs. buy decisions, balancing custom development with foundation model APIs</li>
<li>Define standards for safe rollout in healthcare contexts: shadow testing, staged deployment, and human-in-the-loop workflows</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>End-to-End ML Delivery</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead design and implementation of LLM-powered clinical agents (consultation summarization, clinical coding, evidence-grounded recommendations)</li>
<li>Establish rigorous evaluation frameworks using real-world clinical datasets, ensuring outputs meet clinical accuracy and safety standards</li>
<li>Build production infrastructure: model and prompt versioning, guardrails, uncertainty quantification, cost optimization, and observability</li>
<li>Define a bold strategy for online and offline performance objectives to reach new levels of healthcare professionals satisfaction</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Leadership &amp; Impact</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Mentor Staff or Senior ML Engineers and Applied Scientists, elevating technical standards across the organization</li>
<li>Lead cross-functional initiatives spanning Product, Medical Affairs, Legal, and Compliance teams</li>
<li>Translate clinical needs into research questions and production systems that measurably improve patient outcomes</li>
<li>Partner with our medical, legal, and compliance teams to meet EU regulatory expectations (e.g., GDPR, MDR, upcoming EU AI Act) and market standards.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>Who you are</strong></h2>
<p>Before you read on,&nbsp; if you don't have the exact profile described below, but you feel this job description matches your skill set, we still encourage you to apply.</p>
<ul>
<li>You have 10+ years in ML/AI with 3+ years at Staff+ or Principal level leading complex, multi-team technical initiatives</li>
<li>You have a PhD in Computer Science, AI, Statistics, or related field (or equivalent research experience)</li>
<li>You have deep expertise in <strong>at least two</strong> of: clinical NLP, LLM fine-tuning and evaluation, automatic speech recognition, RAG systems, or reinforcement learning</li>
<li>You are expert in Python, PyTorch/Transformers for training, vLLM/similar for inference with a track record deploying ML systems in production (AWS/GCP)</li>
<li>You have exceptional communication skills and are able to align diverse stakeholders and explain complex technical decisions</li>
</ul>
<p>Now it would be fantastic if you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Publications in top-tier ML/AI conferences (NeurIPS, ICLR, ACL) or medical informatics venues</li>
<li>Experience with EU healthcare regulations (GDPR, MDR, AI Act)</li>
<li>Prior work with clinical data or healthcare applications</li>
</ul>
