<p>As Dataiku scales, we are looking for a new member in our IT team to accompany Dataiku's growth.<br>The main responsibility for this role is to be the owner of the IT Processes and Knowledge Base, allowing us to collectively reach higher levels of reliability, quality and collaboration.</p>
<p>You’ll collaborate closely with every part of the company’s IT organization : IT Support, Operations and Security, Infrastructure, Systems teams (People, Finance, Business) - to optimize and align practices among them.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>What you will do</h3>
<p><em>IT Processes</em></p>
<ul>
<li>Scope: Change Management, Incident Management, Project Management (PM)</li>
<li>Improve and strengthen our processes, following industry best practices/framework (eg: ITIL) and adapted to Dataiku’s context and ways of working</li>
<li>Lead the application on the field: be a support for teams involved in the process, act as a facilitator, organize periodic meeting, ensure the tools are well configured and easy-to-use</li>
<li>Configuration of the tools used to support the processes (ITSM, PM)</li>
<li>Continuous improvement based on your judgement and team’s feedback</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>Knowledge</em></p>
<ul>
<li>Knowledge Management - define process, map responsibilities and tools to use</li>
<li>Knowledge Base - set and met objectives (eg: completeness, keep up-to-date)</li>
<li>System Integration Map - build and maintain a map of existing integration between Application and Systems</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>What success looks like</h3>
<p>First 3 months</p>
<ul>
<li>Fully onboarded members of the IT team (get to know the teams, the tools)</li>
<li>Working closely with IT Support and IT Operations, suggesting and implementing improvements in the processes</li>
<li>Review of our current Knowledge Base</li>
</ul>
<p>First 6 months</p>
<ul>
<li>Solidify our processes and be a mentor on Change/Incident/Project management</li>
<li>Onboarded in our tools management (ITSM, PM)</li>
<li>Help deliver visible improvement in our Knowledge base</li>
<li>Validate a plan to build the integration map</li>
</ul>
<p>First year</p>
<ul>
<li>Leader and referent for IT processes - among all teams composing the IT function</li>
<li>Robust Knowledge Management process in place</li>
<li>Build and maintain an up-to-date version of the Integration Map</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>What we are looking for</em></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of experience in the IT fields - covering at least some of the assignments listed in this position</li>
<li>Genuine interest in IT processes &amp; Knowledge management - and their practical application</li>
<li>You are</li>
<ul>
<li>process-oriented, methodic, and enjoy keeping things well-organized</li>
<li>enjoy being a facilitator, design solution in a collaborative manner</li>
<li>a doer, proactive, looking to have an impact on the team</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><em>Nice to have</em></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience managing an ITSM platform and/or Project Management tool</li>
<li>Experience with System Integration Mapping</li>
<li>Interest in Automation and Generative AI</li>
