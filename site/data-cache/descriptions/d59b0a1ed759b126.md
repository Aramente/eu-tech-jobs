#### **The Mission**

At Qblox, we are orchestrating the "brain" of the quantum computer. Our control stacks are used by the world’s leading research labs to talk to qubits with nanosecond precision. But high-end FPGAs and ultra-low-noise RF circuits are only as powerful as the documentation, user stories, and use cases that support them.

We are looking for a **Team Lead for Quantum Application Engineering** to lead the charge in making our complex technology accessible, scalable, and world-class.

#### **The Role**

You will lead a high-performing team of 7 Application Engineers who sit at the heart of our cross-functional R&D organization. Your team acts as the **Product Owners** for various development projects, ensuring that what we build in the lab translates into value for our global customers.

You aren't just managing a team; you are an ambassador for our technology—bridging the gap between hardware engineering, software development, and the scientific community.

#### **What you will do**

* **Lead & Mentor:** Manage a team of specialists focused on breaking down complex quantum use cases into actionable user stories and compelling tutorials.
* **Orchestrate the Chaos:** Use metrics to triage resources and prioritize workflows, ensuring the team stays agile and focused on the highest-impact tasks.
* **Stakeholder Management:** Act as the operational nexus between senior leadership, external partners, and internal R&D teams to optimize resource allocation.
* **Technical Stewardship:** Act as the final Release Manager for all user-facing documentation and continuously improve our testing lab infrastructure to ensure we stay "future-ready."
* **Culture Carrier:** Translate Qblox values into day-to-day activities, fostering an inclusive environment where knowledge exchange is the default.
