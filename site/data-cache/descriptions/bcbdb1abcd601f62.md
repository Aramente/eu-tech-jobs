We’re responsible for the reliable, fast, and compliant delivery of email, SMS, and push notifications. Our scope includes:

Managing message sending infrastructure;

Enabling message rendering and personalization across channels;

Powering message tracking, analytics, and logs for customers and support;

Collaborating with Product, Support, and Customer Success to deliver high-quality CX
