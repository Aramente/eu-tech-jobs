<p>MinIO is the industry leader in high-performance object storage and the company behind the world’s fastest, most widely deployed object store, powering production infrastructure for more than half of the Fortune 500, including 9 of the 10 largest global automakers and all 10 of the largest U.S. banks. Our enterprise offering, AIStor, is engineered to handle the scale, speed, and pressure of modern AI and analytics workloads—from terabytes to exabytes—all in a single namespace.</p>
<p>We are seeking a technical, creative, and entrepreneurial Enterprise Account Executive to drive MinIO’s next stage of growth in the France region. This is a role for a proven seller who can combine technical depth in storage, data infrastructure, data lake/lakehouse, AI/ML, and HPC environments with the ability to close complex $250K+ enterprise deals. The ideal candidate has experience competing against legacy storage solutions, thrives in a startup environment, and brings a mix of creativity, persistence, and technical credibility to win transformative deals.</p>
<h3><strong>What You Will Do</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Drive New Business Acquisition</strong>: Actively prospect and identify enterprise opportunities, leveraging your network and creative outreach to generate pipeline and close high-value deals</li>
<li><strong>Technical &amp; Vision Selling</strong>: Confidently articulate how MinIO delivers transformational outcomes for data infrastructure, AI/ML, and HPC not just incremental improvements. Translate technical capabilities into compelling business value.</li>
<li><strong>Close Complex Deals</strong>: Own the full sales cycle from lead to close, with a focus on $250K+ opportunities and annual quota attainment of $1.5M+</li>
<li><strong>Compete &amp; Win Against Legacy Software</strong>: Position MinIO against traditional storage vendors by demonstrating the advantages of cloud-native, S3-compatible object storage for modern workloads.</li>
<li><strong>Educate &amp; Influence</strong>: Teach prospects about AI infrastructure optimization and data architecture modernization, leading them beyond product comparisons to strategic transformation.</li>
<li><strong>Quantify Value</strong>: Build business cases and ROI models that include quantified benefits, risk mitigation, and financial justification.</li>
<li><strong>Partner-First Territory Strategy</strong>: Build and execute partner-led co-selling strategies, working with channel and alliance partners to expand territory coverage and accelerate deals.</li>
<li><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong>: Partner with Solutions Engineering, Product, and Marketing to deliver tailored solutions, ensure successful POCs, and create a seamless customer experience.</li>
<li><strong>Forecasting &amp; Planning</strong>: Maintain disciplined pipeline management, accurate forecasting, and clear account strategies that support predictable and scalable revenue growth.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Your Skills and Experience</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Enterprise Sales Expertise</strong>: 7+ years of enterprise sales experience with consistent quota attainment ($1.5M+) and average deal sizes of $250K+.</li>
<li><strong>Technical Acumen</strong>: Strong knowledge of storage, data infrastructure, data lakehouse, AI/ML, and HPC environments. Able to engage technical stakeholders and translate technical concepts into business impact.</li>
<li><strong>Partner-Led Approach</strong>: History of building co-selling strategies with channel and alliance partners to scale market presence.</li>
<li><strong>Vision &amp; Thought Leadership</strong>: Skilled at leading transformation sales by shaping buyer thinking and providing unique customer insights.</li>
<li><strong>Competitive Selling</strong>: Proven ability to sell against legacy storage solutions and displace entrenched vendors with a differentiated value proposition.</li>
<li><strong>Startup Experience</strong>: Demonstrated success in fast-paced, entrepreneurial environments where creativity and adaptability are essential.</li>
<li><strong>Challenger Mindset</strong>: Skilled at teaching, tailoring, and challenging customer perspectives to unlock new opportunities.</li>
<li><strong>Network &amp; Territory Expertise</strong>: Deep relationships within the territory, including decision makers and influencers who can be leveraged to accelerate business.</li>
<li><strong>Communication &amp; Influence</strong>: Exceptional presentation, negotiation, and relationship-building skills with both technical and executive stakeholders.</li>
<li><strong>Education</strong>: BA/BS in computer science, engineering, business, or related field; MBA preferred.</li>
</ul>
