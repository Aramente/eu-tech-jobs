<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<p>As the Workplace Planning Lead, you will ensure our physical footprint advances in lockstep with our company growth, creating environments that empower our teams to deliver more, faster. You will guide the evolution of our global office footprint, addressing space needs and translating business growth into effective workplace planning and execution. Working closely with Workplace Managers, Real Estate partners, and leadership, you will facilitate choices related to office expansions, relocations, and consolidations, ensuring our offices support scaling at pace. This position represents the first global planning layer within the Workplace team and will help shape how our workplace function scales in the coming years.</p>
<h2><strong>The day-to-day</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li class="text-start ">Guide space transitions at pace across existing office locations and develop seat and capacity planning models aligned with headcount growth</li>
<li class="text-start ">Forecast workplace demand across locations, defining workplace standards and seating strategies</li>
<li class="text-start ">Facilitate and implement swift data-informed choices to support operational scaling</li>
<li class="text-start ">Apply LLMs and AI-enabled workflows within a corporate real estate and operational context</li>
<li class="text-start ">Coordinate real estate planning choices, such as lease renewals, expansions, or consolidations, and partner with Real Estate teams to evaluate footprint changes and location strategy</li>
<li class="text-start ">Provide workplace data and capacity insights to inform lease negotiations</li>
<li class="text-start ">Plan workplace requirements for new offices, relocations, and office closures, alongside workplace design and layout planning</li>
<li class="text-start ">Coordinate facility setup with Workplace Managers to ensure smooth office transitions</li>
<li class="text-start ">Develop occupancy metrics and dashboards to track workplace capacity across offices, design simple planning models to support leadership choices, and identify opportunities to optimise workspace utilisation</li>
</ul>
