<p>We are seeking a highly experienced <strong>Senior Security Engineer II</strong> to join our team. As a member of our Cloud Security team, you will play a pivotal role in shaping our cloud defense strategy. This is an individual contributor role, you will function as a <strong>technical lead</strong>, acting as a subject matter expert for the broader engineering organization.</p>
<p>You will be responsible for overseeing the security posture of our multi-cloud environment (<strong>AWS, Azure, GCP</strong>) and managing the tooling that keeps our infrastructure safe. You will act as the bridge between security leadership, the application security team, and the broader engineering organization, ensuring that security is designed into our solutions from day one.</p>
<p>Contentstack is an AI-forward company, and we expect all candidates to bring practical experience using AI tools and technologies as a core part of how they work, learn, and drive impact.</p>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Cloud Posture &amp; Tooling Management</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Multi-Cloud Oversight:</strong> Lead the effort to secure and monitor our footprint across AWS, Azure, and GCP, ensuring consistent security controls are applied regardless of the provider.</li>
<li><strong>Security Tooling Operations:</strong> Manage, tune, and optimize our security tooling stack. You will be the primary owner of our <strong>CrowdStrike</strong> deployment (CNAPP/CSPM) for monitoring misconfigurations and threat detection.</li>
<li><strong>API Security:</strong> Take ownership of our API security strategy by managing and monitoring <strong>Cequence</strong>. You will ensure our API estate is discovered, cataloged, and protected against abuse.</li>
<li><strong>Tool Evaluation:</strong> As the team grows, you will lead the evaluation and implementation of new security tools to replace or augment current solutions as business needs evolve.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Architecture &amp; Engineering Collaboration</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Security Architecture:</strong> Partner with Engineering teams during the design phase of new projects. You will review architectures to ensure new solutions are compatible with our security ecosystem and are designed with a "Security First" mindset.</li>
<li><strong>Shift-Left Initiatives:</strong> Work closely with the <strong>Application Security</strong> team to integrate security checks into CI/CD pipelines and monitor deployments.</li>
<li><strong>Vulnerability Management:</strong> Drive the remediation process for identified cloud and application issues, working with engineering owners to ensure critical vulnerabilities are addressed within established SLAs.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Technical Leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Mentorship:</strong> Act as a senior voice within the Cloud Security team, mentoring others and helping define technical standards and best practices.</li>
<li><strong>Incident Response:</strong> Serve as an escalation point for cloud-related security incidents, investigating alerts regarding attacks, compromises, or severe misconfigurations.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Qualifications</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Required Experience</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>5-8 years</strong> of experience in Information Security, with at least <strong>4 years</strong> dedicated to Cloud Security.</li>
<li>Deep technical mastery of <strong>AWS</strong>, with a working proficiency in <strong>Azure</strong> and/or <strong>GCP</strong>.</li>
<li>Proven experience implementing and managing <strong>CSPM/CNAPP</strong> tools (e.g., CrowdStrike, Wiz, Prisma Cloud) in a production environment.</li>
<li>Experience with <strong>API Security</strong> tools (Cequence, Noname, Salt) or WAF technologies.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation) and how to secure it.</li>
<li>Proficiency in at least one scripting language (Python, Go, or Bash) for automation and tooling integration.</li>
<li>Experience working in an AI-forward environment, with demonstrated ability to use AI tools and technologies as a core part of day-to-day work, problem-solving, and continuous learning.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Soft Skills &amp; Attributes</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Influence without Authority:</strong> Ability to drive security adoption across Engineering teams through collaboration and technical expertise rather than mandate.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Thinking:</strong> Ability to look beyond immediate alerts to identify systemic issues and architectural improvements.</li>
<li><strong>Communication:</strong> Excellent ability to translate complex security risks into actionable advice for developers and DevOps engineers.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Preferred / Nice-to-Have</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Experience writing custom detection rules for cloud environments.</li>
<li>Relevant certifications (AWS Certified Security - Specialty, Azure Security Engineer, CCSP).</li>
<li>Experience transitioning from one security vendor to another (e.g., migrating CSPM tools).</li>
</ul>
