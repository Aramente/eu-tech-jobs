</div></div><h2><strong>The Role</strong></h2>
<p>Neo4j GraphAcademy provides free, self-paced, hands-on online training paths for Developers, Data Engineers, and Data Scientists to achieve success with Neo4j.</p>
<p>We are looking for an enthusiastic Curriculum Developer to create engaging, hands-on online training courses, videos, and technical content that educate and empower Neo4j users of all experience levels.</p>
<p>You can view our existing course catalogue and get a feel for how we teach at <a href="http://graphacademy.neo4j.com">graphacademy.neo4j.com</a>.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Design and produce high-quality technical content (courses, videos, sample applications, diagrams) using existing Neo4j Documentation.</li>
<li>Identify gaps in the course catalog, develop courses to cover them, and build clear learning paths for different graph use cases.</li>
<li>Provide feedback on Neo4j offerings and course catalog to improve the user experience.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the distributed Developer Relations, Marketing, Product Management, Solutions Engineering, and R&amp;D teams.</li>
<li>Be comfortable expressing complex subject matter clearly and simply, and working on camera.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Qualifications</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Must Haves:</strong></li>
<ul>
<li>A background in education or technical writing.</li>
<li>Able to demonstrate experience creating video content that uses visual aids to break complex content down into easy-to-understand content.</li>
<li>Ability to research and develop training materials for users of all ability levels.</li>
<li>An empathy for users and their learning experience.</li>
<li>A strong command of the English language (writing and speaking).</li>
<li>Experience working remotely.</li>
</ul>
<li><strong>Nice to Have:</strong></li>
<ul>
<li>Experience with Generative AI, Large Language Models, and GenAI frameworks.</li>
<li>Experience with software development, databases, data engineering, or machine learning.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Specialities</strong></h2>
<p>We are seeking educators with expertise in one or more of the following domains. Candidates may have direct professional experience in the field or a background in technical education within these areas.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Data Ecosystem Integration:</strong> Experience integrating or migrating data across major cloud platforms and data providers, such as Snowflake, Databricks, and Microsoft Fabric.</li>
<li><strong>Generative AI (GenAI):</strong> Deep expertise in GenAI, including building applications that utilize Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Context Engineering principles.</li>
<li><strong>DevOps and System Administration:</strong> Experience in DevOps practices and administering large-scale systems.</li>
</ul>
