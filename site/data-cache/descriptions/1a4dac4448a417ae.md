Delivering the best Spotify experience possible — to as many people as possible, in as many moments as possible. That’s what the Experience team is all about. We use our deep understanding of consumer expectations to enrich the lives of hundreds of millions of users around the world, bringing the music and audio they love to the devices, apps, and platforms they use every day.

We’re looking for a Group Product Manager to join Core Experience, leading a group of product managers responsible for key parts of Spotify’s end-to-end mobile experience on iOS and Android. This includes features that millions of users interact with every day, for example Your Library, as well as the shared UI and interaction patterns that underpin Spotify’s mobile product.

## What You'll do

- Lead and develop a group of Product Managers, building strong product leadership, clear ownership, and healthy team dynamics.
- Define and drive product vision, strategy, and success metrics for initiatives within Core Experience, aligning teams around shared goals and priorities.
- Own and evolve a multi-team product roadmap, balancing new opportunities, platform investments, technical health, and ongoing experience improvements.
- Partner closely with Design, Engineering, Data Science, and User Research to identify user needs, validate hypotheses, and deliver cohesive end-to-end experiences.
- Collaborate with peers and senior partners across Spotify, communicating plans, progress, and tradeoffs while aligning with broader company objectives.
- Enable adjacent teams and product areas by building Core Experience capabilities and patterns that scale across the organization.
- Champion experimentation and learning, supporting teams in modern A/B testing, data-informed decision-making and iterative delivery.
- Stay close to the work, maintaining a hands-on approach while operating at a strategic, multi-team level.

## Who You Are

- An expert product leader with prior people-management experience, including leading Product Managers across multiple teams.
- Proven in building and scaling consumer-facing products, with strong instincts for high-quality user experiences.
- Comfortable driving product strategy across several teams, navigating interdependencies and tradeoffs.
- Highly collaborative, with deep experience partnering with Design, Engineering, Data, and Insights.
- Broadly technically proficient, with familiarity in AI- and ML-enabled experiences, experimentation, and modern product development practices.
- Data-informed and driven by results, using insights and metrics to guide prioritization and strategy.
- A clear and compelling communicator, able to influence team members across functions and levels.
- Thoughtful and resilient when working through ambiguity, balancing strong product intuition with critical thinking
