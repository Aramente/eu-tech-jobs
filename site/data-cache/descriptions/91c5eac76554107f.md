<p><strong>POSITION SUMMARY</strong></p>
<p>We are seeking an experienced Salesforce Development Lead to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience with Sales Cloud, Service Cloud (especially Case Management), Data Cloud, Agentforce, Telephony integrations, and CRM Analytics. They will also have deep, hands-on experience implementing modern Salesforce DevOps/CI/CD and architecting enterprise integrations between Salesforce and external platforms. As a Salesforce Development Lead, you will be responsible for leading the development team, designing and implementing scalable and high-quality Salesforce solutions, and ensuring the successful and timely delivery of projects. This position is a full-time position and will be onsite in our Gurugram office. We are operating in a 4 day in office, 1 day flexible work arrangement.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Leadership &amp; Team Management:</p>
<ul>
<li>Lead and mentor a team of Salesforce developers, providing technical guidance, code reviews and ensuring best practices and coding standards are followed.</li>
<li>Collaborate with product managers, and stakeholders to gather requirements and translate them into technical solutions.</li>
<li>Own and maintain Salesforce development standards, including code quality, documentation, and version control.</li>
<li>Own the Salesforce DevOps/CI/CD practice end-to-end (Git-based workflows, automated validations/testing, multi-environment release promotion, and deployment/release governance).</li>
<li>Lead troubleshooting and resolution of technical issues across the Salesforce ecosystem</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with KKR Information Security, Data Privacy and governance standards and policies.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Salesforce Development:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, and deploy custom Salesforce solutions using Apex and Lightning Web Components (Aura/Visualforce where needed), and other Salesforce technologies.</li>
<li>Design, implement, and optimize Salesforce architecture across Sales Cloud, Service Cloud (Case Management), Data Cloud, Agentforce, Telephony integrations, and CRM Analytics.</li>
<li>Integrate Salesforce with telephony systems to enhance customer service capabilities.</li>
<li>Architect, build, and operate integrations between Salesforce and external enterprise platforms using Data Cloud, APIs, middleware, and data pipelines, including real-time + batch patterns, strong error handling/retries, monitoring/alerting, and data reconciliation.</li>
</ul>
<p>Technical Expertise:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure the scalability and maintainability of Salesforce solutions.</li>
<li>Stay updated with the latest Salesforce releases and features and recommend improvements.</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to Salesforce implementations.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>QUALIFICATIONS</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science information technology, or a related field.</li>
<li>At least 12 years of technology experience with a minimum of 5 years of experience in Salesforce development.</li>
<li>Proven experience with Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, particularly in Case Management, Data Cloud, Agentforce, etc.</li>
<li>Experience with telephony integrations and CRM Analytics is highly desirable.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency in Apex, Lightning Web Components, and Salesforce automation tools (Visualforce where needed).</li>
<li>In-depth understanding of Salesforce configuration, security, data model and workflows.</li>
<li>Strong understanding of Salesforce Governor limits and performance optimization techniques.</li>
<li>Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to troubleshoot complex issues.</li>
<li>Hands-on knowledge of Salesforce CI/CD, including release governance, and orchestration approaches.</li>
