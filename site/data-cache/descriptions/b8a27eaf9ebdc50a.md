This role is instrumental in equipping our Okta sales, presales and channel partners with the knowledge, skills, and tools they need to promote and implement Okta’s solutions successfully.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><br></strong><strong>The Partner Enablement Manager for Partner Led Countries Role</strong></p>
<p>Reporting to the Senior Manager of Partner Enablement, this role will join our growing Global Partner Enablement team to enable select partners to self-sufficiently drive Okta business in emerging markets. Working with cross-functional teams aligned to the partner-led objectives, this role will be responsible for designing the enablement strategy and execution that meets partner needs to excel in a partner-driven approach. This role requires both virtual and in-person enablement with the potential for international travel as the needs grow to other markets.&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>\*This role requires in-person onboarding and travel to our San Francisco, CA HQ office during the first week of employment.</em>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll be doing&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and execute on an enablement strategy that takes partners from initial recruitment through "Tech Champion" status.</li>
<li>Adapt internal trainings, enablement resources, sales plays, and technical enablement into bespoke partner content through a train-the trainer model in which the partner cascades content to other partners within their ecosystem.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ensure partners are experts in Okta onboarding, product sales and presales, service delivery and customer success training.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Train partner to excel at&nbsp; self-service tools, including self-quoting (New, Upsell, and Renewals), lead management, and deal registration.</li>
<li>Educate partners on partner program elements including licensing models, pricing strategies and incentives.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross functional teams to expedite content creation and partner availability.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><br><br></p>
<p><strong>What you’ll bring to the</strong> <strong>role &amp; what we’re looking for</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>5-10 years in Channel Enablement or Sales/ Presales Training</li>
<li>Familiarity and proven understanding of Identity Security and Access Management (IAM)</li>
<li>Understanding of Quote-to-Cash (QTC) processes and CRM/Partner Portal workflows.</li>
<li>Strategic Mindset: Proven ability to move a territory from a direct-sales motion to an independent, partner-led model.</li>
<li>Fluent English communication skills</li>
</ul>
