<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: rgb(126, 140, 141); font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp; Strategic Customer Success Manager</span></h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: rgb(126, 140, 141); font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><em>&nbsp;&nbsp;Prolific</em></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Prolific is not just another player in the AI space — we are the architects of the human data infrastructure that is reshaping the landscape of AI development. In a world where foundational AI technologies are increasingly commoditized, it's the quality and diversity of human-generated data that truly differentiates products and models.&nbsp; Our Strategic Sales and Success team works with most of the world’s leading frontier AI model creators on one of their highest priority initiatives.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: rgb(126, 140, 141); font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><em>The role</em></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">As a Strategic Customer Success Manager, you will partner with frontier AI model creators who are making headline news every day, ensuring they achieve breakthrough value through our human data offerings.&nbsp; In this role, you will lead adoption, consumption, development, and value realization as a trusted advisor for AI research, program management, and business stakeholders.&nbsp; You will own the end-to-end success lifecycle, guiding customers from onboarding to maturity, expansion, renewal, and advocacy while collaborating with your counterparts on Prolific’s Services, Support, Sales, Solutions Engineering, Product, Marketing, and other teams.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><em><span style="color: rgb(126, 140, 141);">What you’ll bring to the role</span></em></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">6+ years in a customer-facing strategic or enterprise customer success (and/or leadership) role, ideally in a business where value is aligned with increased consumption</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Experience developing strategic success plans and orchestrating cross-functional dynamics to drive positive outcomes with customers</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Operational rigor and experience forecasting consumption to identify opportunities, mitigate risk, and measure growth</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Demonstrated success with navigating large organizations, driving adoption and expansion, and cultivating executive relationships/sponsorship</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Strong business acumen and ability to interface with senior customer stakeholders (C-Suite, VP, research leads) as well as technical teams (engineers, data scientists, product owners)</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Fundamental understanding of AI, machine learning, LLMs, or similar concepts—enough to speak credibly about research use cases and value levers</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Desire to play an influential role in a rapidly growing, innovative business—contributing, sharing ideas and expertise, and helping elevate the team around you</span>

</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: rgb(126, 140, 141);"><em>What you’ll be doing in the role&nbsp;</em></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Partnering with frontier AI model creators to ensure the success of their research initiatives, human data/feedback programs, and quality assurance benchmarks</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Serving as a trusted advisor—translating research and business objectives into actionable strategies for Prolific’s platform and services to achieve</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Lead business reviews and executive engagements with clients, building case studies and advocacy stories that highlight how Prolific is driving frontier AI research for them</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Drive continuous improvement of success playbooks, processes, and customer-facing resources, replicating best practices in usage-based models and research workflows</span></li>
<li style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Spending time face-to-face with customers and with peers in the San Francisco office—collaborating, learning, and growing together in service of shared outcomes</span></li>
</ul>
