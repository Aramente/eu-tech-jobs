Position Summary

• Manage full employee lifecycle for Employer of Record (EOR) clients and employees across multiple countries.

• Handle onboarding, employment inquiries, compliance, and offboarding.

• Collaborate with internal teams, clients, and partners to ensure compliance with local, national, and international employment laws.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

• Manage onboarding, employment changes, and offboarding for EOR employees in assigned countries.

• Respond to client and employee inquiries via ticketing system; manage escalations.

• Monitor local employment law updates and ensure global HR compliance.

• Maintain accurate employee records and ensure HRIS data integrity.

• Prepare employment contracts, addendums, hire guidelines, and HR documents ensuring accuracy and compliance.

• Update company policies to align with local regulations and best practices.

• Advise on statutory leave and country-specific HR practices.

• Manage voluntary terminations; assist with involuntary terminations alongside local counsel if needed.

• Identify and implement operational improvements.

• Collaborate with Partners, ICPs, account managers, payroll, and legal teams.

• Coordinate immigration processes with vendors and counsel.

• Perform other HR-related tasks and projects as assigned.

Marginal Functions

• Participate in cross-functional projects impacting global HR or EOR services.

• Support HR technology rollouts including testing, training, and feedback.

• Represent HR in client meetings regarding compliance and workforce matters.

• Research global HR practices, compensation trends, and labor market conditions.

• Assist in onboarding and mentoring new HR team members.

• Complete special projects as directed by leadership.

• Provide consultative services to clients on specialist country requirements.

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

• Strong understanding of global HR practices and employment law compliance.

• Excellent communication with cultural sensitivity.

• Familiarity with GDPR and international labor standards.

• Ability to handle multiple priorities under pressure.

• Proficient with HRIS systems, Salesforce, and Microsoft Office.

• Problem-solving and process improvement oriented.

Education & Experience

• Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.

• HR Certification preferred.

• Must be fluent in German (read/write/speak).

• Minimum 3 years HR experience, preferably in global or EOR environment.

• Familiar with international employment laws and compliance.

Licenses and/or Certifications

• HR certification or degree preferred.

• Mental: Requires strategic thinking, decision-making, accuracy, problem-solving, multitasking, and stress management.

• Communication: Strong interpersonal and written communication; cultural sensitivity; presentation skills for leadership and stakeholders.

Environmental Conditions
