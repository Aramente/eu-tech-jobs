<p><strong>TEAM OVERVIEW</strong><br>KKR’s Enterprise Technology – Platform Engineering team is responsible for designing, building, and operating secure, scalable, and resilient technology platforms that power the firm’s global investment, portfolio monitoring, analytics, finance, and corporate systems. The team enables business growth by delivering cloud-native infrastructure, automation frameworks, and developer platforms that support mission-critical applications and sensitive financial data.</p>
<p>The Platform Engineering team partners closely with Cybersecurity, Data Engineering, Investment Technology, Finance Systems, and Risk &amp; Compliance teams to ensure platform stability, regulatory compliance, and operational excellence. The team drives enterprise-wide modernization initiatives including cloud transformation, DevOps enablement, infrastructure automation, and reliability engineering while maintaining the highest standards of security, governance, and performance expected in a global private equity firm.</p>
<p><strong>POSITION SUMMARY</strong><br>The role is responsible for architecting, implementing, and managing enterprise-grade cloud and hybrid platforms that support KKR’s global operations. This includes building scalable infrastructure, enabling secure CI/CD pipelines, improving system reliability, and ensuring compliance with regulatory and internal security standards.</p>
<p>The role requires strong technical leadership, a proactive approach to risk mitigation, and the ability to manage complex infrastructure environments supporting high-performance financial and data workloads. The individual will foster a culture of automation, operational excellence, and continuous improvement across platform services. The role requires being highly organized, detail-oriented, and capable of prioritizing competing initiatives in a fast-paced investment environment.</p>
