<p><strong>Inbound Sales Team Lead</strong></p>
<p>📍 Milano | On-site<br>🗣 Italian &amp; English required</p>
<p>At SumUp, we help small businesses win — and we do it with simple, powerful tech used by 4M+ merchants worldwide.</p>
<p>We’re continuing to scale our <strong>Italian Sales hub in Milan</strong>, and we’re looking for an <strong>Inbound Sales Team Lead</strong> to drive performance, develop talent, and play a key role in building a high-performing sales organisation.</p>
<p>This role is for someone who thrives in fast-paced environments, enjoys real ownership, and is motivated by both <strong>people development and business impact</strong>.</p>
<h3><strong>🚀 What You’ll Do</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead, coach, and scale a team of Inbound Sales Representatives working with warm, high-intent leads</li>
<li>Be hands-on in day-to-day operations: calls, deal strategy, objection handling, and performance coaching</li>
<li>Drive hiring and onboarding efforts, ensuring new joiners ramp quickly and effectively</li>
<li>Build and continuously improve training programs that are practical, engaging, and impactful</li>
<li>Use data to monitor performance, identify gaps, and implement solutions to raise the bar</li>
<li>Partner with leadership on team structure, capacity planning, and scaling strategy</li>
<li>Foster a strong team culture built on accountability, ownership, and continuous feedback</li>
</ul>
<p>You’ll be close to the action — <strong>on the floor, with the team</strong>, solving problems and driving improvements every week.</p>
<h3><strong>🧠 Who This Is For</strong></h3>
<p>You might be a great fit if you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Have <strong>experience managing sales teams</strong>, ideally in high-growth or performance-driven environments</li>
<li>Have been <strong>involved in hiring, onboarding, and scaling teams</strong>, not just maintaining them</li>
<li>Bring a <strong>proactive, solution-oriented mindset</strong> — you don’t just highlight problems, you actively work to solve them</li>
<li>Are comfortable balancing <strong>performance delivery with people development</strong></li>
<li>Have strong experience working with <strong>KPIs, dashboards, and sales metrics</strong>, using data to inform decisions</li>
<li>Communicate clearly, lead with credibility, and know how to influence without micromanaging</li>
<li>Speak <strong>Italian and English fluently</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>Experience in <strong>fintech, SaaS, or payments</strong> is a strong plus, but not essential.</p>
