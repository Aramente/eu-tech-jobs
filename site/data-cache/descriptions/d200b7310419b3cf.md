<h2><strong>About Chainguard</strong></h2>
<p>Chainguard is building the future of <strong>secure software supply chains</strong> by delivering <strong>secure-by-default container images, helm charts and language libraries alongside developer tools</strong> that eliminate vulnerabilities before software reaches production.</p>
<p>We partner with <strong>cloud providers, VARs/SIs, and technology platforms</strong> to help organizations adopt secure software development practices at scale.</p>
<p>We are looking for a <strong>Partner Solutions Engineer (PSE)</strong> to help enable and scale our partner ecosystem by building technical champions, developing integrations, and accelerating partner-led adoption of Chainguard solutions.</p>
<p>This role combines <strong>technical depth, partner enablement, and go-to-market collaboration</strong>.</p>
<p>Our partner engagement model follows the <strong>BEPIC framework</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Belief</strong> – Build technical conviction in Chainguard</li>
<li><strong>Executive Sponsorship</strong> – Align with partner leadership</li>
<li><strong>Propensity to Embed</strong> – Integrate Chainguard into partner architectures</li>
<li><strong>Integrated Value Proposition</strong> – Deliver compelling joint solutions</li>
<li><strong>Cadence</strong> – Maintain consistent partner engagement</li>
</ul>
<h2>What You'll Do</h2>
<h3><strong>Build Partner Technical Champions</strong></h3>
<p>Enable technical teams across <strong>cloud providers, VARs/SIs, and technology partners</strong> through workshops, training programs and paths, and hands-on labs. Develop reusable partner assets including <strong>reference architectures, demos, and solution playbooks</strong>.</p>
<h3><strong>Drive Integrations and Architecture</strong></h3>
<p>Work with partners and internal teams to design <strong>secure cloud-native architectures</strong> that incorporate Chainguard into DevOps, platform engineering, and security workflows. Collaborate with product and engineering teams to bring partner integrations to life.</p>
<h3><strong>Develop Joint Partner Solutions</strong></h3>
<p>Co-create partner-led services and offerings that help customers adopt Chainguard solutions. Partner closely with <strong>Sales and Partner Managers</strong> to identify deals, accelerate the sales cycle, help with adoption, and grow partner-influenced revenue across the company.</p>
<h3><strong>Support Strategic Partner Relationships</strong></h3>
<p>Engage with partner technical leaders and executives to align on <strong>technical roadmaps and solution strategies</strong>. Broadcast partners' progress to field teams internally and encourage the close collaboration of Chainguard - Partner SEs for maximum joint value proposition and have a strong influence over product direction.&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>Maintain Strong Partner Engagement</strong></h3>
<p>Run ongoing enablement sessions, workshops, and joint technical activities to ensure partners remain <strong>engaged and capable of delivering Chainguard solutions</strong>. &nbsp;Initiatives to cultivate strong bonds with new and existing partners.</p>
<h2>What We're Looking For</h2>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in <strong>Solutions Engineering, Partner Solutions Architecture, or Sales Engineering</strong></li>
<li>Experience with <strong>cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)</strong></li>
<li>Hands-on experience with <strong>containers, Kubernetes, or cloud-native infrastructure</strong></li>
<li>Familiarity with <strong>DevSecOps, platform engineering, or cloud security</strong></li>
<li>Experience enabling partners through <strong>technical training or solution development</strong></li>
<li>Strong ability to communicate complex technical topics to both <strong>engineers and business stakeholders</strong></li>
<li>Ability to travel up to <strong>50%</strong></li>
</ul>
<h3>Nice to Have</h3>

<p>If you're passionate about <strong>cloud-native technology, open source, security, and partner ecosystems</strong>, we'd love to hear from you.</p>
