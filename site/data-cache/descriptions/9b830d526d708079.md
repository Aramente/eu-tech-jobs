<p><strong>About the job</strong></p>
<p>This position is needed to drive the tactical execution and operational delivery of complex cross-functional projects within Global Operations.<br>As a Delivery Manager, you are the engine of project execution, transforming strategic vision into operational reality. You bridge the gap between business intent and technical execution, ensuring every initiative moves through the lifecycle with precision and speed. By partnering with Enterprise Technology and being the voice of Global Operations, you ensure our projects are predictable, transparent, and high-impact, serving as the primary advocate for operational excellence and seamless product rollouts.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>In this role, you’ll:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Translate</strong> abstract business needs into actionable, prioritized requirements by conducting end-to-end discovery and ensuring both the impact and outcomes of the project are documented.</li>
<li><strong>Cultivate</strong> deep partnerships across the <strong>Global Operations</strong> ecosystem, specifically <strong>Global Customer Services, Global Customer Support Operations, and Onboarding &amp; Compliance</strong>,to ensure project scalability and total operational readiness.</li>
<li><strong>Drive</strong> project momentum by maintaining accountability for timelines and holding cross-functional partners to deadlines while adapting to shifting business capacity.</li>
<li><strong>Identify</strong> and mitigate risks by serving as an "early warning system," mapping complex dependencies across <strong>Enterprise Technology</strong> and <strong>Global Operations</strong> to protect the critical path.</li>
<li><strong>Generate</strong> high-signal, low-noise weekly status updates and maintain rigorous project documentation within Airtable to ensure long-term auditability and stakeholder alignment.</li>
<li><strong>Coordinate</strong> launch synchronization between <strong>Enterprise Technology</strong> releases and <strong>Global Operations</strong> enablement, ensuring technical deliverables map perfectly to business-facing launch dates.</li>
</ul>
