<p>PingCAP is seeking an experienced Revenue Accounting Manager to join our global accounting team. This role is critical to supporting the company’s worldwide growth. We are looking for a self-motivated, detail-oriented professional who thrives in a fast-paced environment and brings strong expertise in revenue accounting, systems, and process improvement.</p>
<p>You will collaborate closely with the Accounting, Sales, Legal, Sales Operations, and Systems teams to design, enhance, and execute scalable revenue processes. Drive the month-end revenue related close process with high quality and velocity. This role is ideal for someone who wants to drive transformation across the order-to-cash (O2C) lifecycle and strengthen our global revenue operations.</p>
<h2>&nbsp;</h2>
<h2><strong>Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>Billing and Collections</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Leveraging Netsuite billing to issue customer invoices on a monthly basis in accordance with contract terms.</li>
<li>Monitor invoice status and collaborate with Sales and other stakeholders to collect overdue receivables.</li>
<li>Lead initiatives to automate billing and collection processes using NetSuite and other tools/technologies.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Month-End Close</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the timely and accurate completion of month-end, quarter-end, and year-end close activities related to revenues.</li>
<li>Ensure revenue and related sales commission are recognized in accordance with <strong>ASC 606</strong> and internal revenue recognition policies.</li>
<li>Assist with technical accounting assessments for non-standard contract terms and ensure proper revenue treatment.</li>
<li>Execute robust month-end revenue procedures, including:（Disaggregated analytical review, Reconciliations (e.g., billing, collections, deferred revenue，Deferred revenue and contract liability classification）</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Apply cash receipts in a timely and accurate manner.</li>
<li>Assist in the accounting and classification of cloud cost, ensuring the accuracy of gross margin.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Process Improvement &amp; Automation</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a key driver in transforming and enhancing order-to-cash processes to improve accuracy, scalability, and efficiency of revenue recognition workflows.</li>
<li>Identify, recommend, and implement opportunities to streamline and automate revenue and accounting processes.</li>
<li>Champion the use of AI technologies within Finance to increase automation, reduce manual work, and improve insights.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Sales Ops, IT/Systems, Product, Customer Success) to ensure data integrity, system improvements, and end-to-end process alignment.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Qualifications</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Bilingual: Proficiency in English (primary working language) and Mandarin Chinese.</li>
<li>Big Four experience and CPA (or equivalent certification) are strong pluses.</li>
<li>7+ years of progressive experience in revenue accounting, ideally within SaaS and Cloud costing or technology companies.</li>
<li>Passionate about adopting new technologies, including AI tools, to automate processes and enhance productivity.</li>
<li>Experience working in a global organization across multiple time zones (APAC + US).</li>
<li>Hands-on, detail-oriented mindset with strong problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.</li>
<li>Experience with NetSuite or other ERP systems preferred.</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of ASC 606 and practical application in real-world scenarios.</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and proven ability to collaborate effectively with both internal and external stakeholders.</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple priorities and thrive in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>
