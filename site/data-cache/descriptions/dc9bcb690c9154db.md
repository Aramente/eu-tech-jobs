<h2><strong>About the Opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for a highly motivated and resourceful Growth &amp; Strategy Intern to join our team and support our expansion in the Spanish market.</p>
<p>This role is a unique hybrid, offering hands-on experience across performance marketing, partner management, and business strategy. You will work within a high-performance team, assisting with everything from influencer outreach to market research and branch monitoring. This is an ideal position for a recent graduate who is eager to understand how a high-growth company scales in a local market through both marketing execution and strategic planning.</p>
<h3><strong>In this role, you will:</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Influencer and Affiliates Marketing Support</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Network Coordination:</strong> Support the day-to-day operations of the Spanish Affiliate and Influencer network, including pipeline building and initial outreach.</li>
<li><strong>Performance Tracking:</strong> Assist in conducting data-driven research to track acquisition rates, costs, and partner performance.</li>
<li><strong>Reporting:</strong> Help prepare weekly growth steering reports, highlighting key insights and channel performance for the Spanish market.</li>
<li><strong>Compliance &amp; Admin:</strong> Ensure all partner content aligns with internal rules and support the processing of payouts and budget tracking.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Growth Marketing Execution</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Campaign Coordination:</strong> Help execute ad-hoc growth initiatives and co-marketing campaigns with local partners to expand our reach.</li>
<li><strong>Offline &amp; Events:</strong> Assist in the planning of offline initiatives, including local event activations and traditional media placements in Spain.</li>
<li><strong>Social &amp; Content:</strong> Support social media collaborations and coordinate content creation with external subject-matter experts to build brand awareness.</li>
<li><strong>Experimental Growth:</strong> Help identify and document new experimental initiatives that support our overall user and revenue growth objectives.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Strategy &amp; ES Branch Support</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Market Research:</strong> Conduct deep-dive research into the Spanish market, focusing on competitor moves, local trends, and new partnership opportunities.</li>
<li><strong>Branch Monitoring:</strong> Support the <strong>ES Branch Manager</strong> in monitoring local operational health, tracking branch-specific KPIs, and identifying bottlenecks.</li>
<li><strong>Product &amp; Business Support:</strong> Provide localized insights to the Product and Business teams to help them better integrate with local partners and Spanish user needs.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Mapping:</strong> Assist in identifying and evaluating high-impact partnership opportunities that align with our long-term Spanish market strategy.</li>
</ul>
