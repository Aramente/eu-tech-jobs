<h2><strong>Job Description</strong></h2>
<p>As the Senior Product Manager you will be responsible for shaping and delivering the vision of HelloFresh’s Global People Data Product Tribe. This role drives the development of reliable, scalable people data solutions that enable informed workforce and talent management decisions. By partnering closely with senior leadership, People teams, Product, Analytics, and Technology stakeholders, the role ensures the creation of high-impact dashboards, data assets, and self-serve analytics tools. The primary purpose is to harmonise complex workforce data systems, define and&nbsp;track key people metrics, and build technology products that empower data-driven&nbsp;decision-making at a global scale while maintaining local flexibility.</p>
<p><strong>You will…</strong></p>
<ul>
