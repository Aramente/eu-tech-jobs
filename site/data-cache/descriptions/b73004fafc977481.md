<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">As Senior Developer Content Manager for Connector SDK, you will drive developer adoption through hands-on enablement content and community engagement. You'll write Python alongside prose, engage directly with developers and open source contributors, and think constantly about onboarding — what's confusing, what's missing, what makes someone give up. You'll work closely with the Connector SDK technical writing team who own the reference documentation, and bridge engineering, product, and go-to-market teams to drive adoption and growth.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This is a full-time position based out of our Bangalore office.
