**About the Role**

We are looking for a sharp, business-minded legal professional to join our Legal Team as**Manager - Legal**. This is a high-impact role at the intersection of law, business, and strategy - shaping key transactions, driving compliance excellence, and enabling the company’s growth journey. You will work closely with leadership and cross-functional stakeholders, acting as both a legal advisor and a business enabler.

## What you will do

* Own and drive the end-to-end contracting lifecycle, including drafting agreements from scratch, reviewing and redlining counterparty templates, leading negotiations, and ensuring timely closure aligned with business objectives.
* Lead the drafting, review, and negotiation of a wide range of commercial agreements, including vendor, distribution, licensing, SaaS/technology, and strategic partnership contracts.
* Provide proactive legal advisory on new business initiatives, product launches, and strategic projects, ensuring legal and regulatory risks are identified, assessed, and mitigated prior to go-live.
* Advise on corporate governance, regulatory, and compliance matters under the Companies Act, FEMA, SEBI regulations, and other applicable laws.
* Support and execute corporate transactions, including M&A, joint ventures, investments, and corporate restructurings.
* Act as the legal partner to the People (HR) team on employment-related matters, including drafting and reviewing employment agreements, ensuring compliance with evolving regulations, and developing, updating, and implementing internal employment policies.
* Partner closely with business teams to anticipate legal risks and develop pragmatic, business-aligned solutions.
* Engage, manage, and coordinate with external counsel for specialized matters, including due diligence and complex advisory.
* Monitor regulatory developments, interpret implications for the business, and proactively recommend policy and process enhancements.
* Drive continuous improvement in contract lifecycle management, compliance monitoring frameworks, and overall risk management processes.

## What you will need

- Qualified LLB with 5-7 years of PQE.
- Experience in a reputed law firm and/or in-house role with exposure to corporate advisory, transactions, and compliance.
- Experience handling cross-border contracts and complex commercial negotiations.
- Possess an analytical and strategic minds.
