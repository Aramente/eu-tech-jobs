**About Fam (previously FamPay)**

Fam is India’s first payments app for everyone above 11. FamApp helps make online and offline payments through UPI and FamCard. We are on a mission to raise a new, financially aware generation, and drive 250 million+ young users in India to kickstart their financial journey super early in their life.  
  
We’re reimagining how the next generation experiences fintech—going beyond payments to build a lifestyle brand that blends money, identity, and everyday experiences into one seamless, intuitive journey.  
  
Founded in 2019 by IIT Roorkee alumni, Fam is backed by some of the most respected investors around the world like Elevation Capital, Y-Combinator, Peak XV (Sequoia Capital) India, Venture Highway, Global Founder’s Capital and the likes of Kunal Shah, Amrish Rao as angel investors.

**About this role**

As a Senior Technical Program Manager at Fam, you will own and drive complex, cross-functional programs that sit at the intersection of product, engineering, risk, compliance, and business. You will play a critical role in scaling Fam’s fintech platform by delivering high-impact initiatives end-to-end—right from problem definition and roadmap planning to execution and launch. This role is delivery-centric, deeply technical, and highly collaborative. You will work closely with senior engineering leaders, product managers, and business stakeholders to ship reliable, secure, and scalable fintech products while continuously improving speed, cost efficiency, and operational excellence.

## On the Job

- **Program Ownership & Delivery-** Own end-to-end delivery of large, cross-functional programs across product, engineering, risk, compliance, operations, and external partners. Drive program planning, roadmap definition, dependency management, execution tracking, and launch readiness. Ensure programs are delivered on time, within scope, and aligned to business priorities.
- **Strategic & Business Impact-**  Lead strategic initiatives focused on reducing time-to-market, improving platform scalability, and optimizing cost across engineering and operations. Partner with leadership to prioritize initiatives based on business objectives, regulatory needs, and customer impact. Balance tactical execution with long-term platform and architectural thinking.
- **Technical Leadership-** Act as a technical problem solver, unblocking teams by driving consensus, identifying innovative solutions, and making sound trade-off decisions. Work closely with engineering teams on system design, architecture reviews, and technical execution. Translate business and regulatory requirements into clear technical plans.
- **Stakeholder Management-** Collaborate with senior business and engineering leadership to define program goals, success metrics, and execution plans. Provide clear, data-driven program updates to leadership, highlighting risks, dependencies, and mitigation strategies. Coordinate across multiple teams to ensure alignment and smooth releases.
- **Process & Execution Excellence -** Define and improve program management processes, release mechanisms, and execution frameworks as FamPay scales. Own risk management, issue tracking, change management, and post-launch retrospectives. Drive a strong culture of accountability, transparency, and execution rigor.
