Job Summary:

• The Aerial Fiber Groundman will work with ADSS fiber.

• Applicants should be familiar with telecommunications lines, bucket trucks, poles, and their functions.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

• Complete aerial fiber optic construction using ADSS fiber.

• Use tools, equipment, and ladders to complete projects.

• Perform additional duties as assigned.

• Complete daily cup sheets documenting work performed.

• Clean and maintain all supplies and equipment.

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

• Ability to work well with others.

• Proficient with lineman tools and equipment.

• Ability to read construction prints.

• Proficient in telecom construction equipment (e.g., Aerial Lifts, Dozers, Lashers, Meters, Plows).

• Able to climb poles safely.

• Ability to work safely, supervised or unsupervised.

• Capability to work standing, at shoulder height and overhead, for extended periods.

• Excellent manual dexterity.

• Ability to follow instructions and communicate effectively verbally and in writing.

• Understand and operate aerial lift devices, lashers, and hand tools.

Education & Experience:

• Authorization to work in the United States.

• Successful completion of pre-employment drug screen, background check, and motor vehicle record check.
