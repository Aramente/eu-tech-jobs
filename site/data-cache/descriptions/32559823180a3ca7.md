<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Model Risk Specialist&nbsp;</strong></span><br><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">We are looking for a Model Risk Specialist in Amsterdam to help drive our model risk management framework as we scale our in-house machine learning and financial models globally. In our flat organization, you’ll move fast and work hands-on to build, represent, and maintain the full scope of model risk as we expand into new products and countries.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Conduct independent validations of models used in risk management, financial forecasting, decision-making, or other business functions;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Assess model assumptions, methodologies, input data, and output for accuracy, robustness and alignment with intended use cases;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Develop and implement benchmark models or alternative approaches to test a model’s performance;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Review and evaluate model documentation, ensuring clarity, completeness, and compliance with internal policies and regulatory guidelines;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Identify model risks, limitations, and weaknesses, provide actionable recommendations to mitigate those risks;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Act as a sparring partner for model developers to resolve findings and enhance model quality;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Ensure compliance with regulatory standards on model risk management;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Participate in the maintenance of a model risk management governance framework and validation procedures;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Carry out risk assessments across the company, across product offerings, across the globe; and</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Further strengthen a high-performing second line risk team that covers all Adyen risks.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>What we’re looking for:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">A proactive, energetic, lifelong learner that has a hands-on mentality;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">3+ years working experience within model validation, either in-house or through a consulting role;</span></li>
<li style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">The ideal candidate will have a strong background in statistical modeling, machine learning, and/or financial modeling, combined with the ability to critically assess model methodologies, assumptions and performance;</span></li>
