<p><strong>Mission</strong></p>
<p>Databricks is at the forefront of innovation in the data and AI industry, empowering organizations to harness the power of data and artificial intelligence.</p>
<p>As Sr. Field Marketing Manager for Italy, Spain and Israel, you will play a pivotal role in driving demand generation and business growth across the region. You will be responsible for crafting and executing innovative regional marketing strategies that generate high-quality leads, convert them into opportunities, and build a robust sales pipeline. This cross-functional role requires close collaboration with teams across Marketing, Sales Development, Sales, Partner Management, and other field teams. You will initially report to the Director, EMEA Continental Marketing.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The impact you’ll have</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build the territory strategy with sales stakeholders and execute regional marketing programs</li>
<li>Lead end-to-end execution of all regional field marketing programmes and events, both physical and virtual, ensuring seamless delivery and high engagement. This includes onsite execution with occasional multi-day travel (currently no travel to Israel until further notice)</li>
<li>Build data-driven and integrated marketing plans in close alignment with corporate and EMEA marketing to increase awareness, drive engagement, generate leads, and support conversion rates.</li>
<li>Evaluate and execute the most effective partner and third party event co-marketing opportunities</li>
<li>Effectively manage budgets and evaluate ROI of all campaigns</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with SDR and sales teams to enhance lead-to-opportunity conversion rates</li>
<li>Generate qualified leads through innovative and cost-effective marketing strategies, driving pipeline growth, consumption and revenue</li>
<li>Leverage data to continuously refine and optimize campaign strategies, ensuring sustained business growth and programme optimization</li>
<li>Establish strong communication channels with sales and regional teams to ensure the delivery of impactful marketing campaigns tailored to the region</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What we look for </strong><strong><br></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience in managing field marketing events, including proprietary, third-party and virtual events, with a track record of successful execution</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to deliver tangible business results through strategic marketing programs</li>
<li>Ability to lead and collaborate with cross-functional teams in a dynamic and fast-paced enterprise software environment</li>
<li>Strong ability to develop and optimize data-driven marketing plans, consistently meeting KPIs within budget and tight deadlines</li>
<li>Proven capability to manage multiple projects and priorities simultaneously, with a focus on execution and delivery</li>
<li>Fluent in English, and at least one of either Italian or Spanish</li>
