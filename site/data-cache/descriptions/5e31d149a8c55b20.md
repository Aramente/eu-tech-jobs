<ul>

<li data-start="911" data-end="984"><span data-teams="true">Drive continuous improvement of finance processes and systems, leveraging automation and AI-driven tools to enhance efficiency, scalability, and reporting quality.</span></li>
<li data-start="987" data-end="1037">Build and mentor a high-performing finance team.</li>
