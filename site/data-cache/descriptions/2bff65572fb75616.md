<p>As an&nbsp;<strong>Employee Relations Specialist</strong>, you’ll be the go-to expert for complex employee matters, helping leaders make fair, consistent, and legally sound decisions while keeping our culture respectful, human, and inclusive.</p>
<p>You’ll work closely with HR, Legal, and Leadership to navigate sensitive situations, manage risk, and continuously improve how we support our people. Please note that this role is dedicated exclusively to Employee Relations; candidates should have substantial, hands-on ER experience, as this is not an HR Generalist or HR Operations position.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Act as a trusted advisor to managers and employees on employee relations and labor law matters</li>
<li>Handle complex and sensitive employee cases, including performance concerns, misconduct, grievances, and conflicts</li>
<li>Lead and support internal investigations (e.g. code of conduct, harassment, whistleblowing cases)</li>
<li>Support disciplinary actions and offboarding processes in a fair, compliant, and people-centric way</li>
<li>Partner closely with HR Business Partners, Legal, Compliance, and external counsel when needed</li>
<li>Collaborate with employee representatives / works councils where applicable</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with local labor laws, internal policies, and global ER standards</li>
<li>Identify patterns and ER risks, and proactively recommend preventative actions</li>
<li>Contribute to ER policies, playbooks, manager guidance, and training sessions</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What We’re Looking For</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>2+ years proven experience in Employee Relations (not HR Operations)</li>
<li>Solid knowledge of local labor law; experience in international or matrix organisations is a plus</li>
<li>Fluency in German (minimum C1, full working proficiency) and English</li>
<li>Comfort around people facing role, this position requires to work from the office 3-4 days a week&nbsp;</li>
<li>Confident handling of sensitive, high-impact employee cases</li>
<li>Strong judgment, empathy, and the ability to stay calm in complex situations</li>
<li>Clear, pragmatic communicator who can influence without authority</li>
<li>High integrity, discretion, and a solution-oriented mindset</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Why You’ll Love Working Here</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>A people-first culture with high trust and autonomy</li>
<li>Real impact on how employee relations are handled in a fast-scaling tech environment</li>
<li>Close collaboration with senior stakeholders and cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Competitive compensation and benefits</li>
<li>A company that values transparency, fairness, and continuous improvement</li>
</ul>
