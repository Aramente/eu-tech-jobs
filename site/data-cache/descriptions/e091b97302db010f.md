<p data-start="94" data-end="315">As a Merchant Operations Associate, you’ll focus on reaching out to merchants who are at risk of becoming inactive. Your goal is to help them get back on track or, if needed, support them in offboarding from the platform.&nbsp;You’ll speak directly with merchants to understand their challenges, solve issues, and improve their experience on Wolt. This role is a mix of outbound calling, problem-solving, and working with data.</p>
<p data-start="519" data-end="660">This role suits someone with around 3+ years of experience who is comfortable making calls, staying organised, and enjoys helping customers succeed.</p>
