Join us as a **Payroll Specialist** to ensure flawless payroll processing per month in France while building a sustainable payroll operation that reduces key-person risk and strengthens compliance controls.

As our **Payroll Manager**, you will work closely with **[Julia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/julia-lecointe/)**, our Head of People Operations and Payroll, to share the workload, improve processes, and elevate payroll quality across Qonto.

You will join a team of 12 people in People Ops and Payroll, collaborating daily with our payroll provider ADP, our Finance team, and all People Operations Managers.

👩‍💻🧑‍💻 **As a Payroll Manager at Qonto, you will:**

• **Run accurate payroll cycles**: Ensure timely and error-free payroll processing for France, including new hires, terminations, salary changes, and all variable elements (bonuses, benefits, deductions);

• **Standardize and improve processes**: Refine all procedures impacting payroll matters—time management, wage garnishments, taxes, benefits—to raise the bar on quality and controls;

• **Own compliance**: Monitor regulatory changes and ensure Qonto stays ahead of current and future French payroll regulations in a fast-paced fintech environment;

• **Train and enable the People Ops team**: Build payroll knowledge across the organization through structured training sessions and clear documentation, helping the team catch errors early and improve data quality;

**🤔 What you can expect**

• **You will be the payroll expert**: Take ownership of French payroll at scale and become a key point of reference within Qonto;

• **Build a sustainable payroll operation**: You will help reduce dependency on a single key person by sharing the workload with Jeannie and creating robust processes and controls;

• **Work in a fast-paced, quality-driven environment**: Join a fintech where precision, compliance, and continuous improvement aren’t just buzzwords — they’re part of our DNA

• **Collaborate cross-functionally**: Partner closely with People Ops Managers (who input data into ADP), Finance, and our external payroll provider to prevent issues and resolve blockers quickly;

• **Use professional tools**: Master ADP for payroll processing, Excel for analysis and controls, and Workday for HR data management. You will also contribute to improving these systems.

**🤝 About your future manager**

Your manager will be [Julia](https://www.linkedin.com/in/julia-lecointe/), who is our Head of People Operations and Payroll.

With her extensive experience in control processes and financial procedures, she brings valuable expertise to the Payroll department.
