## Main Responsibilities

- Provide ongoing local insights on the South Cone market (Argentina and Chile), including cultural context, economic shifts, consumer behavior, and competitive landscape.
- Serve as the South Cone advisor, identifying and mapping relevant VPN and eSIM use cases tied to local events, sports calendars, holidays, and regional moments throughout the year.
- Localize, proofread, and create content adapted to the Argentine market, ensuring linguistic accuracy, cultural relevance, and brand consistency across all formats and channels.
- Monitor daily news across LATAM and Spain, curating relevant content related to brand mentions (NordVPN, NordPass, Saily), political developments, sports events, and digital privacy trends that may impact business performance or public perception.
- Support the Spanish team with proofreading tasks, ensuring content quality and grammatical accuracy in Spanish.
- Contribute to miscellaneous marketing tasks as business needs evolve, adapting quickly to shifting priorities and cross-functional requests.

## Core Requirements

- Argentine native speaker with flawless written grammar and style in Rioplatense Spanish. This is a hard requirement, not a preference.
- Basic knowledge of digital marketing concepts: SEO, social media dynamics, content formats, and online communities.
- Deep knowledge of the South Cone market (Argentina and Chile) and broad familiarity with the wider LATAM region:  you follow the news, know relevant media content and creators, understand the political and economic landscape, and can connect regional dots across countries.
- Strong attention to detail and the ability to distinguish between what's urgent, what's important, and what can wait.
- Familiarity with tools like Google Sheets, Slack, and basic research platforms.  Willingness to learn new tools quickly.
