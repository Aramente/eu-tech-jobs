## Your (space) mission

As the Business Development Manager at cosine you play a key role in shaping and growing our presence in the Italian New Space market. You help turn advanced optical instrumentation into meaningful customer solutions that end up in space, supporting missions for leading commercial and institutional customers.<br><br>You work closely with the Remote Sensing Director and act as a key interface between customers and our technical teams. From early-stage opportunity identification to proposal, negotiation and contract closure, you help drive the full commercial cycle of highly innovative space instrumentation projects.<br><br>In a small and growing organization like cosine Italy, your impact is immediate and visible. You take ownership, build structure, and help define how we engage with the market while working on technology that is quite literally launched into orbit.

## Tasks & responsibilities

In this role you will:<br><ul><li>Identify, develop and follow up on new business opportunities in the space and remote sensing market</li><li>Prepare and coordinate high-quality proposals, tenders and commercial offers</li><li>Build and maintain strong relationships with commercial and institutional customers</li><li>Support the positioning and further standardization of cosine’s product and service offering</li><li>Collaborate closely with engineering teams to align technical solutions with customer needs</li></ul>

## Your profile
