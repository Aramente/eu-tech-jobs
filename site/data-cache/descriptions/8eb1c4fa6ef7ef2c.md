**⭐ Mission**

Join us as a **Product Engineer** and help us build the financial tools that empower 600,000+ SMEs and their accountants to gain full control over their finances. You will work on high-impact financial features that make SMEs and accountants lives easier, all under the guidance of [Yanis](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yanishammada/), our Head of Engineering, and with the sponsorship of [Steve](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steveanavi/), our co-founder.

**👩‍💻🧑‍💻 As a Product Engineer at Qonto, you will**

• **Ship features end-to-end, faster than ever:** You'll own features with Product Managers and Designers - using AI tools (Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot...) and automation to accelerate every step of the process.

• **Challenge the product, not just build it:** Engineers at Qonto actively shape the roadmap. You'll understand the "why" behind the "what" and prototype solutions fast.

• **Work with maximum squad autonomy:** Your squad owns its roadmap, picks its approaches, and ships independently. No layers of approval, no waiting on committees.

**🏅 What we're looking for**

• **Accounting domain knowledge:** You've worked on accounting, bookkeeping, invoicing, or tax-related products before - or you have a strong understanding of how accountants work and what they need from software.

• **Product mindset:** You understand the business value of your code, you're comfortable navigating complex domain logic (like accounting rules), and you challenge specs to build the best product - not just the one that was asked for.

• **AI-native workflow:** You use AI to supercharge how you build, from code generation to testing to deployment. (Click [HERE](https://medium.com/qonto-way/ai-at-qonto-vision-statement-725a7e2f659b) to access our vision statement on AI at Qonto).

• **Solid engineering fundamentals:** You have experience with Go, React, or any modern full-stack environment, and you're willing to learn what you haven't mastered yet.

• **Strong communication in English:** You collaborate effectively with non-technical stakeholders and can articulate technical decisions clearly.

🔧 **What you'll get**

• Unlimited access to the best AI tools on the market - **Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot, Dust, Notion AI.**

• A stack built on **Go, React, Kubernetes, and Kafka.**

• A **squad culture** where **autonomy** is the default: you **own your decisions** and ship **without red tape**.

**🤝 About your future manager**

You'll report to**[Yanis](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yanishammada/), Head of Engineering** - but don't expect micro-management.

Yanis grew from Lead Engineer to Head of Engineering over 4 years at Qonto. He's a pragmatic generalist who's built payment integrations from scratch and is equally comfortable on the frontend and backend.

His approach: give engineers real ownership, create a culture where sharing failures is as valued as sharing wins, and get out of the way so the squad can ship.

**💬 Hiring Process**

• 2 Interviews (1 hour each): first with your Talent Acquisition Manager, and then with Yanis or one of his team leads

• A live exercise (2 hours) to demonstrate how you think and give you a taste of what working at Qonto could be like

We aim to have the hiring process last 10 days max, from first interview to potential offer🤞

*If you've read this far and thought "that sounds like me but I don't check every box" — apply anyway. We hire on potential and mindset, not checklists.*
