<p><strong>About the team</strong><strong><br></strong>We are Project Bonsai, an early-stage experimental startup within JetBrains’ Innovation Hub.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our goal is to build the infrastructure for AI-spec-driven development, where:</p>
<ul>
<li>System requirements, architecture, and code are formally connected.</li>
<li>AI agents are guided by infrastructure requirements and developers' intentions for greater reliability and trust.</li>
<li>The system can detect and prevent spec-code drift.</li>
</ul>

<h3><strong>About the role</strong></h3>
<p>We are looking for a Product Lead / Entrepreneur in Residence to work alongside the technical founder and own the product strategy for Bonsai.</p>
<p>This is not a traditional product management role. Your focus will be on deeply understanding real developer workflows: where specs, architecture, and code break down, what causes friction, and what problems are actually worth solving. You will translate these insights into concrete product requirements and priorities.</p>
<h3><strong>In this role, you will:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Own the product strategy: You’ll define the product direction based on the existing technical foundation and validate the strongest hypotheses.</li>
<li>Run continuous discovery: This involves talking directly to developers to understand workflows, constraints, and failure modes in real systems.</li>
<li>Define the initial scope: You’ll be responsible for identifying the ICP, key scenarios, and what to build first, balancing ambition with feasibility.</li>
<li>Track the ecosystem: You’ll monitor adjacent tools, competitors, and research to inform positioning and priorities.</li>
<li>Work closely with the engineering team: You’ll help everyone maintain focus and alignment in a highly ambiguous, early-stage environment.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>We will be happy to have you on our team if you have:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Hands-on product ownership experience or experience in a role with an equivalent level of responsibility where you owned discovery and delivery end to end.</li>
<li>Technical background or mindset, including the ability to work with complex technical systems; production coding experience is a strong plus.</li>
<li>Comfort with ambiguity, evidenced by experience turning unclear problems into a concrete plan through discovery and iteration.</li>
<li>Zero-to-one exposure, such as experience with early product discovery, design partners, or first real adoption.</li>
<li>Relevant domain experience, for example with developer tools, IDEs, workflow products, or developer productivity platforms.</li>
<li>Familiarity with or curiosity about AI agents and AI-driven tooling used in real systems.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Why join Project Bonsai?</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Foundational work: Help define a new direction in developer tooling at the intersection of programming languages and AI.</li>
<li>Deep technical problems: Work on language design, formal specs, tooling, and AI agents – not surface-level features.</li>
<li>Real ownership: Own the product’s strategy and direction, working in a role closer to that of a founder than a classic PM.</li>
<li>JetBrains environment: Enjoy startup-level autonomy with JetBrains’ engineering culture and long-term mindset.</li>
</ul>
