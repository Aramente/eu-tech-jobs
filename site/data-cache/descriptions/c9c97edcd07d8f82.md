<p><strong>Our TechOps team ⭐</strong></p>
<p>We're looking for a technically-driven and systems-minded TechOps Platform Manager to lead our Platform area. TechOps sits within Monzo's Security collective and is responsible for ensuring every Monzonaut has the tools, access, and technology they need to do their best work. The Platform team is the technical backbone of TechOps, building the observability, resilience, and technical foundations that give Monzo confidence in the internal tools and platforms Monzonauts rely on every day. This means understanding how our enterprise tooling estate is performing, ensuring it's built to scale, and having the right standards in place to change safely and consistently.</p>
<p>In this role, you'll lead and develop a team of specialists, set the technical direction for how our platforms are monitored, strengthened, and evolved, and build the operational depth that allows Monzo to grow with confidence.
