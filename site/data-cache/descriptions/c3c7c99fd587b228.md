<h1><strong>Technical Financial Crime Manager</strong></h1>
<h3><strong>About Paystack</strong></h3>
<p>Over the past nine years, Paystack has established itself as a pioneer in African fintech with a mission to help merchants get paid by anyone, anywhere in the world. Processing over $300 million in monthly transactions, our modern payments infrastructure supports tens of thousands of notable corporations, including MTN, Bolt, and Domino’s Pizza.</p>
<p>As we enter a phase of accelerated growth, we are seeking a Technical Financial Crime Manager to own, design, and scale our fraud and AML detection capabilities. This role sits at the intersection of data, engineering, and financial crime operations, with end-to-end accountability for ensuring our monitoring systems are technically robust, domain-accurate, and scalable across multiple markets.</p>
<p>This is a hands-on technical leadership role. You will define detection logic, guide system design, and directly influence how financial crime risk is identified and managed at Paystack, while also leading and developing high-performing fraud and AML teams.</p>
<h3><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h3>
<p>As the Technical Financial Crime Manager, you will run the day-to-day fraud and AML detection stack; from data and rules to operational outcomes. You will combine deep technical expertise with financial crime domain knowledge to design effective monitoring systems, manage domain specialists, and ensure Paystack remains a safe, trusted payments platform.</p>
<p>You will be accountable for:</p>
<ul>
<li>The technical quality and effectiveness of fraud &amp; AML monitoring logic</li>
<li>The operating model and performance of Financial Crime Monitoring teams</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Translating risk, regulatory, and business requirements into scalable detection systems</strong></p>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<h5>Technical Ownership of Detection &amp; Monitoring</h5>
<ul>
<li>Define, build, test, and optimise fraud and AML detection rules, scenarios, thresholds, and models used in production systems.</li>
<li>Translate complex datasets and domain insights into actionable detection logic embedded in monitoring and alerting platforms.</li>
<li>Establish feedback loops between investigation outcomes and detection logic to continuously improve signal quality.</li>
<li>Measure and manage detection performance using quantitative metrics (precision, recall, false positives, alert-to-case conversion, loss metrics).</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Maintain structured, auditable documentation of rules, logic, assumptions, and changes.<br></strong></p>
<h5>Data Analysis, Modelling &amp; Insights</h5>
<ul>
<li>Analyse large, complex transactional and behavioural datasets to identify emerging fraud and AML risks across markets.</li>
<li>Design and implement statistical models, machine learning approaches, and/or time-series analysis to enhance detection capabilities.</li>
<li>Build and own dashboards and reporting frameworks tracking KPIs, SLAs, alert quality, investigator productivity, and risk outcomes.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Conduct trend analysis, root cause analysis, and deep dives on losses, typologies, and control gaps.</strong></p>
<h5>Financial Crime Oversight</h5>
<ul>
<li>Own the end-to-end fraud and AML detection domain, ensuring alignment between prevention, detection, investigation, and remediation.</li>
<li>Apply deep understanding of fraud typologies, AML/CTF risks, sanctions, and regulatory expectations to detection design.</li>
<li>Manage the Fraud and AML operational teams (specialists and first-line managers) to ensure adequate coverage, capability and day-to-day execution.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Translate regulatory, partner, and audit requirements into scalable technical and operational controls.</li>
<li>Stay ahead of evolving financial crime patterns, market-specific risks, and regulatory developments across Paystack’s footprint.</li>
</ul>
<h5>Tooling, Automation &amp; Scale</h5>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Product and Engineering to embed detection logic into core systems and improve monitoring, alerting, and case management tooling.</li>
<li>Drive automation initiatives to reduce manual effort, improve consistency, and enable scale without compromising control quality.</li>
<li>Identify and prioritise enhancements to monitoring platforms, workflows, and data pipelines.</li>
<li>Ensure fraud and AML tooling evolves in line with transaction growth, new products, and new markets.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Operational Excellence</h3>
<ul>
<li>Build and continuously improve operational processes, SLAs, KPIs, and quality frameworks across Fraud and AML teams.</li>
<li>Use data and metrics to manage performance, capacity, and outcomes, ensuring teams operate efficiently and effectively.</li>
<li>Identify gaps, risks, and inefficiencies, leading initiatives to strengthen controls and scale operations sustainably.</li>
<li>Balance speed, quality, regulatory expectations, and customer experience in day-to-day decision-making.</li>
</ul>
<h5>Cross-Functional &amp; Executive Collaboration</h5>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with Product, Engineering, Data, Risk, Compliance, Legal, and Customer Operations.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Influence roadmap priorities related to fraud, AML, and financial crime tooling.</li>
<li>Provide clear updates to senior stakeholders on operational performance, risks, and emerging issues</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Support audits, partner reviews, and regulatory engagements as a subject matter expert.</strong></p>
