- Develop and implement transformation strategies and roadmaps aligned with the overall business objectives, focusing specifically on the HR and Finance departments.
- Collaborate with HR and Finance department leaders to understand their technology needs and challenges, and develop IT solutions that support their operational efficiency and effectiveness.
