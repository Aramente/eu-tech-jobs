<h3><strong>Workplace Experience Lead</strong></h3>
<p>About the Role</p>
<p>As a Workplace Experience Lead at Doctolib, you will take ownership of one of the largest offices in our portfolio, located in Nantes, with responsibility for our branch office in Niort. Reporting directly to the Workplace Services Director, you will be instrumental in creating a safe, efficient, and inspiring work environment for all employees in your scope.</p>
<p>You will serve as the primary point of contact for all workplace-related matters, balancing operational excellence with a strategic approach to site management.</p>
<h3><strong>Your Responsibilities :&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<p><strong><em>Supplier &amp; Service Provider Management</em></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You manage and coordinate strategic supplier relationships for key services, including office amenities, reception, security, and fleet &amp; travel management</li>
<li>You will ensure compliance with agreed KPIs and SLAs</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Events Coordination</em></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You plan and support internal events throughout the year, fostering company culture through seamless logistics and careful budget management</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Compliance &amp; Safety</em></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You ensure full compliance with HSE standards and drive the implementation of QHSE processes across your sites</li>
<li>You oversee access control, emergency protocols, vendor compliance, and end-to-end issue resolution</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Budget &amp; Procurement Management</em></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You handle purchase order management and invoice processing with rigor and accuracy</li>
<li>You track expenses on an ongoing basis and flag any discrepancies in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Building Management &amp; Site Support</em></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You work hand in hand with the maintenance department to support the smooth day-to-day operations of the Nantes and Niort offices</li>
<li>You act as the primary point of contact for subtenants based in the Nantes building, managing day-to-day requests and ensuring a positive cohabitation within the premises</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Stakeholder Management</em></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>As the single point of contact for all internal and external clients and stakeholders on Workplace matters, you will need to be highly proactive in capturing field signals early to prevent issues from escalating. You will share these insights promptly with your management to define and implement the appropriate action plan.</li>
<li>You build strong working relationships with&nbsp; Real Estate,Facility, IT, &nbsp; Finance, Procurement teams and regional contacts</li>
<li>You ensure clear and consistent communication across&nbsp; locations within your scope</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Your profile</strong></p>
<p><em>Are you absolutely convinced that you have exactly what's needed for the position? Then we encourage you to apply. Even if you don't meet every requirement, you might be the perfect future Doctoliber for this or other positions!</em></p>
<h3><strong>To become part of our team, you bring :</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>
<p>At least 5 years of experience in Workplace or Facilities Management, ideally gained in a fast-growing tech environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strong organizational skills with the ability to juggle multiple priorities and meet deadlines</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Proven experience in budget management and supplier relationship management</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Solid understanding of HSE standards and building compliance requirements</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a naturally collaborative approach</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fluency in both French and English.</p>
</li>
</ul>
