<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Senior UI Designer</strong></span></p>
<p>Adyen is looking for a Heavyweight UI Designer who lives at the intersection of high-end aesthetics and technical innovation. You aren't just a "visual" designer; you are a digital designer who builds scalable, high-fidelity systems.</p>
<p>As a Senior/Heavyweight lead, you will bridge the gap between brand strategy and interactive execution. You will take complex financial concepts and transform them into world-class digital experiences. This role requires a<strong> </strong>tech-forward mindset: we are looking for someone who views AI as a fundamental layer of their creative process and Framer as their playground for high-fidelity deployment.</p>
<h3><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Systemic UI Design:</strong> Architect and evolve sophisticated UI kits and design systems in Figma that empower our global marketing and product teams.</li>
<li><strong>High-Fidelity Prototyping:</strong> Use <strong>Framer</strong> to bring static designs to life, creating production-ready micro-interactions and immersive web experiences that feel tangible.</li>
<li><strong>AI-Driven Workflows:</strong> Lead the charge in integrating AI tools into the design lifecycle, from generative assets to automated layout testing and workflow optimization.</li>
<li><strong>Brand Elevation:</strong> Strategically translate Adyen’s value proposition and brand system into impactful digital visuals, ensuring the brand remains premium, cohesive, and cutting-edge across all touchpoints.</li>
<li><strong>Knowledge share:</strong> Lead and inspire young designers&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>Stakeholder Mastery:</strong> Present high-level creative concepts to executive stakeholders, advocating for design choices with a blend of data-driven logic and creative intuition.</li>
<li><strong>Global Scalability:</strong> Build with a "flex-first" mindset, ensuring UI solutions adapt seamlessly across multiple languages, regions, and device constraints without losing aesthetic integrity.</li>
<li><strong>Execution &amp; Ownership:</strong> Manage high-stakes projects from initial prompt to final hand-off, maintaining "heavyweight" standards of quality and autonomous project management.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What You Bring</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>The Experience:</strong> 10+ years of professional experience in high-growth environments (top-tier agencies, tech startups, or elite in-house teams).</li>
<li><strong>The Tech Stack:</strong> \* <strong>Mastery:</strong> Figma (Components, Variables, Auto-layout) and Framer (CMS, Animations, Components).</li>
<ul>
<li><strong>Proficiency:</strong> Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects).</li>
<li><strong>The Edge:</strong> Deep practical knowledge of <strong>AI tools</strong> (Midjourney, Runway, Magician, etc.) and a hunger for "extra tooling" (e.g., Lottie, Spline, or Rive).</li>
</ul>
<li><strong>The Portfolio:</strong> A showcase of "High Craft." We want to see sophisticated, expert use of grid systems, and digital work that feels "alive."</li>
<li><strong>The Specialist Mindset:</strong> You are a <strong>UI Specialist</strong>. You find joy in the "final 10%", the polish, the motion, and the component architecture.</li>
<li><strong>Note:</strong> This is <em>not</em> a UX role. If your passion is user testing, wireframing, or journey mapping, this won’t be the right fit. We want the visual storytellers and the system builders.</li>
<li><strong>The Communication:</strong> Full professional fluency in English with the ability to articulate "the why" behind "the what."</li>
<li><strong>The Drive:</strong> You are a self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced environment and isn't afraid to "get your hands dirty" in an all-hands-on-deck culture.</li>
</ul>
