<h2><strong>About the Role</strong></h2>
<p>We’re looking for an <strong>Agency Partnerships Specialist</strong> to help us build and grow our agency partnerships across 30+ markets. In this role, you’ll sit at the intersection of strategy, sales, and data, working closely with global and regional teams to turn insights into impactful commercial opportunities.</p>
<p>This is a high-impact individual contributor role where you’ll help shape how we work with agencies at scale, unlocking new revenue streams and strengthening long-term partnerships.</p>
<h2><strong>What You’ll Be Doing</strong></h2>
<p>You’ll play a key role in building and growing our agency business:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Own agency planning:</strong> Support the creation of joint business plans (JBPs) with key partners, bringing together data, insights, and clear opportunities</li>
<li><strong>Turn data into action:</strong> Use Wolt’s first-party data to build dashboards, track performance, and generate insights agencies can use with their clients</li>
<li><strong>Create compelling proposals:</strong> Build presentations, media plans, and recommendations tailored to agency needs and planning cycles</li>
<li><strong>Drive campaign success:</strong> Monitor delivery and performance, and turn results into clear learnings and next steps</li>
<li><strong>Unlock new growth:</strong> Identify new revenue opportunities through agency networks and help grow existing partnerships</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Our Humble Expectations</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>3-6 years Experience working in or closely with Media agencies</li>
<li>Strong understanding of media planning across channels (programmatic, social, retail media/commerce)</li>
<li>Comfortable working with data and translating it into clear insights and recommendations</li>
<li>Familiar with campaign measurement (e.g. reach, ROAS, CPA, incrementality)</li>
<li>Strong presentation and storytelling skills</li>
<li>Able to work across teams and markets in a fast-paced, matrix environment</li>
<li>Solid analytical skills (Excel + data-driven thinking)</li>
<li>Fluent in English (additional European language is a plus)</li>
<li>Proactive, hands-on, and ownership-driven mindset</li>
</ul>
