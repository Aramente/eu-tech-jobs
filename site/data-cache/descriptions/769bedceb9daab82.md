<p>We are hiring a <strong>Linux devices software engineer </strong>on our SnapD team to work on mission-critical Golang code, focused on software delivery, containers, security sandboxing, telemetry and device management.</p>
<p>Snaps are next-generation<a href="https://snapcraft.io/"> software packaging and delivery for Linux</a>. They are designed for safety, security and robustness, with application-specific confinement of containers optimised for IoT devices, desktops and servers. Our goal is that snaps work across multiple Linux flavors to ensure that publishers have the simplest way to reach the whole Linux world.</p>
<p>This role requires an obsession with quality and performance, for work at the heart of the operating system. It also requires an interest in the software publishing and consumption experience. General knowledge and experience with Linux as a developer platform is required. Having previous experience with Golang is very nice to have.&nbsp;</p>
