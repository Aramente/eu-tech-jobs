<p><strong>The Position</strong></p>
<p><strong>The requirements listed in our job descriptions are guidelines, not hard and fast rules. You don’t have to satisfy every requirement or meet every qualification listed. If your skills are transferable and you are in the ballpark experience-wise, we'd love to speak to you!</strong></p>
<p><strong>Our roster has an opening with your name on it</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for a Product Owner to join our high-performance agile team in Edinburgh. As Product Owner, you will be responsible for maintaining the tax lifecycle for our customers. You will be a key player in our product delivery teams, collaborating with colleagues in Product, UXD and Software Engineering. You will serve as a Product champion, working in a fast-paced environment with stakeholders from across the global FanDuel business, including our Commercial, Tax, Marketing and Sportsbook and Casino teams. If you’re excited by this challenge and want to work within a dynamic company then we’d love to hear from you.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The Role</strong></p>
<p>As a Product Owner, you will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Be directly involved in delivering key features and improvements for customers</li>
<li>Be responsible for eliciting requirements, in the form of PRDs, user flows, user stories &amp; acceptance criteria, prioritizing the product backlog to streamline the execution of program priorities while maintaining the conceptual and technical integrity of the features or components</li>
<li>Play a key role in testing different experiences to different customer groups and gathering the results</li>
<li>Establish and maintain domain expertise on the product</li>
<li>Bring fresh ideas to the table when working to solve business problems, using your commercial understanding to generate innovative solutions</li>
<li>Play a key role in self-organizing agile delivery teams</li>
<li>Work within a collaborative environment, liaising with stakeholders across the global organization and the broader delivery team</li>
<li>Contribute toward the internal FanDuel PO community by promoting industry best practice and fostering a culture of knowledge sharing and cross-skilling</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What We’re Looking For</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>A self-starter able to work both autonomously with minimal supervision and collaboratively within an agile team</li>
<li>Experience of, or passion for, Agile delivery processes (used in software or web-based products) and a willingness and enthusiasm to promote a culture of inspect and adapt</li>
<li>Experience conducting stakeholder interviews and facilitating stakeholder workshops</li>
<li>Ability to define and refine user stories, acceptance criteria, and definition of done processes</li>
<li>Exceptional analysis skills</li>
<li>Excellent oral and written communication skills with the ability to communicate at all levels of management</li>
<li>Experience in user testing methods such as A/B testing</li>
<li>High level of personal initiative and motivation; drives high standards for individual accomplishment and works to meet or exceed challenging goals</li>
<li>Ability to constructively challenge and question as well as actively listen</li>
<li>Is used to working in a fast-paced environment helping to provide clarity to delivery teams when priorities change</li>
</ul>
