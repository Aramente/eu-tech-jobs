<h3><strong>What you’ll be doing:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>QA Ownership: Take full accountability for the end-to-end QA process, work closely with other business functions to align testing strategies and ensuring the engineering team adheres to these standards throughout the SDLC.</li>
<li>Automation Leadership: Design, develop, and maintain a robust automation framework with a core focus on Provar and other tooling, creating new scripts to ensure comprehensive test coverage for custom Salesforce solutions, with a focus on efficiency.</li>
<li>Test Implementation: Lead the QA delivery of testing for complex project features, from initial test planning and cross-functional problem solving to final regression and deployment sign-off.</li>
<li>End-to-End Testing: Execute thorough end-to-end testing across the Salesforce ecosystem, including integrations with internal and external systems via APIs.</li>
<li>Peer Review &amp; Governance: Participate in review cycles to ensure both manual and automated tests align with the broader Salesforce strategy and quality benchmarks.</li>
<li>Best Practices: Implement QA best practices, focusing on maintainable automation code, data integrity, and performance testing within the Salesforce environment.</li>
<li>Troubleshooting: Support incident management by identifying root causes through technical troubleshooting and detailed bug reporting.</li>
<li>Requirement Translation: Collaborate with stakeholders and business analysts to translate complex business requirements into detailed test cases and acceptance criteria.</li>
<li>DevOps Integration: Utilise and contribute to CI/CD processes and version control workflows, integrating Provar automation into the deployment pipeline to support high-velocity delivery.</li>
<li>Documentation: Develop and maintain comprehensive QA documentation, including test plans, execution reports, and automation maintenance guides.</li>
<li>Mentorship: Provide guidance and technical coaching to Administrators and junior team members to support their understanding of quality standards and automation.</li>
</ul>

<li>Modern Tooling: Extensive experience with Automation Testing tooling, Salesforce Lightning Experience, and validating Lightning Web Components (LWC).</li>

</ul>
