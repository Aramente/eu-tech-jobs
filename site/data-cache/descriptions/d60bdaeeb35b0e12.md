<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><p><strong>About the team</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide has ambitious hiring plans to support its growth trajectory, launch new products, and expand into new markets. As a result, talent acquisition is a top priority, and we are committed to building a world-class team.</li>
<li>Our Talent team is a diverse group of full-cycle Recruiters based across the UK, Central and Eastern Europe, and India. As a Talent Partner, you’ll be involved in every aspect of the recruitment process, ensuring that no two days are the same.</li>
<li>To thrive in this role, you must be comfortable working in a fast-paced, scale-up environment. This position offers significant freedom to create, innovate, and make a meaningful impact on how Tide attracts top talent.</li>
</ul>
<p><br><strong>As a Talent Partner, you will:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage the full-cycle recruitment process for high-volume Operations and Marketing roles, ensuring a seamless candidate experience.</li>
<li>Develop and implement sourcing strategies to identify, attract, and nurture top talent across various channels.</li>
<li>Conduct interviews and assessments to evaluate candidates’ strengths and development areas, mitigate bias, and provide expert guidance to hiring managers.</li>
<li>Ensure data accuracy and integrity in the Applicant Tracking System (ATS) throughout the recruitment lifecycle.</li>
<li>Analyse pipeline data, uncover trends, and generate actionable insights to refine sourcing strategies and influence hiring decisions.</li>
<li>Build and maintain strong stakeholder relationships, effectively managing expectations and fostering collaboration, even in ambiguous or fast-changing scenarios.</li>
<li>Help us hire exceptional talent while enjoying the journey. Recruiting is both challenging and rewarding, and you'll play a vital role in empowering small businesses globally.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What we are looking for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>2–4 years of hands-on experience in talent acquisition, ideally in high-growth or volume hiring environments.</li>
<li>Experience hiring for Operations and Marketing roles, with a proven ability to manage multiple requisitions efficiently.</li>
<li>Strong sourcing skills and experience engaging passive candidates through various channels, such as LinkedIn, job boards, and professional networks.</li>
<li>A data-driven mindset, with the ability to analyse recruitment metrics and adjust strategies accordingly.</li>
<li>Excellent stakeholder management skills, with the ability to influence hiring decisions and guide managers through the recruitment process.</li>
<li>A passion for delivering a best-in-class candidate experience and a keen interest in employer branding initiatives.</li>
<li>Resilience, adaptability, and a proactive approach to problem-solving in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.</li>
</ul>

</ul>
