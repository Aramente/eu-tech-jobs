The team of over 1400 employees includes more than 400 highly skilled engineers with deep expertise across hardware and software engineering, as well as an in-house AI R&amp;D team.</p></div><h2 id="The-Role" data-local-id="88030a0b7c78" data-renderer-start-pos="4610">The Role</h2>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="4620" data-local-id="cc7f5a061fbb">We’re looking for a <strong data-renderer-mark="true">Network Software Engineer (NetSWE)</strong> to build software that makes network operations safe, scalable, and boring — even as we launch new data centers and expand fast. This is not a “write scripts for configs” role: you’ll build the tooling and services that sit between the network core (switches/ports/VLANs, traffic processors) and the cloud platform on top, using open source where it fits and building the missing pieces where it doesn’t.</p>
<h3 id="Your-responsibilities-will-include:.1" data-local-id="5349627ffeae" data-renderer-start-pos="5090">Your responsibilities will include:</h3>
<ul class="ak-ul" data-local-id="97c8efeb-1385-42a4-914d-6cdf9cefd857" data-indent-level="1">
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5129" data-local-id="c03ef6b77879">Build and maintain services and tooling that automate the network lifecycle: day-0 provisioning, day-N changes, drift detection, and operational verification</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5290" data-local-id="52004a11502b">Make network changes safe and transparent: CI/CD workflows, diff/review tooling, staged rollouts/rollbacks, audit trails, and guardrails</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5430" data-local-id="c3ee27738aba">Develop observability systems that scale across many sites: telemetry pipelines, signal quality, and tooling that shortens incident investigations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5580" data-local-id="3959ba080fa0">Close “last mile” gaps between the network and the platform: integrate source-of-truth data, expose APIs, and build reliable automation around it</p>
</li>
<li>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="5729" data-local-id="59d3576543db">Collaborate closely with network engineers and SREs to turn real operational pain into product-quality tooling</p>
</li>
</ul>
