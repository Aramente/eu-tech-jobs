<p><strong>About the Role</strong></p>
<p>This is a hybrid role based in London, supporting Mixpanel's EMEA business across our London, Barcelona, and Israel offices. Mixpanel's People Team focuses on attracting, developing, and retaining premier talent. As a People Partner, you will work closely with business leaders and people managers as a consultative partner — providing HR guidance across the full employee lifecycle to support strong teams, a great employee experience, and business results.</p>
<p>This role will report to the Senior People Business Partner for Go-To-Market (GTM), and work as an embedded piece of the EMEA GTM organization, partnering with the leadership team and People functions to provide a best-in-class employee experience for all Mixpanelers.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate has a broad understanding of HR and a strong foundation in employment practices across EMEA. You bring strong EQ, sound judgment, and the ability to navigate people situations with care and clarity in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proactively partner with people managers and leaders across EMEA to support and grow high-performing teams.</li>
<li>Collaborate with other members of the People Team on the design and delivery of people programs that elevate the employee experience.</li>
<li>Contribute to change management strategy and own execution — from implementation to communications.</li>
<li>Monitor and evaluate feedback channels and contribute to initiatives that optimize the employee experience.</li>
<li>Investigate and resolve employee relations matters in a way that balances company, internal, and external stakeholder considerations.</li>
<li>Maintain awareness of HR principles, practices, and regulatory environments across EMEA to recommend best practices in the application of HR systems and policies.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>We're Looking For Someone Who Has</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience consulting leaders and driving business results as a People Business Partner.</li>
<li>Broad HR knowledge across topics including talent management, employee relations, employee experience, compensation, and HR policy.</li>
<li>Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and a self-starter approach; proficiency with G-Suite.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and project management skills, with the ability to design clear processes and balance speed with quality.</li>
<li>Strong EQ and empathy, with the judgment to resolve people situations in a balanced and thoughtful way.</li>
<li>Experience acting as a consultative partner to managers and leaders, with the ability to communicate and influence effectively.</li>
<li>Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal skills; adaptable and resilient in ambiguous, fast-moving environments.</li>
</ul>
