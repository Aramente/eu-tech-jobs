As a Residential HVAC Installer at AA Service Co., you will play a vital role in our mission to provide superior HVAC installations for our residential customers. Working in a crew of two, alongside an Install Apprentice, you will be responsible for safely and efficiently installing HVAC systems while upholding the highest standards of craftsmanship and customer service.

## Responsibilities:

- Maintain a consistently high level of installation quality, as evaluated through post-installation inspections and customer feedback.
- Achieve a first-time installation success rate as established by the Installation Manager, ensuring minimal revisits or callbacks due to installation-related issues.
- Provide effective mentorship and guidance to the Install Apprentice, contributing to their skill development and ensuring a positive learning experience.
- Demonstrate excellent communication skills when interacting with customers during installations. Uphold a professional appearance and demeanor while representing AA Service Co. during installations, earning high marks for professionalism from customers.
- Maintain accurate and complete documentation of installation details, equipment used, and any modifications in Service Titan.
- Monitor and track the progress of the Install Apprentice, providing regular feedback and opportunities for growth and skill advancement.
- Maintain a consistent record of punctuality and attendance, arriving at installation sites and appointments on time.
- Additional duties as assigned
