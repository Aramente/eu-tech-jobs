### ⭐ **Mission**

Join us as a Senior Product Manager for our Initial Due Diligence team and help us build one of the most sophisticated, scalable onboarding systems in Europe. This is a highly technical and high-impact role where you will drive the vision and execution of our KYC/KYB workflow engine, directly shaping how tens of thousands of organizations register and validate their accounts every month.

### 👩‍💻🧑‍💻 **As a Senior Product Manager KYC/KYB at Qonto, you will:**

* **Define and own the product vision** for the Initial Due Diligence workflow engine, managing the complex logic required to validate organizations across multiple countries, legal forms, and risk levels.
* **Manage the end-to-end customer KYC journey**, optimizing for conversion while maintaining strict regulatory compliance.
* **Lead vendor management for KYC providers**, ensuring cost efficiency and exceptional quality across our different geographic regions.
* **Design tooling for our Ops teams**, streamlining manual reviews and high-risk customer flows to reduce friction and improve operational speed.
* **Play a key role in a major infrastructure rebuild**, actively contributing to a 12-to-18-month project to scale our onboarding machine to support tens of millions of organizations in compliance.

### 🤔 **What you can expect:**

* **Market/Team Context:** You will take ownership of the critical KYB metric within a fast-paced environment where compliance is a competitive advantage.
* **Methodologies, how we work, and what we value:** We balance rapid product delivery with stringent risk and regulatory requirements. You will work closely with engineering, risk, compliance, and our operations team (Tania's team) to iterate safely and effectively.
* **Tools we use:** We partner closely with best-in-class external identity verification and KYC providers, utilizing complex backend decisioning workflows and sophisticated internal ops dashboards.

### 🏅 **About You**

* **Experience:** You have a proven track record as a Product Manager working on scalable onboarding systems, identity verification, or deep backend compliance workflows (not just generic compliance).
* **Domain Expertise:** You possess a deep, technical understanding of KYC and KYB processes and are familiar with European regulatory requirements.
* **Vendor Management:** You are highly comfortable evaluating, integrating, and managing external technical vendors (like ID verification providers) at scale.
* **Collaborative:** You have strong experience working closely with risk, compliance, and operations teams, seamlessly translating regulatory needs into product features.
* **Systems Thinker:** You enjoy tackling complex backend workflow logic and are capable of building engines designed to process tens of thousands of validations per month.
