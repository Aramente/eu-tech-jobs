<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>We are seeking a highly experienced Graphic Design professional consultant to serve as a consultant on AI training data projects for leading AI model builders and enterprises. Your focus will be to define success criteria, review outputs, and provide targeted guidance to improve quality and speed — directly contributing to the successful delivery of domain-specific annotated datasets that meet the highest technical standards. You will be engaged on specific projects with clearly defined deliverables, milestones, and end dates.</p>
<h2>Components</h2>
<h3>Technical Standard Setting, Quality Control, and Process Improvement</h3>
<ul>
<li>Define domain-specific quality success metrics.</li>
<li>Develop project-specific SOPs, QA rubrics, and reference materials for the specific purpose of meeting client technical standards.</li>
<li>Review project outputs against technical standards, flagging and correcting defects before client delivery.</li>
<li>Perform structured QA passes on design deliverables; flag, track, and resolve defects quickly to hit delivery deadlines.</li>
<li>Return files to contractors with precise remediation notes.</li>
<li>Provide advisory input on design tools, workflows, and asset management systems.</li>
<li>Handle spec changes and edge-case scenarios — e.g., new branding guidelines or accessibility requirements — drafting acceptance criteria or workarounds.</li>
<li>Curate example libraries of “gold standard” design assets for calibration and comparability to agreed reference materials.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Talent Vetting &amp; Output Improvement</h3>
<ul>
<li>Participate in vetting and assessing technical contractor talent for specific projects.</li>
<li>Review sample work from contractors and provide precise, actionable written feedback to improve outputs.</li>
<li>Create targeted training or calibration resources — e.g., brand guideline overviews, design system usage tips.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Project Delivery Support</h3>
<ul>
<li>Advise on technical scoping and requirements during project setup.</li>
<li>Provide expert guidance for edge cases, technical exceptions, and specification changes.</li>
<li>Contribute to post-project reviews to capture lessons learned and improve future standards.</li>
<li>Identify and summarize client model observations and insights.</li>
<li>Build dashboards or trackers with defect categories and recurrence to surface production insights that improve project outcomes.</li>
<li>Conduct post-mortems, analyze defect trends, and propose process tweaks or training refreshers.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Target Profile</h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor of design.</li>
<li>Deep technical expertise and 5+ years professional experience in graphic design, branding, or visual communications.</li>
<li>Mastery of principles of design, visual communication and advertising effectiveness.</li>
<li>Expert proficiency with design software (Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Sketch).</li>
<li>Proven ability to set, enforce, and maintain high technical standards in visual design.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills for delivering clear technical guidance.</li>
<li>Experience producing technical documentation, quality rubrics, or training resources.</li>
<li>Ability to work within fixed project timelines and scope.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, documentation discipline, and commitment to accuracy and consistency.</li>
<li>Fluency in spoken and written English, with clear and concise writing skills.</li>
</ul>
