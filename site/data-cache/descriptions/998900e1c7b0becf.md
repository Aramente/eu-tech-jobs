<p><strong>ABOUT STRONG&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>We are STRONG – A Pilates-inspired, cardio-infused, and designed for a full-body workout that is low-impact and high-intensity. Our diverse classes, including&nbsp;<strong>ROW</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>RIDE</strong>, and&nbsp;<strong>REFORM</strong>, are designed to help clients maximize their performance and achieve their fitness goals.</p>
