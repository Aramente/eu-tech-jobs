<h2><strong>About the role:</strong></h2>
<p>As a <strong>Data Analyst (Strategy &amp; Analytics)</strong>, you will play a central role in shaping how Parloa uses data to drive growth, efficiency, and strategic clarity. You’ll work across Commercial, Operations, Product, and Tech to turn data into actionable insights that inform decision-making at every level of the company.</p>
<p>This is your opportunity to combine deep analytical work with real business impact by building scalable data products, influencing company-wide strategy, and helping Parloa operate with greater precision and speed.</p>
<h2><strong>Areas of ownership:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>End-to-End Analytics:</strong> Own analytics projects from problem definition and hypothesis building to data modeling, insight generation, and executive communication.</li>
<li><strong>Performance &amp; KPI Frameworks:</strong> Partner with cross-functional leaders to define KPIs, reporting structures, and metrics that drive accountability and focus.</li>
<li><strong>Data Products &amp; Reporting:</strong> Design and maintain scalable dashboards and reporting solutions that provide clear visibility into company performance.</li>
<li><strong>Advanced Analytics &amp; Modeling:</strong> Build forecasting and predictive models to support planning, resource allocation, and strategic decision-making.</li>
<li><strong>Business Insights &amp; Optimization:</strong> Conduct deep-dive analyses to uncover growth opportunities, operational inefficiencies, and product usage patterns.</li>
<li><strong>Data Foundation &amp; Quality:</strong> Collaborate with data and analytics engineering teams to improve data models, ensure data quality, and scale reliable reporting.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Who you are:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in data analytics, business analytics, or strategy analytics, ideally in a high-growth tech, consulting, or finance environment</li>
<li>Strong SQL skills; experience in Python or R for advanced analytics is a plus</li>
<li>Solid understanding of statistical methods and machine learning techniques (e.g. regression, classification, forecasting)</li>
<li>Experience building dashboards and reporting frameworks using tools like Tableau, Looker, or similar</li>
<li>Strong understanding of financial data structures and FP&amp;A processes, as well as commercial and operational metrics</li>
<li>Experience supporting FP&amp;A workflows (e.g., budgeting, forecasting, revenue and KPI tracking) by transforming financial data into well-structured reporting layers aligned with accounting logic and business needs.</li>
<li>Proven ability to design metrics and translate business questions into structured analytical approaches</li>
<li>Confidence in communicating insights to senior stakeholders, including executives</li>
<li>Structured, hypothesis-driven mindset with a pragmatic and hands-on approach&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
