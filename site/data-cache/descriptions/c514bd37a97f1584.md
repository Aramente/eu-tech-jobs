<h2><strong>About the role</strong></h2>
<p>At Wolt, we’re looking for an Account Specialist to join our Retail team - an exciting opportunity for someone with a few years of experience who’s ready to take ownership of their own portfolio and drive partner performance.</p>
<p>In this role, you’ll independently manage and develop a portfolio of retail partners, focusing on improving performance, identifying growth opportunities, and building strong day-to-day relationships. You’ll use data to guide your decisions and play a key role in delivering results for both our partners and Wolt.</p>
<p>If you enjoy working hands-on with partners, taking initiative, and turning insights into action, this could be the role for you.</p>
<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<h3><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Own and manage a portfolio of small and medium-sized retail partners on a day-to-day basis</li>
<li>Monitor partner performance and take action to improve key metrics</li>
<li>Prepare and lead regular check-ins with partners, sharing insights and actionable recommendations</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to grow partner performance and execute on agreed actions</li>
<li>Support partners in using Wolt tools such as campaigns, promotions, and ads effectively</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Marketing, Operations, and Support to solve partner needs</li>
<li>Plan and execute campaigns together with partners to drive orders and engagement</li>
<li>Use data to track results and adjust your approach based on what works</li>
<li>Proactively suggest ways to improve partner visibility and performance on the platform</li>
<li>Contribute to team initiatives and support less experienced team members when relevant</li>
</ul>
<h2>Our humble expectations&nbsp;</h2>
<ul>
<li>1–3 years of experience in account management, sales, or another partner-facing role in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Fluent in Swedish and English, with the ability to communicate clearly and adapt your message to different audiences</li>
<li>Comfortable working independently, while knowing when to seek guidance or escalate when needed</li>
<li>Strong relationship-building skills, with a natural ability to connect with partners and colleagues</li>
<li>Analytical mindset, with the ability to turn data into simple, actionable insights</li>
<li>Structured and detail-oriented, with strong follow-through</li>
<li>Proactive and hands-on - you take initiative and get things done</li>
<li>Collaborative and team-oriented, with a willingness to share knowledge and support others</li>
<li>Experience in retail, e-commerce, or FMCG is a plus</li>
</ul>
