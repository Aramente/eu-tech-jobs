<h1><strong><strong>About the opportunity</strong></strong></h1>
<p>We are strengthening our Account &amp; Customer Data expertise within Banking Operations and are looking for a motivated and experienced Banking Operations Analyst (French speaker) to join us.</p>
<p>Within Banking Operations, we act as the centre of competence for the management and processing of special account types, including probate &amp; estates, accounts with legal representatives or custodians, and basic accounts under the Payment Accounts Act. In addition, we coordinate customer queries related to tax proceedings and GDPR-related information requests, ensuring that cases are handled with care while safeguarding N26 Bank SE from legal and regulatory risks.</p>
<p>As an Analyst on the French market, you will bring expertise and ownership to the team. You will handle complex and sensitive cases independently, act as a point of escalation, and actively contribute to improving our operational processes. You will collaborate closely with internal stakeholders such as Legal, Compliance, Anti-Financial Crime, KYC, and Quality Assurance, as well as external partners and authorities.</p>
<p>This role is ideal for someone who wants to go beyond daily case handling and play an active role in shaping how the team evolves and operates.</p>
