In your first year, you'll help strengthen forecasting discipline, improve opportunity distribution and proposal quality, and create more consistent practices for discovery, scoping, estimation, and statement of work development.</p>
<h2>What you’ll do</h2>
<ul>
<li value="1"><strong><strong class="textBold">Manage</strong></strong> a team of Engagement Managers responsible for consultative Professional Services selling across your region.</li>
<li value="2"><strong><strong class="textBold">Manage</strong></strong> regional Professional Services bookings accountability and maintain a timely, accurate weekly forecast.</li>
<li value="3"><strong><strong class="textBold">Coach</strong></strong> team members on pipeline generation, opportunity prioritization, customer discovery, scoping, estimation, statement of work creation, negotiation, and closing.</li>
<li value="4"><strong><strong class="textBold">Work with</strong></strong> Sales Leadership and Customer Success leaders to align territory priorities, forecast updates, and services strategy.</li>
<li value="5"><strong><strong class="textBold">Distribute</strong></strong> customer opportunities across the team to support balanced geographic or segment coverage.</li>
<li value="6"><strong><strong class="textBold">Develop</strong></strong> strategic proposals that align the Engagement Manager team with Sales and Customer Success organizations.</li>
<li value="7"><strong><strong class="textBold">Partner</strong></strong> with Professional Services Delivery, Ecosystem Partners, and Product teams to define common estimation patterns and proposal standards.</li>
<li value="8"><strong><strong class="textBold">Improve</strong></strong> the Professional Services sales process, including proactive services positioning, scalable offerings, and more consistent selling practices.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What you’ll bring</h2>
<ul>
<li value="1"><strong><strong class="textBold">Experience</strong></strong> managing, guiding, or delivering work in Professional Services or Customer Success environments.</li>
<li value="2"><strong><strong class="textBold">Experience</strong></strong> supporting Professional Services sales, including consultative selling and customer-facing solution discussions.</li>
<li value="3"><strong><strong class="textBold">Ability</strong></strong> to work effectively with Account Executives and other go-to-market partners during the sales process.</li>
<li value="4"><strong><strong class="textBold">Knowledge</strong></strong> of subscription-based business models across SaaS and on-premises offerings.</li>
<li value="5"><strong><strong class="textBold">Capability</strong></strong> to coach teams through complex negotiations, prioritization decisions, and structured deal execution.</li>
<li value="6"><strong><strong class="textBold">Comfort</strong></strong> using GitLab in day-to-day work and staying current with product releases and feature updates.</li>
<li value="7"><strong><strong class="textBold">Skill</strong></strong> in guiding distributed teams through clear communication, accountability, and asynchronous collaboration.</li>
<li value="8"><strong><strong class="textBold">Openness</strong></strong> to candidates with transferable experience from related services, customer-facing, or revenue-focused leadership roles.</li>
