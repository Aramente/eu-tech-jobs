<p>We are seeking an experienced and dynamic Finance Controller - India with a strong US GAAP and technical accounting background to manage our local Indian entity and support group activities. This role will prioritize technical accounting expertise while also playing a pivotal role in building out a Center of Excellence (CoE) and managing the day-to-day operations of the Accounts Receivable (AR), Accounts Payable (AP), and General Ledger (GL) teams. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in managing complex financial operations, ensuring compliance with US GAAP and local regulations, and leading process optimization efforts while developing and mentoring teams.</p>
<p><strong>What You’ll Do :</strong></p>
<p><strong>US GAAP and Entity Management:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Ensure financial operations align with US GAAP, technical accounting standards, and local Indian regulations.</li>
<li>Oversee the preparation and submission of statutory financial statements, tax filings, and local audits.</li>
<li>Manage Monthly TDS and GST filings and oversee annual income tax returns and reconciliations across entities.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Group Support Activities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Support the global finance team by contributing to preparatory tasks for group reporting, ensuring accuracy and timeliness of financial data.</li>
<li>Assist with data collection, reconciliations, and processes that facilitate group reporting and consolidation.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure compliance with corporate policies.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Center of Excellence (CoE) Development:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and develop a Center of Excellence to centralize and optimize key financial processes across AR, AP, GL, and other accounting functions.</li>
<li>Drive process improvements and implement best practices to enhance efficiency and accuracy.</li>
<li>Foster a culture of innovation and continuous improvement within the CoE.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, and General Ledger Management:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Accounts Receivable (AR): Oversee invoicing, collections, and cash application, ensuring efficiency and accuracy.</li>
<li>Accounts Payable (AP): Ensure timely vendor payments, invoice processing, and AP aging management, working closely with procurement and operations.</li>
<li>General Ledger (GL): Manage reconciliations, month-end close processes, and ensure compliance with internal controls.</li>
<li>Resolve billing, payment, and general accounting issues in collaboration with internal stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Team Leadership and Development:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build, mentor, and develop a high-performing finance team across AR, AP, GL, and other accounting functions.</li>
<li>Provide guidance, training, and development opportunities to support team growth and professional advancement.</li>
<li>Foster an inclusive and collaborative work environment that emphasizes accountability and excellence.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Stakeholder Collaboration:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with internal and external stakeholders, including auditors, tax advisors, and banking partners.</li>
<li>Serve as a key contact for finance-related matters for the local entity and broader organization.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What You Bring : </strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>A Chartered Accountant (CA) or equivalent qualification with total of 12+ years experience</li>
<li>4+ years of experience in a Big Four accounting firm, specializing in US GAAP and technical accounting.</li>
<li>At least 3 additional years of managerial experience post-Big Four, managing teams in financial operations and accounting.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of Indian accounting standards, tax regulations, TDS, GST, and income tax compliance.</li>
<li>Advanced understanding of US GAAP and local compliance requirements.</li>
<li>Proven ability to lead and manage AR, AP, GL, and other accounting functions.</li>
<li>Leadership skills with a proven ability to build and manage high-performing teams.</li>
<li>Strong analytical, problem-solving, and attention-to-detail capabilities.</li>
<li>Proficiency in financial systems (SAP preferred) and advanced Excel skills.</li>
<li>Excellent communication, interpersonal, and cross-functional collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Adaptability and resilience in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>This role requires a strong balance of leadership capability and hands-on technical expertise. Hence, The ideal candidate should be comfortable managing teams while actively engaging in core accounting and operational tasks.</li>
<li>Must be flexible to work in overlapping US time zones to effectively collaborate with global stakeholders.</li>
