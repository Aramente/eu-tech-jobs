<h3><strong><span data-contrast="auto">The role</span></strong><span data-ccp-props="{}">&nbsp;</span></h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="232" data-local-id="69430a21f68d">We give you the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies in data operations, cloud computing and infrastructure management. As global data center operations grow, there will be ample opportunities for career progression. Working in the data center directly impacts performance, customer satisfaction and efficiency, with the opportunity to contribute to new data center projects.You’ll collaborate with experts in AI data center development and operations, gaining insights from leaders in the field. This environment fosters innovation, and allows you to work on solutions that exceed industry standards in design and deployment.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="874" data-local-id="47fb9b162a1f">As a&nbsp;<strong data-renderer-mark="true">Field Delivery Engineer,&nbsp;</strong>you will be leading the on-site delivery and deployment of hardware in new locations.You will plan, coordinate, and supervise activities from rack acceptance tests through full operational readiness, ensuring that servers, storage, networking, and supporting systems are installed and functioning according to specifications.</p>
<p>You’re welcome to work in our data center in <strong>Paris</strong> or <strong>Béthune.</strong></p>

<p class="p1"><strong>On-Site Delivery &amp; Installation</strong></p>
<ul class="ul1">
<li class="li1">Supervise racking, cabling, and powering of hardware equipment.</li>
<li class="li1">Ensure configuration, firmware upgrades, and integration with management systems follow standards.</li>
<li class="li1">Work closely with vendors, contractors, and local staff during the installation phase.</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1"><strong>Quality &amp; Readiness</strong></p>
<ul class="ul1">
<li class="li1">Conduct functional checks, acceptance tests, and troubleshooting to confirm all systems operate correctly.</li>
<li class="li1">Document installation steps, asset data, and lessons learned for future rollouts.</li>
<li class="li1">Establish and maintain best practices for safe, efficient hardware deployment.</li>
</ul>
<p class="p1"><strong>Collaboration &amp; Reporting</strong></p>
<ul class="ul1">
<li class="li1">Act as the single point of contact on site for deployment activities, providing clear updates to project managers and leadership.</li>
<li class="li1">Support transition of deployed hardware to operations teams for steady-state management.</li>
<li class="li1">Contribute feedback to improve hardware design and deployment playbooks.</li>
</ul>
