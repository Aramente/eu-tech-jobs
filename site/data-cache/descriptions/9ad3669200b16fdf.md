<h3 id="THE-TEAM-AND-ITS-MISSION" data-local-id="512564254c55" data-renderer-start-pos="1">THE TEAM AND ITS MISSION</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="660" data-local-id="d12285ff5eb7">We are an international team developing a <span data-highlighted="true" data-vc="highlighted-text">UWB</span>-based Real-Time Locating System (RTLS) that powers critical industrial applications. Our solutions support production, logistics, and intralogistics processes through products such as KINEXON Fleet Manager and Forklift Guidance, enabling both autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) and manually operated forklifts.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1015" data-local-id="fcc7704f250c">As part of our full-stack team, you will help scale existing platforms and build new KINEXON products in close collaboration with software and hardware engineers. Your work directly impacts the reliability, performance, and usability of real-time fleet management systems used in industrial environments.</p>

<h3 id="OUR-TECH-STACK" data-local-id="97cd23ee4356" data-renderer-start-pos="1323">OUR TECH STACK</h3>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="1983" data-local-id="5eb1fc81836d">We work with a modern, evolving tech stack and choose technologies based on specific product and performance requirements.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="2107" data-local-id="4d2dbde6cf70"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Backend:</strong> Our core backend services are primarily written in Python. Depending on use case and performance needs, we also use Go and TypeScript.<br><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Frontend:</strong> We build single-page applications using Vue.js for configuration and analytics UIs. For high-performance visualizations, we leverage WebGL-based frameworks.<br><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Infrastructure &amp; DevOps:</strong> Our systems are deployed using containerized, Docker-based workflows in a distributed microservice environment.<br><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Developer Enablement:</strong> We actively use AI-powered coding assistants and automated code review tools to accelerate development, prototyping, refactoring, and quality assurance.</p>
