<p><strong>Job Summary</strong></p>
<p>Working within the logical design team, the silicon logical design engineer is responsible for a wide range of logical design tasks. The team is responsible for delivering the Microarchitecture and RTL design to implement the chip architecture specification for Graphcore Silicon, working closely with other engineers within the Silicon team. The successful candidate will be responsible for helping the team deliver high quality micro-architecture and RTL for Graphcore chips, working within the logical design team and with the broader Silicon team to ensure we meet the company objectives for Silicon delivery.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Responsibilities and Duties</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Integrate IP and subsystems into top-level SoC designs</li>
<li>Develop and maintain build and configuration environments</li>
<li>Perform synthesis, linting, CDC/RDC, and timing checks at the SoC level</li>
<li>Support verification and physical design teams through clean interface hand-offs</li>
<li>Debug and resolve integration-related issues across multiple hierarchies</li>
<li>Contribute to the continuous improvement of integration flows and automation</li>
<li>Producing high quality microarchitecture and other documentation</li>
<li>Ensure good communication between sites to maintain consistent working practises</li>
</ul>
