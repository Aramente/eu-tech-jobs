<p>Role is based remote in the United Kingdom</p>
<p><strong>You will:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a senior case owner for complex, time-sensitive U.K. and international legal requests (subpoenas, search warrants, preservation, exigent/emergency requests).&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Act as queue lead during assigned shifts: monitor volumes, triage, prioritize, and ensure SLAs, quality standards, and escalation paths are met.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Lead end-to-end request processing, including validation of legal process, data gathering (CS Tool, Safety Toolbox, JIRA), packaging, and secure delivery via Kodex.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Handle high-risk matters (imminent harm, child exploitation, self-harm, media/high-profile incidents) in close coordination with Critical Harms, Legal, and Comms.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Lead BPO agents and junior FTEs on tooling, policy application, and operational judgment; provide structured feedback into QA.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Own process and tooling improvements for a subset of LE request flows (e.g., automation, templates, documentation); partner with Privacy Eng, T&amp;S Eng, and Kodex on rollout.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Contribute to metrics and reporting (volumes, turnaround time, error/rework rates) and propose data-backed remediations.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Represent LE Ops in cross-functional working groups (e.g., new tooling, preservation, policy changes) as an operational subject-matter expert.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You have:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years in law enforcement response, trust &amp; safety, legal operations, or adjacent investigative/records roles, including regular handling of legal processes.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong working knowledge of ECPA, content vs. non-content standards, U.K. and international LE request types, and emergency disclosure practices.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to run points on complex, high-stakes cases with limited supervision and strict deadlines.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Proficiency with case-management and data tooling (e.g., Zendesk or similar ticketing, internal data tools, SQL-adjacent workflows) and comfort learning new internal tools quickly.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Excellent written judgment and documentation skills; able to translate legal requirements into clear, auditable operational steps.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Experience mentoring or leading others informally (e.g., acting lead, trainer, or SME for a queue or workflow).</li>
