<p>As an <strong>IT Automation and AI Engineer</strong> at Okta, you will be a key contributor to the team building the platforms and services that drive our internal AI transformation. We are moving beyond simple automation to build sophisticated, "agentic" workflows—utilizing <strong>LangGraph</strong> and <strong>Gemini Enterprise</strong> to create stateful, reasoning-capable systems that support our global workforce.</p>
<p>This is a hands-on role for a professional engineer who excels at writing production-grade Python and is eager to bridge the gap between AI research and enterprise-scale applications. You will own the implementation of core features, orchestrating complex LLM interactions while ensuring performance, security, and reliability.</p>
<h3><strong>What You’ll Be Doing</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Build Agentic Workflows:</strong> Develop and maintain stateful multi-agent systems using <strong>LangGraph</strong> to solve complex, non-linear business tasks.</li>
<li><strong>Gemini Integration:</strong> Implement and optimize LLM interactions using <strong>Gemini Enterprise</strong>, focusing on prompt engineering, function calling, and structured outputs.</li>
<li><strong>State &amp; Memory Management:</strong> Design and manage agent state, persistence, and "human-in-the-loop" checkpoints to ensure reliable automated decision-making.</li>
<li><strong>Backend Development:</strong> Write high-quality, production-ready <strong>Python</strong> code to build the APIs and services that power our internal AI ecosystem.</li>
<li><strong>RAG &amp; Grounding:</strong> Work on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines to ensure agents have access to accurate, real-time enterprise context.</li>
<li><strong>Infrastructure &amp; Deployment:</strong> Deploy and manage services on <strong>GCP</strong> using containerized environments (Docker/Kubernetes) and Infrastructure as Code (Terraform).</li>
<li><strong>Operational Excellence:</strong> Promote engineering best practices around testing AI logic, security, and CI/CD to ensure services are production-ready.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What You’ll Bring to the Role</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Professional Python Core:</strong> 2–4 years of software engineering experience with a deep command of Python and its asynchronous ecosystem.</li>
<li><strong>AI Framework Mastery:</strong> Hands-on experience with <strong>LangGraph</strong> (or similar frameworks like LangChain). You understand how to build cycles, nodes, and conditional edges in a stateful application.</li>
<li><strong>LLM Practicality:</strong> Experience working with frontier models like <strong>Gemini</strong> or GPT-4. You know how to manage prompt versioning, token constraints, and model behavior.</li>
<li><strong>System Design Foundations:</strong> Solid understanding of distributed systems, cloud-native architectures, and building/consuming RESTful APIs.</li>
<li><strong>Problem-Solving Autonomy:</strong> The ability to take a defined workflow or prototype and drive it to production with minimal oversight, knowing when to escalate complex trade-offs.</li>
<li><strong>Collaborative Spirit:</strong> Strong communication skills and a desire to work across team boundaries to deliver cohesive, high-impact AI solutions.</li>
</ul>
