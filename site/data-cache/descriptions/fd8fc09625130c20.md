<p><strong>Why you should join the Adyen Account Management team</strong></p>
<p>At Adyen, the Account Management team is critical to the growth and success of our business, as well as expanding the Adyen brand across the globe. Being part of our Account Management team, you’ll manage relationships with innovative merchants. You’ll have the opportunity to work on cross-functional initiatives and projects in a fast-paced, highly entrepreneurial environment with high autonomy.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We are looking for a proactive and adaptable Account Manager to join our team in Berlin. You are excited to work with a global, multicultural team in a collaborative manner and help your team succeed. <p><strong>Account Manager</strong></p>
<p>Our Account Managers are encouraged to challenge each other and sharpen ideas as a team, because winning is more important than ego. In this role, you’ll get your energy by working on a portfolio of accounts within the Platforms vertical.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What You’ll Do:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Relationship management</strong>: Responsible for ramping and growing customers with a focus on commercial growth, customer satisfaction, and reducing churn.</li>
<li><strong>Commercial acumen:</strong> Drive commercial discussions for your accounts, while striving to meet strategic and financial targets.</li>
<li><strong>Project management:</strong>&nbsp;Prioritize and manage short and long term projects with varying timelines across multiple accounts, leveraging internal support teams where relevant.</li>
<li><strong>Feedback</strong>: Serve as a feedback loop for your customers internally.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who You Are:</strong></p>
