## What you will be doing

- Ensure timely completion of all compliance-related activities in accordance with statutory and regulatory timelines.
- Manage statutory and global audits, including preparation and review of financial statements as per IFRS and IND AS.
- Coordinate with Big-4 auditors (global teams) to ensure timely completion and closure of audits, including resolution of audit queries.
- Oversee month-end, quarter-end, and year-end closing processes to ensure accurate and timely financial reporting.
- Ensure compliance with applicable accounting standards, regulatory requirements, and internal control frameworks.
- Review, monitor, and strengthen internal controls to mitigate financial and compliance risks.
- Act as the primary point of contact for auditors, regulators, and internal stakeholders on audit and compliance matters.
- Prepare cash flow forecasts related to tax payments and share projections with the treasury team.
- Monitor changes in accounting standards, statutory regulations, and corporate policies, and assess their impact on financial reporting.
- Support management with financial analysis, audit findings, and recommendations for process improvements.
- Ensure timely closure of audit observations and implementation of corrective action plans.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to improve financial processes and ensure governance standards are met.
- Maintain documentation and records to support audits, compliance reviews, and internal controls.
- Should have prior experience of handling global teams and clients.
