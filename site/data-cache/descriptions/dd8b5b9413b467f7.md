**About Pyka**

Pyka’s goal is to provide society with a new form of safe, clean, and cost-effective transportation enabled by autonomous electric aviation.

To get there, we’re taking a different approach than most. We're applying our technologies to every industry where autonomous electric aircraft can be useful, starting with the highest value and most dangerous jobs. In doing so, we're building game-changing products manufactured at scale, while perfecting the safety, reliability, and capabilities of our autonomy engine and electric propulsion systems.

We design, develop and manufacture an ecosystem of technologies including proprietary flight control software, avionics, high power density motors, motor controllers, batteries, and custom carbon-fiber composite airframes.

Today, we supply autonomous electric aircraft for cargo transport and crop protection to real-world customers across four separate continents and have secured industry-first regulatory approvals from the FAA. Our cargo aircraft enables remote connectivity, enhances express delivery networks, and ensures fast and reliable shipping of critical supplies to areas in need. Our crop protection aircraft offers agricultural services providers and farmers an autonomous tool to make aerial application safer, more precise, and less harmful to surrounding environments. Both vehicles are highly economical to operate, easy to deploy, and significantly reduce C02 emissions in their respective industries.

What you work on at Pyka makes people’s lives better now and brings the future of electric aviation one step closer each day.

**Role Overview**

Pyka is looking for a Finance and Business Manager to lead the financial, administrative, and operational buildout of our Brazilian subsidiary. This is not a traditional back-office finance role as you will be hands-on across customs and import logistics, regulatory compliance, legal project management, and while supporting our local operations team as we scale Pelican aircraft deployments across Brazil and potentially South America.

You will serve as the primary financial point person in Brazil, working closely with Pyka’s US-based leadership and external advisors (legal counsel, accountants, customs brokers) to ensure the subsidiary runs smoothly and compliantly. This is a high-autonomy, high-impact position ideal for someone who thrives in startup environments where the scope of the job extends well beyond the title.

**This role is based in Brazil**. Fluent English is required, as you will communicate daily with Pyka’s headquarters in the San Francisco Bay Area.

## What You’ll Do

*Finance & Accounting*- Oversee treasury activities, managing cash position, liquidity planning and banking relationships
- Own the day-to-day financial management of Pyka Brasil, including budgeting, cash flow forecasting, accounts payable/receivable, and monthly close processes (with our external accountant)
- Drive improvements in financial process, reporting and systems; strengthen internal controls to ensure compliance with company policies.
- Coordinate with external accounting firms and auditors to ensure accurate bookkeeping and tax compliance under Brazilian regulations
- Manage transfer pricing documentation and intercompany agreements in compliance with Brazilian Law 14,596/2023 and OECD guidelines
- Support annual budgeting, re-forecasting, and scenario analysis for the Brazil operation
*Imports, Customs & Supply Chain Operations*- Lead and coordinate the import process for Pelican aircraft, battery packs, spare parts, and related equipment, working with customs brokers and freight forwarders
- Navigate Brazilian customs frameworks including NCM classification, LETEC incentives, Ex-Tarifário applications, and Siscomex/DUIMP procedures
- Develop and maintain the import strategy to minimize duties, optimize logistics costs, and ensure regulatory compliance
- Support the buildout of a spare parts fulfillment network across key operational regions in Brazil
*Legal & Regulatory Project Management*- Serve as the internal project manager for legal workstreams, coordinating with external law firms on corporate governance, labor law (CLT), contracts, and regulatory matters
- Manage the drafting, review, and execution of intercompany agreements, customer contracts, vendor agreements, and employment-related documents
- Ensure compliance with Brazilian workplace regulations (NR-1, PGR, EPI management, employee registries) and liaise with labor consultants as needed
*Operational Support & Local Administration*- Act as the operational backbone of Pyka Brasil, managing vendor relationships, office/warehouse logistics, and administrative processes
- Identify and implement local tools, systems, and processes to scale the Brazil operation efficiently
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Pyka’s flight operations, supply chain, and engineering teams to support field deployments
