**About the role**  
We’re looking for a seasoned Brand Design Lead to guide and co-create with our Graphic Design Team. This role sits between hands-on design and leadership. As a Design Lead, you’ll help designers solve problems, develop stronger concepts, and deliver high-quality work. You’ll act as a design partner and mentor, as well as an art director, while also contributing creatively when needed. You’ll be responsible for design quality, clarity, and consistency while ensuring our visual output meets both brand and business goals.

## Main Responsibilities

- **Orchestrate Visual Systems:** Direct the creation and maintenance of design systems, style guides, and brand book guidelines to ensure cohesive branding across all touchpoints.
- **Define the Standard:** Establish a benchmark for visual excellence that challenges the team and pushes our brand identities to stand out in a global market.
- **Cultivate Creative Talent:** Act as a hands-on mentor, helping designers navigate complex briefs, sharpen their conceptual thinking, and take full ownership of their craft.
- **The Strategic Bridge:** Act as the vital link between Brand, Marketing & Product. You will collaborate closely with Brand leads to ensure our marketing stays grounded in product reality and Product Leads to ensure the brand’s soul is felt within the interface.
- **Merge Logic with Aesthetic:** Use design as a strategic tool to simplify technical complexity, making cybersecurity feel accessible, empowering and human, rather than intimidating.
- **Advocate for the Why:** Translate creative directions into business value when collaborating with stakeholders, ensuring our design choices align with broader company growth.
