Are you excited about driving growth through data-powered marketing insights? This is your chance to shape how data drives acquisition, retention, and brand performance at scale.

## If you want to:

- Translate marketing performance data into insights that drive smarter decisions across acquisition, retention, lifecycle, and brand campaigns.
- Lead the business requirements process: clarify what should be measured, why it matters, and how it links to growth and efficiency.
- Partner with Marketing Leadership to prioritize analytical needs and ensure insights are tied to strategy and impact.
- Work closely with Data Analysts and Data Scientists — shaping what questions to answer, aligning models with business context, and turning outputs into actionable recommendations.
- Analyze customer journeys, funnels, and campaign ROI to uncover growth opportunities, optimize spend, and improve retention.
- Building/collabing incrementality studies and marketing mix modeling, ensuring findings lead to real budget and strategy decisions. (considered as  a plus)

- Proven experience as a business or marketing analyst in SaaS, subscription, or digital businesses.

- Excellent skills in requirement gathering, stakeholder management, and consultative communication — you make the ambiguous clear.
