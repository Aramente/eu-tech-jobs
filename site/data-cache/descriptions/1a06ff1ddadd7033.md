**At Nord Security, we’re creating a safer cyber future.**

We help people and businesses take back control of their online security, privacy, and data. From VPNs to password managers, threat intelligence to eSIMs for travel—our teams turn complex problems into solutions trusted by millions worldwide.

Life is online. In this role, you’ll help people own it.

## Main Responsibilities

- Automate recurring finance data processes (reconciliation, reporting, etc.) to reduce manual work and free capacity for roadmap projects;
- Build and maintain DBT models;
- Own data quality and controls: implement automated checks (completeness, accuracy etc.), anomaly detection, and monitoring/alerting;
- Work closely with Data Engineers, Finance, and Accounting teams to gather data requirements, establish validation rules, and align processes across systems;
- Generate regular reports, documents resolutions, and maintain process documentation for audit and team reference;
- Integrate new product data into existing data flows.

## Core Requirements

- Excellent and proven skills in using SQL, capable of writing easily understandable, maintainable, and efficient SQL commands;
- Proven experience in a Data Analyst role;
- Proficiency in data modeling and skills in data transformation techniques;
- Good understanding of the DBT tool;
- Excellent communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders from different teams.
