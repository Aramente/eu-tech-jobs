**Threat Protection**

With viruses, Trojans, worms, spyware, and other malware being safe online can be a daunting experience. Our threat protection team wants to fix these problems. Creating a safe online world by developing software that protects our customers. Scalable code that will deal with volume, security, distributed and high-level architecture, all this built from scratch. That sounds like the challenge you were looking for, read on!

## Main Responsibilities

- Developing distributed microservices that protect millions of users against malware;
- Focus on the delivery of high-quality code, where scalability and security come first;
- Collaborate with a team of dedicated developers where together you build and maintain our services in Golang;
- Respectfully taking ownership of architecture, design, development, and deployment initiatives.

## Core Requirements

- Experience working with distributed technologies and NoSQL databases, ideally with Cassandra or Kafka;
- System design is nothing new to you, and you know what patterns to use when and why;
- Passionate about building anti-virus and creating a safer online experience where your work will have a direct impact;
- Passionate about backend architecture and technologies, open to speaking with anyone; if you can create scalable and stable code we want to speak with you;
- A collaborative mindset where you love to discuss, rethink, and redesign your ideas that create shared success within your team and the company.
