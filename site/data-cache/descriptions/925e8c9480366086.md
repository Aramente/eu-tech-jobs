<p id="isPasted"><strong>Job Summary:<br></strong></p>
<p>The Field Service Technician (NETA 4) is a project supervising technician responsible for performing complex investigations, tests, measurements, and evaluations. The role involves recommending corrective actions, interacting with engineers and managers, and testing for de-energized locked out/tagged out equipment. The technician is also responsible for setting up and removing test equipment, understanding hazardous electrical energy procedures, record keeping, safety of others, switching, evaluations, safety grounding, interacting with other skills and operations, writing and reviewing reports, and performing troubleshooting. The technician can work independently.</p>
<p><strong>Supervisory Responsibilities:<br></strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Monitors project progress and assigned NETA field technicians. Responsible for project success from start to finish including the final report.</li>
<li>Supervises large crews and ensures efficient and safe work practices.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Duties/Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<p>Complex Tests and Measurements</p>
<ul>
<li>Perform complex electrical tests and measurements.</li>
<li>Collect and record data accurately.</li>
</ul>
<p>Project Management</p>
<ul>

<p>Report Writing</p>
