<div class="content-intro"><p><strong>COMPANY OVERVIEW</strong></p>
<p>KKR is a leading global investment firm that offers alternative asset management as well as capital markets and insurance solutions. KKR aims to generate attractive investment returns by following a patient and disciplined investment approach, employing world-class people, and supporting growth in its portfolio companies and communities. KKR sponsors investment funds that invest in private equity, credit and real assets and has strategic partners that manage hedge funds. KKR’s insurance subsidiaries offer retirement, life and reinsurance products under the management of Global Atlantic Financial Group. References to KKR’s investments may include the activities of its sponsored funds and insurance subsidiaries.</p></div><p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3 data-section-id="1erz3en" data-start="208" data-end="228">POSITION OVERVIEW</h3>
<p data-start="230" data-end="608">KKR is building an enterprise Data Operations group to collect, manage, and harness the power of data across our diverse business activities. The Data Operations Center of Excellence (CoE) is a cross-functional team dedicated to defining and driving KKR’s enterprise data strategy while providing the operating leverage required to bring these strategies and frameworks to life.</p>
<p data-start="610" data-end="941">We are hiring an <strong data-start="627" data-end="661">Assistant Vice President (AVP)</strong> within the Data Operations CoE to lead Finance Reporting &amp; Data. This individual will drive a transformation program to consolidate financial reporting across multiple internal sources and external fund administrators, creating a unified and scalable reporting model for Finance.</p>
<p data-start="943" data-end="1478">The <strong data-start="947" data-end="981">Assistant Vice President (AVP)</strong> will balance strategic leadership with hands-on execution, shaping the roadmap for Finance reporting while designing, implementing, and operating the processes and solutions required to support ongoing BAU reporting. The role requires deep knowledge of finance and fund reporting, strong technical fluency with modern reporting platforms and data tools, and the ability to partner with stakeholders across Finance, Operations, and Technology to deliver timely, accurate, and actionable reporting.</p>
<h3 data-section-id="132jz6r" data-start="1485" data-end="1514">ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</h3>
<ul>
<li><strong data-start="1516" data-end="1546">Transformation Leadership:</strong> Drive a firmwide program to consolidate and modernize financial reporting across internal sources and fund administrators.</li>
<li><strong data-start="1671" data-end="1694">Reporting Delivery:</strong> Own the design, build, and execution of financial reporting processes and outputs (management, investor, and regulatory), ensuring accuracy, timeliness, and consistency.</li>
<li><strong data-start="1866" data-end="1891">Hands-On Solutioning:</strong> Design reporting models, dashboards, and data pipelines leveraging Snowflake and BI/reporting platforms.</li>
<li><strong data-start="1998" data-end="2016">BAU Oversight:</strong> Oversee day-to-day Finance reporting processes, ensuring robust controls, efficiency, and scalability.</li>
<li><strong data-start="2121" data-end="2149">Stakeholder Partnership:</strong> Collaborate with Finance, Operations, and Technology to capture requirements, align priorities, and deliver high-quality reporting outputs.</li>
<li><strong data-start="2291" data-end="2315">Process Improvement:</strong> Identify opportunities to automate reporting, reduce manual intervention, and standardize outputs across Finance.</li>
<li><strong data-start="2431" data-end="2452">Data Integration:</strong> Oversee the integration of administrator and third-party data into core Finance reporting models to enable consolidated views.</li>
<li><strong data-start="2581" data-end="2608">Metrics &amp; Transparency:</strong> Define and publish KPIs on reporting timeliness, accuracy, and process efficiency for senior stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
