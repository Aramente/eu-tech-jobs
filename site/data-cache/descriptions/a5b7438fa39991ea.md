<p><strong>About this team and role:</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Search is the most essential functionality in any browser, and it’s evolving by the day, with new interaction paradigms, AI-powered answers, and agentic browsing. The Firefox Desktop Search team owns one of Mozilla’s most critical product areas — delivering high-quality, privacy-respecting search experiences that help solve key user problems, differentiate Firefox, and drive long-term business value.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This role will help shape Firefox’s strategy in an era where AI is redefining how people search, learn, and get things done online.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As a Senior Product Manager, you will define and drive the strategy and execution for search within the Firefox Desktop browser across Windows, macOS, and Linux. You will help define how Firefox integrates traditional web search with emerging AI capabilities, while upholding Mozilla’s leadership in privacy and user agency. You will operate at the intersection of product experience, revenue growth, and partner strategy to ensure Firefox delivers differentiated, future-facing search experiences.</span></p>
