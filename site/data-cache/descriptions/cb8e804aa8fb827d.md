<div class="content-intro"><h2><strong>Be Part of Building the Future</strong></h2>
<p>Dremio is the unified lakehouse platform for self-service analytics and AI, serving hundreds of global enterprises, including Maersk, Amazon, Regeneron, NetApp, and S&amp;P Global. Customers rely on Dremio for cloud, hybrid, and on-prem lakehouses to power their data mesh, data warehouse migration, data virtualization, and unified data access use cases. Based on open source technologies, including Apache Iceberg and Apache Arrow, Dremio provides an open lakehouse architecture enabling the fastest time to insight and platform flexibility at a fraction of the cost.&nbsp; Learn more at&nbsp;<a class="c-link" href="http://www.dremio.com/" target="\_blank" data-stringify-link="http://www.Dremio.com" data-sk="tooltip\_parent">www.dremio.com.</a></p></div><h3><strong>About the Role</strong></h3>
<p>Dremio's GTM engine is entering its next phase. The foundational systems exist (Marketo, analytics infrastructure, lifecycle programs) but the opportunity in front of us is to make them dramatically smarter, faster, and more autonomous.</p>
<p>As Marketing Operations Manager - AI &amp; Automation, you will own the marketing operations stack end-to-end: email programs, automation workflows, marketing analytics, and most critically, the design and deployment of AI agents that automate repetitive marketing tasks using our agent development platform. Think of this as a product management role for digital selling and the marketing infrastructure that supports it. Your "product" is the system that makes every marketer at Dremio 10x more productive.</p>
<p>You're the person who sees a manual, three-step process and immediately asks: "Why isn't an agent doing this?" You prototype fast, measure everything, and have an opinion on what the data is telling you, not just what it says.</p>
<p>This role reports to the Chief Marketing Officer and partners closely with demand gen, product marketing, developer relations, and sales operations.</p>
<h3><strong>What You'll Be Doing</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Marketing Operations &amp; Email</strong></p>
<p>Own the Marketo instance, email lifecycle programs, lead scoring, and data hygiene. You'll design and optimize the workflows that move prospects from first touch to qualified pipeline — and you'll treat every workflow as a candidate for automation.</p>
<p><strong>Marketing Analytics&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Build and maintain the reporting infrastructure that tells marketing what's working and what isn't. This means helping to drive self service via AI across the team and ensuring we get all of the data we need into our data lake.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>AI Agent Building &amp; Automation</strong></p>
<p>Design, build, and deploy AI-powered workflows using agents and our n8n platform, eliminate manual marketing tasks. Examples include automated lead enrichment and routing, email creation, and lead scoring agents. You'll identify the highest-leverage automation opportunities, prototype solutions, and scale the ones that work.</p>
<p><strong>Cross-Functional Systems Thinking</strong></p>
<p>Partner with sales ops and out BDR lead on lead handoff, lead quality, and data integrity. You're the connective tissue that makes the marketing machine run.</p>
<h3><strong>What We're Looking For</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in marketing operations, growth operations, or a hybrid ops/analytics role, ideally in B2B SaaS or a technical product environment</li>
<li>Deep hands-on experience with Marketo (or comparable marketing automation platform), including campaign builds, lead scoring, nurture programs, and data management</li>
<li>Strong analytical foundation — you understand how to work with and analyst data</li>
<li>Demonstrated experience building automations or AI-powered workflows (n8n, Zapier, Make, or similar); n8n experience is a strong plus</li>
<li>A product-management mindset applied to marketing systems. You think in terms of user experience, prioritization, and iterative improvement</li>
<li>Financial or strategic thinking skills. You understand unit economics, can model scenarios, and tie marketing activity to business outcomes</li>
<li>Bias for action with intellectual curiosity. You ship fast, learn from the data, and iterate</li>
<li>Strong written and verbal communication.You can explain a technical system to a non-technical stakeholder clearly</li>
<li>Comfort with ambiguity and a fast-moving environment; startup or high-growth experience preferred</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Nice to Have</strong></h3>
<ul>
