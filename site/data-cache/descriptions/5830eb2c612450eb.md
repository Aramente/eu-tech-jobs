<h3><strong>What you'll do</strong></h3>
<p>We are seeking a Senior/Lead Product Manager to join our Patient Health Companion team, focusing on creating a comprehensive health companion experience. Reporting to the VP Patient Product, you will play a pivotal role in transforming Doctolib from a transactional appointment booking platform into a continuous health partner for our users.</p>
<p>As a Lead Product Manager, your mission will be to develop and optimize a personalized health companion experience that helps users achieve their health goals through targeted content, tracking tools, and goal-based journeys. You'll define the vision for how users discover, engage with, and benefit from companion features, starting with our Parents vertical and expanding to other health domains.</p>
<p>Your responsibilities will include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Own and define part of the product vision and strategy for the Health Companion experience, focusing on personalized content delivery, health tracking, goal-based frameworks, and user engagement</li>
<li>Ship high-impact features that drive activation, engagement, and retention across Content, Trackers, and goal-based frameworks journeys, measured by WAU, PMF, and retention metrics</li>
<li>Leverage AI/ML to deliver personalized experiences, from recommendation systems (content, tools) to LLM-powered features, balancing user value with technical feasibility</li>
<li>Design and iterate on a personalization engine that adapts content, trackers, and journeys to user health profiles, goals, and behaviors, driving measurable improvements in engagement and health outcomes</li>
<li>Navigate complex trade-offs between user engagement, medical accuracy, regulatory compliance, and business goals, making data-informed decisions with incomplete information</li>
<li>Partner with content, design, and engineering teams to create engaging, medically-accurate health content in various formats (stories, articles, interactive tools)</li>
<li>Define and implement measurement frameworks to track user engagement, goal achievement, and health outcomes</li>
<li>Work with cross-functional teams (design, engineering, content, medical, marketing) to deliver a seamless, integrated health companion experience</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Who you are</strong></h3>
<p>If you meet most of the requirements below and believe this opportunity aligns with your experience and ambitions, we encourage you to apply!</p>
<ul>
<li>Have 6+ years of experience in Product Management, with direct ownership of consumer-facing products: leading discoveries, writing specifications, launching features, measuring impact, and iterating, preferably in health tech or consumer apps with high engagement</li>
<li>Have shipped mobile-first consumer products with strong engagement</li>
<li>Experience in health, parenting, wellness, or lifestyle domains is a plus but not mandatory</li>
<li>Have built retention loops and habit-forming features that demonstrably improved retention and that drive behavior change</li>
<li>Are comfortable working with content management systems, analytics platforms, and personalization engines</li>
<li>Have a highly structured approach to problem-solving, decision-making, and execution, with the ability to prioritize efficiently while managing stakeholder expectations</li>
<li>Have scaled consumer products across multiple markets, navigating complex user needs and regulatory environments</li>
<li>Demonstrate passion for health and wellness, with understanding of how digital tools can improve health outcomes</li>
<li>Have experience in AI-powered products, from integrating machine learning to having large language models in production</li>
<li>Are fluent in English &amp; French (C1 level minimum) to engage with diverse teams and markets</li>
<li>Are ambitious to transform healthcare with an entrepreneurial mindset</li>
</ul>
