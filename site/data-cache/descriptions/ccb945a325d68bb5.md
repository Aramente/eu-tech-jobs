## In this role, you’ll be responsible for:

- Troubleshooting, diagnosing, and repairing electrical issues in a residential environment
- Installing electrical equipment such as generators, panels, and car chargers
- Updating miscellaneous wiring, switches, lighting, outlets, and performing service upgrades
- Evaluating homes and offices for electrical safety and make safety recommendations to customer as needed.
- Maintaining proper stock, parts, tools, and safety equipment in the vehicle
- Traveling to job sites in your service area, and working with dispatchers to ensure the schedule is maintained and delays are properly communicated to the customer

## Qualifications:

- 4 or more years of Residential Electrical Experience
- Generac generator experience a plus.
- Familiarity with the Nexstar Service System is a plus.
- Proficiency in using Service Titan or similar field management software a plus.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to diagnose technical issues accurately.
- Strong communication skills to interact with customers professionally and courteously.
- Dedication to safety, adhering to safety guidelines and promoting a safe work environment.
