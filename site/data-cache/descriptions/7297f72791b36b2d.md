<h2>What you will be doing</h2>
<p>As an ML Software Engineer with a focus on low-level and CUDA-based optimizations, you will play a key role in shaping the design, performance, and scalability of Lightricks’ machine learning inference systems. You’ll work on deeply technical challenges at the intersection of GPU acceleration, systems architecture, and ML deployment.<br>Your expertise in CUDA, C/C++, and performance tuning will be crucial in enhancing runtime efficiency across heterogeneous computing environments. You’ll collaborate with designers, researchers, and backend engineers to build production-grade ML pipelines that are optimized for latency, throughput, and memory use, contributing directly to the infrastructure powering Lightricks' next-generation AI products.<br>This role is ideal for an engineer with strong systems-level thinking, deep familiarity with GPU internals, and a passion for pushing the boundaries of performance and efficiency in machine learning infrastructure.</p>
