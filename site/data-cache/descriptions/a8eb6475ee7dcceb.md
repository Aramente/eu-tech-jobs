<p>The Product Support Specialist, International &amp; Government Requests role will focus on responding to sensitive government and regulatory requests, collaborating with stakeholders across Public Policy, Legal, and safety leadership, and improving our tooling, processes, and workflows designed to scale Roblox’s compliance with regulatory requirements. <p><strong>Please note:</strong> This role may review graphic, controversial, and sometimes offensive content in line with Roblox’s policies. This role will also require some on-call or shift support.</p>
<p>You will report to the Moderation Product Support team manager.</p>
<p><strong>You will</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as policy enforcement subject matter expert and own high-visibility regulatory escalations end-to-end, analyzing and assessing content against Roblox community standards and internal procedure guidelines</li>
<li>Guide a large team of Roblox Compliance agents and staff, ensuring exceptional customer experience and meeting performance metrics</li>
<li>Communicate effectively with internal customers (leadership up to Roblox c-suite) and cross-functional stakeholders, performing comprehensive analysis of ambiguous incidents and make business-critical decisions under time constraints;</li>
<li>Maintain professionalism, confidentiality, and good judgement when handling government requests which may require direct correspondence with regulators;</li>
<li>Manage, maintain, and develop a highly effective&nbsp; processes that meets or exceeds relevant service level agreements;</li>
<li>Coordinate with cross-functional partners and a large team of Roblox moderation agents and their respective staff;</li>
<li>Implement and maintain standard operating procedures for moderation processes;</li>
<li>Analyze operational data and conduct root cause analysis to identify trends, bottlenecks, and optimization opportunities, crafting recommendations for improvement;</li>
<li>Participate in an on-call or shift rotating basis to ensure our team has 24x7 coverage, may include weekends.</li>
</ul>
