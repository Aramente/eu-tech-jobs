<div class="content-intro"><p>From Fivetran’s founding until now, our mission has remained the same: to make access to data as simple and reliable as electricity. With Fivetran, customer data arrives in their warehouses, canonical and ready to query, with no engineering or maintenance required. We’re proud that more organizations continue to leverage our technology every day to become truly data-driven.</p></div><p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>About the Role</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Fivetran’s documentation receives rave reviews from our customers—it is a core part of our product. The Technical Communications Team owns technical documentation and in-product text. Our globally distributed team of technical writers collaborates with Product Managers, Engineers, Designers, and Technical Support to produce world-class documentation and UI copy that helps our users succeed.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">As a Staff Technical writer, your mission is to make Fivetran docs a shining beacon of documentation excellence. You will write and edit enterprise documentation that empowers our customers to use Fivetran and understand how our database and data lake products work. You’ll collaborate with engineers, product managers, support engineers, and other technical writers. You join a friendly team of technical writers distributed among our international offices.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">This is a full-time position based out of our Bangalore office. Our hybrid work model offers a blend of remote flexibility and in-person collaboration, including two days in the office each week to connect and build as a team.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">What You’ll Do</span>&nbsp;</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Create and maintain clear, accurate product documentation and release notes that help customers succeed.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Review and refine customer-facing content, including error messages and UI text.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Use a docs-as-code approach to co-author documentation with engineers, product managers, and contributors across the company.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Improve existing documentation by ensuring technical accuracy, clarity, and consistency.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Collaborate with a global writing team to define and evolve documentation standards, style, and terminology.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Partner with engineers, SMEs, and cross-functional teams to deliver best-in-class technical documentation.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Contribute to style guides, templates, and other shared resources that raise the bar for all company content.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Act as a writing resource for other teams, helping improve content quality across the organization.</span></li>
</ul>

<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Deep technical knowledge:</strong> Experience with complex database and data warehouse technologies, cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), data pipelines (ETL/ELT), and replication techniques.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Scripting, automation, and AI proficiency: </strong>Skilled at leveraging scripting, automation, and AI tools to streamline documentation workflows and enhance technical content delivery.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Exceptional communication skills</strong>: Proven ability to craft clear, concise, and accurate documentation; strong technical editing abilities; impeccable grammar; and consistent application of style guides.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Documentation leadership:</strong> Expertise in content planning, creation, and review processes, with a track record of implementing best practices that elevate documentation quality.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Project management:</strong> Experience leading small to medium-sized documentation projects, mentoring writers, and balancing multiple initiatives in cross-functional environments.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Collaboration and customer focus:</strong> Strong history of working with engineers, writers, and globally distributed teams, translating customer needs into improved documentation that drives customer success.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Strong organizational skills with a growth mindset: </strong>Excellent planning and prioritization, sound judgment and decision-making, adaptability to shifting priorities, and a can-do attitude with a drive for high-quality results.</span></li>
