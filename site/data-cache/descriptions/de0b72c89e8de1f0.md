<p>Make Wayve the experience that defines your career!&nbsp;&nbsp;</p></div><h1><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>The role</strong></span></h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">As a Cloud Site Reliability Engineer at Wayve, you will build and scale the reliability foundations of our AI cloud platform. This includes our Model Development Platform (powering end-to-end model development from raw data to on-road experimentation) and our GPU Compute platform (large-scale, multi-tenant GPU fleets and scheduling systems driving model training and inference at scale).</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This is a founding Cloud SRE role. You won’t inherit a mature SRE function, you’ll help create it. You will define the frameworks, automation, and operational standards that ensure our model development infrastructure, distributed systems, and large compute clusters operate predictably, efficiently, and at scale.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This role sits at the intersection of AI research, large-scale cloud infrastructure, and production operations. Your work will directly enable faster model training, reliable experimentation, and scalable AI deployment by ensuring our cloud infrastructure is resilient and performant.</span></p>
<h1><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Key responsibilities</strong></span></h1>
<h3><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Reliability &amp; Platform Ownership</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Own the reliability, availability, and performance of the Model Dev Platform and GPU Compute environments.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Define and operationalise SLOs, SLIs, and error budgets across platform services.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Improve capacity planning, scaling strategies, and resource efficiency across large GPU-backed clusters.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Partner with ML, platform, and software teams to establish clear production readiness standards.</span><br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Incident Response &amp; On-Call</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Participate in a 24/7 on-call rotation as first-line response for cloud and cluster-related incidents.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Lead incident triage, escalation, communications, and root cause analysis.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Translate post-incident learning into durable architectural or automation improvements.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Continuously reduce alert noise and recurring operational burden.</span><br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Observability &amp; Operational Excellence</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Design and operate monitoring, logging, tracing, and alerting systems that enable rapid detection and recovery.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Build dashboards that reflect real user-centric platform health (not just infrastructure metrics).</span><br><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Improve deployment safety through better change management, validation, and rollback mechanisms.</span><br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>Automation &amp; Tooling</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Build automation for cluster operations, training workflows, remediation, and scaling tasks.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Implement self-healing patterns and resilient recovery workflows.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Harden CI/CD and release processes to improve deployment safety and velocity.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Support infrastructure-as-code and policy-driven guardrails to ensure secure, reliable cloud environments.</span></li>
</ul>
