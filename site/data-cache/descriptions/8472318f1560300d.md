<p><strong>Project Overview</strong></p>
<p>We are seeking independent Product Matching Specialists and data experts to provide their expertise for an e-commerce platform enrichment project. As digital storefronts and influencer platforms evolve, their usability depends entirely on high-quality product matching and accurate categorization. Quality is our top priority. The objective of this project is to evaluate digital content, accurately match new products to existing ones, and create new product entries when needed to ensure the highest standards of data integrity.</p>
<h3><strong>Project Deliverables &amp; Scope</strong></h3>
<p>Operate autonomously to process digital content and provide structured data tagging. Expected deliverables include:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Product Data Enrichment:</strong> Accurately matching, creating, or dismissing product "pins" on the platform based on provided project guidelines.</li>
<li><strong>Quality Assurance Auditing:</strong> Reviewing and updating digital product content to ensure it strictly meets standards for accuracy, clarity, and relevance.</li>
<li><strong>Edge Case Documentation:</strong> Proactively identifying, documenting, and flagging data inconsistencies, anomalies, or edge cases within the platform's ecosystem.</li>
<li><strong>Taxonomy &amp; Logic Feedback:</strong> Providing structured suggestions and expert feedback to improve the platform's overall tagging logic, taxonomy, and data labeling workflows.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Required Expertise &amp; Background</strong></h3>
<p>To successfully fulfill the deliverables of this project, Contractors must possess sharp attention to detail and a quality-first mindset (catching what others miss).</p>
<p><strong>Core competencies and requirements include:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Demonstrable speed and accuracy with data entry, product tagging, or content moderation.</li>
<li>Comfort and proficiency working with digital content, specifically evaluating and matching complex product images.</li>
<li>The ability to independently follow complex guidelines and apply nuanced judgment to ambiguous digital storefront content.</li>
<li>Proactive communication skills to accurately flag issues, document errors, and ask clarifying questions regarding edge cases.</li>
<li><strong>Preferred Experience:</strong> Familiarity with e-commerce platforms, consumer products,&nbsp;</li>
<li>influencer content, or a background in Quality Assurance (QA) and data labeling.</li>
</ul>
