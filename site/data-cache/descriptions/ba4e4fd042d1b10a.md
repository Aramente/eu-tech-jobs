<div class="content-intro"><h3>ABOUT TIDE</h3>
<p>At Tide we help SMEs save time (and money) in the running of their businesses by not only offering business accounts and related banking services, but also a comprehensive set of highly usable and connected administrative solutions from invoicing to accounting.</p>
<p>Tide is transforming the small business banking market with over 1.8 million members globally across the UK, India, Germany and France.</p>
<p>Using advanced technology, all solutions are designed with SMEs in mind. With quick onboarding, low fees and innovative features, we thrive on making data-driven decisions to serve our mission: to help SMEs save both time (and money) so they can get back to doing what they love.</p>
<p><strong>Tide facts:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Tide is available for UK, Indian, German and French SMEs</li>
<li>Over 1.8 million members: 800,000 UK and 1,000,000 in India and growing rapidly</li>
<li>Over $300 million raised in funding</li>
<li>Over 2,500 Tideans globally - we’re diversity champions!</li>
<li>We have offices in Central London, with a member support and technology centre in Sofia, Bulgaria, technology centres in Serbia, Romania, Lithuania and Hyderabad and offices in Gurugram and New Delhi, and in Berlin, Paris and Luxembourg.</li>
</ul></div><h3>ABOUT THE ROLE</h3>
<p>We are looking for a <strong>strategic and execution-focused</strong> Special Projects Manager&nbsp; to lead the <strong>go-to-market&nbsp; and growth strategy</strong> for our <strong>accounting and financial management products</strong>, built specifically for India’s <strong>MSMEs.</strong></p>
<p>This is a high-impact role where you’ll work cross-functionally with Product, Growth, Sales, Marketing, and Leadership teams to define commercial strategy, shape product distribution, drive user acquisition, and ensure scalable growth. You will play a pivotal role in influencing <strong>product direction</strong>, <strong>business outcomes</strong>, and <strong>market expansion</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities -</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Define and own the GTM roadmap for accounting solutions tailored to MSME customers</li>
<li>Collaborate with Product and Engineering teams to localize features based on user needs</li>
<li>Should understand the need of businesses and create 0 to 1 journeys for the same</li>
<li>Conduct market segmentation and develop targeted value propositions for various user personas (retailers, traders, service providers, etc.)</li>
<li>Create pricing strategies and monetization models aligned with business goals</li>
<li>Lead initiatives to drive product adoption, activation, retention, and revenue growth</li>
<li>Plan and execute growth experiments across multiple channels; track performance and iterate</li>
<li>Develop and monitor KPIs; leverage data to drive decision-making and improvements</li>
<li>Scale user acquisition through partnerships, offline/online campaigns, and sales enablement</li>
<li>Act as the bridge between product, business, and functional teams</li>
<li>Manage GTM execution through structured planning, tracking, and performance reviews</li>
<li>Drive cross-functional alignment for product launches and key commercial initiatives</li>
<li>Own budgeting and resource planning for GTM and growth programs</li>
</ul>
<h3>WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR</h3>
<ul>
<li>B.Tech and MBA from a reputed institution</li>
<li>5–11 years of experience in GTM, commercial strategy, or growth leadership roles</li>
<li><strong>Mandatory experience in fintech, SaaS, or MSME-focused digital products</strong>—especially in accounting, invoicing, or financial management tools</li>
<li>Deep understanding of accounting or finance tools used by MSMEs</li>
<li>Proven ability to lead cross-functional projects in a fast-paced, high-growth environment</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills with experience using Excel, Power BI, Looker, or similar tools</li>
<li>Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and influencing skills</li>
<li>Hands-on execution mindset; willingness to work closely with data, field teams, or sales</li>
<li>Familiarity with agile product environments and tools like Jira, Confluence, or Aha!</li>
</ul>
