<p><strong>The Team:</strong></p>
<p>As an IT Service Engineer at Celonis, you will be a vital part of our international IT Service Team, responsible for ensuring seamless IT operations across the company. You will play a critical role in troubleshooting, resolving, or escalating IT-related issues for Celonis employees globally. This is an excellent opportunity for an experienced IT professional with hands-on expertise in both macOS and Windows environments who thrives in a fast-paced, dynamic, and international workplace. A willingness to work in a shift-based support model is essential to ensure global coverage and timely support.</p>
<p><strong>The work you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the first point of contact for IT support requests from employees, resolving issues or escalating tickets as necessary.</li>
<li>Provide hands-on support for both macOS and Windows platforms, troubleshooting hardware, software, and network issues.</li>
<li>Collaborate with the global IT Service Team to resolve technical issues via phone, email, or remote assistance.</li>
<li>Install, configure, and maintain computing equipment and peripherals, including desktop and laptop systems (macOS and Windows), network printers, and video conferencing systems (Google Meet/MS Teams).</li>
<li>Support mobile devices, including smartphones, through mobile device management platforms like Intune and Kandji</li>
<li>Ensure clear and effective communication with users to understand, document, and resolve their IT issues.</li>
<li>Perform basic repairs and replacements for computing equipment when needed.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain documentation and user guides for common issues to improve IT service efficiency.</li>
<li>Participate in a shift-based support schedule to ensure coverage across global time zones.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>The qualifications you need:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of hands-on experience in IT support, with proven ability to troubleshoot and resolve issues across both macOS and Windows environments.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of macOS and Windows 11 operating systems.</li>
<li>Experience with Active Directory as well as Azure Active Directory.</li>
<li>Understanding of TCP/IP networks and fundamental network troubleshooting skills.</li>
<li>Proficiency in M365 administration and support.</li>
<li>Strong knowledge of collaboration tools such as Microsoft Teams and Zoom.</li>
<li>Experience with Mobile Device Management (MDM) solutions like Intune and Kandji</li>
<li>Familiarity with operating and maintaining network components such as video conferencing systems (Google Meet/MS Teams) and network printers.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Required Skills:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong communication skills with the ability to work effectively in a global, multicultural team.</li>
<li>Detail-oriented, with excellent problem-solving abilities and a proactive mindset.</li>
<li>Capable of managing multiple tasks and projects in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>A team player who is motivated to provide excellent customer service and continuous improvement in IT processes.</li>
<li>ITIL V4 Foundation certification.</li>
</ul>
