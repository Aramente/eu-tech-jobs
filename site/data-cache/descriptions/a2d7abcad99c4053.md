<p><strong>Who You Are</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Bring 10+ years of SoC or ASIC experience, including a deep understanding of logic design, verification, and emulation platforms.</li>
<li>Fluent in Verilog or SystemVerilog, C or C++, and SystemC, with hands-on experience using commercial emulators such as ZeBu, Palladium, or Veloce.</li>
<li>Confident in architecting DV and emulation environments, from transactors to monitors to debug hooks, and automate everything you can.</li>
<li>Collaborative and self driven, with a passion for mentoring junior engineers and working cross functionally with architecture, software, and DV teams.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What We Need</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>A passion to build and scale a multi chiplet emulation ecosystem that supports DV, software development, and power or performance debug.</li>
<li>Comfort in developing testbenches, transactors, monitors, and support systems to enable high quality, high efficiency emulation usage.</li>
<li>Experience in driving emulation coverage from functional and performance to low level software enablement, and automate as much of the flow as possible.</li>
<li>To optimize the platform for frequency, resource sharing, and end user support, ensuring Tenstorrent teams get the most from emulation.</li>
</ul>

<li>How Tenstorrent integrates AI engines, RISC V CPUs, die to die links, PCIe, UCIe, and memory controllers into one unified platform.</li>
