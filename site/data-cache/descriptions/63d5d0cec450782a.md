<h2><strong>Role Overview</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for an Integrated B2B Marketing Manager to drive the execution and orchestration of communications and media plans for Wolt Ads &amp; Promotions globally.</p>
<p>This role is critical in ensuring that go-to-market strategies, campaigns, and product communications are delivered on time, on budget, and aligned with business OKRs.</p>
<p>Working closely with the Senior Product Marketing Manager, this person will act as the central orchestrator across Paid, Owned, and Earned channels, translating strategy into world-class marketing initiatives, ensuring seamless collaboration between multiple stakeholders and functions.</p>
<p>This is not a strategy-only role,&nbsp; it is about end-to-end ownership, operational excellence, and making things happen.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>This is a 12 month maternity cover role, with the possibility to extend.</strong></p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Own end-to-end execution of integrated marketing campaigns across Paid, Owned, and Earned channels</li>
<li>Turn strategy into clear, actionable plans — and bring campaigns to life with high quality and attention to detail</li>
<li>Work closely with Product Marketing, Paid, CRM, Content, Brand, and PR to make sure everything comes together seamlessly</li>
<li>Keep multiple projects moving across markets, aligning stakeholders and unblocking challenges along the way</li>
<li>Track performance against OKRs and use insights to continuously improve campaigns and ways of working</li>
<li>Make sure Sales and local teams have the tools, messaging, and support they need to succeed</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What Success Looks Like</strong></h2>

</ul>
