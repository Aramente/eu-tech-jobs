<p>The <strong>Sales Compensation &amp; Incentive Programs Manager</strong> is responsible for designing, governing, and operationalizing compensation programs for Sales, Business Development, and Partnerships teams.<br>This role ensures that incentive structures align pay with performance, support revenue growth, and scale with company strategy. The role collaborates closely with Sales leadership, Finance, C&amp;B, Analytics, and Operations to ensure accurate KPI measurement, bonus calculation, and compensation governance.<br>The role also oversees revenue tracking methodologies, KPI dashboards, and debt collection governance processes to ensure transparency, compliance, and operational efficiency.</p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>Sales Compensation Governance</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and maintain incentive plans for Sales, Business Development, and Partnerships teams.</li>
<li>Ensure alignment between compensation plans and company revenue strategy.</li>
<li>Maintain governance of compensation structures and policies.</li>
</ul>
<p>Bonus Calculation &amp; Revenue Attribution</p>
<ul>
<li>Manage quarterly bonus calculation processes.</li>
<li>Maintain calculation methodology for Booking revenue and KPI inputs.</li>
<li>Coordinate cross-functional validation with Finance, C&amp;B, and Sales leadership.</li>
<li>Manage exception approvals and bonus validation.</li>
</ul>
<p>KPI Framework &amp; Target Setting</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop KPI structures for Sales teams and startup teams.</li>
<li>Build methodologies for setting sales targets by role.</li>
<li>Partner with Analytics teams to build dashboards measuring KPI attainment.</li>
</ul>
<p>Incentive Plan Communication</p>
<ul>
<li>Create compensation one-pagers for sales employees.</li>
<li>Ensure all sales employees understand their compensation structure.</li>
<li>Maintain documentation in HR systems (HiBob).</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales Policy Governance</p>
<ul>
<li>Develop and maintain incentive policy documentation.</li>
<li>Manage updates to compensation policies and new hire incentive plans.</li>
</ul>
<p>Revenue Operations Process Improvements</p>
<ul>
<li>Implement revenue tracking methods using dashboards and contract validation.</li>
<li>Maintain master files for KPI tracking and bonus calculations.</li>
</ul>
<p>Debt Collection Governance</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the debt collection monitoring process across Ops, Sales, Finance, and Accounting.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with SOX requirements.</li>
<li>Implement processes to reduce outstanding customer debts.</li>
</ul>
<p>Sales Enablement &amp; Documentation</p>
<ul>
<li>Maintain Sales Excellence knowledge portal.</li>
<li>Support onboarding of Sales Enablement resources.</li>
<li>Ensure a single source of truth for sales compensation documentation.</li>
</ul>
