<h2><strong>About the Opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">At Klaviyo, the Sales team prides itself on being made up of marketing and product experts. While many companies hire pre-sales Solutions Engineers simply to give demos, fill out questionnaires, and explain setups, that approach makes us yawn.</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">We’re looking for a <span class="s2"><strong>Senior Solutions Engineer</strong></span> who brings deep technical mastery, strategic thinking, and the ability to own the most complex, high-stakes Enterprise deals. This is a high-impact individual contributor role for someone who thrives in ambiguity, rolls up their sleeves to master our product at a molecular level, and uses AI and automation to punch above their weight.</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">As a Senior SE, you’ll be a trusted advisor to both customers and our sales organization — <span class="s2"><strong>leading technical strategy</strong></span> in 6–7 figure deals, owning solution design, and setting the standard for Solutions Engineering excellence through your work.</p>
<p class="p1">&nbsp;</p>

<h2><strong>What We Do as Solutions Engineers</strong></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">We’re technical sellers who drive revenue by winning complex Enterprise deals. Our job is to <span class="s2"><strong>own the technical win</strong></span> — building champions, proving value, and ensuring prospects choose Klaviyo over the competition.</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">We work directly with customers to architect solutions that integrate seamlessly into their tech stacks, taking a consultant-first approach that builds confidence and secures technical buy-in.</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">Beyond closing deals, we use customer engagements as opportunities to prototype and ship improvements to Klaviyo. Recent team projects include AI-powered demo data generators, a public Chrome extension for troubleshooting API calls, web SDK injectors, and templatized mobile apps that showcase our SDK capabilities.</p>
<p class="p2">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="p3">If you’re a creator excited by winning high-stakes deals, prototyping technical solutions, and driving strategic revenue outcomes, this could be the role for you.</p>
