<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<p>As a <strong>Senior Design and MEP Manager, </strong>you will play a key role in delivering <strong>retrofit, refurbishment, and alteration projects</strong> across HelloFresh’s <strong>international distribution center portfolio</strong>. You will support the Property team by combining <strong>strong project management capabilities</strong> with <strong>deep MEP and architectural expertise</strong>, ensuring that operational upgrades, capacity expansions, and compliance-driven modifications are delivered safely, efficiently, and with minimal disruption to live operations.</p>
<p>The role is heavily focused on <strong>brownfield environments</strong>, requiring close coordination with Project Managers, operations, external designers, and contractors to manage design, execution readiness, and technical risk across multiple countries.</p>
<h2><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>Project &amp; Design Management (Retrofits &amp; Alterations)</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Act as a senior technical and project delivery partner for <strong>retrofit, refurbishment, and alteration projects</strong> within live international distribution centers.</li>
<li>Support Project Managers in defining scope, phasing, and execution strategies that minimize operational disruption while meeting performance and compliance requirements.<br>Manage and steer the <strong>MEP and architectural design process</strong> for alterations, upgrades, and extensions, ensuring designs are practical, buildable, and aligned with site constraints.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Technical Coordination &amp; Stakeholder Management</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the primary technical interface between internal stakeholders (Property, Operations, Quality, Safety) and external consultants and contractors.</li>
<li>Coordinate design reviews and technical decision-making, ensuring alignment between operational needs, regulatory requirements, and budget constraints.</li>
<li>Proactively identify technical risks typical to brownfield projects (interfaces, shutdowns, legacy systems) and drive mitigation strategies.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Design Oversight &amp; Consultancy Collaboration</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead the review and validation of MEP and architectural designs produced by external consultants, with a strong focus on <strong>HVAC, cooling, utilities, and food-industry requirements</strong>.</li>
<li>Ensure design solutions are optimized for <strong>cost, constructability, maintainability, and future flexibility</strong>.</li>
<li>Challenge over-engineering and support value engineering without compromising safety, quality, or compliance.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Quality, Cost &amp; Compliance Control</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Support site inspections, design audits, and commissioning activities to ensure works align with approved design intent and HelloFresh standards.</li>
<li>Work closely with Project Managers and procurement to manage design-to-budget, support tender evaluations, and control cost impacts from design changes.</li>
<li>Ensure compliance with&nbsp;<strong style="text-align: justify;">local building codes, food safety standards, sustainability targets, and internal HelloFresh engineering guidelines</strong><span style="text-align: justify;"> across multiple regions.</span></li>
</ul>
