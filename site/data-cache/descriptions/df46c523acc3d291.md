<p>We are looking for a Vulnerability Manager to join the Cyber Security organization, reporting to the Vulnerability Lead within the Cyber Security PMO under the CISO.<br><br>This role focuses on execution and operational management of the organization’s vulnerability management activities. You will work closely with the Vulnerability Lead to drive day-to-day remediation efforts, ensure SLA compliance, and maintain continuous progress across teams.<br><br>The ideal candidate is execution-driven, detail-oriented, and experienced in vulnerability management processes within cloud, infrastructure, and application environments.</p>
<h4>Responsibilities</h4>
<ul>
<li>Vulnerability Management Execution</li>
<li>Drive day-to-day execution of vulnerability management processes across cloud, infrastructure, SaaS, and application environments.</li>
<li>Track vulnerabilities from identification through remediation and validation.</li>
<li>Ensure SLA adherence and timely remediation across teams.</li>
<li>Execute prioritization based on risk, severity, and business impact as defined by the Vulnerability Lead.</li>
<li>Stakeholder Coordination</li>
<li>Work closely with Security, DevOps, Infrastructure, and Engineering teams to drive remediation efforts.</li>
<li>Follow up on tasks, remove blockers, and ensure continuous progress.</li>
<li>Provide regular status updates and reporting to the Vulnerability Lead and PMO.</li>
<li>Tracking &amp; Reporting</li>
<li>Maintain accurate tracking of vulnerabilities, remediation status, and KPIs.</li>
<li>Support dashboards and reporting on SLA compliance, exposure trends, and risk reduction.</li>
<li>Process &amp; Improvement</li>
<li>Support improvement of vulnerability management processes, workflows, and tooling.</li>
<li>Assist in audits, compliance activities, and documentation.</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li>Hands-on experience with vulnerability management processes and tools (e.g., Qualys, Tenable, Rapid7, Wiz).</li>
<li>Experience working in cloud, infrastructure, or application environments.</li>
<li>Strong execution and tracking skills with high attention to detail.</li>
<li>Experience working with cross-functional technical teams.</li>
