<p><strong>About Momence</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://momence.com/">Momence</a> is a next-gen booking and communications platform for experience-based businesses, such as a yoga &amp; pilates studios, gyms, dance schools, salons &amp; spas, golf facilities, swim schools, etc. We help over 1,500 businesses with scheduling &amp; payments, communication, and automation of their business backend. We also have a customer-facing Momence app, where tens of thousands of customers book their wellness experiences every month. At Momence, we are bringing modern-day software into the growing experience economy, that is currently highly underserved by modern software.</p>
<p>We’re a remote-first company, backed by Y Combinator (Summer 2020); moving quickly and growing rapidly. While our team operates remotely worldwide, our Prague-based team hast he option of being in the office or fully remote - up to you.</p>
<p>We are looking for a Frontend Engineer Mobile with experience in React Native, who wants to contribute to the development of a modern product in a US startup with a Czech-based engineering team.</p>
