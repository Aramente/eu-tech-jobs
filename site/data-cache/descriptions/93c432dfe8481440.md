<h3>The Opportunity</h3>
<p>Okta is seeking a Senior Data Engineer to join our Enterprise Data Platform team. Reporting to the Sr. Manager of Enterprise Data Platform, you will be a key hands-on contributor responsible for building the data platform that supports our internal AI use cases with AI-ready data. You will help turn the data product vision into a secure, scalable, and elegant technical reality by developing and implementing core components of our data infrastructure.</p>
<p>This role requires a skilled engineer who is passionate about building high-quality data solutions. You will contribute to the team’s standards for engineering excellence and help ensure our core data platform is trusted and ready to power AI-powered decision-making at Okta.</p>
<h3>What You’ll Do</h3>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain performant data pipelines and data models on the company's AI data platform, using Snowflake, AWS, and dbt.</li>
<li>Implement components of our security and governance framework to ensure high-compliance and security standards are met.</li>
<li>Develop automated solutions for data classification, access control, and contribute to our vulnerability management processes.</li>
<li>Use infrastructure as code (Terraform) and CI/CD (GitHub/GitLab) to build, test, and deploy data infrastructure securely and reliably.</li>
<li>Adhere to and champion best practices in code quality, system design, and operational readiness.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with product managers, senior engineers, and analytics teams to deliver high-quality data solutions that meet business needs.</li>
</ul>
<h3>What You Bring</h3>
<ul>
<li>Experience: 5+ years of data engineering experience, with a focus on building data pipelines, data models, and working on data platforms.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Technical Proficiency: Strong, hands-on experience with cloud data platforms (Snowflake, Databricks, or similar) and AWS. Solid understanding of data pipeline and orchestration tools like dbt, Airflow, or Fivetran, and IaC with Terraform.</li>
<li>Security &amp; Governance Mindset: Experience implementing security controls, and a good understanding of data governance principles within a modern data stack.</li>
<li>Collaborative Spirit: A proven track record of working effectively in a team environment with product managers, analysts, and other engineers to deliver features.</li>
<li>Problem Solver: Ability to tackle well-defined technical problems and deliver high-quality, maintainable engineering solutions.</li>
</ul>
<h3>What Success Looks Like</h3>
<ul>
<li>You are consistently delivering high-quality, reliable, and well-tested data pipelines and platform features.</li>
<li>The infrastructure you build is automated, secure, and aligns with the team’s best practices.</li>
<li>You are a trusted, collaborative, and productive member of the data engineering team.</li>
<li>Your work directly contributes to a cost-optimized and efficient tech stack that accelerates development for your peers.</li>
</ul>
