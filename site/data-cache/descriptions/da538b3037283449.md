We're looking for a Senior Organic Growth Marketing Manager who thinks in full systems. You'll own the content engine that drives discovery, the web experience that converts visitors into leads, and the optimization loops that make both better over time. This is not a "publish and pray" content role. It's a revenue-accountable position that spans content strategy, SEO/AEO, conversion rate optimization, and inbound channel performance.

## Key Responsibilities

*Content Strategy & Owned Content Engine*

- Develop and own a rolling 12-month content roadmap aligned to GTM motions, product launches, ABX campaigns, and sales cycles
- Build a consistent publishing cadence starting with one SEO/AEO-optimized pillar article per week, expanding into guides, case studies, and visual content over time
- Own content atomization — every major asset (webinar, guide, case study) produces multiple downstream formats across channels
- Ensure all content themes align with ICP targeting to serve as both campaign entry points and nurture touchpoints

*SEO & AEO Strategy*

- Own Verifiable's optimization roadmap across traditional search and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO/AI search), including schema markup, semantic triples, and query fan-out structures
- Drive non-branded organic growth — Verifiable captures ~25% of traffic from non-branded terms despite nearly 1M non-branded impressions
- Monitor how LLMs (OpenAI, Gemini, Perplexity) categorize Verifiable and develop strategies to correct miscategorization and improve share of voice
- Partner with web/dev to maintain technical SEO health across the Webflow site

*Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)*

- Own strategy and execution for inbound conversion paths — demo request forms, gated content, landing pages, and key entry points
- Build and manage a continuous A/B and multivariate testing program tied to pipeline outcomes, not just form fills
- Partner with Marketing Ops and RevOps to track lead quality downstream and collaborate with Product Marketing to ensure website messaging resonates with target buyers

*Video & Webinar Programs*

- Lead end-to-end webinar and video production — scripting, guest management, post-production, and distribution
- Scale the webinar program from 2–3 to 6–8 per year, with each program generating net-new pipeline and producing downstream assets including blog posts, social clips, and sales snippets

*Social Media & Community*

- Define and own Verifiable's voice on LinkedIn and Reddit — the two platforms most actively tapped by LLMs for brand sentiment
- Partner with internal SMEs to build a thought leadership content system that extends reach beyond the brand page
- Implement social listening to track sentiment shifts and brand mentions month-over-month
