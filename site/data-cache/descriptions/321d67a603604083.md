Fortune and Fortune Media IP Limited are not affiliated with, and do not endorse products or services of GitLab.</em></p></div><h2><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>An overview of this role</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This position is 100% remote and will be based in the United Kingdom, Netherlands, Germany, or Ireland.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">GitLab is looking for an enthusiastic and strategic Business Development Representative (BDR) to join our growing Sales team. As a BDR at GitLab, you get the opportunity to lead the initial outreach to targeted accounts working in conjunction with the Field and Digital Marketing teams as well as the Sales and Customer Success teams. In this role you'll leverage creative marketing and sales tactics to prospect and engage with multiple buyer personas and roles to introduce GitLab's value. You'll be responsible for generating qualified meetings and pipeline for GitLab's Sales organization.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Our BDRs come from a diverse background. Many have worked in closing roles previously or managed teams.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">We have an extensive onboarding and training program at GitLab and you'll be provided with necessary DevOps and GitLab knowledge to fulfill your role.</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>What you’ll do</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Execute outbound prospecting initiatives to generate qualified meetings and pipeline in your assigned territory.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Conduct high-level discovery conversations with target accounts to understand business needs, qualify opportunities, and create Sales Accepted Opportunities (SAOs).</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Meet or exceed monthly and quarterly BDR-sourced Sales Accepted Opportunity (SAO) targets by consistently converting qualified prospects into opportunities.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Research and prioritize target accounts using business and industry knowledge to identify key players, uncover compelling events, and develop tailored outreach strategies.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Execute a multi-touch outreach cadence (call, email, social, etc.) to all prospects in your assigned territory using Outreach.io to maximize engagement and conversion rates.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Manage, track, and accurately report all prospecting activities and pipeline in Salesforce to provide clear visibility into performance and forecast.</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Collaborate with Field, Corporate, and Digital Marketing, Sales, and Customer Success teams to build targeted account lists, campaigns, and call strategies, and attend regional marketing events to engage participants, generate leads, and drive qualified Sales Accepted Opportunities (SAOs).</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Document and continuously improve Business Development Representative processes in the GitLab handbook, in partnership with your Business Development Manager, and mentor new BDR hires to help them ramp quickly and navigate key accounts.</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong>What you’ll bring</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;">Prior experience prospecting into the Nordics region is required; fluency in Swedish is a strong plus</li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Positive and energetic phone skills, excellent listening skills, strong writing skills</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Meet or exceed daily, weekly, and monthly KPIs</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Proven experience taking initiative and independently driving projects or activities through to successful outcomes</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Alignment with our values and working in accordance with those values</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Knowledge of business process, roles, and organizational structure</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Demonstrated persistence in pursuing goals, learning from setbacks, and continuously improving to achieve strong results</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Passion about being a part of GitLab's journey</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Proficiency in using Salesforce and LinkedIn Sales Navigator</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Previous tech industry experience or experience in sales development, marketing, or sales is a plus</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Proficiency in English, our company language, is required for effective communication</span></li>
</ul>
