<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Role Description:</strong></span></p>
<p>We are looking for a highly skilled and motivated Senior SDET (Software Development&nbsp;Engineer in Test), Media to take a lead role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our media&nbsp;backend and frontend components. In this role, you will design, develop, and maintain test&nbsp;automation frameworks, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and drive a shift-left testing&nbsp;mindset across the organization.</p>
<p><br>As a technical leader, you will be responsible for supporting the execution of the Media Test&nbsp;Strategy, ensuring efficient test coverage, and contributing to the delivery of high-quality media&nbsp;features. Your expertise in media technologies (WebRTC, SIP), strong programming&nbsp;background, and ability to coordinate across teams will make you a key player in building&nbsp;scalable and reliable media solutions.</p>
<p><br>If you are passionate about media technologies, automation, and quality leadership, we&nbsp;would love to hear from you!<br><br></p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Take ownership of the quality strategy for media backend and frontend components.</li>
<li>Design, implement, and maintain robust and scalable test automation frameworks.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with developers, product managers, and QA engineers to drive shift-left testing practices.</li>
<li>Support and improve automated testing across different environments, including VDI, Cloud (GCP/AWS), and Windows 10/11 setups.</li>
<li>Validate and troubleshoot real-time communication systems (WebRTC, SIP).</li>
<li>Perform code reviews, mentor other SDETs, and ensure adherence to testing best practices.</li>
<li>Coordinate with multiple teams and products to ensure seamless integration and quality delivery.</li>
<li>Contribute to continuous improvement of test processes, tools, and methodologies.</li>
<li>Support the execution and evolution of the Media Test Strategy.</li>
</ul>
