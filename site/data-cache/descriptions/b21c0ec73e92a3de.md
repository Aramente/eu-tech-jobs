Overview Join a respected pharmacy team in Daphne, Alabama, where your certification and technical skills are the priority. This role is designed for a detail-oriented professional focused on non-sterile compounding, medication preparation, and maintaining high-standard dispensing workflows within an audit-ready environment.

Key Responsibilities

• Non-Sterile Compounding: Execute compounding activities following strict procedures and clinical supervision.

• Medication Preparation: Assist pharmacists in the precise preparation and dispensing of medications and clinical supplies.

• Pharmaceutical Calculations: Perform essential math to ensure dosing and quantities are consistently accurate.

• Inventory & Compliance: Manage the ordering, receiving, and storage of supplies while strictly adhering to all legal and regulatory safety standards.

• Quality Control: Maintain a clean, organized, and professional workspace, ensuring the facility is always ready for potential audits.

Requirements

• Licensure: Current and valid Certified Pharmacy Technician license.

• Experience: Minimum of 1 year of experience as a Pharmacy Technician.

• Compounding Skills: Hands-on experience with non-sterile compounding is required (sterile experience is not necessary).

• Technical Math: Proficiency in basic pharmaceutical calculations.

• Location: Ability to work on-site in Daphne, AL.

Compensation & Benefits

• Pay Range: $20.00 – $24.00 per hour (based on experience).

• Health & Wellness: Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.

• Work-Life Balance: Generous Paid Time Off (PTO) package.

• Stability: Full-time, permanent position in a professional clinical setting.
