<h2 id="job-responsibilities">Job responsibilities</h2>
<div class="job-responsibilities content">
<ul>
<li>Understand a client’s business problems to build, maintain, and evolve product roadmaps that generate impactful, differentiating solutions.</li>
<li>Engage with and influence senior business stakeholders, facilitating discovery workshops to outline business vision, objectives, and value hypotheses for large-scale transformations.</li>
<li>Apply an agile mindset to iteratively define release plans and drive the identification of "slices of value," ensuring work is delivered incrementally.</li>
<li>Liaise between the client and multidisciplinary teams—including SMEs, C-suite executives, developers, and architects—to manage the product backlog and advocate for shared outcomes.</li>
<li>Assess the "as-is" situation and collaborate with clients to create high-value artifacts such as personas, epics, and stories within agile transformation teams.</li>
<li>Lead work breakdown and backlog refinement activities, including specialized Experimentation backlog management to navigate the R&amp;D nature of AI projects.</li>
<li>Lead the evolution of the analysis community by integrating Data Lifecycle Management and AI ethics into the standard agile toolkit while sharing knowledge on complex data discovery.</li>
<li>Evangelize 'Continuous Development' as it applies to evolving AI models and automated workflows, keeping a close eye on software delivery and team collaboration.</li>
<li>Take accountability for tracking whether delivered solutions are achieving intended business outcomes and KPIs, while encouraging open communication and transparency.</li>
</ul>

<h3 id="technical-skills">Technical Skills</h3>
<div class="qualifications inner-content">
<ul>
<li>Proven ability to work with stakeholders to understand business goals and identify where data, reporting, automation, predictive analytics, or AI can improve outcomes.</li>
