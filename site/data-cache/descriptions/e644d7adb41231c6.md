<h2><strong>Your Impact</strong></h2>
<p>Doctolib is growing, and we are hiring Frontend Software Engineers in Paris, across various teams and seniority levels. Whether you are early in your career or a seasoned developer, there is a place for you within our Engineering teams!</p>
<p>As a Software Engineer, your mission will be to help Doctolib reinforce its position as a leading healthcare technology platform by building high-quality products that deliver value to patients and healthcare professionals.</p>
<p>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with product managers, designers, and engineers to build features that deliver value to healthcare professionals and patients</li>
<li>Design, develop, and maintain React/TypeScript components ensuring performance, accessibility, and scalability standards</li>
<li>Contribute to code quality through comprehensive testing, documentation, and code reviews</li>
<li>Share your expertise and collaborate across teams to continuously improve and evolve Doctolib's frontend architecture</li>
</ul>
