<p>We are looking for a Senior Workplace Manager to join our Workplace Strategy &amp; Operations team — which owns workplace, real estate, employee experience, and physical security globally. You will serve as our senior on-the-ground presence across our EMEA offices, owning daily operations in London and overseeing Berlin, while playing a significant role in our upcoming London office search and transition from co-working to a permanent space. You will report to the Head of Workplace and will be located onsite in London.</p>
<p>If you're based within a pre-determined commuting distance of one of our offices (SF, NY, London, or Berlin), the role includes in-office anchor days on Monday, Tuesday, and Friday, even if the role is listed as remote. For location-specific details, please connect with our recruiting team.</p>
<h2>What You Will Do:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Own daily operations across our London and Berlin co-working locations — including access control, vendor management, food programs, facilities, and general services — maintaining a consistently high standard across both sites.</li>
<li>Play a significant role in the London office search and transition: helping define space requirements, coordinating site visits, evaluating options, and supporting the build-out and move-in process in close partnership with the Head of Workplace, broader workplace team, IT/AV, Finance, and Legal.</li>
<li>Own the employee experience across our EMEA offices — cultural programming, space activations, events, and onboarding moments that reflect Vercel's values.</li>
<li>Serve as the regional point of contact for real estate matters, including lease considerations, space planning, and vendor relationships across both locations.</li>
<li>Travel to Berlin on a regular basis (approximately quarterly) to maintain on-the-ground presence and ensure operational continuity.</li>
<li>Partner cross-functionally with IT, Security, Legal, Finance, and People to coordinate services and contribute to global Workplace programs, team OKRs/KPIs, and special projects.</li>
</ul>
<h2>About You:</h2>
<ul>
<li>6+ years of experience in workplace operations, office management, or a related field, with demonstrated experience operating autonomously in a senior or standalone capacity.</li>
<li>Proven experience contributing to office search, build-out, or lease transition processes — you know how to be an effective partner on real estate workstreams.</li>
<li>Experience managing multiple office locations simultaneously, including serving as the primary point of escalation across sites.</li>
<li>Track record of building or owning employee experience programs, including events, cultural programming, and in-office activations.</li>
<li>Experience collaborating across functions and influencing senior stakeholders across time zones.</li>
</ul>
