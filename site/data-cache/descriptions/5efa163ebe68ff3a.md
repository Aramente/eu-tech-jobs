<div class="job__description body"><div><p><strong>Billing Analyst</strong></p>
<p>The Billing Analyst is responsible for managing and executing timely, accurate billing in accordance with company policies and customer contracts. This role requires a strong operational focus, attention to detail, and comfort navigating complex systems and billing models—including subscription-based, transactional, time &amp; materials (T&amp;M), and tiered revenue</p>
<p><span><strong>What You'll Do:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and process invoices to ensure accuracy and timeliness of monthly invoices across multiple billing models.</li>
<li>Review and analyze customer accounts to verify transactions are billed accurately and timely</li>
<li>Process contract-driven changes, such as adjustments, credits, rebills, and cancellations.</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally to support the onboarding and billing of new customer agreements.</li>
<li>Identify opportunities for process automation and workflow optimization within the billing process.</li>
<li>Document billing processes, SOPs, and changes to maintain accurate operational guides.</li>
<li>Support financial reporting, monthly close, internal audits, and ad hoc data analysis as required.</li>
<li>Support special billing projects and assist in responding to internal and external billing inquiries via the shared inbox.</li>
</ul>
<p><span><strong>What You'll Bring:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li>3–5 years of experience in billing, accounts receivable, revenue operations, or a related operational finance role.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to manage high-volume workloads with speed and accuracy.</li>
<li>Proficiency with enterprise billing or ERP systems; experience with BillingPlatform and/or Intacct strongly preferred.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, with a track record of identifying and resolving discrepancies.</li>
<li>Clear, professional communication skills and comfort working cross-functionally.</li>
<li>Process-oriented mindset and ability to document workflows effectively.</li>
</ul>
