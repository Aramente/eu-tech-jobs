<h2><strong>About Graphcore&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<p>At Graphcore, we’re building the future of AI compute.</p>
<p>We’re a team of semiconductor, software and AI experts, with deep experience in creating the complete AI compute stack - from silicon and software to infrastructure at datacenter scale.</p>
<p>As part of the SoftBank Group, backed by significant long-term investment, we are delivering key technology into the fast-growing SoftBank AI ecosystem.</p>
<p>To meet the vast and exciting AI opportunity, Graphcore is expanding its teams around the world.</p>
<p>We are bringing together the brightest minds to solve the toughest problems, in a place where everyone has the opportunity to make an impact on the company, our products and the future of artificial intelligence.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><span data-contrast="none">Job Summary</span></strong><strong><span data-contrast="none">&nbsp;</span></strong><span data-ccp-props="{}">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">We are looking for a Technical </span><span data-contrast="auto">Product Owner</span><span data-contrast="auto">&nbsp;to work with a&nbsp;</span><span data-contrast="auto">component-based software team</span><span data-contrast="auto">, helping translate product outcomes into clear, prioritised, and deliverable component-level work.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">In this role, you will sit at the intersection of&nbsp;</span><span data-contrast="auto">product intent, technical delivery, and cross-team collaboration</span><span data-contrast="auto">. You will not be responsible for defining product strategy alone, nor for managing people — instead, your focus is on&nbsp;</span><span data-contrast="auto">enabling your team to deliver the right technical outcomes &amp; contributions to wider customer and business outcomes</span><span data-contrast="auto">.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="auto">This role is ideal for someone who enjoys working closely with engineers and developers, navigating dependencies, and bringing clarity to complex systems.</span><span data-ccp-props="{}">&nbsp;</span></p>
