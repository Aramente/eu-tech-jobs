**About the Role**

We are looking for a high-rigor finance professional to **own cost and control intelligence across Valmo’s first mile and last mile nodes**. This role sits within the controllership team and is responsible for validating ground-level data, ensuring accurate provisioning, highlighting cost leakages, and enabling audit-ready reporting for our fulfilment and delivery operations.

## What you will do

- **Own financial governance across key first mile (pickup) and last mile (delivery) nodes** — ensuring cost data integrity, audit traceability, and timely reporting.
- Partner with operations to identify **leakages or deviations** (e.g. excess incentives, incorrect provisioning).
- **Design and own dashboards, trackers, and exception reports** for visibility into cost anomalies or operational inefficiencies.
- **Support provisioning logic** for last-mile incentives, damaged shipments, returns, and COD-related costs — ensuring historical and real-time alignment.
- Conduct **monthly node reviews** with ops and finance teams to bridge actual vs booked cost gaps.
- Facilitate **auditor walkthroughs**, data validation trails, and control documentation for nodes under your scope.

## What you will need

- CA / MBA Finance with 6– years of experience in **finance controllership** or **logistics finance**
- Strong with **Excel/Google Sheets**; familiarity with dashboards, SQL, or BI tools is a plus.
- Ability to work closely with ground teams and bring financial scrutiny to operational flows.
- Strong bias for action, with a questioning mindset and comfort owning metrics independently.
