## Main Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and maintain automated end-to-end test suites for both API and UI layers.
- Collaborate with the Test Automation team to contribute to a shared test library.
- Identify, document, and collaborate with the engineering team to resolve software defects.
- Work closely with product, engineering, and QA teams to ensure high-quality products.

## Core Requirements

- Good understanding of QA methodologies and approaches.
- Solid knowledge of JavaScript.
- Working knowledge of TypeScript and Node.js.
- Strong working knowledge of API testing.
- Proven hands-on experience with Cypress and Playwright (migration from Cypress to Playwright is ongoing).
- Strong general software engineering skills.
- Working knowledge of Git and CI/CD. Experience working with GitLab CI is a plus.
- B1+ level written and spoken English.
- Strong collaboration and communication skills.
