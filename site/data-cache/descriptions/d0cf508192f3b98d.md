<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Strategic Business Partnership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the primary strategic partner to APJ Sales leadership, helping leaders anticipate key questions, form data-backed points of view, and make informed decisions</li>
<li>Bring proactive analysis to the table - moving beyond reporting the news to delivering the What, So What, and Now What</li>
<li>Coach business partners on key presentations, messaging, and the softer dimensions of running a high-performing team</li>
<li>Maintain an objective, unbiased perspective across Sales and other GTM stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>AI-Augmented Operations</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Actively deploy AI tools to accelerate analysis, automate repetitive workflows, and improve the quality of outputs across forecasting, pipeline inspection, and business reviews</li>
<li>Design and prototype AI-assisted workflows - such as automated narrative generation, deal inspection copilots, or signal-based alerting - that reduce manual effort and increase the team's strategic bandwidth</li>
<li>Serve as a thought leader within the GTM Ops team on how AI can be embedded into the rhythm of business, not just used as a productivity aid</li>
<li>Bring a proven track record of having delivered at least one substantive AI-enabled project in a prior GTM, Sales Operations, or Revenue Operations context - with clear before/after impact</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Sales Planning &amp; Commercial Operations</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead APJ contributions to semi-annual planning cycles, including territory design, quota setting, headcount capacity modelling, and carve preparation</li>
<li>Build and maintain operating plans in partnership with VP and FLM leaders, defining roles, measurement frameworks, and a plan-to-make-plan approach</li>
<li>Drive the rhythm of business: forecasting, pipeline inspection, renewal readiness, pipe council, and quarterly business reviews</li>
<li>Contribute to SPIFF design and execution, partnering with Finance and Compensation to cascade and track performance</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Analytics &amp; Insights</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the analytical agenda for APJ - synthesising business performance into clear, actionable takeaways for senior audiences</li>
<li>Conduct monthly metrics reviews across Net ARR, pipeline coverage, headcount, close rates, and forecast accuracy</li>
<li>Prepare and deliver QBR materials and metrics packs; be able to present the data as if running the business yourself</li>
<li>Develop and maintain reporting tools and dashboards in partnership with the GTM Analytics team; actively explore how AI-assisted analysis can reduce cycle time and surface non-obvious insights</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as the connective tissue between APJ Sales and cross-functional partners including Marketing, Enablement, Deal Desk, Finance, CS Strategy, and GTM Systems</li>
<li>Drive accountability and visibility across teams; proactively identify where siloes are creating risk or missed opportunity</li>
<li>Represent APJ Sales Operations interests in global and regional strategic initiatives</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Process &amp; Systems</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and drive process improvement opportunities across the APJ sales motion, with a strong bias toward AI-enabled automation and self-serve solutions</li>
<li>Maintain a prioritised backlog of process and systems needs; partner with GTM Technology and Business Technology teams to sequence and deliver</li>
<li>Ensure data integrity and hygiene across Salesforce; understand key field taxonomy to support accurate reporting and forecasting</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What Success Looks Like</strong></h2>
<p>In your first 90 days, you will have built strong working relationships with APJ Sales leaders and key cross-functional partners, developed a clear point of view on the health and priorities of the business, and established a consistent operating cadence. You will also have identified at least one high-value AI opportunity within the APJ ops workflow and begun moving it toward delivery.</p>
<p>Over time, you will be measured by the quality of your business partnership, the accuracy and timeliness of your analysis, and your ability to translate insights - increasingly AI-assisted - into tangible commercial outcomes.</p>

<ul>
