**About the Role**

We are looking for a Video Script Writer to join our Marketing team. In this role, you will produce high-performing, platform-optimized scripts for our company social accounts and our CEO’s personal brand accounts Your goal is to turn complex business and financial topics into engaging stories that drive viewer retention and generate organic leads. You will own the scriptwriting process from the initial idea to the final filming-ready drafts.

## Key responsibilities

- Create YouTube-Optimized Scripts: Write scripts specifically designed to maximize audience retention and engagement.
- Drive Organic Leads: Use storytelling to naturally convert viewers into customers while maintaining a consistent brand voice.
- Streamline Production: Manage the process from idea to final draft to ensure efficient handovers and faster filming timelines.
- Cross-Team Collaboration: Coordinate with Marketing teams to ensure video messaging aligns with company goals.
- Support departmental Needs: Provide scriptwriting support for other company needs outside of the main YouTube and social channels.

## Skills and qualifications

- YouTube Strategy: Deep understanding of hooks, pacing, and retention structures specific to the YouTube algorithm.
- Adaptable Voice: Ability to write in different tones, switching between a personal brand voice and a corporate/educational brand voice.
- Complex Research: Experience taking technical topics (startups, finance, or tax) and simplifying them for a general audience.
- Process Ownership: Strong organizational skills to manage a high-volume production calendar and meet deadlines consistently.
- Production Awareness: Knowledge of how to format scripts with clear cues for B-roll, graphics, and editors.
- Experience: 1–3 years of professional experience writing scripts for high-growth YouTube channels or digital video platforms

## Bonus qualifications

- Experience with A/B testing video titles, hooks, and script structures to continually optimize performance.
- Experience scripting content specifically for VC backed startups, entrepreneurship, or finance topics.
- Familiarity with YouTube SEO tools like TubeBuddy, VidIQ, or Google Trends.
- Ability to analyze YouTube Studio data (CTR and drop-off points) to improve future script performance.
