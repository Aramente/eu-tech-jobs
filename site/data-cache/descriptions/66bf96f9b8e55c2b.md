<h2><strong>About the opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>Contentful is undergoing a core pivot: evolving from a developer-centric CMS into a unified creation environment where marketers are first-class citizens. We aren’t just adding a UI; we are re-engineering how the world’s biggest brands create digital experiences.</p>
<p>As the Senior PM for Marketer Experience, you will drive this 0 to 1 evolution. You aren’t just "adding a UI", you are driving the evolution of our product to turn our powerful technical engine into an intuitive, high-velocity tool that marketing teams love to use.</p>
<h2><strong>What to expect?&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Drive the Pivot: Evolve a high-level strategic vision into a concrete product. You’ll take the lead on making Contentful a "home" for marketers, ensuring the product stays powerful but becomes accessible.</li>
<li>Define the "Right" Enterprise Scope: You won’t just build "simple" tools. You will deeply understand the needs of complex global enterprises and define a scope that is sophisticated enough for their scale, yet lean enough to ship and iterate quickly.</li>
<li>Obsess Over Usability: Quality for you is measured by how great, powerful, and easy-to-use the tools are for marketers, ensuring user autonomy. You win when a marketer can build and launch a complex global experience independently—without needing a developer for every change.</li>
<li>Cross-Team Collaboration: You must collaborate effectively with other product and engineering teams. This project has high dependencies across the organization; success requires navigating the ecosystem.</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What you need to be successful?</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>0→1 Experience: You’ve taken a product from "messy idea" to "polished reality" before. You thrive in ambiguity and create your own clarity.</li>
<li>The "Surgical" Scoper: You can demonstrate a track record of navigating complex requirements and distilling them into a clear, prioritized product definition that meets user needs.</li>
<li>Persona Empathy: You can speak deeply with a systems engineer about architecture, then turn around and advocate for a marketer’s workflow with equal passion.</li>
<li>Grit: You have stories of pushing through setbacks and evolving a product based on real-world feedback.</li>
<li>Commercial Mindset: You can clearly tie customer problems and product solutions to business outcomes. You understand that solving a marketer's pain point is only a win if it also drives adoption, retention, and competitive advantage.</li>
<li>Exceptional communication abilities, including an executive presence, are necessary, with the expectation of confidently articulating product strategy in discussions with customers and sales teams.</li>
</ul>
