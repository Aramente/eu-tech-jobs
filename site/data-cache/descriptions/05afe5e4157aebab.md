<h2><strong>About the opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>The position of <strong>Area Vice President, Mid-Market EMEA </strong>&nbsp;(f/m/d)is a critical leadership role within our EMEA Sales organisation, reporting directly to the <strong>SVP EMEA &amp; APAC</strong>.</p>
<p>This role carries both <strong>first-line and second-line management responsibilities</strong>, leading a team of Mid-Market Sales Managers and their respective Account Executives. You will be responsible for building, scaling, and developing a high-performing regional sales organisation focused on organisations across EMEA with revenues between <strong>$100m and $1bn USD</strong>.</p>
<p>As a senior sales leader with a strong SaaS background, you will drive regional strategy, operational excellence, and consistent revenue growth across the Mid-Market segment. You will partner cross-functionally to accelerate pipeline generation, optimise go-to-market execution, navigate complex procurement cycles, and act as an executive stakeholder for strategic customers within your region.</p>
<p>As a quota-carrying leader, you own the regional forecast, proactively identify and mitigate risks, capitalise on opportunities, and continuously elevate performance through structured coaching, talent development, and strong sales leadership.</p>
