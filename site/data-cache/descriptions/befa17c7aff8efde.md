<p>We’re looking for a Marketing Specialist to support our marketing strategy in Germany. In this role, you will partner closely with Business Marketing leadership and cross-functional partners to influence and execute the marketing strategy that supports revenue goals and brand visibility within the industry.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Business Marketing leadership to bring the local market strategy to life in Germany, leveraging best practices, learnings, industry trends and insights on both Pinterest and competitors</li>
<li>Assist in localising and creating engaging marketing content, including blog posts, social media content, success studies, and email campaigns in German</li>
<li>Own key initiatives to bring the broader marketing strategy to life, such as Pinterest-owned or third party events, webinars, and advertorial partnerships</li>
<li>Work closely with local and global teams to ensure that global programs resonate locally</li>
<li>Manage the measurement of local marketing activations and identify areas for improvement</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including sales, product, measurement and the wider marketing team</li>
<li>Leverage AI (tools) while maintaining integrity, critical thinking, and ownership of final outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What we’re looking for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>4 years experience in integrated marketing and/or digital marketing, B2B marketing experience is a plus&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment</li>
<li>Excellent organisational and project management skills to effectively balance multiple projects, stakeholders and deadlines</li>
<li>Fluency in both written and spoken German</li>
<li>Business-level English in both writing and verbal communication</li>
<li>Passion for digital marketing and a desire to grow and develop within the field</li>
<li>Familiarity with social media platforms and their advertising capabilities, with a specific focus on Pinterest (prior experience a plus)</li>
<li>Basic design capabilities and experience using design tools like the Adobe Suite, Figma and SplashThat a plus</li>
<li>Sound analytical skills, with the ability to learn how to analyse data and derive actionable insights</li>
<li>Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business or equivalent work experience</li>
</ul>
