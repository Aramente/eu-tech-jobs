<p><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for a Head of Creative to define and lead the evolution of Typeform’s brand and creative expression as the company enters its next stage of growth.</p>
<p>Typeform is expanding from a form builder into a broader platform for building powerful customer interactions and workflows. As the product evolves, the brand must evolve with it. This role will shape how Typeform is understood in the market, ensuring our narrative and visual identity reflect the full ambition of the platform.</p>
<p>As Head of Creative, you will lead the strategy, craft, and systems behind the brand across every marketing touchpoint. From shaping the brand identity to building scalable creative systems that power marketing across channels, you will ensure our creative work communicates clearly, consistently, and with impact.</p>
<p>You will lead and grow a multidisciplinary creative team while establishing the processes and infrastructure needed to deliver high quality creative at scale. Your work will define how Typeform shows up to millions of people around the world.</p>
<p><strong>What you will do</strong></p>
<p>• Lead the evolution and repositioning of the Typeform brand as the product and platform expand<br>• Define the creative vision and ensure consistency across all marketing channels and customer touchpoints<br>• Drive the rollout of a refreshed brand identity and narrative that reflects the company’s product evolution<br>• Build scalable creative systems and processes that support marketing across web, campaigns, product launches, and content<br>• Lead and mentor a multidisciplinary creative team across design, copy, motion, and production<br>• Partner closely with marketing, product design, and leadership to align brand storytelling with product strategy and growth goals<br>• Work with performance marketing and growth teams to ensure creative supports experimentation, conversion optimisation, and channel performance<br>• Collaborate with creative operations and production partners to deliver high quality creative work efficiently and at scale</p>
<p><strong>What you bring</strong></p>
<p>• Significant experience leading creative teams within high growth technology companies<br>• A strong portfolio demonstrating both strategic brand thinking and exceptional creative execution<br>• Experience evolving or repositioning a brand as a company and product expands<br>• Strong cross functional leadership skills with the ability to influence and build alignment across teams<br>• Deep creative craft across design, copy, motion, or production, with the ability to mentor and guide creative talent<br>• Experience building scalable brand systems, creative processes, and operational frameworks<br>• Strong understanding of performance marketing and how creative supports acquisition and conversion<br>• Fluency with modern creative tools such as Figma and Adobe Creative Suite, with familiarity with emerging AI driven creative workflows<br>• Ability to operate at both strategic and detailed levels, translating complex ideas into clear brand storytelling<br><br></p>
<p><strong>Nice to have</strong></p>
<p>• Experience leading a major brand refresh or repositioning<br>• Experience working on products with strong product led growth and brand driven marketing<br>• Experience directing or leading major visual productions such as photo shoots or large scale campaign assets<br>• Experience experimenting with AI tools to improve creative workflows and production efficiency</p>
