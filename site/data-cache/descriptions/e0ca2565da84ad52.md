<div class="content-intro"><p><strong>COMPANY OVERVIEW</strong></p>
<p>KKR is a leading global investment firm that offers alternative asset management as well as capital markets and insurance solutions. KKR aims to generate attractive investment returns by following a patient and disciplined investment approach, employing world-class people, and supporting growth in its portfolio companies and communities. KKR sponsors investment funds that invest in private equity, credit and real assets and has strategic partners that manage hedge funds. KKR’s insurance subsidiaries offer retirement, life and reinsurance products under the management of Global Atlantic Financial Group. References to KKR’s investments may include the activities of its sponsored funds and insurance subsidiaries.</p></div><p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2 data-start="235" data-end="248">Overview</h2>
<p data-start="249" data-end="705">The Client and Product Services Team (CAPS) supports KKR’s global distribution and client retention efforts by delivering high-quality, timely, and strategically positioned responses to client information requests, including RFIs, DDQs, and other monitoring deliverables. CAPS partners closely with investment, product, legal, compliance, and operations teams to ensure consistency, accuracy, and alignment with firm messaging across KKR’s global platform.</p>
<p data-start="707" data-end="1115">The Senior Consultant will serve as a senior individual contributor and day-to-day leader for the Dublin-based team, acting as the representative of the Global Head of CAPS for regional leadership in Dublin. In addition to executing complex client monitoring requests, this role will provide guidance, oversight, and mentorship to team members, while driving quality, consistency, and operational excellence.</p>
