<h3><strong>Project Overview</strong></h3>
<p>We are sourcing independent LaTeX Specialists to provide their expertise for an AI benchmark evaluation project. As AI models increasingly generate complex long-form content that blends mathematical and technical formatting with creative prose, their quality relies entirely on robust, expert-reviewed training data. The objective of this project is to autonomously evaluate, rewrite, and refine AI-generated text to meet specific stylistic guidelines while ensuring flawless LaTeX rendering and proper markdown formatting.</p>
<h3><strong>Project Deliverables &amp; Scope</strong></h3>
<p>Operate autonomously to audit, edit, and refine complex AI outputs. Expected deliverables include:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>LaTeX &amp; Math Rendering Correction:</strong> Identifying and correcting malformed LaTeX expressions, unclosed environments, and inaccurate mathematical notations within text.</li>
<li><strong>Stylistic Rewriting &amp; Editing:</strong> Rewriting AI-generated text to meet strict stylistic, structural, and creative requirements, ensuring engaging prose, varied sentence structure, and pristine grammar.</li>
<li><strong>Markdown Formatting:</strong> Fixing broken markdown elements, including unclosed code blocks, inaccurate language tags, headers, and list numbering to ensure flawless structural formatting.</li>
<li><strong>Consistent Rubric Application:</strong> Applying complex, multi-part evaluation rubrics consistently across high volumes of tasks to generate clean, reliable data for model training.</li>
<li><strong>Localization &amp; Standardization:</strong> Ensuring all generated text adheres strictly to spelling, grammar, and formatting conventions.</li>
</ul>
