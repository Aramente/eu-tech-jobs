**OUR STORY:**

🇪🇺 Join Scaleway and shape the sovereign cloud of tomorrow !

Since 1999, we have been designing secure, sustainable infrastructures aimed at supporting the most ambitious companies.

Historically known for our dedicated servers (Dedibox), we made a strategic shift to cloud computing in 2015. Staying true to our principles of simplicity, flexibility, and technical excellence, we have become one of the leading players in Europe in the sector.

With the rise of artificial intelligence, we have strengthened our commitment, supported by the Iliad Group, which is investing €3 billion to develop a serious, sovereign AI alternative to American and Asian giants.

Every day, thanks to our fast-growing portfolio of cloud and AI products (bare metal, containerization, serverless, AI, etc.), Scaleway proudly serves thousands of customer across the private and public sector, from corporations like France Télévisions or Hachette Livre, to fast-growing startups like Photoroom and Biolevate, to institutions like the City of Copenhagen.

📍 Our offices are located in **Paris, Lille, Toulouse, Rennes, Rouen, Bordeaux and Lyon.**

## **WHY WE NEED YOU ?**

Our growth is driving us to strengthen our **Network Product Edge** team to consolidate and scale our complex stacks while supporting the rapid adoption of our products.

Your mission will be to **build, maintain, and evolve the software and infrastructure components** of our Load Balancer and Edge Services platforms in order to **deliver high-performance, secure, and scalable network solutions** on our sovereign cloud.

## **YOUR FUTURE TEAM**

We work in a collaborative and international environment where the diversity of Scalers, combined with a spirit of sharing, helps bring new projects to life every day, advancing our ambitions together.

You will be part of a team of **5 people** where the Product Manager is an integral part of the group. We operate in Sprints and manage a rich technical ecosystem, including Load Balancer as a Service (L4/L7) and Edge Services (Caching, WAF). You will contribute to the design and implementation of new products like API Gateway and anti-DDoS solutions.

Manager information: **William Paumier**

## **YOUR DAILY ROUTINE**

Tasks:

* Design robust APIs and contribute to the definition of new service architectures.
* Develop new features and evolve the stack for Load Balancer and Edge Services.
* Manage and optimize the CI/CD pipelines, automation, and monitoring tools.
* Contribute to technical debt management, refactoring, and performance optimization.
* Maintain the existing production stack and resolve complex bugs or technical incidents.
* Perform code reviews and participate in team rituals to foster knowledge sharing.
* Collaborate daily with cross-functional teams to integrate network products across the Scaleway ecosystem.
* Conduct technical studies and Proof of Concepts (PoCs) for emerging network technologies.

## **ABOUT YOU**
