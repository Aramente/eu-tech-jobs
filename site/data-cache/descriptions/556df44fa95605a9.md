## Main Responsibilities

- Develop messaging and stories that reflect and amplify the brand’s voice;
- Write creative, clear, and compelling copy for a wide range of channels: video scripts, social media posts, campaign communication, etc.;
- Take complex concepts and bring them to life with simplicity and elegance;
- Interpret briefs to understand project requirements;
- Research and gather additional information to quickly learn a new topic;
- See projects through the whole creative lifestyle, from inception to completion;
- Work on multiple projects at once, prioritizing your tasks and meeting tight deadlines;
- Work as a part of the Brand Communications team;

## Core Requirements

- 3+ years of experience in copywriting, creative strategy, and/or content strategy;
- Strong portfolio of past copywriting work;
- Proven experience working with creative marketing campaigns;
- Native-level English;
- Strong creative thinking skills and ability to think conceptually;
- Ability to transform complex technical concepts into messages that inspire action;
- Ability to switch between writing styles and formats to meet the needs of different audiences;
- Ability to work well independently and in a team;
- Strong organizational skills;
- Active interest in technology and innovation;
- Bonus: Experience in scriptwriting.
