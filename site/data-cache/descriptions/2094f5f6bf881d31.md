<div class="content-intro"><p>Trusted by more than 18,000 Shopify and Shopify Plus stores—like Brooklinen, Ruggable, True Classic and Dr. Squatch—Postscript gives ecommerce brands the tools they need to run a world-class SMS marketing program.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We’re on a mission to make SMS the number-one revenue channel for ecommerce merchants by delivering an easy-to-use platform where they can grow their subscriber list, develop and test their messaging strategy, deliver excellent customer service, and drive sales and loyalty—all in one place. We believe SMS is the most important marketing and sales channel of the decade, and that vision drives everything we do.</p>
<p>Postscript was founded in 2018 as a 100% remote organization. With more than 250 employees (and counting!), we’re backed by Greylock, Y Combinator, and other top investors.</p></div><p>As <strong>Senior Engineering Manager</strong> for the Messaging Channels team at Postscript, you will lead a team of 8 highly skilled product and platform engineers who own the messaging backbone that every Postscript product ships through. Your team is responsible for (1) <strong>Flow Builder</strong>, Postscript’s automation product that merchants use to orchestrate their customer journeys, (2) the <strong>high-volume SMS campaign send engine</strong> that powers some of the largest DTC brands in the world, (3) the <strong>messaging platform and infrastructure</strong> that every other product at Postscript is built on top of, and (4) <strong>expansion into new channels</strong> that meet our merchants wherever their customers are. The team runs its own dedicated on-call rotation and sets the bar for operational rigor at Postscript.</p>
<p>Engineering Managers are directly responsible for day-to-day people management of their direct reports. They focus on leadership, people management, and delivery/execution duties. Our backend is primarily Python within an AWS architecture, with select services in Go, backed by Kafka, Postgres, and MongoDB, and is continually evolving to meet the scalability and complexity demands of high-volume messaging at ecommerce scale.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate has multiple years of experience leading both senior IC engineers at their career pinnacle and managers of smaller engineering teams. Additionally, the ideal candidate has a passion for operational excellence, high-volume distributed systems, running internal platforms for other engineering teams, and delivering great work that drives business value and improves the customer experience. Exposure to AI-powered product surfaces is a plus. Channels is the foundation that many of Postscript’s AI products are built on top of.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>This is a fully remote position based in the US or Canada.&nbsp;</strong></p>
