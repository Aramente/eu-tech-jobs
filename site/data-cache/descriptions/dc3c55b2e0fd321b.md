<p>We are seeking a seasoned Senior Manager – Finance Technology to lead our India-based FinTech engineering organization supporting global Finance systems. This leader will own strategy, architecture, delivery, and operational excellence across core finance platforms including NetSuite, Coupa, SAP Concur, and RPA/AI-driven automation solutions.</p>
<p>This role requires strong<strong> </strong>functional expertise in Finance (O2C, P2P, R2R, FP&amp;A, Treasury, Tax) combined with deep systems knowledge. The ideal candidate can bridge Finance, Engineering, and Business stakeholders while building a high-performing team in India.</p>
<p>We are looking to speak to candidates who are based in Gurugram for our hybrid working model.</p>

<ul>

<li>Drive global alignment between India engineering and US/EMEA Finance teams</li>
</ul>
