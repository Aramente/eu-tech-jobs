<h2><strong>About Parloa</strong></h2>
<p>Parloa’s mission is to make every customer conversation feel effortless for both customers and the companies serving them. As agentic AI accelerates, Parloans are shaping the foundation of a new era in customer experience, one where customer support is no longer transactions, but meaningful exchanges. It is not just a vision; Parloa has powered over ONE BILLION interactions between global enterprise brands and their customers, with companies like <a href="http://booking.com">Booking.com</a>, HealthEquity, Allianz, SAP, BarmeniaGothaer, and TUI already deploying Parloa at scale.</p>

<p>As an <strong>Engineering Manager</strong>&nbsp;at Parloa, you will be entrusted with one of the most critical missions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Building and leading a <strong>high-talent, high-performance team</strong> of engineers.</li>
<li>Ensuring that the team delivers <strong>enterprise-grade, resilient, and innovative AI solutions</strong> that customers love.</li>
<li>Raising the bar on <strong>technical excellence, cultural leadership, and operational discipline</strong>.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<p>You are both a <strong>builder and a leader</strong>. You dive deep into technical challenges, but your true leverage comes from how you <strong>develop engineers, scale teams, and drive alignment with Parloa’s strategic goals</strong>. <li><strong>Collaborate cross-functionally:</strong> Partner with Product, Design, and Business leaders to ensure technical execution and product vision are tightly aligned.</li>
</ul>
