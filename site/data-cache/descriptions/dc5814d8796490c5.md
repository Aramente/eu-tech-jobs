<p>As an Account Executive for Auth0 in Korea, you will be the driving force behind our territory growth. <ul>

<li><strong>Account Strategy:</strong> Identify, develop, and execute strategies for high-potential accounts to drive sales opportunities and deliver predictable bookings.</li>
<li><strong>Executive Alignment:</strong> Target and gain access to C-level leaders and decision-makers, building a robust network of influence within prospect accounts.</li>
<li><strong>Stakeholder Management:</strong> Highly experienced in running Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs) and maintaining executive stakeholder alignment.</li>
<li><strong>Full Sales Cycle:</strong> Scope, negotiate, and close complex agreements, ensuring a seamless transition from initial pitch to final contract.</li>
<li><strong>Ecosystem Collaboration:</strong> Access and utilize the Okta/Auth0 ecosystem (xDRs, Partners, Presales, Customer First) and maintain a proven track record of partnering with key SIs and Cloud providers like AWS.</li>
<li><strong>Technical Advocacy:</strong> Create compelling artifacts, documents, and presentations to convey technical ideas to both practitioners and executives.</li>
<li><strong>Travel:</strong> Travel as necessary to build and nurture face-to-face relationships with customers and prospects across the region.</li>

<h3><strong>What you’ll bring to the role:</strong></h3>
