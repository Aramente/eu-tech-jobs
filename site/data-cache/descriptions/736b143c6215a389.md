<p>Own the product vision, strategy, and execution for Algolia’s data collection and transformation capabilities within the Data Platform. <ul>
<li>Design and evolve capabilities that convert raw, heterogeneous data (e.g., JSON, CSV, databases, APIs, 3rd-party systems) into optimised, Algolia-ready formats.</li>
<li>Drive schema alignment, normalisation, and mapping so customers can quickly make their data searchable and usable.</li>
<li>Partner with Connector and Data Platform teams to provide seamless end-to-end ingestion + transformation workflows.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>2. Data Quality &amp; Platform Services</strong></p>
<ul>

<li>Define platform metrics and instrumentation that demonstrate business impact, such as integration time, error rates, and enrichment coverage.</li>
</ul>
