<h2><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Role Summary</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">As the Onboarding Lead for EMEA, you will be the architect of the first-year experience for our new hires across the region. You will design, manage, and deliver a world-class onboarding program that integrates new employees into our culture and accelerates their time-to-productivity. Working closely with global peers and regional stakeholders, you’ll balance local nuances with global consistency to ensure every new joiner feels supported and empowered. We are looking for a high-energy program manager who is as comfortable coaching executive speakers as they are diving into the data to measure program success.</span></p>
