<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<div>As the Supply Chain Manager for the UK, you will take full ownership of our regional procurement architecture during a period of significant scaling. This role is central to ensuring that our physical systems and AI technologies are supported by a resilient, compliant, and sovereign supply chain. You will lead the acquisition of critical hardware, manage essential contractor relationships, and build the procedural foundations required for high-stakes defence projects. Your impact will be measured by the robustness of our UK supplier network and our readiness to meet stringent audit and regulatory standards as we expand our local footprint.</div>
<h2><strong>The day-to-day</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Manage and oversee key contractors to ensure delivery timelines and quality standards are met across UK projects</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead the end-to-end procurement of specialised technical hardware, such as hydrophones, ensuring sovereign control over critical components</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Establish and implement rigorous supply chain processes to ensure the UK entity remains audit-ready and compliant with defence industry standards</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Optimise and support the use of SAP (S4 HANA) for accurate data entry, reporting, and streamlined procurement execution</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and execute a strategic roadmap for 2027, specifically focused on increasing the proportion of UK-based suppliers to enhance regional resilience</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with engineering and finance teams to translate technical requirements into actionable sourcing strategies with quantifiable cost and lead-time benefits</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identify potential supply chain bottlenecks and proactively implement mitigation strategies to maintain project momentum</p>
</li>
</ul>
