<h2>The Role:</h2>
<p>Ziina is looking for a Senior Data Platform Engineer to join our team. This role is an exciting opportunity to build the foundations of our data platform from the ground up — the pipelines, warehouse, and tooling that will power decision-making, analytics, and future ML/AI capabilities across the company. We're at a major inflection point in our growth, and we're looking for the right person to shape how data is ingested, modeled, and consumed at Ziina as we scale.</p>
<p>As part of a small, agile team, our ideal candidate is a creative problem-solver with the vision to build from the ground up and the skill to own systems end-to-end. You'll thrive in our fast-paced environment by making sound tradeoffs and focusing on high-impact solutions. We're looking for someone who is as ambitious as we are to deliver high-quality products to our users. In short, we want owners who are ready to build big things with us.</p>
<h2>As a Senior Data Platform Engineer at Ziina you will:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain scalable data pipelines and processing systems that ingest data from transactional databases, object storage, and external APIs</li>
<li>Design and evolve our data warehouse architecture and data models to support analytics, reporting, and ML/AI use cases</li>
<li>Improve the reliability, observability, and performance of our data systems</li>
<li>Implement data quality checks and monitoring to ensure trusted, accurate data across the company</li>
<li>Enable self-service data access and analytics so product, engineering, and business teams can make data-driven decisions independently</li>
<li>Support the foundational capabilities that will power our ML and AI use cases as they grow</li>
</ul>

<h2>Our tech stack:</h2>
<p>The data platform is a core investment area at Ziina. Our current stack is:</p>
<ul>
<li>Snowflake as our data warehouse, with dbt for data transformation and modeling</li>
<li>Dagster for orchestrating data pipelines, with Python for building ETL/ELT workflows</li>
<li>Data ingestion from PostgreSQL, S3, and external APIs, supporting both transactional and event-driven use cases</li>
<li>Metabase for analytics and internal reporting</li>
</ul>
<p>The broader Ziina engineering stack that the data platform integrates with:</p>
<ul>
<li>Typescript, Node.js and Nest.js for our main application's backend</li>
<li>GraphQL Federation for our client-facing APIs and Kafka for our inter-service communication</li>
<li>PostgresSQL for consistent and durable storage, Redis for quick fetching, Elasticsearch for quick searching</li>
<li>AWS for hosting our cloud infrastructure and Kubernetes for orchestrating our workloads</li>
<li>Terraform for IaC, GitHub Actions for CI/CD</li>
