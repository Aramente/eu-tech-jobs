<p>Auth0 protects over 5 billion login transactions monthly. Every pixel, interaction, and moment of friction shapes how millions experience digital identity. We're looking for a product designer with deep craft, strategic thinking, and the ability to lead through complexity.</p>
<p>You'll design experiences that developers and enterprise admins use daily—balancing simplicity with the sophistication that security demands.</p>
<h2><strong>What you'll do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Own end-to-end design for Auth0's Dashboard, Universal Login, and developer experiences. From discovery research through polished UI.</li>
<li>Lead strategic initiatives with product managers and engineering leads to define roadmaps and shape product direction</li>
<li>Create pixel-perfect interfaces with attention to visual design, interaction patterns, and accessibility</li>
<li>Prototype and validate ideas through iteration, user research, and data</li>
<li>Evolve our design system, establishing patterns that scale across Auth0 and Okta</li>
<li>Mentor designers and raise the bar for craft and process across the team</li>
<li>Represent user needs in a security-focused domain where clarity builds trust</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>7+ years designing digital products, with a portfolio showing strong visual design and systems thinking</li>
<li>Experience simplifying complex, multi-step workflows without losing power or flexibility</li>
<li>Strong prototyping skills in Figma or similar tools; comfort with code-based prototypes</li>
<li>Research fluency and knowing when to run usability tests, review analytics, or trust informed intuition</li>
<li>Clear communication that influences stakeholders across technical and non-technical audiences</li>
