Position Summary

• Title: Payroll Tax Supervisor

• Responsibilities include daily payroll tax processes covering federal, state, and local payroll tax compliance.

• Preparation of various payroll tax payments and returns.

• Training new department staff and providing essential tools for success.

• Strong problem-solving skills and customer service orientation required.

• Must develop relationships with clients across different industries and personalities.

• Knowledge of the PEO model is required.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

• Ensure timely federal, state, and local tax payments for multi-state entities.

• Understand PEO and ASO organizations related to state filing requirements.

• Manage reports and filings for CFO for AUP quarterly tax audits.

• Develop new guidelines and procedures for payroll tax processes.

• Train and assist in resolving complex tax reconciliations, variances, credits, and errors.

• Supervise and train staff to ensure quality daily processes and tax notice resolution.

• Manage EFTPS enrollments, annual wage reconciliation, payroll tax adjustments, W-2, and W-2C preparation.

• Maintain professional and technical payroll tax knowledge through workshops and research.

• Preferred experience in PEO or related industry.

• Hands-on tax return processing experience required.

• Strong knowledge of federal, state, and local wage laws.

• MasterTax and Prism/HRP experience preferred.

• Ability to analyze complex regulatory tax notices and data.

• Results-oriented with multi-tasking ability in a fast-paced environment.

• Strong analytical skills, attention to detail.

• Work independently and collaboratively with strong service skills.

• Intermediate to advanced Excel skills.
