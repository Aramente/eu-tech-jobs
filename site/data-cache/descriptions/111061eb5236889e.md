<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">We’re seeking a dynamic and strategic marketing leader to own and scale our UK and Ireland regional marketing motion.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">As the Lead Marketing Manager, UKI, you will be responsible for building high-quality, predictable pipeline for the regional sales team, with field marketing and demand generation as your primary focus. <p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">You will define and execute the UKI go-to-market strategy in alignment with regional revenue targets, territory priorities, and segment strategy. <p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">This role demands strong cross-functional alignment, operational discipline, and a clear focus on revenue impact.
