📄 JOB DESCRIPTION

Today we're looking for a software engineer who will join the Post Sales team. Post Sales is the product allowing Members to handle their personal data and settings, follow order delivery and raise questions to support.

## 🎯 TASKS

- Develop new and redesign existing features of our products;
- Suggest possible improvements that might be done;
- Keep the code clean and review your team members’ designs if needed;
- Ensure the reliability and scalability of developed applications;
- Respect the production deadlines;
- Review Merge Requests and collaborate with the team
- Write technical documentation;
- Handle the technical interviews with other developers willing to join <veepeeTech/>.

## 👉 MUST HAVE skills

- 5+ years of experience in software development;
- Hands-on experience with C# and .Net Core;
- Proficiency with SQL (MSSQL & Postgresql) databases;
- You are familiar with cloud programming tools and willing to extend your knowledge;
- You assure the quality of your code with unit and integration tests;
- You have experience in building APIs or other web services;
- You are fluent in English;
- You are an agile follower and open source contributor;
- You have experience coordinating small teams of developers;
- You are result and product-oriented.

## 👉 NICE TO HAVE skills

Experience with Javascript;
