<h2><strong>About the Opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for a highly motivated and resourceful Growth Marketing Manager to join our team and lead our expansion in the Spanish market.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This role is ideal for a results-driven professional who is eager to manage complex external partnerships and drive user acquisition while maintaining a high-impact, operational mindset across all performance marketing channels. You will join a high-performance team where you will be expected to manage everything from strategic negotiations to technical troubleshooting in a fast-paced environment.</p>
<h3><strong>In this role, you will:</strong></h3>
<h3><strong>Influencer and Affiliates Marketing</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Primary focus will be defining, optimizing and executing our Affiliate and Influencer marketing strategy, ensuring total alignment with our business goals and local market nuances.</li>
<li>Manage the day-to-day operations of the Spanish Affiliate and Influencer network, including pipeline building, relationship management, negotiation, and performance optimization.</li>
<li>Conduct data-driven research and analysis to support decision-making and provide the insights needed to scale channel performance. This includes deep dive analysis on acquisition rates, costs, profitability, product penetration and long term retention,</li>
<li>Own the reporting of channel performance, key insights, and strategic plans during weekly and monthly growth steering meetings.</li>
<li>Help define and improve marketing processes related to partnership tracking, attribution, and payout/budgeting for the Spanish market.</li>
<li>Ensure content compliance and internal rules and controls processing.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Growth Marketing</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and execute ad-hoc growth initiatives to drive user and revenue growth.</li>
<li>Develop and manage co-marketing campaigns with strategic business partners to expand reach and acquire new audiences.</li>
<li>Plan and coordinate offline growth initiatives, including traditional media placements, displays, and event activations.</li>
<li>Lead social media collaborations with key partners to increase brand visibility and engagement.</li>
<li>Coordinate content creation with external subject-matter experts and speakers to build public brand awareness and thought leadership.</li>
<li>Proactively<strong> </strong>identify and execute new growth opportunities<strong> </strong>and experimental initiatives that support overall marketing and business objectives.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What you need to be successful:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>The Foundation:</strong> +5 years of experience within high-paced marketing agencies or performance marketing roles, demonstrating a resourceful and versatile marketing background.</li>
<li><strong>Specialized Experience:</strong> At least 2 years of direct experience managing Affiliate and Influencer Marketing programs.</li>
<li><strong>Market Mastery:</strong> A proven track record of independently building and scaling new partner relationships specifically within the Spanish market.</li>
<li><strong>Data Excellence:</strong> Proficiency in data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Looker, or PowerBI) and advanced Excel/Google Sheets skills (Pivot tables, VLOOKUPs, data modeling) to audit partner performance and attribution models.</li>
<li><strong>Commercial Grit:</strong> Demonstrated ability to negotiate and structure diverse commercial deals, including CPA, CPL, fee structures, and commission tiers.</li>
<li><strong>Linguistic Fluency:</strong> Professional fluency in Spanish is required; fluency in English is essential for collaboration with our international team.</li>
<li><strong>The "Plus":</strong> Experience in fintech or banking is a significant advantage.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Skills:</strong></h3>
<ul>

<li><strong>Analytical Mindset:</strong> Strong ability to work with data, track KPIs, and extract actionable insights to drive strategic decisions.</li>
