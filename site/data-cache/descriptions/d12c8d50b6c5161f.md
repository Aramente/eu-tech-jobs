<p>At Wolt, you can grow your sales career with a fun and fast-scaling global business. Part of the DoorDash team, we’re building a global logistics platform that can deliver almost anything in your city in minutes. Today, our products are used by 55+ million registered users who order from 200,000+ merchants across 30+ countries.</p>
<p>To support our expanding merchant offering, we are scaling our Commerce Platform, which consists of two core products.<br><br>Wolt Commerce Platform Products in a Nutshell</p>
<ul>
<li>Wolt Drive – A delivery-as-a-service solution (DaaS) enabling merchants to add fast, reliable last-mile delivery to their own ordering channels.</li>
<li>Wolt Storefront – A white-label online ordering platform that helps merchants create branded digital stores with seamless checkout and increased conversion.</li>
</ul>
<p>The Drive &amp; Storefront Account Specialist role focuses purely on acquiring new partners for both products - selling the value, closing the deals, and handing partners over to onboarding teams.</p>
<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p>As Commerce Platform Sales Manager, your mission is clear: identify, pitch, and close new merchant partnerships for Wolt Drive and Wolt Storefront. You will build a strong pipeline, run a highly structured sales process, and secure high-quality, scalable partnerships.</p>
<p><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</strong></p>
<p><strong>Own the full outbound sales cycle</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Systematically explore and prospect the market to uncover new partnership opportunities.</li>
<li>Initiate and lead outreach to restaurants, retailers, and e-commerce merchants across verticals.</li>
<li>Deliver compelling product pitches for both Drive and Storefront, tailored to merchant needs.</li>
<li>Negotiate commercial terms and close deals that drive sustainable growth.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Manage a strong, disciplined pipeline</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Keep a structured pipeline with clear stages, next steps, and revenue potential.</li>
<li>Prioritize accounts based on strategic value, scalability, and readiness to adopt Commerce solutions.</li>
<li>Work toward monthly targets with precision and consistency.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Craft winning sales materials</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and refine pitch decks, case studies, and proposals to support high-impact conversations.</li>
<li>Translate product capabilities into clear merchant value propositions.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>&nbsp;Collaborate with internal teams</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Coordinate with onboarding and operational teams to ensure a smooth handover after closing.</li>
<li>Share market insights with Product, Ops, and Support — without owning implementations or account management.</li>
</ul>

<p>You’ll succeed in this role if you:</p>
<ul>

<li>Have a proven track record of closing deals and consistently hitting sales targets.</li>
<li>Are a natural hunter — proactive, persistent, and driven by pipeline momentum.</li>
<li>Possess excellent communication and pitching skills, with the ability to simplify complex solutions.</li>
<li>Use data to support negotiation and refine sales decisions.</li>
