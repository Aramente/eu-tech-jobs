<h3><strong>The Role:</strong></h3>
<p>We are a digital business where continuous uptime and a smooth IT experience are central to our success. We are seeking a proactive, driven <strong>IT Technician</strong> to join our IT team. This is a fantastic entry-level opportunity for a technically minded individual eager to kick-start a career in technology.</p>
<p>From day one, you will be a key part of the team, learning to manage your time and responsibilities, approaching problems with a positive, service-oriented mindset, and taking genuine ownership of our IT environment. The majority focus of the role is first-line end-user support across devices, applications, access, and connectivity. Other focuses include office infrastructure support such as AV/meeting rooms, asset management, office moves/expansions, and new technology rollouts.</p>
<p>You’ll join a fast-growing, motivated team with clear progression. We provide hands-on training, modern tooling, and structured development so you can build breadth and depth quickly.</p>
<p>This is a full-time, office-based role requiring on-site presence to support colleagues directly.</p>
<h3><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Serve as the first point of contact</strong> via our service desk and support channels (walk-ups, email, chat): log, categorize, prioritize, resolve, or escalate with clear ticket notes and timely user updates.</li>
<li><strong>Identity &amp; access (Okta):</strong> assist with sign-in issues, MFA re-registration, factor resets, and basic SSO troubleshooting; coordinate with identity administrators for changes requiring elevated privileges.</li>
<li><strong>Collaboration suites (Google Workspace &amp; Microsoft 365):</strong> perform email troubleshooting, password resets, account unlocks/suspends, basic group membership updates, and license assignment under least-privilege roles.</li>
<li><strong>Joiner/Mover/Leaver (JML):</strong> prepare equipment, provision/adjust access, recover assets, and de-provision accounts in line with policy.</li>
<li><strong>Endpoint support:</strong> troubleshoot macOS/iOS (Apple-first) and foundational Windows issues: Wi-Fi/VPN, printing, peripherals, software installs/updates, and device configuration.</li>
<li><strong>Device deployment &amp; management:</strong> enroll and support Macs via <strong>Jamf</strong> (with Apple Business Manager/Automated Device Enrollment) and Windows devices via <strong>Microsoft Intune</strong>; follow build standards and compliance checks.</li>
<li><strong>Asset management:</strong> track inventory and assignments, maintain stock levels, and ensure accurate records for audits.</li>
<li><strong>Meeting room &amp; AV readiness:</strong> perform routine checks, provide first-line support for conference rooms and events, and coordinate vendor engagement when required.</li>
<li><strong>Office operations:</strong> support office moves/expansions and new technology rollouts; perform basic network smart-hands tasks (e.g., patching/labeling under guidance).</li>
<li><strong>Knowledge &amp; quality:</strong> create/update simple “how-to” articles and quick-resolution guides to drive first-contact resolution and consistent support.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Who you are:</strong></h3>
<p>This is an <strong>entry-level</strong> position suitable for someone starting an IT career.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>A-Levels or equivalent</strong> qualification.</li>
<li>People-oriented and emotionally intelligent communicator (in <strong>English</strong>); able to explain technical topics simply and <strong>de-escalate</strong> calmly.</li>
<li>Positive, team-oriented, and <strong>composed under pressure</strong> with a natural willingness to go the extra mile.</li>
<li>Strong <strong>problem-solver</strong> comfortable with ambiguity who <strong>takes ownership</strong> through to resolution.</li>
<li><strong>Growth mindset</strong> with eagerness to learn and develop new technical skills in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrable passion for technology, especially <strong>Apple (macOS/iOS)</strong>, with a willingness to learn <strong>Windows</strong>.</li>
<li>Basic understanding of <strong>IT concepts</strong> (operating systems) and interest in <strong>Okta</strong>, <strong>Google Workspace</strong>, and <strong>Microsoft 365</strong> administration.</li>
<li><strong>Nice to have:</strong> CompTIA A+, Apple device support/deployment learning, Okta fundamentals, and Microsoft certifications.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What you’ll gain:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Meaningful responsibility from day one:</strong> own well-defined problems and see them through to resolution with support from experienced engineers.</li>
<li><strong>Structured development:</strong> hands-on training and mentorship across Okta, Google Workspace, M365, Jamf, Intune, modern AV, and service-management practices.</li>
<li><strong>Certification support:</strong> encouragement and resources to pursue industry-recognised certifications (e.g., CompTIA, Apple, Microsoft).</li>
<li><strong>Clear progression path:</strong> opportunities to advance into IT Engineer, Senior IT Engineer,&nbsp; or <strong>specialise</strong> in areas like cybersecurity or network administration.</li>
<li><strong>High-performance environment:</strong> collaborative team culture with high standards, constructive feedback, and continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Work model:</strong> Onsite; occasional flexibility for early/late support during moves, rollouts, or maintenance windows.</p>
