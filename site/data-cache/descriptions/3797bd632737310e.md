<h2>About the opportunity</h2>
<p>Are you ready to take the next step in your career? N26 is looking for a Team Lead to lead and further develop the Risk Governance &amp; Capital Planning team.</p>
<p>In this role, you will take ownership of key risk controlling processes across capital and liquidity adequacy, with a focus on Pillar 1 topics within the normative perspective of the ICAAP framework as well as ILAAP. You will ensure alignment with regulatory requirements and internal risk strategy while further strengthening governance frameworks, methodologies, and risk controlling practices.</p>
<p>As the lead of the team, you will guide and develop team members and play a key role in assessing, controlling, and reporting N26’s risks. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional stakeholders and act as a key counterpart for auditors and regulators.</p>
<h2>In this role, you will:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Lead and develop the Risk Governance &amp; Capital Planning team, providing guidance, mentorship, and expertise across ICAAP (Pillar 1) and ILAAP topics</li>
<li>Own and further enhance capital planning processes and Pillar 1 ICAAP governance, including stress testing frameworks and scenario design</li>
<li>Own and further develop the ILAAP framework, including liquidity risk methodologies, stress testing, and key liquidity risk metrics</li>
<li>Oversee key risk governance components, including RWA calculation, risk appetite framework, risk policies, limit setting and monitoring, and internal controls</li>
<li>Own and further develop the risk inventory and recovery plan (Sanierungsplan), ensuring consistency across capital and liquidity perspectives</li>
<li>Drive the continuous improvement of risk reporting across ICAAP and ILAAP, delivering high-quality, data-driven insights for senior management</li>
<li>Ensure strong data governance and data quality in line with BCBS 239 principles for key inputs used for capital and liquidity risk assessments (e.g. balance sheet, RWA, liquidity metrics).</li>
<li>Ensure adherence to regulatory requirements (e.g., MaRisk, CRR, ICAAP and ILAAP guidelines, EBA regulatory expectations), act as key contact for auditors and regulators, and collaborate closely with cross-functional stakeholders (e.g., Finance, Treasury, Risk)</li>
<li>Leverage a passion for and a track record of using state-of-the-art technology to simplify processes, automate risk controlling workflows, and increase overall operational efficiency.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What you need to be successful:</h2>
<p><strong>Background:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Master’s degree in business/economics, finance, mathematics, or a related field</li>
<li>7+ years of experience in risk management, risk controlling, capital planning, liquidity risk, or a related function within a regulated financial institution or leading consultancy</li>
<li>Strong experience across ICAAP, capital planning, and/or ILAAP and liquidity risk management</li>
<li>Proven experience in leading or mentoring team members and driving complex initiatives</li>

<p><strong>Skills:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong understanding of ICAAP frameworks (Pillar 1 focus), including capital planning, risk inventory, stress testing, and recovery planning</li>

</ul>
