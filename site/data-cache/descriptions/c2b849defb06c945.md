<ul>

<li>Drive data analysis initiatives: Define problems, prepare and preprocess data, conduct statistical analyses, and validate results to support product decisions</li>
<li>Quantify the impact of product releases: Measure and analyze the impact of new features and algorithmic changes through before/after analysis, sizing, hypothesis testing, and segmentation</li>
<li>Build and maintain performance dashboards: Create and maintain dashboards for your pillar and squads to track functional adoption and key performance indicators, including shared dashboards with Customer Success and Sales teams.</li>
<li>Automate and build internal AI-powered tools: Create tools to facilitate team collaboration and automate recurring tasks, such as specialized support agents for PSP switch projects, product Q&amp;A agents, and benchmarking tools specific to your functional scope<br><br></li>
</ul>
