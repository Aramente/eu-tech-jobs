<p>About the Role</p>
<p>Adyen is looking for a <strong>Software Engineer</strong> to join our <strong>Streaming Platform </strong>team. This team is part of the Platform Engineering group and is at the heart of Adyen's real-time data streaming capabilities, providing the essential infrastructure and services that power critical(e.g. Reporting, Observability, Telemetry) operations across the company. You will play a key role in designing, scaling, building, and maintaining highly reliable and scalable streaming solutions that empower all engineers at Adyen to leverage real-time data.</p>
<p>Our team is responsible for providing robust services around <strong>Apache Kafka</strong> and <strong>Apache Flink</strong>, ensuring the utmost reliability for real-time data processing. We strive to create a seamless and efficient streaming experience, enabling our product engineering teams to build innovative features and make data-driven decisions at lightning speed.</p>
<p>What You'll Do</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, develop, and deploy scalable, high-performance streaming services and platforms primarily involving <strong>Apache Kafka</strong> and <strong>Apache Flink</strong>.</li>
<li>Enhance the quality, reliability, and performance of our existing streaming infrastructure.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product engineers and other platform engineering teams, to build and deliver effective and scalable solutions.</li>
<li>Drive the company-wide adoption of best practices for real-time data streaming and stream processing&nbsp;</li>
<li>Contribute to the strategic vision and roadmap for Adyen's streaming platform.</li>
<li>Provide technical guidance and mentorship to other engineers within the team.</li>
<li>Provide technical support to streaming platform customers.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Participate in on-call rotations&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p>Who You Are</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>7+ years </strong>of backend engineering experience with strong knowledge of distributed systems</li>
<li>Deep understanding of <strong>Java</strong>, including <em>util.concurrent</em> and concurrency primitives, dependency injection principles. Good scripting skills and ability to pick up new languages.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Deep understanding of stream processing concepts and hands-on experience with <strong>Apache Kafka</strong> Or <strong>Apache Flink</strong>. Experience with other streaming technologies is a strong plus</li>
<li>Experience with highly available/fault tolerant, replicated data storage systems, large scale data processing systems is a strong plus</li>
<li>Experience with using and building on <strong>Kubernetes</strong> is a strong plus</li>
<li>Infrastructure and Platform Experience&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative, and dynamic environment.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
</ul>
