<p><strong>About Axiom:</strong></p>
<p></p>
<p>Axiom is a leading global marketplace for on‑demand legal talent and a pioneer in redefining how legal services are delivered. For more than 25 years, Axiom has disrupted the traditional reliance on law firms and in‑house legal teams by connecting highly credentialed lawyers and legal professionals with impactful work across industries and practice areas.</p>
<p>Today, Axiom serves more than half of the Fortune 100 and is recognised for market‑leading Net Promoter Scores from both clients and employees. Our model enables clients to operate more efficiently while empowering legal professionals to pursue more of the work they love.</p>
