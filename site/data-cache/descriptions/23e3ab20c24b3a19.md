<h3><strong>What You'll Do</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Location: </strong>Remote anywhere in Australia</p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to building and maintaining reliable, scalable, and secure cloud platforms and tools.</li>
<li>Apply site reliability engineering principles to improve system stability and performance.</li>
<li>Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code</li>
<li>Troubleshoot and debug issues in distributed cloud-native systems.</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineering teams to support service reliability and operational excellence.</li>
<li>Create and maintain documentation to support engineers and improve self-service capabilities.</li>
<li>Participate in on-call rotations and incident response, learning best practices along the way.</li>
<li>Continuously develop your skills and contribute to team knowledge sharing.</li>
</ul>

<h3><strong>What You Bring </strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>2–5 years of relevant experience in software engineering, SRE, or infrastructure roles</li>
<li>Solid understanding of operating systems, preferably Linux</li>
<li>Experience with cloud platforms such as Azure, AWS, or similar</li>
<li>Experience with Kubernetes platforms like AKS, EKS, or similar</li>
<li>Experience with at least one programming language such as Python, Go, C#, Java, or similar</li>
<li>Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and automation practices</li>
<li>Experience with observability tools (logging, metrics, APM)</li>
<li>Exposure to Infrastructure as Code concepts (e.g., Terraform, ARM, etc.)</li>
<li>Strong problem-solving skills and a willingness to learn</li>
<li>Good communication and collaboration skills</li>
<li><strong>Australian government security clearance, or ability to obtain clearance (AUS citizenship) required</strong></li>
</ul>
