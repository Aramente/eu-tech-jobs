### **Mission Statement**

The Platform team creates the technology that enables Spotify to learn quickly and scale easily, enabling rapid growth in our users and our business around the globe. Spanning many disciplines, we work to make the business work; creating the infrastructure, tooling, frameworks, and capabilities needed to welcome a billion customers.

### **About the Team**

You’ll join the Data Platform within the Platform mission, where we build the foundations that help Spotify teams better understand and serve our listeners. Our work enables engineers and data scientists to explore user behavior at scale and make smarter, faster decisions.

As part of a collaborative and distributed engineering team, you’ll help shape the tools and SDKs that power data-driven product development across Spotify.

## What You'll Do

- Build and evolve Android SDKs that enable engineers and data scientists to analyze user behavior at scale
- Contribute to a high-performing, autonomous team of engineers, designers, and product managers
- Develop scalable, testable, and maintainable code used across multiple teams and projects
- Help define and improve Android engineering practices across Spotify
- Collaborate closely with platform and data teams to deliver impactful solutions
- Drive innovation and continuously improve developer productivity and experience
- Support the broader Android community at Spotify and influence technical direction

## Who You Are

- You are experienced in building Android applications and SDKs at scale
- You have a deep understanding of modern Android development and tooling
- You know how to write readable, maintainable, and performant code in Kotlin
- You have some working experience with C++
- You are comfortable supporting iOS if the need arises
- You care about quality, testing practices, and performance
- You are comfortable working in a collaborative, cross-functional environment
- You have experience working with large-scale systems or data-heavy environments
- You are curious, proactive, and eager to learn new technologies and domains
- You are familiar with developer tooling, build systems, or data-related technologies

## Where You'll Be

- This role is based in London
- We offer you the flexibility to work where you work best! There will be some in person meetings, but still allows for flexibility to work from home.
