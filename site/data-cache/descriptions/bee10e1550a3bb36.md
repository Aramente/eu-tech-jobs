#### **The Mission**

Qblox is scaling at lightning speed. As we lead the revolution in quantum computing control stacks, our internal structures must evolve just as fast as our technology. We are looking for a seasoned **Project Lead** to act as the glue between our strategic ambitions and operational reality.

In this role, you won't just be managing tasks; you will be building the infrastructure that allows our global Go-To-Market (GTM) and Support teams to thrive. Reporting directly to the CRO, you will have a front-row seat to our transformation and a direct impact on our international footprint.

#### **Key Responsibilities**

You will lead high-impact projects that streamline how we sell, support, and scale. Your core focus areas will include:

* **Global Onboarding Excellence:** Partner with leadership to design and implement a world-class onboarding program for our global GTM teams, ensuring new hires hit the ground running.
* **Support Optimization:** Drive the evolution of our global support and customer success programs, improving workflows and tools to maintain our high standards of customer success as we grow.
* **Playbook Development:** Codify our "secret sauce" by building comprehensive playbooks for sales, account management, and support operations.
* **Operational Transformation:** Identify bottlenecks in our current growth trajectory and implement scalable solutions and standardized processes.
* **Stakeholder Alignment:** Act as the primary point of coordination between Sales, Marketing, Support, and Finance to ensure project milestones are met.
