<p><strong>About the team:</strong></p>
<p>As we expand our product offering and international footprint, we are seeking a Transfer Pricing Operations Specialist with technical transfer pricing knowledge and operational transfer pricing experience. This role will focus on streamlining and enhancing transfer pricing processes, and implementing scalable transfer pricing tools, automation, and workflow solutions.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<p>The Transfer Pricing Specialist will be a hands-on team member, who works collaboratively across the organization to implement and execute compliant and practical transfer pricing policies and processes. This individual will be an excellent communicator, with the ability to explain transfer pricing concepts and requirements to non-tax people. The ideal candidate will have experience in a high-growth technology or financial services company, partnering with various functional teams and other members of the tax department.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Execute and implement transfer pricing policies.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Dive deep into financial information to: (1) design and implement transfer pricing calculations; (2) streamline the process of transfer pricing calculation preparation and review; (3) manage transfer pricing automation tools.</li>
<li>Design efficient utilization of software tools, processes and procedures for transfer pricing calculations and intercompany transaction reporting.</li>
<li>Build and/or streamline transfer pricing models for clarity, efficiency, and ease of use.</li>
<li>Enable transfer pricing process improvement efforts with an eye for automation, efficiency, and simplification.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Partner with internal stakeholders (such as Internal Systems,&nbsp; Accounting, and Legal) to support process improvement efforts.</li>
<li>Adapt transfer pricing tools and processes to business changes.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who you are:</strong></p>
<p>We’re looking for someone who meets the minimum requirements to be considered for the role. If you meet these requirements, you are encouraged to apply. The preferred qualifications are a bonus, not a requirement.</p>
