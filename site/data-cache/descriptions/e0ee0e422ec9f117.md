**At NordPass, we’re making password security effortless.**

We believe logins should be safer and simpler. Join the team behind NordPass— a password manager trusted by 7M+ users and 10,000+ global companies to secure credentials, cards, and sensitive data.

**Your impact?** Helping millions secure their digital lives by creating smarter, safer ways to log in.

This isn't your typical "manage a backlog and run standups" PM role. You'll be the strategic glue across three worlds that rarely intersect: product growth metrics, compliance labyrinths, and cutting-edge tech innovation. One day you're optimizing activation funnels. Next, you're untangling GDPR requirements. By Friday, you're spec'ing out CLI tools or getting us ready for the agentic AI authentication revolution.

If that sounds overwhelming, this isn't for you. If it sounds exhilarating, keep reading.

## Main Responsibilities

- Lead a fully cross‑functional, autonomous NordPass product team;
- Own product discovery, validation, and roadmap planning and turn insights into shipped value;
- Define and track a focused set of product health metrics (e.g., activation, adoption, retention) to guide daily, monthly, and quarterly decisions;
- Partner closely with stakeholders across user research, sales, marketing, legal, application security, and data analytics to drive aligned outcomes;
- Operate as a triad with your Engineering Manager and Product Designer to balance near‑term ROI with bold, long‑term bets;
- Champion and enforce Secure Software Development Life Cycle (SSDLC) practices throughout delivery;
- Develop deep expertise in authentication and identity for both consumer and business segments, translating domain trends into product tactics;
- Explore and shape the future of secure browsing and agentic AI–ready authentication; incubate innovations such as developer‑friendly CLIs and integrations;
- Run experiments, synthesize findings, and iterate quickly while maintaining high quality and compliance;
- Communicate decisions clearly, document trade‑offs, and align teams around crisp goals and measurable outcomes;

## Core Requirements

- 3+ years in product management, ideally in SaaS;
- Comfort with analytics and growth metrics. You know your way around activation funnels and cohort analysis;
- Technical fluency. You don't need to code, but you can hold your own with engineers discussing APIs, CLIs, or AI authentication protocols;
- A generalist mindset. You thrive in ambiguity and context-switching doesn't drain you, it energizes you;
- Exceptional communication skills. You translate between business, legal, and engineering effortlessly;
