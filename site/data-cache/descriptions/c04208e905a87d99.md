Spanning many disciplines, we work to make the business work; creating the infrastructure, tooling, frameworks, and capabilities needed to welcome a billion customers.  
About the Team  
  
The Platform Design team is on a mission to fuel innovation through design. We don’t just build tools — we shape ecosystems and create high-impact solutions that help organizations thrive in an increasingly complex digital world.  
As part of the Shipping Our Expertise (SOE) team, you’ll help productize the “Spotify way” into a new generation of SaaS products. This is a unique opportunity to define how leading companies — from global enterprises to fast-moving startups — operate, collaborate, and scale.

## What You'll Do

- Frame complex problem spaces and define clear design strategies, exploring multiple directions before landing on strong, well-reasoned solutions
- Own the end-to-end design quality of sophisticated user journeys, ensuring consistency across UX, interaction, and visual language
- Contribute to and evolve a scalable design system for a growing portfolio of external-facing SaaS products
- Design intuitive, high-utility experiences for a wide range of users, from executives to technical specialists
- Translate ideas into clear artifacts such as strategy briefs, high-fidelity concepts, flows, and product specifications
- Represent design in cross-functional forums and influence decisions in high-impact product discussions
- Partner closely with Product, Engineering, and Data Science to shape strategy and challenge assumptions
- Support and mentor other designers, helping raise the bar for craft, clarity, and impact across the team

## Who You Are

- You have deep experience designing complex digital products, ideally within platforms, SaaS environments, or scaled tech ecosystems
- You bring strong visual design instincts and care about the details while delivering practical, high-impact user experiences
- You understand how to translate brand into product, creating experiences that feel consistent, human, and purposeful
- You have worked closely with cross-functional partners and know how to navigate ambiguity in fast-moving environments
- You are comfortable shaping product narratives and have experience bringing ideas to life through content and storytelling
- You can simplify complex workflows and design for diverse user needs across different roles and contexts
- You communicate clearly and support your ideas with thoughtful rationale, insights, and data
- You are curious, adaptable, and motivated to grow in an evolving SaaS and platform landscape

## Where You'll Be

- This role is based in London (UK) or Stockholm (Sweden).
- We offer you the flexibility to work where you work best! There will be some in person meetings, but still allows for flexibility to work from home.
