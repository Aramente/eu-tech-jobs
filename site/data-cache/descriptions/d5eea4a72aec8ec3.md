<h2><strong>Who we are</strong></h2>
<p>Helsing is a defence AI company. Our mission is to protect our democracies. We aim to achieve technological leadership, so that open societies can continue to make sovereign decisions and control their ethical standards.<br>As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously.<br>We are an ambitious and committed team of engineers, AI specialists and customer-facing programme managers. We are looking for mission-driven people to join our European teams – and apply their skills to solve the most complex and impactful problems. We embrace an open and transparent culture that welcomes healthy debates on the use of technology in defence, its benefits, and its ethical implications.</p>
<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<p>We are seeking a People Business Partner to join our Paris team for a 6-month maternity cover. This is a builder-operator role focused on rigorous performance development and partnering directly with leaders as a trusted HRBP.&nbsp;This is a builder and operator role. You will design scalable processes from the ground up, run them end-to-end, and work closely with Helsing’s leadership to ensure our people are equipped to deliver on our mission of defending democratic values whilst partnering with the business regarding all things People.</p>

<li class="text-start "><strong>Strategic Partnering:</strong> Advise leadership on organisational design, talent planning, and effectiveness, providing data-driven guidance to navigate rapid scaling.</li>
<li class="text-start "><strong>Capability Building:</strong> Design and deploy scalable playbooks, tools, and training that equip managers and individual contributors with the capabilities required for a high-velocity environment.</li>

<li class="text-start "><strong>Legal &amp; Compliance Leadership:</strong> Manage sensitive individual cases in coordination with Legal, ensuring strict adherence to French labour laws.</li>
<li class="text-start "><strong>CSE Representation:</strong> Serve as the primary representative for the CSE (Comité Social et Économique) in France, fostering productive collaboration between management and employee representatives.</li>
</ul>
