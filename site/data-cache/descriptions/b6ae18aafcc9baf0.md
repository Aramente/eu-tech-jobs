<p>We are now looking for an Enterprise Sales Manager to join our Retail team in Germany. In this role, you will play a critical part in driving Wolt’s growth within the Enterprise retail segment, owning complex sales cycles and building long-term strategic partnerships with some of the largest retailers in the market.</p>
<h2>What you’ll be doing:</h2>
<p>As an Enterprise Sales Manager, you will be responsible for owning the full sales lifecycle for a portfolio of strategic retail accounts—from pipeline generation through to deal closing and long-term account growth. You will operate in a highly cross-functional environment, working closely with internal stakeholders while engaging senior decision-makers on the client side.</p>
<h2>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Own end-to-end sales execution for Enterprise retail accounts, including pipeline development, negotiation, and deal closure&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Build and manage a strong pipeline (3–5x quota), ensuring consistent deal flow and high conversion rates&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Lead complex negotiations with C-level and senior stakeholders across large retail organizations&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Develop and execute account plans to drive long-term growth, retention, and expansion&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with Operations, Product, Marketing, Finance, and Legal to structure and implement deals&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Drive data-informed decision-making using pipeline analytics, forecasting tools, and performance metrics&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Leverage AI tools for deal prioritization, account research, and forecasting accuracy&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ensure smooth handovers from signed deals to operational teams, minimizing friction and accelerating time-to-launch&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<h2>Our humble expectations:</h2>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience in Retail, E-commerce, Marketplace, or Omnichannel environments&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong track record in Enterprise B2B sales, including ownership of pipeline, deal closing, and revenue targets&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Proven experience managing long and complex sales cycles with multiple stakeholders&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Excellent negotiation and stakeholder management skills, including C-level engagement&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong commercial and financial acumen (pricing, unit economics, deal structuring)&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Ability to operate in ambiguous, fast-paced environments and drive deals forward independently&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Data-driven mindset with experience in pipeline management and forecasting&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong cross-functional collaboration skills&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<h2>Nice to have:</h2>
