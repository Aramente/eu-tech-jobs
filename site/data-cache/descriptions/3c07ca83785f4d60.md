<p>Vercel is hiring a Head of Commercial sales, EMEA (excl. DACH) to lead and scale our Commercial sales team across EMEA (excl. DACH). This is a high-impact leadership role for someone who thrives in a fast-paced, high-velocity environment and knows how to build the systems, culture, and talent needed to consistently hit and exceed revenue targets in the mid-market and growth segment.</p>
<p>Commercial is one of Vercel's most important growth levers in EMEA - it's where we convert developer love and self-service momentum into contracted, expanding revenue with scaling tech companies and digital-native businesses. This leader will own that motion end-to-end: hiring and developing a team of Commercial AEs, driving pipeline efficiency, and ensuring Vercel is well-positioned to win in a competitive and fast-moving market.</p>
<p>You'll be based in London and work closely with Marketing, SDR leadership, Solutions Engineering, and Customer Success to build a repeatable, scalable commercial sales engine across the region.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>If you’re based within a pre-determined commuting distance of one of our offices (SF, NY, London, or Berlin), the role includes in-office anchor days on Monday, Tuesday, and Friday. <h2>What You Will Do:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Own the EMEA Commercial sales number - pipeline, forecasting, and revenue attainment across the mid-market and growth segment</li>
<li>Hire, onboard, and develop a team of Commercial AEs, building a culture of high activity, sharp qualification, and consistent execution</li>
<li>Drive a high-velocity sales motion with shorter cycles, strong outbound discipline, and efficient deal management</li>
<li>Coach AEs on territory planning, prospecting strategies, and how to sell to technical and business stakeholders at scaling companies</li>
<li>Partner with Marketing and SDR leadership to build and convert pipeline across EMEA - inbound and outbound</li>
<li>Develop and iterate on Commercial-specific messaging and positioning that resonates with engineering leaders and CTOs at growth-stage companies</li>
<li>Accurately forecast and report on team activity and revenue to EMEA sales leadership</li>
<li>Build scalable processes, playbooks, and onboarding frameworks that let the team ramp quickly and perform consistently</li>
<li>Collaborate cross-functionally with CS, Product, and Legal to ensure smooth handoffs and strong retention in the commercial segment</li>
<li>Identify expansion opportunities within the existing commercial customer base and coach AEs to grow accounts over time</li>
<li>Represent Vercel at EMEA industry events, developer conferences, and customer-facing engagements</li>
</ul>
