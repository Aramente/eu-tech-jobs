<div><strong>We are looking for a Senior Account Executive to own and grow Elastic's business across the Defence and Police in Sweden, Norway, and Finland.</strong><br>You will work with the Armed Forces, Ministries of Defence, Police authorities, and related national security agencies, driving pipeline, building relationships, and closing complex deals in some of the most demanding buying environments that exist.&nbsp;This role is not for generalists. It is for someone who has been in this world long enough to understand how it works, and who has the network, credibility, and patience to succeed in it.<br><br>Eligibility for national security clearance is a hard requirement.<br><br><strong>What You Will Do</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Own strategic accounts across Defence, Armed Forces, and Police in all three countries</li>
<li>Engage senior stakeholders across IT, operations, intelligence, and leadership</li>
<li>Navigate government procurement, tenders, and compliance frameworks with confidence</li>
<li>Position Elastic around use cases like cyber defence, operational intelligence, situational awareness, and large-scale data analysis</li>
<li>Lead full deal cycles from identification and qualification through negotiation and close</li>
<li>Collaborate with Solutions Architects, partners, and leadership on complex, security-sensitive engagements</li>
</ul>
<div><strong>What You Bring</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Direct experience working with or selling into Defence, Police, or national security organisations in the Nordics, this is non-negotiable</li>
<li>An established network and the ability to open doors across relevant agencies</li>
<li>Proven track record closing large, complex deals in regulated or government environments</li>
<li>Understanding of military operations, intelligence workflows, or public safety environments</li>
<li>The ability to work independently and build trust with highly demanding stakeholders</li>
<li>Eligibility for national security clearance</li>
<li>Fluency in Swedish and strong English; additional Nordic languages are a plus</li>
</ul>
<div><strong>What Sets You Apart</strong><br>A background in Defence, Police, or intelligence. Experience selling into classified or sensitive environments. The kind of domain knowledge that earns credibility before a pitch even starts.</div>
