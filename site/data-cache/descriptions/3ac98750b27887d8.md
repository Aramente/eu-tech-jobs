Hidden Valley Eye Care is a growing family practice located in Draper, UT. We are seeking a full-time Optician to assist our doctor and work closely with patients. Pay rate is competitive and based on experience. The right candidate will have great customer service skills, pay attention to detail, and multi-task efficiently. Duties include, but are not limited to:

Using Electronic Medical Records to record exams, materials, etc.

Pre-testing patients

Assisting patients with picking frames and providing spectacle lens packages/quotes

Taking measurements to ensure lenses are made correctly

Teaching new contact wearers how to insert and remove contact lenses

Ordering and dispensing contact lenses and spectacles

Adjusting frames

Scheduling appointments

Verifying vision insurance and submitting claims

Tagging new inventory.

Light cleaning

Providing exceptional customer service

Qualifications:

Strong multi-tasking abilities

Customer service oriented

Ability to work independently and as part of a team

ABO certification and optical experience not required, but is a plus
