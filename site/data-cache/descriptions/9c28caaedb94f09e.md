Join us as a **Growth Ops Engineer (Salesforce)** and design, build, and improve the systems that enable Qonto's Growth teams to operate efficiently and scale across 8 European markets.

You'll turn complex business needs into scalable technical solutions — in a fast-evolving, AI-driven environment — under the guidance of **Julien Gateaud**, our Growth Ops Engineering Lead.

### ➡️ What you'll do

* **Analyse and solve business problems end-to-end:** Dig into root causes, break topics into actionable steps, and implement solutions across Salesforce, Segment, and data tools — no hand-holding required.
* **Build scalable technical solutions:** Design and develop robust **Salesforce** features using **Apex** and **LWC**, automations, and integrations that directly improve the performance and efficiency of our Sales, Partnerships, and Account Management teams.
* **Own high-impact topics end-to-end:** Take full ownership of your scope, ship solutions with measurable business impact, and partner closely with PMs and Growth teams to keep delivery quality high.
* **Own the stack's reliability:** Ensure production quality and maintainability — audit, clean, and document what you build so the system stays solid as Qonto scales.
* **AI Leveraging:** be at the core of optimizing revenue generation and improving operational efficiency across the growth engine.

### ➡️ What we're looking for

* **Salesforce engineering experience:** You have hands-on experience building solutions using **Apex and LWC** in a production Salesforce org
* **Architecture mindset:** You design scalable and maintainable systems, and make sound technical decisions beyond quick fixes.
* **Strong data modeling skills**: You design efficient object models, manage relationships, and ensure data integrity and performance at scale.
* **Autonomous problem-solver:** You identify root causes, structure solutions independently, and proactively move things forward without waiting to be told what to do.
* **Maximize value for your users:** You deeply understand how Sales, Partnerships, and Account Management teams work, and you design solutions that maximize the value they get out of every feature you ship.

### ➡️ What you'll get

* **A role at the heart of Qonto’s growth engine**: Growth Ops Engineering plays a key role in driving revenue and boosting operational efficiency, with direct, measurable impact.
* **Exposure to both technical depth and business challenges:** You'll work on Salesforce, MarTech stacks, data tooling, and AI topics — a rare mix that accelerates your technical and strategic growth.
* **An AI-first team culture:** The team runs on different LLM models, maintains dedicated AI development guidelines, and actively experiments — you'll be encouraged to automate yourself into better problems.
* **A profile that goes beyond Salesforce:** you leverage a broad, evolving toolstack to solve growth and ops challenges, not limiting yourself to a single ecosystem.

### ➡️ Your future manager

You'll report to [**Julien Gateaud**,](https://www.linkedin.com/in/juliengateaud/) our Growth Ops Engineering Lead. Julien combines strong technical expertise in Salesforce and systems with a genuine business-oriented mindset. Don't expect micro-management — he's focused on enabling your autonomy, encouraging real problem-solving, and helping you build solutions that last.
