<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<div>
<p>Finance at Helsing is being built from first principles. There are no legacy systems, no inherited processes, and no established playbooks. As FP&amp;A Team Lead, you will be one of the first senior finance hires and will shape the function from the ground up — designing the planning infrastructure, the reporting architecture, the capital model, and the financial narrative that will take Helsing through its next phase of growth and towards a public listing.</p>
<p>This is not a role where you will inherit a tool stack or a process. You will build both. Working as a financial partner to the CEO and VP Business Finance, you will produce the numbers that steer the business, the models that support capital allocation decisions, and the investor-grade reporting that positions Helsing for IPO. You will operate at the intersection of defence contract economics — cost-plus, time and materials, fixed-price, government milestone billing — and modern financial architecture. You will need to understand both, model both, and explain both to a Board that includes some of the most sophisticated investors in European technology.</p>
</div>
<h2><strong>The day-to-day</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Financial Planning &amp; Forecasting</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Own the annual budget and multi-year long-range plan across all business units, domains and geographie</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build and maintain a rolling 12-month forecast updated monthly, with full P&amp;L, balance sheet, cash flow and capital plan integration</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Design scenario models (bull / base / bear) that stress-test revenue against contract win rates, programme timelines and geopolitical variables</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lead the monthly financial review cycle — packaging actuals vs forecast vs budget with clear variance analysis and management commentary</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Own overhead cost tracking and cross-functional cost allocation models, ensuring programme margins are correctly stated</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>IPO Readiness &amp; Investor Reporting</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Build the financial model underpinning Helsing's IPO equity story — revenue quality, gross margin trajectory, EBITDA bridge, cash burn and path to profitability</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and maintain the investor KPI dashboard: ARR, NRR, contract backlog, revenue per employee and gross margin by product line and domain</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Prepare investor reporting, board packs and capital markets materials to the standard expected by institutional investors</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Capital &amp; Strategic Finance</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Build and own the capital allocation framework — how Helsing decides which programmes, markets and M&amp;A targets to invest in</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Model CAPEX vs OPEX trade-offs for major investment decisions including facility expansion, owned hardware platforms and data centre infrastructure</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyse and propose capital optimisation levers including lease vs own, build vs buy, and government grant or subsidised financing opportunities (EIB, export credit agencies, national defence industrial funds)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Support the CFO on term sheet analysis, financing structures and debt covenant modelling</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Own working capital management — programme billing milestones, payment terms with governments and primes, and cash flow optimisation</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Business Partnering &amp; Decision Support</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Translate operational plans into financial outcomes as a direct partner to the VP Business Finance and CEO</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build financial models for major business decisions: new market entry, domain expansion, pricing strategy and headcount investment cases</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Analyse defence contract economics across contract types — cost-plus, T&amp;M, fixed-price, IDIQ — and ensure bidding and revenue recognition reflect the correct financial treatment under IFRS 15</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Partner with the M&amp;A team on financial due diligence for acquisition targets and post-close integration financial modelling</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Systems, Data &amp; AI-First Finance</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Lead the selection and implementation of Helsing's FP&amp;A tooling — from planning platform (Anaplan, Pigment, Cube, or equivalent) to management reporting stack</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build AI-powered forecasting models that draw on contract backlog data, programme burn rates and external defence budget signals</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Design the finance data architecture — ensuring planning data is clean, auditable and connected to the ERP</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop and deploy AI agents for automated variance analysis, forecast generation and board reporting — reducing manual effort and increasing analytical depth</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Define and own the single source of truth for all financial KPIs across the organisation</p>
</li>
</ul>
