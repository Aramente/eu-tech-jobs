<p>Join us in revolutionizing Observability for Developers! We’re on a mission to redefine how engineers monitor, debug, and scale their production applications using ClickStack. An open-source platform that transforms telemetry data into actionable insights. Imagine a world where logs, metrics, traces, and session replays come together seamlessly to pinpoint root causes faster than ever before. If you've ever been jolted awake at 2 AM, frustrated with Grafana, Datadog, or Elastic for not delivering the answers you need, you’ll resonate with the problem we’re solving. And now, you can help us solve it.</p>
<p>We’re hiring a <strong>Senior Software Engineer (Infrastructure)</strong> to help us build a petabyte-scale, high-performance observability platform with a laser focus on crafting an <strong>amazing developer experience</strong>.</p>
<h3><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Build the core platform:</strong> Design and implement backend systems and APIs that power ClickStack, enabling engineers to ingest, query, and analyze observability data at massive scale.</li>
<li><strong>Scale deployments and infrastructure:</strong> Architect, deploy, and maintain cloud-native systems that ensure reliability, scalability, and performance. You’ll use Kubernetes, Helm, and infrastructure-as-code to make deployments simple and resilient.</li>
<li><strong>Ensure maintainability and operational excellence: </strong>Define best practices for CI/CD, monitoring, logging, and alerting. Drive automation across testing, scaling, and incident response to keep our platform healthy and developer-friendly.</li>
<li><strong>Engineer for scale: </strong>Design and operate ingestion and data processing pipelines that remain performant, resilient, and observable—even as we grow to petabyte-level workloads.</li>
<li><strong>Engage with the community:</strong> Collaborate with open-source contributors and customers, solve their challenges, and incorporate their feedback into our roadmap.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Who You Are</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>A backend engineer who thrives on building high-performance, distributed systems.</li>
<li>Passionate about reliability, scalability, and creating systems that “just work” in production.</li>
<li>Excited by observability, infrastructure, and making deployments effortless for both engineers and operators.</li>
<li>A creative problem-solver who thrives in a fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>Passionate about open source, developer tools, and the craft of building reliable systems.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What You Bring</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of backend engineering experience.</li>
<li>Strong TypeScript and Node.js skills (bonus for additional languages).</li>
<li>Deep understanding of APIs, event-driven systems, and high-throughput data pipelines.</li>
<li>Proficiency in SQL and experience working with analytical databases (ClickHouse experience a plus).</li>
<li>Experience with Docker and Kubernetes, plus Helm for managing production deployments.</li>
<li>Experience with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Pulumi, or similar).</li>
<li>Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, monitoring systems, and production-grade alerting practices.</li>
<li>A passion for building reliable, maintainable, cloud-native systems.<br><br></li>
</ul>
