<p>💼 <strong>Role Summary</strong><strong><br></strong>We are looking for a Lead Product Designer to take ownership of complex product areas within Cortex, partnering closely with Product and Engineering to shape solutions end-to-end. You will be responsible for translating ambiguous, complex problems into clear, thoughtful product experiences, making informed tradeoffs, and driving decisions that move the product forward.</p>
<p>This role is focused on product thinking, decision-making, and execution in a fast-moving startup environment. We are looking for someone who thrives in ambiguity, collaborates closely with product and engineering, and can take ownership of complex problems from concept through launch.<br><br></p>
<p><strong>🛠️ Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Drive the design vision</strong>: Lead and execute the design strategy for major product initiatives, setting a new standard for design and user experience at Cortex. You are relentless about crafting a delightful, industry-leading experience.</li>
<li><strong>Elevate product quality and UX flow</strong>: Proactively identify and resolve complex user experience issues, focusing on streamlining core workflows and improving overall product usability and polish.</li>
<li><strong>Partner closely with engineering to shape scalable, thoughtful product experiences:</strong> Work in tight collaboration with engineers to define solutions, make tradeoffs, and ensure what we build is both usable and technically sound.</li>
<li><strong>Operate effectively in ambiguity:</strong> Take loosely defined problems, bring structure, and drive them through to clear, actionable solutions.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Prototyping and Testing</strong>: Design, plan, and execute high-fidelity prototypes and user testing to validate concepts and inform design direction across the product management and engineering teams.</li>
<li><strong>End-to-End Feature Delivery</strong>: Own the design process from concept development, through wireframing, high-fidelity mockups, and final handoff. Collaborate with Product Managers and Engineers to ensure features meet a high bar for user experience.</li>
<li><strong>Maintain exceptional craft</strong>: Maintain an unwavering attention to detail to ensure product consistency, completeness, and a professional, polished user experience.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>✔️ Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>5-7+ years of experience as a Product Designer working on complex B2B or enterprise SaaS products. Experience with developer tools is a plus. Startup experience is highly desirable.</li>
<li>Proven experience leading end-to-end design projects with significant business impact.</li>
<li>Expert proficiency with Figma, prototyping tools, and user research methodologies.</li>
<li>Experience working across complex product surfaces with an ability to balance consistency, scalability, and product needs.</li>
<li>You can point to a portfolio of work that clearly demonstrates your ability to solve challenging user problems and execute with exceptional craft and attention to detail.</li>
<li>Passionate about creating beautiful, useful, and usable interfaces guided by user feedback, while maintaining the ability to make strong decisions in ambiguous situations.</li>
</ul>
