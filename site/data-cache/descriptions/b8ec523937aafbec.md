“We believe technology shouldn’t make you vulnerable — it should keep you safe. So we make software to help machines protect you.”

Since 2008, MacPaw has been creating complex technology and packaging it into Mac apps that everyone can use. As cyber threats loomed over the Apple ecosystem, they became a special focus of our work, leading to the creation of**[Moonlock](https://moonlock.com/)** — MacPaw’s dedicated cybersecurity product.

We’re looking for a **Product Manager** to join Moonlock and play a vital role in shaping the product. You’ll turn user feedback and early signals into clear product decisions, and lead features end-to-end — from problem definition to delivery. Your work will help us deliver cybersecurity technology that people can trust and rely on every day.

**If you’re ready to build the future of Mac security and shape an ambitious new product, we’d love to hear from you!**

**In this role, you will work on:**

* Lead hands-on product improvement by collecting and synthesizing user feedback, research, and data from a live product
* Identify product opportunities based on early signals, usage patterns, and emerging cybersecurity threats
* Translate insights into clear product requirements and actionable tasks for the team
* Drive features end-to-end — from problem framing and validation to supporting the delivery of the engineering team
* Maintain and refine the roadmap within your scope, aligning priorities with the broader product direction
* Work closely with engineering, design, analytics, copy, marketing, and QA to ensure high-quality delivery
* Track product performance through key metrics and feedback, and continuously improve existing functionality.

**Skills you’ll need to bring:**

* 3+ years of product management experience, ideally in B2C desktop applications
* Proven experience owning the end-to-end features and product lifecycle: from research, translating user needs and hypothesis generation to delivery and impact measurement
* Deep understanding of user experience, interface design, and product analytics
* Strong knowledge of prioritization frameworks, research methods, and metrics
* Requirements definition & documentation
* Strong experience working closely with designers and engineers on UX decisions, scope trade-offs, and delivery timelines

**As a plus:**

* Background working with cybersecurity features
* Familiarity with Apple ecosystems, especially macOS
* Experience working with AI or LLM-based products or integrating AI into existing solutions
