<p>As the Head of Treasury at Xendit, you will be the primary architect of our treasury engine. You will sit at the intersection of finance, product, and strategy, taking full ownership of our Treasury P&amp;L and ensuring that every dollar—and every local currency—is working as profitably as possible.</p>
<p>We are looking for a strategic leader who thinks like an owner. You will navigate complex cross-border liquidity challenges, negotiate high-stakes FX spreads, and optimize yield on Xendit’s held funds. Whether it’s designing sophisticated liquidity strategies or integrating cutting-edge financial instruments (like stablecoins) into our flow, you will be responsible for balancing risk with aggressive growth. If you are a seasoned treasury expert who thrives in high-velocity environments and enjoys the challenge of building a world-class function from the ground up, this role would be a good fit for you.</p>
<h3>Minimum Qualifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>7-10 years of Treasury experience, with at least 3-5 years heading up Treasury in a Fintech or similar</li>
<li>Experience Optimizing yields on held funds (interest rate, time deposits etc.)</li>
<li>Experience Managing FX (advising on spreads, hedging FX risk, netting strategies)</li>
<li>Experience designing and implementing funding and liquidity strategies</li>
<li>Experience selecting, negotiating with and managing financial partners related to liquidity provisions (e.g., banks, debt providers)</li>
<li>Experience defining technology / tool stack for Treasury function</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to build and maintain internal and external relationships effortlessly</li>
</ul>
<h3>Preferred Qualifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Experience with stablecoins</li>
<li>Experience in managing FX risk</li>
<li>Experience in fast moving FinTech startup</li>
</ul>
