<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Team Assistant – Corporate Development</strong>&nbsp;will provide high-quality administrative and coordination support to the Corporate Development team. This role is critical to ensuring the team operates efficiently in a dynamic, fast-paced environment by managing scheduling, travel, meeting logistics, documentation, and day-to-day coordination across stakeholders.</p>
<p>The role contributes to the effectiveness of the Corporate Development function by enabling smooth team operations, supporting internal and external interactions, and ensuring strong organization across multiple priorities. The Team Assistant will work closely with senior stakeholders and cross-functional partners to help the team stay focused, well-prepared, and operationally effective.</p>
