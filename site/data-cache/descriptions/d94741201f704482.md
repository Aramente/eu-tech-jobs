<h4>About the team:</h4>
<p>Our Small Markets Customer Support team is the direct connection between SumUp and the merchants who rely on us every day. We work across multiple channels — phone, chat, and email — to make sure our merchants get fast, friendly, and effective support in their own language.</p>
<p>This role exists because we're growing. With a Finnish-speaking agent recently promoted within the team, we need someone to step in, make the role their own, and help us keep our Finnish merchant community well supported. It's a great entry point into fintech customer support, with real ownership from day one and a clear path to grow.</p>
<h4>What you'll do:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Support Finnish and English-speaking merchants across phone, chat, and email, helping them resolve technical and account-related issues quickly and clearly</li>
<li>Listen carefully to merchant concerns, investigate the root cause, and find the right solution — not just the quickest one</li>
<li>Spot patterns in recurring issues and contribute ideas to improve processes and reduce merchant friction</li>
<li>Work closely with other teams across SumUp to make sure merchants get the right outcome, even when the answer sits outside your team</li>
<li>Be an active, collaborative member of an international, multilingual support team</li>
</ul>

<li>A natural troubleshooting mindset — comfortable digging into a problem rather than passing it on</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail, especially when handling sensitive merchant information or account queries</li>

</ul>
