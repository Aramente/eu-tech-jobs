<p>We’re looking for a Lead Payroll Analyst to join our Global Payroll team – a high-impact individual contributor role that combines deep payroll expertise with functional leadership. You’ll serve as the subject matter expert and day-to-day guide for our International Senior Payroll Specialist, while personally driving accuracy, compliance, and continuous improvement across our six-country EMEA and APAC payroll footprint.</p>
<p>This is an exciting opportunity for an experienced payroll professional ready to step into a lead role – influencing how payroll operates globally, championing automation and AI-driven improvements, and partnering closely with People, Finance, and Tech teams to build a best-in-class function.</p>
<p>Job location: Dublin, IE (3 days p/week in-office)<br><br>Full-time permanent role</p>
<h1><strong>What You’ll Own</strong></h1>
<p><strong>Functional Leadership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as functional lead for international payroll operations across six countries: UK, Ireland, France, Germany, Singapore, and Australia</li>
<li>Provide day-to-day guidance, quality review, and mentorship to the International Senior Payroll Specialist</li>
<li>Serve as the primary escalation point for complex payroll queries, compliance issues, and vendor matters</li>
<li>Partner with the Global Payroll Manager to set team priorities, improve workflows, and ensure consistent delivery across all regions</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Global Payroll Execution</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own end-to-end payroll processing and sign-off for UK and Ireland, ensuring accuracy and on-time delivery every cycle</li>
<li>Oversee and review payroll delivery for France, Germany, Singapore, and Australia through CloudPay and in-country vendors</li>
<li>Manage and validate payroll inputs in Workday, ensuring data integrity from hire to pay</li>
<li>Ensure full compliance with local payroll legislation, tax law, and statutory requirements across all jurisdictions</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Finance &amp; Compliance Partnership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead payroll reconciliations, variance analysis, and internal controls across all pay cycles</li>
<li>Own month-end and year-end payroll close activities with accounting partners.</li>
<li>Support internal and external audits, providing documentation and analysis as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Cross-Functional Collaboration</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner closely with People Operations, HRBPs, Benefits, Finance, and People Tech to deliver a seamless employee payroll experience</li>
<li>Act as a subject matter expert in cross-functional projects, including system enhancements, benefits integration, and global mobility scenarios</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Innovation &amp; Process Improvement</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Identify and lead initiatives to streamline, automate, and modernize payroll processes</li>
<li>Champion the use of AI tools for data validation, anomaly detection, and payroll reporting insights</li>
<li>Document processes, build scalable playbooks, and drive standardization across the payroll team</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h1><strong>What We’re Looking For</strong></h1>
<p>You’re an experienced payroll professional who brings technical depth, a collaborative mindset, and a natural instinct for leading others without needing a formal management title.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Required Experience</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of payroll experience, with at least 3 years in a multi-country or global payroll environment</li>
<li>Deep expertise in UK and/or Ireland payroll (essential) – you know the legislation, the nuances, and the edge cases</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with payroll in at least two of the following: France, Germany, Singapore, Australia</li>
<li>Experience providing guidance, review, or informal leadership to other payroll team members</li>
<li>Proven track record of managing complex reconciliations, payroll controls, and compliance reporting</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Systems &amp; Tools</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Proficiency with Workday HCM and/or payroll input workflows</li>
<li>Experience with CloudPay or a comparable global payroll aggregator/vendor platform</li>
<li>Comfort with Excel or Google Sheets for data analysis and payroll reconciliation</li>
<li>Exposure to or active interest in automation tools and AI applications within payroll or finance operations</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Skills &amp; Attributes</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>High attention to detail with the ability to manage competing deadlines across time zones</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills – you spot issues before they become problems</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills; able to translate complex payroll topics for non-payroll stakeholders</li>
<li>Proactive, self-directed work style with a continuous improvement mindset</li>
