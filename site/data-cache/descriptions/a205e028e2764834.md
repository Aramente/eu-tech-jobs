<p><br><strong>AREA CONTEXT</strong></p>
<p>The Unix area is dedicated to the management of server operating systems, ensuring the stability, security, and performance of business-critical applications. It covers the deployment, patching, and monitoring of Unix (Linux, AIX) servers across data centres and cloud environments. The area addresses operational challenges such as end-of-life hardware, legacy systems, and the need for rapid response to incidents. It supports business continuity through robust backup and recovery processes and aligns with best practices for system administration.</p>
