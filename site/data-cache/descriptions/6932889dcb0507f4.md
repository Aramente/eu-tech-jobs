<p>As the Engineering Manager for Mobile, you will lead and grow the team that designs the technology powering<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span><a href="https://my.axon.com/s/axon-device-manager?language=en\_US">Axon Device Manager</a>,<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span><a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/axon-device-checkout/id1588322129">Axon Device Checkout</a>, and the<span class="Apple-converted-space">&nbsp;</span><a href="https://www.axon.com/products/axon-fleet-3">Fleet 3 Dashboard</a>—mobile IoT applications that connect Axon devices to officers in the field, promoting transparency and protecting truth worldwide. Looking ahead, this role is scoped for expansion: you will help define and staff the next wave of Axonʼs mobile offerings, consolidating experiences where it makes sense and scaling our platform to support additional applications and features still on the roadmap.</p>
<p>You'll collaborate daily with software engineers, product managers, designers, and a Release Manager to turn bold product concepts into secure, reliable mobile and frontend experiences. Your deep expertise in Android, iOS, or modern frontend development (React, TypeScript), along with broad knowledge of modern application architecture, release practices, and observability, will enable you to set technical direction, unblock delivery, and raise the bar on code quality and system design. Based in Ho Chi Minh City, you'll lead a team of engineers in Vietnam while partnering closely with peer engineering leaders and product teams in the U.S., fostering a culture of innovation and continuous learning that accelerates feature velocity without compromising quality and stability.<br><br>Our mobile products are already trusted by major agencies across the globe. Under your leadership, enhanced mobile capabilities—real-time video streaming, AI-driven insights, and seamless device management—will unlock even more value for new and existing customers. The software you shepherd to market will empower officers to act faster, capture truth, and ultimately Protect Life.<br><br></p>
