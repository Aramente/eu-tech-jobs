<p><strong>Optimization Manager</strong></p>
<p>Adyen is seeking a strategic and analytical Optimization Manager to join our Performance Optimization team in Tokyo. In this role, you will be responsible for managing full-funnel payment performance to drive tangible business impact and optimize merchant success at scale.</p>
<p>You will act as a key partner to our merchants, leveraging data-driven insights to enhance their payment ecosystems. Success in this position requires a blend of excellent project management skills and the ability to collaborate seamlessly with cross-functional teams, including Product, Commercial, and Data Analytics, within a fast-paced and global environment.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate possesses deep payments expertise, a strong focus on risk and fraud prevention, and the ability to translate complex technical findings into clear, actionable recommendations for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.</p>
<p><strong>What you'll do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with commercial teams to drive Adyen’s value proposition and merchant adoption, using data-driven insights (with Data Analysts) to strengthen conversations</li>
<li>Lead high-level customer discussions, enable commercial teams with tools/trainings, and act as a payment optimization SME across risk, fraud, conversion, and cost efficiency</li>
<li>Analyze merchant portfolios and payment data to identify optimization opportunities, run playbooks, and drive performance and risk improvements</li>
<li>Identify and support adoption of Adyen products (e.g. Protect, Uplift) in collaboration with Product teams</li>
<li>Build dashboards/tools and scalable analytical solutions from large datasets to generate actionable insights</li>
<li>Operate with autonomy and a global, strategic perspective across high-value customers and cross-functional teams</li>
<li>Support team operations, mentor team members, and build strong relationships with regional customers and stakeholders</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who you are</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>5+ years’ experience in Risk &amp; Fraud, Payments Performance, Data Analytics, Technical Account roles, or similar within fintech/payments, with strong understanding of regional payments landscape (scheme rules, regulations, risk considerations)</li>
<li>Good stakeholder management across customers and cross-functional teams, with an ownership mindset and ability to prioritize and execute independently</li>
<li>Comfortable working in fast-paced, global, cross-functional environments with a continuous improvement mindset</li>
<li>Strong data analysis skills with experience handling complex datasets; BI tools (e.g. Looker, Tableau) is a plus</li>
<li>Excellent communication skills in English &amp; Japanese, able to simplify complex topics for both technical and non-technical audiences</li>
</ul>
