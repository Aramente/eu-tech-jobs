## Your Mission

We are looking for a <strong>Senior Software Engineer</strong> to join our <strong>Driving Functions</strong> domain development team, with a strong focus on <strong>instrument cluster software development</strong>. In this role, you will design, develop, and maintain high‑performance, safety‑critical software used in next‑generation digital cockpits. You will work closely with system architects, UI/UX designers, and cross‑functional engineering teams across the full software lifecycle.

## Sneak preview of your future tasks

<ul><li>Design, develop, and maintain instrument cluster software for automotives. </li><li>Implement high‑performance, real‑time, and multi‑threaded applications</li><li>Implement UI and HMI components using modern UI frameworks (e.g. Qt/QML, Unity)</li><li>Work across the full software development lifecycle: requirements analysis, design, implementation, testing, integration, and maintenance</li><li>Develop and review C / C++ (primary) and C# / Python components where applicable</li><li>Design and use IPC mechanisms (shared memory, sockets, message queues, etc.)</li><li>Work with QNX and embedded Linux based systems</li><li>Integrate software in virtualized / hypervisor-based environments</li><li>Apply automotive standards and best practices (e.g. functional safety, coding guidelines, quality processes)</li><li>Apply clean architecture principles and proven software design patterns</li><li>Contribute to and improve CI/CD pipelines, automated builds, and testing strategies</li><li>Collaborate closely with system architects, UI/UX designers, and cross‑functional teams </li><li>Support debugging, performance optimization, and root cause analysis on target hardware</li></ul>

## Your Profile

<strong>Required Qualifications</strong><ul><li>Strong experience as a Senior Software Engineer in embedded or automotive systems</li><li>Excellent proficiency in C, C++, Python</li><li>Hands‑on experience with UI frameworks such as Qt/QML and/or Unity</li><li>Solid experience with embedded Linux and/or QNX</li><li>Strong understanding of multi‑threaded systems and concurrency concepts</li><li>Hands‑on experience with IPC mechanisms</li><li>Solid knowledge of software architecture and design patterns in complex systems</li><li>Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and modern development workflows</li><li>Experience working across the full software lifecycle in complex systems</li><li>Knowledge of automotive software standards and development processes</li><li>Strong analytical and problem‑solving skills</li><li>Ability to work effectively in cross‑functional and international teams</li><li>Fluent in English (German is a plus)</li></ul><strong>Nice to Have / Plus</strong><ul><li>Experience with Android Automotive OS (AAOS)</li><li>Knowledge of Java and/or Kotlin</li><li>Knowledge of C#, Unity</li><li>Experience with hypervisors and virtualized embedded systems</li><li>Prior work on instrument clusters, digital cockpits, or automotive HMIs</li><li>Exposure to automotive safety or quality standards (e.g. ISO 26262, ASPICE)</li></ul>
