<h2><strong>The role</strong></h2>
<div>As an Avionics Systems Engineer for Safety Critical Systems, you will be responsible for the development and delivery of safety critical flight control and vehicle management hardware and software, working closely with industry partners.&nbsp; You will be responsible not only for defining the system, but also driving it through all development stages and ultimately onto the flying platform.&nbsp; In achieving this goal you will work with a diverse,&nbsp; high-calibre team of experts, strategists and partners.</div>
