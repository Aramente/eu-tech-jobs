* Support financial reporting processes, ensuring accuracy and timely delivery under tight deadlines.

**Skills you’ll need to bring:**

* 3+ years of experience in accounting, ideally in an international or group environment.
* Strong practical knowledge of US GAAP or IFRS.
* Hands-on experience with consolidation, intercompany reconciliations, and elimination entries.
* Experience preparing financial statements and reporting packages.
* Experience working with external audits and ability to independently handle auditor requests.
* Strong Excel / Google Sheets skills (pivot tables, XLOOKUP, data analysis).
* Understanding of multi-currency accounting and FX translation.
* High attention to detail and ability to work under tight reporting deadlines.
* Advanced English and native Ukrainian.

**As a plus:**

* Experience with AI tools (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot).
