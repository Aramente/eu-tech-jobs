<h2><strong>The Role</strong></h2>
<p>Your mission is clear: <strong>you help turn players into paying subscribers, and keep them, across Yousician and GuitarTuna.</strong></p>
<p>You will run paywall, pricing, and offer experimentation end to end for both products. Hypothesis, brief, ship, readout, decision. You will maintain the live revenue forecast that ties every test back to the quarterly plan, and reconcile it monthly against what actually landed. Multiple tests run every week. Every one of them has a written brief and a clean readout.</p>
<p>This is a senior specialist role. You come in with a point of view on paywall mechanics, price point elasticity, trial length trade-offs, holdout discipline, and primary-metric selection. You will report to the Head of Revenue and work closely with them to translate monetization priorities into a concrete experimentation roadmap. You will work day to day with embedded engineers on both products, a test designer, and a data partner. You are a strong monetization specialist and a key thought partner on experimentation, pricing, paywalls, and offers.</p>
<h2><strong>What You'll Do</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Run the Experiment Cadence:</strong> Own the weekly operating rhythm for monetization experimentation across both products, including planning, launch readiness, readouts, and decision follow-through. Ensure each experiment has a clear hypothesis, success metric, guardrails, and decision rule before it goes live.</li>
<li><strong>Own the Test Portfolio Execution:</strong> Translate agreed monetization priorities into a concrete test roadmap across Yousician, GuitarTuna mobile, and the GuitarTuna web experience. Design variants, write briefs, specify subscription-platform configuration, and work with engineering on delivery.</li>
<li><strong>Maintain the Revenue View:</strong> Maintain the live monetization forecast and experiment-level revenue view, and help reconcile plan vs actual together with the Head of Revenue and Finance.</li>
<li><strong>Partner with Finance on Pricing:</strong> You build the case for every price change, walk Finance through the numbers, and manage the rollout across iOS, Android, and Web with the regional and store constraints that come with it.</li>
<li><strong>Keep the Scorecard Honest:</strong> Maintain the weekly Monetization Tracker and experiment readout cadence, ensuring that key metrics, forecasts, and decisions stay consistent and reliable. Partner with the Head of Revenue, Data, and Finance to investigate mismatches and keep the monetization view decision-ready.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Who You Are</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>You bring strong monetization craft:</strong> You have shipped across the full spectrum: paywall copy, paywall placement, price points, trial variants, intro offers, winback campaigns. You know where the wins usually come from and where the traps are.</li>
<li><strong>You are resourceful and proactive:</strong> You do not wait for perfect conditions. You can spot practical opportunities, identify problems worth solving, and move things forward in an under-resourced or ambiguous setup. Whether through tooling, lightweight prototyping, or creative cross-functional problem solving, you know how to turn momentum into shipped work.</li>
<li><strong>You think in numbers:</strong> Baseline, uplift, volume, EUR. When you pitch a test, you already know what it is worth. When you kill one, you can explain why in one sentence.</li>
<li><strong>You have opinions on methodology:</strong> You have views on MDE-setting, holdout design, sample ratio mismatch, guardrail thresholds, and sequential testing. You can defend them and you update them when the data tells you to.</li>
<li><strong>You decide by the data:</strong> You have killed winning-looking tests when the stats did not support them, and you have shipped underwhelming-looking ones when the logic did. You do not fall in love with hypotheses.</li>
<li><strong>You understand the full system:</strong> Monetization decisions do not live in isolation. A price change can affect winbacks, a paywall test can change retention, and a trial decision can shift downstream value. You can think across the full subscriber lifecycle and make decisions with LTV in mind, not just immediate conversion.</li>
<li><strong>You communicate directly:</strong> You write briefs that engineers can build and readouts that Finance can trust. Conclusion first, numbers second, caveats third.</li>
</ul>
