<h2>What you'll do</h2>
</div>
</div>
<div data-testid="markdown-response">
<div>
<ul>
<li>Own Mission Control (our sales knowledge base and microsite) making sure content is easy to find, genuinely useful, and built for how reps actually work (think, field sales who are on the move)</li>
<li>Build relationships with sales leaders across EU markets, connecting regularly to understand what resources their teams need, then bringing those to life. For example one-pagers, guides, video tutorials, slide decks and more</li>
<li>Research, evaluate, and propose platforms and tools that can make content delivery smarter and more scalable across countries and teams</li>
<li>Collaborate with local trainers to make sure materials land correctly in each market</li>
<li>Work closely with subject matter experts across Sales, Marketing, and Product to curate and develop content that reflects our latest positioning, competitive landscape, and product updates before key launches</li>
<li>Lead the rollout of our rebrand across all sales enablement materials, keeping quality and consistency high across every market</li>
</ul>

<h2>You'll be great for this role if</h2>
</div>
</div>
<div data-testid="markdown-response">
<div>
<ul>
<li>Strong stakeholder management comes naturally, e.g. proactively reaching out to sales leaders, collecting requirements, knowing when to push back, and clearly communicating prioritisation decisions with confidence and context.</li>

<li>Leverage a naturally collaborative approach, with the communication skills to build strong relationships across multiple teams.</li>

<li>Comfort working independently as a sole contributor in a fast-moving, ambiguous environment, where priorities shift and adaptability is key.</li>
<li>Genuine curiosity about AI and new tools, with the willingness to step outside a traditional content comfort zone to find smarter, more scalable ways of working.</li>
