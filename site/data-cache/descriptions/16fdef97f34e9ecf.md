**The Role - Senior Client Partner**

If you’re passionate about Digital Marketing and have a proven track record in Media Sales, then we invite you to talk to us about the Client Partner role.

- Be a part of a dynamic team that works across all strategic ad product initiatives.

- Attend industry events and conferences to generate new business leads
- Track revenue performance across the business portfolio, determine and action corrective measures where needed.

- You have experience with online advertising, analytics, and social media
