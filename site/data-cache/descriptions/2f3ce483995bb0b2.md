<div class="content-intro"><div>Pelago is the world’s leading virtual clinic for Substance Use Management. Our program provides guidance, support and treatment for members seeking to overcome their tobacco, alcohol and opioid use. From unhealthy habits to active substance use disorders, Pelago delivers a personalized solution based on individual health, habits, genetics, and goals, providing care for members wherever they might be on the substance use spectrum.</div>

<h3><strong>Overview of the Role:</strong>&nbsp;</h3>
<p>As a Backend Engineer, you’ll play a critical role in building and scaling the backend systems that power Pelago’s core services. You’ll design and implement robust APIs, maintain our serverless AWS infrastructure, and work with complex data systems to ensure reliability, performance, and scalability. You’ll collaborate closely with engineers, product managers, and other stakeholders, contributing to an event-driven architecture and taking ownership of mission-critical features from concept to deployment.</p>
<p>Your work will directly support Pelago’s mission by enabling secure, scalable, and efficient systems that connect our members to the care they need. <strong>This role is based in our London office, with in-person collaboration two days per week.</strong></p>
<h3><strong>In this role, you will:&nbsp;</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Build scalable, performant, secure, and well-tested APIs.</li>
<li>Maintain and evolve our entirely serverless AWS-based architecture using Node.js and TypeScript.</li>
<li>Contribute to the development of an event-driven system, owning core feature implementation and refactoring critical functionality as needed.</li>
<li>Design and optimize MySQL databases at scale, including schema design and query performance improvements.</li>
<li>Manage and integrate third-party APIs, ensuring reliability, security, and maintainability.</li>
<li>Implement best practices for code quality, testing, and deployment automation in a collaborative agile environment.</li>
<li>Partner with cross-functional teams to translate business requirements into scalable technical solutions.</li>
</ul>
