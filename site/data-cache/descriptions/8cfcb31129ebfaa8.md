<p>You’ll work closely with teams across the business to identify and escalate issues, helping to maintain a safe and trusted financial platform.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Your Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>As a 1st Line AML / KYC Analyst, you will be responsible for conducting customer due diligence (CDD), enhanced due diligence (EDD), and transaction monitoring activities to ensure the Bank's compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations.</li>
<li>You’ll play a critical role in identifying and mitigating financial crime risk at the frontline of customer interaction.</li>
<li>Conduct AML/KYC reviews on new and existing customers in line with internal policies and regulatory requirements.</li>
<li>Collect and verify customer documentation (e.g., ID, proof of address, source of funds/wealth).</li>
<li>Perform screening and background checks using internal systems and third-party tools.</li>
<li>Investigate alerts from transaction monitoring systems, escalating suspicious activity as appropriate.</li>
<li>Liaise with customers and internal teams to obtain missing information or resolve KYC issues.</li>
<li>Contribute to the overall continuous improvement and development of the AML/KYC Operations Team by supporting the implementation of internal controls, process improvements and documentation updates.</li>
<li>Assist in the preparation of internal reports and MI relating to Financial Crime and AML.</li>
<li>Stay current on AML/CTF legislation, typologies, and regulatory expectations.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Your Profile</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years' experience in a KYC, AML, or compliance role within a financial institution (preferably experience retail banking or wealth management).</li>
<li>Working knowledge of the UK regulatory environment, including the Money Laundering Regulations, JMLSG Guidance, and FCA Handbook.</li>
<li>Familiarity with screening and case management tools e.g. LexisNexis, ComplyAdvantage, Dow Jones.</li>
<li>Team player with a proactive attitude and ethical mindset.</li>
<li>Strong attention to detail and investigative mindset.</li>
<li>Good written and verbal communication skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work under pressure and manage workload in line with SLAs.</li>
<li>Experience in using open-source intelligence to identify any relevant adverse media on our customers and partners.</li>
<li>Strong communication skills including the ability to forge new connections with existing stakeholders and escalate risks diligently in line with agreed processes.</li>
<li>Keen eye for detail and problem solving with an investigative mind and the desire to find solutions to problems independently and quickly using a risk based approach and by applying a consistent and defensible rationale in line with relevant legislation&nbsp;set, committed to continuous improvement and excellence in AML operations.</li>
</ul>
