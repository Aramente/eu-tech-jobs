HR Coordinator

Full-Time \u007C In-Person

Position Summary

We are seeking a hands-on, people-focused HR Coordinator to support our growing team and operational staff in Spartanburg, SC. This role is fully on-site and will serve as the primary HR presence for employees building relationships, providing day-to-day support, and ensuring a positive employee experience across the organization.

The ideal candidate is compassionate, approachable, highly organized, and thrives in a fast-paced environment where employees rely on HR as a trusted resource.
