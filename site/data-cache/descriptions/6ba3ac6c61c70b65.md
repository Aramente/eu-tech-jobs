<p>We are looking for an experienced Revenue Operations Manager to join our team in the Asia-Pacific and Japan (APJ) region. This is a critical role that serves as the strategic bridge between our operational initiatives and regional execution.</p>
<p>While this position acts as the primary operational partner for the APJ territory, it is not solely a regional support role. You will be a vital member of the Revenue Operations team, overseeing projects and initiatives, driving system enhancements, and serving as a subject matter expert on processes that impact the entire organization.</p>
<p><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own APJ territory execution, ensuring bookings adhere to company policy, maintain accuracy, and meet compliance requirements throughout the process. Serve as a subject matter expert for complex, high-value, and non-standard transactions.</li>
<li>Serve as a primary execution and escalation partner for the Finance Global Capability Center (FGCC), providing guidance on complex scenarios and supporting consistent application of policy and process.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Sales, Deal Desk, Finance, and related teams to support complex deal structures and ensure transactions are executed in line with defined processes and controls.</li>
<li>Support month-end and quarter-end close activities, including preparing, reviewing, improving, and troubleshooting reconciliations as required.</li>
<li>Proactively identify discrepancies, trends, and opportunities for process improvement through ongoing review of operational data and workflows.</li>
<li>Develop and analyze business performance reporting to ensure an accurate reflection of operational results, creating reports and dashboards that support planning and decision-making.</li>
<li>Oversee and support multiple operational and process improvement initiatives, ensuring work is completed efficiently and aligned to expected outcomes.</li>
<li>Work with Business Technology, and Finance Transformation teams to support system enhancements, including UAT participation, validation of changes, and issue resolution as needed.</li>
<li>Support training and enablement efforts both within the team and cross-functionally on processes, complex scenarios, and operational best practices.</li>
<li>Utilize automation and AI-enabled tools, where appropriate, to improve efficiency, accuracy, and scalability across Revenue Operations workflows.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who You Are</strong></p>
<p>You are a strong operational contributor with a solid understanding of end-to-end quote-to-cash processes and how upstream and downstream decisions impact outcomes.</p>
<p>You bring:</p>
<ul>
<li>Strategic curiosity and a practical approach to identifying issues and supporting continuous improvement.</li>
<li>Operational strength, with experience working through complex processes, policies, and systems.</li>
<li>Sound judgment when evaluating information, prioritizing work, and handling non-standard scenarios.</li>
<li>Clear and effective communication skills, with the ability to work across teams and explain processes or decisions.</li>
<li>Adaptability and comfort managing multiple priorities in a dynamic environment.</li>
<li>A forward-looking mindset, including openness to using automation and AI-enabled tools to improve how work is done.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>6–8+ years of experience in Order Management, Sales Operations, Deal Desk, or a related role&nbsp;</li>
<li>Advanced knowledge of operations systems and tools such as Salesforce, CPQ, NetSuite, Tableau, or similar platforms preferred</li>
<li>Ability to move fast, adapt, and thrive in a dynamic and fast changing environment</li>
<li>Fluent in English and Japanese language&nbsp;<br><br></li>
</ul>
