<h2 style="color: #0000ee;">What you’ll do</h2>
<ul>
<li>Build and ship flight-critical embedded software for long-endurance stratospheric aircraft</li>

<li>Write firmware for MCU-based avionics, sensors, actuators, power systems, and comms interfaces</li>
<li>Integrate sensors and peripherals (IMU, GNSS, air data, magnetometer, servos, radios) and build reliable drivers and middleware</li>
<li>Design robust telemetry, logging, and health monitoring to support long-duration autonomous operations</li>

<li>Support integration, production bring-up, and flight ops in the desert</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="color: #0000ee;">What we’re looking for</h2>
<ul>

<li>Strong C/C++ fundamentals and comfort working close to hardware</li>
<li>Experience with RTOS or bare-metal firmware, interrupts, timing, and deterministic systems</li>
<li>Experience with common embedded interfaces and protocols (SPI, I2C, UART, CAN, Ethernet)</li>
