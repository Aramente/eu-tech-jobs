From product and engineering to sales, marketing, and data — our teams work closely together to deliver secure, scalable solutions trusted by companies around the world.

**Your impact?** Empowering businesses everywhere to keep their people, systems, and data safe.

## Main Responsibilities

- Set up test automation in the project from the ground up.
- Develop and maintain efficient automated test suites across different supported platforms.
- Report software defects and work across development teams to resolve them.
- Work closely with product managers, developers, and other QA team members in ensuring high-quality products.

## Core Requirements

- Expertise in developing and maintaining comprehensive native testing solutions.
- Strong knowledge of JS and TS.
- Solid understanding of native applications.
- Advanced knowledge of Appium and its ecosystem.
- Ability to leverage AI coding tools effectively while maintaining full ownership of code quality, architecture decisions, and outcomes.
- You are sharp-eyed with attention to detail.
- Strong ownership and proactivity; feeling comfortable in taking initiative and creating from scratch.
- Direct, straightforward communication; clear and concise in writing and speech.
