<h4>Team Summary</h4>
<p>The Radio Frequency team plays a crucial role in the modern engineering process for wireless devices. Our daily work focuses on key areas such as Antenna, RF front-end, EMI/EMC, Signal Integrity, design, validation and optimisation of wireless subsystems included to our products.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4>Job Summary</h4>
<p>As Radio Frequency Intern in SQUAD you will evaluate and analyse proposed solutions with ongoing optimisation process. In this role you will work closely with Radio Frequency Engineers on a daily basis and&nbsp; learn about the modern engineering process for wireless devices.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
