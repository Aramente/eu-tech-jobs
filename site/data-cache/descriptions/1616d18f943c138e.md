</div></div><h3><strong>About the Role</strong></h3>
<p>CoreWeave is seeking a strategic and execution-oriented <strong>Sr.</strong> <strong>Manager, Supplier Materials Management</strong> to lead a high-impact team responsible for supplier materials readiness across our rapidly scaling global infrastructure. This role owns the end-to-end supplier materials strategy across critical OEM/ODM partners, ensuring capacity alignment, supply continuity, and execution readiness in support of aggressive data center expansion.</p>
<p>Operating at the intersection of Strategic Sourcing, Planning, Engineering, Manufacturing, and Infrastructure Deployment, this leader will shape long-range supply strategy, drive executive-level supply risk visibility, and build scalable processes that support CoreWeave’s hypergrowth environment. This role combines organizational leadership, supplier strategy, and operational rigor.</p>
<h3><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h3>
<h3><strong>Organizational Leadership &amp; Capability Building</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Lead, develop, and scale a team of Supplier Materials Managers responsible for supplier execution and materials readiness.<br><br></li>
<li>Establish clear performance objectives, development plans, and succession strategies.<br><br></li>
<li>Build organizational capability in capacity planning, shortage mitigation, allocation management, and supplier performance analytics.<br><br></li>
<li>Design and standardize tools, dashboards, KPIs, and operational rhythms across the team.<br><br></li>
<li>Drive a culture of accountability, urgency, and data-driven decision-making.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Supplier Materials Strategy &amp; Long-Range Planning</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Own the 12–24 month supplier materials strategy aligned to platform roadmaps and global deployment forecasts.<br><br></li>
<li>Ensure supplier capacity, lead times, and inventory strategies support infrastructure build plans.<br><br></li>
<li>Lead supplier capacity validation and ramp readiness for new platform introductions (NPI).<br><br></li>
<li>Partner in make/buy, capacity reservation, and supplier investment discussions.<br><br></li>
<li>Develop contingency strategies including dual sourcing, buffer inventory, and alternate supply pathways.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Executive Supply Risk Leadership</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Consolidate and communicate supply risk visibility to senior leadership.<br><br></li>
<li>Represent Supplier Materials Management in executive supply reviews.<br><br></li>
<li>Lead executive-level supplier escalations related to shortages, delivery risk, or execution gaps.<br><br></li>
<li>Drive cross-functional tradeoff decisions balancing cost, lead time, buffer strategy, and deployment schedules.<br><br></li>
<li>Own aggregate supplier performance metrics including OTD, lead time adherence, capacity attainment, shortage cycle time, and supply-related deployment misses.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Commercial &amp; Strategic Sourcing Alignment</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Partner closely with Technical Strategic Sourcing to ensure operational execution aligns with negotiated commercial agreements.<br><br></li>
<li>Provide operational input into supplier selection decisions, assessing scalability, execution capability, and manufacturing maturity.<br><br></li>
<li>Contribute to total cost of ownership (TCO) modeling through inventory strategy, flexibility, and lead time optimization.<br><br></li>
<li>Support allocation strategy development during constrained supply environments.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Cross-Functional Execution &amp; Infrastructure Scaling</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Planning, Engineering, Manufacturing, Logistics, and Data Center Operations to align supply with demand signals and build schedules.<br><br></li>
<li>Oversee supply readiness for accelerated infrastructure deployments.<br><br></li>
<li>Drive implementation of scalable supply control tower mechanisms as volume increases.<br><br></li>
<li>Improve forecasting accuracy, materials visibility, and ERP planning maturity across the supply base.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Qualifications</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of experience in supply chain, materials management, supplier operations, or manufacturing environments.<br><br></li>
<li>3+ years of people leadership experience managing supply chain or materials teams.<br><br></li>
<li>Experience supporting complex hardware systems, OEM/ODM environments, or hyperscale infrastructure preferred.<br><br></li>
<li>Strong expertise in supplier capacity management, MRP/ERP systems, and long-range supply planning.<br><br></li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to influence executive stakeholders and manage high-impact escalations.<br><br></li>
<li>Proven success operating in high-growth, high-ambiguity, fast-scaling environments.<br><br></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What Success Looks Like</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Supplier capacity consistently aligned with deployment forecasts.<br><br></li>
<li>Reduced supply volatility and improved predictability across critical hardware platforms.<br><br></li>
<li>Clear executive-level visibility into supply risk and mitigation plans.<br><br></li>
<li>A high-performing, scalable Supplier Materials Management organization.<br><br></li>
<li>Strong integration between sourcing strategy, commercial agreements, and operational execution.</li>
</ul>
