<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">As the ideal candidate, you will be an ambitious and strategic account manager at a pivotal point in your career, driven by exceeding your revenue targets and constantly seeking ways to elevate your partners' performance. Your ability to build genuine relationships with restaurant owners will be your superpower—you're the person who understands that great partnerships are built on trust, service excellence, and shared success.</p>
<p>You must also be a team player and willing to chip in with tasks outside of the day-to-day - it’s important that we keep the ‘hands on’ ethos that has made our team what it is!</p>
<h3><strong>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Own and grow your restaurant portfolio:</strong> Strategically manage a diverse set of venue partners, identifying growth opportunities and driving Gross Order Value (GOV) expansion through targeted upselling initiatives.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Be the growth catalyst:</strong> Partner closely with restaurant owners and managers to understand their business needs, analyze their performance data, and co-create strategies that boost their success.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Master the data:</strong> Leverage analytical dashboards (Looker, Power BI) to uncover performance insights, identify trends, and present compelling reports that drive decision-making.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Collaborate across teams:</strong> Work hand-in-hand with Marketing, Operations, and other departments to execute campaigns and operational improvements that directly impact your accounts' bottom line.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Navigate the local market:</strong> Stay ahead of market trends, competitive dynamics, and partnership opportunities in Almaty's vibrant restaurant scene.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Nurture relationships that matter:</strong> Conduct regular partner meetings, provide exceptional service, and adapt your approach to suit each partner's unique personality and business model.</li>
</ul>

A mix of commercial strategy and operational understanding is ideal.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Commercial acumen:</strong> You understand (or are eager to master) P&amp;Ls, revenue drivers, and how to translate data into actionable growth strategies. <li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Upselling mindset:</strong> A natural ability to identify expansion opportunities and effectively communicate value propositions that excite your partners.</li>

<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Relationship wizard:</strong> Exceptional interpersonal skills with the ability to adapt your communication style—equally comfortable discussing operations with a head chef or business strategy with an owner.</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>True service first:</strong> Structured, detail-oriented, and genuinely committed to your partners' success. You're the person they want on their team.</li>

<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Passion for food &amp; hospitality:</strong> A genuine love for restaurants and the ambition to help the best venues in Almaty reach their full potential.</li>
