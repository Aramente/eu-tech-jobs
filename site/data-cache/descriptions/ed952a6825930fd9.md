<li>Third-Party Integrations: Securely managing customer credentials and integrating with external observability providers, ensuring encryption at rest and in transit</li>
<li>Cross-team Collaboration: Partnering with the broader Customer Observability teams to provide unified telemetry and monitoring experiences</li>
</ul>
