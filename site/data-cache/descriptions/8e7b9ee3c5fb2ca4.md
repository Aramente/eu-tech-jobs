<p><strong>THE POSITION</strong></p>
<p>Our roster has an opening with your name on it</p>
<p>We are looking for a <strong>Data Engineer</strong> to join our growing data engineering team and help build the pipelines and infrastructure that power analytics, machine learning, and business decision-making across the company. In this role, you’ll contribute to the design, development, and maintenance of reliable data systems while collaborating with stakeholders to support high-impact data use cases.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a strong technical contributor who enjoys working with data at scale, solving practical problems, and continuously learning in a fast-paced environment.&nbsp;If you’re excited by this challenge and want to work within a dynamic company, then we’d love to hear from you.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>THE GAME PLAN</strong></p>
<p>Everyone on our team has a part to play</p>
<p>Build &amp; Maintain Data Pipelines&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Design, build, and maintain scalable batch and streaming data pipelines to support analytics and business operations.</li>
<li>Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code using tools like Python, SQL, and Spark.</li>
<li>Ensure data is reliable, accurate, and delivered in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
<p>Collaborate Across Teams&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Work with data analysts, data scientists, and product managers to understand requirements and deliver actionable data solutions</li>
<li>Translate business questions into engineering tasks and contribute to technical planning.</li>
<li>Participate in code reviews, sprint planning, and retrospectives as part of an agile team.</li>
</ul>
<p>Data Quality &amp; Operations&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Monitor data pipelines and troubleshoot issues in a timely, systematic manner</li>
<li>Implement data quality checks and contribute to observability and testing practices</li>
<li>Document data sources, transformations, and architecture decisions to support long-term maintainability</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>THE STATS</strong><br>What we're looking for in our next teammate</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in data engineering, analytics engineering, or software engineering with a focus on data.</li>
<li>Strong SQL skills and familiarity with at least one programming language (e.g., Python, Java, or Scala).</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with modern data tools such as Databricks, Airflow, DBT, Spark, or Kafka.</li>
<li>Understanding of data modeling concepts, data warehousing, and ETL/ELT best practices.</li>
<li>Experience working with cloud-based data platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Experience supporting BI, analytics, or data science teams.</li>
<li>Familiarity with version control, CI/CD, and collaborative development workflows.</li>
<li>Exposure to data governance, privacy, or compliance practices.</li>
<li>Eagerness to learn new technologies and contribute to the growth of the team.</li>
</ul>
