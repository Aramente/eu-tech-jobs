<h2 data-section-id="1l3goxx" data-start="668" data-end="691"><strong data-start="671" data-end="689">About the Role</strong></h2>
<p data-start="692" data-end="973">Onboarding Specialists at Klaviyo play a critical role in setting our customers up for long-term success. As the first point of contact post-sale, you’ll guide new customers through their first 30–90 days, helping them successfully implement Klaviyo and achieve fast time-to-value.</p>
<p data-start="975" data-end="1176">You’ll act as a trusted advisor—combining project management, product expertise, and strategic thinking to drive product adoption, reduce friction, and build strong customer relationships from day one.</p>
<h2 data-section-id="1n4ueij" data-start="1183" data-end="1220"><strong data-start="1186" data-end="1218">How You’ll Make a Difference</strong></h2>
<ul data-start="1221" data-end="2126">
<li data-section-id="ykqjdz" data-start="1221" data-end="1353">Own a portfolio of new customers from post-sale through successful go-live, managing multiple onboarding journeys simultaneously</li>
<li data-section-id="hofu5y" data-start="1354" data-end="1449">Design and execute tailored onboarding plans aligned to each customer’s goals and use cases</li>
<li data-section-id="ormni5" data-start="1450" data-end="1552">Guide customers through platform setup, configuration, testing, and early performance optimization</li>
<li data-section-id="10rxoyy" data-start="1553" data-end="1687">Provide proactive, 1:1 support across both technical and non-technical topics (e.g., deliverability, campaign setup, integrations)</li>
<li data-section-id="v5yrqh" data-start="1688" data-end="1783">Track onboarding progress, ensuring milestones are met and holding stakeholders accountable</li>
<li data-section-id="15fhofi" data-start="1784" data-end="1881">Monitor onboarding health metrics to identify risks early and take action to improve outcomes</li>
<li data-section-id="1w3i5l3" data-start="1882" data-end="2012">Partner cross-functionally with Sales, Support, and Product teams to resolve issues and deliver a seamless customer experience</li>
<li data-section-id="fy0fyt" data-start="2013" data-end="2126">Act as a trusted advisor, helping customers build a strong foundation for their marketing and growth strategy</li>
</ul>
