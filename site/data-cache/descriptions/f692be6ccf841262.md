<p><strong>Why you should join the Adyen Account Management team&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>At Adyen, the Account Management team is critical to the growth and success of our business, as well as&nbsp; expanding the Adyen brand across the globe. Being part of our Account Management team, you’ll&nbsp; manage relationships with innovative international merchants, covering multiple industries and channels.&nbsp; You’ll have the opportunity to work on cross-functional initiatives and projects in a fast-paced, highly&nbsp; entrepreneurial environment with high autonomy. You’ll become an expert in the global payments&nbsp; landscape and level up your skills through our AM Academy.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We are looking for a proactive and adaptable Account Manager to join our team in Amsterdam. The ideal&nbsp; candidate is a strategic thinker with a hands-on mentality; someone who is eager to help the world’s&nbsp; biggest brands grow while changing an industry. You are excited to work with a global, multicultural team&nbsp; in a collaborative manner and help your team succeed. Your focus will be on delivering an outstanding&nbsp; customer experience, finding new opportunities, and driving mutual success for Adyen and our&nbsp; customers.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Account Manager&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p>Our Account Managers are encouraged to challenge each other and sharpen ideas as a team, because&nbsp; winning is more important than ego. In this role, you’ll get your energy by working on a wide portfolio of&nbsp; accounts across a variety of verticals. You’ll work alongside a group of talented and driven Account Managers, and together, you’ll develop customer relationships and drive commercial growth for your&nbsp; accounts.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What You’ll Do:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Collaboration: As an Account Manager, you will work closely with other Account Managers and collaborate on many different accounts.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Relationship management: Responsible for ramping and growing customers with a focus on&nbsp; commercial growth, customer satisfaction, and reducing churn.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Commercial acumen: Drive commercial discussions for joint accounts, while striving to meet&nbsp; strategic and financial targets.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Project management: Prioritize and manage short and long term projects with varying timelines&nbsp; across multiple accounts, leveraging internal support teams where relevant.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Feedback: Serve as a feedback loop for your customers internally.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who You Are:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You have 2+ years of experience in a client-facing, commercial role, like account management, sales or in the payments industry from a merchant side (bonus if in complex enterprise&nbsp; software, technical infrastructure or financial services)&nbsp;</li>
<li>Enjoy engaging others and developing strong working relationships internally and externally with&nbsp; customers&nbsp;</li>
<li>Passionate about working on a wide portfolio of accounts and having the opportunity to learn&nbsp; about many aspects of the payments industry&nbsp;</li>
<li>Eager to challenge yourself to identify commercial opportunities and drive commercial&nbsp; discussions with customers&nbsp;</li>
<li>Strong time management skills and constantly looking for ways to work efficiently&nbsp;</li>
<li>You have full professional written and verbal proficiency in Dutch and English</li>

</ul>
