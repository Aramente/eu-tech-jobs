* Work closely with the Brand Design Supervisor and wider team to deliver consistent, on-brand creative across all outputs;

* Ensure all work aligns with brand guidelines, maintaining a high level of craft, consistency, and attention to detail;
* Prepare and deliver assets across multiple formats, including digital, print, and event environments;
* Collaborate with Brand Marketing, Social, Events, and Content teams to deliver creative that meets both brand and business needs.
