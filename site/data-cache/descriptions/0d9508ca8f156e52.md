If you’re a skilled negotiator, driven by results, and eager to work in a supportive, fast-paced environment, this role is for you.

## Your day-to-day:

- Taking ownership of revenue goals and driving sales growth.
- Leading the outbound sales process, from discovery to contract negotiations.
- Developing strategies to identify and engage with potential clients in target industries, such AI, as e-commerce, retail, cybersecurity.
- Building and managing a small team of BDRs, guiding them on account priorities and outreach techniques.
- Attending sales meetings with prospects, tailoring pitches, and crafting compelling value propositions.

## Your Skills & Experiences:

- Couple of years of experience in outbound B2B sales, ideally in SaaS, IaaS, or tech solutions.
- Proven success in managing full-cycle sales with enterprise-level clients.
- Advanced negotiation, presentation, and objection-handling skills.
- A track record of exceeding sales quotas and closing deals with diverse buyer groups.
- Familiarity with modern sales tools (e.g., Salesforce, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Outreach) and a data-driven approach to prospecting.

**Nice to have:**

- Knowledge of IT infrastructure, APIs, or scraping technologies.
- Experience selling to engineering teams or industries like e-commerce, retail, or cybersecurity.
- An additional foreign language (always a plus in international sales).
