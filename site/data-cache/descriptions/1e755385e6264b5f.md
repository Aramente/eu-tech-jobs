Now we’re building the delivery of (almost) everything and you’ll find us in over 500 cities in 30 countries around the world. Courier supply is one of the core marketplace levers—if it works, everything works; if it doesn’t, service degrades, costs spike, and growth slows.</p>
<p>As Head of Courier Supply for the South Region, you will own courier supply end-to-end in Greece. You will report into the South Head of Operations. This role combines strategy, execution, and team leadership, with a strong focus on building scalable, increasingly automated systems that move us from reactive operations to predictive, AI-driven decision-making.</p>
<h2>What you’ll be doing</h2>
<p>This role is about turning demand into reliable, cost-efficient delivery capacity at scale. You will own forecasting, acquisition, incentives, and real-time supply-demand balancing across multiple markets, while building systems that scale without linear headcount growth.</p>
<h3>Day-to-day in this role you’ll:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Own courier supply KPIs across your cities, including availability, utilization, cost efficiency, and service reliability</li>
<li>Lead and develop a team of ~5 people, driving performance and building a high-performing organization</li>
<li>Define and execute supply strategies across different time horizons (hourly, daily, seasonal)</li>
<li>Drive forecasting, incentive design, and supply planning using increasingly AI-driven tools and models</li>
<li>Optimize incentive structures (who, where, when, how much) to ensure efficient growth and controlled costs</li>
<li>Identify and implement scalable solutions to improve courier acquisition, retention, and engagement</li>
<li>Partner with Product, Analytics, Finance, and Global Ops to improve tooling, automation, and decision-making frameworks</li>
<li>Work closely with local teams and external partners (fleet partners, courier partners, authorities) to ensure strong market execution</li>
<li>Detect operational anomalies early and act quickly to prevent service degradation</li>
</ul>

<li>Strong marketplace understanding (supply–demand mechanics) and operational rigor across multiple time horizons</li>
<li>Proven analytical and problem-solving skills, with solid financial acumen (forecasting, ROI, cost control)</li>
