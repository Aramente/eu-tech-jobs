<ul>

<h4><br><strong>What You’ll Do </strong></h4>
<p><strong>Location: </strong>Remotely from the United States. Must live within an hour of a major airport and on the road 40-60% of time.<br><strong>Reports to: </strong>You will report into a Regional Vice President of Sales and operate as a key member of the U.S. State &amp; Local Law Enforcement leadership team.</p>
<p>Lead and Develop a High-Performing Sales Team</p>
<ul>
<li>Directly lead a team of T1200 Account Executives covering Axon’s most strategic State &amp; Local Law Enforcement accounts.</li>
<li>Recruit, onboard, and develop top sales talent; build a high-bar, accountable, performance culture.</li>
<li>Set clear expectations around territory planning, pipeline hygiene, prospecting, and ecosystem selling.</li>
<li>Conduct regular 1:1s, field rides, and deal reviews focused on coaching to outcomes, not just activity.</li>
<li>Create an environment of continuous learning around Axon’s expanding product set, competitive landscape, and public safety trends.</li>
</ul>
<p>Own Segment Strategy and Execution</p>
<ul>
<li>Build and execute a comprehensive go-to-market plan for the T1200 segment aligned with Axon’s strategic priorities.</li>
<li>Define account segmentation, coverage models, and territory design to maximize growth and customer impact.</li>
<li>Drive multi-product, multi-year, enterprise agreements that land and expand Axon’s ecosystem within agencies.</li>
<li>Champion AI and real-time operations offerings as core to the Axon value proposition, not add-ons.</li>
<li>Partner closely with Majors, Mid City, Small City, Inside Sales, and New Products leaders to ensure a coordinated coverage model across shared customers.</li>
</ul>
<p>Drive Forecasting, Pipeline, and Operational Rigor</p>
<ul>
<li>Own forecast accuracy and segment performance against quarterly and annual bookings targets.</li>
<li>Establish disciplined pipeline standards (coverage ratios, stage definitions, conversion benchmarks) and hold the team accountable.</li>
<li>Run tight operating cadences: QBRs, weekly forecast calls, and deal strategy sessions grounded in data.</li>
<li>Leverage Salesforce and BI tools to understand trends in win/loss, product mix, pricing, and cycle time, and translate insights into specific actions.</li>
</ul>
<p>Lead Strategic Deals and Customer Relationships</p>
<ul>
<li>Act as executive sponsor on the highest-impact opportunities and renewals in the T1200 segment.</li>
<li>Build deep relationships with chiefs, sheriffs, command staff, IT leaders, procurement, and political stakeholders to navigate complex decision processes.</li>
<li>Guide the team through large, multi-year RFPs and competitive bids, ensuring we position Axon’s differentiated value and total cost of ownership.</li>
<li>Orchestrate cross-functional deal teams (Sales Engineering, Professional Services, Customer Success, Legal, Finance) to deliver clean, scalable, and winnable proposals.</li>
</ul>
<p>Partner Cross-Functionally to Scale Impact</p>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with Product, Marketing, Sales Strategy, Revenue Intelligence, and Customer Success to:</li>
<li>Shape segment narratives and campaigns tailored to State &amp; Local Law Enforcement</li>
<li>Identify whitespace, expansion, and cross-sell opportunities</li>
<li>Influence product roadmap with field feedback from strategic agencies</li>
<li>Represent the voice of the T1200 customer in internal planning, helping Axon prioritize what matters most to large agencies.</li>
</ul>
<p><br><strong>What You Bring</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>10+ years of progressive experience in enterprise or strategic sales, with at least 5+ years leading frontline sales teams.</li>
<li>Proven success leading high-performing sales teams in complex, multi-product environments (SaaS, hardware + software, or public sector technology strongly preferred).</li>
<li>Experience selling to public sector / SLED, ideally State &amp; Local Law Enforcement or adjacent public safety markets.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to:
<ul>
<li>Exceed multi-million-dollar bookings and revenue targets through team leadership</li>
<li>Guide teams through long, complex sales cycles with multiple stakeholders and formal procurement</li>
<li>Structure multi-year, multi-product agreements that balance customer outcomes and Axon’s growth</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Strong operational discipline: expert in pipeline management, forecasting, and territory planning.</li>
<li>Excellent executive presence and communication skills—comfortable presenting to C-level, elected officials, command staff, and Axon executives.</li>
<li>High learning agility with the ability to quickly understand and coach others on Axon’s full technology ecosystem.</li>
<li>Deep alignment with Axon’s mission to protect life, capture truth, and accelerate justice; genuine empathy for the challenges facing law enforcement and communities today.</li>
</ul>
