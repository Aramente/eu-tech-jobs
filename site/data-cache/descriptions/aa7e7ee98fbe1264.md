**Who is Sonar?**

Sonar is driving the future of agent-centric software development. As the leader in AI code review and verification, we solve a critical problem: ensuring that software generated by AI-assisted developers or autonomous agents is reliable, secure, and maintainable.

Integrating seamlessly with Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, and Devin, we help over 75% of the Fortune 100 build trusted, reliable, compliant software. Customers who use Sonar are 44% less likely to report an outage due to AI-generated code.

We believe code verification is the critical missing link in the Agent-Centric Development Cycle (AC/DC). * SonarSweep & Sonar Context Augmentation: Providing the enterprise-grade context and constraints agents need to be truly effective.

**Position Overview**

The Director, Global Systems Integrators (GSI) is a GTM leader responsible for driving strategic partnerships and business growth with top-tier GSIs.  This role will lead our strategy, develop executive-level relationships, and ensure successful SDLC platform integrations & joint go-to-market initiatives that maximize revenue, partner engagement, and customer success. This role will be based in India.  The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of working with tier one GSIs, integrating software into SDLC & CI/CD frameworks that benefit application/data modernization and software/platform development.  Additionally, experience building successful joint go-to-market initiatives that maximize revenue, partner engagement and customer success is required.

## Key Responsibilities

**Partner Strategies**

- Create and execute a GSI engagement strategy aligned with Sonar’s global objectives.  Win new Alliance Agreements and develop existing ones to drive innovation, revenue and customer success
- Structure transformative alliance deals, drive joint value through technology integrations, co-innovation, and ecosystem partnerships

**Go-to-Market Leadership**

- Lead partner sales motions, solution POCs and limited Pilots that use Sonar’s suite of tools. Drive joint pre-sales trainings, marketing initiatives, industry and GSI specific events to accelerate ecosystem visibility and pipeline development
- Collaborate with sales, product, and marketing teams to ensure alignment on GSI initiatives and pipeline development.
- Facilitate sales team interlocks for joint opportunity pursuits

**Executive Engagement**

- Leverage existing connections and build new ones with C-suite, Managing Directors & Practice Leadership within the GSIs headquartered in India.  Represent Sonar at in region advisory boards, partner councils and executive forums

**Ecosystem Development**

- Enable GSIs through certifications, training and solution value sales readiness programs
- Foster innovation in AI, cloud and industry specific applications development
- Spearhead global initiatives to supercharge software engineering organizations with autonomous AI agents, actionable code intelligence towards software modernization with high quality and security at speed. By driving value, accelerate growth and market expansion, leading to significant ROI of GSIs and joint-customers.

**Performance Management**

- Track partner objectives and milestones, pipeline growth. Report progress to leadership. Adjust engagement models as market dynamics dictate
