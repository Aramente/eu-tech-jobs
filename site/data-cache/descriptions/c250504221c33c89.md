**⭐ Mission**

Join us as a **Product Manager** and help us build the accounting products that will make Qonto the default finance workspace for 600,000+ SMEs and their accountants across Europe. You will own high-impact accounting features end-to-end - from discovery to delivery - all under the guidance of [Paul](https://www.linkedin.com/in/paulmourot/), our Head of Product for Accounting, and with the sponsorship of [Steve](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steveanavi/), our co-founder.

This is not a generic PM role. You'll be working in one of the most complex and regulated domains in fintech - accounting, bookkeeping, and e-invoicing - where deep domain understanding is what separates great products from mediocre ones.

**👩‍💻🧑‍💻 As a Product Manager at Qonto, you will**

• **Own the product roadmap for your scope, end-to-end:** You'll define what we build, why, and in what order - working with engineers, designers, and domain experts to ship features that make a real difference for SMEs and accountants.

• **Go deep on accounting workflows:** You'll develop a sharp understanding of how accounting actually works - production workflows, regulatory constraints (e-invoicing, FEC, OEC), and the day-to-day pain points of both SMEs and their accountants.

• **Ship faster with AI:** You'll use AI tools (Dust, Notion AI, Claude...) and leverage AI as a product lever - identifying where automation and intelligence can transform accounting from a chore into a competitive advantage. (Click [HERE](https://medium.com/qonto-way/ai-at-qonto-vision-statement-725a7e2f659b) to access our vision statement on AI at Qonto).

• **Challenge the status quo:** PMs at Qonto don't just execute a backlog. You'll question assumptions, prototype fast, and push for the best possible product - not just the one that was asked for.

• **Work with maximum squad autonomy:** Your squad owns its roadmap, picks its approaches, and ships independently. No layers of approval, no waiting on committees.

**🏅 What we're looking for**

• **Deep accounting domain expertise:** You've worked at or closely with an accounting-tech company or in a role where you built products for accountants and SMEs. You know the difference between a FEC and a liasse fiscale - and why it matters for the product.

• **Product mindset:** You understand the business value of what you ship. You think in outcomes, not features, and you're comfortable making hard trade-offs.

• **AI-native workflow:** You use AI to supercharge how you work - from research and analysis to spec writing and user testing. You see AI as a product lever, not just a productivity hack.

• **Track record of shipping:** You have 5+ years of experience as a PM, with concrete examples of products you took from 0 to 1 or significantly scaled.

• **Strong communication in English:** You collaborate effectively across functions and can articulate complex domain problems clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders.

• **French is a strong plus** given the regulatory and market context of accounting in France.

🔧 **What you'll get**

• Unlimited access to the best AI tools on the market - **Claude, Dust, Notion AI, Cursor, Copilot**.

• A **squad culture** where **autonomy** is the default: you **own your decisions** and ship **without red tape**.

• A **domain** that's being completely transformed by **AI and regulation** - if you want to build something from scratch at European scale, this is the place.

**🤝 About your future manager**

You'll report to **[Paul Mourot](https://www.linkedin.com/in/paulmourot/), Head of Product for Accounting**.

Paul joined Qonto to build the accounting product line from the ground up. He brings deep experience in complex, regulated product domains and believes in giving PMs real ownership - you'll define the vision for your scope and be accountable for the results.

• 2 Interviews (1 hour each): first with your Talent Acquisition Manager, and then with Paul
