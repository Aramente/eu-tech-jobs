<h3><strong>Your Mission</strong></h3>
<p>You will make an impact that matters by:</p>
<ul>
<li>Contribute to smooth finance operations at Bitmovin, strive for constant process optimization and quality of data while adhering to the 80/20 rule&nbsp;</li>
<li>Co-own the annual budgeting process and monthly rolling forecasts, and collaborate across departments to ensure alignment with our strategic goals.</li>
<li>Co-own the development and maintenance of SaaS-specific &amp; business performance KPIs, analyze them for trends and improvements.</li>
<li>Co-own short-term and long-term liquidity planning, optimizing cash flow.</li>
<li>Deep-dive into single areas of the business in collaboration with respective teams to identify cost-saving and efficiency opportunities</li>
<li>Ensure accurate and timely reporting for the management, board and investors, and provide insightful analysis.</li>
<li>Partner with the accounting team to streamline the month-end and year-end closing processes, and contribute to financial audits (both locally in Austria and globally).</li>
<li>Handle ad-hoc requests from management autonomously and create compelling presentations for informed decision-making.</li>
<li>Liaise with the Revenue Ops team: review deals, track revenue, assist in calculating sales commissions &amp; scorecards</li>
<li>Manage documentation and filings for grants, R&amp;D tax credits (research premium), and other local financial incentives</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What you are</strong></p>
<p><strong>The "Full-Stack" Professional:</strong> You have a solid foundation in accounting (you know your way around a P&amp;L and Balance Sheet) but a heart for FP&amp;A (you love building models that tell a story).</p>
<p><strong>Systems Thinker:</strong> You are highly proficient in Excel and have experience managing or optimizing ERP systems. You prefer automation over manual entry.</p>
<p><strong>Ownership Mindset:</strong> You don't wait for a checklist. You see a process that is broken or data that is messy, and you take the initiative to fix it.</p>
<p><strong>Communicator:</strong> You can translate complex financial data into actionable insights for both the CFO and non-financial stakeholders.</p>
