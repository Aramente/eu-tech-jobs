<p>As a ​Software Engineering Manager​ you will lead and manage the software engineering team to deliver OEM enablement projects. This fast-paced environment requires excellent time management, deep knowledge of the Linux system​, low-level debugging, critical thinking, problem-solving skills and management ability.</p>

<ul>
<li>Recruit, motivate, mentor, and enable your team to succeed in delivering quality products</li>

<li>Maximise the results of team members and support their career growth</li>
<li>Coordinate with project managers, HWE, QA and certification management</li>
<li>Monitor projects from kickoff to post-mortem and handle escalations</li>
