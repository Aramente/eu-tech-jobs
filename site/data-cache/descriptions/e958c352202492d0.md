<div><strong>About the Role</strong></div>
<p>We’re looking for a design intern who’s curious, hands-on, and excited to actually make things. You’re not just going to watch from the sidelines but actually work on real projects, and see it live with millions of users. If you enjoy thinking through ideas, and believe good design is as much about the words as the visuals, you’ll fit right in.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>Work closely with our design team to shape experiences that feel simple, clear, and genuinely helpful for our users. We’re looking for someone who likes to explore, try things out, and isn’t afraid to experiment. You’ll be thinking through layouts, visuals, and hierarchy, and using words to make ideas land clearly. From campaign creatives and in-app moments to small bits of microcopy, you’ll explore how design and language work together, experiment with different approaches, and learn what helps financial ideas feel simple and inviting.</p>

<li>Understands how words and visuals work together to make ideas clearer</li>
