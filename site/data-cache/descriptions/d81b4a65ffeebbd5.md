## What you will do:

- Reporting to the Director of Engineering, you will lead a team, developing performance testing frameworks and utilities to ensure the scalability and reliability of our systems.
- Collaborate with development teams to identify and address performance bottlenecks in software applications and infrastructure.
- Build system testing strategies, tools and infrastructure to test the reliability and resilience of Sysdig platform.
- Implement custom tooling for automated performance evaluations in CI/CD lifecycle to provide real-time feedback on system performance.
- Conduct system performance analysis and provide optimization recommendations to enhance the efficiency of cloud and container environments.
- Participate in the architectural design reviews to ensure performance considerations are included in the development of new features.

## What you will bring with you:

- Experience in a Manager or Technical Lead role
- 5+ years of experience with performance automation tools such as Locust, Jmeter, K6.
- Programming skills in Python, Golang or Java with a focus on developing performance tooling.
- Experience with cloud environments (AWS / Azure / GCP) and experience working with these platforms.
- Demonstrated ability in using profiling tools to identify performance issues.
- Knowledge in Observability and Monitoring tools (Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, …)
- Ability to collaborate effectively across global time zones
- Strong project tracking capabilities with the ability to drive initiatives forward, maintain strategic direction, and manage a team

## What we look for:

- Experience with container technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
- Background in managing large-scale distributed systems and an understanding of network protocols and security.
- Curious in learning new technologies and methodologies in performance engineering.
- Experience building scalable, resilient, and observable distributed systems
