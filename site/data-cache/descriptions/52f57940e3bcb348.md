<p>As an <strong><strong class="textBold">IT Enterprise Applications Engineer</strong></strong>, you'll help GitLab run critical business processes that support how the company sells, buys, accounts, and scales. You'll focus on <strong><strong class="textBold">NetSuite</strong></strong> and <strong><strong class="textBold">Zuora Revenue</strong></strong>, building and supporting the systems, configurations, workflows, and code that keep processes like <strong><strong class="textBold">Quote to Cash</strong></strong>, <strong><strong class="textBold">Procure to Pay</strong></strong>, and <strong><strong class="textBold">Record to Report</strong></strong> efficient, compliant, and reliable. In this role, you'll join the <strong><strong class="textBold">Enterprise Applications G&amp;A team</strong></strong> and report to the <strong><strong class="textBold">Senior Manager, G&amp;A Systems</strong></strong>, partnering closely with business stakeholders to turn operational needs into maintainable technical solutions.</p>
<p>This is a strong fit if you want ownership across both day-to-day administration and longer-term improvements. You will work independently on medium-complexity problems, support SOX-compliant change management, contribute to system roadmap decisions, and help the team deliver scalable application support in a fully remote environment.</p>
<h2>What you’ll do</h2>
<ul>
<li value="1"><strong><strong class="textBold">Administer</strong></strong> and configure <strong><strong class="textBold">NetSuite</strong></strong> and <strong><strong class="textBold">Zuora Revenue</strong></strong> to support GitLab business processes and compliant system operations.</li>
<li value="2"><strong><strong class="textBold">Translate</strong></strong> business needs into technical requirements, workflows, customizations, and application changes that improve process efficiency.</li>
<li value="3"><strong><strong class="textBold">Partner</strong></strong> with Finance, business systems peers, and other stakeholders to ensure applications are functioning as designed and meeting operational needs.</li>
<li value="4"><strong><strong class="textBold">Support</strong></strong> SOX-compliant change management, including documentation, governance, and adherence to data, security, and legal guidelines.</li>
<li value="5"><strong><strong class="textBold">Manage</strong></strong> inbound integrations and data flows between enterprise systems, including troubleshooting issues across the ERP and revenue systems landscape.</li>
<li value="6"><strong><strong class="textBold">Contribute</strong></strong> code, customizations, and integrations in development environments using GitLab SDLC practices and internal engineering standards.</li>
<li value="7"><strong><strong class="textBold">Conduct</strong></strong> code reviews and support maintainable, well-documented technical delivery aligned with team guidelines.</li>
<li value="8"><strong><strong class="textBold">Run</strong></strong> small projects and contribute to team planning for new features, process improvements, and system roadmap decisions.</li>
</ul>
<h2>What you’ll bring</h2>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree, preferably in Computer Science, Information Technology, Computer Engineering, or related IT discipline; or equivalent experience.<br>3-5 years of experience in a system administration role, ideally with an enterprise SaaS software model.</li>
<li>Hands-on administrator experience in NetSuite and Zuora Revenue:&nbsp;</li>
<li>Intermediate knowledge of NetSuite data objects, workflows, custom fields, and custom forms</li>
<li>Ability to troubleshoot and understand the integration landscape to and from our ERP</li>
<li>Understanding of NetSuite SuiteScripting &amp; SQL is a plus</li>
<li>SOX experience, working with auditors to provide effective walkthroughs on our system processes</li>
<li>Strong business process knowledge of finance/accounting processes is strongly preferred (procure to pay, record to report)</li>
<li><strong>Zuora Revenue (RevPro) Administration</strong>: Configure and maintain revenue rules, POB templates, SSP allocations, and accounting treatments supporting ASC 606 compliance across multi-element arrangements.</li>
<li><strong>Data Integration &amp; Pipeline Management: </strong>Manage inbound data flows from upstream billing and CRM systems (e.g., Zuora Billing, Salesforce), including staging table validation, error triage, and held transaction resolution.</li>
<li><strong>Revenue Close &amp; GL Reconciliation: </strong>Support period-end close processes, including journal entry generation, GL account mapping, revenue waterfall review, and subledger-to-ERP reconciliation (e.g., NetSuite).</li>
<li><strong>Troubleshooting &amp; Exception Management</strong> — Investigate and resolve revenue processing exceptions, contract modifications, reallocation events, and data quality issues across the order-to-revenue lifecycle.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred:&nbsp;</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Secondary system knowledge is a plus: Salesforce, DocuSign CLM, Zuora Billing, Coupa, Zip, Navan, Workday, ADP, Ironclad, Xactly, and Workato</li>
<li>Knowledge of GitLab SDLC &amp; DAP</li>
<li>Knowledge of Claude tools is a plus (Claude cowork, Claude code, &amp; MCP)</li>
<li>Knowledge of VSCode, Git, Javascript</li>
<li>Multiple industry certifications preferred</li>
<li>Public company and SOX ITGC controls experience preferred.</li>
</ul>
