Join us as an **HR Product Manager (AI & Automation)** to shape how AI transforms people operations for our **People team** (75 people). You'll spot where AI creates the most impact, build the backlog, and ship the tools yourself - from smart automations to full AI-native products - under the guidance of [**Mélodie**](http://linkedin.com/in/m%C3%A9lodie-moulin-262a3840?originalSubdomain=fr), our Head of People Systems.

**👩‍💻🧑‍💻  As a HR Product Manager (AI & Automation) at Qonto, you will :**

* **Map the people stack end-to-end:** You'll uncover the workflows and pain points across recruiting, onboarding, performance, compensation and HR operations that cost the team the most time.
* **Own the backlog:** You'll build and maintain a prioritised list of opportunities, ranked by impact and feasibility.
* **Turn feedback into shippable problems:**  You'll collect input from HRBPs, recruiters, managers, and all Qontoer’s and go further with discovery practices (UX research, POC/tests). You will translate those insights into clear problem statements, ready to build.
* **Ship products end-to-end:** You will define the best technical solutions to tackle pain points and build our own HR product. You'll leverage AI to build your applications, prompts, evals, integrations, and automations yourself — not just spec them.
* **Measure what you ship:** You'll define success metrics upfront and track them rigorously after launch.

**🤔 What you can expect**

* **A blank page with real scope:** You start from zero on a role that touches every Qontoer.
* **Leadership visibility:** Your work directly shapes how Qonto applies AI in HR.
* **Unlimited AI tooling:** Every AI tool you need, no permission slips.
* **A culture that measures impact:** You'll iterate fast and track outcomes, not just outputs.

**🤝  Your future manager**

You'll report to  [Mélodie](http://linkedin.com/in/m%C3%A9lodie-moulin-262a3840?originalSubdomain=fr), Head of People Systems.

She joined Qonto in April 2025 and brings deep functional HR expertise with a product-minded, operational-excellence lens.

She will provide you with the trust and autonomy needed to innovate, while strategically guiding the team through challenging organizational transformations.
