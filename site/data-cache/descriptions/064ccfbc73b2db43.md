<div class="meSK8 M7eMe"><strong>Role Description</strong></div>
<div class="spb5Rd OIC90c">
<p>We are seeking a high-energy, hands-on&nbsp;<strong>Director of Finance</strong> who thrives in the intersection of day-to-day execution and long-term strategy. This is a "player-coach" role: while you will have the support of a bookkeeper, you are expected to be deeply involved in the daily financial operations, system optimization, and data integrity. You aren't just reviewing the books; you are mastering them.</p>
<p><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Direct Daily Operations:</strong> Lead and help execute all day-to-day accounting functions, including AP/AR, payroll, and general ledger management. You will be the primary driver of the monthly, quarterly, and annual close processes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Master QuickBooks Online:</strong> Act as the internal expert for <strong>QuickBooks Online</strong>, ensuring all transactions are categorized correctly and the system is utilized to its full potential for accuracy and scalability.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Proactive Cash Flow Management:</strong> Take full ownership of cash flow, aggressively maintaining low A/R levels and ensuring timely collections to fuel business growth.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Systems &amp; Process Engineering:</strong> Evaluate and "roll up your sleeves" to improve financial systems and internal controls, ensuring our processes are efficient, GAAP-compliant, and audit-ready.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Data &amp; Power BI Integration:</strong> Partner closely with the Reporting Manager to ensure financial data integrates seamlessly into <strong>Power BI</strong>. You will actively engage with dashboards to monitor performance and verify data integrity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Budgeting &amp; Forecasting:</strong> Lead the development of annual budgets and rolling forecasts, translating operational data into actionable financial roadmaps.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Strategic Advisory:</strong> Provide the executive team with high-level financial insights, leveraging data to inform long-term business planning and sustainable growth.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Mentorship:</strong> Manage and mentor our staff bookkeeper, providing clear direction and fostering a high-performance, collaborative environment.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>7+ years of experience</strong> in accounting or financial management, with a proven track record of managing "the books" while contributing to "the big picture."</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>B2B Expertise:</strong> Extensive experience in a B2B environment, specifically working with <strong>physical products</strong> and inventory-heavy operations.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>QuickBooks Power User:</strong> Advanced proficiency in <strong>QuickBooks Online</strong> is required; you should be comfortable navigating every corner of the platform.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Technical Literacy:</strong> Ability to work fluently within <strong>Power BI</strong> for data analysis and performance tracking.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Educational Background:</strong> Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field. A CPA is a strong plus but not a requirement.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Mindset:</strong> Highly organized, detail-obsessed, and passionate about building financial systems that support both creativity and scale.</p>
</li>
</ul>
