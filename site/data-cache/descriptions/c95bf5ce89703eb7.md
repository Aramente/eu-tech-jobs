<p>At JetBrains, we’re building Air, an AI-native platform for software development that connects developer workflows, team-level collaboration, and organizational control into a single coherent system. This platform will serve as the execution and governance layer for AI-driven development, deeply integrated with developer tools and designed to work across teams, products, and environments. This is a long-term strategic investment for JetBrains and a key pillar of our vision for the future of software development.</p>
<p>The Air team is looking for a Developer Advocate to help developers understand, adopt, and shape the future of agentic development.</p>
<p>In this role, you will represent Air in the developer community. You’ll create technical content, demonstrate real-world use cases, engage with developers online and in person, and bring community feedback directly to the product team.</p>
<p>You’ll help developers understand how to use agents effectively in their workflows, while also helping the Air team learn how developers actually want to build with them.</p>
<p>This role sits at the intersection of developer experience, content, and community.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p>Create technical content</p>
<ul>
<li>Produce videos, demos, and written guides that showcase real-world Air workflows.</li>
<li>Explore agent-driven development scenarios and share practical experiments.</li>
<li>Build example projects and tutorials.</li>
</ul>
<p>Engage with the developer community</p>
<ul>
<li>Participate in conversations on X, Reddit, and other developer platforms.</li>
<li>Answer technical questions and help developers get started with Air.</li>
<li>Share updates and insights from the Air team.</li>
</ul>
<p>Represent Air publicly</p>
<ul>

<li>Host Air community meetups and workshops.</li>

</ul>
<p>Collaborate with the product team</p>
<ul>
<li>Share insights and feedback from the developer community.</li>

<li>Contribute to product messaging and developer documentation</li>
</ul>
