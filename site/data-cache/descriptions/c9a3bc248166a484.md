<h2><strong>JOIN YOUSICIAN</strong></h2>
<p>Yousician impacts and enriches the lives of millions of people by empowering them to play music they love, learn something new, and nourish their well-being. We merge music and technology to amplify the everyday. We reimagine how people learn music so that everyone can play.</p>
<p>And you don’t need to be a musician to join Yousician!</p>
<p>We’re a band of passionate professionals who care deeply about quality, creativity, and impact. From beginners picking up their first instrument to lifelong players, we build experiences that make learning music feel personal, joyful, and motivating.</p>
<h2><strong>WHY YOU SHOULD APPLY</strong></h2>
<p>We’re building the future of music education: an <strong>AI-powered music teacher you can talk to</strong>, one that understands your goals, adapts to your level, and gives you meaningful, personalized guidance.</p>
<p>You’ll join a <strong>small, senior team</strong> working end-to-end on this vision — from user experience and animation polish to backend integrations of AI-driven features. This is a hands-on role with real ownership, where your technical decisions will directly shape how millions of people learn.</p>
<p>If you enjoy crafting smooth, responsive interfaces, aren’t afraid to dive into backend code when needed, and want to work on a product that blends AI, creativity, and education, this role is for you.</p>
