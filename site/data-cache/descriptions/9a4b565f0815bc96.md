<p>&nbsp;</p></div><p><strong>Staffing Specialist (Recruiter)</strong></p>
<p>As a Staffing Specialist, you’ll play a vital role in connecting top talent with meaningful job opportunities while helping our clients grow their workforce. This role is focused on supporting our manufacturing, warehouse, and light industrial clients, requiring a hands-on, fast-paced approach to staffing and operations.</p>
<p><strong><em>This&nbsp;role&nbsp;requires&nbsp;candidates&nbsp;to&nbsp;be&nbsp;based&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;CST&nbsp;or&nbsp;MST&nbsp;Time&nbsp;Zones&nbsp;and&nbsp;able&nbsp;to&nbsp;consistently&nbsp;flex&nbsp;their&nbsp;schedule,&nbsp;typically&nbsp;working&nbsp;hours&nbsp;such&nbsp;as&nbsp;9:00am–6:30pm&nbsp;or&nbsp;10:00am–7:30pm&nbsp;CST.</em></strong></p>
<p>If you’re resourceful, people-savvy, and passionate about making a difference, we want to meet you.</p>
<p><strong>What You’ll Do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage a high volume of daily activity including outbound calls, candidate outreach, resume review, and consistent follow-up with both talent and clients.</li>
<li>Recruit and place top talent for manufacturing, warehouse, and light industrial roles through job boards, social media, referrals, and community outreach. Manage the full hiring process from sourcing to onboarding.</li>
<li>Support and coach your talent throughout their assignments — checking in regularly, offering feedback, and helping them grow.</li>
<li>Build strong client relationships by understanding their workforce needs, conducting regular check-ins, and delivering thoughtful hiring solutions.</li>
<li>Drive growth by skill-marketing standout candidates, collaborating with sales, and identifying new opportunities to serve clients.</li>
<li>Keep operations running smoothly by supporting tasks like payroll, timekeeping, and compliance.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who Thrives Here:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Naturally curious and people-oriented</li>
<li>Highly organized and adaptable, comfortable in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Motivated by results, but guided by empathy</li>
<li>Passionate about connecting people with opportunity</li>
</ul>
