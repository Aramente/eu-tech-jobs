</div></div><h2><strong>Role Overview</strong></h2>
<p>As a Senior Consulting Engineer at Neo4j, you will work closely with enterprise customers across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region to design and implement graph-powered solutions. Partnering with customer engineering teams, you will guide architecture decisions, design graph data models, and help deliver successful Neo4j implementations from initial design through to production deployment.</p>
<p>You will act as a trusted technical advisor during consulting engagements, working hands-on with Neo4j technology while helping customers adopt best practices for graph data modelling, application development, and modern AI-powered data architectures.</p>
<p>You will also help customers solve complex data and analytics challenges by applying graph technology to deliver practical, production-ready solutions.</p>
<h2><strong>Key Responsibilities</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Work closely with customer engineering teams to lead and contribute to Neo4j implementations across the full project lifecycle, from initial design through to production roll-out</li>
<li>Act as the technical subject matter expert on consulting engagements, providing hands-on guidance and technical leadership for succeeding with Neo4j technology</li>
<li>Design and implement graph data models that represent complex relationships and support high-performance querying and analytics</li>
<li>Architect and build solutions that meet both functional and non-functional requirements using Neo4j</li>
<li>Support modern data and AI architectures by structuring graph data to enable advanced analytics, search, and emerging AI patterns such as RAG</li>
<li>Collaborate with customer AI and data teams to integrate Neo4j into modern orchestration frameworks such as LangChain or LlamaIndex</li>
<li>Provide guidance to customers on best practices for Data Engineering, focusing on optimising data ingestion patterns into Neo4j.</li>
<li>Develop solution architectures that address the technical requirements of customer projects</li>
<li>Translate complex technical concepts into clear guidance for customer engineers and stakeholders</li>
<li>Contribute to solution proposals, effort assessments, SOWs and technical recommendations for customer engineering and leadership teams</li>
<li>Train and mentor customer teams on Neo4j best practices and graph development techniques</li>
<li>Work collaboratively with Neo4j sales and support teams to ensure successful customer outcomes</li>
<li>Travel within Australia or internationally may be required depending on customer engagement needs (Up to 50%)<br><br></li>
</ul>
