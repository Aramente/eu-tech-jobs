<p>Accelerate your career by joining Okta as our <strong>AI Operations Lead</strong>. You will be the map-maker for our AI ecosystem, ensuring that as we innovate at the speed of SaaS, we maintain a clear, documented, and compliant understanding of what data fuels our AI and how it is used.</p>

<h3><strong>What you’ll be doing</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>The Enterprise AI Command Center</strong>: Establish and oversee the enterprise-wide AI Asset Library, serving as the definitive, central source of truth for all models, internal workflows, and third-party integrations.</li>
<li><strong>Revenue Acceleration &amp; Path Clearing</strong>: Act as a strategic liaison between Legal, Engineering, and Sales to build cross-functional bridges that turn complex hurdles into competitive advantages, directly accelerating our market growth.</li>
<li><strong>Data Provenance &amp; Lineage Architecture</strong>: Map and document the entire lifecycle of data, from source origins to final usage, ensuring every AI output is explainable, audit-ready, and legally sound.</li>
<li><strong>Operationalizing Ethics at Scale</strong>: Design and execute high-velocity operational workflows (AIIAs and Model Cards) to assess privacy, ethical, and legal risks, ensuring we reach production with speed and safety.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Vendor Gatekeeper</strong>: Maintain and evolve procurement processes to ensure every new AI tool meets our internal trust standards before it is integrated into our ecosystem.</li>
<li><strong>Rigorous Third-Party Auditing</strong>: Conduct deep-dive operational audits of vendor AI capabilities, evaluating data retention and opt-out mechanisms to protect our most valuable company and customer data.</li>
<li><strong>Policy-to-Playbook Transformation</strong>: Translate high-level governance into actionable, high-performance playbooks, ensuring compliance is built into our daily operations rather than treated as an afterthought.</li>
<li><strong>Global Content Curation</strong>: Independently lead the effort to ensure our AI messaging remains consistent, clear, and authoritative both internally and for our global customer base.</li>
<li><strong>Remote-First Internal Diplomacy</strong>: Expertly navigate complex stakeholder interests across our global organization to ensure AI initiatives move forward at pace without compromising security.</li>
<li><strong>Critical Incident Response Leadership</strong>: Provide the essential data maps and asset intelligence needed by Legal and Security to resolve potential incidents with precision and speed.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
