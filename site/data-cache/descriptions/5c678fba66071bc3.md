<h3><strong>Team description:</strong></h3>
<p>You’ll join the <strong>Complaints &amp; compliance </strong><strong>Team</strong>, a group of specialists dedicated to supporting our merchants by ensuring transparent, fair, and compliant payment experiences. The team works closely with <strong>Customer Support, Risk &amp; Fraud, Payment Operations, and Product</strong> to investigate complex payment-related issues and improve processes. We value collaboration, accountability, and a pragmatic approach:&nbsp; balancing customer empathy with regulatory and operational precision.</p>
<p><strong>What You’ll Do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Manage, investigate, and resolve merchant-facing payment complaints within regulatory timelines and internal SLAs.<br><br></li>
<li>Perform root-cause analysis across processors, gateways, and payout systems, documenting findings and actions taken.<br><br></li>
<li>Draft clear, compliant, and customer-friendly final responses aligned with regulatory standards and privacy requirements.<br><br></li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Payments Ops, Risk/Fraud, Engineering, Finance, Customer Support) to resolve complex cases.<br><br></li>
<li>Maintain accurate and audit-ready documentation in the complaints management system.<br><br></li>
<li>Monitor and prioritise complaint queues, escalations, and regulator inquiries based on urgency and impact.<br><br></li>
<li>Identify recurring issues and contribute to playbooks, SOPs, and internal knowledge bases.<br><br></li>
<li>Support team members through coaching on complaint handling, documentation, and compliance topics.<br><br></li>
<li>Partner with Data/Analytics to validate impact and measure resolution effectiveness.<br><br></li>
</ul>
