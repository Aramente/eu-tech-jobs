<ul>
<li>Lead all direct and indirect tax matters for the APAC region, partnering closely with Finance, Legal, and Business teams to ensure compliance, manage risk, and support growth.</li>
<li>Own end-to-end management of Corporate Income Tax, VAT/GST, WHT, and other local taxes across APAC jurisdictions.&nbsp; Review and sign off on tax returns, estimates, and payments prepared by internal teams and external advisors.</li>
<li>Support the preparation and review of tax provisions and deferred taxes for APAC entities in collaboration with the global tax accounting team.</li>
<li>Serve as the primary advisor for APAC on the tax implications of new business models, commercial contracts, and market launches in the region.</li>
<li>Partner with the global tax team to monitor transfer pricing policies for accuracy and completeness and offer key APAC insights.&nbsp; Implement, execute, and defend holistic transfer pricing strategies.&nbsp; Oversee the preparation of transfer pricing documentation as it relates to the APAC region.</li>
<li>Lead the management of tax audits, inquiries, and controversy across APAC, including strategy, documentation, and negotiations.</li>
<li>Monitor legislative and regulatory changes across APAC and proactively assess the impact on the business.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Support global tax initiatives (e.g., BEPS, Public Country-by-Country Reporting, digital services taxes) and drive regional implementation.</li>
<li>Drive standardization and automation of tax processes across the region, partnering with Finance Systems and IT teams.</li>
<li>Represent APAC Tax in cross‑functional projects.&nbsp; Foster a culture of accountability, continuous improvement, and strong business partnership.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What we’re looking for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, Law, or related field; Accredited Tax Advisor (ATA) qualified or equivalent.</li>
<li>8+ years of relevant experience at a public accounting firm and/or tax department of a multinational public company with responsibility for regional tax matters in APAC, including emerging markets.</li>
<li>Strong technical knowledge of corporate income tax, indirect tax (GST/VAT), and withholding tax rules in APAC markets.</li>
<li>Experience with tax accounting and reporting under US GAAP and/or IFRS.</li>
<li>Exposure to transfer pricing design and documentation for cross‑border intercompany transactions.</li>
<li>Proven track record managing tax audits and interactions with tax authorities.</li>
<li>Familiarity with global tax reform and its impact on APAC.</li>
<li>Experience working in a multinational environment and collaborating across time zones and cultures.</li>
<li>Excellent analytical, problem‑solving, and communication skills, with the ability to explain complex tax issues to non‑tax stakeholders.</li>
</ul>
