Mayflower is a technology company that alters the entertainment industry to a new level of perception and engagement.

Now we look for an **Accountant** to join our Accounting team.

#### Job Responsibilities

* Record daily financial transactions, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger entries;
* Perform Accounts Reconciliations (Customers, Vendors, Bank, eWallets, Payment Processors) for a large volume of data;
* Create and manage invoices, monitor outstanding payments, and follow up on overdue accounts;
* Preparation and analysis of monthly management reports (income statements and balance sheets);
* Preparation and submission of VAT (OSS, VIES) and other VAT declarations;
* Coordinate with auditors for year-end audits, providing necessary documentation;
* Keeping proper books of account and financial records in the Accounting Software.
