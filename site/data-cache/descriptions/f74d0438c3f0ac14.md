<p>We are looking for a <strong>Senior Product Manager</strong> to join the <strong>Practice Efficiency</strong> team in <strong>Milan.</strong></p>
<p>As a Senior Product Manager for Doctolib Telehealth, you'll shape how millions of patients and healthcare professionals connect remotely across Europe. With over 10 million patients already using Doctolib Telehealth annually, you'll define both the immediate user experience and the long-term vision for synchronous and asynchronous remote consultations. Working at the intersection of product strategy, clinical expertise, and AI innovation, you'll own the full healthcare professional user experience while collaborating closely with teams across doctor-patient communication, patient data, and the broader patient domain. This is a global role where you'll work across multiple European healthcare markets to ensure Doctolib Telehealth succeeds at scale.</p>
<h2><strong>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>Define and execute the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for Doctolib's telehealth capabilities within the Remote Care stream</li>
<li>Drive both short-term product execution and long-term strategic vision for synchronous and asynchronous remote consultations</li>
<li>Make high-level architectural choices in close collaboration with your engineering counterpart, bringing a tech-savvy approach to product decisions</li>
<li>Collaborate with engineers, designers, clinical experts, and go-to-market stakeholders to build solutions serving multiple user personas across all Doctolib markets</li>
<li>Conduct user research and competitive analysis to identify unmet needs and drive data-grounded prioritization</li>
<li>Foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning within the broader product management team, with the potential to lead other product managers</li>
</ul>
