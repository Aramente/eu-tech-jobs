<p><strong>About The Role</strong></p>
<p>Cloudflare ANZ is investing in a focused&nbsp;Digital Native channel strategy, and we are hiring a Partner Account Manager to build and scale this motion.</p>
<p>This is not a traditional channel management role.&nbsp;This is a strategic, builder-focused position responsible for recruiting, developing, and scaling a small number (3–5) of high-impact cloud-native and DevOps-focused partners who serve Digital Native customers across markets including, but not limited to (SaaS, fintech, e-commerce, marketplaces, AI startups, scale-ups).</p>
<p>The role reports to the&nbsp;Director of ANZ Partner Sales&nbsp;and works in close partnership with Cloudflare’s&nbsp;Sales Director (ANZ)&nbsp;and the Digital Native Account Executive team. Success in this role requires tight co-sell alignment, shared pipeline accountability, and strong cross-functional collaboration.</p>
<p>You will operate as a business builder, creating a scalable partner ecosystem that positions Cloudflare as the connectivity and security platform of choice for high-growth cloud-native companies.</p>
<p>Travel will be required across ANZ to support partner recruitment, enablement, customer meetings, executive alignment, and strategic governance.</p>
<p><strong>Why this role matters</strong></p>
<p>Digital Native companies are among the fastest-growing and most security-conscious segments in ANZ. They demand modern, developer-friendly, scalable platforms.</p>
<p>This role is central to embedding Cloudflare within cloud-native architecture decisions, driving revenue in close alignment with the ANZ Sales Director and Digital Native Account Executives.</p>
<p>You will directly shape the ANZ Digital Native partner ecosystem and build a durable, scalable co-sell engine.</p>
