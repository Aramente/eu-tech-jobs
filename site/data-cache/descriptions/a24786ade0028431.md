<h2><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h2>
<p>At Doctolib, we're on a mission to transform the way healthcare is delivered by leveraging the power of AI. As a Machine Learning Engineer, you'll play a critical role in developing and implementing cutting-edge AI solutions that will simplify the access and quality of care for all.</p>
<p>In this role, you'll have the opportunity to work with a team of talented ML Engineers, Software Engineers, MLOps and Healthcare professionals to develop and deploy AI models that will have a real impact on people's lives.</p>
<p>You will work at simplifying how patients find their healthcare practitioner, handle their care plan in the long term and work with business team to contribute expansion of Doctolib in several new markets.</p>
<p>Doctolib is looking for a Machine Learning Engineer to join our ML Engineering team in charge of our patient solutions.</p>
<p>Your responsibilities include but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Find the right technical solution to solve the product domain goals</li>
<li>Implement your ideas and test them</li>
<li>Deploy your algorithms in production guided by our ML platform team</li>
<li>Measure the uplift and continuously improve your approach&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Who you are</strong></h2>
<p>You could be our next team mate if you:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Have strong analytical skills, are result oriented and user first</li>
<li>Have between 3/7 years of experience in the domain of Machine Learning / Deep Learning / AI Engineering, including taking models from prototype to production at scale.</li>
<li>Have<strong>&nbsp;experience in Information Retrieval and modern retrieval stacks</strong>, including some of the following:
<ul>
<li>Hybrid search (sparse + dense)</li>
<li>Large-scale embeddings and vector databases&nbsp;</li>
<li>Multi-stage retrieval and re-ranking pipelines</li>
<li>RAG architectures and retrieval pipelines on multimodal use cases</li>
<li>Tools and MCP-based integrations to bring in external data and capabilities</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Proficient in LLM/VLM application development, including:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Fine-tuning LLM and VLM models</li>
<li>Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures (via LiteLLM or Model Garden)</li>
<li>Knowledge Distillation&nbsp;</li>
<li>Prompt engineering and tool use</li>
<li>Evaluation and benchmarking of LLM/VLM systems</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Have <strong>hands-on experience with agentic AI</strong> (e.g. building and orchestrating agents on top of ADK).</li>
<li>Have demonstrated <strong>strong scientific rigor and benchmarking skills</strong>:<br>
<ul>
<li>Designing metrics aligned with product goals,</li>
<li>Running controlled end to end experiments with W&amp;B, MLFlow or Braintrust</li>
<li>Analyzing and communicating results to guide product and technical decisions.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Have experience with <strong>large scale applications in production</strong> (monitoring, reliability, performance, observability).</li>
</ul>
<p>Now, it would be fantastic if you:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Have experience in B2C marketplace.</li>
<li>Have experience in other ML methodologies : pattern mining, recommendation, experimentation or causal inference.</li>
</ul>
