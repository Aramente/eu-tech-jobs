<p><strong>Internal Control Officer - Financial Risk</strong></p>
<p>Adyen is looking for an Internal Control Officer with a focus on financial risk in our Amsterdam office. You will join the global Internal Control team, which contributes to power Adyen’s sustainable growth. You are experienced in internal control and have the ability and eagerness to operate independently in a global environment.&nbsp;</p>
<p>You will mainly focus on assessing and strengthening Adyen’s global financial and reporting processes and controls to meet internal and external expectations and to enable controlled growth. We work as a team across cultures and time zones, and you will support the company in building a great business, preserving the company culture and achieving its long-term goals by taking our risk management to the next level.</p>
<p>We don’t hide behind email, instead we pick up the phone. This is one of the values in our Adyen Formula, which is the foundation of our strong culture and an essential guiding principle for the way we work. We always ask why and are critical, we don’t just tick the box, and we always look for improvement and automation. These are some of the values from our Adyen way of being in control formula, which the Internal Control team promotes globally.</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Strengthen the internal control framework and support management with internal control matters.</li>
<li>Evaluate Adyen’s financial and reporting processes and controls to identify opportunities for improvement.</li>
<li>Perform independent review and testing of controls.</li>
<li>Identify and evaluate control deficiencies.</li>
<li>Manage requests from auditors to ensure successful audit cycles (e.g. SOC1/SOC2 audits).</li>
<li>Establish a professional network and serve as an advisor to Finance teams to strengthen the financial control framework and support various projects.</li>
<li>Collaborate with your peers from the Internal Control team in managing the global internal control framework and contribute to various projects.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Who you are:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Relevant working experience (6+ years) within an external audit, risk management or internal control function. Experience within a big-4 and/or the financial services industry is a plus.</li>
<li>Experience with designing and implementing internal control frameworks.</li>
<li>Critical thinking skills and eagerness to solve challenges.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Able to operate comfortably and independently within a global environment.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Eagerness to work in an innovative technology company within the financial sector.</li>
<li>You thrive in an unstructured, fast-paced environment, challenging the status-quo and a ‘can-do’ mentality.</li>
<li>Good communicative and stakeholder management skills.</li>
</ul>
