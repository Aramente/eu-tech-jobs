<p><strong>THE WORK:</strong></p>
<p>At Ripple Treasury, we empower finance and treasury teams around the world to work smarter, faster, and with greater confidence. Our cloud-based treasury and risk management platform helps organizations gain real-time visibility into their cash, liquidity, and risk positions—enabling better decisions and stronger financial outcomes. We believe in delivering not just software, but transformative solutions that make a measurable difference for our clients.</p>
<p>As an Engagement Manager, you will be the strategic driver behind complex, client-facing professional services projects—ensuring that Ripple Treasury solutions are implemented seamlessly, on time, and with exceptional client satisfaction. You’ll be the trusted partner for our enterprise customers, orchestrating cross-functional teams, managing multiple workstreams, and ensuring that every implementation delivers measurable business value. Your leadership will directly influence how clients experience Ripple Treasury from day one, setting the stage for long-term success.</p>
<p><strong>WHAT YOU’LL DO:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead end-to-end delivery of large-scale, multi-phase Ripple Treasury solution implementations for enterprise clients.</li>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact for client executives and project stakeholders, building trust and ensuring alignment on goals, timelines, and deliverables.</li>
<li>Develop and maintain detailed program plans, resource allocations, issue and escalation logs across multiple concurrent projects.</li>
<li>Coordinate cross-functional teams—including Professional Services, Product, Engineering, and Support—to ensure smooth execution and issue resolution.</li>
<li>Ensure projects stay on track and within scope by identifying and managing risks proactively to minimize issues and escalations.</li>
<li>Facilitate executive-level status updates, steering committee meetings, and post-implementation reviews.</li>
<li>Champion best practices in program governance, change management, and client communication.</li>
<li>Mentor project managers and consultants, fostering a culture of excellence and continuous improvement.</li>
</ul>
