### **About the Team**

You will be part of the Seller Growth team, responsible for driving the end-to-end seller lifecycle—from acquisition and activation to retention and scale. The team focuses on onboarding high-quality sellers, improving their early success, and enabling sustained business growth on the platform.

Operating in a fast-paced, high-ownership environment, the team combines structured problem-solving with rapid execution to unlock supply-side growth at scale.

### **About the Role**

We are looking for a Assistant Manager– Performance Marketing to join our Seller Growth team.

In this role, you will own and scale seller acquisition and activation through performance marketing channels such as Google, Meta, and emerging platforms. You will focus on driving high-quality seller onboarding, improving activation rates, and optimizing CAC and payback periods.

This role requires a strong analytical mindset, experimentation-led thinking, and the ability to translate data into actionable growth strategies.

## What You will Do

**Seller Acquisition & Channel Ownership**

- Own and scale seller acquisition across performance marketing channels (Google, Meta, affiliates, etc.)
- Drive high-quality seller sign-ups with a focus on downstream activation and retention
- Optimize campaigns for cost-efficient acquisition (CAC) while maintaining seller quality benchmarks

**Funnel Optimization (Signup → Activation → First Success)**

- Improve seller funnel metrics such as signup → KYC completion → catalog upload → first order
- Identify and reduce drop-offs across onboarding journeys
- Work closely with product and lifecycle teams to improve conversion rates

**Experimentation & Growth**

- Run structured experiments across audiences, creatives, landing pages, and funnel interventions
- Identify new growth levers such as regional expansion, category-specific acquisition, and vernacular targeting
- Test and scale new channels, formats, and campaign strategies

**Performance & Efficiency Management**

- Monitor and optimize key metrics such as CAC, activation rate, payback period, LTV/CAC, CVR, CTR, CPM
- Manage budget allocation across channels to maximize incremental seller growth
- Track and mitigate issues like audience saturation, creative fatigue, and declining marginal returns

**Insights & Analytics**

- Analyze seller cohorts by category, geography, quality, and lifecycle stage
- Derive insights on seller behavior to improve targeting and messaging
- Build frameworks for incrementality and attribution for seller acquisition

**Cross-functional Collaboration**

- Partner with product, analytics, design, and category teams to align growth efforts
- Influence onboarding experience, landing pages, and communication strategy based on performance insights

## What Will You Need

- 3+ years of experience in **performance marketing**, preferably in consumer internet, e-commerce, or app-based businesses
- Strong hands-on expertise in **Meta Ads & Google Ads**, along with a solid understanding of **incrementality frameworks**
- Strong understanding of key growth and performance metrics such as **CPM, CTR, CTI, hook rate, CAC, reach, frequency, and attribution models**
- Proficiency in **Excel/Google Sheets**; working knowledge of **SQL or analytics tools** is a plus
- Familiarity with seller or partner acquisition funnels is a plus
- Strong analytical thinking with a **structured, problem-first approach** and a **bias for action**
- Ability to operate with **high ownership** in a fast-paced environment
- A strong growth mindset with a passion for **experimentation, learning, and continuous improvement**
- Clear and effective communication skills, with the ability to work **cross-functionally and collaboratively**

Democratising internet commerce for everyone — Meesho (Meri Shop) started with a single idea in mind: to be an e-commerce destination for Indian consumers and to enable small businesses to succeed online.

Today, sellers nationwide are growing their businesses by tapping into Meesho’s large and diverse customer base, state-of-the-art tech infrastructure, and pan-India logistics network through trusted third-party partners.

Affordable, relatable merchandise that mirrors local markets has helped us connect with internet users and serve customers across urban, semi-urban, and rural India. Our unique business model and continuous innovation have established us as a part of India’s e-commerce ecosystem.
