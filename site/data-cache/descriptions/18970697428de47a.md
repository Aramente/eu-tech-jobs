<p>We are seeking an experienced and strategic Analytics Manager to lead the Everyday Banking domain (Payments, Cards, and Core Account Lifecycle) , one of our cross-functional areas shaping the development of one of the world's leading FinTech products.</p>
<h2><strong>About the opportunity</strong></h2>
<p>As an Analytics Manager, you will own the analytics strategy and execution for our Product &amp; Business Segments within the respective domain.</p>
<p>You are responsible for ensuring data is at the core of decision-making, fostering a strong data culture, and enabling teams to deliver measurable business impact. You will work in a cross-functional setup, leading a team of ~4 data analysts while partnering closely with engineers, designers, product managers, and senior business stakeholders.</p>
<p>This is a <strong>hands-on leadership role</strong>, where you are expected to split your time between:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>~50% individual contribution</strong>, focusing on the most complex and high-impact analytical problems</li>
<li><strong>~50% team leadership and management</strong>, ensuring your team delivers high-quality work and continues to grow</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>In this role, you will:</strong></h2>
<p><strong>Help us set the right goals: </strong>Define and align domain-level KPIs and OKRs, ensuring teams are focused on the highest-impact opportunities. Establish frameworks that connect product initiatives to measurable business outcomes.</p>
<p><strong>Inform product roadmap:</strong>Drive the analytics roadmap for your domain, proactively identifying strategic opportunities, risks, and trends. Ensure insights consistently shape product and business strategy across multiple teams.</p>
<p><strong>Be a strategic partner: </strong>Act as a trusted advisor to senior stakeholders, influencing decision-making through high-level insights and clear recommendations. Translate complex data into business strategy.</p>
<p><strong>Handle stakeholders: </strong>Own relationships with senior stakeholders across Product, Marketing, Tech and Leadership. Align cross-functional priorities and ensure analytics work is tightly integrated with broader business goals.</p>
<p><strong>Evaluate initiatives: </strong>Oversee experimentation and measurement strategy across teams. Ensure robust test design, execution, and interpretation, maintaining a high bar for analytical rigor and business relevance.</p>
<p><strong>Contribute to self-serve data platform: </strong>Drive the development of scalable analytics foundations in partnership with engineering. Ensure teams have access to reliable data, tools, and frameworks to independently generate insights.</p>
<p><strong>Provide monitoring tools: </strong>Ensure the team delivers high-quality dashboards and monitoring systems that track performance against company OKRs. Define standards for clarity, usability, and impact.</p>
<p><strong>Improve data literacy of organisation: </strong>Scale data literacy across the organization by enabling teams to better understand and use data. Establish training programs, frameworks, and best practices that empower self-serve analytics.</p>
<p><strong>Be part of our data-community: </strong>Lead collaboration across analytics teams, contributing to shared standards, tooling, and strategic initiatives. Act as a thought leader within the broader data organization.</p>
<p><strong>Support some of the following product &amp; business team initiatives (not limited to):</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Guide teams in discovering new product opportunities, identifying user friction points, and prioritizing high-impact initiatives</li>
<li>Ensure continuous product releases are evaluated against business goals using robust analytical methods</li>
<li>Partner with UX research to combine qualitative and quantitative insights for stronger decision-making</li>
<li>Ensure data tracking and instrumentation are properly designed to enable future analysis and measurement</li>
<li>Personally lead the most complex and ambiguous analyses within the domain, setting the standard for quality and impact</li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>What you need to be successful:</strong></h2>
<h3><strong>Background:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>5+ years of experience as an individual contributor in analytics or a related field</li>
<li>1+ year of experience managing people, leading a team of 3–4 data analysts</li>
<li>Proven track record of driving business impact through data</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Skills:</strong></h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Causal inference &amp; impact measurement:</strong> Deep expertise in methods (uplift modeling, quasi-experiments, counterfactuals) to measure true business impact beyond surface metrics.</li>
<li><strong>Experimentation:</strong> Strong track record designing and scaling A/B, multivariate, and observational analyses when experimentation isn’t feasible.</li>
<li><strong>Strategic problem-solving:</strong> Ability to structure ambiguous business problems into rigorous analytical frameworks that inform product and growth strategy.</li>
<li><strong>Analytics &amp; Engineering:</strong> High proficiency in SQL and Python/R for analysis, experimentation, and modeling.</li>
<li><strong>LLM &amp; self-serve analytics:</strong> Experience leveraging LLMs and conversational interfaces to enable scalable, self-serve analytics.</li>
<li><strong>Data storytelling:</strong> Communicates complex insights clearly using modern BI tools (Tableau, Looker, Metabase, Superset).</li>
<li><strong>Stakeholder influence:</strong> Confident communicator with fluency in English, who challenges assumptions, aligns cross-functional teams, and drives data-informed decisions.</li>
</ul>
