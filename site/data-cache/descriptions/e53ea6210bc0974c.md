We are looking to hire an experienced business development and partnerships professional to scale our efforts with agencies, systems integrators, consultancies, and commercial partners (resellers) across APAC. <p>This is an exciting opportunity to join an emerging team at Figma where you will play an active role in shaping our long-term partner strategy. You will build new partner programs from scratch and scale our existing motions to new customers and partners across APAC.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This role is based in our Singapore hub on a hybrid basis.</p>
<h4><strong>What you'll do at Figma:</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Identify, evaluate, and onboard new prospects for our official distribution partner program</li>
<li>Manage existing service and commercial partners to drive revenue for Figma while serving the needs of our APAC customers</li>
<li>Partner with Figma’s GTM teams to source, scope, and progress partner-influenced sales opportunities</li>
<li>Manage stakeholder relationships with both official and unofficial distribution partners - including identifying new co-marketing and co-selling opportunities</li>
<li>Foster long-term relationships with partners by creating mutual success plans - including documenting wins and monitoring defined KPIs</li>
<li>Evaluate opportunities to mature and streamline our relationships with resellers in context of Figma's existing sales model</li>
<li>Educate our wider partner ecosystem on Figma’s products, services, and capabilities in tandem with the partner enablement team</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>We’d love to hear from you if you have:</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>7+ years of total experience in equivalent or relevant fields</li>
<li>3+ years of experience in a GTM organization working with external professional services partners (SIs, agencies, consulting firms) and / or commercial partners (channel resellers, marketplaces, referral partners)</li>
<li>Built partner tactics, programs, and/or strategies from scratch</li>
<li>Managed relationships with multiple strategic partners at once to drive new business</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>While it’s not required, it’s an added plus if you also have:</strong></h4>
<ul>
