<p>If you’re a Product Owner who enjoys working on technically complex products, collaborating deeply with engineers, and having real influence over how a platform evolves, this role offers the opportunity to do exactly that - with plenty of room to learn and grow along the way.</p>

<p><strong>What You’ll Be Doing as a Product Owner</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Technical Product Ownership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Make informed decisions on scope, trade-offs, and release planning in collaboration with stakeholders and development teams.</li>
<li>Monitor product metrics and KPIs to assess value delivery and guide future priorities.</li>
<li>Evaluate and prioritise technical debt alongside feature development, balancing short-term delivery with long-term platform sustainability.</li>
<li>Define non-functional requirements, with a focus on scalability, performance, and capacity.</li>
<li>Assess technical feasibility and complexity of proposed features, including dependencies on platform capabilities, third-party services, and system integrations.</li>
<li>Participate in technical design reviews and architecture discussions to ensure alignment between business requirements and technical implementation.</li>
<li>Conduct impact analysis for proposed changes in collaboration with the development team.</li>
<li>Champion engineering best practices, including automated testing strategies, code quality standards, and documenting requirements.</li>
<li>Take ownership and accountability for technical and feature requirements.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Business Analysis</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Create comprehensive process flows, use cases, user stories, data models, and system integration specifications.</li>
<li>Perform gap analysis between current and future state to identify opportunities for process and system improvements.</li>
<li>Translate complex business and technical requirements into clear, testable user stories with well-defined acceptance criteria.</li>
<li>Validate solutions against business requirements throughout the development lifecycle.</li>
<li>Investigate and analyse specialist insurance practices, including underwriting processes, claims handling, rating/pricing, and policy administration.</li>
<li>Communicate specialist insurance workflows and business logic clearly to development teams.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Stakeholder Management</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and maintain strong relationships with business stakeholders and delivery partners, understanding their needs and managing expectations.</li>
<li>Facilitate requirements workshops, backlog refinement sessions, and stakeholder demonstrations.</li>
<li>Collaborate with insurance subject matter experts, actuaries, and underwriters to capture specialised knowledge and ensure accurate representation in technical solutions.</li>
</ul>
