You will join a team focused on expanding Spotify’s experience beyond our core apps into partner ecosystems and connected devices. This team works at the intersection of design, external partnerships, and emerging interaction models to bring Spotify to listeners, wherever they are. Together with product managers, engineers, researchers, and content designers, you’ll help shape seamless and adaptive experiences across integrations that reach millions of users globally.

## What You'll Do

- Build thoughtful, accessible experiences across AI assistants, chat interfaces, and connected devices.
- Use AI-assisted tools and in-build prototyping to iterate fast while maintaining design quality and clarity.
- Collaborate with localization teams and regional partners  to ensure experiences feel relevant and intuitive.
- Work closely with Content Designers (Prompt / Conversational), User Research, Product, and Engineering to deliver cohesive outcomes.
- Apply and extend Spotify’s build language to new partner contexts while maintaining craft consistency.
- Use research findings to advise decisions and continually improve accessibility and happiness in every experience.

## Who You Are

- Experience in Product Design experience across platforms, integrations, or global products.
- Competent in scaling partner agnostic foundations into partner surfaces and contexts.
- Skilled in Figma and prototyping tools; eager to explore AI-enabled workflows.
- Collaborative and curious, motivated by working with cross-cultural teams and external partners.
- Comfortable balancing speed, structure, and creativity in a fast-moving environment.
- It's a plus if you have experience crafting for APAC markets or AI-adjacent interfaces (assistants, messaging apps, OEM devices).
