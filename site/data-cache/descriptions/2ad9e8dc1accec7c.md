We are looking for an experienced **Lead Accountant** to join our Accounting team in the Limassol Office.

#### Job Responsibilities

* Responsible for performing month-end accounting activities such as reconciliations and journal entries;
* Manage and monitor accounts receivable, ensuring timely collection of fees and accurate tracking of outstanding balances;
* Lead and coordinate the annual external audit process, acting as the primary point of contact for auditors and ensuring all documentation is prepared accurately;
* Preparation of management reports and analyse organizations financial status;
* Evaluate manual and automated work processes, systems, and procedures which involve financial accounting to ensure efficiency of workflow, internal controls and adherence to accounting and auditing standards;
* Recommend and assist in the implementation of new or revised accounting systems, procedures and records;
* Preparation and maintenance of budgets and budgetary controls;
* Liaise with various external stakeholders (e.g. Banks, Auditors etc.);
* Handle VAT and TAX issues for both local and international transactions;
* Perform other accounting and finance tasks as requested by management.
