<p>Are you passionate about building food safety standards and driving product quality? <p>Our Food Safety &amp; Quality Assurance team is recruiting an experienced and highly driven FSQA Manager, based in France (FR), to support our growing local ingredient sourcing strategy while contributing to our international FSQA agenda.</p>
