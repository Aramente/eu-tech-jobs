Superside is hiring a Senior Account Manager to manage and grow a portfolio of core and growth-stage clients. You’ll own the customer experience end-to-end, driving renewals, maintaining account health, and ensuring consistent, high-quality delivery.

This role combines client partnership and operational execution. You’ll work closely with Project Managers and leadership to keep accounts stable, aligned, and positioned for long-term growth.

## What you’ll do

* Own the end-to-end relationship for a portfolio of clients, ensuring retention and strong stakeholder trust.
* Manage renewals and support moderate growth opportunities within defined commercial frameworks.
* Guide Project Managers to ensure smooth delivery and consistent quality across engagements.
* Monitor account health, revenue performance, and provide forecasting inputs to senior leadership.
* Identify and resolve risks early, acting as the main point of escalation for your accounts.
* Translate customer goals into clear, structured creative programs.
* Use performance data and insights to support QBRs and renewal conversations.
* Maintain a strong understanding of Superside’s services to identify value-driving opportunities.
* Partner with cross-functional teams to ensure alignment on delivery and account strategy.

## What you'll need to succeed

* 5+ years of experience in account management or client services within creative, digital, or professional services environments.
* Experience managing and retaining client accounts with ownership of renewals.
* Ability to lead commercial conversations within defined pricing and deal structures.
* Strong stakeholder management skills, with experience working with senior clients.
* Ability to balance strategic thinking with day-to-day execution.
* Experience working with cross-functional teams such as Project Managers and Producers.
* Comfort using data to inform account decisions and forecasting.
* Strong communication skills in English, with the ability to connect delivery to business outcomes.
