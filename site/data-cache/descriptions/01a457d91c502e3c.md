<h2><strong>What you will work on / How you will leave your footprint</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Compute &amp; sensor set design:</strong> work closely with stakeholders on definition of system architecture, components selection, and integration of compute, sensors (incl. cleaning), networking, and communication components into vehicle platforms.</li>
<li><strong>Prototype integration:</strong> plan and execute bring-up, commissioning, mechanical and electrical integration, and troubleshooting of new hardware in vehicle prototypes.</li>
<li><strong>Design verification &amp; validation:</strong> lead HW validation plans, execute test campaigns, and drive design improvements through data and root-cause analysis.</li>
<li><strong>Harnessing:</strong> design and evolve harnesses from prototype builds to <strong>production-grade</strong> solutions for autonomous defence logistics vehicles (routing, shielding, connectors, strain relief, serviceability, manufacturing readiness).</li>
<li><strong>Breadboard &amp; HIL:</strong> design, build, and commission breadboards and <strong>Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL)</strong> test setups for rapid iteration and regression testing.</li>
<li><strong>Vehicle networks:</strong> design and validate architectures using <strong>CAN</strong> and <strong>Automotive Ethernet (100/1000BASE-T1)</strong>; manage vehicle internal network configuration and interfaces.</li>
<li><strong>Connectivity systems:</strong> specify, integrate, and test external network configurations including <strong>GNSS</strong>, <strong>4G/5G</strong>, <strong>Starlink</strong>, and <strong>tactical data links</strong>.</li>
<li><strong>Integration &amp; testing:</strong> execute system-level integration, define acceptance criteria, and support field testing with fast feedback loops into engineering.</li>
<li><strong>Hands-on workshop support:</strong> actively support build-ups, harness builds, rework, and vehicle integration in the workshop—especially during intense prototype phases.</li>
<li><strong>Cross-functional leadership:</strong> work closely with autonomy, software, mechanical, manufacturing, and operations teams; provide technical direction and drive decisions.</li>
</ul>
