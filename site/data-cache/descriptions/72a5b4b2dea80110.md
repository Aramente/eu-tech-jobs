The Sewer/Water Main Team Leader is responsible for leading a 2–3 person crew in residential and small commercial underground utility projects. This role oversees the installation, repair, and replacement of sewer and water mains while ensuring safety, quality workmanship, and efficient job completion. The ideal candidate is experienced in operating small machinery and is ready to provide 24/7 emergency response support when needed.

Key Responsibilities

- Lead daily operations 2 to 4 man crew for sewer and water main replacement jobs.
- Operate small machinery including mini excavators, skid steers, compact loaders, and similar equipment.
- Drive and operate small dump trucks safely and efficiently.
- Ensure all work complies with municipal codes, OSHA standards, and industry best practices.
- Perform daily job-site safety assessments, including trench safety and shoring inspections.
- Implement and enforce proper trenching, shoring, sloping, and shielding procedures.
- Maintain strict compliance with OSHA (Excavations) guidelines.
- Train and guide crew members on proper safety protocols and equipment usage.
- Communicate with customers, inspectors, and internal staff to ensure high-quality service.
- Support 24/7 emergency sewer/water main repair response as needed.

Qualifications

- Minimum 2–5 years of underground utility experience (sewer/water preferred).
- Proven ability to lead a crew on residential utility jobs.
- Strong experience operating small excavation machinery.
- Valid driver’s license required; CDL a plus.
- Ability to read and interpret utility plans and as-builts.
- Strong problem-solving skills and mechanical aptitude.
- Excellent communication and leadership qualities.
- Able to work outdoors in varying weather conditions and perform physical labor.
