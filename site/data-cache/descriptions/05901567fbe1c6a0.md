### Field Marketing Manager – DACH

📍 Cologne, Germany (Hybrid)

### About Ibexa

Ibexa is a European marketing orchestration platform that empowers organisations to deliver seamless, data-driven customer experiences across the entire digital journey.  
By unifying content management, customer data, engagement, product information, and interactive data collection capabilities — including solutions such as Qualifio, Raptor, Quable, and Actito — Ibexa enables marketing and digital teams to break down silos and orchestrate high-impact, personalised experiences at scale.  
We are a team of more than **350 professionals across Europe**. As we continue to expand our presence in the **DACH region**, we are looking for an experienced and commercially driven marketer to lead field marketing initiatives and support regional pipeline growth.

### The Role

As **Field Marketing Manager – DACH**, you will own field marketing execution across **Germany, Austria, and Switzerland**, driving pipeline growth through a strong mix of **events, campaigns, and partner marketing activities**.  
Working closely with the **Head of Field Marketing and regional Sales teams**, you will translate global marketing strategy into **high-impact local programmes** that reflect the distinct dynamics of the German-speaking markets.  
This role is ideal for a **hands-on marketer who enjoys the fast-paced operational side of B2B marketing while contributing directly to commercial growth.**

### Key Responsibilities

#### Regional Campaign Execution

* Plan, execute, and optimise **campaigns, events, roundtables, and webinars** targeting the DACH markets
* Own the **regional marketing calendar** and coordinate local vendors
* Deliver campaigns end-to-end, from concept through **post-event follow-up with SDR teams**
* Ensure all marketing initiatives align with **quarterly Sales priorities and pipeline goals**

#### Events & Trade Shows

* Lead Ibexa’s participation in **key trade fairs, conferences, and industry events across DACH**
* Manage the full event lifecycle including **event selection, booth planning, speaker coordination, logistics, and on-site execution**
* Coordinate **post-event lead nurture and campaign follow-up**
* Identify and evaluate new **high-impact event opportunities** in the region

#### Partner & Channel Marketing

* Work closely with the **Partner team** to execute joint marketing initiatives with **regional channel partners and technology alliances**
* Co-create campaigns, co-host events, and support partner enablement initiatives
* Ensure partner marketing activities contribute to **shared pipeline targets**

#### Localization & Content Adaptation

* Localize global campaigns, content, and messaging for **German-speaking audiences**
* Ensure linguistic quality and cultural relevance in all regional marketing assets
* Work with Product Marketing to adapt **value propositions and positioning** to the competitive landscape in DACH

#### Sales Alignment & Pipeline Contribution

* Partner closely with **DACH Sales leadership** to define shared targets and priority accounts
* Deliver measurable **lead generation and pipeline contribution**
* Participate in **regional pipeline reviews** and adjust marketing plans based on commercial feedback

#### Budget Management & ROI Tracking

* Manage the **regional event and campaign budget**
* Ensure efficient allocation across activities and programmes
* Track performance and **measure ROI for all initiatives**
* Share insights and recommendations with the **Head of Field Marketing and wider marketing team**

#### Customer Marketing Collaboration

* Work with Customer Marketing to identify and activate **DACH customer success stories, case studies, and references**
* Support marketing initiatives that drive both **new customer acquisition and expansion**

#### Market Intelligence

* Provide structured insights on **local market dynamics, competitor activity, audience trends, and partner ecosystems**
* Share learnings and best practices with the **broader field marketing team across regions**
