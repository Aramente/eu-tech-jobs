<p data-renderer-start-pos="28" data-local-id="1f27e5a244f5"><strong>About the role</strong></p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="28" data-local-id="1f27e5a244f5">Biological AI models (protein folding, protein design, and large foundation models) are powerful but heavy and expensive to run. This project focuses on making them faster and more efficient at inference time without significantly hurting biological quality.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="288" data-local-id="4b1234ac6e87">You will work on profiling bottlenecks, applying model compression and architectural optimizations, and building efficient inference pipelines. The goal is to make these models practical for real-world research and production use.</p>
<p data-renderer-start-pos="522" data-local-id="c47be6e5034c"><strong data-renderer-mark="true">Your responsibilities will include:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="561" data-local-id="b9bbd1febe3c">Profile inference bottlenecks in selected biological models</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="624" data-local-id="2ab06e88ab4f">Implement and test optimization techniques (quantization, pruning, distillation)</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="708" data-local-id="960464f268eb">Explore efficient attention and architecture-level improvements</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="775" data-local-id="c346fdc4f7a4">Build and benchmark optimized inference pipelines</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="828" data-local-id="38031e6b2515">Evaluate speed, memory, and accuracy trade-offs</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="879" data-local-id="e19563c04d73">Write clean, well-documented experimental code</li>
<li data-renderer-start-pos="929" data-local-id="a70aacad77f8">Share results and practical deployment recommendations</li>
</ul>
