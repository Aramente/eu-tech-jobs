<div class="content-intro"><p><strong>COMPANY OVERVIEW</strong></p>
<p>KKR is a leading global investment firm that offers alternative asset management as well as capital markets and insurance solutions. KKR aims to generate attractive investment returns by following a patient and disciplined investment approach, employing world-class people, and supporting growth in its portfolio companies and communities. KKR sponsors investment funds that invest in private equity, credit and real assets and has strategic partners that manage hedge funds. KKR’s insurance subsidiaries offer retirement, life and reinsurance products under the management of Global Atlantic Financial Group. References to KKR’s investments may include the activities of its sponsored funds and insurance subsidiaries.</p></div><p><strong>TEAM OVERVIEW</strong></p>
<p>The role will join the KKR Global Tax team, a world-class team of tax professionals dedicated to supporting KKR’s global investment activities. Led by the Global Head of Tax based out of New York, the team encompasses experts in various areas of taxation, including transaction structuring, compliance, and strategic planning. With a global presence and a focus on collaboration, KKR Global Tax plays a pivotal role in guiding the firm’s tax strategy and ensuring compliance with tax regulations across jurisdictions. The team fosters a culture of innovation, excellence, and continuous learning, providing opportunities for professional growth and development. As part of KKR Global Tax, the EMEA Tax (House &amp; Funds) role will have the opportunity to work alongside industry-leading professionals and contribute to the success of one of the world’s premier investment firms.</p>
<p><strong>POSITION SUMMARY </strong></p>
<p>KKR is seeking a skilled and experienced tax professional to join its EMEA Tax team in London, reporting directly to the EMEA Head of Tax. The role will be a senior point of ownership for tax matters relating to KKR’s EMEA sub-advisers and the global funds they manage, with a focus on house corporate tax matters, fund structuring, investor-related tax issues and tax authority interactions. &nbsp;</p>
<p>The scope of the role is broad and therefore a strong understanding of the asset management industry, including its key complexities and focus areas, is essential, together with robust technical expertise in fund structuring and international tax.</p>
<p><strong>RESPONSIBILITIES</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Group/House Tax Structuring:
<ul>
<li>Lead and oversee tax matters relating to KKR’s EMEA house and sub-adviser entities, including corporate structuring, permanent establishment risk, transfer pricing, governance and ongoing risk management.</li>
<li>Take ownership of tax authority interactions and enquiries relating to house matters, including managing tax authority audits, investigations and dispute processes across a range of areas (e.g. partnership tax matters, employment-related securities, transfer pricing and other corporate tax matters).</li>
<li>Advise on the design and implementation of sustainable group structures across jurisdictions and support house M&amp;A and strategic initiatives.</li>
<li>Provide technical input on complex or judgement-heavy tax positions affecting house entities, including partnership matters, employment-related securities and broader international tax developments (e.g. Pillar II, CbCR and transfer pricing).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Fund Structuring &amp; LP Interface:
<ul>
<li>Represent EMEA in global fund structuring discussions, ensuring regional considerations are appropriately reflected across fund design, holding structures and investor requirements.</li>
<li>Provide tax input into new fund launches, restructurings and product evolution, including coordination with Global Tax, Legal and product teams.</li>
<li>Advise on the tax implications of fund structures for a diverse investor base, including institutional and wealth channels, ensuring alignment with commercial objectives and market practice.</li>
<li>Act as a senior point of contact for LP tax queries within EMEA, providing clear, commercially focused responses in coordination with Investor Relations and Global Tax.</li>
<li>Support the preparation and review of investor-facing tax materials, including disclosures, fund documentation and responses to due diligence requests.</li>
<li>Identify and assess emerging investor-driven tax requirements and market trends, ensuring KKR’s fund structures remain competitive and robust.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Carry and Co-Invest Structuring Support:
<ul>
<li>Advise on the structuring and ongoing management of carry, co-invest and executive incentive arrangements.</li>
<li>Oversee calculations and reporting relating to executive remuneration structures, including IBCI/AHP, carried interest and associated tax implications.</li>
<li>Partner with Product Teams, Legal, Human Capital and Finance to ensure structures are technically robust, commercially aligned and appropriately implemented.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Executive Tax Support:
<ul>
<li>Lead the implementation and ongoing management of executive tax reporting processes across EMEA, ensuring consistency and accuracy.</li>
<li>Provide corporate tax input on executive mobility and cross-border employment considerations.</li>
<li>Coordinate with Human Capital, Finance, Legal and external advisers to ensure executive tax matters are managed proactively and aligned with firm policies.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Corporate Filings
<ul>
<li>Oversee corporation tax compliance across house entities, including review of filings, coordination of external advisers and ensuring appropriate governance and risk management.</li>
<li>Oversee employment-related securities, PSA, s.431 elections, and IR35 assessments across house entities, in coordination with the Human Capital team.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>House Pillar II:
<ul>
<li>Act as EMEA lead on Pillar II implementation for House entities and coordinate with the rest of the EMEA and Global Tax teams and advisers as required.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Strategic Projects:
<ul>
<li>Support broader strategic initiatives as required and relevant for the KKR House entities.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>QUALIFICATIONS</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Professional certification such as LLM, CA, ACA, CTA, or equivalent required.</li>
<li>Minimum of ten years of relevant tax experience in a law firm, Big 4 accounting firm or in-house private equity/asset management role, with strong experience in fund structuring, carry and international tax.</li>
<li>Strong analytical skills and attention to detail, with the ability to assess complex tax issues and propose practical commercially grounded solutions.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively collaborate across diverse teams and stakeholders and exercise sound judgement.</li>
<li>Proven ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment and meet tight deadlines.</li>
</ul>
