<p>Postscript was founded in 2018 as a 100% remote organization. With more than 250 employees (and counting!), we’re backed by Greylock, Y Combinator, and other top investors.</p></div><p>As a <strong>Senior Backend Engineer</strong> on the <strong>App Systems</strong> team, you'll be a high-ownership contributor to Fondue — Postscript's cashback, discounting, and incentives product — as well as broader offers and incentives infrastructure across the platform. App Systems owns some of Postscript's most critical systems: billing, entitlements, onboarding, and core merchant data. This role sits at the center of high-margin revenue and a product with a competitive, high-visibility roadmap.</p>
<p>You'll be working on evolving, distributed systems with significant greenfield opportunity — implementing AI-first development practices, decoupling monolithic dependencies, and driving the technical standards that allow Fondue to scale alongside Postscript's core platform. This is a high-autonomy role that demands independent investigation, strong technical design instincts, and the ability to move fast without sacrificing system health.</p>
<p><strong>This position is fully remote and can be based in the US or Canada.</strong></p>
