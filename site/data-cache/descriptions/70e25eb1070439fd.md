<p><strong>Job Description</strong></p>
<div><strong>Financial Reporting &amp; Close</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Lead the month-end closure process, ensuring timely completion of books and effective coordination with Business Finance, Accounts Receivable (AR), and Accounts Payable (AP) teams.</li>
<li>Prepare complete financial statements for the entity and present financial results to internal stakeholders with clarity and actionable insights.</li>
<li>Review and ensure accurate calculation of Expected Credit Loss (ECL) provisions (Ind AS 109) in alignment with the entity’s underlying credit risk, in collaboration with the Risk team.</li>
<li>Develop and prepare accounting entries for new or complex transactions, ensuring compliance with applicable accounting standards.</li>
<li>Maintain strong working knowledge and application of Ind AS 109 (Financial Instruments) and Ind AS 115 (Revenue from Contracts with Customers), with a particular focus on ECL.</li>
</ul>
<div><strong>Regulatory Compliance &amp; Reporting (NBFC / RBI)</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Prepare and file all mandatory RBI returns on a monthly, quarterly, and annual basis.</li>
<li>Act as a subject-matter expert on RBI guidelines for NBFCs, including Digital Lending Guidelines and Scale-Based Regulations (SBR), ensuring accurate interpretation and implementation.</li>
<li>Collaborate closely with the Compliance team to ensure closure of all RBI compliance-related matters.</li>
</ul>
<div><strong>Audit &amp; Governance</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the primary point of contact for Internal, Tax, and Statutory auditors, ensuring timely and successful audit completion.</li>
<li>Work with the Secretarial team for quarterly Board Meetings, setting agendas and ensuring all statutory and regulatory resolutions are appropriately passed.</li>
<li>Monitor and report all related-party transactions (RPTs), and update the omnibus approval list as new transactions arise.</li>
</ul>
<div><strong>Stakeholder Management &amp; Strategy</strong></div>
<ul>
<li>Participate in the preparation of the quarterly Board Deck for both the Audit Committee and the Board.</li>
<li>Present liquidity risk and key business performance metrics to the ALCO (Asset Liability Committee).</li>
<li>Partner with the Treasury team by providing necessary financial data for fund-raising activities and addressing lender queries and requirements.</li>
</ul>
