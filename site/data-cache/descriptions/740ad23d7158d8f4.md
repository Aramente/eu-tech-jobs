We're looking for a high-performing **Partner Development Manager** to help scale our global ecosystem of marketing agencies.

This role will focus on identifying, recruiting, and onboarding top-tier agencies into the Omnisend Partner Program. You will play a critical role in expanding our partner ecosystem and driving new merchant acquisition through agency relationships.

Also this role is an individual contributor position with a clear path to team leadership as the agency recruitment function scales.

You’ll work closely with our partnerships, marketing, and sales teams to build a strong pipeline of agency partners and help them successfully adopt Omnisend.

## What You’ll Do:

**Recruit new agency partners**

- Identify and prospect marketing agencies globally;
- Build relationships with agency founders, marketing leaders, and consultants;
- Champion the Omnisend Partner Program with prospective agencies;
- Convert qualified agencies into program partners.

**Manage the agency recruitment pipeline**

- Maintain partner pipeline and outreach activity in HubSpot;
- Track agency recruitment metrics and report on progress;
- Continuously refine partner qualification criteria;
- Communicate internally to all stakeholders.

**Drive partner onboarding**

- Guide new agencies through the Omnisend partner onboarding process;
- Introduce partners to enablement resources and tools;
- Coordinate handoff to to Agency Onboarding or Partner Engagement Manager.
