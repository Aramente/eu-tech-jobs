<h2><strong>What you’ll do</strong></h2>
<p>We are looking for a Patient Marketing Manager to drive, analyze and improve our patient growth. As a key strategic partner, you will deploy 360° marketing plans that generate appointments, develop our B2C brand positioning and image, while following analytical performance.</p>
<p>Reporting to the Head of B2C Marketing France, you will be the strategic partner to the B2B, Sales teams, and Brand teams.</p>
<p><strong>Your Responsibilities : </strong><strong><br></strong></p>
<p><strong>Drive patient acquisition and generate business impact</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Develop, execute and monitor 360° marketing plans (organic + paid) to generate appointments on targeted specialties, in coordination with the performance Marketing expert</li>
<li>Be the strategic marketing partner to Sales and B2B teams by aligning patient acquisition efforts with business priorities</li>
<li>Analyze campaign performance, patient behaviors and market trends with a constant focus on ROI</li>
<li>Design marketing tools for practitioners with B2B teams: in-office visual materials, tools that facilitate new patient acquisition</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Patient data lead for the team</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Drive patient data analysis: monitor key KPIs (acquisition, conversion, ROI, usage) and identify trends</li>
<li>Manage strategic dashboards and conduct deep dives to inform decisions</li>
<li>Transform data into strategy: analyze patient behaviors, evaluate initiative effectiveness, produce actionable recommendations</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Lead brand strategy</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Evolve Doctolib's brand positioning beyond appointment booking with patients: define and deploy our brand strategy</li>
<li>Lead brand campaigns: strategy, creative brief, agency coordination, multi-channel deployment, impact measurement</li>
<li>Monitor and challenge brand indicators: awareness, perception, consideration</li>
<li>Ensure brand consistency across all patient initiatives</li>
</ul>
