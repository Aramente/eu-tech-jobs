<h2>The role of a Senior Growth Engineer at Canonical</h2>
<p>Canonical has provided developers with open source since 2004, helping them build innovations such as public cloud, machine learning, robotics or blockchain. Marketing at Canonical means being at the forefront of technology adoption, for our customers and for our own martech stack. We are looking for an experienced Senior Growth Engineer to join our team and take ownership of our marketing technology stack, data architecture, and infrastructure. This role is critical for designing and scaling the systems that power marketing automation, lifecycle marketing, and data-driven growth initiatives.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate will be passionate about technology, data, and the use of technology in marketing. You will thrive in an environment that emphasizes ownership, collaboration, learning, and a drive to continually improve. If you love to solve complex problems, get hands-on with cutting-edge tools, and build the automated systems that fuel a global business, we want to talk with you.</p>
<p>This role sits in the Marketing team reporting to the Growth Engineering Manager.</p>
<p><strong>Location: </strong>This role will be based remotely in the EMEA region.</p>
<h2>What your day will look like</h2>
<ul>
<li>Design, build, and maintain our core data infrastructure and martech stack to support marketing and sales goals.</li>
<li>Develop and optimize robust data pipelines, automations, and integrations between our CRM, marketing platforms, and data warehouse.</li>
<li>Architect and manage APIs for seamless system integration and data flow across the entire stack.</li>
<li>Collaborate with marketing, sales, and product teams to understand their needs and architect technical solutions.</li>
<li>Implement and champion software engineering best practices, including clean code, CI/CD, automated testing, and version control.</li>
<li>Explore and integrate cutting-edge tools to enhance our data capabilities and operational efficiency.</li>
<li>Support marketing team members with strategic expertise, setting up and monitoring automations</li>
<li>Build on automation and AI to improve lead management</li>
<li>Develop Canonical's martech stack to industry leading standard</li>
</ul>
<h2>What we are looking for in you?</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>System Design:</strong> Strong expertise in system design, particularly in the data world, with a strong understanding of networking, proxies, and data ingress/egress patterns.</li>
<li><strong>Cloud &amp; Databases:</strong> Proficiency with cloud data warehousing (e.g., BigQuery), relational databases (e.g., Postgres), and object stores (e.g., S3). Knowledge of Docker, Kubernetes, and data stores like Redis is a plus.</li>
<li><strong>API Architecture:</strong> Deep knowledge of API architecture and design, with a primary focus on REST. Experience with GraphQL is beneficial.</li>
<li><strong>Programming:</strong> Senior-level knowledge of <strong>Python</strong> (OOP, APIs, asyncio, scientific libraries) and <strong>SQL</strong> (experience with tools like Trino and dbt is highly valued).</li>
<li><strong>Web Technologies:</strong> A solid understanding of web application backend-frontend processes. Working knowledge of <strong>JavaScript</strong> is required; experience with React is a nice-to-have.</li>
<li><strong>Engineering Best Practices:</strong> A commitment to writing clean, maintainable code, with experience in CI/CD, automated testing, and version control (Git).</li>
<li><strong>Mindset:</strong> A curious and collaborative problem-solver with a growth mindset and a keen interest in the commercial domains of marketing and sales. Plus, exceptional interpersonal skills and aptitude for forging trusting relationships across diverse, cross-functional teams</li>
</ul>
