<ul>

We generate insights that build customer empathy, drive product strategy and shape products that deliver real value. <li>Data Scientists in RAD partner with teams across R&amp;D to help Intercom make sense of our users, our products and our business, using metrics, data and analysis. </ul>
<h2><strong>What will I be doing?&nbsp;</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li>You’ll partner with product teams to help them identify important questions and answer those questions with data</li>
<li>You’ll work closely with product managers, designers and engineers to develop key product success metrics, to set targets, to measure results and outcomes, and to size opportunities</li>
<li>You’ll design, build and update end-to-end data pipelines, working closely with stakeholders to drive the collection of new data and the refinement of existing data sources and tables.</li>
<li>You’ll partner closely with product researchers to build a holistic understanding of our customers, our products and our business.</li>
<li>You’ll influence our product roadmap and product strategy through exploratory analysis and quantitative research</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Increasingly, you will also:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Use AI-assisted tools (e.g. Claude Code, Cursor) to accelerate analysis, coding and insight generation</li>
<li>Identify opportunities to automate your own workflows and reduce time spent on repetitive tasks</li>
<li>Build scalable data products (dashboards, models, internal tools) that enable stakeholders to self-serve insights</li>
<li>Help teams interact more directly with data (e.g. through better abstractions, semantic layers, or AI-powered interfaces)</li>
<li>Raise the bar for how AI is used within RAD- sharing patterns, workflows and best practices</li>
<li>Craft clear, compelling data stories and communicate recommendations across R&amp;D and the broader company</li>
<li>Contribute to and shape core RAD foundations, improving how the team operates</li>
</ul>
