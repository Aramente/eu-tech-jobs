<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="66" data-end="190">Prolific is not just another player in the AI space – we are building the biggest pool of quality human data in the world.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="192" data-end="365">Over 35,000 AI developers, researchers, and organizations use Prolific to gather data from paid study participants with a wide variety of experiences, knowledge, and skills.</p>

<p style="text-align: left;" data-start="380" data-end="791">We’re looking for <strong data-start="398" data-end="443">AI Trainer – Machine Learning Specialists</strong> to help train and evaluate cutting-edge AI models using real ML expertise.
