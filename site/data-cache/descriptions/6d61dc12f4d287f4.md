<p>We are seeking an experienced <strong>EMEA Benefits Manager</strong> to oversee and optimize our regional benefit programs. Based in Dublin and reporting to our US-based leadership, you will lead the implementation, day-to-day administration, and compliance of Okta's Total Rewards offerings across the EMEA region.</p>
<p>The ideal candidate is a data-driven problem solver with strong business acumen, capable of translating complex data into financial projections and actionable strategy. You must be an execution-focused leader who excels at managing multiple deadlines, building cross-functional relationships, and influencing stakeholders at all levels of a global, matrixed organization.</p>
<h3><strong>What you’ll be doing</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Deep Regional Expertise:</strong> Serve as the subject matter expert for complex, region-specific programs, particularly focusing on pension governance, healthcare trusts, and employee councils across EMEA.</li>
<li><strong>Vendor Procurement &amp; Governance:</strong> Own the full vendor lifecycle for regional insurance carriers, brokers, and third-party administrators. Lead comprehensive onboarding, overseeing contract negotiations, security protocols, and data privacy reviews, while driving ongoing accountability through SLAs and Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs).</li>
<li><strong>Strategic Administration &amp; Integration:</strong> Manage the end-to-end administration of health, retirement, and wellbeing initiatives. Drive seamless data exchange between vendors, payroll, and internal HR platforms to minimize administrative friction and maximize data integrity.</li>
<li><strong>Compliance, Risk &amp; Benchmarking:</strong> Proactively monitor the EMEA regulatory landscape and conduct regular audits to ensure strict compliance with local laws. Conduct regional market research to ensure offerings remain highly competitive.</li>
<li><strong>Cross-Functional Partnership:</strong> Partner closely with EMEA Payroll, People Operations (POPS), Finance, and Legal to ensure flawless program delivery, accurate taxation, and cohesive alignment across G&amp;A functions.</li>
<li><strong>Data &amp; AI Integration:</strong> Utilize Workday and ServiceNow to track and report on key metrics. Leverage AI tools to analyze plan utilization, forecast costs, and personalize employee communication.</li>
<li><strong>Program Evolution:</strong> Challenge the status quo to streamline administrative burdens. Document, implement, and automate processes in close partnership with the People Operations team.</li>
<li><strong>Employee Experience &amp; Escalations:</strong> Act as the highest level of regional escalation for complex benefits inquiries, ensuring a high-touch, empathetic, and seamless experience for Okta employees.</li>
<li><strong>Communication &amp; Education:</strong> Act as a subject matter expert for the People &amp; Places community, managers, and employees, ensuring clear, effective delivery of benefit updates and educational resources.</li>
<li><strong>Global Strategy Alignment:</strong> Maintain the team’s global project portfolio via SmartSheets and assist with overarching global rewards strategy initiatives as required, ensuring EMEA is represented in global design.</li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>What you’ll bring to the role</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li><strong>Experience:</strong> Degree in a related discipline with 8+ years of experience in a regional Benefits role within a multinational environment.</li>
<li><strong>EMEA Expertise:</strong> In-depth knowledge of complex benefits regulations, labor laws, and tax compliance across the EMEA region.</li>
<li><strong>Analytical &amp; Financial Rigor:</strong> A metrics-focused mindset with the ability to interpret quantitative/qualitative data, project financial impacts, and translate insights into actionable business recommendations.</li>
<li><strong>Execution &amp; Project Management:</strong> Highly execution-driven with the ability to manage multiple, deadline-focused initiatives with high precision and autonomy in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.</li>
<li><strong>Relationship Building &amp; Influence:</strong> A track record of building trust across a matrixed organization, with the communication skills to present information effectively and influence diverse stakeholders at all levels.</li>
<li><strong>Technical Proficiency:</strong> Strong command of Google Workspace and Workday, AI tools (i.e. Gemini, Notebook LM).</li>
</ul>
