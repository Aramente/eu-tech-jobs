<p><strong>The Team</strong> You will lead the evolution of our global recruiting operations function. In this role, you will partner closely with Talent Acquisition, People, Finance, and cross-functional stakeholders to improve hiring efficiency, elevate decision-making, and enhance the experience for candidates, interviewers, and hiring managers.</p>
<p><strong>Role:</strong> This is an opportunity to scale a high-impact recruiting engine at a critical phase of growth. You will design and operationalize the programs, processes, and enablement strategies that power consistent, high-quality hiring outcomes across the business. If you view recruiting as a system of interconnected workflows and are excited about improving how teams hire through data, insights, and operational rigor, this role is for you.</p>
<p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p>
<h4><strong>Recruiting Operations &amp; Process Excellence</strong></h4>
<ul>
<li>Design and continuously improve scalable, global recruiting processes that enable high-quality hiring at speed</li>
<li>Design and implement a recruiting coordination process that's scalable and efficient&nbsp;</li>
<li>Identify friction points across the hiring lifecycle and drive process improvements that increase efficiency and consistency</li>
<li>Establish clear operating rhythms, SLAs, and performance standards across recruiting teams</li>
<li>Lead change management efforts to roll out new processes and ensure adoption across regions and functions</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Enablement &amp; Hiring Excellence</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build and deliver onboarding and enablement programs for recruiters and hiring managers to improve hiring effectiveness</li>
<li>Develop frameworks for structured interviewing, evaluation quality, and consistent decision-making</li>
<li>Create playbooks, training, and best practices that elevate hiring manager capability and interviewer performance</li>
<li>Partner with TA leadership to reinforce high standards for candidate experience and hiring outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Data, Insights &amp; Performance</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Define and track key recruiting metrics (e.g., time-to-fill, pipeline health, conversion rates, interviewer effectiveness)</li>
<li>Translate data into actionable insights that improve team performance and inform decision-making</li>
<li>Partner with Finance and TA leadership on hiring forecasts, capacity planning, and performance analysis</li>
<li>Build reporting frameworks that bring visibility and accountability to hiring outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Cross-Functional Partnership</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner closely with Talent Acquisition, People, Finance, and business leaders to align on hiring priorities and execution</li>
<li>Collaborate with systems and IT partners to ensure tools effectively support recruiting workflows (without owning the systems directly)</li>
<li>Act as a connective layer between strategy and execution, ensuring hiring goals translate into operational outcomes</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Leadership &amp; Organizational Impact</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Build, lead, and develop a high-performing Recruiting Operations team</li>
<li>Drive consistency and operational excellence across global recruiting teams</li>
<li>Serve as a strategic partner to TA leadership on performance, process improvements, and scaling the function</li>
<li>Create a culture of continuous improvement, accountability, and data-driven decision-making</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>Requirements</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>8+ years of experience in Recruiting Operations, Talent Operations, or related roles.</li>
<li>Proven track record of improving recruiting processes and driving operational excellence at scale.</li>
<li>Strong experience with recruiting metrics, analytics, and performance management.</li>
<li>Experience building enablement programs and improving hiring manager and interviewer effectiveness.</li>
<li>Excellent stakeholder management and cross-functional collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Strong program management and change management capabilities.</li>
<li>A practical, data-driven mindset with a focus on execution and outcomes.</li>
</ul>
