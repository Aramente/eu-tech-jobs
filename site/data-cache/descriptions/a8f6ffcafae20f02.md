### **Position description**

As as Group Product Manager at Sonar, you own the product vision and strategy for the Code Quality domain, leading a team of Product Managers to define and enforce modern coding practices for millions of developers.

## What you will do

- **Strategy & Vision:** Define a compelling product direction and roadmap that connects company goals to technical innovation in code analysis.
- **Team Leadership:** Lead, coach, and grow a squad of Product Managers, ensuring high standards in product practices and career development.
- **Business Impact:** Drive measurable outcomes by balancing new revenue, protecting Sonar's market moat (coverage depth/breadth), and fostering innovation in detection capabilities.
- **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Partner with Engineering, UX, and Domain Leadership to align on investments, prioritization, and domain-level operating mechanisms.

## Experience and qualifications

- **Leadership:** Typically 8+ years in Product Management, with 3+ years specifically managing PMs or leading multi-squad areas in B2B/developer-focused companies.
- **Technical Domain:** Proven track record in complex technical domains (e.g., developer tools, static analysis, or platforms).
- **Execution:** Expertise in problem discovery, outcome-oriented roadmapping, and translating technical complexity into simple narratives.
- **Tooling Knowledge:** Familiarity with modern SDLC tools like CI/CD, IDEs, and DevOps practices.
