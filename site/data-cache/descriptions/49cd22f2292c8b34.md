- Strong understanding of **databases and data systems**, including PostgreSQL and other storage models; demonstrated ability to diagnose and resolve performance and reliability issues.
- Hands-on experience with **cloud infrastructure**, containers, and orchestration technologies, including Docker and Kubernetes.
- High standards of engineering quality, with an emphasis on maintainable, testable, secure, and well-structured code.
- Excellent communication skills and the ability to influence technical direction across teams.
- Strong commitment to continuous learning, proactive problem identification, and improvement of systems, processes, and engineering practices within the Cyber Threat Intelligence field.
